Amazfit is a Chinese smart wearable brand, established in September 2015 and headquartered in Hefei, Anhui Province. Its products are manufactured and owned by Zepp Health. The brand offers wearable devices including smartwatches, fitness bands,smart ring and equipment related to health and sports.

History
In August 2015, Huami Corporation(later renamed Zepp Health Corporation) launched Amazfit, a new smart wearable device brand. In August 2016, the first smartwatch of Amazfit was introduced. In April 2017, it launched the Amazfit Health Band.

In March 2019, a self-developed chipset called the "Huangshan-1"  was exhibited at Mobile World Congress and applied to Amazfit devices in 2019.

In April 2020, its parent company-Huami Corporation partnered with a laboratory led by Zhong Nanshan to use wearable devices to track respiratory diseases.

By the end of 2021, the brand had a market share of 5.1% in smartwatches, ranking fifth in the market.
