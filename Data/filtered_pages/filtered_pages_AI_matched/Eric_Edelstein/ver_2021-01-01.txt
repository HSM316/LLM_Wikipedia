Eric Edelstein (born April 23, 1977) is an American actor, character actor and voice actor. He is best known for playing the role of Bobby Mallison in Shameless, Chad in Clarence, B.O.B. in the Monsters vs. Aliens television adaptation, and Grizzly "Grizz" in We Bare Bears.

He also had a supporting role in the 2015 horror film Green Room, and he plays a paddock supervisor in Jurassic World.
