Ashley Chastain is an American softball coach who is the current head coach at Charlotte.

Coaching career
College of Charleston (asst.)
On October 22, 2013, Chastain was announced as assistant coach of the College of Charleston softball program.
Michigan State (asst.)
On July 20, 2016, Chastain was announced as the new pitching coach of the Michigan State softball program.
Ole Miss (asst.)
On November 1, 2017, Chastain was announced as the new pitching coach of the Ole Miss softball program.
Charlotte
On June 6, 2019, Chastain was announced as the new head coach of the Charlotte softball program.
