Yacine Bammou (Arabic: ياسين بامو; born 11 September 1991) is a professional footballer who plays as a striker for Turkish club Alanyaspor, on loan from Stade Malherbe Caen, and the Morocco national team.

Club career
Bammou made his Ligue 1 debut on 9 August 2014 against RC Lens in a 1–0 home win replacing Fernando Aristeguieta after 64 minutes. One minute later he scored the winning goal.

In July 2018, he signed a four-year contract with Stade Malherbe Caen.
