Emile Paul Tendeng (born 9 March 1992 in Senegal) is a Senegalese professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Ilves Tampere.http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.paul-emile.tendeng.105720.en.html

Career
The assistant coach of Casa Sport describes him as “football car crash, prodigy and player with extraordinary potential”, and compares him with Iain Dowie.http://fr.allafrica.com/stories/201003240870.html

In 2013, Tendeng moved to FC Vaslui on loan from Casa Sport for six-months.

In February 2016, Tendeng signed a two-year contract with Finnish club FC Ilves.

On 30 October 2017, Tendeng signed a one-year contract with Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho, with the option of an additional year.
