 is the 13th single by Pink Lady. It was released on 5 July 1979, with a peak position on the Oricon charts of #4, and also a #4 position on the Japanese Music Labo chart.

The B-side, an alternate version of the song used on their Magical Musical Tour studio album, featured The Beach Boys as backing vocals.

The single was also featured on the Japanese music program The Best Ten, where it peaked at #7.

It sold approximately 550,000 copies.
