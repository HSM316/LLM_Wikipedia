The 2008 Ondrej Nepela Memorial () was the Ondrej Nepela Memorial competition for the 2008–09 figure skating season. It was the 16th edition of the Ondrej Nepela Memorial to be held. The Ondrej Nepela Memorial is an annual senior-level international figure skating competition held in Bratislava, Slovakia. Skaters competed in the disciplines of men's singles, ladies' singles, and ice dancing.

The 2008 competition was held at the Ondrej Nepela Ice Rink between November 22 and 23, 2008. The compulsory dance was the Paso Doble.
