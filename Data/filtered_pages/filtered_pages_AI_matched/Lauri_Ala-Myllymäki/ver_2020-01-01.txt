Lauri Ala-Myllymäki (born 4 June 1997 in Pirkkala) is a Finnish football midfielder playing for Finnish Veikkausliiga club FC Ilves . He has played for Finland in various youth national teams and is regarded as one of the most promising Finnish players in his age group.
