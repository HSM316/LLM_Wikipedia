Aleš Mandous (born 21 April 1992) is a Czech professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Olomouc.

Club career

FC Viktoria Plzeň
On 23 March 2013, Mandous made his competitive debut in the second leg of the fourth round of the Czech Cup against FC Hradec Králové. Plzeň won the game 1-0 and advanced to the next round.

FK Bohemians Prague
He spent the following season on loan at FK Bohemians Prague. Mandous played 18 games in this campaign, which ended with the club being relegated to the third flight of Czech football.

FK Baník Most 1909
In the summer of 2014, he was sent on loan. This time he joined FK Baník Most 1909, who were at that time in the second tier of Czech football. Mandous played in 18 matches and was relegated with the club in 2015.

MŠK Žilina
Mandous joined MŠK Žilina as a free agent in June 2015, when he signed a two-year contract with the club. On 12 August 2015, he made his competitive debut in the Slovak Cup match against ŠK Strážske, which Žilina won 9-0.

International career
On 4 June 2013, Mandous played his sole game for the Czech Under-21 team. In this match, the Czech Republic lost 3-0 to Austria.
