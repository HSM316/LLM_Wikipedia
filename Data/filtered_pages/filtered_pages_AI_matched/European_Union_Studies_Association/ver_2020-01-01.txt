The European Union Studies Association (EUSA) (founded 1988) is a scholarly and professional association with its focus on the European Union, the processes of its integration, and its transatlantic relations. http://www.eustudies.org/

It notes valuable contributions to European Studies via various prizes including the EUSA Award for Lifetime Achievement in European Studies.
