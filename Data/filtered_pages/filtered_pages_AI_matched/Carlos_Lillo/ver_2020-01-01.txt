Carlos Lillo (born October 2, 1915, date of death unknown) was a Chilean boxer, who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.

In 1936, he was eliminated in the quarter-finals of the lightweight class, after losing his fight to the upcoming silver medalist Nikolai Stepulov.
