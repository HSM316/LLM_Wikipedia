Maha Purusha (Kannada: ಮಹಾಪುರುಷ) is a 1985 Indian Kannada film, produced and directed by Joe Simon, H. G. Raju, K. S. Nataraju and D. J. Nayak. The film stars Vishnuvardhan, Gayatri, Jai Jagadish and Chandrashekar in the lead roles. The film has musical score by Chellapilla Satyam.
