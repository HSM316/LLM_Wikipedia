Celedonio B. "Don" Trollano Jr. (born January 7, 1992) is a Filipino professional basketball player for the Blackwater Elite of the Philippine Basketball Association (PBA).

College and amateur career

Trollano studied at Adamson University and played for the Adamson Falcons from 2012 to 2014.UAAP Profile at PBA-Online!  He also suited up for the Cagayan Rising Suns of the PBA Developmental League.

Professional career

Trollano was drafted by the Rain or Shine Elasto Painters with the 15th overall pick in the 2015 PBA draft.

On October 25, 2015, Trollano recorded 8 points, 3 rebounds and 4 assists in a win over the Columbian Dyip in his 2nd career game played.

On November 11, 2015, Trollano recorded his first double-digit scoring output as he scored 12 points to go along with 3 rebounds in a win over the Alaska Aces.
