Indina Ramayana () is a 1984 Indian Kannada-language film, directed by Rajachandra and produced by Dwarakish. The film stars Vishnuvardhan, Gayatri, Sridhar and Tulasi. The film has music by Vijay Anand. It is a remake of the Tamil film Oorukku Upadesam. The movie had a successful run of 25 weeks in Bangalore and Mysore.
