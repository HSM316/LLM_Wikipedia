Indina Ramayana () is a 1984 Indian Kannada-language film, directed by Rajachandra and produced by Dwarakish. The film stars Vishnuvardhan, Gayatri, Sridhar and Tulasi. The film has musical score by Vijayanand. The movie is a remake of the Tamil movie Oorukku Upadesam. The movie had a successful run of 25 weeks in Bangalore and Mysore.
