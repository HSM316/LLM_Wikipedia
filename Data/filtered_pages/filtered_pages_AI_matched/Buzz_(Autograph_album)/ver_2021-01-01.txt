Buzz is the fifth studio album by American hard rock band Autograph. Apart from lead singer and songwriter Steve Plunkett, the band has a completely new line up of musicians. The original band recorded three albums in the mid-eighties, and called it quits in 1989. The "Buzz" lineup did the same in 2005.
