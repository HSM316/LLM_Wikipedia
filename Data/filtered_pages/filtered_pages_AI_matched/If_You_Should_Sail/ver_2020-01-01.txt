"If You Should Sail" is a song by American soft rock duo Nielsen Pearson. It was released as a single in 1980 from their self-titled album.

The song peaked at No. 38 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 35 on the Adult Contemporary chart, the latter becoming the duo's only Top 40 hit.
