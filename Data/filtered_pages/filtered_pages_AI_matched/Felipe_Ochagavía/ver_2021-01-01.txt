Felipe Ochagavía Eguiguren, (born 9 September 1993) is a Chilean retired footballer that who played as goalkeeper.

Career

Youth career

Ochagavía started his career at Primera División de Chile club O'Higgins. He progressed from the under categories club all the way to the senior team.

O'Higgins
Ochagavía won the Apertura 2013-14 with O'Higgins. In the tournament, he didn't play in any game.http://eltipografo.cl/2013/12/el-cielo-es-celeste/

In 2014, he won the Supercopa de Chile against Deportes Iquique. In that match, he was in the bench as the second goalkeeper.http://www.anfp.cl/noticia/20957/ohiggins-gano-la-supercopa-al-vencer-a-deportes-iquique

He participated with the club in the 2014 Copa Libertadores where they faced Deportivo Cali, Cerro Porteño and Lanús, being third and being eliminated in the group stage.
