Göcek Tüneli () is a road tunnel in Dalaman, Muğla. The tunnel goes through D400 road. It takes its name from Göcek. It was opened on 9 September 2006, by the president of the term Recep Tayyip Erdoğan. The tunnel was built as a single tube in the first phase. In 2015, the second tube section was built by the state for 65 million liras in the then currency.
