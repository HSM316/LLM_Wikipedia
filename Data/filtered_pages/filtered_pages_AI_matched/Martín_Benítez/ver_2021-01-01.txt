Martín Nicolás Benítez (born June 17, 1994) is an Argentine footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder and winger for Independiente.

Club career
Benítez began playing football in his home town team called The Picada.http://afterdeportivo.wordpress.com/2010/07/23/martin-benitez-%E2%80%9Cgracias-a-mi-tio-estoy-en-independiente%E2%80%9D-2/ He caught the eye of Boca Juniors who offered a trial that never materialized.http://ponealospibes.blogspot.com/2011/06/martin-benitez-goleador-de-las.html

As a result, Benitez quit soccer because of depression.http://www.misionesonline.net/noticias/07/02/2011/benitez-el-juvenil-que-pisa-fuerte-en-independiente He was encouraged back into football by his family and Independiente called him up for a trial in 2009.http://www.ole.com.ar/independiente/sueno-pibito_0_594540655.htmlhttp://www.afa.org.ar/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=13602:fue-designado-el-plantel-que-disputara-el-campeonato-sudamericano&catid=167:seleccion-sub-17&Itemid=69

He travelled to the Netherlands with the reserve team to play a friendly against Ajax.http://www.infiernorojo.com/independiente/2011/07/duro-traspie-en-holanda/ Although the team lost 5-1, he scored the only goal against goalkeeper Kenneth Vermeer.

On October 13, 2011 he was called up to the first team by coach Ramón Díaz.

His debut came on November 19, when Independiente defeated Olimpo de Bahia Blanca 3-0 playing the last twenty minutes of the match. His debut goal came on December 4, 2011 against Newell's Old Boys in a 1-1 draw.

His second goal via a header came on December 8, 2011 against San Lorenzo de Almagro.http://www.ole.com.ar/futbol-primera/Necesidades-diferentes_0_605339802.html
