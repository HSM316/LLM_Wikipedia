Daniel John Mooney (born 3 July 1999) is a Welsh professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Altrincham.

Club career
Mooney is a product of the Fleetwood Town Academy. On 9 October 2018 Mooney made his debut for Fleetwood in the starting line up for a 2–0 defeat against Rochdale in the EFL Trophy. In May 2019 Mooney signed a one year contract extension with Fleetwood.Mooney extends Fleetwood contract

On 4 January 2020, it was announced Mooney had joined National League North side Altrincham for an undisclosed fee. He made his debut the same day, starting in a 5–2 win against Telford.
International career
Mooney has played for Wales at under-19 and under-21 levels.
