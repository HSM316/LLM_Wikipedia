Amine Khammas (born 6 April 1999) is a professional footballer who plays as a left back for Belgian side Waasland-Beveren.

 Career 
Khammas played with Genk as a junior. He made his first team debut with K.R.C. Genk in the Belgian Pro League on 29 July 2017 against Waasland-Beveren.

International career
Khammas was a youth international for Belgium at the U17 level. He later represented the Morocco U18s in a pair of friendlies against Portugal national under-18 football team in January 2017. He received a call-up to the senior Morocco national football team in August 2017.
