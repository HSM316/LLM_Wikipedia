Heather Oesterle is the current head coach of Central Michigan women's basketball team. Oesterle was named CMU’s head women’s basketball coach in July, 2019, succeeding her long-time mentor Sue Guevara. Oesterle served for nine seasons on Guevara’s staff at CMU, helping lead the program to unprecedented heights including three Mid-American Conference championships, two MAC Tournament titles, and five MAC West Division crowns. She earned her bachelor’s degree in kinesiology from Michigan in 2002 and her masters in sports studies from Miami in 2008.
