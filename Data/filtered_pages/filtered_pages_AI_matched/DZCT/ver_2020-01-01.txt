DZCT, broadcasting as Radio City 97.5, is a music FM radio station owned and operated by DCG Radio-TV Network. The station's studio and transmitter are located at 1022 DCG Tower 1, Maharlika Hi-Way, Brgy. Isabang, Tayabas, Quezon.NTC 2014 List

The frequency is formerly owned by Vanguard Radio Network, under the brand Big Sound FM from January 5, 1997 to September 14, 2014.
