Marco Gabriel Damasceno Alves (born 11 April 1996), known as Marco Damasceno, is a Brazilian footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Real.

Club career
Born in Brasília, Distrito Federal, Damasceno joined Atlético Paranaense's youth setup in 2011, after representing a Santos youth school in his hometown.
