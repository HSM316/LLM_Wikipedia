Aristote M'Boma (born 30 June 1994 in Kinshasa) is a Congolese professional footballer who plays as a forward for IFK Mariehamn.

Career
On 12 November 2018, M'Boma joined fellow Veikkausliiga side IFK Mariehamn from SJK on a two-year contract.
