Les Présages is a ballet choreographed by Léonide Massine to music from Tschaikovsky's Symphony No. 5, with sets and costumes by André Masson. The premiere was performed on 13 April 1933 at the Opéra de Monte Carlo by the Ballets Russes de Monte-Carlo.Norton, p.154

The ballet was Massine's first experiment with a plotless work based on symphonic music.BolshoiMoscow.com: extract from Massine's autobiography, My Life in Ballet
