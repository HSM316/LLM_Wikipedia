The 2007 WKU Hilltoppers football team represented Western Kentucky University (WKU) during the 2007 NCAA Division I FBS football season. The team's head coach was David Elson. After leaving the Gateway Football Conference, it was the team’s first of two seasons as an NCAA Division I FBS independent team. The Hilltoppers played their home games at Houchens Industries–L. T. Smith Stadium in Bowling Green, Kentucky.
