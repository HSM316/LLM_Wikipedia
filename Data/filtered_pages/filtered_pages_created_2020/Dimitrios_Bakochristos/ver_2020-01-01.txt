Dimitrios Bakochristos is a Greek Paralympic powerlifter. He represented Greece at the 2016 Summer Paralympics and he won the bronze medal in the men's 54 kg event. Bakochristos lifted 162 kg, the same as Bruno Carra who represented Brazil, and Bakochristos won the medal as his body weight was lower.

At the 2019 World Para Powerlifting Championships he won the bronze medal in the men's 54 kg event.
