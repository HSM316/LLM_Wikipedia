Saket Elhami () is an Iranian football manager who recently managed Tractor in Persian Gulf Pro League.

He played as a player for Pas, Esteghlal Ahvaz, and Tractor. He also was Firouz Karimi's assistant in Esteghlal between 2007 and 2008 years.
