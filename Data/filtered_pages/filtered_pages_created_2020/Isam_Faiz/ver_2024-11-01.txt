Isam Faiz (; born 6 March 2000) is a footballer who plays as a midfielder for UAE Pro League club Ajman and the United Arab Emirates national team. Born in Morocco, he acquired Emirati citizenship in 2023.

International career
Faiz made his debut for the senior United Arab Emirates national team on 26 March 2024 in a World Cup qualifier against Yemen.
