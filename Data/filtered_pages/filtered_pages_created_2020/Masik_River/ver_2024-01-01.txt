The Masik River is a river on Banks Island, in Canada's Northwest Territories.  It is a part of the Arctic Ocean watershed."Masik River". Canadian Geographical Names Data Base. Natural Resources Canada. Retrieved 7 March 2020.

The river is named after the polar researcher August Masik (1887–1976).Härm, Tiiu (2010). Jää voolab: Eesti polaarränduri August Masiku maailm. Tallinn: Eesti Raamat.
