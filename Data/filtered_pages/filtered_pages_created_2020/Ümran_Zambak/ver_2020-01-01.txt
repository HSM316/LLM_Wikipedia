Ümran Zambak (born 27 November 2001\0) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Kayserispor .

Professional career
On 3 September 2019, Zambak signed his first professional contract with Kayserispor. Zambak made his professional debut for Kayserispor in a 6-2 Süper Lig loss to Trabzonspor on 28 December 2019.
