Sacing Prue Jerry is a Bangladeshi politician from Bandarban belonging to Bangladesh Nationalist Party. He was a member of the Jatiya Sangsad. He is the son of Aung Shwe Prue Chowdhury. His aunt Ma Mya Ching  is a former member of the Jatiya Sangsad.

Biography
Jerry is a prince of Bohmong Circle. He was elected as a member of the Jatiya Sangsad from Bandarban in the Sixth General Election of Bangladesh.  He also served as the chairman of Bandarban Sadar Upazila and Bandarban Hill District Council.
