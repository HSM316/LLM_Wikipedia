The 1989–90 season is Real Madrid Club de Fútbol's 88th season in existence and the club's 59th consecutive season in the top flight of Spanish football.

 Season 
Real Madrid finished the campaign as league champions for the fifth season running, also, new coach John Benjamin Toshack and the team reached the record of most goals in a season. Mexican striker Hugo Sanchez scored 38 goals in league equalizing Telmo Zarra record. This was its 25th league title in history.
