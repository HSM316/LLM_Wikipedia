Yaqoob Ijaz Butt (born 9 September 1988) is a footballer who plays as a defender. Born in Denmark, he represents Pakistan at the international level.

 International career 
Yaqoob was first called by the Pakistan national team coach Graham Roberts for the 2010 Asian games, but incomplete documents prevented his participation.

He received his first international cap against Nepal in 2013, as a replacement for the injured international defender Atif Bashir under the coach Zaviša Milosavljević.

 Personal life 
His brother, Yousuf Butt, is also a footballer and has played alongside him for Pakistan.
