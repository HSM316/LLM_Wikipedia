Andrei Sepci (7 October 1911 – 3 December 1992) is a Romanian former footballer and manager. He was the manager that guided Știința Cluj in it's successful 1964–65 Cupa României campaign.

International career
Andrei Sepci played at four games at international level for Romania, including three clean sheets in the successful 1933 Balkan Cup.
