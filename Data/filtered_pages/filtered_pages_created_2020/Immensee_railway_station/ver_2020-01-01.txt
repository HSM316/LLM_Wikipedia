Immensee railway station () is a railway station in Küssnacht, in the Swiss canton of Schwyz. It sits at the junction of three railway lines: the Gotthard railway, Lucerne–Immensee railway, and the Aargau Southern Railway. It is served by local trains only.

 Services 
Immensee is served by the S2 of the Zug Stadtbahn and S3 of the Lucerne S-Bahn:

 : peak-hour service over the Aargau Southern Railway to Rotkrezu and the Gotthard railway to Flüelen.
 : hourly service over the Lucerne–Immensee railway to Luzern and the Gotthard railway to Brunnen.
