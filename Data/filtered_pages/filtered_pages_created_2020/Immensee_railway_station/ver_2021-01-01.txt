Immensee railway station () is a railway station in the municipality of Küssnacht, in the Swiss canton of Schwyz. It located at the junction of three standard gauge lines of Swiss Federal Railways: the Gotthard, Lucerne–Immensee, and Rupperswil–Immensee.

 Services 
The following services stop at Immensee::

 Zug Stadtbahn : peak-hour service over the Rupperswil–Immensee line to Rotkrezu and the Gotthard line to Flüelen.
 Lucerne S-Bahn : hourly service over the Lucerne–Immensee line to  and the Gotthard line to Brunnen.
