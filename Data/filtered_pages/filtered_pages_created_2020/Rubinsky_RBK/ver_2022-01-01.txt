Manuel Enrique Núñez Espino, musically known as Rubinsky RBK, is a Dominican Christian music rapper from Santo Domingo.

 Career 
He is considered one of the most promising artists of Christian rap. Some of his most successful songs are: "Sigo aquí", "Amor y Pasión", "Na" and "Te Necesito".

Rubinsky RBK has collaborated with artist such as Redimi2, Lizzy Parra, Manny Montes, Dkano and others. He gained fame with the song “Trapstorno” with Redimi2, Natan El Profeta and Philippe, which gained 100 million views on YouTube.
