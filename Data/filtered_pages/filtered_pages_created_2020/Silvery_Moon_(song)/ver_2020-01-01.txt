Silvery Moon is the tenth single by Australian rock band Sherbet, released in 1974.45cat - Sherbet - Silvery Moon It was released as the third and final single from the album Slipstream and it was written by Garth Porter and Clive Shakespeare.Australian Charts - Sherbet - Silvery Moon The song reached number 5 on the Kent Music ReportAustralian Music Database - Sherbet - Silvery Moon
