The Europe/Africa Zone is one of the three zones of the regional Davis Cup competition in 1990.

In the Europe/Africa Zone there are two different tiers, called groups, in which teams compete against each other to advance to the upper tier. Winners in the Europe Zone Group II advance to the Europe/Africa Zone Group I.
