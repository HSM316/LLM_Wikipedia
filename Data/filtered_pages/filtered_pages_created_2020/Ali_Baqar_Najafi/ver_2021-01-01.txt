Ali Baqar Najafi (born 15 September 1963) is a Pakistani jurist and a senior Judge of the Lahore High Court since 16 April 2012 and member of Administration Committee of Lahore High Court.

Early life and education
Najafi was born on 15 September 1963 in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. His father Ali Huzoor Najafi was an advocate  Supreme Court of Pakistan. He completed his basic education from Government Muslim Model High School located in Urdu Bazaar, Lahore. He completed his LLB from Punjab University Law College, Lahore in 1989.

 Family life 
He hails from a noble family, his father was a senior Advocate Supreme Court of Pakistan and enjoy a high value respect in legal fraternity and he was an active Rotarian and member of Lahore Gymkhana. His wife and elder son Ali Najfi are medical Dr. and government officers whereas his younger son Haidar Najfi ia an Advocate who joined Mandviwalla & Zafar & Legal Consultants. His younger brother is settled in Australia.

Career
In 1989, he started his legal career as an advocate under the mentorship of his father in Lahore. He registered as an advocate of High Courts in 1990. He continued on to become the advocate of Supreme Court of Pakistan. He attended and read papers in many international conferences and seminars etc.

He was a faculty at Punjab University Law College, Lahore for twelve years. He also taught in private law colleges for eighteen years. He also served as internal and external examiner for University of the Punjab.
