Madison Lake State Park is a state park in the Darby Plains region of Madison County, Ohio.Madison Lake State Park.  Ohio Department of Natural Resources.  2020.  It surrounds the  Madison Lake for which it was named.

The lake was formed in 1947 on Deer Creek, in Madison County, Ohio, and handed over to the Ohio Department of Natural Resources in 1950.  The small community of Madison Lake, Ohio, also named after the reservoir, lies on the western shores of the lake.Cross, Tom. Fishing Ohio: An Angler's Guide To Over 200 Fishing Spots In The Buckeye State. Rowman & Littlefield. May 1, 2008. Pgs. 55-56.

The park offers a beach, boat ramp, picnic area, and shelter house, as well as maintaining a hiking trial around the lake.  Although the park headquarters are located at Deer Creek State Park, a small office is maintained on site.
