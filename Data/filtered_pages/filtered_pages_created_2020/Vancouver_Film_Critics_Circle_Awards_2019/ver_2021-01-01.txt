The nominations for the 19th Vancouver Film Critics Circle Awards, honoring the best in filmmaking in 2019, were announced on December 16, 2019.Adrian Mack, "Marriage Story dominates Vancouver Film Critics Circle noms". The Georgia Straight, December 13, 2019. Winners in the international categories were announced on December 16,Etan Vlessing, "'Parasite' Named Best Picture by Vancouver Film Critics Circle". The Hollywood Reporter, December 16, 2019. and winners in the Canadian film categories were announced on January 6, 2020."Vancouver film critics award 'The Body Remembers When the World Broke Open'". Tri-City News, January 7, 2020.
