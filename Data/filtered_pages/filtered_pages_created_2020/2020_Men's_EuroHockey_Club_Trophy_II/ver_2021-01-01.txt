The 2020 Men's EuroHockey Club Trophy II would have been the 44th edition of the EuroHockey Club Trophy, Europe's tertiary men's club field hockey tournament organized by the European Hockey Federation and the first edition since it was renamed from the EuroHockey Club Trophy. It would be held in Lisburn, Northern Ireland from 28 to 31 May 2020.

The tournament was canceled on 23 March 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
