Sylvia Marianne Malmberg Liljefors (born November 9, 1944; also known as Sylvia Malmberg and Sylvia Malmberg-Liljefors) is a Swedish female curler.Landslagsspelare genom tiderna – Damer - Svenska Curlingförbundet (Swedish female national curlers; look at "Malmberg, Sylvia")

In 2005 she was inducted into the Swedish Curling Hall of Fame.
