He served as the head football coach at Northern Arizona University, then known as Arizona State Teachers College at Flagstaff, from 1947 to 1948. He also serve as the school's head men's basketball coach during that span.

He previously served as a head coach at a number of high schools in Arizona, including Miami High School in Miami, Arizona.

Ragus played college football at Saint Mary's College of California.
