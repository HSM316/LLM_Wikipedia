Küssnacht am Rigi railway station () is a railway station in Küssnacht, in the Swiss canton of Schwyz. It is an intermediate stop on the Lucerne–Immensee railway and is served by local and long-distance trains.

 Services 
 Long-distance 
Küssnacht am Rigi is served by the Voralpen Express, which runs hourly between Lucerne and St. Gallen.

 Local
