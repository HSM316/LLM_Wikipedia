The 2020 Men's EuroHockey Club Trophy I will be the first edition of the EuroHockey Club Trophy I, Europe's secondary men's club field hockey tournament organized by the European Hockey Federation. It will be held in Vienna, Austria from 29 May to 1 June 2020.
