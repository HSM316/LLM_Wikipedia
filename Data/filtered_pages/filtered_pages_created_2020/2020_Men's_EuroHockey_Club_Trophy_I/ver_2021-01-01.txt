The 2020 Men's EuroHockey Club Trophy I would have been the first edition of the EuroHockey Club Trophy I, Europe's secondary men's club field hockey tournament organized by the European Hockey Federation. It would be held in Vienna, Austria from 29 May to 1 June 2020.

The tournament was canceled on 23 March 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
