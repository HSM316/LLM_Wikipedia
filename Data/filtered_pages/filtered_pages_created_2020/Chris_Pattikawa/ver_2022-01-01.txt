Christian Pattikawa (1 October 1940 â€“ 1 January 2020) was an Indonesian film director and producer. 

Pattikawa was born in Ambon. In 1974, he led the film company P.T. Aurea Aristo Film. Actress Rina Hassim was his wife. They had a son, singer Jean Pattikawa. Chris was a contemporary of composer Enteng Tanamal and directors Nico Pelamonia and Jopijaya Burnama.

Death

He died in Jakarta on 1 January 2020, at the age of 79. He was buried few days later at Pondok Ranggon Cemetery. Sutradara Chris Pattikawa Meninggal Dunia
