The Barge Inn is an independent public house and restaurant in Grimsby, United Kingdom. Housed within a refitted former grain barge, it has been moored in the town centre Riverhead quay since 1982.

History
The Barge was crane lifted into Grimsby's Riverhead quay from the adjacent Alexandra Dock in 1982. In 2017, the establishment was threatened with removal to accommodate North East Lincolnshire Council plans to build a cinema complex in Grimsby town centre.
