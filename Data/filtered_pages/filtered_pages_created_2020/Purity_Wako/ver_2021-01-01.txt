Purity Kategaya Wako is a Ugandan life coach, nutritionist and activist.

Wako was born in Ibanda and studied at Makerere University Business School. She currently lives in Kampala.

As a senga, she advises new wives on how to please their husbands, but expands the role, wanting Ugandan women to have equal rights in marriage. She founded her own company, KweraBITS, in 2013.

In 2019, she was listed among the BBC's 100 Women, a list of 100 inspiring and influential women.
