Ma Mya Ching is a Bangladeshi politician from Bandarban belonging to Bangladesh Nationalist Party. She was a member of the Jatiya Sangsad.

Biography
Ma Mya Ching is Aung Shwe Prue Chowdhury's co-sister-in-law and she is Saching Prue Jerry's aunt. She was elected as a member of the Jatiya Sangsad from Reserved Women's Seat-30 in the 5th Jatiya Sangsad Election.
 She was also elected as a member of the Jatiya Sangsad from Reserved Women's Seat in the Sixth General Election of Bangladesh.
