Ece Çeşmioğlu (born 26 November 1990) is a Turkish actress.

Biography
Ece Çeşmioğlu was born on 26 November 1990 in Istanbul. She graduated from the Theater Department of Mimar Sinan University State Conservatory. She started her acting career at the age of 12 in Çocuklar Duymasın. Çeşmioğlu then drew attention with her performances in various TV series. In 2017, she played the character of Zeynep in the series Yüz Yüze. (English: Face to Face).
