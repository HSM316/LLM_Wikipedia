Shafey Kidwai (born 8 April 1960) is an Indian academic, columnist and author. He is the head of the Department of Mass Communications at Aligarh Muslim University. He writes a weekly column for The Hindu. Kidwai has produced twelve books in Urdu and English. His 2017 work Sawaneh-e-Sir Syed: Ek Bazdeed - a biography of Sir Syed won the 2019  Sahitya Akademi Award for Urdu. A previous work Urdu Literature and Journalism: Critical Perspective was published by Cambidge University Press. In 2018, Kidwai was awarded the Iqbal Samman by the Government of Madhya Pradesh for his service to promotion of Urdu literature
