Altstätten Stadt railway station () is a railway station in Altstätten, in the Swiss canton of St. Gallen. It is the eastern terminus of the  Altstätten–Gais line and is served by local trains only. 

 Connections 
The station is located near the center of the town and ca.  to the west of Altstätten SG railway station, which is an intermediate stop on the  Chur–Rorschach line with additional local and long-distance services. The two stations are linked by bus lines  and  of Rheintalbus.
