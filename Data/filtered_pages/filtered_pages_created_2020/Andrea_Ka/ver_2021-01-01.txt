Andrea Daravy Ka (born 25 April 1992) is a Cambodian tennis player. She is the first Cambodian tennis player to win an ITF tennis tournament.

Ka has a career high WTA singles ranking of 560, achieved on 12 June 2018. She also has a career high WTA doubles ranking of 466, achieved on 24 September 2018.

In 2017, Ka won a bronze medal in the Women's singles event at the 2017 Southeast Asian Games. She also represented Cambodia at the 2014 Asian Games in Incheon.
