The KBF Premier League is the premier basketball league for clubs in Kenya. The league consist out of twelve teams. As of December 2019, the defending champions are Ulinzi Warriors.

The champions of the KBF Premier League are eligible to play in the qualifying rounds of the Basketball Africa League (BAL).
