Biography
Roaza was born on 9 January 1946 in Kawkhali, Rangamati. He provided financial help and arms to the freedom fighters of Chittagong during the Liberation War of Bangladesh. He was elected as a member of the Jatiya Sangsad from Chittagong Hill Tracts-2 as an independent candidate in 1973.

Roaza died on 9 January 1994 at the age of 63.
