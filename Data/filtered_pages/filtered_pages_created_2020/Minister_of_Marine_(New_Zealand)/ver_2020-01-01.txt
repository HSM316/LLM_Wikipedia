The Minister of Marine in New Zealand was a former cabinet member appointed by the Prime Minister to be responsible for New Zealand's aquaculture and fishing industries. Similar duties are preformed today by the Minister of Fisheries.

List of ministers
The following ministers held the office of Minister of Marine.
