Kajenglei or Leitreng is a Manipuri headdress worn especially by goddesses, Royal ladies and dancers. It is believed to be developed during the reign of King Gambhir Singh in the kingdom of Manipur. It is made from the plant Khekwai which grows abundantly in Hilly areas of Manipur.
