Biberegg railway station () is a railway station in Rothenthurm, in the Swiss canton of Schwyz. It is an intermediate stop on the Pfäffikon–Arth-Goldau railway and is served by local trains only.

 Services 
Biberegg is served by the S31 of the Lucerne S-Bahn:

 : hourly service between Arth-Goldau and Biberbrugg.
