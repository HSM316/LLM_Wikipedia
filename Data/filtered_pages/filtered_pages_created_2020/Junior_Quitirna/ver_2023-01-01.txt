Armando Junior Quitirna (born 25 April 2000) is a Bissau-Guinean professional footballer who plays for Waterford in the League of Ireland Premier Division.

Career

Charlton Athletic
Quitirna made two appearances for Charlton Athletic, both in cup games before it was announced on 18 May 2021 that he would leave the club at the end of his contract.

Waterford
It was announced that had Quitirna made the move to League of Ireland Premier Division side Waterford on 9 July 2021. On the same day, he made his debut for the club, scoring a penalty in a 1–0 win away to Finn Harps. On 30 July 2021, he came off the bench away to Drogheda United in the 75th minute and scored the winning goal 8 minutes later in a 2–1 win for his side. He made a total of 20 appearances in all competitions over the season, scoring 7 goals as the club were relegated to the League of Ireland First Division.
