Charles J. Boyle (c. 1877 – April 21, 1947) was an American football player and coach. 

Boyle played college football for Dartmouth in 1896, 1897, 1898, 1899, and 1900, graduating in 1901.https://books.google.com/books?id=XpQXAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA349&lpg=PA349&dq=boyle+wentworth+dartmouth&source=bl&ots=d8UIxFLSvz&sig=ACfU3U1vJ2l43fUJFzCMT3zxMe0n-BnEXA&hl=en&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjzh_ny6t7mAhWEY98KHb6dCnwQ6AEwA3oECAgQAQ#v=onepage&q=boyle%20wentworth%20dartmouth&f=false  He was an assistant football coach at Dartmouth for a couple years and was the head football coach at Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio in 1903.
