Jorge Luiz Barcellos (born 17 April 1967) is a Brazilian football manager.

Career
Barcellos was the head coach of the Brazil women's national team at the 2007 FIFA Women's World Cup, 2008 Summer Olympics and 2012 Summer Olympics. He was also the head coach of Saint Louis Athletica in 2009.
