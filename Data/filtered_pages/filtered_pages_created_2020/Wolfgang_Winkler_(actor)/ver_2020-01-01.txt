Wolfgang Winkler (2 March 1943 – 7 December 2019) was a German actor, born in Görlitz. He was best known for starring in films such as The Rabbit Is Me, Das Mädchen auf dem Brett and I Was Nineteen, as well as playing  Hauptkommissar Herbert Schneider in the television series Polizeiruf 110. Wolfgang Winkler (†76) Hallescher Polizeiruf-Star ist gestorben
