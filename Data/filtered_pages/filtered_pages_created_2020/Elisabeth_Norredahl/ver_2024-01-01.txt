Anna Margareta Elisabet "Bettan" Norredahl (née Hansson; born 2 October 1967 in Östersund) is a Swedish female curler.Landslagsspelare genom tiderna – Damer - Svenska Curlingförbundet (Swedish female national curlers; look at "Hansson, Elisabeth") (web archive)

In 2013 she was inducted into the Swedish Curling Hall of Fame.
