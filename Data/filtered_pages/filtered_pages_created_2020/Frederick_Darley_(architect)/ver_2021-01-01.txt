Frederick Darley, was an Irish Architect, who designed and built many buildings in Trinity College Dublin. He was also responsible for a number of civic and Church buildings. He was a son of the builder and architect Frederick Darley Senior, his father Alderman Darley served as Lord Mayor of Dublin in 1808-09. His mother was Elizabeth (Guinness) Darley, eldest daughter of Arthur Guinness of Beaumont, Drumcondra.Frederick Darley (Senior) Dictionary of Irish Architects.
