Adrian Stoicov (12 November 1967 – 15 March 2017) was a Romanian footballer who played as a left-back. After he ended his playing career he worked as an assistant and youth coach. He died at 49 years after suffering from lung cancer.

Honours
Politehnica Timișoara
 Divizia B: 1988–89, 1994–95
 Cupa României: Runner-up 1991–92 
UM Timişoara
 Divizia C: 1998–99
