Sattel-Aegeri railway station () is a railway station in Sattel, in the Swiss canton of Schwyz. It is an intermediate stop on the standard gauge Pfäffikon–Arth-Goldau line of Südostbahn.

 Services 
The following services stop at Sattel-Aegeri:

 Lucerne S-Bahn : hourly service between Arth-Goldau and Biberbrugg.
