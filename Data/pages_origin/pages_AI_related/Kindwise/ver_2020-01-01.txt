A Czech company founded in 2014 by three PhD students Ondřej Veselý, Ondřej Vild and Jiří Řihák<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.firmo.cz/en/spolecnost/3283526/flowerchecker-sro}}</ref>. The company provides a plant identification service using the mobile application Flowerchecker for Android<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=cz.thran.flowercheckerplus&hl=en_US|title=FlowerChecker+, plant identify - Apps on Google Play|website=play.google.com}}</ref> and iOS<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://apps.apple.com/us/app/flowerchecker-plant-identify/id916709270|title=‎FlowerChecker, plant identify|website=App Store}}</ref>.

The application was nominated in 2014 for the Křišťálová lupa<ref>{{cite web |title=Křišťálová lupa 2014 |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141021231235/http://kristalova.lupa.cz/2014/nominovane-projekty/ |website=Křišťálová lupa}}</ref>, a Czech award for the most exciting project on the internet in the mobile service category.

Plants are identified by specialists in FlowerChecker. It helps internally develop a machine learning system that classifies photos and suggests species names to specialists<ref>{{cite web |title=Aplikace rozpoznává rostliny z celého světa. Díky botanikům i umělé inteligenci |url=https://brnensky.denik.cz/zpravy_region/aplikace-rozpoznava-rostliny-z-celeho-sveta-diky-botanikum-i-umele-inteligenci-20160908.html |website=Brněnský deník}}</ref> 

In May 2018, the company launched Plant.id <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://plant.id/|title=Plant.id - Plant identification app|website=plant.id}}</ref>- a fully automatic plant learning service by machine learning <ref>{{cite web |title=Tvůrci FlowerCheckeru spouštějí Shazam pro kytky. Plant.id staví na AI a má velké plány |url=https://tyinternety.cz/startupy/tvurci-flowercheckeru-spousteji-shazam-pro-kytky-plant-id-stavi-na-ai-a-ma-velke-plany/ |website=Ty internety}}</ref>.  A year later, Plant.id won Nápad roku (Idea of ​​the Year) award at the AI ​​Awards <ref>{{cite web |title=V soutěži AI Awards uspěli tvůrci aplikací na určování rostlin nebo na rozpoznávání únosců po hlase |url=https://byznys.ihned.cz/c1-66563320-v-soutezi-ai-awards-uspeli-tvurci-aplikaci-na-urcovani-rostlin-nebo-na-rozpoznavani-unoscu-po-hlase |website=iHNed.cz}}</ref> The service uses the neural network trained in plant photos designed by specialists for this classification task<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LU0VwSlrSfI|title=MLMU.cz - FlowerChecker: Exciting journey of one ML startup – O. The service is offered in the form of an API and is focused (the target client are companies)  on companies, such a B2B business model.<ref>{{cite journal |last1=NIEDERMEIEROVÁ |first1=Jana |title=Aplikace pomocí umělé inteligence určí téměř 7000 druhů rostlin. |journal=Hospodářské novin |date=2019-05-15 |volume=2019 |issue=Speciální příloha Umělá Inteligence. |page=3.}}</ref>
