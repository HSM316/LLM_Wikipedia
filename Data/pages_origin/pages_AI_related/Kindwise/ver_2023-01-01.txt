The '''FlowerChecker app''' was launched in April 2014 for Android and later that year for iOS.<ref name=":6" /><ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /> It requires users to submit photographs of the object they want to identify.<ref name=":6" /><ref name=":0" /> The photographs are then examined by an international team of experts from the field of [[botany]] and [[horticulture]].<ref name=":6" /><ref name=":0" />

'''Plant.id''' is a machine learning-based plant identification API launched in 2018,<ref name=":7" /> with the plant diseases identification API - Plant.id Health Assessment released in April 2022.<ref name=":9" /> The Plant.id API is suitable for integration into other software such as mobile apps.<ref name=":5">{{cite journal |last1=Jones |first1=Hamlyn G |title=What plant is that? Tests of automated image recognition apps for plant identification on plants from the British flora |journal=AoB Plants |date=23 September 2020 |volume=12 |issue=6 |pages=plaa052 |doi=10.1093/aobpla/plaa052 |pmid=33173573 |pmc=7640754 |url=|issn=2041-2851}}</ref>

== Recognition ==
In 2019, FlowerChecker won the Idea of the Year award in the AI Awards organized by the Confederation of Industry of the Czech Republic.<ref name=":8">{{cite web |title=V soutěži AI Awards uspěli tvůrci aplikací na určování rostlin nebo na rozpoznávání únosců po hlase |url=https://byznys.ihned.cz/c1-66563320-v-soutezi-ai-awards-uspeli-tvurci-aplikaci-na-urcovani-rostlin-nebo-na-rozpoznavani-unoscu-po-hlase |language=cs|trans-title=Authors of applications for plant identification or voice recognition of abductors were successful at the AI Awards|website=ihned.cz |date=13 May 2019 |accessdate=14 November 2020 |quote=Quote: ‘Dalším zajímavým projektem, který si ze soutěže AI Awards odnesl ocenění za nápad roku, je Plant.id. Mobile application is a follow-up to the 4 year old FlowerChecker app project.
