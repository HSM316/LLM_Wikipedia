== Features & Tools ==
FlowerChecker offers two different solutions - FlowerChecker mobile app and Plant.id API.<ref name=":7">{{cite web |title=Tvůrci FlowerCheckeru spouštějí Shazam pro kytky. Plant.id staví na AI a má velké plány |url=https://tyinternety.cz/startupy/tvurci-flowercheckeru-spousteji-shazam-pro-kytky-plant-id-stavi-na-ai-a-ma-velke-plany/|language=cs|trans-title=The creators of FlowerChecker are launching Shazam for plants. Plant.id is based on AI and has big plans|website=tyinternety.cz | date=7 May 2018 |accessdate=14 November 2020}}</ref>

The '''FlowerChecker app''' was launched in April 2014 for Android and later that year for iOS.<ref name=":6" /><ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /> It requires users to submit photographs of the object they want to identify.<ref name=":6" /><ref name=":0" /> The photographs are then examined by an international team of experts from the field of [[botany]] and [[horticulture]].<ref name=":6" /><ref name=":0" />

'''Plant.id''' is a machine learning-based plant identification API launched in 2018,<ref name=":7" /> with the plant diseases identification API - Plant.id Health Assessment released in April 2022.<ref name=":9" /> The Plant.id API is suitable for integration into other software such as mobile apps.<ref name=":5">{{cite journal |last1=Jones |first1=Hamlyn G |title=What plant is that? Tests of automated image recognition apps for plant identification on plants from the British flora |journal=AoB Plants |date=23 September 2020 |volume=12 |issue=6 |pages=plaa052 |doi=10.1093/aobpla/plaa052 |pmid=33173573 |pmc=7640754 |url=|issn=2041-2851}}</ref>

== Recognition ==
In 2019, FlowerChecker won the Idea of the Year award in the AI Awards organized by the Confederation of Industry of the Czech Republic.<ref name=":8">{{cite web |title=V soutěži AI Awards uspěli tvůrci aplikací na určování rostlin nebo na rozpoznávání únosců po hlase |url=https://byznys.ihned.cz/c1-66563320-v-soutezi-ai-awards-uspeli-tvurci-aplikaci-na-urcovani-rostlin-nebo-na-rozpoznavani-unoscu-po-hlase |language=cs|trans-title=Authors of applications for plant identification or voice recognition of abductors were successful at the AI Awards|website=ihned.cz |date=13 May 2019 |accessdate=14 November 2020}}</ref> In 2020, an academic study comparing ten free automated image recognition apps showed that the Plant.id's performance excelled in the most of the studied parameters.<ref name=":5" /> In an independent study comparing different image-based species recognition models and their suitability for recognizing invasive alien species, the Plant.id API achieved the highest accuracy compared to other plant species recognition tools.<ref name=":10">{{cite journal |last1=Jakuschona |first1=Nick |last2=Niers |first2=Tom |last3=Stenkamp|first3=Jan |last4= Bartoschek |first4=Thomas |last5=Schade |first5=Sven |date=18 January 2022 |title=Evaluating image-based species recognition models suitable for citizen science application to support European invasive alien species policy |url=https://op.europa.eu/en/publication-detail/-/publication/91584805-79a1-11ec-9136-01aa75ed71a1/language-en |journal=European Commission, Joint Research Centre |doi=10.2760/97305 |isbn=9789276467212 |access-date=27 June 2022}}</ref>
