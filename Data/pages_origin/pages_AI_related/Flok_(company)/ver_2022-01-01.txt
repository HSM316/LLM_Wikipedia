'''flok''' (formerly Loyalblocks)<ref>{{cite news|title=Your next loyalty 'punch card' will be driven by AI|url=https://venturebeat.com/2016/04/07/your-next-loyalty-punch-card-will-be-driven-by-ai/|accessdate=26 July 2016|work=[[VentureBeat]]}}</ref> was an American [[tech startup]] based in [[New York City]] that provides marketing services such as [[chatbots]]/[[AI]],<ref>{{cite web|title=Flok uses Bluetooth and chatbots to lure people inside thousands of small businesses|date=26 July 2016|url=https://venturebeat.com/2016/07/26/flok-uses-bluetooth-and-chatbots-to-lure-people-inside-thousands-of-small-businesses/|publisher=[[VentureBeat]]|accessdate=27 July 2016}}</ref> [[Loyalty program|customer loyalty programs]], [[mobile apps]] and [[Customer relationship management|CRM]] services to local businesses.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Forbes Communications Council |title=How Conversational UX And The Internet Of Things Are Changing The Way We Communicate|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbescommunicationscouncil/2016/07/06/how-conversational-ux-and-the-internet-of-things-are-changing-the-way-we-communicate/#60c8184a249e|accessdate=26 July 2016|work=[[Forbes]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Should Your Business Create an App?|url=https://www.americanexpress.com/us/small-business/openforum/articles/business-create-an-app/|accessdate=26 July 2016|work=[[American Express]] OPEN Forum|date=25 March 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Small Business Loyalty Programs : flok|url=http://www.trendhunter.com/trends/flok|publisher=Trend Hunter|accessdate=26 July 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Loyalty Is Not Enough â€” It's All About Customer Relationship Management Now|url=http://www.geomarketing.com/loyalty-is-not-enough-its-all-about-customer-relationship-management-now|accessdate=26 July 2016|work=[[Geomarketing]]|date=7 June 2016}}</ref>

In January 2017, the company was acquired by [[Wix.com]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2017/01/19/wix-buys-flok-to-add-loyalty-and-mobile-crm-to-its-suite-of-business-tools/|title=Wix buys Flok to add loyalty and mobile CRM to its business tools|last=Lunden|first=Ingrid|date=19 January 2017|website=[[TechCrunch]]|publisher=|access-date=23 January 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.globes.co.il/en/article-wix-acquires-israeli-crm-co-flok-1001173124|title=Wix acquires Israeli CRM co flok - Globes|website=Globes|date=22 January 2017|language=he|access-date=2018-06-07}}</ref>

Around March 2017, flok ceased communication.

On October 1, 2019, flok communicated via email stating that Flok is shutting down on March 1st, 2020.

On March 1, 2020, flok communicated via email stating that [https://stampme.com/is-flok-closing-down-or-being-discontinued/ Flok has officially shut down].<ref>{{cite web |title=Is Flok Closing Down or Being Discontinued? |url=https://stampme.com/is-flok-closing-down-or-being-discontinued/ |website=Stamp Me loyalty Solutions|date=22 November 2019 }}</ref>

==Background==
flok was founded in 2011 by Ido Gaver and Eran Kirshenboim and has offices in [[Tel Aviv]], [[Israel]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Zimmerman|first1=Eilene|title=LoyalBlocks Simplifies Mobile App Creation for Small Merchants|url=http://boss.blogs.nytimes.com/2014/09/16/loyalblocks-simplifies-mobile-app-creation-for-small-merchants/?_r=1|accessdate=26 July 2016|work=[[The New York Times]]}}</ref> In May 2013, flok secured a $9 million Series A Round from [[General Catalyst Partners]] with participation from Founder Collective and existing investor Gemini Israel Ventures.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thenextweb.com/insider/2013/05/23/nyc-based-loyalblocks-raises-9m-for-its-automated-mobile-loyalty-program/#gref|title=LoyalBlocks Nabs $9M for Its Automated Mobile Loyalty Program|date=23 May 2013}}</ref> In total, flok has raised over $18 million in [[venture capital]] in three rounds.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Taylor|first1=Colleen|title=LoyalBlocks Lands $9 Million Led By General Catalyst To Scale Out Its Mobile-Based Loyalty Program|url=https://techcrunch.com/2013/05/23/loyalblocks-9-million-funding/|publisher=[[TechCrunch]]|accessdate=26 July 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Your next loyalty 'punch card' will be driven by AI|date=7 April 2016|url=https://venturebeat.com/2016/04/07/your-next-loyalty-punch-card-will-be-driven-by-ai|publisher=[[VentureBeat]]|accessdate=26 July 2016}}</ref>

In May 2014, flok announced a self-service loyalty platform for SMBs to build their own programs with beacon integration. At that time, approximately 40,000 businesses were using the service.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mediapost.com/publications/article/225373/loyalblocks-adds-beacon-to-self-serve-loyalty-plat.html|title=LoyalBlocks Adds Beacon to Self-Serve Loyalty Platform for SMBS}}</ref> In 2016, flok released a turnkey chatbot service for local businesses,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://venturebeat.com/2016/07/26/flok-uses-bluetooth-and-chatbots-to-lure-people-inside-thousands-of-small-businesses/|title=Flok uses Bluetooth and chatbots to lure people inside thousands of small businesses|date=26 July 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://marketingland.com/flok-wants-bring-chatbots-small-business-masses-186056|title = Flok wants to bring chatbots to the small business masses|date = 27 July 2016}}</ref> and was featured in [[AdWeek]] for developing the first "weed bot" chatbot for a [[California]] [[cannabis]] business.<ref>{{cite web|title=This California Marijuana Dispensary Is Using Bots to Understand Customers' Preferences|url=http://www.adweek.com/news/technology/california-marijuana-dispensary-using-bots-understand-customers-preferences-172238|publisher=[[Adweek]]|accessdate=26 July 2016}}</ref> 

==Services==
flok offers an eponymous customer-facing app<ref>{{cite web|title=flok - get rewarded on the App Store|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/flok-get-rewarded/id376560037?mt=8|publisher=[[App Store (iOS)]]|accessdate=26 July 2016}}</ref> that consumers use to receive rewards and deals form partner businesses,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/startup-loyalblocks-helps-small-businesses-zap-deals-rewards-customers-smartphones-article-1.1154135|title = Startup LoyalBlocks helps small businesses zap deals and rewards to customers' smartphones}}</ref> and a flok business app for merchants to manage the platform.<ref>{{cite web|title=flok business on the App Store|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/flok-business/id1102450236?mt=8|publisher=[[App Store (iOS)]]|accessdate=26 July 2016}}</ref> Both are available for iOS and Android operating systems.{{Citation needed|date=May 2019}} flok's main products center around customer loyalty and rewards. The platform offers a digital punch card, [[proximity marketing]], push messaging and CRM services in addition to its chatbots and AI features.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://streetfightmag.com/2016/07/26/flok-launches-chatbots-to-bolster-customer-engagement-for-local-businesses/|title = Flok Launches Chatbots to Bolster Customer Engagement for Local Businesses &#124; Street Fight|date = 26 July 2016}}</ref>

flok provides partner businesses with [[beacons]] that can locate customers and verify store visits.<ref>{{cite web|title=Beacons 101: What Smart Brick-and-Mortar Retailers Need to Know {{!}} Payment Week|url=http://paymentweek.com/2016-5-17-beacons-101-what-smart-brick-and-mortar-retailers-need-to-know-10534/|publisher=Payment Week|accessdate=26 July 2016|date=17 May 2016}}</ref> As of July 2016, flok had a 4-star rating on Merchant Maverick review site.<ref>{{cite web|title=Flok Review - Merchant Maverick|url=https://www.merchantmaverick.com/reviews/flok-review/|publisher=Merchant Maverick|accessdate=26 July 2016}}</ref>
