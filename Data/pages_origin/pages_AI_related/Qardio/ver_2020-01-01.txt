'''Qardio, Inc.''' is an American technology company that specializes in heart health monitoring products. It was founded by Marco Peluso and Rosario Iannella in early 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfgate.com/technology/article/Dad-inspires-app-linked-blood-pressure-heart-5134844.php |title=Dad inspires app-linked blood pressure, heart monitors - San Francisco Chronicle |author=Benny Evangelista|date=11 January 2014|work=San Francisco Chronicle}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.businessinsider.com/qardioarm-and-qardiocore-2014-1?IR=T|title=QardioArm and QardioCore - Business Insider|author=Megan Rose Dickey|date=8 January 2014|work=Business Insider}}</ref>

Qardio's first product, QardioArm, is a wireless blood pressure monitor that connects with Apples's [[Health (Apple)|Health app]]; it received FDA 510K clearance in June 2014.<ref>{{cite news|title=FDA clears Qardio's sleek, connected blood pressure device|url=http://www.mobihealthnews.com/34310/fda-clears-qardios-sleek-connected-blood-pressure-device/|website=MobiHealthNews|author= Aditi Pai|date=20 June 2014}}</ref>

At CES 2015, Qardio announced two new products, QardioBase – smart scale and body analyzer and QardioMD – a medical dashboard for doctors.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://9to5mac.com/2015/01/05/9to5macs-ces-2015-sponsor-qardio-offers-first-look-at-its-new-iphone-connected-health-sensors/|title=9to5Mac’s CES 2015 sponsor Qardio offers first look at its new iPhone-connected health sensors|website=9to5Mac|author=Jeremy Horwitz|date=5 January 2015}}</ref> QardioBase became available for sale in November 2015.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.androidguys.com/2015/11/17/qardiobase-smart-scale-and-body-composition-analyzer-now-available-in-time-for-the-holidays/|title=QardioBase Smart Scale and body composition analyzer now available just in time for the holidays|website=Android Guys|date=17 November 2015}}</ref>

Qardio launched the second generation of its [[Weighing scale|smart scale]], Qardio Base 2, in September 2017.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://9to5mac.com/2017/08/31/qardio-launches-new-qardiobase-with-improved-sensors-wider-base/|title=Qardio Launches New QardioBase with Improved Sensors and Wider Base|website=9to5Mac|date=31 August 2017|author=Greg Barbosa}}</ref>

QardioCore ambulatory ECG/EKG monitor received [[CE marking]] in August 2017.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.getqardio.com/news/qardiocore-receives-ce-clearance-launch-uk-eu/|title=QardioCore receives CE Mark and launches commercially in the European Union and the UK|website=Get Qardio}}</ref>
