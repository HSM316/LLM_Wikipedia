'''Qardio, Inc.''' is an American technology company that specializes in heart health monitoring products. It was founded by Marco Peluso and Rosario Iannella in early 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfgate.com/technology/article/Dad-inspires-app-linked-blood-pressure-heart-5134844.php |title=Dad inspires app-linked blood pressure, heart monitors{{dash}}San Francisco Chronicle |author=Benny Evangelista|date=11 January 2014|work=San Francisco Chronicle}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.businessinsider.com/qardioarm-and-qardiocore-2014-1?IR=T|title=QardioArm and QardioCore{{dash}}Business Insider|author=Megan Rose Dickey|date=8 January 2014|work=Business Insider|access-date=23 February 2016|archive-date=2 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160302122938/http://www.businessinsider.com/qardioarm-and-qardiocore-2014-1?IR=T|url-status=live}}</ref>

Qardio's first product, QardioArm, is a wireless [[Sphygmomanometer|blood pressure monitor]] that connects with Apple's [[Health (Apple)|Health app]]; it received FDA [[510(k)]] clearance in June 2014.<ref>{{cite news|title=FDA clears Qardio's sleek, connected blood pressure device|url=http://www.mobihealthnews.com/34310/fda-clears-qardios-sleek-connected-blood-pressure-device/|website=MobiHealthNews|author=Aditi Pai|date=20 June 2014|access-date=17 October 2017|archive-date=20 August 2024|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240820010206/https://www.mobihealthnews.com/34310/fda-clears-qardios-sleek-connected-blood-pressure-device/|url-status=live}}</ref>

At CES 2015, Qardio announced two new products, QardioBase, a smart scale and body analyzer, and QardioMD, a medical dashboard for doctors.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://9to5mac.com/2015/01/05/9to5macs-ces-2015-sponsor-qardio-offers-first-look-at-its-new-iphone-connected-health-sensors/|title=9to5Mac's CES 2015 sponsor Qardio offers first look at its new iPhone-connected health sensors|website=9to5Mac|author=Jeremy Horwitz|date=5 January 2015|access-date=23 February 2016|archive-date=23 February 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160223050812/http://9to5mac.com/2015/01/05/9to5macs-ces-2015-sponsor-qardio-offers-first-look-at-its-new-iphone-connected-health-sensors/|url-status=live}}</ref> QardioBase became available for sale in November 2015.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.androidguys.com/2015/11/17/qardiobase-smart-scale-and-body-composition-analyzer-now-available-in-time-for-the-holidays/|title=QardioBase Smart Scale and body composition analyzer now available just in time for the holidays|website=Android Guys|date=17 November 2015}}</ref>

Qardio launched the second generation of its [[Weighing scale|smart scale]], Qardio Base 2, in September 2017.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://9to5mac.com/2017/08/31/qardio-launches-new-qardiobase-with-improved-sensors-wider-base/|title=Qardio Launches New QardioBase with Improved Sensors and Wider Base|website=9to5Mac|date=31 August 2017|author=Greg Barbosa|access-date=8 November 2017|archive-date=20 August 2024|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240820010137/https://9to5mac.com/2017/08/31/qardio-launches-new-qardiobase-with-improved-sensors-wider-base/|url-status=live}}</ref>

QardioCore ambulatory ECG/EKG monitor received [[CE marking]] in August 2017.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.getqardio.com/news/qardiocore-receives-ce-clearance-launch-uk-eu/|title=QardioCore receives CE Mark and launches commercially in the European Union and the UK|website=Get Qardio|access-date=2017-11-08|archive-date=2024-08-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240820010159/https://www.qardio.com/news/qardiocore-receives-ce-clearance-launch-uk-eu/|url-status=live}}</ref>

Qardio was selected as "Small Business of the Year" at the 2018 Entrepreneur Awards by Consumer Tech Association.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cta.tech/Resources/Newsroom/Media-Releases/2018/CTA-Honors-Qardio,-BenjiLock-and-Founder-of-Parkop|title=CTA Honors Qardio, BenjiLock and Founder of Parkopedia as 2018 Innovation Entrepreneur Award Winners|website=www.cta.tech|access-date=2020-04-03|archive-date=2024-08-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240820010139/https://www.cta.tech/Resources/Newsroom/Media-Releases/2018/CTA-Honors-Qardio,-BenjiLock-and-Founder-of-Parkop|url-status=live}}</ref>

QardioCore [[Wireless ambulatory ECG|ambulatory ECG/EKG]] monitor received FDA 510(k) clearance in February 2021.{{Citation needed|date=July 2022}}

Former Medtronic Executive, Mike Alvarez, joins Qardio as CEO in 2021 and pivots from consumer to professional care as the primary focus of the organization.{{Citation needed|date=August 2024}}

Qardio launches LIVE ECG with their QardioCore giving healthcare professionals the ability to monitor patients cardiac function at home or remotely in real time.{{Citation needed|date=August 2024}}
