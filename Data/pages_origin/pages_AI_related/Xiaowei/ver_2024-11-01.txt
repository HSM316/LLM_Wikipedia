'''Xiaowei''' is a [[virtual assistant (artificial intelligence)|virtual assistant]] from [[Tencent]], the developer of [[WeChat]]. 
Xiaowei has weather reports, traffic updates, music requests, and news snippets. [[Speech recognition|Voice recognition]] and [[facial recognition system|facial recognition]], and a "Skill platform" [[Software development kit|SDK]] are also available.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.techinasia.com/tencent-voice-assistant-launch|title=Tech in Asia - Connecting Asia's startup ecosystem|website=www.techinasia.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://techwireasia.com/2017/07/tencent-jumps-ai-voice-assistant-game-xiaowei/|title=Tencent jumps into AI voice assistant game with Xiaowei - Tech Wire Asia|date=3 July 2017|publisher=}}</ref>
