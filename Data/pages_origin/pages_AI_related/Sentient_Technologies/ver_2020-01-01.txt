'''Sentient Technologies''' is an American [[artificial intelligence]] (AI) based technology company based in [[San Francisco]].<ref name=ft/> Sentient was founded in 2007 and has received over $143 million in funding since its inception.<ref name=mit/><ref name=bloom/> As of 2016, Sentient is the world's most well-funded AI company. Focused on e-commerce and online content as well as trading,<ref name=tele/> 

==History==
Sentient originally operated in [[stealth mode]] as Genetic Finance Holding Ltd. The company was founded in 2007 by Antoine Blondeau, Babak Hodjat and [[Adam Cheyer]] who created the [[Natural language processing|natural language technology]] that led to [[Siri]], [[Apple Inc.|Apple's]] voice recognition software.<ref name=wsj/><ref name=b2/> Sentient raised a $2 million Series A round of funding, and $38 million in a Series B round led by Horizons Ventures.<ref name=wsj/> Sentient emerged from stealth mode in November 2014 with $103.5 million in Series C funding.<ref name=wsj/><ref name=bloom/><ref name=biz/>

Sentient worked with [[Massachusetts Institute of Technology]]'s Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory to analyze blood pressure to predict the likelihood of [[sepsis]] in ICU patients.<ref name=wsj/><ref name=mit/> The technology was researched in association with [[St. Michael's Hospital (Toronto)|Saint Michael's Hospital]] at the [[University of Toronto]].<ref name=nyt/> The platform has also been used to successfully automate financial services for Sentient's subsidiary, Sentient Investment Management.<ref name=wired/>

In 2015, Sentient launched an AI powered product recommendation and personalization platform leveraging deep learning and online learning. Shoes.com, a Vancouver-based online shoe retailer, was Sentient's first retail customer for this service.<ref name=nyt/><ref name=ibt/><ref name=mm/> Since its launch, Sentient has other retailer customers.<ref name=cn/><ref name=wsj/><ref name=cos/> 

Sentient Ascend was launched in September 2016 as a [[Software as a service|SaaS]] AI based [[conversion rate optimization]] platform, largely based off the same [[evolutionary computation|AI methods]] used in its [[financial technology]] IP.<ref name=econ/><ref name=mt/>

==Technology==
Sentient's platform<ref name=nyt/><ref name=ge/> combines evolutionary computation, which mimics biological evolution, and [[deep learning]], which is based on the structure of nervous systems.<ref name=wsj/> Sentient's algorithms work across as many as two million CPU cores and 5000 GU cards across 4,000 physical sites across the world, making it one of the largest known systems dedicated to AI.<ref name=tele/><ref name=ibt2/>
