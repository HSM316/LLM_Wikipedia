'''Peak''' is an [[artificial intelligence]] company headquartered in [[Manchester]], [[United Kingdom|UK]].<ref>{{cite web |last=Chapman |first=Stephen |url=https://www.prolificnorth.co.uk/news/digital/2018/02/artificial-intelligence-company-takes-top-floor-£8m-manchester-development |title=Artificial Intelligence company takes top floor of £8m Manchester development |work=Prolific North |date=22 February 2018}}</ref> It was founded in December 2014<ref>{{cite web|url=https://technorthhq.com/business/northern-stars-richard-potter-peak-bi/|title=Northern Stars: Interview with Richard Potter (Peak)|date=22 October 2015|publisher=Tech Nation}}</ref> and has additional offices in [[Jaipur]], [[India]], and [[Edinburgh]], [[Scotland]]. It is known for its ''AI System'', a [[Software_as_a_Service|SaaS]] platform that invokes AI workflows upon ingested data and exposes the results via [[Application_programming_interface|APIs]] and built-in UI functionality, as well as abstracting the underlying [[Cloud_computing|cloud infrastructure]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://venturebeat.com/2020/04/23/peak-ai-raises-12-million-to-bolster-enterprise-ai-adoption/|title=Peak.AI raises $12 million to bolster enterprise AI adoption|first=Kyle|last=Wiggers|date=2020-04-23|website=VentureBeat|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>.

== History ==

The company was founded by CEO Richard Potter, David Leitch and Atul Sharma.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/business/business-news/data-analytics-company-formed-pub-13607095|title=Data analytics company formed in pub secures £2.5m funding|first=Lucy|last=Roue|date=12 September 2017|publisher=}}</ref>. In 2015, Peak was one of the winners of the Tech North Northern Stars competition.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://technorthhq.com/business/northern-stars-grand-final/|title=Northern Stars Grand Final 201|date=4 December 2015|publisher=Tech Nation}}</ref> In 2017, it secured £2.5 million in [[Series A round|Series A capital funding]] from [[MMC Ventures]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://medium.com/mmc-writes/mmcs-investment-in-peak-57e11e345819|title=MMC's investment in Peak|last=Coker|first=Jon|date=2017-09-11|website=MMC writes|access-date=2018-04-09}}</ref> for investment in [[machine learning]] and [[artificial intelligence]] technologies<ref>{{cite web |last=Austin |first=Simon |url=https://www.prolificnorth.co.uk/digital/2017/09/manchester-data-analytics-firm-peak-secures-£25m-funding |title=Manchester data analytics firm Peak secures £2.5m funding |work=Prolific North |date=11 September 2017}}</ref> and was named as one of the top five startups in Manchester by [[Wired (magazine)|''Wired'' magazine]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wired.co.uk/article/startup-city-manchester|title=Startup Cities: Manchester|last=Volpicelli|first=Gian|work=Wired|access-date=2018-04-09}}</ref> In 2018, it was chosen to work as part of [[Arsenal F.C.]]'s Innovation Lab<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.prolificnorth.co.uk/news/digital/2018/01/peak-signed-arsenal-innovation-lab|title=Peak signed up for Arsenal Innovation Lab|date=17 January 2018|publisher=}}</ref> and it was one of the 37 fastest growing technology companies in the UK selected to join the [[East London Tech City|Tech City UK]] Upscale programme.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.techcityuk.com/blog/2018/01/37-of-the-uks-fastest-growing-tech-companies-join-upscale-2018/|title=37 of the UK's fastest growing Tech Companies join Upscale 2018|date=2018-01-25|work=Tech City UK|access-date=2018-04-09|language=en-US}}</ref>

CEO Richard Potter was named in the 2018 DataIQ 100 list of influential people in data-driven business<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.businesscloud.co.uk/news/information-commissioner-tops-2018-dataiq-100-list|title=Information commissioner tops 2018 DataIQ 100 list|website=BusinessCloud.co.uk|language=en|access-date=2018-04-09}}</ref> and has been quoted by ''[[The Daily Telegraph]]'' as saying of Manchester's start-up scene: "There are great networking opportunities and a genuine willingness from others for your business to succeed. We’re spoilt for choice with an abundance of business-related programmes, events and competitions in which to get involved."<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/connect/small-business/tech-hubs-outside-of-london-share-secret-to-success/|title=4 tech hubs outside of London share the secret to success|last=Rahim|first=Hajra|date=2017|work=The Telegraph|access-date=2018-04-09|language=en-GB|issn=0307-1235}}</ref> He has also provided comment about the [[General Data Protection Regulation|General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)]] to ''[[Forbes]]''<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/rganatra/2018/02/28/getting-ready-for-gdpr/#72235eda6f43|title=Getting Ready For GDPR|last=Ganatra|first=Rupa|work=Forbes|access-date=2018-04-09|language=en}}</ref> and ''[[Daily Express|The Express]]''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.express.co.uk/finance/city/942370/GDPR-2018-will-GDPR-changes-in-May-spell-disaster-for-Britains-businesses|title=GDPR 2018: Will GDPR changes in May spell disaster for Britain's businesses?|last=Gough|first=Owen|date=2018-04-07|work=Express.co.uk|access-date=2018-04-09|language=en}}</ref>

In April 2020, the company raised $12 million in extended series A funding, required for sustaining its growth, commercial expansion, and R&D efforts<ref>{{cite web|url=https://venturebeat.com/2020/04/23/peak-ai-raises-12-million-to-bolster-enterprise-ai-adoption/|title=Peak.AI raises $12 million to bolster enterprise AI adoption|first=Kyle|last=Wiggers|date=2020-04-23|website=VentureBeat|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>.
