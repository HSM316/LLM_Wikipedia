The '''Czech Institute of Informatics, Robotics and Cybernetics''' ({{lang-cs|Český institut informatiky, robotiky a kybernetiky}}, CIIRC) was established as a part of the [[Czech Technical University in Prague]] on July 1, 2013. In the premises of the CTU in [[Dejvice]] there has been two new buildings for CIIRC built since November 2013. CIIRC will reside in a new building, which used to serve as a Technical University canteen and one of the biggest [[Billa (supermarket)|Billa]] [[hypermarket]]s.

CIIRC consists of eight research departments,  CYPHY: Cyber-physical systems, INTSYS: Intelligent systems, IIG: Industrial informatics, RMP: Robotics and machine perception, IPA: Industrial production and automation, COGSYS: Cognitive systems and neurosciences, BEAT: Biomedical engineering and Assistive technologies, PLAT: Research Management of Platforms.{{Importance inline|date=March 2023}} The head of CIIRC is [[Ondřej Velek]].
