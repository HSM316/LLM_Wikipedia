The '''European Laboratory for Learning and Intelligent Systems''' ('''ELLIS''') is a pan-European [[nonprofit organization]] for the promotion of [[artificial intelligence]] with a focus on [[machine learning]]. The organization's goal is to establish top AI research institutes, strengthen basic research and create a European [[PhD programme]] for AI.

== History ==
The organization was inspired by the Learning in Machines and Brains program of the [[Canadian Institute for Advanced Research]]. ELLIS was first proposed in an open letter to European governments in April 2018, which stated that Europe was not keeping up with the US and China. It urged that European governments act to provide opportunities and funding for world-class AI research in Europe.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/science/2018/apr/23/scientists-plan-huge-european-ai-hub-to-compete-with-us|title=Scientists plan huge European AI hub to compete with US|last=Sample|first=Ian|date=2018-04-23|work=The Guardian|access-date=2019-12-12|language=en-GB|issn=0261-3077}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/samshead/2018/04/24/european-scientists-call-for-ai-institute-as-us-and-china-pull-away/|title=European Scientists Call For AI Institute As US and China Pull Away|last=Shead|first=Sam|website=Forbes|language=en|access-date=2019-12-11}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sciencemag.org/news/2018/04/15-billion-artificial-intelligence-research-europe-pins-hopes-ethics|title=With €1.5 billion for artificial intelligence research, Europe pins hopes on ethics|last=|first=|last2=|date=2018-04-25|website=Science {{!}} AAAS|language=en|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-12|last3=|last4=|first4=}}</ref>

It was founded on 6 December 2018 at the [[Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems]] (NeurIPS).<ref>{{Cite journal|url=https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-018-07730-1|title=Europe’s AI researchers launch professional body over fears of falling behind|date=2018-12-10|journal=Nature|language=en|doi=10.1038/d41586-018-07730-1|last=Else|first=Holly}}</ref>

== ELLIS research groups ==
ELLIS has set up so-called ELLIS units, research groups, at 17 locations. In an open competition, the research groups were selected by an international commission of scientists according to their scientific excellence in the field. 

These institutions will invest 200 million euros in artificial intelligence research over the next 5 years.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://is.tuebingen.mpg.de/news/tubingen-becomes-an-ellis-unit|title=Tübingen becomes an ELLIS unit {{!}} Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems|last=Williams|first=Jon|date=|website=is.tuebingen.mpg.de|language=en|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-11}}</ref>
