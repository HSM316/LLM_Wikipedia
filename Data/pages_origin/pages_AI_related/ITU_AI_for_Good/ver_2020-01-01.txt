'''AI for Good''' is a [[United Nations]] platform, centered around annual Global Summits, that fosters the dialogue on the beneficial use of [[Artificial Intelligence]], by developing concrete projects. The impetus for organizing global summits that are action oriented, came from existing discourse in [[artificial intelligence]] (AI) research being dominated by research streams such as the [[Netflix Prize]] (improve the movie recommendation algorithm).
<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.itu.int/en/ITU-T/AI/2018/|title=AI for Good 2018|last=|first=|date=17 May 2018|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190923082803/https://www.itu.int/en/ITU-T/AI/2018/Pages/default.aspx|archive-date=2019-09-23|accessdate=1 June 2018}}</ref> The AI for Good series aims to bring forward Artificial Intelligence research topics that contribute towards more global problems,<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Butler|first=Declan|date=2017-06-06|title=AI summit aims to help world’s poorest|url=https://www.nature.com/news/ai-summit-aims-to-help-world-s-poorest-1.22112|journal=Nature|language=en|volume=546|issue=7657|pages=196–197|doi=10.1038/546196a|issn=0028-0836|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190430190609/https://www.nature.com/news/ai-summit-aims-to-help-world-s-poorest-1.22112|archive-date=2019-04-30|via=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.technologyreview.com/s/608049/how-can-we-optimize-ai-for-the-greatest-good-instead-of-profit/amp/|title=How Can We Optimize AI for the Greatest Good, Instead of Profit?|website=www.technologyreview.com|language=en|access-date=2018-06-29}}</ref> in particular through the [[Sustainable Development Goals]], while at the same time avoiding typical UN style conferences where results are generally more abstract. The fourth AI for Good Global Summit will be held from 4–8 May 2020 in Geneva, Switzerland.

==Subcategories==
Several related terms have been used to further categorize AI for Good and AI for Good applications. These include '''AI for Earth''', referring specifically to environmental applications;<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nationalgeographic.org/grants/grant-opportunities/ai-earth-innovation/|title=RFP: AI for Earth Innovation|last=Society|first=National Geographic|website=www.nationalgeographic.org|language=en|access-date=2019-03-10}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/ai-for-earth|title=Microsoft AI for Earth|website=www.microsoft.com|language=en-us|access-date=2019-03-10}}</ref> '''Humanitarian AI''', referring to applications for [[Humanitarianism|humanitarian]] ends;<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/ai-for-humanitarian-action|title=AI for Humanitarian Action|website=www.microsoft.com|language=en-us|access-date=2019-03-10}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.meetup.com/Humanitarian-AI/|title=Humanitarian AI (Cambridge, MA)|website=Meetup|language=en|access-date=2019-03-10}}</ref> and '''AI for Healthcare'''.<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Wang|first=Yongjun|last2=Shen|first2=Haipeng|last3=Dong|first3=Qiang|last4=Wang|first4=Yilong|last5=Ma|first5=Sufeng|last6=Li|first6=Hao|last7=Dong|first7=Yi|last8=Zhi|first8=Hui|last9=Jiang|first9=Yong|date=2017-12-01|title=Artificial intelligence in healthcare: past, present and future|url=https://svn.bmj.com/content/2/4/230|journal=Stroke and Vascular Neurology|language=en|volume=2|issue=4|pages=230–243|doi=10.1136/svn-2017-000101|issn=2059-8688|pmc=5829945|pmid=29507784}}</ref>

== AI Repository ==
In order to further promote Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning research that advance the achieving of the [[Sustainable Development Goals]], an AI Repository was created, which includes AI for Good projects and examples.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.itu.int/en/ITU-T/AI/Pages/ai-repository.aspx|title=AI Repository|website=www.itu.int|language=en-US|access-date=2018-06-29}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://news.itu.int/tell-world-about-your-ai-project-itus-ai-repository/|title=Tell the world about your AI for Good project on ITU’s AI Repository|last=ITU|date=2018-04-25|work=ITU News|access-date=2018-06-29|language=en-US}}</ref>
