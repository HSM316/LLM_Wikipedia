'''Notion''' is a [[note-taking]] software platform designed to help members of companies or organizations manage their knowledge for greater efficiency and productivity.

==History==
Notion Labs Inc is a [[Startup company|startup]] based in [[San Francisco]], founded in 2013 by Ivan Zhao, Chris Prucha, Jessica Lam, Simon Last and Toby Schachman.<ref>{{Cite web |last= |first= |title=Notion Labs, Inc. Company Profile: Financials, Valuation, and Growth |url=https://system.privco.com/company/notion-labs |access-date=2022-07-21 |website=PrivCo |language=English}}</ref> At that time, they declined to meet any [[Venture capital|venture capitalists]] or discuss acquiring a higher [[Valuation (finance)|valuation]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.businessinsider.com/notion-has-vc-knocking-on-its-doors-to-invest-2019-2|title=This enterprise software startup has VCs literally knocking on its doors to invest, but for now, it's not interested|last=Bastone|first=Nick|website=[[Business Insider]]|access-date=2020-02-05}}</ref>

In March of 2018, Notion 2.0 was released. It was positively received by [[Product Hunt]] and rated #1 Product of the Month.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.producthunt.com/posts/notion-2-0|title=Notion 2.0 - The all-in-one workspace - notes, tasks, wikis, & databases|website=Product Hunt|access-date=2020-02-05}}</ref> At that point, the company had fewer than 10 employees.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://usefyi.com/notion-history/|title=How Ivan Zhao's Notion Is Going After Atlassian and Why It Just Might Win|last=Shah|first=Hiten|date=2019-05-28|website=FYI|access-date=2020-02-05}}</ref>

In June of 2018, an official [[Android (operating system)|Android]] app was released.<ref>{{cite web |url = https://www.theverge.com/2018/6/7/17434754/notion-android-app-notes-productivity-review |title = Notion brings its powerful note-taking app to Android |date = 7 June 2018 |work = [[The Verge]]}}</ref>

In September of 2019, the company announced it had reached 1 million users.<ref name=":3" /> In April 2020, it became valued at two billion dollars.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=2020-08-11|title=레고블록처럼 사용하는 생산성 앱 '노션' 한국어 버전 정식 출시|trans-title=Full release of productivity app 'Notion' in Korean|url=https://www.techm.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=74234|url-status=live|access-date=2021-05-07|website=테크M|language=ko}}</ref>

In January 2020, Notion received $50 million in investments from [[Index Ventures]] and other investors.<ref name=":3">{{Cite news|last=Griffith|first=Erin|date=2020-04-01|title=Notion, Maker of Collaboration Software, Raises $50 Million|language=en-US|work=The New York Times|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2020/04/01/technology/notion-startup-fund-raising.html|access-date=2021-09-08|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>

On September 7, 2021, Notion acquired Automate.io, a [[Hyderabad]]-based startup.<ref>{{cite web |title=Notion acquires India's Automate.io in push to accelerate product expansion |url=https://techcrunch.com/2021/09/08/notion-acquires-indias-automate-io-in-push-to-accelerate-product-expansion/ |website=Techcrunch |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref> In October of that year, a new round of funding led by [[Coatue Management]] and [[Sequoia Capital]] helped Notion raise $275 million. The investment valued Notion at $10 billion, and the company had 20 million users.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Konrad|first=Alex|title=Notion Reaches $10 Billion Valuation, Boosted By Remote Work — And TikTok|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/alexkonrad/2021/10/08/notion-raises-at-10-billion-valuation-boosted-by-remote-work-tiktok/|access-date=2022-01-28|website=Forbes|language=en}}</ref>

In 2022, Notion launched the Notion Certified Program, an official accreditation allowing users to expand their expertise.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Lawrence |first=Lizzy |date=2022-03-02 |title=Notion wants to mobilize your Notion-obsessed employees |url=https://www.protocol.com/workplace/notion-conference-enterprise-productivity-influencer |access-date=2022-03-08 |website=Protocol |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Finnegan |first=Matthew |date=2022-03-04 |title=Coda and Notion turn up the heat on Microsoft Office |url=https://www.computerworld.com/article/3652691/coda-and-notion-turn-up-the-heat-on-microsoft-office.html |access-date=2022-03-08 |website=Computerworld |language=en}}</ref> It also joined the Security First Initiative, a coalition of tech companies pledging to proactively share security information with their customers.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Release |first=Press |date=2022-03-08 |title=Utah-based Whistic leads out in new security-first initiative consortium |url=https://www.utahbusiness.com/utah-based-whistic-leads-out-in-new-security-first-initiative-consortium/ |access-date=2022-03-09 |website=Utah Business |language=en-US}}</ref>

In June 2022, Notion acquired calendar software Cron to add it to its suite of productivity apps.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Notion acquires calendar app Cron |url=https://www.protocol.com/bulletins/notion-acquires-calendar-app-cron |date=2022-06-09 |website=[[Protocol (website)|Protocol]] |last=Lawrence |first=Lizzy |language=en}}</ref>

In November 2022, Notion announced its official Japanese release.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Notion「日本語正式版」になる |url=https://www.watch.impress.co.jp/docs/news/1454615.html |date=2022-11-10 |website=www.watch.impress.co.jp |accessdate=2022-11-10 }}</ref>

== Software ==
'''Notion''' is a [[Collaborative software|collaboration]] platform with modified [[Markdown]] support that integrates [[kanban]] boards, tasks, [[Wiki software|wikis]] and databases. It is an all-in-one workspace for [[Notetaking software|notetaking]], [[Knowledge management software|knowledge]] and [[Spreadsheet|data]] management, and [[Project management software|project and task management]].<ref>{{cite web|date=8 June 2018|title=Notion goes where Evernote dares not: what you need to know|url=https://androidcommunity.com/notion-goes-where-evernote-dares-not-what-you-need-to-know-20180608/|work=Android Community}}</ref> It is a file management tool offering a unified workspace, allowing users to comment on ongoing projects, participate in discussions, and receive feedback.<ref>{{Cite web |last=updated |first=Collin Probst last |date=2022-07-13 |title=5 things you need to know about Notion |url=https://www.techradar.com/features/5-things-you-need-to-know-about-notion |access-date=2022-12-20 |website=TechRadar |language=en}}</ref> It can be accessed by [[Cross-platform software|cross-platform]] apps and by most [[web browser]]s.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Raphael|first=J. R.|date=2021-10-27|title=3 fantastic ways Notion can make you more efficient|url=https://www.computerworld.com/article/3636153/3-fantastic-ways-notion-can-make-you-more-efficient.html|access-date=2022-02-08|website=Computerworld|language=en}}</ref>

It includes a tool for "clipping" content from webpages.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Newton|first=Casey|date=2019-03-06|title=Notion’s new web clipper makes it a powerful competitor to Evernote|url=https://www.theverge.com/2019/3/6/18251787/notion-evernote-web-clipper-import-tool|access-date=2022-02-02|website=The Verge|language=en}}</ref> It helps users schedule tasks, manage files, save documents, set reminders, keep agendas, and organize their work. [[LaTeX]] support allows writing and pasting equations in block or inline form.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Lee |first=Joel |date=2019-02-05 |title=The 7 Best Note-Taking Apps for Programmers and Coders |url=https://www.makeuseof.com/tag/best-note-taking-tools-programmers/ |access-date=2022-10-27 |website=MUO |language=en-US}}</ref>  Users can also [[wikt:embed|embed]] online content in their Notion pages using Embed.ly, with real-time notes added to embedded video playing in [[Picture-in-picture|PiP]] mode.{{Citation needed|date=December 2021}}

=== Pricing ===
Notion has a four-tiered [[Subscription business model|subscription]] model: Free, Personal, Team and Enterprise. Users can also earn credit via [[Referral marketing|referrals]]. Users are not charged if they have a remaining balance in their accounts.{{citation needed|date=March 2020}} An academic email address allows a free Personal plan. As of May 2020, the company upgraded the Personal plan to allow unlimited blocks, a change from the previous cap in the plan.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Newton|first=Casey|date=2020-05-19|title=Notion just removed the biggest limit on its free plan|url=https://www.theverge.com/2020/5/19/21261811/note-taking-app-notion-free-plan-personal-use-unlimited-blocks|access-date=2021-11-07|website=The Verge|language=en}}</ref>
