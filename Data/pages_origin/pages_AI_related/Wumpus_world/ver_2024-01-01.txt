'''Wumpus world''' is a simple world use in [[artificial intelligence]] for which to represent knowledge and to reason. Wumpus world was introduced by Genesereth, and is discussed in Russell-Norvig Artificial intelligence book ({{ISBN|0136042597}}) inspired by 1972 video game [[Hunt the Wumpus]] .
