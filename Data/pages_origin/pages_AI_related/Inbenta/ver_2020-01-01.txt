'''Inbenta''' is an [[AI]] company that originated in [[Barcelona]], [[Spain]] in 2005. Inbenta is currently headquartered in [[Foster City, California]] with offices in: [[São Paulo|São Paulo, Brazil]]; [[Toulouse|Toulouse, France]]; and [[Tokyo|Tokyo, Japan]]. Inbenta raised $12 Million<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.inbenta.com/en/blog/inbenta-announces-12-million-series-b-investment/|title=Inbenta announces $12 million Series B investment - Inbenta|date=2016-09-14|work=Inbenta|access-date=2017-04-21|language=en-US}}</ref> in their Series B funding round to extend the reach of their artificial intelligence for business solutions.

Inbenta provides [[natural language processing]] and [[semantic search]] through [[Artificial Intelligence]].

On 23 June 2018, [[Ticketmaster]] UK identified malicious software on a customer support product hosted by Inbenta Technologies, compromising personal data and payment details for thousands of Ticketmaster customers.<ref>{{Cite|url=https://security.ticketmaster.co.uk/?int_cmp_name=Ticketmaster-Marketing&int_cmp_id=UK-Home-601&int_cmp_creative=Home-featured-1&tm_link=tm_ccp_Home_featured_Ticketmaster-Marketing|title=Ticketmaster - Data security incident by third-party supplier}}</ref>
