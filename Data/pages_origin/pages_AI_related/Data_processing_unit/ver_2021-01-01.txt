A '''data processing unit (DPU)''' is a programmable [[electronic component]] that processes streams of data.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://packetpushers.net/peek-inside-fungible-data-center-startup-juniper-cofounder-pradeep-sindhu/|title=A Peek Inside Fungible, A Data Center Startup From Juniper Cofounder Pradeep Sindhu - Packet Pushers|website=packetpushers.net|access-date=2019-08-06}}</ref> The data is transmitted to and from the component as multiplexed packets of information. DPUs have the generality and the programmability of [[central processing unit]]s but are specialized<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://siliconangle.com/2018/11/30/junipers-founder-turns-to-custom-silicon-for-handling-the-data-tsunami-mayfield50/|title=To handle the data tsunami, Juniper's founder turns to custom silicon|date=2018-11-30|website=SiliconANGLE|language=en-US|access-date=2019-08-06}}</ref> to operate efficiently on [[Network packet|networking packets]], storage requests or analytics requests.<ref>{{Cite patent|number=20190012350|title=Data Processing Unit for Stream Processing|gdate=2019-01-10|invent1=Sindhu|invent2=Frailong|invent3=Noureddine|invent4=Marti|inventor1-first=Pradeep|inventor2-first=Jean-marc|inventor3-first=Wael|inventor4-first=Felix A.|url=http://www.freepatentsonline.com/y2019/0012350.html}}</ref><ref>Hennessy, John. “[https://www.kisacoresearch.com/sites/default/files/presentations/09.00_-_alphabet_-_john_hennessy.pdf. The End of Moore's Law, CPUs (as We Know Them), and the Rise of Domain Specific Architectures].” AI Hardware Summit. Stanford University. September 29, 2019. </ref>

A DPU differentiates itself from a CPU by a larger degree of parallelism (required to process many requests) and from a GPU by a [[MIMD]] architecture rather an [[SIMD]] architecture (required as each request needs to make different decisions and follow a different path through the chip).<ref>{{Cite web|last=Deierling|first=Kevin|date=2020-05-21|title=What’s a DPU?|url=https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/2020/05/20/whats-a-dpu-data-processing-unit/|access-date=2020-10-03|website=The Official NVIDIA Blog|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://social.techcrunch.com/2019/08/15/the-renaissance-of-silicon-will-create-industry-giants/|title=The renaissance of silicon will create industry giants|website=TechCrunch|language=en-US|access-date=2019-08-16}}</ref>
