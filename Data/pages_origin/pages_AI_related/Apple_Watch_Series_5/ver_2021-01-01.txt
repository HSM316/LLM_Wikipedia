The '''Apple Watch Series 5''' is the sixth-generation model of the [[Apple Watch]]. It was released on September 20, 2019 during an [[List of Apple Inc. media events|Apple Special Event]] alongside the [[iPhone 11]]. Its principal improvement over its predecessor is the inclusion of an always-on display.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://9to5mac.com/guides/apple-watch-series-5/|title=Apple Watch Series 5: Features, Release Date, Price, etc|website=9to5Mac|language=en-US|access-date=September 21, 2019}}</ref> It has received mostly positive reviews from critics, with reviewers praising the always-on display, but criticizing that there are few improvements from the previous watch. It was discontinued on September 15, 2020, following the release of [[Apple Watch Series 6]].

==Overview==
The Apple Watch Series 5 includes an always-on display with energy saving [[Low-temperature polycrystalline silicon|LTPO]] [[OLED]] screen and low-power screen driver capable of refresh rates as low as once per second. The always-on display was not included in the [[Apple Watch Series 4]].<ref name=":0" /> Additional new features include International Emergency Calling which enables emergency calls in over 150 countries, a more power-efficient S5 processor, improved ambient light sensor, doubling of storage capacity to 32 GB, and the addition of a [[magnetometer]], which enables a compass function. It also brought back the "Edition" tier, with a ceramic model absent from the previous generation, and a new titanium model which comes in two colors: Natural and Space Black.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.apple.com/apple-watch-edition/|title=Apple Watch Edition|website=[[Apple Inc.]]|access-date=September 12, 2019}}</ref>

==Reception==
Critics generally gave it a positive review. [[CNET]] gave it a score of 4/5, concluding, "The Apple Watch continues to be one of the best smartwatches, but it remains limited by being an iPhone accessory for now."<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnet.com/reviews/apple-watch-series-5-2019-review/|title=Apple Watch Series 5, revisited: still the best smartwatch|last=Stein|first=Scott|website=CNET|language=en|access-date=November 26, 2019}}</ref> [[Digital Trends]] gave it a score of 4.5/5.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.digitaltrends.com/smartwatch-reviews/apple-watch-series-5-review/ |title=Apple Watch Series 5 review: A smartwatch close to perfection |website=www.digitaltrends.com |date=November 8, 2019|access-date=November 26, 2019}}</ref> [[The Verge]] gave it a score of 9/10.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theverge.com/2019/9/18/20869098/apple-watch-series-5-always-on-screen-features-price-best-smartwatch|title=Apple Watch Series 5 review: the best smartwatch|last=Bohn|first=Dieter|date=September 18, 2019|website=The Verge|language=en|access-date=November 26, 2019}}</ref>
