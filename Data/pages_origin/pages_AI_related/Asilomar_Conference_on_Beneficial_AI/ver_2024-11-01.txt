The '''Asilomar Conference on Beneficial AI''' was a [[academic conference|conference]] organized by the [[Future of Life Institute]],<ref name="aiethics">“Future of Life Institute 2017 Asilomar Conference.” ai-ethics.com. https://ai-ethics.com/2017/08/11/future-of-life-institute-2017-asilomar-conference/ accessed 1 December 2017</ref> held January 5–8, 2017, at the [[Asilomar Conference Grounds]] in [[California]]. More than 100 thought leaders and researchers in economics, law, ethics, and philosophy met at the conference, to address and formulate principles of beneficial AI.<ref name="kurz">"Beneficial AI conference develops ‘Asilomar AI principles’ to guide future AI research." Kurzweil Network. http://www.kurzweilai.net/beneficial-ai-conference-develops-asilomar-ai-principles-to-guide-future-ai-research accessed 1 December 2017</ref> Its outcome was the creation of a set of guidelines for AI research – the 23 Asilomar AI Principles.<ref name="principles23">{{Cite web |title=Asilomar AI Principles |url=https://futureoflife.org/open-letter/ai-principles/ |access-date=2024-09-24 |website=Future of Life Institute |language=en-US}}</ref>

The principles, published as an open letter, received signatures from scientist [[Stephen Hawking]], CEO of [[Google DeepMind|Google Deepmind]] [[Demis Hassabis]], AI researchers [[Ilya Sutskever]], [[Yann LeCun]], [[Yoshua Bengio]], and [[Stuart J. Russell|Stuart Russell]], philosophers [[Sam Harris]] and [[William MacAskill|Will MacAskill]], and actor [[Joseph Gordon-Levitt]], amongst others.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-11-18 |title=How OpenAI CEO Sam Altman Was Fired by Rival Board Members |url=https://sfstandard.com/2023/11/17/openai-sam-altman-firing-board-members/ |access-date=2024-09-24 |website=The San Francisco Standard |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Cuthbertson |first=Anthony |date=2017-01-31 |title=Elon Musk and Stephen Hawking Warn of AI Arms Race |url=https://www.newsweek.com/ai-asilomar-principles-artificial-intelligence-elon-musk-550525 |access-date=2024-09-24 |website=Newsweek |language=en}}</ref><ref name="principles23" />
