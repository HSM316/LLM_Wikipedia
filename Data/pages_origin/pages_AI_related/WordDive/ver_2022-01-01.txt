'''WordDive''' is an [[Artificial intelligence|AI]]-based online language [[Educational software|learning software]] and mobile application. Ten languages are currently offered: [[English language|English]], [[Estonian language|Estonian]], [[Finnish language|Finnish]], [[French language|French]], [[German language|German]], [[Italian language|Italian]], [[Japanese language|Japanese]], [[Russian language|Russian]], [[Spanish language|Spanish]] and [[Swedish language|Swedish]].

== Concept ==
The method is based on the use of multiple senses, individual optimization<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.goodnewsfinland.com/feature/word-dive-created-a-clever-way-of-learning-languages/|title=WordDive created a clever way of learning languages|newspaper=Good News from Finland|language=en-US|access-date=2017-01-24}}</ref> and game-like elements.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://tampereallbrightmagazine.fi/articles/worddive-makes-language-studies-addictive|title=WordDive makes language studies addictive - Tampere – All Bright! Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=2017-01-24}}</ref> With the individual optimization feature, the course is modified based on the user's learning ability.

== Recognition and awards ==
WordDive has been awarded as the Best e-Learning Solution in Finland 2011<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.eoppimiskeskus.fi/en/eeemeli/previous-winners|title=eOppimiskeskus - Previous Winners|language=en-gb|access-date=2017-01-24}}</ref> as well as the Best Mobile Service in Finland 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://2014.bestmobileservice.fi/winners|title=The Best Mobile Service in Finland 2014|website=2014.bestmobileservice.fi|language=en|access-date=2017-01-24}}</ref> In spring 2015, WordDive was selected as a [[Red Herring (magazine)|Red Herring]] Europe Top 100 winner.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.redherring.com/events/red-herring-europe/2015-winners/|title=2015 Top 100 Europe: Winners — Red Herring|newspaper=Red Herring|language=en-US|access-date=2017-01-24}}</ref> In November 2018 WordDive was certified for pedagogical quality by [[Education Alliance Finland]], a teacher-led certification agency specialized in quality verification of educational solutions.<ref>{{cite web |title=Education Alliance Finland |url=https://educationalliancefinland.com/products/worddive |website=Catalog of certified products |access-date=12 May 2020}}</ref>

== Development ==
The company is based in [[Tampere]], [[Finland]]. The service was launched in 2010. In 2020, the platform had 850,000 users in 150 countries.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Tammilehto |first1=Pirkko |title=Tamperelainen yritys valjasti tekoälyn kielten opetukseen – Mobiilisovelluksen liikevaihto kovassa kasvussa |url=https://www.kauppalehti.fi/uutiset/tamperelainen-yritys-valjasti-tekoalyn-kielten-opetukseen-mobiilisovelluksen-liikevaihto-kovassa-kasvussa/52a223ac-c29b-445a-834f-a0b845c43af8 |website=[[Kauppalehti]] |access-date=18 February 2020 |language=fi |date=18 February 2020}}</ref>

In Finland and [[Germany]] WordDive is best known for its English prep course.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://yle.fi/uutiset/3-8742463|title=Älypuhelin preppaa jo kymmeniä tuhansia abeja – suomalaisfirman tähtäimessä seuraavaksi Kiina ja Saksa|website=Yle Uutiset|language=fi|access-date=2017-01-24}}</ref>

In Germany, the company is working together with brand ambassador [[Samu Haber]], the front man of the band [[Sunrise Avenue]] and a judge in [[The Voice of Germany]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.radionova.fi/uutiset/ajankohtaista/saksalaisabit-paasevat-opiskelemaan-englantia-samu-haberin-opastuksella-102857|title=Saksalaisabit pääsevät opiskelemaan englantia Samu Haberin opastuksella|date=2015-12-01|newspaper=Radio Nova|access-date=2017-01-25}}</ref>

In January 2017 WordDive secured 1.2 million euros via the [[Invesdor]] crowdfunding platform<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.worddive.com/de|title=WordDive|date=2018-08-28|access-date=2017-10-06|language=en-US}}</ref> and in April 2018 1.5 million euros in Privanet.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://around.fi/around2/investor/rounds/closed|title=Around|website=around.fi|access-date=2018-08-24}}</ref>
