'''iCarbonX''' is a company founded by Chinese genomicist [[Jun Wang (scientist)|Jun Wang]], former CEO of [[Beijing Genomics Institute|Beijing Genomic Institute (BGI)]], in 2015. iCarbonX combines [[genomics]] with other health factors such as [[metabolite]]s, [[bacteria]] and lifestyle choices to create a digitalized form of life.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.chinamoneynetwork.com/2017/01/05/alpha-unicorn-icarbonx-eyes-800m-wechat-users-to-build-global-precision-health-giant|title=Alpha Unicorn iCarbonX Eyes 800M WeChat Users To Build Global Precision Health Giant – China Money Network|website=www.chinamoneynetwork.com|language=en-US|access-date=2017-10-03}}</ref>

==History==
iCarbonX was founded on October 27, 2015. On September 10, 2016, iCarbonX acquired Imagu Vision Technologies, an Israeli [[Artificial intelligence|AI]] and [[Digital image processing|image processing]] company,<ref>{{Cite web |date=2016-09-13 |title=Tencent-backed Chinese AI platform iCarbonX acquires Israel's Imagu Vision Technologies |url=https://www.dealstreetasia.com/stories/chinese-ai-platform-icarbonx-acquires-israeli-imagu-vision-technologies-53213/ |access-date=2022-12-07 |website=DealStreetAsia |language=en}}</ref> in order to establish an iCarbonX-Israel R&D center.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.asianscientist.com/2016/09/tech/icarbonx-acquires-imagu/|title=China's iCarbonX Acquires Israel-Based Imagu For A.I. Technology|date=2016-09-22|work=Asian Scientist Magazine {{!}} Science, Technology and Medicine News Updates From Asia|access-date=2017-10-03|language=en-US}}</ref> 

On January 5, 2017, iCarbonX announced its Digital Life Alliance with seven other companies including [[SomaLogic]], HealthTell, [[PatientsLikeMe]],<ref name=":0" /> [[AOBiome]], GALT, Imagu and Robustnique.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.diagnosticsworldnews.com/2017/1/9/patientslikeme-joins-wangs-icarbonx-digital-life-alliance-cornerstone.aspx|title=Diagnostics World News|website=www.diagnosticsworldnews.com|access-date=2017-04-11|archive-date=2017-08-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170823160625/http://www.diagnosticsworldnews.com/2017/1/9/patientslikeme-joins-wangs-icarbonx-digital-life-alliance-cornerstone.aspx|url-status=dead}}</ref>

iCarbonX has raised over $600 million in investment. [[Tencent|Tencent Holdings Limited]] – owner of social-media app [[WeChat]] – and Zhongyuan Union Cell & Gene Engineering Corp. invested $200 million<ref>{{cite journal |last=Cyranoski |first=David |date=12 January 2017 |title=Chinese AI company plans to mine health data faster than rivals |journal=Nature |volume=541 |issue=7636 |pages=141–142 |bibcode=2017Natur.541..141C |doi=10.1038/541141a |pmid=28079091 |doi-access=free}}</ref> in iCarbonX, which made iCarbonX one of only three Chinese healthcare startups with a valuation of more than $1 billion (considered a [[Unicorn (finance)|Unicorn]]). The company has about  100 employees.<ref>{{Cite news |date=2016-06-29 |title=How to Predict Your Body's Health With a Digital DNA Avatar |work=Bloomberg.com |url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-06-29/disease-predicting-avatars-fuel-billion-dollar-chinese-startup |access-date=2017-10-03}}</ref>

On January 5, 2017, iCarbonX released Meum, a digital health management platform.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20170105005285/en/iCarbonX-Expands-Digital-Life-Alliance-Accelerate-Development|title=iCarbonX Expands Digital Life Alliance to Accelerate Development of Global Health Ecosystem|website=www.businesswire.com|date=5 January 2017|language=en|access-date=2017-10-03}}
</ref> On the same day, iCarbonX announced a partnership with [[PatientsLikeMe]], an online network for patients with chronic conditions. iCarbonX funded PatientsLikeMe with $100M with plans for the two companies to work together on genetic sequencing projects.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2017-01-05 |title=PatientsLikeMe secures $100M, partners with health data company iCarbonX |url=https://www.mobihealthnews.com/content/patientslikeme-secures-100m-partners-health-data-company-icarbonx |access-date=2023-12-12 |website=MobiHealthNews |language=en}}</ref>

The company name, “iCarbonX,” symbolizes the use of the internet and artificial intelligence to improve life, of which a central element is carbon.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |date=2015-10-30 |title=Is iCarbonX the next Google in Biotech? - Business - Chinadaily.com.cn |url=https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/business/2015-10/30/content_22326548.htm |access-date=2022-12-07 |website=chinadaily.com.cn}}</ref> The “i” and “X” indicate the company's plans to combine the Internet and artificial intelligence to create something new.<ref name=":0" />
