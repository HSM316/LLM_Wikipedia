'''iCarbonX''' is a company founded by Chinese genomicist [[Jun Wang (scientist)|Jun Wang]], former CEO of [[Beijing Genomics Institute|Beijing Genomic Institute (BGI),]] in 2015. iCarbonX combines genomics with other health factors such as [[Metabolite|metabolites]], [[bacteria]] and lifestyle choices to create a digitalized form of life.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.chinamoneynetwork.com/2017/01/05/alpha-unicorn-icarbonx-eyes-800m-wechat-users-to-build-global-precision-health-giant|title=Alpha Unicorn iCarbonX Eyes 800M WeChat Users To Build Global Precision Health Giant – China Money Network|website=www.chinamoneynetwork.com|language=en-US|access-date=2017-10-03}} </ref>

iCarbonX has raised over $600 million in investment. [[Tencent|Tencent Holdings Limited]] – owner of social-media app [[WeChat]] – and Zhongyuan Union Cell & Gene Engineering Corp. invested $200 million<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.nature.com/news/chinese-ai-company-plans-to-mine-health-data-faster-than-rivals-1.21258|title=Chinese AI company plans to mine health data faster than rivals|journal=Nature|first=David|last=Cyranoski|date=12 January 2017|publisher=|volume=541|issue=7636|pages=141–142|doi=10.1038/541141a|pmid=28079091}}</ref> in iCarbonX, which made iCarbonX one of only three Chinese healthcare startups with a valuation of more than $1 billion ([[Unicorn (finance)|Unicorn]]). The company has about  100 employees.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-06-29/disease-predicting-avatars-fuel-billion-dollar-chinese-startup|title=How to Predict Your Body's Health With a Digital DNA Avatar|date=2016-06-29|work=Bloomberg.com|access-date=2017-10-03}} </ref>
==History==
iCarbonX was founded on October 27, 2015. On September 10, 2016 iCarbonX acquired Imagu Vision Technologies, an Israeli AI and image processing company,<ref>[http://www.dealstreetasia.com/stories/chinese-ai-platform-icarbonx-acquires-israeli-imagu-vision-technologies-53213/ http://www.dealstreetasia.com/stories/chinese-ai-platform-icarbonx-acquires-israeli-imagu-vision-technologies-53213/.]</ref> in order to establish an iCarbonX-Israel R&D center.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.asianscientist.com/2016/09/tech/icarbonx-acquires-imagu/|title=China’s iCarbonX Acquires Israel-Based Imagu For A.I. Technology|date=2016-09-22|work=Asian Scientist Magazine {{!}}  Science, Technology and Medicine News Updates From Asia|access-date=2017-10-03|language=en-US}} </ref> On January 5, 2017 iCarbonX announced its Digital Life Alliance with seven other companies including SomaLogic, HealthTell, [[PatientsLikeMe]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/business/2015-10/30/content_22326548.htm|title=Is iCarbonX the next Google in Biotech?  - Business - Chinadaily.com.cn|last=何旖旎|website=www.chinadaily.com.cn|access-date=2017-10-03}}</ref> [[AOBiome]], GALT, Imagu and Robustnique.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.diagnosticsworldnews.com/2017/1/9/patientslikeme-joins-wangs-icarbonx-digital-life-alliance-cornerstone.aspx|title=Diagnostics World News|website=www.diagnosticsworldnews.com|access-date=2017-04-11}} </ref>

On January 5, 2017 iCarbonX released Meum, a digital health management platform.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20170105005285/en/iCarbonX-Expands-Digital-Life-Alliance-Accelerate-Development|title=iCarbonX Expands Digital Life Alliance to Accelerate Development of Global Health Ecosystem|website=www.businesswire.com|language=en|access-date=2017-10-03}}
</ref> The company name, “iCarbonX,” symbolizes the use of the internet and artificial intelligence to improve life, of which a central element is carbon.<ref>https://article.wn.com/view/2015/10/30/Is_iCarbonX_the_next_Google_in_Biotech/</ref> The “i” and “X” indicate the company’s plans to combine the Internet and artificial intelligence to create something new.
