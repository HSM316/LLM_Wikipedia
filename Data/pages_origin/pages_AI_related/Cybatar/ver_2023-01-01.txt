'''Cybatar''' is a [[technology company]] of the full cycle specialized in [[cloud-based technology]] platforms and software. 

The company was founded by [[Phiwa Nkambule]] in October 2014 initially as a social networking company but pivoted into a [[SaaS]] platform for [[On-demand economy|on-demand]] services in 2015.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://pressportal.co.za/it-new-media-and-software/story/8117/new-social-network-cybatar-com-hits-the-web.html |title=New Social Network: Cybatar.com Hits the Web - MyPressportal - Free press releases South Africa, RSS |publisher=Pressportal.co.za |date= |accessdate=2015-10-31 |archive-date=2018-07-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180724062700/https://www.pressportal.co.za/it-new-media-and-software/story/8117/new-social-network-cybatar-com-hits-the-web.html |url-status=live }}</ref> Cybatar has been involved in the development of other technology [[Startup company|startups]] including [[financial technology|financial technology company]] [[Riovic]].<ref name="autogenerated1">{{cite web |author=Brendyn Lotz |url=http://www.htxt.co.za/2015/10/14/award-in-sight-for-the-app-that-brings-you-petrol/ |title=International award in sight for SA app that brings you petrol - htxt.africa |publisher=Htxt.co.za |date=2015-10-14 |accessdate=2015-10-31 |archive-date=2017-01-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170112025453/http://www.htxt.co.za/2015/10/14/award-in-sight-for-the-app-that-brings-you-petrol/ |url-status=live }}</ref> In 2015 the company announced [[Africa]]'s first fuel delivery network<ref>{{cite web |author= |url=http://www.itnewsafrica.com/2015/10/cybatar-fillapp-to-eliminate-the-need-to-go-to-a-filling-station/ |title=Cybatar FillApp to eliminate the need to go to a filling station &#124; IT News Africa- Africa's Technology News Leader |publisher=IT News Africa |date= |accessdate=2015-10-31 |archive-date=2018-07-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180701103552/http://www.itnewsafrica.com/2015/10/cybatar-fillapp-to-eliminate-the-need-to-go-to-a-filling-station/ |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author= |url=http://www.nunnovation.com/2015/10/12/fillapp-africas-first-fuel-delivering-service/ |title=FillApp: Africa's First Fuel Delivering Service |publisher=Nunnovation |date= |accessdate=2015-10-31 |archive-date=2016-02-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160220112243/http://www.nunnovation.com/2015/10/12/fillapp-africas-first-fuel-delivering-service/ |url-status=live }}</ref> which was received as evidence that South African [[entrepreneurs]] had embraced the idea of the On Demand Economy,<ref>{{cite web |last=Thomas |first=Stuart |url=http://ventureburn.com/2015/10/dont-fancy-queuing-petrol-apps-got-covered/ |title=Don't fancy queuing for petrol? In the wake of the [[FeesMustFall]] protests in South Africa, the company launched a [[scholarship|university scholarship]] [[crowdfunding]] platform and [[Advocacy|campaign]] to save students across the country who were in tuition debt and were facing financial exclusions in 2016.<ref>{{cite web |last=Coetzee |first=Jacques |url=http://ventureburn.com/2015/10/crowdfunding-platform-edufund-seeks-to-save-sa-students-from-tuition-debt/ |title=Crowdfunding platform EduFund seeks to save SA students from tuition debt – Ventureburn |publisher=Ventureburn.com |date=2015-10-26 |accessdate=2015-10-31 |archive-date=2018-07-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180701103538/http://ventureburn.com/2015/10/crowdfunding-platform-edufund-seeks-to-save-sa-students-from-tuition-debt/ |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Mzekandaba |first=Simnikiwe |url=http://www.itweb.co.za/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=147373 |title=Crowdfunding to aid #FeesMustFall campaign |publisher=ITWeb |date= |accessdate=2015-10-31 |archive-date=2015-10-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151029153026/http://www.itweb.co.za/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=147373 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author=Lungelo Shezi |url=http://www.htxt.co.za/2015/10/28/crowdfunding-site-launched-to-help-500-sa-students-cover-tuition-fees-by-end-of-2015/ |title=Crowdfunding site launched to help 500 SA students cover tuition fees by end of 2015 - htxt.africa |publisher=Htxt.co.za |date= |accessdate=2015-10-31 |archive-date=2017-06-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170623233155/http://www.htxt.co.za/2015/10/28/crowdfunding-site-launched-to-help-500-sa-students-cover-tuition-fees-by-end-of-2015/ |url-status=live }}</ref>

The [[software industry|software company]] also received international recognition for being [[Lean startup|lean]] and its technological contribution to the community in South Africa through a nomination for the African Corporate Excellence Awards 2015 under the [[Information Technology]] category.<ref name="autogenerated1"/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.corp-vis.com/awards/2015-african-excellence-awards/ |title=2015 African Excellence Awards |publisher=Corporate Vision |date= |accessdate=2015-11-01 |archive-date=2015-10-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151026071906/http://www.corp-vis.com/awards/2015-african-excellence-awards |url-status=live }}</ref> It was then named Best [[Cloud Computing]] Company - Africa in November 2015 in the awards.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.cv-magazine.com/2016-cybatar-inc |title=Corporate Vision - Cybatar Inc |access-date=2021-02-20 |archive-date=2020-01-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200115215107/https://www.cv-magazine.com/2016-cybatar-inc |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://corp-vis.com/awards/2015-african-excellence-awards/results-2/details/cybatar-inc |title=2015 Best Cloud Computing Company - Africa |publisher=Corporate Vision |accessdate=2016-01-02 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304212532/http://corp-vis.com/awards/2015-african-excellence-awards/results-2/details/cybatar-inc |archivedate=2016-03-04 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bizcommunity.com/InBrief/196/708/12338.html|title=SA Startup Awarded for Excellence|publisher=BizCommunity|access-date=2016-01-13|archive-date=2016-03-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304095925/http://www.bizcommunity.com/InBrief/196/708/12338.html|url-status=live}}</ref>
