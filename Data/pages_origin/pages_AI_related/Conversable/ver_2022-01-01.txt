'''Conversable''' is a [[SaaS]] based [[Artificial Intelligence]] (AI) powered conversational platform, headquartered in [[Austin]], [[Texas]]. It allows customers to create intelligent, automated response flows through conversations in any messaging channel or voice platforms.<ref name="tc">{{cite news|last1=Mannes|first1=John|title=Conversable chatbot will offer allergy info along with a side of Wingstop wings|url=https://techcrunch.com/2016/06/07/conversable-chatbot-will-offer-allergy-info-along-with-a-side-of-wingstop-wings/|publisher=[[TechCrunch]]|date=June 7, 2016}}</ref><ref name="d">{{cite news|last1=Abril|first1=Danielle|title=Dallas Serial Entrepreneur's Bots Take Flight with Wingstop|url=https://www.dmagazine.com/frontburner/2016/06/dallas-serial-entrepreneurs-bots-take-flight-with-wingstop/|publisher=[[D Magazine]]|date=June 7, 2016}}</ref> It has offices in [[Austin]] and [[Dallas]].

== History ==
In 2015, Conversable Inc. was founded by Ben Lamm, founder and CEO of digital creative design studio [[Chaotic Moon Studios]], which was acquired by [[Accenture]], and Andrew Busey, former CEO & co-founder of social game company Challenge Games Inc., which was acquired by [[Zynga]].<ref name="bizj">{{cite news|last1=Calnan|first1=Christopher|title=Conversable launches after two years in stealth mode|url=https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/blog/techflash/2016/06/conversable-launches-after-two-years-in-stealth.html|publisher=[[American City Business Journals]]|date=June 7, 2016}}</ref> It received funding of $2 million from angel investors.<ref name="vb"/>

In March 2017, Conversable launched a new product called AQUA (Answer Questions Using [[Artificial intelligence|AI]]), which is a [[business intelligence]] (BI) platform.<ref>{{cite news|title=Conversable launches service to help businesses build more intelligent bots|url=https://venturebeat.com/2017/03/02/conversable-launches-service-to-help-businesses-build-more-intelligent-bots/|publisher=[[Venture Beat]]|date=March 2, 2017}}</ref><ref name="sil">{{cite news|title=Conversable Launches New Software Program AQUA and Plans to Triple in Size This Year|url=http://www.siliconhillsnews.com/2017/03/02/conversable-launches-new-software-program-aqua-and-plans-to-triple-in-size-this-year/|publisher=siliconhillsnews.com|date=March 2, 2017}}</ref><ref name="cio"/>

In 2018, Conversable was acquired by [[LivePerson]] to "help LivePerson continue to accelerate its goal of providing conversational commerce products to customers," according to CEO [[Robert LoCascio]].<ref>{{cite news|title=LivePerson acquires Conversable, a conversational AI company|url=https://venturebeat.com/2018/10/01/liveperson-acquires-conversable-a-conversational-ai-company/|publisher=[[Venture Beat]]|date=October 1, 2018}}</ref>

== Overview ==
It helps companies to deliver on-demand content, customer self-service, and conversational commerce via messaging channel and voice applications.<ref name="bizj"/><ref name="vb">{{cite news|last1=Blaise|first1=Zerega|title=Conversable launches conversational commerce platform for Facebook Messenger, Twitter, and SMS|url=https://venturebeat.com/2016/06/07/conversable-launches-conversational-commerce-platform-for-facebook-messenger-twitter-and-sms/|publisher=[[Venture Beat]]|date=June 7, 2016}}</ref>
