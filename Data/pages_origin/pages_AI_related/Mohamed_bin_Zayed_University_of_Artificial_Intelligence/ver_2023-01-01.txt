'''Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI)''' is a graduate-level, research-based academic institution located in [[Abu Dhabi]], [[United Arab Emirates]]. The current president, Professor [[Eric Xing]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bizjournals.com/pittsburgh/news/2020/12/01/cmu-professor-takes-president-role-at-first-ai-uni.html|access-date=2022-02-08|website=www.bizjournals.com}}</ref> joined in January 2021. Sir [[J. Michael Brady]] served as the founding, interim president.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-09-10|title=World's first graduate-level AI university set to welcome global cohort of students|url=http://saudigazette.com.sa/article/597784|access-date=2022-02-08|website=Saudigazette|language=English}}</ref>

The establishment of MBZUAI is part of the United Arab Emirates strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2031, for which came the appointment of the world's first Minister of State for Artificial Intelligence.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/news/education/university-of-artificial-intelligence-to-be-established-in-uae-|title=World's first artificial intelligence varsity in Abu Dhabi|author=Staff Reporter|website=Khaleej Times|language=en|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref>

As of June 2022, MBZUAI ranks 127th globally<ref>{{Cite web |last=National |first=The |date=2022-06-20 |title=MBZUAI ranked among world's top computer science institutions |url=https://www.thenationalnews.com/uae/education/2022/06/20/mbzuai-ranked-among-worlds-top-computer-science-institutions/ |access-date=2022-07-07 |website=The National |language=en}}</ref> among institutions that conduct research in computer science according to [https://csrankings.org/#/index?all&us CSRankings]. In [[artificial intelligence]], [[computer vision]], [[machine learning]], and [[natural language processing]] specifically the university ranks 30th globally.

The university provides admitted students with a full scholarship, including benefits such as a monthly allowance, health insurance, and accommodation.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-08-15|title=Global expert on robots joins Abu Dhabi's AI university as provost|url=https://www.thenationalnews.com/business/future/2021/08/15/global-expert-on-robots-joins-abu-dhabis-artificial-intelligence-university-as-provost/|access-date=2022-02-08|website=The National|language=en}}</ref> The university also secures internships by working with local and global companies. The initial class of graduate students were slated to start coursework in September 2020, but due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] coursework began in January 2021.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://gulfnews.com/uae/education/mohamed-bin-zayed-university-of-artificial-intelligence-welcomes-first-batch-of-students-1.76436112 |title=Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence welcomes first batch of students}}</ref>

==Academics==
MBZUAI seeks to empower a new generation of all AI leaders through exceptional education and a unique model of academia. The university offers graduate-level students a range of postgraduate degrees, focusing on core components of the AI industry: Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing. MBZUAI offers graduate-level degrees that are research-intensive and focused on practical AI. The university has the following programs:<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://aawsat.com/english/home/article/1949436/university-artificial-intelhttps://aawsat.com/english/home/article/1949436/university-artificial-intelligence-established-abu-dhabiligence-established-abu-dhabi|title=Home Page {{!}} Asharq Al Awsat|website=aawsat.com|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref>

==Research==
The university serves as a hub for AI research and education and aims to be an epicenter of AI innovation by finding solutions to the world's most pressing challenges. Moreover, as a new institution, the university's ambition is to become the go-to place for government<ref>{{Cite web|author=Staff Reporter|title=MBZUAI opens admissions cycle for Fall 2021 cohort|url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/business/local/mbzuai-opens-admissions-cycle-for-fall-2021-cohort|access-date=2021-09-27|website=Khaleej Times|language=en}}</ref> and enterprises when seeking AI expertise, solutions or advice.
