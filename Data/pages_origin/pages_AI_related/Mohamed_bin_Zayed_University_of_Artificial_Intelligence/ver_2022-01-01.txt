'''Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI)''' is a graduate-level, research-based academic institution that is located in [[Abu Dhabi]], [[United Arab Emirates]].

The university was established in 2019, and offers programs in [[Artificial intelligence|Artificial Intelligence]] field for both local and international students.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://mbzuai.ac.ae/|title=MBZUAI {{!}} Home|last=Dubai|first=TenTwenty {{!}} Webdesign, Webshops & E.-marketing {{!}}|website=MBZUAI|language=en|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref> The establishment of MBZUAI is part of the United Arab Emirates strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2031, for which came the appointment of the world's first Minister of State for Artificial Intelligence.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/news/education/university-of-artificial-intelligence-to-be-established-in-uae-|title=World's first artificial intelligence varsity in Abu Dhabi|author=Staff Reporter|website=Khaleej Times|language=en|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref>

The university will provide admitted students with a full scholarship, including benefits such as a monthly allowance, health insurance, and accommodation.{{citation needed|date=May 2021}}  The university will secure internships by working with local and global companies. The initial class of graduate students were slated to start coursework in September 2020, but due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] coursework began in January 2021.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://gulfnews.com/uae/education/mohamed-bin-zayed-university-of-artificial-intelligence-welcomes-first-batch-of-students-1.76436112 |title=Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence welcomes first batch of students}}</ref>

==Academics==
MBZUAI seeks to empower a new generation of AI leaders through exceptional education and a unique model of academia. The University offers graduate level students a range of postgraduate degrees, focusing on core components of the AI industry: Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing. MBZUAI offers graduate level degrees that are research intensive and focused on practical AI.The university has the following programs:<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://aawsat.com/english/home/article/1949436/university-artificial-intelhttps://aawsat.com/english/home/article/1949436/university-artificial-intelligence-established-abu-dhabiligence-established-abu-dhabi|title=Home Page {{!}} Asharq AL-awsat|website=aawsat.com|access-date=2019-12-17}}</ref>

==Professional Services==
MBZUAI provides consulting and support services <ref>{{Cite web|last=MBZUAI|first=Professional Services|title=MBZUAI {{!}} Professional Services|url=https://mbzuai.ac.ae/professional-services|url-status=live|access-date=2021-08-03|website=MBZUAI|language=en}}</ref> to address specific needs and challenges. These include feasibility studies, benchmarks and assessments, business processes enhancement, and specific expert guidance and mentoring for AI startups.

Furthermore, MBZUAI offers training services <ref>{{Cite web|title=MBZUAI launches AI for Leadership Training Course for Government Sector Leaders|url=https://www.mediaoffice.abudhabi/en/education/mohamed-bin-zayed-university-of-artificial/|access-date=2021-08-03|website=www.mediaoffice.abudhabi|language=en}}</ref> such as introductory and bespoke courses, which provide orientation and knowledge of AI where attendees learn about AI's impact on business and how it can enhance business operations.

==Research==
The university serves as a hub for AI research and education and aims to be an epicenter of AI innovation by finding solutions to the world's most pressing challenges. Moreover, as a new institution, the university's ambition is to become the go-to place for government<ref>{{Cite web|author=Staff Reporter|title=MBZUAI opens admissions cycle for Fall 2021 cohort|url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/business/local/mbzuai-opens-admissions-cycle-for-fall-2021-cohort|access-date=2021-09-27|website=Khaleej Times|language=en}}</ref> and enterprises when seeking AI expertise, solutions or advice.

Research focuses on the following themes:<ref name=":0" />

* Services and Quality of Life.
* Industrial and Manufacturing Technologies.
* Future Game Changers.
* Sustainability of Vital Resources and Environment.
