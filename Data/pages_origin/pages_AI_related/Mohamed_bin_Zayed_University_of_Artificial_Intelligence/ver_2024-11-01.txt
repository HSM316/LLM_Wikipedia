'''Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI)''' is a graduate-level, research-based academic institution located in [[Abu Dhabi]], [[United Arab Emirates]]. The current president, Professor [[Eric Xing]],<ref>{{Cite web |last=Mericle |first=Julia |date=December 1, 2020 |title=Carnegie Mellon University professor named president of artificial intelligence university |url=https://www.bizjournals.com/pittsburgh/news/2020/12/01/cmu-professor-takes-president-role-at-first-ai-uni.html |access-date=2022-02-08 |website=www.bizjournals.com |archive-date=2020-12-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201202152229/https://www.bizjournals.com/pittsburgh/news/2020/12/01/cmu-professor-takes-president-role-at-first-ai-uni.html |url-status=live }}</ref> joined in January 2021. Sir [[J. Michael Brady]] served as the founding, interim president.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2020-09-10 |title=World's first graduate-level AI university set to welcome global cohort of students |url=http://saudigazette.com.sa/article/597784 |access-date=2022-02-08 |website=Saudigazette |language=English |archive-date=2022-02-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220208065730/https://saudigazette.com.sa/article/597784 |url-status=live }}</ref> Professor [[Ling Shao]] was the initiator and the founding provost and executive vice president. 

The establishment of MBZUAI is part of the United Arab Emirates strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2031, for which came the appointment of the world's first Minister of State for Artificial Intelligence.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web |url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/news/education/university-of-artificial-intelligence-to-be-established-in-uae- |title=World's first artificial intelligence varsity in Abu Dhabi |author=Staff Reporter |website=Khaleej Times |language=en |access-date=2019-12-17 |archive-date=2019-11-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191101000928/https://www.khaleejtimes.com/news/education/university-of-artificial-intelligence-to-be-established-in-uae- |url-status=live }}</ref>

As of June 2022, MBZUAI ranks 127th globally among institutions that conduct research in computer science according to CSRankings.<ref>{{Cite web |last=National |first=The |date=2022-06-20 |title=MBZUAI ranked among world's top computer science institutions |url=https://www.thenationalnews.com/uae/education/2022/06/20/mbzuai-ranked-among-worlds-top-computer-science-institutions/ |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220707082428/https://www.thenationalnews.com/uae/education/2022/06/20/mbzuai-ranked-among-worlds-top-computer-science-institutions/ |archive-date=2022-07-07 |access-date=2022-07-07 |website=The National |language=en}}</ref> In [[artificial intelligence]], [[computer vision]], [[machine learning]], and [[natural language processing]] specifically the university ranks 30th globally.{{cn|date=September 2024}}

The university provides admitted students with a full scholarship, including benefits such as a monthly allowance, health insurance, and accommodation.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2021-08-15 |title=Global expert on robots joins Abu Dhabi's AI university as provost |url=https://www.thenationalnews.com/business/future/2021/08/15/global-expert-on-robots-joins-abu-dhabis-artificial-intelligence-university-as-provost/ |access-date=2022-02-08 |website=The National |language=en |archive-date=2022-02-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220208065102/https://www.thenationalnews.com/business/future/2021/08/15/global-expert-on-robots-joins-abu-dhabis-artificial-intelligence-university-as-provost/ |url-status=live }}</ref> The university also secures internships by working with local and global companies. The initial class of graduate students were slated to start coursework in September 2020, but due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] coursework began in January 2021.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://gulfnews.com/uae/education/mohamed-bin-zayed-university-of-artificial-intelligence-welcomes-first-batch-of-students-1.76436112 |title=Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence welcomes first batch of students |date=12 January 2021 |access-date=9 February 2021 |archive-date=20 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210420130845/https://gulfnews.com/uae/education/mohamed-bin-zayed-university-of-artificial-intelligence-welcomes-first-batch-of-students-1.76436112 |url-status=live }}</ref>
