'''UiPath''' is a global software company that develops a platform for [[robotic process automation]] (RPA or RPAAI).<ref name="inc42.com">{{Cite web|url=https://inc42.com/buzz/with-india-focus-global-robotics-company-uipath-raises-225-mn/|title=With India Focus, Global Robotics Company UiPath Raises $225 Mn|date=September 18, 2018|website=Inc42 Media}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rpaai.com/|title= RPAAI|date=August 18, 2019|website=RPAAI}}</ref>

== History ==
UiPath was founded in 2005 by the [[Romania]]n entrepreneurs, Daniel Dines and Marius Tîrcă. The company started from [[Bucharest, Romania]] and later opened offices in [[London]], [[New York City]], [[Bangalore]], [[Paris]], [[Singapore]], [[Washington, D.C.]], and [[Tokyo]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.romania-insider.com/romanian-tech-startup-uipath-raises-usd-30-mln-develop-intelligent-software-robots/|title=Romanian tech startup UiPath raises USD 30 mln to develop intelligent software robots|website=Romania-Insider.com|accessdate=March 9, 2018}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> In 2017, the company reported 590 employees and moved its headquarters to New York to be closer to its international customer base.<ref name=":1">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/reuters/2018/03/06/technology/06reuters-europe-venturecapital-romania.html|title=Romanian Firm Emerges as Leader in Automating Business Tasks|date=March 6, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180306184325/https://www.nytimes.com/reuters/2018/03/06/technology/06reuters-europe-venturecapital-romania.html|archive-date=March 6, 2018|url-status=dead|agency=Reuters}}</ref> In 2016, the company had 100 customers which rose to 700 customers in 2017.<ref name=":1" /> In 2019, UiPath crossed 5,000 customers worldwide.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.strategy-business.com/article/Making-automation-easy?gko=13364|title=PwC Strategy+Business: Automation Made Easy|website=https://www.strategy-business.com/|accessdate=20 Nov 2019}}</ref>

According to Gartner market share data, UiPath advanced from #5 in share in 2017 to #1 in share in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2019-06-24-gartner-says-worldwide-robotic-process-automation-sof|title= Gartner Says Worldwide Robotic Process Automation Software Market Grew 63% in 2018|website=www.gartner.com|accessdate=24 June 2019}}</ref>

In September 2019, UiPath was ranked #3 on the Forbes Cloud 100.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/cloud100/|title=Forbes Cloud 100|website=www.forbes.com|accessdate=11 September 2019}}</ref> The company was the featured cover story of the September 30, 2019 Forbes print edition with UiPath CEO Daniel Dines called "Boss of the Bots."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://forbes.com/cloud100/|title= From Communism To Coding: How Daniel Dines Of $7 Billion UiPath Became The First Bot Billionaire |website=www.forbes.com|accessdate=11 September 2019}}</ref>

UiPath was ranked first in the Deloitte Technology Fast 500 on November 6, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www2.deloitte.com/us/en/pages/technology-media-and-telecommunications/articles/fast500-winners.html|title=2019 Technology Fast 500 winners|website=www.deloitte.com|accessdate=6 November 2019}}</ref>

In April 2020, UiPath was named the top tech company and #2 overall in the Financial Times 1000, FT1000 ranking: Americas' fastest growing companies 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ft.com/americas-fastest-growing-companies-2020|title=2020 FT1000 winners|website=www.ft.com|accessdate=6 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ft.com/content/b0a5e02e-7412-11ea-ad98-044200cb277f|title=FT ranking: the Americas’ fastest-growing companies|last=Kelly|first=Maxine|date=2020-04-06|website=www.ft.com|language=en-GB|access-date=2020-04-17}}</ref>

In June 2020, UiPath was named to the CNBC Disruptor 50 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2020/06/16/uipath-disruptor-50.html|title=2020 CNBC Disruptor 50|website=www.cnbc.com|accessdate=13 June 2020}}</ref>

On September 16, 2020, UiPath was ranked #3 on Forbes Cloud 100 2020 for the second consecutive year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/cloud100/#37c358225f94|title="Forbes Cloud 100 2020"|website=Forbes|language=en-US}}</ref>

On December 17, 2020, Bloomberg reported that UiPath filed confidentially for an initial public offering (IPO)<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2020-12-17/uipath-is-said-to-be-close-to-filing-confidentially-for-ipo|title="UiPath Says It Filed Confidentially for IPO, Could Be Valued at More Than $20 Billion"|website=Bloomberg|language=en-US}}</ref>

== Funding ==
In August 2015, UiPath closed an initial seed funding round of US$1.6 million led by the Earlybird Venture Capital, with Credo Ventures and Seedcamp as backers.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://business-review.eu/news/the-story-of-uipath-how-it-became-romanias-first-unicorn-164248|title=The story of UiPath – How did it become Romania's first unicorn?|date=April 9, 2018|website=business-review.eu}}</ref>

In April 2017, UiPath received a $30 million investment in one of the biggest Series A rounds of funding in Europe, led by Accel. Previous investors Earlybird Venture Capital, Credo Ventures and Seedcamp also joined.<ref name=":0" />

On March 6, 2018, UiPath received a $153 million investment from Accel, [[CapitalG]], and [[Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers]], valuing the company at $1.1 billion.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://techcrunch.com/2018/03/06/uipath-confirms-153m-at-1-1b-valuation-from-accel-capitalg-and-kp-for-its-software-robots/|title=TechCrunch: UiPath Confirms $153 M at $1.1B Valuation|last1=Lunden|first1=Ingrid|date=March 6, 2018|accessdate=March 6, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/brief-uipath-raises-153-million-series-b/brief-uipath-raises-153-million-series-b-led-by-accel-idUSFWN1QO0CZ|title=UiPath Confirms $153 M at $1.1B Valuation|date=March 6, 2018|accessdate=March 6, 2018}}</ref>

On September 18, 2018, UiPath raised $225 million in the funding round led by CapitalG and Sequoia Capital at a $3 billion valuation.<ref name="inc42.com"/>

On April 30, 2019, UiPath raised $568 million in a Series D round of funding led by hedge fund Coatue Management, with participation from Alphabet’s CapitalG, Sequoia, Accel, Madrona Venture Group, IVP, Dragoneer, Wellington, Sands Capital, and funds advised by T. Rowe Price & Associates. The company also now claims a valuation of $7 billion.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2019/04/30/uipath-rpa-series-d/|title=UiPath nabs $568M at a $7B valuation to bring robotic process automation to the front office|website=TechCrunch|language=en-US}}</ref>

On July 13, 2020, UiPath raised $225 Million at a valuation of $10.2 Billion.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2020/07/13/uipath-reels-in-another-225m-as-valuation-soars-to-10-2b|title="UiPath reels in another $225M as valuation soars to $10.2B|website=TechCrunch|language=en-US}}</ref>

On December 17, 2020, UiPath filed a confidential draft registration statement with the United States Security and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2020/12/17/uipath-files-confidential-ipo-paperwork-with-sec/|title="UiPath files confidential IPO|website=TechCrunch|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2020-12-17/uipath-is-said-to-be-close-to-filing-confidentially-for-ipo|title=Software maker UiPath says it has confidentially filed for IPO|website=Bloomberg|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2020/12/17/uipath-files-for-ipo-could-become-one-of-nycs-biggest-tech-companies.html|title=UiPath files for IPO, could become one of New York City's largest tech companies|website=CNBC|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.barrons.com/articles/uipath-files-confidentially-for-ipo-51608252665|title=UiPath: Get ready for another big software IPO|website=Barrons|language=en-US}}</ref>
