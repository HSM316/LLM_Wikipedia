'''''Fuzzy Sets and Systems''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] international [[scientific journal]] published by [[Elsevier]] on behalf of the [[International Fuzzy Systems Association]] (IFSA) and was founded in 1978. The editors-in-chief (as of 2010) are Bernard De Baets of the Dept. of Applied Mathematics, Biometrics and Process Control, (at the University of Gent in Belgium), [[Didier Dubois (mathematician)|Didier Dubois]] (of IRIT, Université Paul Sabatier in Toulouse, France) and Eyke Hüllermeier (of the Dept. of Mathematics and Computer Science, Universität Marburg, Germany).  The journal publishes 24 issues a year. ''Fuzzy Sets and Systems'' is abstracted and indexed by [[Scopus]] and the [[Science Citation Index]]. According to the [[Journal Citation Reports]] released in 2010, its 2-year impact factor calculated for 2009 is 2.138 and its 2010 5-year impact factor for 2009 is 2.551.
