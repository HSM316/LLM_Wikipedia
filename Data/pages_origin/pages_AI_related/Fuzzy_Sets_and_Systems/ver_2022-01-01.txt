'''''Fuzzy Sets and Systems''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] international [[scientific journal]] published by [[Elsevier]] on behalf of the [[International Fuzzy Systems Association]] (IFSA) and was founded in 1978. The editors-in-chief (as of 2010) are Bernard De Baets of the Department of Applied Mathematics, Biometrics and Process Control, (at the [[Ghent University]] in Belgium), [[Didier Dubois (mathematician)|Didier Dubois]] (of IRIT, [[Université Paul Sabatier]] in Toulouse, France) and Eyke Hüllermeier (of the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science, [[Universität Marburg]], Germany).  The journal publishes 24 issues a year.<ref>{{Cite journal |author-last1=Laengle |author-first1=Sigifredo |author-last2=Lobos |author-first2= Valeria |author-last3=Merigo |author-first3=Jose M. |author-last4=Herrera-Viedma |author-first4=Enrique |author-last5=Cobo |author-first5=Manuel J. |author-last6=De Baets |author-first6=Bernard |date=2021|title=Forty years of Fuzzy Sets and Systems: A bibliometric analysis |journal=Fuzzy Sets and Systems | volume=402 | pages=155–183 |doi=10.1016/j.fss.2020.03.012 |s2cid=216229348 |issn=0165-0114}}</ref> ''Fuzzy Sets and Systems'' is abstracted and indexed by [[Scopus]] and the [[Science Citation Index]]. According to the [[Journal Citation Reports]] released in 2010, its 2-year impact factor calculated for 2020 is 3.343 and its 5-year impact factor for 2020 is 3.213.
