The '''International Semantic Web Conference''' ('''ISWC''') is a series of [[academic conferences]] and the premier international forum for the [[Semantic Web]], [[Linked Data]] and Knowledge Graph Community.<ref>[http://libra.msra.cn/RankList?entitytype=3&topdomainid=2&subdomainid=15&last=10] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.today/20130116011335/http://libra.msra.cn/RankList?entitytype=3&topdomainid=2&subdomainid=15&last=10 |date=2013-01-16 }}: Rank 2 in WWW category.</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://academic.research.microsoft.com/RankList?entitytype=3&topdomainid=2&subdomainid=18&last=10 |title=Microsoft Academic |accessdate=2012-08-10 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://archive.today/20130104011047/http://academic.research.microsoft.com/RankList?entitytype=3&topdomainid=2&subdomainid=18&last=10 |archivedate=2013-01-04 }}: Rank 4 in Database category.</ref><ref>[http://arnetminer.org/page/conference-rank/html/SW,Logic.html]: Rank 2 in SW category.</ref> Here, scientists, industry specialists, and practitioners meet to discuss the future of practical, scalable, user-friendly, and game changing solutions.<ref>[http://iswc2012.semanticweb.org/]:ISWC2012 web site.</ref> Its proceedings are published in the [[Lecture Notes in Computer Science]] by [[Springer-Verlag]].

The annual ISWC conference series was established in 2002 by the [https://swsa.semanticweb.org/ Semantic Web Science Association] (SWSA) after the success of the [https://files.ifi.uzh.ch/ddis/iswc_archive/iswc/pps/web/swws-zip/index.htm Semantic Web Working Symposium], a three-day conference held at Stanford University in the summer of 2001. SWSA continues to oversee the ISWC conference and choose organizers for future ISWC instances based on bids.
