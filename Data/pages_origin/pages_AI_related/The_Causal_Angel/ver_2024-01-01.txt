'''''The Causal Angel''''' is the third [[science fiction]] [[novel]] by [[Hannu Rajaniemi]] featuring the protagonist [[List of characters in the Jean le Flambeur series#Jean le Flambeur|Jean le Flambeur]].<ref>{{cite web | title=Fiction Book Review: The Causal Angel | url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/978-0-7653-2951-6 | publisher=Publishers Weekly | accessdate=2014-08-24}}</ref> It was published in July 2014 by [[Victor Gollancz Ltd|Gollancz]] in the UK and by [[Tor Books|Tor]] in the US. The novel is the finale of a [[trilogy]].<ref name=Guard>{{Cite news| url=https://www.theguardian.com/books/2010/nov/09/hannu-rajaniemi-quantum-thief | title=Hannu Rajaniemi: the science of fiction | author=Richard Lea | work=[[The Guardian]] | accessdate=2014-08-24 | location=London | date=2010-11-09}}</ref> The previous novels in the series are ''[[The Quantum Thief]]'' (2010) and ''[[The Fractal Prince]]'' (2012).

==Synopsis==
After the events of ''The Fractal Prince'', Jean le Flambeur and [[List of characters in the Jean le Flambeur series#Mieli|Mieli]] are separated and their sentient spacecraft [[List of characters in the Jean le Flambeur series#Perhonen|Perhonen]] is destroyed. The two protagonists, each in their own way, struggle to decide where their loyalties lie and how to proceed in the catastrophically altered situation. Meanwhile, the [[Solar System]] is plummeting into an all-out war of unprecedented scale and consequence. The most powerful factions, the Sobornost and the Zoku, are gathering their forces and making their plays, while simultaneously being torn apart by internal strifes.
