==History==
Aware was incorporated in 1986 in [[Cambridge, Massachusetts]] at which time it was envisioned by its founders as a "mathematical engineering company".<ref name=waveletanalysis />

Aware went [[public company|public]] in 1996 to commercialize its [[DSL]] products<ref>{{cite web|last1=LaPedus|first1=Mark|title=Siemens To Expand DSL Biz Into Consumer Market With Aware License|url=http://www.eetimes.com/document.asp?doc_id=1119308|website=[[EETimes]]|accessdate=29 January 2017}}</ref> and [[intellectual property]],<ref>{{cite web|last1=Knox|first1=Noelle|title=INVESTING IT; Choosing Between Phone and Cable for Internet Access|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1998/01/25/business/investing-it-choosing-between-phone-and-cable-for-internet-access.html?rref=collection%2Ftimestopic%2FAware%20Inc.&action=click&contentCollection=business&region=stream&module=stream_unit&version=latest&contentPlacement=1&pgtype=collection|website=The New York Times|accessdate=11 December 2016|date=25 January 1998}}</ref> at which time it also had an established business supplying [[biometrics]] software for [[law enforcement]] applications.<ref>{{cite web|title=AWARE INC. JUMPS 56% ON INTEL SOFTWARE DEAL|url=https://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-8572154.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170215202737/https://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-8572154.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=15 February 2017|website=[[The Boston Globe]]|accessdate=11 December 2016|date=22 October 1999}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Chant|first1=Tim De|title=Biometrics and the Future of Identification|url=https://www.pbs.org/wgbh/nova/next/tech/biometrics-and-the-future-of-identification/|website=[[Pbs.org|PBS]]|accessdate=11 December 2016|date=18 June 2013}}</ref>

Between 2009 and 2012, Aware [[divestment|divested]] several assets unrelated to its current business.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Hammerschmidt|first1=Christoph|title=Lantiq acquires home networking IP|url=http://www.eetimes.com/document.asp?doc_id=1172023|website=[[EETimes]]|accessdate=18 February 2016|date=20 October 2009}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Aware to sell patents for $75 mln|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/aware-idUSL3E8FR6IH20120427|publisher=[[Reuters]]|accessdate=18 February 2016|date=27 April 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Gold|first1=Jon|title=Intel buys $75 million worth of patents from Aware Inc.|url=http://www.networkworld.com/article/2188108/wireless/intel-buys--75-million-worth-of-patents-from-aware-inc-.html|website=[[Network World]]|accessdate=11 December 2016}}</ref>

As of 2019, Aware is focused exclusively on providing [[biometrics]] software and services.  Its products apply [[fingerprint recognition]], [[face recognition]], [[iris recognition]], and [[speaker recognition]] for applications including [[multi-factor authentication]] and [[biometric identification]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.aware.com |title=Aware corporate website |author=<!--Not stated--> |date= |website= |publisher= |access-date=11 July 2019 |quote=}}</ref>
