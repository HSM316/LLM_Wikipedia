'''SmartAction''' provides artificial intelligence-based voice self-service.  IVA is a cloud-based, hosted service. The company was founded by inventor and entrepreneur Peter Voss<ref name="kurzweil">"[http://www.kurzweilai.net/peter-voss Contributors, Kurzweil, (Retrieved Jan 5, 2015)]</ref><ref>"El último paso lo ha dado Adaptive AI, que ha presentado el primer sistema artificial de inteligencia. El SmartAction "es un sistema interactivo de respuesta por voz que podrá ser instalado en robots", dice Peter Voss, su creador." Juan Manuel Daganzo [http://www.publico.es/ciencias/tecnologia/209465/generacion?orden=FECHA&asc=no&aleatorio=0.5 Generación Robot]; Los investigadores calculan que, en diez o quince años, los robots tendrán capacidades humanas muy desarrolladas 13/03/2009 Publico (Spain)</ref> and is headquartered in El Segundo, California.

== History ==
In 2001, Peter Voss founded an R&D startup, Adaptive AI, Inc., to research and develop a prototype [[artificial general intelligence]] system based on his theory of intelligence.<ref name=kurzweil/> In 2009 Voss founded Smart Action Company, LLC to commercialize this technology. Created out of Voss' research and development, SmartAction created its first practical application of this new technology focused on the management of inbound and outbound calls for contact centers.<ref name="speechtechmag090112">"[http://www.speechtechmag.com/Articles/News/Industry-News/Start-Up-SmartAction-Starts-with-IVR-Release-52266.aspx Start-Up SmartAction Starts with IVR Release]", SpeechTechMag.com 2009-01-12. Retrieved on 2009-08-11.</ref> 

== Technology ==
The company provides a Voice Registration platform that uses natural language speech recognition and is based on an object-oriented coding framework.<ref name="intelligent voice automation">[http://www.smartaction.com/our-difference/artificial-intelligence Intelligent Voice Automation], SmartAction, (Retrieved Jan. 5, 2015).</ref> This platforms utilises a proprietary AI engine drive the conversation with the caller, intended to improve over time.<ref name="about">[http://smartaction.com/about.html About], SmartAction, (Retrieved Jan 5, 2015).</ref><ref name="io9 090208">"[http://io9.com/5148075/im-sorry-i-cant-connect-your-call-dave I'm Sorry, I Can't Connect Your Call, Dave]", io9.com 2009-02-08. Retrieved on 2009-09-01.</ref> The platform is intended to make development and updates to the system quicker than older technology by using built-in knowledge and skills.
