'''Evergage''' was a [[cloud-based]] [[software]] that allows users to collect, analyze, and respond to user behavior on their websites and web applications in real-time.<ref name="WebInno">{{Cite news |title=WebInnovators: Apptegic |first=David |last=Beisel |url=http://webinnovatorsgroup.com/2012/06/22/webinnovators-apptegic |date=June 22, 2012 |accessdate=January 4, 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130419043917/http://webinnovatorsgroup.com/2012/06/22/webinnovators-apptegic |archive-date=April 19, 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref>  In 2020, Evergage was acquired by [[Salesforce]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.evergage.com/blog/evergage-has-joined-the-salesforce-family/|title=Evergage Has Joined the Salesforce Family|last=Wirthâ€¢|first=Karl|date=2020-02-03|website=Evergage|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cmswire.com/customer-experience/salesforce-acquires-evergage-cdp-industry-reached-1b-in-2019-and-more-cx-news/|title=Salesforce Acquires Evergage, CDP Industry Reached $1B in 2019 and More CX News|website=CMSWire.com|language=en|access-date=2020-03-26}}</ref> It has been rebranded to Interaction Studio.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=sf.mc_isev_interaction_studio.htm&type=5|title=Interaction Studio (Formerly Evergage)|language=en}}</ref> The company positions itself as a "real-time personalization and customer data platform (CDP)."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cop.evidon.com/companies/evergage|title=Company Info|website=cop.evidon.com|access-date=2020-02-04}}</ref>

The company was founded as Apptegic in 2010 by Karl Wirth and Greg Hinkle, who met while working at [[Red Hat]].<ref>{{Cite news |title=Apptegic, deciphering how customers interact with Web-based apps, attracts angel funding from Netezza founder Jit Saxena |first=Scott |last=Kirsner |url=http://www.boston.com/business/technology/innoeco/2011/06/apptegic_deciphering_how_custo.html |date=June 9, 2011 |accessdate=December 11, 2012 |work=The Boston Globe}}</ref> In May 2012, Evergage was a finalist in the [[TechCrunch]] Disrupt Startup Battlefield and shortly thereafter launched its cloud-based service.<ref name="techcrunch">{{Cite news |title=Apptegic Uses Big Data Analysis To Help Companies Retain And Upsell Their Customers |first=Ryan |last=Lawler |url=https://techcrunch.com/2012/05/22/apptegic/ |date=May 22, 2012 |accessdate=December 11, 2012}}</ref><ref name="Web Host Industry Review">{{Cite news |title=Behind the Web Hosting Decision of a Boston Startup: Apptegic |first=Nicole |last=Henderson |url=http://www.thewhir.com/web-hosting-news/behind-the-web-hosting-decision-of-a-boston-startup-apptegic |date=June 29, 2012 |accessdate=December 26, 2012}}</ref>   In August, 2013, the company changed its name to Evergage.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Brooke|first1=Eliza|title=Evergage, The Disrupt Finalist Formerly Known As Apptegic, Launches A Freemium B2B Service For Improving Website Click-Throughs|url=https://techcrunch.com/2013/08/08/evergage-the-disrupt-finalist-formerly-known-as-apptegic-launches-a-freemium-b2b-service-for-improving-website-click-throughs/|publisher=TechCrunch|date=August 8, 2013}}</ref>

In 2015, Evergage expanded its capabilities to include A/B and multivariate testing,<ref>{{cite news|title=Evergage Launches Multivariate Testing Functionalities|url=http://www.retailtouchpoints.com/features/solution-spotlight/evergage-launches-multivariate-testing-functionalities|publisher=Retail Touchpoints|date=December 8, 2015}}</ref> as well as automated product and content recommendations driven by [[machine learning]]. The platform also added personalization support for mobile apps<ref>{{cite news|title=New 'Evergage For Mobile Apps' Solution Automatically Captures Mobile App User Behavioral Data For Omnichannel Personalization|url=http://www.innovativeretailtechnologies.com/doc/new-evergage-data-for-omnichannel-personalization-0001|publisher=Innovative Retail Technologies|date=September 23, 2015}}</ref> and, in 2016, open-time email personalization,<ref>{{cite news|last1=Anderson|first1=Steve|title=Evergage for Email brings Personalization, Relevance to Email Marketin|url=http://www.madmarketer.com/topics/news/articles/421508-evergage-email-brings-personalization-relevance-email-marketing.htm|publisher=Mad Marketer|date=May 25, 2016}}</ref> and triggered email in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.destinationcrm.com/Articles/ReadArticle.aspx?ArticleID=120507|title=Evergage Launches Personalized Triggered Email Capabilities|website=www.destinationcrm.com|access-date=2020-02-04}}</ref> Evergage acquired e-commerce and email personalization provider MyBuys in 2018.{{cn|date=November 2021}}
