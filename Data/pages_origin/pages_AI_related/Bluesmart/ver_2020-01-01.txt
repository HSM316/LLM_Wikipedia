'''Bluesmart Inc.''' was a global travel technology company that developed and produced [[Internet of Things]] travel products. The brand launched a [[hand luggage|carry-on]] [[suitcase]], the Bluesmart One, on the [[crowdfunding]] website [[Indiegogo]] in 2014.<ref>https://www.engadget.com/2014/10/20/bluesmart-connected-luggage-indiegogo-campaign/</ref> The campaign raised $2.2 million USD in contributions and became the highest crowdfunding project ever for a travel product at that time.<ref name="tnooz.com">http://www.tnooz.com/article/how-bluesmart-became-most-successful-crowdfunding-ever/</ref>

On May 1, 2018, Bluesmart Inc. announced they would be shutting down operations, due to a ban by major US airlines on smart luggage with non-removable batteries.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theverge.com/2018/5/1/17306410/smart-luggage-ban-bluesmart-shutdown-refunds|title=Bluesmart shuts down after airlines ban smart luggage|last=O'Kane|first=Sean|date=1 May 2018|work=[[The Verge]]|access-date=1 May 2018|publisher=[[Vox Media]]}}</ref> The company's intellectual property was sold to Travelpro and the company's products will no longer be supported.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://techcrunch.com/2018/05/01/bluesmart-sells-assets-to-travelpro-following-smart-luggage-ban/|title=Bluesmart sells assets to Travelpro following smart luggage ban|last=Heater|first=Brian|date=1 May 2018|work=[[TechCrunch]]|access-date=1 May 2018}}</ref>

==History==
===Crowdfunding campaign===

In October 2014, the company launched its first product on Indiegogo, asking for support of their campaign - offering pre-orders of their product as rewards.<ref>https://techcrunch.com/2014/11/17/bluesmart-indiegogo/</ref> The company met its goal in 2 hours and broke records in the Hardware category by pre-selling 10,000 units to 114 countries and collecting more than $2.2 million USD in contributions.<ref>https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/bluesmart-world-s-first-smart-connected-carry-on/x/8655534</ref> In November 2014, while their extended [[Indiegogo]] campaign was still ongoing, the company was selected by Silicon Valley-based startup accelerator [[Y Combinator (company)|Y Combinator]] to be part of its program for the Winter 2015 batch.<ref>https://techcrunch.com/2015/01/23/yc-backed-bluesmart-raises-2-million-for-its-self-tracking-suitcase/</ref>

===Production of the first smart carry-On===
After Bluesmart Inc.'s manufacturing [[prototype]] passed its first reliability tests in June 2015, they set up a Mass Production division and local team in China which quickly grew to 15 employees.{{Citation needed|date=April 2018}} Bluesmart Inc. began shipping what would be known as the Bluesmart One in August 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://oooo.bluesmart.com/2015/09/01/our-most-important-milestone-so-far-we-started-to-ship/|title=Our Most Important Milestone So Far: We Started to Ship!|publisher=Bluesmart|accessdate=2015-08-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171015150316/http://oooo.bluesmart.com/2015/09/01/our-most-important-milestone-so-far-we-started-to-ship/|archive-date=2017-10-15|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Products==

In late 2015, the brand released Bluesmart One.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/bluesmart-world-s-first-smart-connected-carry-on#/updates/all|title=Bluesmart: World's First Smart, Connected Carry-On|date=November 20, 2015|website=Indiegogo|access-date=April 12, 2018}}</ref>

In 2016, the brand released the premium Bluesmart Black Edition carry-on, which was more durable, and had an upgraded exterior anti-theft system. {{Citation needed|date=April 2018}}

In June 2017, Bluesmart Inc. introduced its Series 2 products including the Bluesmart Cabin, Check, Laptop Bag and Passport Pouch.<ref>https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/bluesmart-series-2-smart-luggage-system-travel/</ref> 

Bluesmart Inc. was awarded the Red Dot Best of the Best Design Award category.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://red-dot.de/pd/online-exhibition/work/?lang=en&code=15-01401-2016&y=2016&c=169&a=0 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2017-06-07 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171015150231/http://red-dot.de/pd/online-exhibition/work/?lang=en&code=15-01401-2016&y=2016&c=169&a=0 |archive-date=2017-10-15 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
