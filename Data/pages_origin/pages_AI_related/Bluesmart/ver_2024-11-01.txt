'''Bluesmart Inc.''' was a global travel technology company that developed and produced [[Internet of Things]] travel products. The brand launched a [[hand luggage|carry-on]] [[suitcase]], the Bluesmart One, on the [[crowdfunding]] website [[Indiegogo]] in 2014.<ref>{{Cite news | url=https://www.engadget.com/2014/10/20/bluesmart-connected-luggage-indiegogo-campaign/ | title=Bluesmart wants to crowdfund the 'world's first' connected luggage | first=Aaron  |last=Souppouris | work=[[Engadget]] | date=October 20, 2014}}</ref> The campaign raised US$2.2 million in contributions and became the highest crowdfunding project ever for a travel product at that time.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.phocuswire.com/How-Bluesmart-became-one-of-the-most-successful-crowdfunding-campaigns-ever | title=How Bluesmart became one of the most successful crowdfunding campaigns ever | first=Nick | last=Vivion | work=Phocuswire | date=December 17, 2014}}</ref>

On May 1, 2018, Bluesmart Inc. announced they would be shutting down operations, due to a ban by major US airlines on smart luggage with non-removable batteries.<ref>{{Cite news| url=https://www.theverge.com/2018/5/1/17306410/smart-luggage-ban-bluesmart-shutdown-refunds|title=Bluesmart shuts down after airlines ban smart luggage| last=O'Kane|first=Sean|work=[[The Verge]] |publisher=[[Vox Media]] |date=1 May 2018}}</ref> The company's intellectual property was sold to Travelpro and the company's products will no longer be supported.<ref>{{Cite news| url=https://techcrunch.com/2018/05/01/bluesmart-sells-assets-to-travelpro-following-smart-luggage-ban/|title=Bluesmart sells assets to Travelpro following smart luggage ban|last=Heater|first=Brian |work=[[TechCrunch]] |date=1 May 2018}}</ref>

==History==
===Crowdfunding campaign===

In October 2014, the company launched its first product on Indiegogo, asking for support of their campaign – offering pre-orders of their product as rewards.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2014/11/17/bluesmart-indiegogo/|title=Self-Tracking Suitcase Passes $1M on Indiegogo|date=17 November 2014 }}</ref> The company met its goal in two hours and broke records in the Hardware category by pre-selling 10,000 units to 114 countries and collecting more than $2.2 million in contributions.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/bluesmart-world-s-first-smart-connected-carry-on/x/8655534|title=Bluesmart: World's First Smart, Connected Carry-On}}</ref> In November 2014, while their extended [[Indiegogo]] campaign was still ongoing, the company was selected by Silicon Valley–based startup accelerator [[Y Combinator (company)|Y Combinator]] to be part of its program for the Winter 2015 batch.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2015/01/23/yc-backed-bluesmart-raises-2-million-for-its-self-tracking-suitcase/|title=YC-Backed Bluesmart Raises $2 Million for Its Self-Tracking Suitcase|date=23 January 2015 }}</ref>

===Production of the first smart carry-on===
After Bluesmart Inc.'s manufacturing [[prototype]] passed its first reliability tests in June 2015, they set up a Mass Production division and local team in China which quickly grew to 15 employees.{{Citation needed|date=April 2018}} Bluesmart Inc. began shipping what would be known as the Bluesmart One in August 2015.<ref name=indiegogo/>

==Products==

In late 2015, the brand released Bluesmart One.<ref name=indiegogo>{{Cite web| url=https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/bluesmart-world-s-first-smart-connected-carry-on#/updates/all |title=Bluesmart: World's First Smart, Connected Carry-On| website=[[Indiegogo]]|date=November 20, 2015}}</ref>

In 2016, the brand released the premium Bluesmart Black Edition carry-on, which was more durable, and had an upgraded exterior anti-theft system. {{Citation needed|date=April 2018}}

In June 2017, Bluesmart Inc. introduced its Series 2 products including the Bluesmart Cabin, Check, Laptop Bag and Passport Pouch.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/bluesmart-series-2-smart-luggage-system-travel/|title = Bluesmart Series 2: Smart Luggage System}}</ref> 

Bluesmart Inc. was awarded the Red Dot Best of the Best Design Award category.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://red-dot.de/pd/online-exhibition/work/?lang=en&code=15-01401-2016&y=2016&c=169&a=0 |title=Bluesmart Carry-on Suitcase - 2016 &#124; work &#124; Red Dot Award: Product Design |access-date=2017-06-07 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171015150231/http://red-dot.de/pd/online-exhibition/work/?lang=en&code=15-01401-2016&y=2016&c=169&a=0 |archive-date=2017-10-15 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
