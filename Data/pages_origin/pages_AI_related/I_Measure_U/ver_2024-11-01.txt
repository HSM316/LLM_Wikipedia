'''IMeasureU, Ltd.''' ('''IMU''') is a company that specializes in [[wearable technology]]. They develop [[inertial measurement unit]]s that analyze body movements in sports.<ref>[http://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11284537 Small business: Commercialising innovation - Mark Finch, I Measure U] NZ Herald</ref> The company combines sensor data with computational models to model human body movement. IMeasureU has collaborated with [[Athletics Australia]] runners.<ref>[http://www.nbr.co.nz/article/i-measure-u-launch-kickstarter-campaign-consumer-running-app-bd-167362 I Measure U to launch Kickstarter campaign for consumer running app]{{Dead link|date=August 2024 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} NBR</ref>

In July 2017, the company was acquired by Vicon, an English company specializing in [[motion capture]]. The acquisition aimed to integrate Vicon's camera systems with IMeasureU's sensors.<ref>[http://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11894925 Kiwi tech helps sports superstars recover] NZ Herald 2</ref>

On 23 June 2015, IMeasureU launched an [[Indiegogo]] campaign seeking to raise US $200,000 to develop a consumer product.<ref>[https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/imu-run-reducing-your-risk-of-running-injury IMU-Run: Reducing Your Risk of Running Injury] indiegogo</ref><ref>[http://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11414897 Your Business: Wearable tech - Mark Finch, I Measure U] NZ Herald</ref>
