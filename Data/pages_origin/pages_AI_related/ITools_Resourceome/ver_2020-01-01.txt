'''iTools'''<ref>{{cite journal|vauthors=Dinov ID, Rubin D, Lorensen W, Dugan J, Ma J, Murphy S, Kirschner B, Bug W, Sherman M, Floratos A, Kennedy D, Jagadish HV, Schmidt J, Athey B, Califano A, Musen M, Altman R, Kikinis R, Kohane I, Delp S, Parker DS, Toga AW | title=iTools: A Framework for Classification, Categorization and Integration of Computational Biology Resources | journal=PLoS ONE |volume=3|issue=5|pages= e2265| doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0002265 | year=2008 | pmid=18509477 | pmc=2386255}}</ref> is a distributed infrastructure for managing, discovery, comparison and integration of [[computational biology]] resources. iTools employs [[Biositemap]] technology to retrieve and service meta-data about diverse bioinformatics data services, tools, and web-services. iTools is developed by the [[National Centers for Biomedical Computing]] as part of the [https://web.archive.org/web/20100611171315/http://nihroadmap.nih.gov/ NIH Road Map Initiative].
