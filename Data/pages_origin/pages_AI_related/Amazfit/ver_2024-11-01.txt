'''Amazfit''' is a Chinese smart wearable brand,<ref>{{cite web | title=Amazfit launches three new smartwatches | publisher=Khaleej Times | date=October 26, 2021 | url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/business-technology-review/amazfit-launches-three-new-smartwatches }}</ref> established in September 2015<ref>{{cite web | last=Sharma | first=Karan | title=Amazfit focused on making smart products that empower people says Jacob Jin, Director of South Asia | publisher=Pinkvilla | date=December 3, 2021 | url=https://www.pinkvilla.com/tech/news/amazfit-focused-on-making-smart-products-that-empower-people-says-jacob-jin-director-of-south-asia-957288 | access-date=May 2, 2022 | archive-date=November 30, 2022 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221130024709/https://www.pinkvilla.com/tech/news/amazfit-focused-on-making-smart-products-that-empower-people-says-jacob-jin-director-of-south-asia-957288 | url-status=dead }}</ref> and headquartered in [[Hefei]], [[Anhui Province]]. Its products are manufactured<ref>{{cite web |url=https://qz.com/india/1382203/as-iphone-struggles-in-india-apple-watch-beats-fitbit-and-samsung/|title=Apple's early-generation smartwatch rules a market where the iPhone is struggling|first=Kuwar|last=Singh|date=September 10, 2018|accessdate=|work=[[Quartz (publication)|Quartz]]}}</ref> and owned by Zepp Health.<ref>{{cite web | author= | title=Amazfit ranks third in global smartwatch shipments in Q3 | publisher=[[The Peninsula (newspaper)|The Peninsula]]| date=December 1, 2021 | url=https://thepeninsulaqatar.com/article/01/12/2021/amazfit-ranks-third-in-global-smartwatch-shipments-in-q3 }}</ref> The brand offers wearable devices including [[smartwatches]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://metro.co.uk/2020/12/11/the-lust-list-new-tech-we-want-this-week-11-13731970/|title=The Lust List: new tech we want this week|first=Lucy |last=Hedges|date=11 Dec 2020|accessdate=|work=[[Metro (British newspaper)]]}}</ref> [[fitness band]]s,<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.zdnet.com/article/noise-canceling-earbuds-that-help-you-sleep-through-any-snore/|title=Noise-canceling headphones that help you sleep through any snore|first=Ross|last=Rubin|date=July 28, 2020|work=[[ZDNet]]}}</ref>[[smart ring]]<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.tomsguide.com/wellness/fitness-trackers/i-wore-the-amazfit-helio-ring-every-day-for-a-week-heres-what-happened | title=Amazfit Helio Ring review: I may never track my sleep with a smartwatch again | date=6 August 2024 }}</ref> and [[equipment]] related to health and sports.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.pcquest.com/huamis-amazfit-debuts-mwc-opening-new-chapter-globalization/|title=Amazfit is first staged at MWC in Barcelona|author=|date=2019-03-01|accessdate=|work=[[PCQuest]]}}</ref>

==History==
In August 2015, Huami Corporation(later renamed Zepp Health Corporation) launched Amazfit, a new smart wearable device brand. In August 2016, the first smartwatch of Amazfit was introduced.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.firstpost.com/tech/news-analysis/xiaomi-unveils-amazfit-smartwatch-3687679.html|title=Xiaomi unveils Amazfit smartwatch|author=|date=August 30, 2016|work=[[Firstpost]]}}</ref> In April 2017, it launched the Amazfit Health Band.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bgr.in/news/xiaomi-weloop-hey-s3-huami-amazfit-health-band-fitness-trackers-launched-heres-everything-you-need-to-know-457788/|title=WeLoop Hey S3, an Apple Watch-clone, launched on Xiaomi's MIJIA platform, priced at $78|author=|date=April 28, 2017|accessdate=|work=[[Boy Genius Report|BGR]]}}</ref>

In March 2019, a self-developed [[chipset]] called the "Huangshan-1" <ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.eetimes.com/wearables-firm-invests-8-million-in-risc-v-startup/|title=Wearables Firm Invests $8 Million in RISC-V Startup|first=Nitin|last=Dahad|date=March 1, 2019|work=[[EE Times]]}}</ref> was exhibited at [[Mobile World Congress]] and applied to Amazfit devices in 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.firstpost.com/tech/news-analysis/xiaomis-huami-launches-the-amazfit-verge-health-band-1s-and-a-new-soc-in-china-5212251.html|title=Xiaomi's Huami launches the Amazfit Verge, Health Band 1S and a new SoC in China|author=|date=September 19, 2018|accessdate=|work=[[Firstpost]]}}</ref>

In April 2020, its [[parent company]]-Huami Corporation partnered with a [[laboratory]] led by [[Zhong Nanshan]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.scmp.com/abacus/tech/article/3079351/china-wants-use-fitness-trackers-monitor-coronavirus-survivors|title=China wants to use fitness trackers to monitor coronavirus survivors | last=Chiu | first=Karen|date=10 Apr 2020|accessdate=|work=[[South China Morning Post]]}}</ref> to use wearable devices to track [[respiratory diseases]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://techcrunch.com/2020/05/17/fitbits-rival-amazfit-transparent-reusable-mask/|title=Fitbit's Chinese rival Amazfit mulls a transparent, self-disinfecting mask|author=John Artman|date=May 18, 2020|accessdate=|work=[[TechCrunch]]}}</ref>

By the end of 2021, the brand had a market share of 5.1% in smartwatches, ranking fifth in the market.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.counterpointresearch.com/insights/global-smartwatch-market-2021/|title=Smartwatch Market Grows 24% YoY in 2021, Records Highest Ever Quarterly Shipments in Q4 | date=Mar 14, 2022 | work=Counterpoint Technology Market Research}}</ref>
