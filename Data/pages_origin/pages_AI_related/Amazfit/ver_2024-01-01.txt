'''Amazfit''' is a Chinese smart wearable brand<ref>{{cite web | title=Amazfit launches three new smartwatches | publisher=Khaleej Times | date=October 26, 2021 | url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/business-technology-review/amazfit-launches-three-new-smartwatches }}</ref> established in September 2015<ref>{{cite web | last=Sharma | first=Karan | title=Amazfit focused on making smart products that empower people says Jacob Jin, Director of South Asia | publisher=Pinkvilla | date=December 3, 2021 | url=https://www.pinkvilla.com/tech/news/amazfit-focused-on-making-smart-products-that-empower-people-says-jacob-jin-director-of-south-asia-957288 | access-date=May 2, 2022 | archive-date=November 30, 2022 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221130024709/https://www.pinkvilla.com/tech/news/amazfit-focused-on-making-smart-products-that-empower-people-says-jacob-jin-director-of-south-asia-957288 | url-status=dead }}</ref> and headquartered in [[Hefei]]. Its products are manufactured<ref>{{cite web |url=https://qz.com/india/1382203/as-iphone-struggles-in-india-apple-watch-beats-fitbit-and-samsung/|title=Apple's early-generation smartwatch rules a market where the iPhone is struggling|first=Kuwar|last=Singh|date=September 10, 2018|accessdate=|work=[[Quartz]]}}</ref> and owned by Zepp Health.<ref>{{cite web | author= | title=Amazfit ranks third in global smartwatch shipments in Q3 | publisher=[[The Peninsula (newspaper)|The Peninsula]]| date=December 1, 2021 | url=https://thepeninsulaqatar.com/article/01/12/2021/amazfit-ranks-third-in-global-smartwatch-shipments-in-q3 }}</ref> The brand offers wearable devices including [[smartwatches]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://metro.co.uk/2020/12/11/the-lust-list-new-tech-we-want-this-week-11-13731970/|title=The Lust List: new tech we want this week|first=Lucy |last=Hedges|date=11 Dec 2020|accessdate=|work=[[Metro (British newspaper)]]}}</ref> [[fitness band]]s,<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.zdnet.com/article/noise-canceling-earbuds-that-help-you-sleep-through-any-snore/|title=Noise-canceling headphones that help you sleep through any snore|first=Ross|last=Rubin|date=July 28, 2020|work=[[ZDNet]]}}</ref> and [[equipment]] related to health and sports.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.pcquest.com/huamis-amazfit-debuts-mwc-opening-new-chapter-globalization/|title=Amazfit is first staged at MWC in Barcelona|author=|date=2019-03-01|accessdate=|work=[[PCQuest]]}}</ref>

In January 2020, it attended the [[Consumer Electronics Show]] 2020 in [[Las Vegas]].<ref>{{cite web | last=Chan | first=Tim | title=From 8K TVs to Stylish Smartglasses: The 11 Best Things We Saw at CES 2020 | publisher=Robb Report | date=January 13, 2020 | url=https://robbreport.com/lifestyle/news/the-best-11-things-at-this-years-ces-2892325/ }}</ref> At the middle of October 2021, Amazfit updated its brand identity with the slogan "Up Your Game".<ref>{{cite web|title=Amazfit Refreshes Image, Launches GTR 3 And GTS 3 Smartwatch Lines|url=https://www.twice.com/product/accessories/digitalhealth/amazfit-refreshes-image-launches-gtr-3-and-gts-3-smartwatch-lines|language=|first=Thomas |last=Waller|date=November 8, 2021|access-date=|work=[[Twice (magazine)|Twice]]}}</ref>

==History==
In 2016, the first smartwatch of Amazfit was introduced.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.firstpost.com/tech/news-analysis/xiaomi-unveils-amazfit-smartwatch-3687679.html|title=Xiaomi unveils Amazfit smartwatch|author=|date=August 30, 2016|work=[[Firstpost]]}}</ref> In April 2017, it launched the Amazfit Health Band.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bgr.in/news/xiaomi-weloop-hey-s3-huami-amazfit-health-band-fitness-trackers-launched-heres-everything-you-need-to-know-457788/|title=WeLoop Hey S3, an Apple Watch-clone, launched on Xiaomi's MIJIA platform, priced at $78|author=|date=April 28, 2017|accessdate=|work=[[Boy Genius Report|BGR]]}}</ref>

A self-developed [[chipset]] called the "Huangshan-1" <ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.eetimes.com/wearables-firm-invests-8-million-in-risc-v-startup/|title=Wearables Firm Invests $8 Million in RISC-V Startup|first=Nitin|last=Dahad|date=March 1, 2019|work=[[EE Times]]}}</ref> was exhibited at [[Mobile World Congress]] and applied to Amazfit devices in 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.firstpost.com/tech/news-analysis/xiaomis-huami-launches-the-amazfit-verge-health-band-1s-and-a-new-soc-in-china-5212251.html|title=Xiaomi's Huami launches the Amazfit Verge, Health Band 1S and a new SoC in China|author=|date=September 19, 2018|accessdate=|work=[[Firstpost]]}}</ref>

In July 2019, the lens of the photoelectric module of an Amazfit smartwatch dislodged, causing a [[End user|user]]'s [[skin]] to be burned.<ref>{{cite web | title=An Amazfit smartwatch's photoelectric module lens falls off and burns the user's skin | website=Myzaker.com | date=July 3, 2019 | url=http://app.myzaker.com/news/article.php?pk=5d1c7f668e9f0947574c0449|editor=Wang Ziyan}}</ref>

After the launch of Amazfit GTR series smartwatches, some problems were revealed, such as the screen was easy to be scratched and the positioning was severely shifted, which affected the usage.<ref>{{cite web | title=The "boom" and bubble of Huami's second quarter earnings report | work=[[iResearch Consulting Group|iResearch]] | date=August 30, 2021 | url=https://column.iresearch.cn/b/202108/919206.shtml | first=Yu |last=Jian}}</ref>

In April 2020, its [[parent company]] partnered with a [[laboratory]] led by [[Zhong Nanshan]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.scmp.com/abacus/tech/article/3079351/china-wants-use-fitness-trackers-monitor-coronavirus-survivors|title=China wants to use fitness trackers to monitor coronavirus survivors | last=Chiu | first=Karen|date=10 Apr 2020|accessdate=|work=[[South China Morning Post]]}}</ref> to use wearable devices to track [[respiratory diseases]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://techcrunch.com/2020/05/17/fitbits-rival-amazfit-transparent-reusable-mask/|title=Fitbit's Chinese rival Amazfit mulls a transparent, self-disinfecting mask|author=John Artman|date=May 18, 2020|accessdate=|work=[[TechCrunch]]}}</ref>
