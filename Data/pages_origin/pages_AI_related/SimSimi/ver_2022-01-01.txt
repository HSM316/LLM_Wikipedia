'''SimSimi''' is an [[artificial intelligence]] conversation program created in 2002 by ISMaker.<ref name=IBT>{{cite news|title=What is Simsimi? All About the iPhone and Android App That Has the World Laughing Out Loud|url=http://www.ibtimes.com/articles/285978/20120123/what-simsimi-iphone-android-app.htm|accessdate=25 February 2012|newspaper=International Business Times|date=23 January 2012}}</ref> The application has led to controversy and protests in [[Thailand]] for some of its responses containing [[profanity]] and criticisms of leading politicians.<ref>{{cite news|last=Sambandaraska|first=Don|title=SimSimi chatbot banned in Thailand|url=http://www.telecomasia.net/blog/content/simsimi-chatbot-banned-thailand|accessdate=25 February 2012|newspaper=TelecomAsia|date=7 February 2012}}</ref> It grows its artificial intelligence day by day assisted by a feature that allows users to teach it to respond correctly. SimSimi, pronounced as "shim-shimi", is from a Korean word ''simsim'' (심심) which means "bored".{{citation needed|date=March 2012}} It has an [[application software|application]] designed for [[Android (operating system)|Android]], for [[Windows Phone]] (developed by Kevin Tan) and for [[iOS]].

SimSimi reached some popularity around 2015 when it was featured in a YouTube video by YouTuber [[Comedy Shorts Gamer]],<ref>{{cite web|title=BY FAR THE MOST RACIST THING EVER!!|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_798RwjdDvY}}</ref> the video reached 4 million views by 2017.
