The '''Apple Watch Series 4''' is a smartwatch by [[Apple Inc.]]. It was released during the 2018 Apple Special Event held at the [[Steve Jobs Theater]] in California. It has received mostly positive reviews from critics.

==Specifications==
[[File:Apple S4 module.png|thumb|The [[Apple S4]] processor|100px]]
The Apple Watch Series 4 features the [[Apple S4]] dual-core processor, which Apple claims can deliver double the [[Apple S3|S3]]'s performance. It also features 16GB of storage, a larger display compared to the Series 3, and a new electrical heart sensor that measures the potential difference between the wrist and a finger from the opposing hand into the S4 Module over 30 seconds. The [[electrocardiography|ECG]] system has received clearance from the United States [[Food and Drug Administration]], a first ever for a consumer device, and is supported by the [[American Heart Association]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://9to5mac.com/2018/09/12/apple-watch-ecg/|title=Apple Watch now offers ECG, the first over-the-counter consumer device to do so|date=September 12, 2018|work=[[9to5Mac]]|access-date=November 18, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wired.com/story/apple-watch-series-4/|title=Apple Watch's Update Adds Heart-Monitoring Capabilities|last=Goode|first=Lauren|date=September 12, 2018|work=[[Wired (magazine)|Wired]]|access-date=December 12, 2018|issn=1059-1028}}</ref> This device can also detect falls and will automatically contact emergency services unless the user cancels the outgoing call. The microphone was moved to the opposite side between the side button and the digital crown to improve call quality. Other changes include the digital crown incorporating haptic feedback with the Apple Haptic Engine and includes the new Apple-designed [[Apple W series|W3]] wireless chip.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2018/09/redesigned-apple-watch-series-4-revolutionizes-communication-fitness-and-health/|title=Apple Watch Series 4: Beautifully redesigned with breakthrough communication, fitness and health capabilities.|work=[[Apple Inc.]]|access-date=September 12, 2018}}</ref>

==Reception==
===Critical Reception===
The watch received mostly positive reviews from critics. TechRadar gave it a score of 4.5/5, calling it one of the top smartwatches, while criticizing the short battery life.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.techradar.com/reviews/apple-watch-4-review|title=Apple Watch 4 review|last=Beavis 2019-09-18T13:18:06Z|first=Gareth|website=TechRadar|language=en|access-date=2020-01-24}}</ref> Digital Trends gave it a score of 5/5, calling it Apple's best product and praising the design, build quality, and software, among others, while criticizing the battery life.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Apple Watch Series 4 review|url=https://www.digitaltrends.com/smartwatch-reviews/apple-watch-series-4-review/|access-date=2020-01-24|website=www.digitaltrends.com}}</ref> CNET gave it a score of 8.2/10, calling it the "best overall smartwatch around", while criticizing the battery life and lack of watch face options.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnet.com/reviews/apple-watch-series-4-review/|title=Apple Watch Series 4 review: ECG, and a lot of refinements|last=Stein|first=Scott|website=CNET|language=en|access-date=2020-01-24}}</ref> T3 gave it a score of 5/5, calling it a "truly next-gen smartwatch" due to its thinner body and bigger screen compared to the Series 3, and health features.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.t3.com/us/reviews/apple-watch-series-4-review|title=Apple Watch Series 4 review|last=Bolton 2019-11-15T15:20:08Z|first=Matthew|website=T3|language=en|access-date=2020-01-24}}</ref>
===Commercial Reception===
The Apple Watch Series 4 sold 11.5 Million units in 2018. It was the best selling smartwatch in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://9to5mac.com/2019/06/25/apple-watch-shipments-2/|title=Apple Watch shipments grew 22% in 2018, thanks to Series 4|last=Lovejoy|first=Ben|date=2019-06-25|website=9to5Mac|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-15}}</ref>
