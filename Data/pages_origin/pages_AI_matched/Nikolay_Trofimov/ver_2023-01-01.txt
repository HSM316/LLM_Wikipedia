}}'''Nikolay Nikolaevich Trofimov''' ({{lang-ru|Никола́й Никола́евич Трофи́мов}}; January 21, 1920, [[Sevastopol]] —  November 7, 2005, [[St. Petersburg]]) was a Soviet and Russian theater and film actor. [[People's Artist of the USSR]] (1990).

== Biography ==
Nikolay Trofimov was born into a working-class family. Stage career began in 1934.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://capella-spb.ru/ru/article/show/content/id/1755 |title=Государственная академическая капелла Санкт-Петербурга<!-- Заголовок добавлен ботом --> |access-date=2017-03-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160403043111/http://capella-spb.ru/ru/article/show/content/id/1755 |archive-date=2016-04-03 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

During the [[Great Patriotic War]] he served in the [[navy]].<ref>[http://www.zwezda.ru/trofimov.html Интервью на сайте «Звёздный Журнал»]</ref>

On the stage [[Tovstonogov Bolshoi Drama Theater]] played more than 40 roles. In the cinema, Nikolai Trofimov played mostly small roles.

Nikolay Nikolaevich Trofimov died on the night of November 7, 2005 in the St. Petersburg Alexandrovsky hospital from the consequences of a stroke. He was buried on November 14 at the Literary Sheds of [[Volkovskoye Cemetery]].<ref>[http://litmostki.ru/trofimov/  Могила Н. Н. Трофимова на Волковском кладбище СПб]</ref>
