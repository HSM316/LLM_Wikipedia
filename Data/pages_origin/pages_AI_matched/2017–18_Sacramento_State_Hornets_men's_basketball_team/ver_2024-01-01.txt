The '''2017–18 Sacramento State Hornets men's basketball team''' represented [[California State University, Sacramento]] during the [[2017–18 NCAA Division I men's basketball season]]. The Hornets, led by 10th-year head coach [[Brian Katz]], played their home games at the [[Hornets Nest (Sacramento State)|Hornets Nest]] in [[Sacramento, California]], as members of the [[Big Sky Conference]]. They finished the season 7–25, 4–14 in Big Sky play to finish in 11th place. They lost in the first of the [[2018 Big Sky Conference men's basketball tournament|Big Sky tournament]] to [[2017–18 Portland State Vikings men's basketball team|Portland State]]

==Previous season==
The [[2016–17 Sacramento State Hornets men's basketball team|Hornets]] finished the [[2016–17 NCAA Division I men's basketball season|2016–17 season]] 13–18, 9–9 in Big Sky play to finish in seventh place. As the No. 7 seed in the [[2017 Big Sky Conference men's basketball tournament|Big Sky tournament]], they defeated [[2016–17 Idaho State Bengals men's basketball team|Idaho State]] in the first round, before losing to [[2016–17 Eastern Washington Eagles men's basketball team|Eastern Washington]] in the quarterfinals.
