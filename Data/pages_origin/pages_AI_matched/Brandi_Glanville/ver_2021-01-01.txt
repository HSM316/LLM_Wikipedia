'''Brandi Lynn Glanville''' (born November 16, 1972) is an American television personality, author, fashion designer, and former model. She is best known for appearing on the reality television series ''[[The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills]]'' (2011‚Äì2016, 2019‚Äìpresent). She also hosts a podcast called ''Brandi Glanville Unfiltered'' and has appeared on television shows ''[[Celebrity Apprentice]]'', ''[[Celebrity Big Brother 1 (U.S.)|Celebrity Big Brother]]'', ''[[Famously Single]]'', and ''[[My Kitchen Rules (U.S. TV series)|My Kitchen Rules]]''. Glanville has released two books, ''Drinking and Tweeting: And Other Brandi Blunders'' (2013) and ''Drinking and Dating'' (2014), which became [[The New York Times Best Seller list|''New York Times'' Best Sellers]].<ref>Harnick, Chris.
