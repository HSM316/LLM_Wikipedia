'''Lavrio Indoor Hall''', or Kleisto Gymnastirio Lavriou ([[Greek language|Greek]]: Κλειστό Γυμναστήριο Λαυρίου), is an [[indoor arena|indoor sporting arena]] that is located in the city of [[Lavrio]], [[Attica Region|Attica]], [[Greece]]. The [[seating capacity]] of the arena for [[basketball]] games is 1,700 people. The arena is owned by the municipality of [[Lavreotiki]].

==History==
The arena has been used as the home arena of the Greek [[professional sports|professional]] basketball team [[Lavrio BC|Lavrio]], of the [[Greek Basket League]].<ref name="esake">{{cite web|url=http://www.esake.gr/el/action/EsaketeamView?idteam=F7D46144&mode=4|title=Ομάδα - Ε.Σ.Α.Κ.Ε.|website=esake.gr|accessdate=2018-09-19}}</ref>
