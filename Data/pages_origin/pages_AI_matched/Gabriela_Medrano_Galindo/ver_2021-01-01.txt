'''Gabriela Medrano Galindo''' (born 27 June 1983) is a [[Mexican people|Mexican]] politician affiliated with the [[Ecologist Green Party of Mexico|PVEM]]. As of 2013 she served as Deputy of the [[LXII Legislature of the Mexican Congress]] representing [[Quintana Roo]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Perfil del legislador|url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=9216882|publisher=Legislative Information System|accessdate=2 December 2013}}</ref>
