'''Heather Oesterle''' is the current head coach of [[Central Michigan Chippewas women's basketball|Central Michigan]] women's basketball team.<ref>{{cite web |title=HEATHER OESTERLE |url=https://cmuchippewas.com/coaches.aspx?rc=43 |website=CMUChippewas.com |publisher=Central Michigan University |accessdate=31 July 2019}}</ref> Oesterle was named CMU's head women's basketball coach in July, 2019, succeeding her long-time mentor [[Sue Guevara]]. Oesterle served for nine seasons on Guevara's staff at CMU, helping lead the program to unprecedented heights including three Mid-American Conference championships, two MAC Tournament titles, and five MAC West Division crowns. She earned her bachelor's degree in kinesiology from Michigan in 2002 and her master's degree in sports studies from Miami in 2008.

==Coaching career==
===Central Michigan===
On July 12, 2019, Oesterle was announced as the new head coach of the Central Michigan women's basketball program.<ref>{{cite web |title=Heather Oesterle Named Women's Basketball Head Coach |url=https://cmuchippewas.com/news/2019/7/12/heather-oesterle-named-womens-basketball-head-coach.aspx?path=wbball |website=CMUChippewas.com |publisher=Central Michigan University |accessdate=31 July 2019}}</ref>
