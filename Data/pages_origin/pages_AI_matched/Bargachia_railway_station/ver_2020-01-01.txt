'''Bargachia railway station''' is a railway station on [[Santragachi–Amta branch line]] of [[South Eastern Railway zone|South Eastern Railway]] section of the [[Kharagpur railway division]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.totaltraininfo.com/station/BAC/|title=BAC / Bargachia Railway Station {{!}} Train Arrival / Departure Timings at Bargachia|website=www.totaltraininfo.com|language=en|access-date=2019-10-25}}</ref> It is situated beside Bargachia-Kamlapur Road, Sandhya Bazar at [[Bargachia]] in [[Howrah District]] in the [[India|Indian]] state of [[West Bengal]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiarailinfo.com/station/map/bargachia-bac/7691|title=Bargachia Railway Station Map/Atlas SER/South Eastern Zone - Railway Enquiry|last=Pradhan|first=Avradip|website=indiarailinfo.com|access-date=2019-10-25}}</ref>

== History ==
[[Howrah Junction railway station|Howrah]] to Amta Narrow gauge track was built in 1897 in [[British India]]. This route was the part of the [[Martin's Light Railways]] which was closed in 1971.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/politics-and-nation/pm-narendra-modi-highlights-project-delay-by-talking-of-74km-howrah-champadanga-railway-line/articleshow/60077314.cms|title=PM Narendra Modi highlights project delay by talking of 74km Howrah-Champadanga railway line|last=Sharma|first=Aman|date=2017-08-15|work=The Economic Times|access-date=2019-10-25}}</ref> Howrah–Amta new Broad Gauge line, including the Bargachia-Champadanga branch line was re constructed and opened in 2002-2004.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ieor.iitb.ac.in/node/1918|title=Narrow gauge in Bengal {{!}} IEOR @ IIT Bombay|website=www.ieor.iitb.ac.in|access-date=2019-10-25}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thehindubusinessline.com/todays-paper/tp-others/article29068502.ece|title=Howrah-Amta BG line section inaugurated|website=@businessline|language=en|access-date=2019-10-25}}</ref>
