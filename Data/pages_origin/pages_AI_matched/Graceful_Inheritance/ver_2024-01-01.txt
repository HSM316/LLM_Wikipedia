'''''Graceful Inheritance''''' is the debut album by an American [[heavy metal music|metal]] band [[Heir Apparent (band)|Heir Apparent]], released in 1986 by [[France|French]] label Black Dragon Records. It was originally a vinyl-only release. Black Dragon re-released "Graceful Inheritance" on CD in October 1986, making it the first CD released by an independent label in Europe.{{citation needed|date=October 2014}}
