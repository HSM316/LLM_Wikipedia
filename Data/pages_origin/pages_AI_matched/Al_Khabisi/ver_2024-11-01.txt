'''Al Khabaisi''' ({{langx|ar|الخبيصي}}) is a locality in [[Dubai]], [[United Arab Emirates]] (UAE).  Al Khabaisi is located in eastern Dubai in the region of [[Deira, Dubai|Deira]].  The community borders [[Hor Al Anz]] to the north, [[Dubai International Airport]] to the south, [[Al Muraqqabat]] to the west and [[Al Twar]] to the east.

It is bounded in the north by route [[D 80 road|D 80]] (Salahuddin Road) and to the south by route [[E 11 road (United Arab Emirates)|E 11]] (Al Ittihad Road).  Al Khabaisi is a largely a residential community.  However, several corporations and automobile dealerships are located in Al Khabaisi, along Al Ittihad Road.  The area is also close to the [[Hor Al Anz]] bus stand and [[New Medical Center]].
