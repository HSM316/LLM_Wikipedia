The '''1998 Baylor Bears football team''' (variously "Baylor", "BU", or the "Bears") represented [[Baylor University]] in the [[1998 NCAA Division I-A football season]]. They were represented in the [[Big 12 Conference]] in the South Division. They played their home games at [[Floyd Casey Stadium]] in [[Waco, Texas]]. They were coached by head coach [[Dave Roberts (American football)|Dave Roberts]].
