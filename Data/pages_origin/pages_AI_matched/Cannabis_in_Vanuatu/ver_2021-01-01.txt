{{short description|Use of cannabis in Vanuatu}}
'''[[Cannabis]] in [[Vanuatu]]''' is illegal for recreational purposes but is legal for medical and industrial purposes.

==Enforcement==
A 2017 news report noted recent increases in cannabis arrests as police attempted to clarify and enforce cannabis law on remote islands where "locals did not adapt."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vanuatuindependent.com/2017/03/20/cannabis-sale-and-cultivation-becoming-serious-issue/|title=Cannabis sale and cultivation becoming serious issue - Vanuatu Independent|first=Richard M.|last=Nanua|date=20 March 2017|access-date=10 October 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181011014041/https://vanuatuindependent.com/2017/03/20/cannabis-sale-and-cultivation-becoming-serious-issue/|archive-date=11 October 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref> A 2018 report recounted an incident on the [[Epi Island]] to Port Vila ferry where some 60 young people from Malekula were being transported under police escort for cannabis cultivation, distribution, and consumption.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vanuatuindependent.com/2017/01/23/cannabis-plant-legalized-says-concerned-parent/|title=Cannabis plant should be legalized, says concerned parent - Vanuatu Independent|first=Flo|last=Vanua|date=23 January 2017|access-date=10 October 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181011013941/https://vanuatuindependent.com/2017/01/23/cannabis-plant-legalized-says-concerned-parent/|archive-date=11 October 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Medical reform==
A September 2018 news report noted that Vanuatu's national health care system was considering clinical trials of a cannabis-based drug to treat diabetes.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vanuatuindependent.com/2018/09/22/research-seeks-cannabis-treatment-diabetes/|title=Research seeks cannabis treatment for diabetes - Vanuatu Independent|first=Newsroom|last=upload|date=22 September 2018|access-date=10 October 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181011014042/https://vanuatuindependent.com/2018/09/22/research-seeks-cannabis-treatment-diabetes/|archive-date=11 October 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref> On September 20, 2018 the government's Council of Ministers issued Decision 157/2018 allowing for the establishment of industries for the production of medical cannabis and industrial hemp.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vanuatuindependent.com/2018/10/24/vanuatu-legalizes-medical-cannabis//|title=Vanuatu legalizes medical cannabis - Vanuatu Independent|last=Independent Staff|date=24 October 2018|access-date=12 November 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181113025901/https://vanuatuindependent.com/2018/10/24/vanuatu-legalizes-medical-cannabis//|archive-date=13 November 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref>
