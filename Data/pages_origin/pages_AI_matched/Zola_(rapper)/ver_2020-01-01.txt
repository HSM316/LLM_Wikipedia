'''Aurélien N'Zuzi Zola''' (born November 16, 1999 in [[Évry, Essonne|Évry]], [[Essonne]]), better known by the mononym '''Zola''', is a [[France|French]] rapper of [[Democratic Republic of Congo|Congolese]] descent.<ref>[https://intrld.com/zola-on-en-parle/ Intrld.com: Il est temps que l’on parle de Zola] {{fr icon}}</ref><ref>[https://www.trunksmagazine.com/zola-futur-prometteur-jeune-rappeur/ Trunks Magazine: Zola, un futur prometteur pour ce jeune rappeur?]</ref> ''Cicatrices'', his debut album was released in 2019.<ref>[https://www.booska-p.com/album-cicatrices-a1567.html Booska: CICATRICES de Zola - Le premier album de Zola devrait sortir avant la fin de l'annee 2018.] {{fr icon}}</ref> His song "California Girls" was featured in the soundtrack of the film ''[[Taxi 5]]''.<ref>[https://wave.fr/en/zola-drops-a-new-freestyle-named-manger-221988 Wave.fr: Zola drops a new freestyle named “Manger”]</ref>.
