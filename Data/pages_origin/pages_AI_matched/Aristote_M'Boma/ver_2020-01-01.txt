'''Aristote M'Boma''' (born 30 June 1994 in [[Kinshasa]]) is a [[Democratic Republic of the Congo|Congolese]] professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[IFK Mariehamn]].

==Career==
On 12 November 2018, M'Boma joined fellow [[Veikkausliiga]] side [[IFK Mariehamn]] from [[Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho|SJK]] on a two-year contract.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ifkfotboll.ax/nyheter/ny-anfallare-klar-ifk|title=Ny anfallare klar för IFK|publisher=[[IFK Mariehamn]]|date=12 November 2018|accessdate=23 November 2018|language=Swedish}}</ref>
