'''Aristote M'Boma''' (born 30 June 1994) is a [[Democratic Republic of the Congo|Congolese]] professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]]. He also holds Finnish citizenship.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.veikkausliiga.com/pelaajat/80759/mboma-aristote |title=#14 Mboma, Aristote |publisher=[[Veikkausliiga]] |language=fi |access-date=8 June 2021}}</ref>

==Career==
On 12 November 2018, M'Boma joined fellow [[Veikkausliiga]] side [[IFK Mariehamn]] from [[Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho|SJK]] on a two-year contract.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ifkfotboll.ax/nyheter/ny-anfallare-klar-ifk|title=Ny anfallare klar för IFK|publisher=[[IFK Mariehamn]]|date=12 November 2018|accessdate=23 November 2018|language=Swedish}}</ref> Following rape accusations against him, M'Boma was released by Mariehamn in May 2020.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://yle.fi/urheilu/3-11347344 | title=IFK Mariehamn antoi potkut raiskauksista epäillylle liigapelaajalle | date=12 May 2020 | language=fi | publisher=YLE | location=Finland | accessdate=7 March 2021 }}</ref>

==Personal life==
In July 2020, M'Boma was given a four-year prison sentence for two counts of aggravated rape.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/veikkausliiga/a/97202377-b0e3-4fef-b0ad-ece393123543 |title=Entiselle Veikkausliiga-pelaajalle neljän vuoden vankeustuomio muun muassa törkeistä raiskauksista |website=[[Iltalehti]] |language=fi |access-date=}}</ref>
