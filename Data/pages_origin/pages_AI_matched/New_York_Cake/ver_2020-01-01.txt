'''New York Cake''' is a 1981 [[Italo disco]]  album by [[Italy|Italian]] band [[Kano (band)|Kano]], recorded for [[Full Time Records]] and released in the [[United States]] under [[Mirage Records|Mirage]] label.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.discogs.com/Kano-New-York-Cake/master/35895|title=Kano at Discogs|work=discogs|accessdate=2011-08-23}}
</ref> It was produced by its members, namely Luciano Ninzatti, Matteo Bonsanto and {{ill|Stefano Pulga|it}}. The album features Italian Top 3 hit "Baby Not Tonight" and American #89 Hit "Can't Hold Back (Your Loving)".

The album itself entered the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Billboard 200|200]] and [[Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums|R&B Albums]] in 1982.<ref name="OMG"/>

The Album included 'chic-esque' funky basslines, especially by the Tracks A1, B3
and the 2009 Bonustrack.

In 2009 the British label 'Funky Town Grooves' released the album, in a limited edition, for the first time on CD (FTG-162) with the Bonustrack "Can't Hold Back (Your Loving)" (Extended 12"). The album was taken from a vinyl LP not the master-tapes. 

The song "Can't Hold Back (Your Loving)" was featured on video game [[Grand Theft Auto V]].
