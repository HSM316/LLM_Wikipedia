'''Molltown''' is a small [[Unincorporated area|unincorporated community]] in central [[Berks County, PA|Berks County]], [[Pennsylvania]], located in the [[Fleetwood Area School District]]. The small village is located near [[Lake Ontelaunee]], and is located on Maidencreek Road, a back road in the area.

A post office called Molltown was established in 1839, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1920.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?task=display&state=PA&county=Berks | title=Berks County | publisher=Jim Forte Postal History | access-date=31 May 2015}}</ref> The community was named for the Moll family of settlers.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=bzkyAAAAIBAJ&sjid=UaMFAAAAIBAJ&pg=2069%2C79143 | title=Township settled by Friends | work=Reading Eagle - | date=Apr 1, 2002 | access-date=31 May 2015 | pages=B8}}</ref>
