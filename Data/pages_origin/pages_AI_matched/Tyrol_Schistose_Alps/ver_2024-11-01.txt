The '''Tyrol Schistose Alps''' (''Tiroler Schieferalpen'' in [[German language|German]]) is the proposed name for a subdivision of [[mountain range]]s in a new, and as yet unadopted, classification of the [[Alps]], located in [[Austria]].

== Geography ==
Administratively the range belongs to the [[States of Austria|Austrian state]] of [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]] and, marginally, of [[Salzburg (state)|Salzburg]].
The whole range is drained by the [[tributary|tributaries]] of the [[Danube]] river.

=== Subdivision ===
The range is divided in two Alpine subsections:<ref name=soiu />
* [[Tux Alps]] ([[German language|DE]]:''Tuxer Alpen'') - [[SOIUSA code]]:II/B-23.I;
* [[Kitzbühel Alps]] ([[German language|DE]]:''Kitzbüheler Alpen'') - [[SOIUSA code]]:II/B-23.II.
