'''Gene Robillard''' (January 15, 1929 &ndash; April 24, 2007) was a [[Canadian football]] [[quarterback]] who played one season with the [[BC Lions]] of the [[Canadian Football League]]. He played [[Canadian Interuniversity Sport football|CIS football]] at [[McGill University]] and attended [[Ottawa Technical High School]] in [[Ottawa]], [[Ontario]]. Robillard was also a member of the [[Montreal Alouettes]]. He later coached the [[Ottawa Sooners]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.cflapedia.com/Players/r/robillard_gene.htm | title=Gene Robillard | publisher=cflapedia.com | accessdate=9 August 2014}}</ref>
