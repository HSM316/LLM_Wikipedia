'''''Network''''' is a [[Television in Canada|Canadian]] [[variety television|variety]] [[television series]] which aired on [[CTV television network|CTV]] for one season during the [[1962-63 Canadian network television schedule|1962-63 television season]]. The show was co-hosted by [[Bill Brady (journalist)|Bill Brady]] and [[Denyse Ange]]. [[Live television|Live]] and [[videotape|taped]] segments were aired from either the studio or elsewhere in Canada.
