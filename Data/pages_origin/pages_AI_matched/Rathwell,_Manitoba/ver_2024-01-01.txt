{{Location map|Canada Manitoba|lat=49.3849|long=-98.3247|width=200|caption=Location of Rathwell in [[Manitoba]]|label=Rathwell}}'''Rathwell''' is an unincorporated urban centre in the province of [[Manitoba]]. It is part of the agricultural area of south central Manitoba, [[Canada]] and is situated in the [[Municipality of Norfolk Treherne]].
