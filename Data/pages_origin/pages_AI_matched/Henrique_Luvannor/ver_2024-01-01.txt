'''Luvannor Henrique de Sousa Silva''' (born 19 May 1990), commonly known as '''Luvannor''', is a professional footballer who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[FC Sheriff Tiraspol|Sheriff Tiraspol]]. Born in Brazil, he played for the [[Moldova national football team|Moldova national team]].

After a few successful seasons with [[Sheriff Tiraspol]] in the [[Moldovan National Division]], Luvannor obtained [[Moldovan nationality law|Moldovan citizenship]], and in November 2013 he made his debut for [[Moldova national football team]] in the friendly match against [[Lithuania national football team|Lithuania]] on 18 November, in which he played a major role in the equalizing goal in the 72nd minute.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.noi.md/ru/news_id/31313 | script-title=ru:Молдова проведет сегодня последнюю игру года | language = ru| publisher = http://www.noi.md | date = 18 November 2013  | access-date = 23 November 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Luvannor makes debut with goal pass for national team of Moldova |url=http://www.allmoldova.com/en/moldova-news/1249057184.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131202231002/http://www.allmoldova.com/en/moldova-news/1249057184.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=2 December 2013 |website=Moldova News |access-date=26 June 2018}}</ref> His brother, [[Klysman De Sousa Henrique|Klysman]] also played for Sheriff Tiraspol.

==Club career==
Luvannor began his career with [[Paranoá Esporte Clube]] in 2009, playing in the third division of the [[Campeonato Brasiliense]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Esporte Candango |title=Renovo bate Paranoá mesmo com três a menos em campo |url=http://www.esportecandango.com.br/site/index.php/futebol-candango/2009/item/53740-renovo-bate-paranoa-mesmo-com-tres-a-menos-em-campo |language=pt |date=11 October 2009 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140506205404/http://www.esportecandango.com.br/site/index.php/futebol-candango/2009/item/53740-renovo-bate-paranoa-mesmo-com-tres-a-menos-em-campo |archive-date=6 May 2014 }}</ref>

Luvannor signed with Moldovan club Sheriff Tiraspol in 2011. Contract is until 2016. In first 2 seasons Luvannor played as a defender and score only 5 goals. The season 2013-2014 was extremely successful for Luvannor. In the first part of Moldavian Football League he scored 18 goals in 18 matches. Also, his team qualified for the Europa League Group Stage. At home, with [[Anzhi]], he scored from a cross. He put in a spectacular performance against  [[Tottenham Hotspur]] in Tiraspol the best player.

On 1 July 2021, Luvannor returned to [[FC Sheriff Tiraspol]].<ref>{{cite web |title=С возвращением, Луваннор Энрике |url=http://www.fc-sheriff.com/ru/vse/s-vozvrascheniem-luvannor-enrike/ |website=fc-sheriff.com/ |publisher=FC Sheriff Tiraspol |access-date=1 July 2021 |language=Russian |date=1 July 2021}}</ref>

On 28 August 2021, Luvannor joined [[Al-Taawoun FC|Al-Taawoun]].<ref>{{cite web |title=التعاون يوقع مع المولدوفي لوفانور هنريكي|url=https://www.alriyadh.com/1904201}}</ref> On 8 February 2022, Luvannor was released by Al-Taawoun.<ref>{{cite web |title=التعاون يضم زي لويس.. ويخالص لوفانور |url=https://arriyadiyah.com/758280}}</ref>

On 3 July 2023, Sheriff Tiraspol announced the return of Luvannor.<ref>{{cite web |title=С возвращением домой, Лува |url=http://www.fc-sheriff.com/ru/vse/s-vozvrascheniem-domoy-luva/ |website=fc-sheriff.com/ |publisher=FC Sheriff Tiraspol |access-date=4 July 2023 |language=Russian |date=3 July 2023}}</ref>

==International career==
In 2013, he acquired Moldovan citizenship and he expressed his desire to play for [[Moldova national football team]]. In 2012, he wedded with a Moldovan girl. In the third match for the [[Moldova national football team]] against Sweden he scored a  beautiful goal. He also scored in a friendly match against Andorra.
In the summer of 2014 FIFA announced that Luvannor will not be able to play for the national team of Moldova in qualifying for the European Championship 2016. The reason is the lack of a five-year period of residence in Moldova. However, Moldova Football Federation sent an official letter to FIFA with a request to make an exception to the rule and has not yet received a reply, football player can not play for the national team.
