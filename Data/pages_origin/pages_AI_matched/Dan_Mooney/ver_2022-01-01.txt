'''Daniel John Mooney''' (born 3 July 1999) is a Welsh professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Altrincham F.C.|Altrincham]].

==Club career==
Mooney is a product of the Fleetwood Town Academy. On 9 October 2018 Mooney made his debut for Fleetwood in the starting line up for a 2–0 defeat against [[Rochdale F.C.|Rochdale]] in the [[EFL Trophy]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.skysports.com/football/fleetwood-vs-rochdale/400947 |title=Fleetwood Town 2–0 Rochdale |publisher=Sky Sports |date=9 October 2018 |accessdate=10 December 2019}}</ref> In May 2019 Mooney signed a one year contract extension with Fleetwood.<ref>[https://www.blackpoolgazette.co.uk/sport/football/dan-mooney-extends-fleetwood-town-contract-1-9764553/amp Mooney extends Fleetwood contract]</ref>

On 4 January 2020, it was announced Mooney had joined [[National League North]] side [[Altrincham F.C.|Altrincham]] for an undisclosed fee.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.altrinchamfc.com/news/robins-swoop-for-fleetwood-winger |title=Robins swoop for Fleetwood winger |publisher=Altrincham FC |date=4 February 2020 |accessdate=5 February 2020}}</ref> He made his debut the same day, starting in a 5–2 win against [[A.F.C. Telford United|Telford]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.altrinchamfc.com/news/rampant-robins-turn-on-the-style |title=Rapant Robins turn on the style |publisher=Altrincham FC |date=4 February 2020 |accessdate=5 February 2020}}</ref>
==International career==
Mooney has played for Wales at [[Wales national under-19 football team|under-19]] and [[Wales national under-21 football team|under-21]] levels.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Dan Mooney |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/player_summary/dan-mooney/ |access-date=19 June 2020 |website=worldfootball.net |language=en}}</ref>
