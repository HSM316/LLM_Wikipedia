'''Dan Mooney''' (born 3 July 1999) is a Welsh professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder (association football)|midfielder]] for [[EFL League One|League One]] side [[Fleetwood Town F.C.|Fleetwood Town]].

==Playing career==
Mooney is a product of the Fleetwood Town Academy. On 9 October 2018 Mooney made his debut for Fleetwood in the starting line up for a 2–0 defeat against [[Rochdale F.C.|Rochdale]] in the [[EFL Trophy]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.skysports.com/football/fleetwood-vs-rochdale/400947 |title=Fleetwood Town 2–0 Rochdale |publisher=Sky Sports |date=9 October 2018 |accessdate=10 December 2019}}</ref> In May 2019 Mooney signed a one year contract extension with Fleetwood.<ref>[https://www.blackpoolgazette.co.uk/sport/football/dan-mooney-extends-fleetwood-town-contract-1-9764553/amp Mooney extends Fleetwood contract]</ref>
