'''Daniel John Mooney''' (born 3 July 1999) is a professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for {{English football updater|BostonUn}} club [[Boston United F.C.|Boston United]].

== Early life ==
Daniel was educated at [[Wellacre Academy]] in [[Flixton, Greater Manchester]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Daniel over the Mooney with pro contract |url=https://www.messengernewspapers.co.uk/sport/15252728.daniel-over-the-mooney-with-pro-contract/ |access-date=24 May 2024 |work=Messenger Newspapers |date=28 April 2017}}</ref>

== Club career ==
Mooney is a product of the Fleetwood Town Academy. At the beginning of the 2017–18 season he was sent out on loan to [[Northern Premier League]] Division One North side [[Bamber Bridge F.C.|Bamber Bridge]], where he was in excellent form as the side made a fine start to the season.<ref name="LEP">{{cite news |url=https://www.lep.co.uk/sport/football/reynolds-praise-for-youngster-mooney-673873|title=Reynolds praise for youngster Mooney |publisher=Lancashire Evening Post|date=14 December 2017 |access-date=31 May 2022}}</ref> He left the club in December 2017, subsequently signing a one-month youth loan for [[National League North]] side [[Chorley F.C.|Chorley]], having impressed manager, [[Matt Jansen]], when playing against the ''Magpies'' for Bamber Bridge in the Lancashire FA Challenge Trophy quarter-final.<ref name="LEP"/> In February 2018, he signed for Northern Premier League Premier Division side [[Ashton United F.C.|Ashton United]] on loan.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://nonleague.pitchero.com/headlines/two-new-forwards-ashton/|title=Two New Forwards For Ashton |publisher=Non League Pitchero|date=3 February 2018|access-date=1 June 2022}}</ref> On 9 October 2018 Mooney made his debut for Fleetwood in the starting line up for a 2–0 defeat against [[Rochdale F.C.|Rochdale]] in the [[EFL Trophy]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.skysports.com/football/fleetwood-vs-rochdale/400947 |title=Fleetwood Town 2–0 Rochdale |publisher=Sky Sports |date=9 October 2018 |access-date=10 December 2019}}</ref> In May 2019 Mooney signed a one-year contract extension with Fleetwood.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.blackpoolgazette.co.uk/sport/football/dan-mooney-extends-fleetwood-town-contract-1-9764553/amp|title=Mooney extends Fleetwood contract|website=Blackpoolgazette.co.uk|access-date=3 June 2022}}</ref>

On 4 January 2020, it was announced Mooney had joined [[National League North]] side [[Altrincham F.C.|Altrincham]] for an undisclosed fee.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.altrinchamfc.com/news/robins-swoop-for-fleetwood-winger |title=Robins swoop for Fleetwood winger |publisher=Altrincham FC |date=4 February 2020 |access-date=5 February 2020 |archive-date=4 February 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200204162606/https://www.altrinchamfc.com/news/robins-swoop-for-fleetwood-winger |url-status=dead }}</ref> He made his debut the same day, starting in a 5–2 win against [[A.F.C. Telford United|Telford]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.altrinchamfc.com/news/rampant-robins-turn-on-the-style |title=Rapant Robins turn on the style |publisher=Altrincham FC |date=4 February 2020 |access-date=5 February 2020}}</ref>

Mooney joined Southend United on 26 May 2022. "Dan has obvious qualities," said Blues Head Coach Kevin Maher.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.southendunited.co.uk/news/2022/may/blues-sign-dan-mooney/|title=BLUES SIGN DAN MOONEY|website=Southendunited.co.uk|access-date=3 June 2022}}</ref> He left the club in December 2023 after his contract was cancelled by mutual consent, and rejoined former club Altrincham.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Phillips |first1=Chris |title=Dan Mooney leaves Southend United after contract is cancelled |url=https://www.echo-news.co.uk/sport/23988979.dan-mooney-leaves-southend-united-contract-cancelled/ |website=Echo |access-date=14 December 2023 |date=14 December 2023}}</ref> He departed Altrincham at the end of the [[2023–24 National League|2023–24 season]].<ref> {{cite web|url=https://altrinchamfc.com/blogs/news/robins-announce-retained-list-as-squad-for-next-season-begins-to-take-shape|title=ROBINS ANNOUNCE RETAINED LIST AS SQUAD FOR NEXT SEASON BEGINS TO TAKE SHAPE|website=altrinchamfc.com|date=8 May 2024|accessdate=12 June 2024}}</ref>

In June 2024, Mooney joined newly promoted National League club [[Boston United F.C.|Boston United]].<ref> {{cite web|url=https://www.bostonunited.co.uk/news/mooney-becomes-third-arrival-2856024.html|title=Mooney becomes third arrival|website=www.bostonunited.co.uk|date=12 June 2024|accessdate=12 June 2024}}</ref>

==International career==
Mooney has played for Wales at [[Wales national under-19 football team|under-19]] and [[Wales national under-21 football team|under-21]] levels.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Dan Mooney |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/player_summary/dan-mooney/ |access-date=19 June 2020 |website=worldfootball.net |language=en}}</ref>
