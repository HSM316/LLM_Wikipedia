'''Oakgrove School''' is a [[Mixed-sex education|coeducational]], [[Preschool|nursery]], [[primary school]], [[secondary school]] and [[sixth form]] with [[Academy (English school)|academy status]], located in the [[Middleton (Milton Keynes Village)|Middleton]] (Oakgrove Secondary) and [[Oakgrove, Milton Keynes|Oakgrove]] (Oakgrove Primary & Nursery) districts of south-east [[Milton Keynes]], England. It is the flagship school of the Kingsbridge Educational Trust<ref>{{cite web|title=Kingsbridge Educational Trust|url=http://www.ket.education/}}</ref>

The school originally opened on 1 September 2005, intaking roughly 60 students of both Year 7 and Year 8. In September 2016 the school expanded further to include a primary and nursery school section, for children aged 4 to 11.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.oakgrove.school/primary-and-nursery/|title=Primary & Nursery - Oakgrove School|website=www.oakgrove.school|access-date=2016-09-05}}</ref>

== Oakgrove Primary & Nursery ==
The Primary & Nursery section of Oakgrove School was introduced along with the development of the [[Oakgrove, Milton Keynes|Oakgrove]] district. The current headteacher (Opening - Present) is Mark Sim.

== Oakgrove Secondary ==
Oakgrove Secondary (Originally opened as Oakgrove School) was founded in 2005, from 2005 to 2015 (2014 - 2015 Academic Year) the headteacher was Peter Barnes (Current Executive Headteacher of Kingsbridge Educational Trust<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ket.education/Welcome-from-the-EH|title=Welcome from the Executive Headteacher {{!}} Kingsbridge Educational|website=www.ket.education|access-date=2019-08-13}}</ref>) however from 2015 to present Ian Tett currently holds with position.
