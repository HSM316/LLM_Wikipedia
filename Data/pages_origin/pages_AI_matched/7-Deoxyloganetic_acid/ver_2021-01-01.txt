'''7-Deoxyloganetic acid''' is an [[iridoid]] [[monoterpene]].  It is produced from [[nepetalactol]] or [[iridodial]] by the enzyme [[iridoid oxidase]] (IO).<ref>Miettinen, Dong, Navrot, Schneider, Burlat, et al. (2014) The seco-iridoid pathway from Catharanthus roseus. Nat Commun. 5</ref>  7-Deoxyloganetic acid is a substrate for [[7-Deoxyloganetic acid glucosyltransferase|7-deoxyloganetic acid glucosyltransferase]] (7-DLGT) which synthesizes [[7-Deoxyloganic acid|7-deoxyloganic acid]].<ref>Salim, Wiens, Masada-Atsumi, Yu and De Luca (2014) 7-Deoxyloganetic acid synthase catalyzes a key 3 step oxidation to form 7-deoxyloganetic acid in Catharanthus roseus iridoid biosynthesis. Phytochemistry. 101(0). 23-31</ref>
