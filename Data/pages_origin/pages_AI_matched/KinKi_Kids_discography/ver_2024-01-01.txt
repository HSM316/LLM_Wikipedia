The discography of Japanese group [[KinKi Kids]] consists of 17 studio albums, 6 compilation albums, 25 video albums and 44 singles. All of KinKi Kids' singles have reached number one in Japan on [[Oricon]] charts. All of their music has been released by [[Johnny & Associates|Johnny's Entertainment]] in Japan. In [[Taiwan]], Kinki Kids' music has been released under [[:ja:豐華唱片|Forward Music]] from 1997 to 2000, [[:ja:擎天娛樂|Skyhigh Entertainment]] and [[:ja:上華唱片|What's Music]] in 2001, and [[Avex Taiwan]] from 2002 to the present.
