'''Luann Marie Ryon''' (January 13, 1953 – December 27, 2022) was an American [[archery|archer]] who won a gold medal at the [[1976 Summer Olympics]] in [[Montreal]], Quebec.<ref name=sr>[https://web.archive.org/web/20200418083125/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ry/luann-ryon-1.html Luann Ryon]. sports-reference</ref>

Ryon was the second American to win Olympic gold in Women's Individual Archery. No American woman has won an Olympic medal in the event since. Ryon also earned world archery championships in 1976, 1977, 1978, and 1982.  <ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.rccathletics.com/alumni/hof/bios/Luann_Ryon?view=bio | title=Riverside City College Athletics }}</ref>

Ryon died on December 27, 2022, at the age of 69.<ref>[https://everloved.com/life-of/luann-ryon/?flow=201 Luann Marie Ryon]</ref>
