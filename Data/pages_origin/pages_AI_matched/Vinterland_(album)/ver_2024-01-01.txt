'''''Vinterland''''' is the fifth studio by Swedish singer [[Sarah Dawn Finer]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blixten.se/musik/sarah-dawn-finer|title=Boka Sarah Dawn Finer – Blixten & Co|work=Blixten & Co|language=Swedish|date=2014|accessdate=13 November 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141129024102/http://www.blixten.se/musik/sarah-dawn-finer|archive-date=29 November 2014|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ginza.se/product/dawn-finer-sarah/vinterland-2014-signerad-/251529/|title=Vinterland|publisher=Ginza musik|date=2014|accessdate=14 November 2014}}</ref> It was released on 13 November 2014. The album has been described as a "winter's album", and consists of many [[Christmas music|Christmas songs]], just like the album ''[[Winterland (Sarah Dawn Finer album)|Winterland]]'' which Sarah Dawn Finer released in 2010.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.svd.se/sarah-dawn-finer-gor-vinterskiva|title=Sarah Dawn Finer gör vinterskiva|publisher=Svenska dagbladet|language=Swedish|date=11 August 2014|accessdate=21 December 2018}}</ref>
