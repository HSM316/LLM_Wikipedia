'''Sven Müller''' (born February 7, 1992 in [[Mainz]]) is a German-Swiss racing driver.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.autosprint.ch/en/motorsport/nuerburgring-halber-schweizer-in-reihe-1/|title=Nürburgring: "Half" Swiss on row 1|website=AutoSprint CH|date=12 May 2018|access-date=8 September 2022}}</ref> He took the [[Porsche Supercup]] title in [[2016 Porsche Supercup|2016]].

== Career ==
Müller began his racing career in karting 2004.<ref>[http://www.sven-mueller-racing.de/ „About Sven“] (sven-mueller-racing.de; 2012-02-20)</ref> He remained in karting until 2009. [[2010 ADAC Formel Masters season|2010]] he started for Eifelland Racing in the [[ADAC Formel Masters]], a [[formula racing]] series. He regularly scored points and concluded his first season on the ninth championship position. [[2011 ADAC Formel Masters|2011]] Müller participated in the ADAC Formula Masters for ma-con Motorsport. He won four races and finished 12 times on the podium. He became third in the standings behind [[Pascal Wehrlein]] and Emil Bernstorff.<ref>[http://www.motorsport-magazin.com/motorsport/news-128701-lokalmatador-wolf-holt-sich-letzten-saisonsieg-abschlusstabelle-mit-spitzenreiter-wehrlein-nur-vorlaeufig.html „Formel Masters - Lokalmatador Wolf holt sich letzten Saisonsieg“] (Motorsport-Magazin.com, 2011-10-03)</ref><ref>[http://www.motorsport-magazin.com/motorsport/news-135158-bernstorff-doch-noch-vizemeister-positionswechsel-nach-entscheidung-am-gruenen-tisch.html „Formel Masters - Bernstorff doch noch Vizemeister“] (Motorsport-Magazin.com, 2012-02-13)</ref>

[[2012 Formula 3 Euro Series|2012]] Müller starts for [[Prema Powerteam]] in the [[FIA Formula 3 European Championship]].<ref>[http://www.motorsport-magazin.com/motorsport/news-135037-prema-setzt-2012-auf-youngster-mueller-vertrauen-rechtfertigen.html „F3 Euro Series - Prema setzt 2012 auf Youngster Müller“] (Motorsport-Magazin.com, 2012-02-10)</ref> He collected three podiums and the 8th place in the final standings. In 2013 he competed in the first seven rounds with Ma-con and the remaining three with Van Amersfoort, resulting 9th with a podium and four top 5 finishes.

Müller raced at the [[Porsche Carrera Cup Germany]] in 2014, after joining the [[Porsche Junioren]] factory team. 2015 he celebrated his first win of Porsche Mobil 1 Supercup in Hungary on the Hungaroring.<ref>[http://newsroom.porsche.com/en/motorsports/porsche-mobil-1-supercup-budapest-race-11263.html „Victory for Porsche-Junior Sven Müller“] (newsroom.porsche.com, 2015-07-28)</ref>
