'''Hellas SC''' is an amateur soccer club based in [[Winnipeg]], [[Manitoba]]. The club plays in the [[Manitoba Major Soccer League]].

They won the [[Manitoba Soccer Association Cup]] in 2008, 2009, 2022 and 2024, as well as the [[Canadian National Challenge Cup]] in [[2009 Canadian Challenge Trophy|2009]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.canadasoccer.com/history-s15518 |title=History {{!}} Canada Soccer |website=www.canadasoccer.com |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120514025329/http://www.canadasoccer.com/history-s15518 |archive-date=2012-05-14}} </ref>
