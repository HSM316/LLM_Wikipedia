'''United States Senate elections in Wyoming''' occur when voters in [[Wyoming]] select an individual to represent the state in the [[United States Senate]] in either of the state's two seats allotted by the [[United States Constitution|U.S. Constitution]]. Regularly scheduled [[general election]]s occur on [[Election Day (United States)|Election Day]], coinciding with various other federal, statewide, and local races.
