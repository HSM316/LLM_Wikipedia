'''Lauri Elias Ala-Myllymäki''' (born 4 June 1997) is a [[Finland|Finnish]] professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as an [[Midfielder#Attacking midfielder|attacking midfielder]] for [[Ilves (football)|Ilves]].<ref>{{Soccerway|lauri-ala-myllymaki/296459|accessdate=3 July 2019}}</ref> He began his senior club career playing for [[Ilves (football)|Ilves]], before signing with [[Venezia F.C.|Venezia]] in 2021.<ref>[https://www.aamulehti.fi/jalkapallo/art-2000007470063.html Läheiset ylistävät liigan maalitykkiä: ”Unelmavävy” – Lapsena otettu valokuva kertoo paljon Lauri Ala-Myllymäen rakkaudesta jalkapalloon], [[Aamulehti]], 17 July 2019</ref>

Ala-Myllymäki has played for Finland in various youth national teams and is regarded as one of the most promising Finnish players in his age group. He is known as a set-piece specialist.<ref name="yle">Vainikka, Jussi (20 July 2019). [https://yle.fi/urheilu/3-10879221 Kukkapuskat saivat kyytiä, kun Lauri Ala-Myllymäki harjoitteli vapaapotkuja kotipihalla – nyt pallot viuhuvat verkkoon ja siirto Ilveksestä ulkomaille on vain ajan kysymys] ''Yle Urheilu.'' Retrieved 12 July 2020.</ref>

==Club career==

===Ilves===
Ala-Myllymäki debuted on senior level on 15 June 2013 at the age of 16 in the ranks of [[Ilves (football)|Ilves]] in a match against [[Kotkan Työväen Palloilijat|KTP]].<ref>{{cite web | url = https://ilvesedustus.fi/kiitos-lauri-lauri-ala-myllymaki-siirtyy-veneziaan/ | title = Kiitos, Lauri! Lauri Ala-Myllymäki siirtyy Veneziaan |trans-title=Thank you, Lauri! Lauri Ala-Myllymäki is moving to Venezia | language = Finnish | publisher = [[Ilves (football)|Ilves]] | accessdate = 28 November 2022}}</ref>

===Venezia===
On 5 November 2020, he signed a 3.5-year contract with Italian club [[Venezia F.C.|Venezia]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Venezia F.C.|Venezia]]|url=https://www.veneziafc.it/news/ufficiale-lauri-ala-myllymaki-in-arancioneroverde/|title=Ufficiale: Lauri Ala-Myllymäki in arancioneroverde|date=5 November 2020|language=it}}</ref>

On 31 January 2022, Ala-Myllymäki joined [[U.S. Triestina Calcio 1918|Triestina]] on loan until 30 June 2023.<ref>{{cite press release|publisher=[[U.S. Triestina Calcio 1918|Triestina]]|url=https://www.triestinacalcio.club/apc/229|title=Calciomercato: Lauri Ala-Myllymaki è un nuovo giocatore dell'Unione!|date=31 January 2022|access-date=31 January 2022|language=it}}</ref>

During his time in Venezia, Ala-Myllymäki suffered a severe knee-injury and was sidelined for several months.<ref>[https://yle.fi/a/74-20013000/64-3-134393 Lauri Ala-Myllymäki palaa Italiasta Ilves-paitaan], [[YLE]], 24 January 2023</ref> He suffered from a knee injury also while with Triestina and played with a bad knee.<ref>[https://www.is.fi/veikkausliiga/art-2000009687702.html Lauri oli lähellä suurta unelmaansa, kun hän heräsi ja huomasi ettei kaikki ollut hyvin – siitä alkoi vuosien epäonni], [[Ilta-Sanomat]], 18 July 2023</ref>

===Return to Ilves===
On 24 January 2023, Ala-Myllymäki returned to his former club [[Ilves (football)|Ilves]] on a two-year contract.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Ilves|url=https://ilvesedustus.fi/lauri-ala-myllymaki-palaa-ilvekseen/|title=LAURI ALA-MYLLYMÄKI PALAA ILVEKSEEN|date=24 January 2023|access-date=24 January 2023|language=fi}}</ref> On 17 October 2024, his deal was extended until the end of 2026.<ref>[https://www.ilvesfootball.com/ilves-jatkosopimuksiin-omien-kasvattiensa-tatu-miettusen-ja-lauri-ala-myllymaen-kanssa/ Ilves jatkosopimuksiin omien kasvattiensa Tatu Miettusen ja Lauri Ala-Myllymäen kanssa], ilvesfootball.com, 17 October 2024</ref>

==International career==
He has represented Finland at international youth levels and he played in 8 out of 10 [[2019 UEFA European Under-21 Championship qualification]] matches.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.footballdatabase.eu/en/player/details/204294-lauri-alamyllymaki | title = Lauri Ala-Myllymäki |publisher = footballdatabase.eu | accessdate = 27 January 2023}}</ref>
