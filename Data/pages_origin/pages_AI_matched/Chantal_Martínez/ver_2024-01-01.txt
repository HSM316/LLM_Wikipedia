'''Chantal Liseth Martínez Rodriguez''' (born 28 December 1990) is a [[Panamanians|Panamanian]] former [[professional boxer]] who competed from 2006 to 2017. She held the [[World Boxing Association|WBA]] female [[super-bantamweight]] title from 2011 to 2012 and challenged once for the [[World Boxing Council|WBC]] female super-bantamweight title in 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://boxrec.com/en/proboxer/368816|title=BoxRec: Chanttall Martinez|website=boxrec.com|access-date=2019-12-03}}</ref>

==Professional career==
Martínez began her professional career at 15 on 15 July 2006 in [[El Valle de Antón]], where she lost her first fight to [[Carolina Alvarez]] of [[Venezuela]] and again on 29 December. On 27 April 2007, she won her first fight against Juseth Araúz in [[Panama City]].<ref>{{cite web|title=SHANTAL MARTINEZ|url=http://www.wban.org/biog/shantalmartinez.htm|website=wban.org|publisher=Women Boxing Archive Network|accessdate=30 September 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111010064111/http://www.wban.org/biog/shantalmartinez.htm|archive-date=10 October 2011|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In April 2011, Martínez won the 122-pound super bantamweight championship of the World Boxing Association after defeating Trinidadian [[Lisa Brown (boxer)|Lisa Brown]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Aparicio|first1=Gustavo|title='La Fiera' sacó la cara por Panamá|url=http://www.diaadia.com.pa/archivo/04042011/dep01.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110407091003/http://www.diaadia.com.pa/archivo/04042011/dep01.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=7 April 2011|publisher=Día a Día|accessdate=30 September 2011}}</ref> In June of that year, she made her defense of the title and defeated fellow Panamanian Paulina Cardona.<ref>{{cite web|title=Chantal "La Fiera" Martínez se preprara para próxima pelea|url=http://www.telemetro.com/deportes/boxeo/2011/06/01/nota73680.html|archive-url=https://archive.today/20120911061119/http://www.telemetro.com/deportes/boxeo/2011/06/01/nota73680.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=September 11, 2012|website=telemetro.com|accessdate=30 September 2011|language=es|date=1 June 2011}}</ref> In September, she again successfully defended her title by defeating Dominican [[Marilyn La Cachorrita Hernández]], a fight that [[Romina Arroyo]] of [[Argentina]] refereed.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Omaña|first1=Joniel|title='La Fiera' retiene título súper gallo|url=http://www.prensa.com/uhora/la-fiera-martinez-retiene-titulo-super-gallo/23718|work=Diario La Prensa|date=9 September 2011|access-date=6 November 2017|archive-date=19 October 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111019084630/http://prensa.com/uhora/la-fiera-martinez-retiene-titulo-super-gallo/23718|url-status=dead}}</ref>

After a two-year and three month hiatus starting on 20 April 2013 (that day being defeated by [[Alicia Ashley]]), Martínez returned to the boxing scene to challenge [[Liliana Palmera]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Caballero|first1=Elmer M.|title=“La Fiera” Martínez ya dejó de hibernar|url=http://laestrella.com.pa/deportes/boxeo/fiera-martinez-dejo-hibernar/23874173|accessdate=8 November 2017|work=La Estrella de Panama|date=21 June 2015|language=es}}</ref>
