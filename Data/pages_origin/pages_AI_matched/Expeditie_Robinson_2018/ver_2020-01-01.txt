'''Expeditie Robinson 2018''' is the nineteenth season of the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] version of the Swedish television series ''[[Expedition Robinson]]''. This season, seven non-celebrities compete against two teams of seven celebrities to win [[Euro|€]]25,000 and win the title of Robinson 2018.<ref>https://www.televizier.nl/amusement/de-onbekende-deelnemers-van-expeditie-robinson-zijn-bekend</ref><ref>https://www.rtlnieuws.nl/rtlboulevard/dit-zijn-de-kamp-zuid-deelnemers-van-expeditie-robinson</ref> The season premiered on 6 September 2018.<ref>https://www.gids.tv/artikel/3094/dit-zijn-de-onbekende-deelnemers-van-expeditie-robinson-2018</ref>
