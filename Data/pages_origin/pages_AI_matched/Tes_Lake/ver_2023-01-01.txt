'''Tes Lake''' ({{lang-id|Danau Tes}}, {{IPA-id|ˈdanau̯ˌtɛs}}) is a natural tourist attraction in the [[Lebong Regency]]. It is one of the largest lakes in [[Indonesia]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Danau Tes |url=https://www.indonesia-tourism.com/bengkulu/danau_tes.html |access-date=2022-07-19 |website=www.indonesia-tourism.com}}</ref> Tes Lake is the main [[Hydroelectricity|hydroelectric]] (hydropower) supplier for nearly all of [[Bengkulu Province]]. The lake covers an area of approximately 750 [[hectare]]s.

[[File:COLLECTIE TROPENMUSEUM Het meer van Tas TMnr 60014434.jpg|thumb|1926 photo of the lake]]

== Area description ==
Based on the Decree of Forestry Minister No. 385/Kpts-II/1985, December 27, 1985, Tes Lake covers an area of 3.230 Hectares.

==Flora==
Because of its climate, crops such as [[Coffee production in Indonesia|coffee]] are mainly grown. Other florae that grow around the area are [[Shorea|Meranti]], [[Dyera costulata]], [[Cinnamomum porrectum|Kayu Gadis]], [[Alstonia scholaris|Pulai]], [[Melaleuca|Gelam]], and other plants which also grow elsewhere in Bengkulu Province.

==Fauna==
A lot of animals can be found in Tes Lake including [[tiger]]s, [[gibbon]]s, [[monkey|monkeys]], [[snake]]s, [[bear]]s, many kinds of domestic [[bird]]s, [[dog]]s, many types of domestic [[fish]] and many other creatures. {{Citation needed|date=June 2022}}

==Location==
Tes Lake lies in the south of [[Lebong Regency]], [[Bengkulu]]. Settlements near the lake include Karang Anyar, Kutai Donok (Central Village), Talang Ratu Village and Tes Village.

=== Topography ===
Its elevation is 950m to 600m above sea level.

==Geology and climate==
Based on information from an exploration conducted in 1964, the main types of soil found in Tes Lake are regosol, andosol, latosol, and podzol (brown podzol soil).

With more wet seasons on average,
Tes Lake receives an average annual rainfall intensity of 2.700-3.500 mm. The area is classified as type A, with a Q value of approximately 0.9% - 7.7%, according to classifications from Schimidt and Ferguson. {{sfn|Usman & Suhartoyo|2021|p=2}}
