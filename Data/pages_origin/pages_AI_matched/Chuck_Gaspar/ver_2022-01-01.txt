'''Chuck Gaspar''' (January 25, 1938 – January 15, 2009) was an American [[special effect]]s artist who worked on 70 different films and TV shows, such as ''[[The Birds (film)|The Birds]]'', ''[[Ghostbusters]]'', ''[[Pale Rider]]'' and ''[[Armageddon (1998 film)|Armageddon]]''.

He was nominated at the [[57th Academy Awards]] in the category of [[Academy Award for Best Visual Effects|Best Visual Effects]] for his work on the film ''[[Ghostbusters]]''. He shared his nomination with [[John Bruno (special effects)|John Bruno]], [[Richard Edlund]] and [[Mark Vargo]].<ref name="Oscars1985">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1985 |title=The 57th Academy Awards (1985) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=April 8, 2014|work=oscars.org}}</ref>

In addition, he received a Technical achievement Oscar five years after his death.

==Family==

His hungarian father was a special effects artist named Géza Gaspar. His sons, Michael Gaspar and Dan Gaspar, are special effects artist as well.
