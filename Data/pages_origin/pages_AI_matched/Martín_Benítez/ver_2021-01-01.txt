'''Martín Nicolás Benítez''' (born June 17, 1994) is an [[Argentina|Argentine]] [[Association football|football]]er who plays as an [[Attacking midfielder|attacking midfielder]] and [[Forward (association football)|winger]] for [[Club Atlético Independiente|Independiente]].

==Club career==
Benítez began playing football in his home town team called ''The Picada''.<ref>http://afterdeportivo.wordpress.com/2010/07/23/martin-benitez-%E2%80%9Cgracias-a-mi-tio-estoy-en-independiente%E2%80%9D-2/</ref> He caught the eye of [[Boca Juniors]] who offered a trial that never materialized.<ref>http://ponealospibes.blogspot.com/2011/06/martin-benitez-goleador-de-las.html</ref>

As a result, Benitez quit soccer because of depression.<ref>http://www.misionesonline.net/noticias/07/02/2011/benitez-el-juvenil-que-pisa-fuerte-en-independiente</ref> He was encouraged back into football by his family and [[Club Atlético Independiente|Independiente]] called him up for a trial in 2009.<ref>http://www.ole.com.ar/independiente/sueno-pibito_0_594540655.html</ref><ref>http://www.afa.org.ar/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=13602:fue-designado-el-plantel-que-disputara-el-campeonato-sudamericano&catid=167:seleccion-sub-17&Itemid=69</ref>

He travelled to the [[Netherlands]] with the reserve team to play a friendly against [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]].<ref>http://www.infiernorojo.com/independiente/2011/07/duro-traspie-en-holanda/</ref> Although the team lost 5-1, he scored the only goal against goalkeeper [[Kenneth Vermeer]].

On October 13, 2011 he was called up to the first team by coach [[Ramón Díaz]].

His debut came on November 19, when Independiente defeated [[Olimpo de Bahia Blanca]] 3-0 playing the last twenty minutes of the match. His debut goal came on December 4, 2011 against [[Newell's Old Boys]] in a 1-1 draw.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.infiernorojo.com/independiente/2011/12/mira-el-golazo-de-benitez/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2011-12-04 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111208035956/http://www.infiernorojo.com/independiente/2011/12/mira-el-golazo-de-benitez/ |archivedate=2011-12-08 }}</ref>

His second goal via a header came on December 8, 2011 against [[San Lorenzo de Almagro]].<ref>http://www.ole.com.ar/futbol-primera/Necesidades-diferentes_0_605339802.html</ref>

==International career==
He has made 7 appearances with Argentina U-17 team scoring twice,<ref>http://www.misionesonline.net/noticias/25/03/2011/sub-17-el-misionero-martin-benitez-anoto-en-el-triunfo-de-argentina</ref> participating in two tournaments.<ref>http://espndeportes.espn.go.com/futbol/jugador/_/id/168966/martin-benitez</ref>
 
* [[2011 South American U-17 Football Championship]]
* [[2011 FIFA U-17 World Cup]]
