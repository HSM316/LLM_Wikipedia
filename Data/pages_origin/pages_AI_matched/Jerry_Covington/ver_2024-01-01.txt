'''Jerry Covington''' is a custom motorcycle builder and owner of Covingtons Customs.

== Background ==

[[File:Jerry Covington Chopper.jpg|thumb|Psychedelic by Jerry Covington]]
Jerry started building custom [[motorcycles]] ([[chopper (motorcycle)|choppers]]) in the early seventies and founded Covingtons Customs in [[Woodward, Oklahoma]] in 1993.
He has become well known in the motorcycle industry as one of the top custom builders for his clean designs and high quality custom motorcycles, and has appeared in numerous TV shows Including the Discovery Channel's [[Biker Build-Off]] series,<ref>{{Citation|url=http://turbo.discovery.com/biker-build-off/slideshows/roundfour/roundfour_10.html |title=Turbo: Biker Build-Off |publisher=Discovery Communications |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080413063342/http://turbo.discovery.com/biker-build-off/slideshows/roundfour/roundfour_10.html |archive-date=2008-04-13 }}</ref> Carlos Mencia's [[Mind of Mencia]],<ref>{{Citation |url=http://www.film.com/celebrities/jerry-covington/14847856 |title=Film.com }}</ref> and Corbin's Ride On.<ref>{{Citation |url=http://www.locatetv.com/tv/corbins-ride-on/1871384 |title=Locate TV |access-date=2009-07-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120309112923/http://www.locatetv.com/tv/corbins-ride-on/1871384 |archive-date=2012-03-09 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
Jerry has also been featured in several custom motorcycle related books including "Art of The Chopper",<ref>{{Citation |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=k560CsT719oC&pg=PA51 |title=Art of the chopper |first1=Tom |last1=Zimberoff |publisher=MotorBooks/MBI Publishing Company |year=2003 |isbn=9780760315729}}</ref> and "Top Chops".<ref>{{Citation |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=noQDBgezmeQC&pg=PA49 |title=Top Chops: Master Chopper Builders |first1=Dave |last1=Nichols |first2=Michael |last2=Lichter |first3=Billy (FRW) |last3=Lane |publisher=MotorBooks/MBI Publishing Company |year=2005 |isbn= 9780760322970}}</ref>
Jerry's one of a kind motorcycles have won many awards, have been featured in dozens of magazines, including Easyriders,{{citation needed|date=April 2011}} HotBike,<ref>{{Citation |url=http://www.hotbikeweb.com/features/0405_covington_cycle_city_old_school/index.html |periodical=Hot Bike |title=Old-School Looks... At An Old-School Price}}</ref> and Street Chopper,<ref>{{Citation |title=Covingtons Challenge Two Bikes for Friends |first=Howard |last=Kelly |periodical=Street Chopper |url=http://www.streetchopperweb.com/features/0406stc_2003_covington_street_chopper/index.html }}</ref> and have been photographed by Michael Lichter Photography.<ref>{{Citation|url=http://lichterphoto.com/m_pages/builder/j_covi/j_covi_main.html |title=Michael Lichter Photography Blog |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080413044344/http://lichterphoto.com/m_pages/builder/j_covi/j_covi_main.html |archive-date=2008-04-13 }}</ref>
Jerry has built motorcycles for celebrities such as comedian [[Carlos Mencia]], musician [[Sammy Hagar]], and race car driver [[Billy Boat]].
