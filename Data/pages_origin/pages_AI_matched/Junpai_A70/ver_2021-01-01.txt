The '''Junpai A70''' is a subcompact sedan produced by the [[FAW Group]] under the [[Junpai]] brand. 

==Overview==
The Junpai A70 was launched in the Chinese market in November 2015, with prices ranging from 64,800 yuan to 87,800 yuan.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.autohome.com.cn/3979/#pvareaid=100125|title=This is the Besturn B30 sedan for China}}</ref> The Junpai A70 shares the same platform as the Besturn B30 subcompact sedan produced by [[FAW Group]], and was classified as the A-class segment of the Chinese car market which is equivalent to the B-segment in foreign markets or subcompact cars.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://carnewschina.com/2017/04/06/faw-to-launch-new-compact-sedan-on-the-shanghai-auto-show-in-china/|title=FAW To Launch New Compact Sedan On The Shanghai Auto Show In China}}</ref>

The Junpai A70 debuted on the 2016 [[Beijing Auto Show]] in April 2016,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.autohome.com.cn/news/201604/886674.html|title=北京车展将亮相 骏派新轿车更多实车图}}</ref> with the market launch in August 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.autohome.com.cn/news/201608/891393.html#p1|title=前脸造型个性 天津一汽骏派A70实车到店}}</ref>

An electric version is available as the A70E.
