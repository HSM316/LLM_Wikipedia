'''Ion Bucan''' (born [[Pașcani]], 27 January 1955), is a Romanian former rugby union player.<ref>[http://airugby.ro/bucan-ioan/ Asociația Internaționalilor de Rugby România - Ioan Bucan]</ref> He played as prop.

==Career==
He first played in 1970, for [[CS Politehnica Iași (rugby union)|Politehnica Iași]], where he played until 1978, when he started to play for [[Știința Petroșani]] in the championship. Bucan later moved for [[CS Dinamo București (rugby)|Dinamo București]] in 1984. He debuted for [[Romania national rugby union team|Romania]] on  21 September 1976, against [[Bulgaria national rugby union team|Bulgaria]], in [[Burgas]],<ref>[http://en.espn.co.uk/statsguru/rugby/player/8585.html Ion Bucan international stats] ESPN Scrum</ref> during the [[1976-77 FIRA Trophy]]. Bucan was also part of the Romania squad for the [[1987 Rugby World Cup]], where he played all three matches in the tournament. His last cap was against [[France national rugby union team|France]], on 11 November 1987, in [[Agen]].
