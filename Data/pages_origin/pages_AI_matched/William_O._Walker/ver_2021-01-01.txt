'''Dr. William O. Walker''' '''aka W.O. Walker''' (born '''William Otis Walker''' on September 19, 1896 - died October 29 
1981), was an African-American publisher, [[Cleveland]]-area politician, and editor of the, ''[[Call and Post]]'', an African-American newspaper based in Cleveland.
