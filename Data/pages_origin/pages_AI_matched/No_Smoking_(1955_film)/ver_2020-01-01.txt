'''''No Smoking''''' is a 1955 British [[comedy film]] directed by [[Henry Cass]] and starring Reg Dixon, [[Peter Martyn (actor)|Peter Martyn]], [[Belinda Lee]] and [[Lionel Jeffries]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.comedy.co.uk/film/no_smoking/ |website=British Comedy Guide |accessdate=16 May 2019|title=No Smoking - Film }}</ref> It was based on a play by [[George Moresby-White]].

== Synopsis ==

A scientist who invents a pill that can cure smokers of their [[nicotine]] addiction faces strong opposition from both the [[tobacco]] industry and the government.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0048427/?ref_=nv_sr_2?ref_=nv_sr_2 |website=IMDB |accessdate=16 May 2019|title=No Smoking }}</ref>
