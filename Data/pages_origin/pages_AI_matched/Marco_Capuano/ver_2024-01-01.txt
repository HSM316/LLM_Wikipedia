'''Marco Capuano''' (born 14 October 1991) is an Italian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Centre-back|central defender]].<ref>{{Soccerway|marco-capuano/149876}}</ref>

== Club career ==
=== Pescara ===
Capuano made his debut with the senior team in the away draw for 1–1 against [[Frosinone Calcio|Frosinone]]. He obtained 10 appearances in the 2010–11 [[Serie B]] season. He also signed a new 3-year contract in December 2010.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.pescaracalcio.com/stagione/archivio-news/3894-triennale-per-marco-capuano.html|title=Triennale per Marco Capuano|date=30 December 2010|accessdate=26 December 2011|work=Delfino Pescara 1936|language=Italian|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120427025509/http://www.pescaracalcio.com/stagione/archivio-news/3894-triennale-per-marco-capuano.html|archivedate=27 April 2012}}</ref>

During the 2011–12 season, Capuano became a more regular starter under new manager [[Zdenek Zeman]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Gli 'strani' eroi dell'Under spettatori la domenica|url=http://www.repubblica.it/sport/calcio/nazionale/2011/11/16/news/protagonisti_under_ma_sconosciuti-25100467/|publisher=[[La Repubblica]]|date=16 November 2011|language=Italian}}</ref> His run in the starting eleven was soured by a controversial straight red card which led to a penalty in the 20 November match against [[A.S. Gubbio 1910|Gubbio]].

He made his Serie A debut on 26 August 2012, in the match lost 3–0 against [[Inter Milan]].

===Frosinone===
On 17 August 2018, Capuano signed with [[Serie A]] side [[Frosinone Calcio|Frosinone]].

===Ternana===
On 2 September 2021, he signed a two-year contract with [[Ternana Calcio|Ternana]] in [[Serie B]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Ternana Calcio|Ternana]]|url=https://ternanacalcio.com/ingaggiato-il-difensore-marco-capuano-sara-rossoverde-per-due-stagioni/|title=Ingaggiato il difensore Marco Capuano: sarà rossoverde per due stagioni|date=2 September 2021|accessdate=9 November 2021|language=it}}</ref>
