'''Enos Briggs''' (1746-1819)  was an American [[Shipbuilding|shipbuilder]].

== Life ==
He was born on 20 Jul 1746 in [[Pembroke, Massachusetts]].<ref>[https://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Briggs-4608]</ref>

He was the son of Seth Briggs, another shipbuilder.<ref>[https://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Briggs-4608]</ref>

He died in [[Salem, Massachusetts]] in 1819.<ref>[http://www.salemweb.com/tales/briggs.php]</ref> 

== Career ==
He is most famous for being the builder of the ship [[USS Essex (1799)]], which was captured by the British in the [[ War of 1812]].
