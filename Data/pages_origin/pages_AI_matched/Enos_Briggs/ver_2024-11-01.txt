'''Enos Briggs''' (1746&ndash;1819)  was an American [[Shipbuilding|shipbuilder]].

== Life ==
He was born on July 20, 1746, in [[Pembroke, Massachusetts]].

He was the son of Seth Briggs, another shipbuilder.

He died in [[Salem, Massachusetts]] in 1819.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web | url=http://www.salemweb.com/tales/briggs.php | title=Salem Massachusetts - Salem Tales - Enos Briggs}}</ref> 

== Career ==
He is most famous for being the builder of the warship {{USS|Essex|1799|6}} on [[Winter Island]] in Salem, which was captured by the British in the [[War of 1812]].
