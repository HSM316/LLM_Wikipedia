'''''Lycorea''''' is a [[genus]] of [[butterflies]] from the family [[Nymphalidae]] found in [[Mexico]], [[Central America]], and [[South America]].

==Species==
Listed alphabetically.<ref>[http://ftp.funet.fi/pub/sci/bio/life/insecta/lepidoptera/ditrysia/papilionoidea/nymphalidae/danainae/lycorea/ "''Lycorea'' Doubleday, [1847&#93;"] at Markku Savela's ''Lepidoptera and Some Other Life Forms''</ref>
*''[[Lycorea halia]]'' <small>(Hübner, 1816)</small> – tropical milkweed butterfly
*''[[Lycorea ilione]]'' <small>(Cramer, [1775])</small> – clearwing mimic queen
*''[[Lycorea pasinuntia]]'' <small>(Stoll, [1780])</small> – pasinuntia mimic queen
