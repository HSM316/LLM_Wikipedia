'''Agustín Parrado y García''' (5 October 1872&mdash;8 October 1946) was a Spanish [[Cardinal (Catholicism)|Cardinal]] of the [[Roman Catholic Church]] who served as [[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Granada|Archbishop of Granada]] from 1934 until his death, and was elevated to the [[Cardinal (Catholicism)|cardinalate]] in 1946 by [[Pope Pius XII]].

==Biography==
Agustín Parrado y García was born in [[Fuensaldaña]], and studied at the [[seminary]] in [[Valladolid]] before being [[Holy Orders|ordained]] to the [[Priesthood (Catholic Church)|priesthood]] on 21 September 1895. He then taught at the Valladolid seminary and at the [[Pontifical University of Valladolid]]. After becoming Vice-[[Rector (ecclesiastical)|Rector]] of the seminary of Valladolid, Parrado was made [[Canon (priest)|canon penitentiary]] of the [[cathedral]] [[Chapter (religion)|chapter]] of [[Astorga, Spain|Astorga]], [[Executive director|director]] of its [[Diocese|diocesan]] [[newspaper]], and an official of the [[Curia (Roman Catholic Church)|diocesan curia]].

He was later made a diocesan official of [[Salamanca]], [[Dean (education)|dean]] of the [[Theology|Theological]] [[Faculty (university)|Faculty]] at the [[Pontifical University of Salamanca]], and [[Monsignor|Domestic Prelate of His Holiness]] on 14 June 1922.

On 20 May 1925, Parrado was appointed [[Bishop of Palencia]] by [[Pope Benedict XV]]. He received his [[Bishop (Catholic Church)|episcopal consecration]] in the same year from Archbishop [[Julián de Diego y García Alcolea]], with Bishops [[Manuel de Castro Alonso]] and [[Manuel María Vidal y Boullón]] serving as [[Consecrator|co-consecrators]], in the [[New Cathedral, Salamanca|Cathedral of Salamanca]].

Parrado was later named [[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Granada|Archbishop of Granada]], ending a nearly four-year-long [[Sede vacante|vacancy]], on 7 March 1934 and an [[Assistant at the Pontifical Throne]] on 17 October 1945. [[Pope Pius XII]] created him [[Cardinal (Catholicism)|Cardinal Priest]] of ''[[Basilica of Sant'Agostino|Sant'Agostino]]'' in the [[Papal consistory|consistory]] of 22 February 1946.

The Cardinal died in [[Granada]], at age 74. He is buried in the [[Granada Cathedral|metropolitan cathedral of Granada]].
