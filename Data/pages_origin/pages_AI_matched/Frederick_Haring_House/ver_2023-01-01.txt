The '''Frederick Haring House''' is located in [[Old Tappan, New Jersey|Old Tappan]], [[Bergen County, New Jersey|Bergen County]], [[New Jersey]], United States. The house was added to the [[National Register of Historic Places]] on January 10, 1983.
