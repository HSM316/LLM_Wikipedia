The '''Tunisia women's national youth handball team'''({{lang-ar|منتخب تونس للإناث تحت 17 سنة لكرة اليد}}), nicknamed ''Les Aigles de Carthage (The Eagles of Carthage or The Carthage Eagles)'', represents [[Tunisia]] in the international handball competitions and it is controlled by the [[Tunisian Handball Federation]]
