'''Celedonio B.''' "'''Don'''" '''Trollano Jr.''' (born January 7, 1992) is a Filipino professional basketball player for the [[NLEX Road Warriors]] of the [[Philippine Basketball Association]] (PBA).

==College and amateur career==

Trollano studied at [[Adamson University]] and played for the [[Adamson Falcons]] from 2012 to 2014.<ref>[http://www.pba-online.net/basketball/uaap/profile/Celedonio-Trollano/12/ UAAP Profile] at PBA-Online!</ref>  He also suited up for the [[Cagayan Rising Suns]] of the [[PBA Developmental League]].<ref>{{ cite news |last1=Flores |first1=Celest R. |title=Trollano shines in PBA D-League debut as Cagayan beats Cafe France |url=http://sports.inquirer.net/125925/trollano-shines-in-pba-d-league-debut-as-cagayan-beats-cafe-france |work=[[Philippine Daily Inquirer]] |date=October 24, 2013 |access-date=September 29, 2015 }}</ref>

==Professional career==

Trollano was drafted by the [[Rain or Shine Elasto Painters]] with the 15th overall pick in the [[2015 PBA draft]].<ref>{{cite news |title=SUMMARY - 2015 PBA Rookie Draft |url=http://www.interaksyon.com/interaktv/live-results-2015-pba-rookie-draft |work=InterAksyon |date=August 23, 2015 |access-date=September 25, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924102153/http://www.interaksyon.com/interaktv/live-results-2015-pba-rookie-draft |archive-date=September 24, 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

On October 25, 2015, Trollano recorded 8 points, 3 rebounds and 4 assists in a win over the Columbian Dyip in his 2nd career game played.

On November 11, 2015, Trollano recorded his first double-digit scoring output as he scored 12 points to go along with 3 rebounds in a win over the Alaska Aces.
