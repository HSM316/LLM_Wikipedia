'''Caitlin McHugh''' is an American actress, writer, and model.

==Early life==
McHugh is of Irish and Filipino ancestry.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rmh-newyork.org/Media/Default/ronaldreports/Ronald%20Report%20Archive%20-%20July%202012%20-%20Ronald%20McDonald%20House%20New%20York.html |title=Wednesday Night Team Luau |first=Megs |last=DiDario |work=The Ronald Report |publisher=[[Ronald McDonald House Charities|Ronald McDonald House New York]] |date=July 2012 |accessdate=2018-01-10 |archive-date=2020-02-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200229150923/https://www.rmh-newyork.org/Media/Default/ronaldreports/Ronald%20Report%20Archive%20-%20July%202012%20-%20Ronald%20McDonald%20House%20New%20York.html |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Career==
She first began to find bit parts in film and television in 2007 with a minor role in ''[[I Am Legend (film)|I Am Legend]]'', and earned nine more credits over the next six years. In 2014, she portrayed Sloan in the fifth season of the TV series ''[[The Vampire Diaries]]''. In 2017, she co-wrote the romantic comedy short film ''Ingenue-ish'' with fellow actor [[John Stamos]], who also co-starred with her.

McHugh is signed with Wilhelmina Models.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wilhelmina.com/los-angeles/models/direct/women/585506/caitlin-mchugh|title=Wilhelmina International - caitlin mchugh los angeles direct portfolio|first=cDs|last=Global|website=www.wilhelmina.com|access-date=2017-12-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171228054437/http://www.wilhelmina.com/los-angeles/models/direct/women/585506/caitlin-mchugh|archive-date=2017-12-28|url-status=dead}}</ref> In 2016, she appeared in television commercials for [[Colgate toothpaste]] and the following year, for [[Buick]] SUVs.

==Personal life==
McHugh married restaurateur Massimo Lusardi on June 3, 2011. They separated a few months later and divorced in 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://starcasm.net/caitlin-mchughs-age/|title=PHOTOS VIDEO Caitlin McHugh's age and full bio, wedding dress and ring|date=February 6, 2018}}</ref>

In 2011, McHugh met actor [[John Stamos]] when they both guest-starred on an episode of ''[[Law & Order: Special Victims Unit]]''. They began dating in 2016 and became engaged in October 2017.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/john-stamos-and-caitlin-mchugh-are-engaged/ |title=John Stamos and Caitlin McHugh Are Engaged |first=Kathy |last=Campbell |work=[[Us Weekly]] |date=2017-10-23 |accessdate=2018-01-10}}</ref> They married in February 2018,<ref>{{cite news |date=2018-02-03 |title=John Stamos and Caitlin McHugh Are Married |website=ENews Online |url=http://m.eonline.com/news/911419/john-stamos-and-caitlin-mchugh-are-married |accessdate=2018-02-03}}</ref> and their son was born in April 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BhnqDhqhKPg/ |archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/iarchive/s/instagram/BhnqDhqhKPg |archive-date=2021-12-26 |url-access=registration|title=John Stamos on Instagram: "From now on, the best part of me will always be my wife and my son.
