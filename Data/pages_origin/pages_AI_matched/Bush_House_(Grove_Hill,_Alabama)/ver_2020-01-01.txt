The '''Bush House''' is a historic house in [[Grove Hill, Alabama|Grove Hill]], [[Alabama]].  The two-story [[Colonial Revival architecture|Colonial Revival]] style house was built in 1912.  It was added to the [[National Register of Historic Places]] on July 28, 1999.<ref name="NRIS"/>  It was listed due to its architectural significance as a part of the Clark County [[Multiple Property Submission]].<ref name="CCMPS">{{cite web|url={{NRHP url|id=64500005}}|title=Clarke County MPS|work=National Register of Historic Places|publisher=National Park Service |accessdate=2008-10-06}}</ref>
