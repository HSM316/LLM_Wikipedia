The '''Mutdapilly fossil locality''' is a [[fossil site]] in the geological [[formation (geology)|formation]] [[Walloon Coal Measures]] of [[Queensland]], Australia. It contains fossilized impressions of flora dating back to the [[Middle Jurassic]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fossilworks.org/cgi-bin/bridge.pl?a=collectionSearch&collection_no=21196)|title=Fossilworks: Gateway to the Paleobiology Database|website=fossilworks.org|access-date=17 December 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Mutdapilly fossil locality |url=https://www.mindat.org/paleo_loc.php?id=76933 |access-date=24 March 2024 |website=Mindat}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal |last=Mcloughlin |first=Stephen |last2=Drinnan |first2=Andrew |date=March 1995 |title=A Middle Jurassic flora from the Walloon Coal Measures, Mutdapilly, Queensland, Australia |url=https://biostor.org/reference/260360 |journal=Memoirs of the Queensland Museum |volume=38 |issue=1 |pages=257-272}}</ref>
