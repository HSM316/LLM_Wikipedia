The '''Mutdapilly fossil locality''' is a geological [[formation (geology)|formation]] in the [[Walloon Coal Measures]] of [[Queensland]], Australia. It contains fossilized impressions of flora dating back to the [[Middle Jurassic]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=collectionSearch&collection_no=21196)|title=Fossilworks: Gateway to the Paleobiology Database|website=fossilworks.org|access-date=2019-06-03}}</ref>
