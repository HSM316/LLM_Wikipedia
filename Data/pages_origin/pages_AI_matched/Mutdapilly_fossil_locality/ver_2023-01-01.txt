The '''Mutdapilly fossil locality''' is a [[fossil site]] in the geological [[formation (geology)|formation]] [[Walloon Coal Measures]] of [[Queensland]], Australia. It contains fossilized impressions of flora dating back to the [[Middle Jurassic]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fossilworks.org/cgi-bin/bridge.pl?a=collectionSearch&collection_no=21196)|title=Fossilworks: Gateway to the Paleobiology Database|website=fossilworks.org|access-date=17 December 2021}}</ref>
