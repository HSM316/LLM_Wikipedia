'''State Route 276''' ('''SR 276'''), also known as '''Thompson Creek Road''', is a short {{convert|3.2|mi|km}} long north-south state highway in eastern [[Bedford County, Tennessee]], United States. It connects the community of [[Raus, Tennessee|Raus]], and [[Tennessee State Route 130|SR 130]], with [[U.S. Route 41A in Tennessee|U.S. Route 41A]] (US 41A). It is a two-lane highway for its entire length and traverses rural and flat farmland for the majority of its course.<ref name=TDOT-Bedford-County-map> [https://www.tn.gov/content/dam/tn/tdot/maps/county-maps-(us-shields)/a-g/Bedford%20County.pdf] </ref><ref name=Googlemaps> [https://www.google.com/maps/place/TN-276,+Tennessee/@35.4289125,-86.3281873,13z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x8863deb251961307:0x93db07a5b697023] </ref>
