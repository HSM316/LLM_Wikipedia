'''State Route 276''' ('''SR 276'''), also known as '''Thompson Creek Road''', is a short {{convert|3.2|mi|km|adj=mid|-long}} north–south state highway in eastern [[Bedford County, Tennessee]], United States. It connects the community of [[Raus, Tennessee|Raus]], and [[Tennessee State Route 130|SR 130]], with [[U.S. Route 41A in Tennessee|U.S. Route 41A]] (US 41A). It is a two-lane highway for its entire length and traverses rural and flat farmland for the majority of its course.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tn.gov/content/dam/tn/tdot/maps/county-maps-(us-shields)/a-g/Bedford%20County.pdf|title=Map of Bedford County|website=tn.gov|access-date=15 September 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.google.com/maps/place/TN-276,+Tennessee/@35.4289125,-86.3281873,13z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x8863deb251961307:0x93db07a5b697023 | title=TN-276 · Tennessee }}</ref>
