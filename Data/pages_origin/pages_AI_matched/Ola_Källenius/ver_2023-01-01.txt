'''Sten Ola Källenius''' (born 11 June 1969) is a Swedish business executive. He is the chairman of the board of management of [[Mercedes-Benz Group]], and [[Chief Executive Officer|CEO]] of [[Mercedes-Benz]]. He is the first non-German in both positions.

== Education ==
Källenius graduated from the [[Stockholm School of Economics]] and the [[University of St. Gallen]], with a master's degrees, the [[Global Alliance in Management Education|CEMS Master in International Management (CEMS MIM)]] and Finance and Accounting.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.daimler.com/company/corporate-governance/board-of-management/kaellenius/|title=Ola Källenius – Biography {{!}} Daimler|work=Daimler|access-date=2018-10-04|language=en}}</ref>

== Career ==
Källenius began his career in 1993 at the former Daimler-Benz AG, where he worked in the "International Junior Research Group". After other early assignments in management control, he assumed various management positions within Daimler AG, including head of the "Powertrain" purchasing department of the Mercedes-Benz Cars group.

In 2010, he took over the chairmanship of the management of [[Mercedes-AMG|Mercedes-AMG GmbH]], which he relinquished to [[Tobias Moers]] on 1 October 2013.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://blog.mercedes-benz-passion.com/2013/09/tobias-moers-wird-neuer-amg-chef/|title=Tobias Moers wird neuer AMG-Chef|date=2013-09-06|work=Mercedes-Benz Passion Blog|access-date=2018-10-04|language=de-DE}}</ref> Since October 2013, he has been a member of the executive board of Mercedes-Benz Cars and was responsible for sales. He was appointed to the board of management of Daimler AG on 1 January 2015.<ref name=":0" /> On 1 January 2017, he took over the management of the  "Group Research and Mercedes-Benz Cars Development" division as a member of the executive board.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://blog.mercedes-benz-passion.com/2016/02/aufsichtsrat-bestellt-ola-kaellenius-als-vorstand-fuer-konzernforschung-und-mercedes-benz-cars-entwicklung/|title=Aufsichtsrat bestellt Ola Källenius als Vorstand für Konzernforschung und Mercedes-Benz Cars Entwicklung|date=2016-02-16|work=Mercedes-Benz Passion Blog|access-date=2018-10-04|language=de-DE}}</ref> He became the successor to Thomas Weber, who had been responsible for this division since 2004.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.stuttgarter-zeitung.de/inhalt.designierter-daimler-vorstandschef-ola-kaellenius-ein-faible-fuer-american-football.34ab0b09-5f20-4dd7-8c41-9a85019aee74.html|title=Designierter Daimler-Vorstandschef Ola Källenius: Ein Faible für American Football|work=[[Stuttgarter Zeitung]]|access-date=2018-10-04|language=de-DE}}</ref> Britta Seeger succeeded Källenius as board member for Mercedes-Benz Cars Sales.

Källenius succeeded [[Dieter Zetsche]] as the Chairman of the Board of Management of Daimler AG on 22 May 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.daimler.com/company/corporate-governance/board-of-management/kaellenius |title=Ola Källenius|author=<!--Not stated--> |website=daimler.com |access-date=2019-06-07 |quote=Ola Källenius has been a member of the Board of Management of Daimler AG since January 1, 2015, and Chairman of the Board of Management of Daimler AG since end of Annual Meeting of Daimler AG on May 22, 2019.}}</ref>

== Positions ==
Källenius is a strong advocate of [[plug-in hybrid]]s and [[electric vehicle]]s within Daimler. He has pushed the new electric model initiative, product and technology brand EQ, along with a $12 billion investment into Mercedes-Benz's new line of battery-powered vehicles.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.automotiveworld.com/news-releases/daimler-interview-ola-kallenius-least-one-electrified-alternative-every-model-series/|title=Daimler: Interview with Ola Källenius: "At least one electrified alternative in every model series" {{!}} Automotive World|website=www.automotiveworld.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-10-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-11-08|title=Ola Källenius|url=https://www.economicclub.org/events/ola-k%C3%A4llenius|access-date=2020-06-02|website=The Economic Club of Washington D.C.|language=en}}</ref> Within the initiative he has proposed and advocated for a series of ten new and purely electric Mercedes-Benz models to be launched by 2022.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnet.com/roadshow/news/dieter-zetsche-daimler-mercedes-benz-ceo-ola-kallenius/|title=Daimler's Dr. Z readies for retirement, names Ola Källenius successor|date=2018-09-26|work=Roadshow|access-date=2018-10-09|language=en}}</ref> The first car was the [[Mercedes-Benz EQC]] SUV, launched in the first half of 2019.<ref name=":1" />

== Personal life ==
Källenius is married; the couple have three sons. His wife is another green influencer (part of the Green Globe Organisation) and is pushing the plug-in hybrid and wants him to make the entire Mercedes-Benz range, including the flagship [[Mercedes-Benz G-Class|G-Wagon]], fully electric.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.faz.net/1.4068570|title=Daimlers Kronprinz: "Hi, ich bin der Ola"|last=Meck|first=Georg|work=FAZ.NET|access-date=2018-10-04|language=de|issn=0174-4909}}</ref>
