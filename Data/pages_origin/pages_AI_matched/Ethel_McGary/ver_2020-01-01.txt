'''Ethel M. McGary''' (October 21, 1907 – May 5, 1975), also known by her married name '''Ethel Engelsen''', was an American competition [[swimming (sport)|swimmer]] who represented the United States at the [[1928 Summer Olympics]] in Amsterdam.  McGary competed in the semifinals of the [[Swimming at the 1928 Summer Olympics – Women's 400 metre freestyle|women's 400-meter freestyle]].
