The '''1952–53 Connecticut Huskies men's basketball team''' represented the [[University of Connecticut]] in the 1952–53 collegiate men's basketball season. The Huskies completed the season with a 17–4 overall record. The Huskies were members of the [[Yankee Conference]], where they ended the season with a 5–1 record. They were the Yankee Conference regular season champions. The Huskies played their home games at Hawley Armory in [[Storrs, Connecticut]], and were led by seventh-year head coach [[Hugh Greer]].<ref name=autogenerated1>{{cite book|url=http://grfx.cstv.com/photos/schools/conn/sports/m-baskbl/auto_pdf/2011-12/misc_non_event/historicalsupplement.pdf|publisher=UConn Huskies|title=2012 Connecticut Basketball History|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |last=Davis |first=Ken |date=September 30, 2010 |title=The University of Connecticut Basketball Vault: The History of the Huskies|location=Atlanta, GA |publisher=Whitman LLC |isbn=0794828035}}</ref>
