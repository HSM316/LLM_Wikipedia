'''Bagnall Beach Observatory''' ([[List of observatory codes|obs. code]]: [[IAU code#433|433]]) is a privately owned astronomical [[observatory]] by [[Greg Crawford]], located in [[Corlette, New South Wales]], on the east coast of [[Australia]]. The observatory has discovered 3 minor planets:

* {{mp|(124033) 2001 FP|127}}
