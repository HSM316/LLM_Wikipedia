'''Sutu''' born '''Stuart Campbell''' (1981) is an [[Eisner Awards|Eisner]]-nominated, Gold Ledger and [[Webby Awards|Webby Award]]-winning [[Australians|Australian]] artist and director whose life is devoted to social impact, and pioneering new kinds of digital experiences. Art and technology have merged in his creative practice with his projects including interactive web comics, augmented reality stories, virtual reality films and virtual concerts.

== Career ==
Sutu often locates his projects and presentations among isolated populations and involves the residents creating interactive web content including [[Neomad]] - a digital interactive comic series created for the iPad in collaboration with the community of Roebourne in northern western Australia. Neomad was recently adapted as a live-action television series in Australia and inspired the  Neo-learning curriculum in Australia.
