'''''Top Management''''' ({{Korean|hangul=탑 매니지먼트|||Tap Maenijimeonteu|mr=}}) is a 2018 South Korean series that premiered on October 31, 2018 on [[YouTube Premium]].{{efn|The first three episodes are currently available for free on the [[YouTube]] channel of [[Kakao M#Music Content Company|1theK]].<ref name="1thek" /><ref name="kph" /><ref name="yna" />}}<ref>{{cite web|title=YouTube Red is getting its first K-drama soon|url=https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2018/04/26/youtube-red-getting-its-first-k-drama-soon|website=SBS|accessdate=November 29, 2018|language=en|date=April 26, 2018}}</ref> It is based on an original novel by Jang Woo-san about idols who dream about going on stage, which was published in 2015 by Munpia.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hancinema.net/new-web-drama-top-management-peeks-into-korean-drama-market-123601.html|title=New Web-drama "Top Management" Peeks Into Korean Drama Market|date=October 29, 2018|website=Hancinema}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://novel.munpia.com/47247|title=탑 매니지먼트|author=Jang, Woo-san|website=[[Munpia]]|language=ko|publisher=Munpia Inc.}}</ref> The series stars [[Seo Eun-soo]], [[Ahn Hyo-seop]] and [[Cha Eun-woo]].<ref name="kph" /><ref name="yna" />

==Synopsis==
The story centers on Eun-sung, a former girl [[Korean idol#Trainee system|idol trainee]] with the [[Precognition|power to foresee the future]], who becomes the [[Talent manager|manager]] of the aspiring, but struggling, [[Boy band|boy]] [[Korean idol|idol group]] "S.O.U.L."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/art/2018/10/682_256378.html|title=Rising actors, singers collaborate for new band S.O.U.L|date=October 13, 2018|website=The Korea Times}}</ref>
