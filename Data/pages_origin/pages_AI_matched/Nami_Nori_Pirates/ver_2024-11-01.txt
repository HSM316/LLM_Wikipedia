{{nihongo4|"'''Nami Nori Pirates'''"|波乗りパイレーツ|Nami Nori Pairētsu|lit. "Surfing Pirates"}} is the 13th single by [[Pink Lady (duo)|Pink Lady]]. It was released on 5 July 1979, with a peak position on the [[Oricon]] charts of #4,<ref name="JPNO">{{cite web|url=http://www.pinkladyamerica.com/pinkdiscography.html|title= Pink Lady singles|access-date=2009-08-08}}</ref> and also a #4 position on the Japanese [[Music Labo]] chart.<ref name="BillboardAugust1979">{{cite magazine |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=fiQEAAAAMBAJ&pg=PT71|title=Billboard Magazine, August 11, 1979 |magazine=Billboard|date=11 August 1979 }}</ref> It was also the duo's last top-five single.

The B-side, an alternate version of the song used on their ''[[Magical Music Tour]]'' studio album, features members of [[The Beach Boys]] on backing vocals.<ref>{{cite book  | title =音楽CD検定公式ガイドブック下巻  | publisher = レコード検定協議会  | page = 136}}</ref>

The single was also featured on the Japanese music program ''The Best Ten'', where it peaked at #7.<ref>{{cite book  | last =Yamada  | first =Osamuji| title =The Best Ten  | publisher = ソニー・マガジンズ  | year = 2008  | isbn = 978-4789733724}}</ref> It sold approximately 550,000 copies.<ref name="JPN">{{cite web|url=http://pinklady.gooside.com/Dg.html|title= Pink Lady single sales|access-date=2012-05-28}}</ref>
