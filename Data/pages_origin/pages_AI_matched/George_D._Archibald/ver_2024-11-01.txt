'''George D. Archibald''' (1820–1902) was a Presbyterian minister, author, and academic.<ref name=cat />

==Biography==
George D. Archibald was born in [[Washington County, Pennsylvania]] on February 15, 1820.<ref name=Cyclopaedia>{{Cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=U11DAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA125 |title=The National Cyclopaedia of American Biography |volume=II |publisher=James T. White & Company |page=125 |year=1921 |access-date=2021-05-03 |via=Google Books}}</ref>  He graduated from [[Washington & Jefferson College|Jefferson College]] in 1847.<ref name=Cyclopaedia/><ref name=cat>{{cite book| title = Biographical and Historical Catalogue of Washington and Jefferson College| publisher = Elm Street Printing Company| year = 1889| location = Cincinnati, Ohio| page = 136| chapter = Archibald, George D.| chapter-url = https://books.google.com/books?id=-ahBAAAAIAAJ| url = https://books.google.com/books?id=-ahBAAAAIAAJ}}</ref> He was president of [[Hanover College]] from 1868 to 1870.<ref name=cat /><ref name=Cyclopaedia/>

He died at his home in [[Covington, Kentucky]] on September 25, 1902.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.newspapers.com/clip/76985256/george-d-archibald-formerly-president/ |title=George D. Archibald, Formerly President of Hanover College |newspaper=[[Indianapolis Journal]] |location=Cincinnati |page=6 |date=1902-09-26 |access-date=2021-05-03 |via=Newspapers.com}}</ref>
