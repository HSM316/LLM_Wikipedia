'''Egil Iversen''' is a [[Norwegian people|Norwegian]] [[orienteering]] [[Competition|competitor]]. He received a bronze medal in the ''individual'' event at the 1985 [[World Orienteering Championships]] in [[Bendigo, Victoria|Bendigo]].<ref name=iof-woc1985>{{cite web | url=http://orienteering.org/events/?event_id=26 | title=World Orienteering Championships 1985 |publisher=[[International Orienteering Federation]] |accessdate=16 July 2017}}</ref>

==National championships==
Iversen became [[Norway|Norwegian]] champion (individual event) in 1982, and was awarded the ''King's Cup''. He received gold medals in the relay event in 1982, 1983, 1984 and 1985, competing for the club [[IF Sturla]].
