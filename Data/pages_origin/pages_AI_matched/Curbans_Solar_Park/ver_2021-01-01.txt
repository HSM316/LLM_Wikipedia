'''Curbans Solar Park''' is a 33 MW solar farm near [[Curbans]], in [[France]]. It has 145,000 [[Yingli]] PV panels, and is located at an altitude of 1000 m.<ref>[http://www.pvresources.com/PVPowerPlants/Top50.aspx Large-Scale Photovoltaic Power Plants] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160101115142/http://www.pvresources.com/PVPowerPlants/Top50.aspx |date=January 1, 2016 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.renewable-energy-sources.com/2010/02/10/gdf-suez-to-build-the-largest-photovoltaic-solar-power-facility-in-france/ GDF SUEZ To Build The Largest Photovoltaic Solar Power Facility in France] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120426063350/http://www.renewable-energy-sources.com/2010/02/10/gdf-suez-to-build-the-largest-photovoltaic-solar-power-facility-in-france/ |date=2012-04-26 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.pv-tech.org/project_focus/curbans_france Curbans, France]</ref><ref>[http://www.grupoclavijo.net/idiomas/ingles/renovables/noticias/noticias.php?id=17 Grupo Clavijo Complets France's Largest Photovoltaic Plant] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120427013050/http://www.grupoclavijo.net/idiomas/ingles/renovables/noticias/noticias.php?id=17 |date=2012-04-27 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.rechargenews.com/energy/solar/article215185.ece Yingli cements EU footprint with major French contract]</ref>
