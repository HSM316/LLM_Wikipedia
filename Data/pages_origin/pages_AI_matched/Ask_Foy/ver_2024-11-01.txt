'''The Foy Information Desk''', or the '''Foy Information Line''', is a telephone and walk-in information service provided by [[Auburn University]] and hosted in the new Student Union building.  The service has been in continuous operation since the 1950s.<ref name=5Nums>{{cite web |url=http://today.msnbc.msn.com/id/21983239/ |title=5 phone numbers that will change your life |accessdate=2008-04-28 |date=2007-11-27 |publisher=MSNBC/Today |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080426093445/http://today.msnbc.msn.com/id/21983239/ |archivedate=26 April 2008 |url-status=dead }}</ref> At one time, the service was available 24 hours a day, but is now available from 7 am to 11pm [[Central Standard Time]]. The Foy Information Desk service was initially designed as a resource for Auburn students who were looking for course information, grades, or campus services, but now accepts calls from the general public.<ref name=5Nums/>  The operators are Auburn University students, and the free service attempts to answer any question within reason asked by a caller or visitor about Auburn University.<ref>{{cite episode |title=Phone Numbers That Can Change Your Life |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lI1IDHYxz0I |series= [[Today (NBC program)|Today Show]]  |airdate=2007-11-27}}</ref> Auburn's Foy Information Desk service is one of the busiest information lines, receiving around 1000 calls each day.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://media.www.thelantern.com/media/storage/paper333/news/2006/05/31/Campus/Fact-Lines.Help.Students.Entertain.Bar.Patrons-2014975-page3.shtml |archive-url=https://archive.today/20110806053047/http://media.www.thelantern.com/media/storage/paper333/news/2006/05/31/Campus/Fact-Lines.Help.Students.Entertain.Bar.Patrons-2014975-page3.shtml |url-status=dead |archive-date=2011-08-06 |title=Fact lines help students, entertain bar patrons |accessdate=2008-04-28 |date=2006-05-26 |author=Bryan Bullock |publisher=The Lantern: The Student Voice of Ohio State University }}</ref>

The service was featured on the [[Today (NBC program)|Today Show]] on November 27, 2007.<ref name=5Nums/>

On October 10, 2014, an employee at the Foy Information Desk received a [[bomb threat]] from a caller. The building was evacuated while police searched for explosives, finding none. The call was traced back to a 15-year-old in Tennessee.<ref>{{cite news |last=Williams |first=Corey |date=28 January 2015 |title=UPDATE: Juvenile found responsible for Student Center bomb threat |url=http://theplainsman.com/view/full_story/25936879/article-UPDATE--Juvenile-found-responsible-for-Student-Center-bomb-threat? |newspaper=The Auburn Plainsman |location=Auburn, AL |access-date=2015-01-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141016201654/http://www.theplainsman.com/view/full_story/25936879/article-UPDATE--Juvenile-found-responsible-for-Student-Center-bomb-threat |archive-date=16 October 2014 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
