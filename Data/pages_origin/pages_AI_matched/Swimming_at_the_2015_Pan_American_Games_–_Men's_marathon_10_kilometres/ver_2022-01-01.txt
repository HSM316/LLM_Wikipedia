{{Swimming at the 2015 Pan American Games}}
The '''men's marathon 10 kilometres''' competition of the [[Swimming (sport)|swimming]] events at the '''[[2015 Pan American Games]]''' took place on July 12 at the [[Ontario Place|Ontario Place West Channel]], where athletes completed six 1.67&nbsp;km laps. The [[Swimming at the 2011 Pan American Games – Men's marathon 10 kilometres|defending]] [[Pan American Games]] champion was [[Richard Weinberger]] of [[Canada]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.uana-aquatics.org/uana/UserFiles/File/TOR2015/TO2015QualificationSystem-Aquatics_OpenWaterSwimming_ENG.pdf |title=Qualification System Aquatics – Open Water swimming |website=www.uana-aquatics.org/ |publisher=[[TO2015]] |accessdate=24 December 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141224223646/http://www.uana-aquatics.org/uana/UserFiles/File/TOR2015/TO2015QualificationSystem-Aquatics_OpenWaterSwimming_ENG.pdf |archivedate=24 December 2014 }}</ref>
