The '''2000 Polish Figure Skating Championships''' ({{langx|pl|Mistrzostwa Polski w łyżwiarstwie figurowym 1999/2000}}) were the [[Polish Figure Skating Championships]] of the 1999/2000 [[figure skating season]]. The Polish Championships are held annually to determine the National Champions of [[Poland]]. They were held in [[Warsaw]], between January 14 and January 16, 2000.
