'''Ronnie Larsen''' is a playwright and film director specializing in writing plays about sex.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://business.avn.com/articles/37062.html |title=Mickey's Quickies; Ringing in a Gay Porn 2010 |publisher=AVN |date=30 December 2009 |accessdate=2010-03-13}}</ref>

His play ''Making Porn'' was about the gay porn industry in the 1980s, and the production was notable for casting gay porn actors. Productions have starred Blue Blake, Rex Chandler and [[Ryan Idol]].

In 1997 Larsen made a documentary about the gay porn industry entitled ''Shooting Porn''. It is not a filmed version of the play ''Making Porn''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.avn.com/movies/17851.html |title=Shooting Porn |publisher=AVN |accessdate=2010-03-13}}</ref><ref name=nyt>{{imdb title|0120123|Shooting Porn|1997}}</ref><ref name=Holden19970711>{{cite news |title=Revealing All and That's All |last=Holden |first=Stephen |date=July 11, 1997 |newspaper=[[The New York Times]] |accessdate=2010-03-13 |url=https://www.nytimes.com/1997/07/11/movies/revealing-all-and-that-s-all.html}}</ref> The film featured figures from the [[gay pornography]] industry including Gino Colbert, [[Chi Chi LaRue]], Blue Blake, Adam Rom, Rip Stone and Adam Wilde.<ref name=nyt/>
