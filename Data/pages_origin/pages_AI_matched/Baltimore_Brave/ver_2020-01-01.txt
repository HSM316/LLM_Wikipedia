The '''Brave''' are a [[Women's Professional Lacrosse League]] (WPLL) professional women's [[field lacrosse]] team.  They have played in the WPLL since the [[2018 WPLL season]].  In the 2018 season, the five teams in the WPLL will play on a [[barnstorm (sports)|barnstorming]] format, with all five teams playing at a single venue.
