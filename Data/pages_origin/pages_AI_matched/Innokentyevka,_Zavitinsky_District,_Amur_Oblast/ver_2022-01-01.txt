'''Innokentyevka''' ({{lang-ru|Иннокентьевка}}) is a [[types of inhabited localities in Russia|rural locality]] (a [[Village#Russia|selo]]) and the administrative center of Innokentyevsky Selsoviet of [[Zavitinsky District]], [[Amur Oblast]], Russia. The population was 239 as of 2018.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://amurstat.gks.ru/wps/wcm/connect/rosstat_ts/amurstat/resources/d600c10043769f3eb9effbdd898fc419/07_1_4.htm |title=Оценка численности постоянного населения Амурской области по городам и районам |access-date=2018-11-27 |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6gvsqa6qi?url=http://amurstat.gks.ru/wps/wcm/connect/rosstat_ts/amurstat/resources/d600c10043769f3eb9effbdd898fc419/07_1_4.htm |archive-date=2016-04-21 |url-status=dead }}</ref> There are 8 streets.

== Geography ==
Innokentyevka is located 29 km southwest of [[Zavitinsk]] (the district's administrative centre) by road. Ivanovka is the nearest rural locality.<ref>[http://allroutes.ru/rasstoyanie_innokentevka-2-28_zavitinsk Расстояние от Иннокентьевки до Завитинска]</ref>
