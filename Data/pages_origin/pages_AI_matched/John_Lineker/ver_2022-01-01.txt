'''John Lineker dos Santos de Paula''' (born June 12, 1990)<ref name=fullname /> known professionally as '''John Lineker''', is a Brazilian [[mixed martial artist]] currently signed to [[ONE Championship]]. Lineker formerly competed in the [[Bantamweight (MMA)|Bantamweight]] division of the [[Ultimate Fighting Championship]]. A professional competitor since 2008, he is the former Jungle Fight Bantamweight Champion. Lineker is known for his aggressive fighting style, punching power, and durability.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2019/07/john-lineker-released-ufc-contract-16-fights|title = John Lineker released from UFC contract after 16-fight tenure|date = 3 July 2019}}</ref> He is ranked #1 in the [[ONE_Championship#MMA|ONE Championship bantamweight rankings]].<ref>{{cite web |title=ONE Championship Unveils First Official Athlete Rankings |url=https://www.onefc.com/news/one-championship-unveils-first-official-athlete-rankings/ |website=ONE FC.com |date=27 April 2020 |accessdate=27 April 2020}}</ref>

==Mixed martial arts career==

===Early career===
Lineker competed in various promotions in his native Brazil, including Samurai FC, [[Shooto]] Americas, and [[Jungle Fight]].  He is the former Jungle Fight Bantamweight Champion and Nitrix Fights 2011 Bantamweight Grand Prix Champion.

Lineker made his professional MMA debut in September 2008.  Originally he fought in the lightweight and featherweight divisions, outside of his natural weight class.  He competed 11 times in the first 15 months of his career, resulting in a lackluster record of six wins and five losses.

Beginning in 2010, Lineker moved down in weight classes to the bantamweight and flyweight divisions. After that move, he was undefeated in the 13 bouts prior to his UFC debut.

===Ultimate Fighting Championship===
In December 2011, it was announced that Lineker had signed with the Ultimate Fighting Championship to compete in their new flyweight division.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mmamania.com/2011/12/14/2636995/jungle-fight-bantamweight-champion-john-lineker-signed-by-the-ufc-set|title=Jungle Fight Bantamweight Champion John Lineker signed by the UFC, set to compete at flyweight|publisher=mmamania.com|date=May 3, 2012}}</ref>

Lineker made his UFC debut against [[Louis Gaudinot]] at [[UFC on Fox: Diaz vs. Miller]] on May 5, 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mmajunkie.com/news/27269/ufc-on-fox-3-adds-flyweights-louis-gaudinot-vs-john-lineker.mma |title=UFC on FOX 3 adds flyweights Louis Gaudinot vs. newcomer John Lineker |publisher=mmajunkie.com |date=February 3, 2012 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120205010942/http://mmajunkie.com/news/27269/ufc-on-fox-3-adds-flyweights-louis-gaudinot-vs-john-lineker.mma |archive-date=February 5, 2012 }}</ref>  The bout was contested at a catchweight of 127&nbsp;lb, as Lineker missed weight.  Gaudinot defeated Lineker via technical submission (guillotine choke) in the 2nd round after an action-packed, back-and-forth first two rounds.  Both participants earned ''Fight of the Night'' honors for their performances.

Lineker was expected to face [[Yasuhiro Urushitani]] on September 1, 2012, at [[UFC 151]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.tatame.com/2012/07/16/John-Lineker-meets-Yasuhiro-Urushitani-at-UFC-151 |title=John Lineker meets Yasuhiro Urushitani at UFC 151 |publisher=tatame.com |last=Cruz |first=Guilherme |date=July 17, 2012 }}{{dead link|date=November 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>  However, after [[UFC 151]] was cancelled, Lineker/Urushitani was rescheduled and took place on November 10, 2012, at [[UFC on Fuel TV 6]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mmajunkie.com/news/30369/mizugaki-hougland-urushitani-lineker-move-from-ufc-151-to-ufc-on-fuel-tv-6-in-macau.mma|title=Urushitani-Lineker move from UFC 151 to UFC on FUEL TV 6 in Macau|publisher=mmajunkie.com|date=August 24, 2012|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120827024650/http://mmajunkie.com/news/30369/mizugaki-hougland-urushitani-lineker-move-from-ufc-151-to-ufc-on-fuel-tv-6-in-macau.mma|archive-date=August 27, 2012}}</ref> In a competitive three rounds, Lineker's power was the difference in the fight as he edged Urushitani via unanimous decision.

As a result of the cancellation of [[UFC 233]],<ref name="changes">{{Cite news|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2018/12/multiple-ufc-233-fights-get-new-dates-locations-after-jan-26-card-scratched|title=Multiple UFC 233 fights get new dates, locations after Jan. 26 card scratched|publisher=mmajunkie.com|author=Staff|date=2018-12-12|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref><ref name="changes2">{{Cite news|url=http://mmadna.nl/veel-gevechten-van-gecancelde-ufc-233-card-verplaatst-naar-andere-evenementen/|title=Many battles of canceled UFC 233 card moved to other events|publisher=mmadna.nl|author=Marcel Dorff|date=2018-12-13|access-date=2018-12-13|language=nl}}</ref> Lineker was rescheduled to face Cory Sandhagen at [[UFC on ESPN+ 1]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2018/12/john-lineker-vs-cory-sandhagen-added-to-ufc-on-espn-plus-1-in-brooklyn|title=John Lineker vs. Cory Sandhagen added to UFC on ESPN+ 1 in Brooklyn|date=2018-12-22|website=MMAjunkie|language=en|access-date=2018-12-23}}</ref> However, it was reported on January 10, 2019, that Lineker was pulled from the bout due to rib injury.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.sherdog.com/news/news/Report-John-Lineker-Injured-Out-of-Bout-with-Cory-Sandhagen-at-UFC-on-ESPN43-1-148051|title=Report: John Lineker Injured, Out of Bout with Cory Sandhagen at UFC on ESPN+ 1|website=m.sherdog.com|access-date=2019-01-11}}</ref>

The bout with Sandhagen was rescheduled and eventually took place on April 27, 2019, at [[UFC Fight Night: Jacaré vs. Hermansson]].<ref name="taketwo">{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2019/02/john-lineker-vs-cory-sandhagen-bantamweight-ufc-on-espn-3-miami|title=John Lineker vs. Cory Sandhagen rescheduled for UFC on ESPN 3 in Miami|publisher=mmajunkie.com|author=Steven Marrocco|date=2019-02-13|access-date=2019-02-13}}</ref> Lineker lost the fight via split decision.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2019/04/ufc-fort-lauderdale-results-cory-sandhagen-beats-john-lineker-split-decision|title=UFC on ESPN+ 8 results: Cory Sandhagen survives late scare, beats John Lineker by split decision|date=2019-04-28|website=MMA Junkie|language=en|access-date=2019-04-29}}</ref>

Lineker was scheduled to face [[Rob Font]] on June 22, 2019, at [[UFC on ESPN+ 12]], replacing injured [[Cody Stamann]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mmanews.com/cody-stamann-hurt-john-lineker-now-fights-rob-font-at-ufc-greenville/|title=Cody Stamann Hurt; John Lineker Now Fights Rob Font At UFC Greenville • MMA News|last=Shelton|first=Cole|date=2019-06-05|website=MMA News|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-06}}</ref> In turn Lineker was pulled from the bout for an undisclosed reason.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2019/06/ufc-greenville-john-lineker-withdraws-from-rob-font-fight-last-minute|title=John Lineker out of UFC on ESPN+ 12 fight vs. Rob Font|date=2019-06-21|website=MMA Junkie|language=en|access-date=2019-06-21}}</ref>

On July 2, 2019, it was announced that Lineker had been released by UFC, <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2019/07/john-lineker-released-ufc-contract-16-fights|title=John Lineker released from UFC contract after 16-fight tenure|date=2019-07-03|website=MMA Junkie|language=en|access-date=2019-07-03}}</ref>and he was signed by [[ONE Championship]] on July 8, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sherdog.com/news/news/Recently-Released-UFC-Vet-John-Lineker-Announces-Signing-with-One-Championship-157977|title=Recently Released UFC Vet John Lineker Announces Signing with One Championship|last=Sherdog.com|website=Sherdog|access-date=2019-07-09}}</ref>

===ONE Championship===
Lineker made his promotional debut against Muin Gafurov at [[2019 in ONE Championship#ONE Championship: Dawn Of Valor|ONE Championship: Dawn Of Valor]] on October 26, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mmafighting.com/2019/9/12/20862255/zebaztian-kadestams-john-lineker-one-dawn-of-valor-mma|title=Zebaztian Kadestam's title defense, John Lineker's debut official for ONE: Dawn of Valor in October|author=Guilherme Cruz|publisher=mmafighting.com|date=September 12, 2019}}</ref> He won the fight by unanimous decision.

Lineker made his sophomore appearance in the organization against [[Kevin Belingon]] at [[2020_in_ONE_Championship#ONE_Championship:_Inside_the_Matrix_3|ONE Championship: Inside the Matrix 3]] on November 13, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://asianmma.com/kevin-belingon-faces-john-lineker-at-one-inside-the-matrix-part-iii/|title= Kevin Belingon faces John Lineker at ONE: 'Inside the Matrix' Part III|date=November 2, 2020|website=asianmma.com}}</ref> He defeated Belingon by second-round technical knockout.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mmafighting.com/2020/11/13/21563769/one-championship-results-video-john-lineker-knockout-kevin-belingon-singapore|title=ONE Championship results, video: John Lineker knocks out former champ Kevin Belingon in Singapore|website=MMA Fighting|date=2020-11-13|access-date=2021-02-26}}</ref> 

Lineker was scheduled to face former [[Brave Combat Federation#BRAVE Combat Federation Bantamweight Championship|Brave CF Bantamweight Champion]] Stephen Loman at [[2021 in ONE Championship#ONE on TNT 4|ONE on TNT 4]] on April 28, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mmafighting.com/2021/2/25/22302387/john-lineker-stephen-loman-one-on-tnt-4-april|title=John Lineker vs. Stephen Loman set for ONE on TNT 4 in April|website=MMA Fighting|date=2021-02-25|access-date=2021-02-26}}</ref> Loman would later withdraw from the card, as he tested positive for [[COVID-19]], and was replaced by Troy Worthen.<ref name="Loman, Adiwang">{{cite web |last1=Patrick |first1=Joseph |title=Worthen Steps In For Loman; Other 'ONE on TNT' Card Changes Confirmed |url=https://www.ibtimes.com/worthen-steps-loman-other-one-tnt-card-changes-confirmed-3176232 |website=ibtimes.com |date=7 April 2021 |access-date=8 April 2021}}</ref>He won the bout via KO in the first round. <ref>{{Cite web|last=Sherdog.com|title=One Championship 'One on TNT 3' Play-by-Play & Results|url=https://www.sherdog.com/news/news/One-Championship-One-on-TNT-3-PlaybyPlay-Results-178872|access-date=2021-04-22|website=Sherdog}}</ref>

Lineker is scheduled to face reigning champion [[Bibiano Fernandes]] for the [[List_of_ONE_Championship_champions#Bantamweight_Championship|ONE Bantamweight Championship]] at [[2021 in ONE Championship#ONE Championship: X|ONE Championship: X]] on December 3, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-09-15|title=Demetrious Johnson returns Dec. 5 for special rules bout vs. muay Thai champ Rodtang Jitmuangnon|url=https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/2021/09/one-x-demetrious-johnson-vs-rodtang-jitmuangnon-special-rules-fight-booked-dec-5|access-date=2021-09-15|website=MMA Junkie|language=en-US}}</ref>
