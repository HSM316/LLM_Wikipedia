'''Gauntlet Hair''' was an [[indie rock]] band from Denver, Colorado. The members consisted of Craig Nice and Andy Rauworth. After releasing two extended plays, they signed to record label [[Dead Oceans]] and have released their self-titled debut album.

== Biography ==
Nice and Rauworth had been playing music together since their freshman year of high school. Previously located in Chicago, they moved to Lafayette to hone their sound, and subsequently relocated to Denver.

In 2011, Gauntlet Hair signed to independent record label Dead Oceans, and started to record their debut LP. ''Gauntlet Hair'' was released on October 18, 2011 and has met with mixed to positive reviews.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Pitchfork.com|title= Gauntlet Hair: Gauntlet Hair|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/15884-gauntlet-hair/}}</ref><ref>[http://www.spin.com/reviews/gauntlet-hair-gauntlet-hair-dead-oceans Review], [[Spin magazine|SPIN]]</ref><ref>[http://www.allmusic.com/album/gauntlet-hair-mw0002207303 Review], [[Allmusic]]</ref><ref>[http://www.cmj.com/reviews/gauntlet-hair-gauntlet-hair/ Review], [[CMJ]]</ref><ref>[http://www.dustedmagazine.com/reviews/6735 Review], Dusted</ref><ref>[http://www.nme.com/reviews/gauntlet-hair/12367 Review], [[NME]]</ref>

Gauntlet Hair released their song "My Christ" as a single on January 24, 2012. The "Human Nature" music video was released as a preview to "Stills," their sophomore record (release date July 16, 2013) with the independent record label Dead Oceans.

On August 12, 2013 the band announced via their Facebook page that they were splitting writing "Gauntlet Hair has reached its end. Thank you all for your support these past 4 years. Love, Andy & Craig".

On May 31, 2018 the band announced via their Facebook page that they have started a new project named Cindygod and that new music shall follow. <ref>https://www.facebook.com/gauntlet.hair/photos/a.425967780750665/2130100197004073/?type=3&theater</ref>
