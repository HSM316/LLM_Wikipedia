'''''Artistically Beatles''''' is a studio album by keyboardist [[Vince DiCola]]. DiCola covers ten [[The Beatles|Beatles]] songs that he arranged in a [[New-age music|New-age]] instrumental genre. It was released in 1993 and features [[Roger Voudouris]] on lead vocals on the first track. On the bottom, front cover of the artwork it reads, "New Contemporary Instrumental Arrangements."
