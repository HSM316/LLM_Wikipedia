'''''Euschistus''''' is a genus of stink bugs in the family [[Pentatomidae]]. There are at least 20 described species in ''Euschistus''.<ref name=bugref>{{cite web|url=https://bugguide.net/node/view/7207|title=''Euschistus'' Genus Information|website=BugGuide.net|publisher=Iowa State University|accessdate=2018-01-26}}</ref><ref name=itis>{{cite web|url=https://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN&search_value=108839|title=''Euschistus'' Report|website=Integrated Taxonomic Information System|accessdate=2018-01-26}}</ref>
[[File:Euschistus tristigmus P1650905a.jpg|thumb|''Euschistus tristigmus'']]
