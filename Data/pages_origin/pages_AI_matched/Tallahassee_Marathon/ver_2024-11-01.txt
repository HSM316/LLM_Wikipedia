The '''Tallahassee Marathon''' is a marathon held in [[Tallahassee]], the state capital of [[Florida]], since 1975.

This race is a [[USATF]] certified course and an official qualifier for the [[Boston Marathon]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=report |first=Democrat staff |title=Here's what you need to know about Sunday's Tallahassee Marathon, road closures |url=https://www.tallahassee.com/story/sports/local/2023/02/04/what-you-need-to-know-about-sundays-tallahassee-marathon-road-closures/69869594007/ |access-date=2024-02-14 |website=Tallahassee Democrat |language=en-US}}</ref>

== History ==

The marathon was first held in 1975 with just two participants.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tallahasseemarathon.com/about/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200228042030/http://www.tallahasseemarathon.com/about/|archive-date=2020-02-28|title=About &#124; Tallahassee Marathon & Half Marathon}}</ref>

The 2021 edition of the race was cancelled due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic in Florida|coronavirus pandemic]].<ref name="2021.cancellation">{{cite web |url=http://tallahasseemarathon.com/ |title=Tallahassee Marathon & Half Marathon |website=tallahasseemarathon.com |access-date=15 January 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201011003354/http://tallahasseemarathon.com/ |archive-date=11 October 2020 |url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wtxl.com/news/local-news/2021-tallahassee-marathon-canceled-due-to-covid-19|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20201011003550/https://www.wtxl.com/news/local-news/2021-tallahassee-marathon-canceled-due-to-covid-19|archive-date = 2020-10-11|title = 2021 Tallahassee Marathon canceled due to COVID-19|date = 9 October 2020}}</ref>

The 2024 race included over 1,600 participants from 21 states and six countries.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2024-02-11 |title=Tallahassee Marathon brings runners from around the world |url=https://www.wtxl.com/college-town/tallahassee-marathon-brings-runners-from-around-the-world |access-date=2024-02-14 |website=WTXL ABC 27 Tallahassee News |language=en}}</ref>

== Course ==

The race starts in Downtown, moves through the Midtown district and through [[Myers Park (Tallahassee)|Myers Park]]. Later, the course passes [[Cascade Park (Tallahassee)|Cascade Park]] and the [[Florida State University]] campus.
