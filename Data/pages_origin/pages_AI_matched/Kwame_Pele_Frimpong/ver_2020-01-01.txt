'''Kwame Pelé Frimpong''' (born {{birth date|1983|9|24| df=yes}} in [[Cape Coast]]) is a [[Ghana]]ian professional [[Association football|footballer]], currently playing for [[Ebusua Dwarfs]] in the [[Ghana Premier League]].

==Career==
Frimpong previously played for Greek clubs [[Ilisiakos F.C.]], [[Chaidari F.C.]], [[Kalamata F.C.]] and [[Marko F.C.]].<ref name="Frimpong Kwame-Pele">{{cite web|publisher=Hellenic League|title=Frimpong Kwame-Pele|url=http://www.epae.org/player.fds?categ=2&team=33&player=7357&pagecode=04.02.02&langid=1|language=Greek|accessdate=2009-11-24}}</ref> In January 2012, Frimpong transferred from [[Egaleo F.C.]] to Ghana top-flight club [[Ebusua Dwarfs]] for an undisclosed fee.<ref name="Frimpong Kwame-Pele"/>

==International career==
Frimpong played for the [[Ghana national under-17 football team]] in the [[1999 FIFA U-17 World Championship]] in New Zealand.<ref>{{FIFA player|175532}}</ref>
