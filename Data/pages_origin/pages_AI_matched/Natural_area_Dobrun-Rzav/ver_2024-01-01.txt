'''Natural area Dobrun-Rzav''' is a [[protected area]] in [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. It is located in the canyon of the river [[Beli Rzav]], which flows through [[Donji Dobrun]] near [[Višegrad]] and merges with the river [[Drina]].<ref name="WDPA1">{{cite web |title=Drina |url=https://www.protectedplanet.net/555698327 |website=[[Protected Planet]] |access-date=17 June 2021}}</ref>  The area is planned for protection by the [[International Union for Conservation of Nature|IUCN]].<ref>{{cite web|title=ПРИЈЕДЛОГ ИЗМЈЕНЕ И ДОПУНЕ ПРОСТОРНОГ ПЛАНА РЕПУБЛИКЕ СРПСКЕ ДО 2025. ГОДИНЕ|url=http://nasljedje.org/docs/pdf/pp_rs_2025.pdf|website=Republic Institute for Protection of Cultural and Natural Heritage|access-date=15 September 2015|page=108|language=sr}}</ref>
