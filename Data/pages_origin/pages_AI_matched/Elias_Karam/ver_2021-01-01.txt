'''Elias Karam''' ({{lang-ar|الياس كرم}}) (born 1960) is an [[Assyrian people|Assyrian]] singer from [[Syria]], born in the city of [[Al-Hasakah]] in the Northeastern governorate of [[Al-Hasakah Governorate|Al-Hasakah]].

==Biography==
Elias was born [[Syria]] in the Northeastern governorate of [[Al-Hasakah Governorate|Al-Hasakah]]. He learned playing [[Oud]] from his father<ref>[http://www.rcinet.ca/arabe/chronique/arts-culture-canada/14-11_2011-06-10-/ سيّد الطرب إلياس كرم يُسلطن بصوته الجيّاش ولحنه الراقي], [[Radio Canada International]]</ref> and was inspired by [[Wadih El Safi]] at a young age, prompting him to write his first song at 16.<ref name="fest">http://www.fesfestival.com/en07/elias-karam.php</ref> After turning professional at the age of 20, Elias started composing music. His wrote the song ‘Amari, Tarab ya kalbi and Koullon ‘anak saalouni.<ref name="fest"/><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://aldoukan.chainreactionweb.com/index.php/cPath/32_221_337 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2009-08-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110708133430/http://aldoukan.chainreactionweb.com/index.php/cPath/32_221_337 |archive-date=2011-07-08 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Elias has toured all over the [[Arab world]], Canada, United States, and a number of European countries. He also appeared in some TV series in Syria.<ref>[http://www.syria-news.com/var/articlem.php?id=2477   الياس كرم: بعد أن يسمع الملحنون أغنياتي يفضلون البقاء بعيدين..] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160319065251/http://syria-news.com/var/articlem.php?id=2477 |date=2016-03-19 }}, Syria-news</ref>
