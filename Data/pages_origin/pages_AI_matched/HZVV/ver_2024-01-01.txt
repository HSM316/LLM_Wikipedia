'''Hoogeveense Zaterdag Voetbal Vereniging''', commonly known as '''HZVV''', is a [[Football in the Netherlands|Dutch association football club]] from [[Hoogeveen]], [[Drenthe]]. They are currently members of the [[Eerste Klasse]], the sixth tier of the [[Dutch football league system]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Indelingen Divisies en hoofdklassen bekend|date=2017-06-28|access-date=2017-07-28|author=Redactie|url=http://www.voetbalrotterdam.nl/2017/06/indelgen-divisies-en-hoofdklassen-bekend/ |work=Voetbal Rotterdam |language=nl}}</ref>
