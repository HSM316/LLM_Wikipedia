'''''Labidodemas''''' is a [[genus]] of marine [[sea cucumber]]s in the [[Family (biology)|family]] [[Holothuriidae]].

==Species==
The [[World Register of Marine Species]] recognises the following species:
* ''[[Labidodemas americanum]]'' <small>Deichmann, 1938</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas maccullochi]]'' <small>(Deichmann, 1958)</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas pertinax]]'' <small>(Ludwig, 1875)</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas pseudosemperianum]]'' <small>Massin, Samyn & Thandar, 2004</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas punctulatum]]'' <small>Haacke, 1880</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas quadripartitum]]'' <small>Massin, Samyn & Thandar, 2004</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas rugosum]]'' <small>(Ludwig, 1875)</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas semperianum]]'' <small>Selenka, 1867</small>
* ''[[Labidodemas spineum]]'' <small>Massin, Samyn & Thandar, 2004</small>
