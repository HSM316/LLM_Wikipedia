The '''Free Social Constitutional Party''' or '''Al-Hizb Al-Distouri''' ({{lang-ar|الحزب الدستوري الاجتماعي الحر}}) is a [[political party]] in [[Egypt]]. The Free Social Constitutional Party might be considered as a [[liberal democracy|liberal democratic]] and a [[liberal socialism|liberal socialist]] party.

== Platform ==
* Drafting a new Constitution.
* Reforming education.
* Reforming the health sector.
* Creating a favorable investment atmosphere, ensuring swift justice, eliminating red tape, reducing taxes and providing high-quality services.
* Supervising the State Budget
* Approving the appointment of senior State officials by the People's Assembly.
* Establishing a poor bank.
