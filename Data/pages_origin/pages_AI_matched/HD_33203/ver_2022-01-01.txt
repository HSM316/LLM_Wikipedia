'''HD 33203''' is [[double star]] in the northern [[constellation]] of [[Auriga (constellation)|Auriga]]. It includes a bright [[giant star]]<ref name=apjss52_7/> with a [[stellar classification]] of B2II.<ref name=apjs17_371/> The two components have an [[angular separation]] of 1.617[[arc second|″]] along a [[position angle]] of 222.1°.<ref name=aj142_2_56/>
