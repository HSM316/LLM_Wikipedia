'''Eric Edelstein''' (born April 23, 1977) is an American actor. He is best known for playing the role of Bobby Mallison in ''[[Shameless (U.S. TV series)|Shameless]]'', Chad in ''[[Clarence (2014 TV series)|Clarence]]'', B.O.B. in the ''[[Monsters vs. Aliens (TV series)|Monsters vs. Aliens]]'' television adaptation, and Grizzly "Grizz" in ''[[We Bare Bears]]''.

He also had a supporting role in the 2015 horror film ''[[Green Room (film)|Green Room]],''<ref>{{cite news |last1=Cipriani |first1=Casey |title=Watch: ‘Sit and Wait’ for Patrick Stewart in ‘The Green Room’ Clip |url=https://www.indiewire.com/2015/05/watch-sit-and-wait-for-patrick-stewart-in-the-green-room-clip-61750/ |access-date=7 September 2020 |work=[[IndieWire]] |date=18 May 2015}}</ref> plays a paddock supervisor in ''[[Jurassic World]],''<ref>{{cite news|last=Ong|first=Sally|url=http://www.christianitydaily.com/articles/4323/20150617/bryce-dallas-howards-talk-about-gruesome-deaths-in-jurassic-world-and-somewhat-wishes-her-character-met-the-same-fate.htm|title='Jurassic World' Dinosaurs: Bryce Dallas Howards Talk About Gruesome Deaths in Film and Somewhat Wishes Her Character Met the Same Fate|access-date=2015-06-24|date=17 June 2015|work=[[Christianity Daily]]}}</ref>  and voiced the character Richard Brynn in the game ''[[Before Your Eyes]]''.
