'''''Redemption Road''''' is the sixty-first album by American folk singer-songwriter [[Tom Paxton]], released in March 2015.

==Overview==
Over 60 days, from 30 July 2014 to 28 September 2014, Tom Paxton called on [[Kickstarter]]'s services to finance his new studio album, collecting a $32,177 pledge (of a $20,000 goal) from 552 backers.<ref name="Kickstarter Tom Paxton Redemption Road">{{cite web |url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1971199080/tom-paxtons-new-recording-redemption-road |title=Tom Paxton's New Recording-Redemption Road |date= 28 September 2014 |website=[[Kickstarter]] |accessdate= 22 March 2015}}</ref> Though the album was not officially released until 10 March 2015, it was recorded in 2014, and can be fully streamed on Tom's official [[SoundCloud]] page since 6 January 2015.<ref name="Tom Paxton's Official SoundCloud Page">{{cite web |url=https://soundcloud.com/tompaxton |title=Tom Paxton Free Listening on SoundCloud |date= 6 January 2015 |website=[[SoundCloud]] |accessdate= 24 June 2015}}</ref>
