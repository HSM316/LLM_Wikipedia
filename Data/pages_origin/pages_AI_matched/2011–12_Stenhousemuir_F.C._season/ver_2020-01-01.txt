The [[2011–12 in Scottish football|2011–12 season]] was [[Stenhousemuir F.C.|Stenhousemuir's]] third consecutive season in the [[Scottish Football League Second Division|Scottish Second Division]], having been promoted from the [[Scottish Third Division]] at the end of the [[2008–09 in Scottish football|2008–09 season]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.scottishfootballleague.com/stats/records/league-championship/league-tables/2000-2007/200809/ |title=Scottish Football League 2008/09 |publisher=[[Scottish Football League]] |accessdate=10 May 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121216051952/http://www.scottishfootballleague.com/stats/records/league-championship/league-tables/2000-2007/200809 |archivedate=16 December 2012 }}</ref> Stenhousemuir also competed in the [[2011–12 Scottish Challenge Cup|Challenge Cup]], [[2011–12 Scottish League Cup|League Cup]] and the [[2011–12 Scottish Cup|Scottish Cup]].

==Summary==
Stenhousemuir finished fifth in the [[Scottish Second Division|Second Division]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://soccernet.espn.go.com/tables/_/league/sco.3/scottish-division-two?cc=5739# | title=2011/12 Scottish Division Two Table / Standings | publisher=ESPN Soccernet | work=[[ESPN]] | accessdate=26 May 2012}}</ref> They reached the first round of the [[Scottish Challenge Cup|Challenge Cup]],<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfootballleague.com/football/challenge-cup/results/ | title=Ramsdens Cup Results | publisher=[[Scottish Football League]] | accessdate=26 May 2012 | archive-url=https://archive.is/20121204155914/http://www.scottishfootballleague.com/football/challenge-cup/results/ | archive-date=4 December 2012 | url-status=dead | df=dmy-all }}</ref> the second round of the [[Scottish League Cup|League Cup]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfootballleague.com/football/league-cup/results/ | title=The Scottish Communities League Cup Results | publisher=[[Scottish Football League]] | accessdate=26 May 2012 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120531202139/http://www.scottishfootballleague.com/football/league-cup/results/ | archive-date=31 May 2012 | url-status=dead | df=dmy-all }}</ref> and the fourth round of the [[Scottish Cup]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/scottish_cup_fixture_details.cfm?page=3201&matchID=115513 | title=Ross County F.C. v Stenhousemuir F.C. | publisher=[[Scottish Football Association]] | date=7 January 2012 | accessdate=26 May 2012}}</ref>
