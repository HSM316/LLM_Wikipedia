The '''Pittsburgh Pet Expo'''<ref>{{cite web|title=Pittsburgh Pet Expo|url=http://pghpetexpo.com/|website=Pghpetexpo.com|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref> is a consumer pet trade show that takes place annually in mid-Autumn at the [[David L. Lawrence Convention Center]] located in [[Downtown Pittsburgh]], [[Pennsylvania]], [[United States]]. With an estimated 14,000 spectators<ref>{{cite web|last1=Wilson Fuoco|first1=Linda|title=Pet Expo lures thousands of families, pets Downtown|url=http://www.post-gazette.com/local/city/2006/10/09/Pet-Expo-lures-thousands-of-families-pets-Downtown/stories/200610090169|website=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref> during the three days, the Pittsburgh Pet Expo is the second largest consumer pet trade show in the United States,<ref>{{cite web|last1=Lang|first1=Heather|title=Furry Tails: Pittsburgh Pet Expo Returns To Convention Center|url=http://pittsburgh.cbslocal.com/2015/10/19/furry-tails-pittsburgh-pet-expo-returns-to-convention-center/|website=CBS Pittsburgh}}</ref> covering over 2 1/2 acres of indoor space. The event originated in 2004,<ref>{{cite web|last1=Gormly|first1=Kellie B.|title=Pet owners, animals converge Downtown for Pet Expo|url=http://triblive.com/x/pittsburghtrib/ae/more/s_260849.html|website=TribLIVE|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref> with an ever-widening national recognition as one of the premier pet events in the country. The event is highlighted by the annual Pet Olympics which includes the International Judges Association (IJA) sanctioned Rescue Me Rodeo Grooming Competitions, Dock Diving,<ref>{{cite web|last1=Arbogast|first1=Sarah|title=Over 150 Exhibits Featured At Annual Pet Expo|url=http://pittsburgh.cbslocal.com/2012/11/02/over-150-exhibits-featured-at-annual-pet-expo/|website=CBS Pittsburgh|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref> National Dachshund Races, and various Agility competitions.

The event hosts roughly 250 local and national exhibitors and over 30 Animal Rescue Groups. In addition to the exhibits of pet-related products & services, the event hosts various types of entertainment and other hands-on activities.<ref>{{cite web|title=WTAE Editorial: Family fun at the Pittsburgh Pet Expo|url=http://www.wtae.com/news/wtae-editorial-family-fun-at-the-pittsburgh-pet-expo/36009318|website=WTAE Pittsburgh|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref> Dogs have made up the majority of pets accompanying owners, but cats, birds, lizards and other animals also participate.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Williams|first1=Candy|title=Pet expo offers services, fun for animals, owners|url=http://triblive.com/x/pittsburghtrib/lifestyles/family/s_531963.html|website=TribLIVE|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref>
