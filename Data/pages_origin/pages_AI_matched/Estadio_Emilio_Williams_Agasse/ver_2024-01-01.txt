The '''Estadio Emilio Williams Agasse''' is a [[Association football|football]] stadium in [[Choluteca, Choluteca|Choluteca]], [[Honduras]].  It has a seating capacity of 8 000 and it host the home games for [[Lobos UPNFM]][[Broncos del Sur F.C.|.]] in the [[Liga Nacional de Fútbol Profesional de Honduras|Honduran Liga Nacional]] .

==History==
The stadium was named after businessman [[Abraham Emilio Williams]], who had been recently assassinated in [[Choluteca, Choluteca|Choluteca]].<ref>[http://www.elheraldo.hn/sucesos/1005905-466/matan-a-machetazos-a-empresario-hondure%C3%B1o-emilio-williams-en-choluteca ElHeraldo.hn – Matan a machetazos a empresario hondureño Emilio Williams en Choluteca] – 5 October 2016</ref>  The stadium was inaugurated on 8 September 2017 and the first two games were played a day after the in a double header where [[Broncos del Sur F.C.]] hosted [[F.C. Municipal Valencia]] for a [[Honduran Liga Nacional de Ascenso|Liga de Ascenso]] encounter, followed by the [[Honduran Superclásico]] as the primetime match.<ref>[http://tiempo.hn/super-clasico-capitalino-motagua-olimpia-se-jugara-nuevo-estadio-choluteca/ Tiempo.hn – Súper clásico capitalino Motagua-Olimpia se jugará en Choluteca] – 17 July 2017</ref>
