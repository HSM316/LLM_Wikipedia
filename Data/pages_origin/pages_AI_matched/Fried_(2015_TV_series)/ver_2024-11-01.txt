'''''Fried''''' is a [[British sitcom]] that aired on [[BBC Three]]. The show stars [[Katy Wix]],<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |date=19 August 2015 |title=Katy Wix takes on fast food in BBC's Fried |url=https://www.digitalspy.com/tv/a664080/not-going-out-star-katy-wix-takes-on-fast-food-in-new-series-fried-people-thought-it-was-a-real-chicken-shop/ |access-date=14 February 2024 |website=[[Digital Spy]] |language=en-GB}}</ref> [[Mandeep Dhillon]], [[Matthew Cottle]], William Melling, [[Imran Yusuf]] and [[Lorna Gayle]].{{Citation needed|date=February 2024}} It began airing on 25 August 2015,<ref name=":0" /> and ran for six episodes until 29 September 2015.<ref>{{Cite web |date=25 August 2015 |title=Fried's Mandeep Dhillon: 'Cast becomes like family, and I wouldn't change it for the world' |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/tv/entries/68f2b404-27ac-4f3f-8101-8e46dcdfc346 |access-date=1 March 2024 |website=[[BBC]] |language=en-GB}}</ref> A show [[pilot episode|pilot]] was aired on [[BBC iPlayer]] in 2014.

==Synopsis==
''Fried'' is focused on staff who work at a chicken shop in [[Croydon]],{{Citation needed|date=February 2024}} ''Seriously Fried Chicken''. Mary Fawn ([[Katy Wix]]) is the manager,<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web |year=2014 |title=Series 3: 2014 Comedy Feeds |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p022vgzy/p022vfsn |access-date=14 February 2024 |website=[[BBC]] |language=en-GB}}</ref> much to the annoyance of her co-worker Derek Wom ([[Matthew Cottle]]) whose ambition of becoming a manager is thwarted by Mary's management. Other cast members include Amara ([[Mandeep Dhillon]]), who is only working there to earn money for her father; Joe (William Melling), who desperately wants the love of Amara; Ed ([[Imran Yusuf]]); and Shontal ([[Lorna Gayle]]).
