'''Rajna Dragićević''', PhD, ({{lang-sr-cyr|'''Рајна Драгићевић'''}}) is a [[Serbian people|Serbian]] [[linguist]], [[lexicologist]] and [[lexicographer]].<ref>Serbian Academy of Sciences and Arts - Bulletin of Scientific Research 2004 [http://www.sanu.ac.rs/english/projekti/bilten/bulletin2004.pdf]</ref> She is a full professor at the Faculty of Philology, the University of Belgrade, Serbia.

Dragicevic is the author of over 150 articles published in Serbian and international linguistic journals. She published four books, ''Pridevi sa značenjem ljudskih osobina u savremenom srpskom jeziku'' (2000), ''Leksikologija srpskog jezika'' (2007, 2010),<ref>[http://www.kubon-sagner.de/opac.html?record=7034215 Leksikologija srpskog jezika, (Zavod za udžbenike, 2010)]</ref> ''Verbalne asocijacije kroz srpski jezik i kulturu'' (2010),<ref>[http://www.kubon-sagner.de/opac.html?record=7031116 Verbalne asocijacije kroz srpski jezik i kulturu]</ref> ''Leksikologija i gramatika u skoli'' (2012). She collaborated in making of four dictionaries, ''Asocijativni rečnik srpskoga jezika'' (2005), ''Semantičko-derivacioni rečnik'', II sveska (2007), ''Rečnik srpskoga jezika'' (2007) publish by Matica srpska, and ''Obratni asocijativni rečnik srpskoga jezika'' (2011).  Dragicevic is also a recipient of the Serbian linguistic award ''Pavle Ivić''.<ref>[http://www.slavistickodrustvo.org.rs/o_nama/nagrada_pi.htm Pavle Ivić Award (in Serbian)]</ref>
