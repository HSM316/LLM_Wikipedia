'''''Semecarpus kraemeri''''', or '''Chuuk poisontree''', is a species of [[plant]] in the family [[Anacardiaceae]]. It is [[Endemism|endemic]] to the island of [[Chuuk State|Chuuk]] within the [[Federated States of Micronesia]].  A fellow endemic, the [[Faichuk white-eye|great Truk white-eye]] is thought to depend on this plant for survival.
