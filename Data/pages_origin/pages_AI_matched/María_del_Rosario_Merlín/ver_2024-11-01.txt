'''María del Rosario Merlín García''' (born 1 November 1964) is a Mexican politician. Formerly affiliated with the [[Party of the Democratic Revolution]] (PRD), she later shifted allegiance to the [[National Regeneration Movement]] (Morena).

Merlín García was born in [[Acapulco, Guerrero]], in 1964. In 2000–2003 she served in the [[Congress of Guerrero]].<ref name="sil-lxii"/>
She was elected to the [[Chamber of Deputies (Mexico)|Chamber of Deputies]] for the [[ninth federal electoral district of Guerrero|ninth district]] of [[Guerrero]] on three occasions: [[2012 Mexican general election|in 2012]] for the PRD,<ref name="sil-lxii">{{cite web |title=Perfil: Dip. María del Rosario Merlín García, LXII Legislatura |url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?Referencia=9216558 |publisher=[[Secretariat of the Interior|SEGOB]] |website=Sistema de Información Legislativa (SIL) |accessdate=17 August 2024}}</ref> and [[2018 Mexican general election|in 2018]] and [[2021 Mexican legislative election|2021]] for Morena.<ref name="sil-lxiv">{{cite web |title=Perfil: Dip. María del Rosario Merlín García, LXIV Legislatura |url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?Referencia=9223041 |publisher=[[Secretariat of the Interior|SEGOB]] |website=Sistema de Información Legislativa (SIL) |accessdate=17 August 2024}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Perfil: Dip. María del Rosario Merlín García, LXV Legislatura |url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?Referencia=9227258 |publisher=[[Secretariat of the Interior|SEGOB]] |website=Sistema de Información Legislativa (SIL) |accessdate=17 August 2024}}</ref> 
Between March and May 2021 she took a leave of absence from her seat<ref name="sil-lxiv"/>  to seek Morena's nomination for the [[List of municipal presidents of Acapulco|municipal presidency of Acapulco]] but lost to [[Abelina López Rodríguez|Abelina López]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Trujillo |first1=Javier |title=Abelina López gana encuesta como candidata a la presidencia municipal de Acapulco |url=https://www.milenio.com/politica/elecciones-2021/abelina-lopez-candidata-presidencia-municipal-acapulco |access-date=17 August 2024 |work=Milenio |date=25 March 2021}}</ref>

She was the last person to represent the ninth district before its dissolution in 2022.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Ramírez García |first1=Rosalba |title=Confirma el INE que Guerrero pierde un distrito con la nueva demarcación electoral |url=https://suracapulco.mx/impreso/1/confirma-el-ine-que-guerrero-pierde-un-distrito-con-la-nueva-demarcacion-electoral/ |access-date=17 August 2024 |work=El Sur: Periódico de Guerrero |date=23 December 2022}}</ref>
