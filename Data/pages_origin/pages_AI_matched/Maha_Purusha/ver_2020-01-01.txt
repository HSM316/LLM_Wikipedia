'''''Maha Purusha''''' (Kannada: ಮಹಾಪುರುಷ) is a [[Kannada films of 1985|1985]] [[Cinema of India|Indian]] [[Kannada language|Kannada]] film, produced and directed by [[Joe Simon (film director)|Joe Simon]], H. G. Raju, K. S. Nataraju and D. J. Nayak. The film stars [[Vishnuvardhan (actor)|Vishnuvardhan]], [[Gayatri (actress)|Gayatri]], [[Jai Jagadish]] and Chandrashekar in the lead roles. The film has musical score by [[Chellapilla Satyam]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.filmibeat.com/kannada/movies/mahapurusha.html|title=Maha Purusha|accessdate=2015-01-23|publisher=filmibeat.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.apunkachoice.com/titles/mah/mahapurusha/mid_35883/|title=Maha Purusha|accessdate=2015-01-23|publisher=apunkachoice.com}}</ref>
