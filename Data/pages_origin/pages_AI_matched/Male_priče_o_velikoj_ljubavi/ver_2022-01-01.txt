'''''Male priče o velikoj ljubavi''''' ({{trans|The Little Stories About a Great Love}}) is the fourth [[studio album]] by Yugoslav rock band [[Zabranjeno Pušenje]] released in 1989.<ref>{{cite web |title=Diskografija Zabranjeno pušenje |url=http://www.zabranjeno-pusenje.com/home/diskografija/ |website=zabranjeno-pusenje.com |accessdate=21 September 2018}}</ref> It's released through [[Diskoton]] in [[Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia|SFR Yugoslavia]]. This was the last album before splitting up of the band in early 1990.

The album was re-released in 1999 through [[TLN-Europa]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Zabranjeno Pušenje: Male Priče O Velikoj Ljubavi |url=https://www.discogs.com/Zabranjeno-Pu%C5%A1enje-Dok-%C4%8Ceka%C5%A1-Sabah-Sa-%C5%A0ejtanom/release/4284780 |website=discogs.com |accessdate=8 August 2018}}</ref>
