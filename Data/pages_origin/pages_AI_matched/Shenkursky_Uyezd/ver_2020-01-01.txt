'''Shenkursky Uyezd''' (''Шенкурский уезд'') was one of the subdivisions of the [[Arkhangelsk Governorate]] of the [[Russian Empire]]. It was situated in the southern part of the governorate. Its administrative centre was [[Shenkursk]].

==Demographics==
At the time of the [[Russian Empire Census]] of 1897, Shenkursky Uyezd had a population of 76,759. Of these, 99.6% spoke [[Russian language|Russian]], 0.3% [[Belarusian language|Belarusian]] and 0.1% [[Romani language|Romani]] as their native language.<ref>[http://www.demoscope.ru/weekly/ssp/rus_lan_97_uezd.php?reg=27] Демоскоп Weekly - Приложение. Справочник статистических показателей</ref>
