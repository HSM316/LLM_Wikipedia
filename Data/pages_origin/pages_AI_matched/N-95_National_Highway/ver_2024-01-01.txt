The '''National Highway 95''' ({{Lang-ur|{{Nastaliq|قومی شاہراہ ۹۵}}}}), also known as the '''N-95''' or the '''Chakdara-Kalam Highway''', is one of the [[National Highways of Pakistan]], running from the town of [[Chakdara]] in [[Lower Dir District]] to the town of [[Kalam, Swat|Kalam]] in [[Swat District]] via [[Mingora]], [[Manglawar]], [[Charbagh, Swat|Charbagh]], [[Khwazakhela]], [[Madyan, Pakistan|Madyan]], and [[Bahrain, Swat|Bahrain]] in the [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of [[Pakistan]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://nha.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/NHA-Road-Network-Maps-of-Projects-02.01.2012-Part-011.pdf | title=NHA-Road-Network-Maps | publisher=National Highway Authority (Pakistan) | accessdate=8 December 2014 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140620094537/http://nha.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/NHA-Road-Network-Maps-of-Projects-02.01.2012-Part-011.pdf | archive-date=20 June 2014 | url-status=dead }}</ref> 

Its total length is 135&nbsp;km, and it is maintained and operated by the [[National Highway Authority (Pakistan)|National Highway Authority]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://downloads.nha.gov.pk/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=20:national-highways-of-pakistan&catid=56:services&Itemid=58 | title=N-95 (Chakdaram–kalam) | publisher=National Highway Authority | accessdate=8 December 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last= |title=National Highway 95 (Chakdara-Kalam) N-95 |url=https://nha.gov.pk/roads/topic/15994 |access-date=2022-04-18 |website=[[National Highway Authority (Pakistan)|National Highway Authority]] |language=en}}</ref>
