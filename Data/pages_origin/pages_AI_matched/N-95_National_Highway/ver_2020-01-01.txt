The '''National Highway 95''' or the '''N-95''' is one of [[Pakistan National Highway]] running from town of Chakdara in [[Lower Dir District]] to the town of [[Kalam, Swat|Kalam]] in [[district of Swat]] via [[Mingora]], Manglawar, [[Charbagh, Swat|Charbagh]] [[Khwazakhela]], [[Madyan, Pakistan|Madyan]] and [[Bahrain, Swat|Bahrain]] in [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of [[Pakistan]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://nha.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/NHA-Road-Network-Maps-of-Projects-02.01.2012-Part-011.pdf | title=NHA-Road-Network-Maps | publisher=National Highway Authority (Pakistan) | accessdate=8 December 2014}}</ref> Its total length is 135&nbsp;km, the highway is maintained and operated by Pakistan's [[National Highway Authority (Pakistan)|National Highway Authority]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://downloads.nha.gov.pk/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=20:national-highways-of-pakistan&catid=56:services&Itemid=58 | title=N-95 (Chakdaramâ€“kalam) | publisher=National Highway Authority | accessdate=8 December 2014}}</ref>
