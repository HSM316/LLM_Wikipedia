The '''2002 [[Mr. Olympia]]''' contest was an [[International Federation of BodyBuilders|IFBB]] [[professional bodybuilding]] competition held October 16–20, 2002 at the [[Mandalay Bay Arena]] in [[Las Vegas Valley|Las Vegas]], [[Nevada]].

==Results==

The total prize money awarded was $381,000.  A 2003 Cadillac Escalade was presented to the first-place winner by Mel Rich and Steve Stern of the Bodyonics Pinnacle company which donated the prize.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bodybuildingpro.com/mrolympiareport2002.html|title=Mr. Olympia Report 2002|publisher=bodybuildingpro.com|access-date=2012-12-23|archive-date=2012-11-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121111154130/http://bodybuildingpro.com/mrolympiareport2002.html|url-status=dead}}</ref>
The Crowd was not happy when Gunter's position  was announced at 5th.
