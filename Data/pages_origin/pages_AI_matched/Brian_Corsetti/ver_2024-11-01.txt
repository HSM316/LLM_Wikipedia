'''Brian Stephen Corsetti''' is an American car builder and television personality.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.diynetwork.com/brian-corsetti-bio/bio/index.html|title = Brian Corsetti - Bio | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150326060016/http://www.diynetwork.com/brian-corsetti-bio/bio/index.html | archive-date=2015-03-26}}</ref>

== Early and personal life ==
Brian Corsetti grew up in [[St. Petersburg, Florida]] and graduated from [[Hunter College]] in [[New York City]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://womensphilanthropy.typepad.com/stephaniedoty/2010/05/philanthropy-project.html |title=PHILANTHROPY PROJECT – Women's Philanthropy--Women's Issues |accessdate=2013-01-16 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130313083338/http://womensphilanthropy.typepad.com/stephaniedoty/2010/05/philanthropy-project.html |archivedate=2013-03-13 }}</ref> While attending college he started his career working for the ''[[Ron and Fez]]'' radio show at WNEW in New York City (2002–2003). He also appeared on The [[Opie and Anthony]] Show in 2003, and later served alongside music producer [[Matt Serletic]] at Virgin Records, in [[New York City]] and [[London]].

== Career ==

Brian first got on the scene with The Dew Report, hosting a six episode extreme sports show where he traveled across the country in search for the ultimate extreme sport. He then had his own show on the DIY Network called ''Garage Mahal'' where he, along with co-host pro wrestler [[Bill Goldberg]], transform ordinary garages.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.caranddriver.com/features/garage-mahal-overhauls-our-garage|title="Garage Mahal" Overhauls our Garage|date = 20 May 2010}}</ref>  Following that, he hosted The [[Victoria's Secret Fashion Show]] Behind the Scene Coverage on CBS which won him multiple awards.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1677495/|title = Victoria Secret TV|website = [[IMDb]]}}</ref>

In 2010 Brian appeared alongside [[Alec Baldwin]], [[John O'Hurley]], and [[Robert Kennedy Jr.]], when he hosted ''A Celebrity Sports Invitational'' in Banff, Canada, and one in Jamaica for NBC.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1598619/|title = The Fairmont Banff Springs Sports Invitational|website = [[IMDb]]}}</ref>  In 2011, he acted as an executive producer, and television host in the [[Fox Broadcasting Company|FOX]] Sports show ''The Great Ride'', where he traveled around the US in search of the ultimate motorcycle ride.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1880845/|title = The Great Ride|website = [[IMDb]]}}</ref>

Other shows include ''Hollyscoop'' on FOX  and "ENTV", an entertainment show, for ION Television, Yahoo, and AOL, and the nationally syndicated show THE LIST.

He won an [[Emmy]] for his talent on the [[E. W. Scripps Company]] owned show THE LIST in 2016.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://rockymountainemmy.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/2016-Emmy-Recipients-for-PDF.pdf |title=Newscast |access-date=2017-03-03 |archive-date=2016-11-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161106015656/http://rockymountainemmy.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/2016-Emmy-Recipients-for-PDF.pdf |url-status=dead }}</ref>

In 2017 he won his second Emmy Award as best TV Host in a syndicated series for E. W. Scripps Company <ref>{{Cite web |url=http://rockymountainemmy.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/2017-Rocky-Mountain-Emmy-Recipients-.pdf |title=Christmas Under The Stars w/BYU Vocal Point BYU Broadcasting |access-date=2017-10-16 |archive-date=2017-10-17 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171017043326/http://rockymountainemmy.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/2017-Rocky-Mountain-Emmy-Recipients-.pdf |url-status=dead }}</ref>

== Other Ventures ==

In 2018 [[Jay Leno]] featured Brian on his show [[Jay Leno's Garage]] regarding his Land Cruiser collection.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XbqLV8Md25A&t=101s|title="jay leno's garage"| website=[[YouTube]] |date = 18 October 2019}}</ref> Shortly after Brian launched ''Corsetti Cruisers'' in 2019 <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/search/business|title="biz file online"}}</ref> where he builds classic 4X4's from the ground up. Specifically the [[Toyota Land Cruiser]].

In 2023, Corsetti Cruisers collaborated with [[Joel McHale]] and [[Super73]] on a special edition Corsetti SE all electric bike. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://super73.com/collections/adventure-series|title="super 73 corsetti se"}}</ref> This special edition series was inspired by the Corsetti Cruiser Brian built for Joel which included an 430 horse power LS3 motor, upgraded technology and retro style decals on the side of a completely restored 1990 Toyota Land Cruiser.
