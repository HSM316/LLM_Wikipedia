'''Hristina Milenova Pencheva''' ({{langx|bg|Христина Миленова Пенчева}}), better known by her stage name '''Tita''', (born 19 July 1999) is a Bulgarian singer, model, and actress who rose to fame in late 2014 after participating in ''[[X Factor (Bulgarian TV series)|X Factor Bulgaria]]''.  Tita is currently signed to Bulgarian hip hop artist [[Krisko]]'s [[Imprint label|record label imprint]] Adamand Records.

== Career ==
===2014–2015: X-Factor and Sweet 16===
{{Main|Sweet 16 (group)}}
Born in [[Tutrakan]], in 2014 Hristina auditioned in the Bulgarian version of the reality show ''[[X Factor (Bulgarian TV series)|The X Factor]]'' as part of the [[Sweet 16 (group)|Sweet 16]] girl band.<ref>[http://xfactor.novatv.bg/people/view/45/Sweet-16/ Presentation of the group to the site of the "X-factor"]</ref> She joined the show as a solo performer, but later X-Factor judges formed a girl's group with Hristina, [[Gery-Nikol]] and Mishel Straminski, with Bulgarian singer Zaki as their mentor. They left the competition after being eliminated in the seventh live show.<ref>{{cite news|title=Sweet 16 напуснаха "Х фактор"|url=http://www.vesti.bg/temi-v-razvitie/tema-magiiata-na-h-faktor/sweet-16-napusnaha-h-faktor-6028518|work=vesti.bg|date=4 December 2014}}</ref>

===2016–present: Adamand Records===
Following her departure from ''X Factor Bulgaria'', Hristina started performing as a model, presenting for Bulgarian modeling agency Megz Angels. On 6 October 2016 she was announced as the first recording artist to be signed to [[Krisko]]'s label Adamand Records. Hristina subsequently adopted the stage name ''Tita'' and her debut single "Voodoo Kukla" was released on the same day featuring Krisko, accompanied by a music video.<ref>[http://www.dnes.bg/muzika/2016/10/07/pyrvata-na-krisko-blondinkata-tita.317882 Първата на Криско – блондинката Tita]</ref><ref>[https://lifestyle.bg/bulgarian/hristina-detronira-geri-nikol-video.html Христина детронира Гери-Никол]</ref> Her second single entitled "Kasay" was revealed on 19 May 2017.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=56b7a_FJzpQ | title=TITA - КЪСАЙ &#91;Official 4K Video&#93; | website=[[YouTube]] }}</ref> On 6 December 2017 she presented her third single "Antilopa" to the public.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S37CvSvQN7o | title=TITA - ANTILOPA &#91;Official 4K Video&#93; | website=[[YouTube]] }}</ref> In 2018, she made her first guest appearance on Krisko's song "Iskam Da Buda S Teb", where she performed the chorus.

In 2019 Tita was one of the stars of the Bulgarian TV show "Kato Dve Kapki Voda".

==Acting==
In mid-2017, Hristina appeared on the Bulgarian web-based [[teen drama]] series ''Sledvai Me'' (''Follow Me''), where she played the [[protagonist]], Bela.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nova.bg/accents/view/2017/05/18/182566/%D0%BF%D0%BE%D0%BF-%D0%B7%D0%B2%D0%B5%D0%B7%D0%B4%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%B0-%D1%82%D0%B8%D1%82%D0%B0-%D0%BF%D0%BB%D0%B5%D0%BD%D1%8F%D0%B2%D0%B0-%D1%82%D0%B8%D0%B9%D0%BD%D0%B5%D0%B9%D0%B4%D0%B6%D1%8A%D1%80%D0%B8%D1%82%D0%B5-%D0%B2-%D0%BD%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%B8%D1%8F-%D1%83%D0%B5%D0%B1-%D1%81%D0%B5%D1%80%D0%B8%D0%B0%D0%BB-%D1%81%D0%BB%D0%B5%D0%B4%D0%B2%D0%B0%D0%B9-%D0%BC%D0%B5/ |title=Поп звездата Тита пленява тийнейджърите в новия уеб сериал "Следвай ме" - NOVA |publisher=Nova.bg |date=2017-05-18 |accessdate=2022-09-03}}</ref>
