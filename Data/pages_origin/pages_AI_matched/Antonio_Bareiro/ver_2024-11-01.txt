'''Antonio Bareiro Álvarez''' (born 24 April 1989) is a Paraguayan professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[winger (association football)|winger]] and [[forward (association football)|forward]] for Paraguayan side [[Club Libertad]].<ref name="SW">{{Soccerway|136369}}</ref>

==Career==
Bareiro was promoted to the [[Club General Díaz (Luque)|Club General Díaz]] first team in the fall of 2009. He was transferred to [[Sportivo Trinidense]] in early 2010, but returned to General Díaz 12 months later. He was quickly loaned out to [[Club Rubio Ñu]], where he immediately earned a spot in the starting lineup. He returned to General Díaz in 2013, scoring the game-winning goal in the 77th minute in his first game back, a league match against [[Deportivo Capiatá]] in February.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2013/02/10/paraguay/division-profesional/club-general-diaz/deportivo-capiata/1431728/|title=GENERAL DÍAZ VS. DEPORTIVO CAPIATÁ  2 - 1|date=27 April 2013|work=Soccerway|accessdate=15 June 2015}}</ref> He finished the [[2013 Paraguayan Primera División season#Torneo Clausura|2013 Clausura]] season as the 9th leading goalscorer, with 6.<ref>[http://www.livefutbol.com/goleadores/par-primera-division-2013-clausura/ Primera División 2013 Clausura » Máximos goleadores]</ref>

Bareiro was signed to [[Club Libertad]] on a four-year contract in January 2014.<ref>{{Cite press release |url=http://www.clublibertad.com.py/index.php?id=cmp-noticias&n=2813|title=BAREIRO Y PÉREZ DOS NUEVOS GUMARELOS|language=Spanish|date=6 January 2014|accessdate=15 June 2015|publisher=[[Club Libertad]]}}</ref> He made his team debut on 14 February against [[Club Guaraní]] as a part of the starting XI. Libertad won both the [[2014 Paraguayan Primera División season#Torneo Apertura|Apertura]] and [[2014 Paraguayan Primera División season#Torneo Clausura|Clausura]] tournaments in 2014.
