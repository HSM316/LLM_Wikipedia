'''Great Mission Public School''' is a group of [[co-educational]] schools run by Prabhas Educational and Welfare Society in [[New Delhi]], [[India]].<ref name="Society">{{cite web | url=http://gmps.officialpage.info/society.htm | title=Society}}</ref>

There are two branches of the school at [[New Delhi]]. <ref name="Contact Us">{{cite web | url=http://gmps.officialpage.info/contact.htm | title=Branches}}</ref>

The school located at [[Dwarka]], [[New Delhi]], is formally known as Neo Great Mission Public School. <ref name="List">{{cite web | url=http://www.edudel.nic.in/mis/eis/frmSchoolList.aspx?type=nTH2RniV/856b7vqlJz82IhrSGpAqr63vMJ1eA9NkD1BETO0JNRqOMaAOpCX/By2 | title=List of Recognized Schools}}</ref>

== History ==
The first branch of the school was founded in 1993 at [[Uttam Nagar]], [[New Delhi]]. Subsequently, in 2003, Dwarka branch of the school was established. <ref name="About">{{cite web | url=http://gmps.officialpage.info/About.htm | title=About the School}}</ref>

Foreign languages like [[German language|German]], [[French language|French]], and Indian languages like [[Sanskrit language|Sanskrit]] are on the academic curriculum of the school.

"Sa Vidya Ya Vimuktaye" which translated from Sanskrit means "Knowledge is that which liberates" is the motto of the school.<ref name="Motto">{{cite web | url=http://www.cultureunplugged.com/play/8088/Sa-Vidya-Ya-Vimuktaye--Knowledge-is-that-which-Liberates | title = Motto}}</ref>

==Current Status and Memberships==
The school is a member of the Eco Club of [[Govt. of Delhi]].<ref name="Eco Club">{{cite web | url=http://www.delhi.gov.in/wps/wcm/connect/Environment/environment/plan+schemes/eco+clubs+in+schools+and+colleges/eco+clubs+in+schools+and+colleges | title=Eco Club Delhi}}</ref> Principal of the school, Ms. Radha Ghai has been awarded the Dr. S. Radhakrishnan National Teacher's Award in 2009.
