'''São Francisco do Glória''' is a Brazilian municipality located in the state of [[Minas Gerais]]. The city belongs to the mesoregion of [[Zona da Mata (Minas Gerais)|Zona da Mata]] and to the microregion of Muriaé.  As of 2020, the estimated population was 4,800.<ref>[https://cidades.ibge.gov.br/brasil/mg/sao-francisco-do-gloria/panorama IBGE 2020]</ref>
