'''Yacine Bammou''' ({{langx|ar|ياسين بامو}}; born 11 September 1991) is a professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|striker]] for [[China League One]] club [[Guangxi Pingguo Haliao F.C.|Guangxi Pingguo Haliao]].<ref name="soccerway">{{Soccerway|257712|accessdate=2014-08-19}}</ref> Born in France, he plays for the [[Morocco national football team|Morocco national team]].

==Club career==
Bammou made his [[Ligue 1]] debut on 9 August 2014 against [[RC Lens|Lens]] in a 1–0 home win replacing [[Fernando Aristeguieta]] after 64 minutes. One minute later he scored the winning goal.<ref name="soccerway2">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2014/08/09/france/ligue-1/fc-nantes/racing-club-de-lens/1686695/|title=Nantes vs. Lens – 9 August 2014 – Yannis was linked to Leeds United via Waccoe on the 1st August 2016 Soccerway|publisher=soccerway.com|access-date=2014-08-19}}</ref>

In July 2018, he signed a four-year contract with [[Stade Malherbe Caen|Caen]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.smcaen.fr/2018-2019/actualites/groupe-pro/yacine-bammou-signe-quatre-ans-au-stade-malherbe|title=YACINE BAMMOU SIGNE QUATRE ANS AU STADE MALHERBE|publisher=Stade Malherbe Caen|date=24 July 2018|access-date=6 February 2019}}</ref>

On 2 July 2021, he returned to Turkey and joined [[Ümraniyespor]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Ümraniyespor]]|url=https://www.facebook.com/Umraniyespor/posts/10159505172109461|title=Yacine Bammou|date=2 July 2021}}</ref>

On 25 December 2023, [[China League One]] club [[Guangxi Pingguo Haliao F.C.|Guangxi Pingguo Haliao]] announced the signing of Bammou, taking effect on 1 January 2024.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.douyin.com/video/7316352492558683426|title=#广西平果哈嘹足球俱乐部 #外援|publisher=Douyin|date=25 December 2023|access-date=25 December 2023|language=zh}}</ref>
