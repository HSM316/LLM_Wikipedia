'''Yacine Bammou''' ([[Arabic]]: ياسين بامو; born 11 September 1991) is a professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|striker]] for [[Stade Malherbe Caen]] and the [[Morocco national football team|Morocco national team]].<ref name="soccerway">{{Soccerway|257712|accessdate=2014-08-19}}</ref>

==Club career==
Bammou made his [[Ligue 1]] debut on 9 August 2014 against [[RC Lens]] in a 1–0 home win replacing [[Fernando Aristeguieta]] after 64 minutes. One minute later he scored the winning goal.<ref name="soccerway2">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2014/08/09/france/ligue-1/fc-nantes/racing-club-de-lens/1686695/|title=Nantes vs. Lens - 9 August 2014 - Yannis was linked to Leeds United via Waccoe on the 1st August 2016 Soccerway|publisher=soccerway.com|accessdate=2014-08-19}}</ref>

In July 2018, he signed a four-year contract with [[Stade Malherbe Caen]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.smcaen.fr/2018-2019/actualites/groupe-pro/yacine-bammou-signe-quatre-ans-au-stade-malherbe|title=YACINE BAMMOU SIGNE QUATRE ANS AU STADE MALHERBE|publisher=Stade Malherbe Caen|date=24 July 2018|accessdate=6 February 2019}}</ref>
