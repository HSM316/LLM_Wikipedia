'''''By Dom Kennedy''''' is the third studio album by American rapper [[Dom Kennedy]]. The album was released on June 2, 2015. The album features the lone guest appearance from Bonic of [[Philly's Most Wanted]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/by-dom-kennedy/id994273343 |title=iTunes - Music - By Dom Kennedy by DOM KENNEDY |publisher=Itunes.apple.com |date=1984-08-22 |accessdate=2015-06-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Cole |first=Sally |url=http://thesource.com/2015/05/19/heres-the-tracklist-for-dom-kennedys-new-album-dom-kennedy/ |title=Here’s the Tracklist for Dom Kennedy’s New Album, ‘Dom Kennedy’ |publisher=The Source |date= |accessdate=2015-06-03}}</ref>

==Commercial performance==
The album debuted at number 23 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]], selling 9,000 copies.<ref>{{cite web|last=Harling |first=Danielle |url=http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.34198/title.hip-hop-album-sales-lil-durk-jason-derulo-boosie-badazz |title=Hip Hop Album Sales: Lil Durk, Jason Derulo & Boosie Badazz |publisher=HipHopDX |date= |accessdate=2015-06-10}}</ref>
