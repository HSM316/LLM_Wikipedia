'''The HALO Corporation''' is a [[San Diego]]-based company that provides [[safety]], [[security]] and [[disaster relief]].<ref name="auto">{{cite web|author=|url=http://www.sddt.com/reports/article.cfm?RID=648&SourceCode=20080924crg} |title=San Diego Source : Special Reports : Brad Barker, President |website=Sddt.com |date= |accessdate=2017-06-01}}</ref>

Halo was started in 2006 after [[Hurricane Katrina]] to fill the gaps between security and humanitarian aid.<ref name="auto"/> Halo's work was featured on ''Kidnap and Rescue'' on [[The Discovery Channel]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://dsc.discovery.com/tv/kidnap-and-rescue/ |title=Kidnap & Rescue : Discovery Channel |accessdate=2011-02-01 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110130055102/http://dsc.discovery.com/tv/kidnap-and-rescue/ |archivedate=2011-01-30 }}</ref> The President of Halo narrates the show.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hollywoodtoday.net/2011/01/27/kidnap-and-rescue/ |title=Kidnap and Rescue |accessdate=2011-02-01 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110131183647/http://www.hollywoodtoday.net/2011/01/27/kidnap-and-rescue/ |archivedate=2011-01-31 }}</ref>

Halo assists private citizens as well as government agencies and departments who need rescue, training, planning or logistical support.<ref name="auto"/>  Halo instructors come from military and government agencies. Halo offers courses on [[counterterrorism]], public safety, and operational/tactics.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://p8.hostingprod.com/@thehalocorp.com/pdf/2009-HALO-Curriculu-Guide-Rev-1.6.pdf |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2011-02-01 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110712221555/http://p8.hostingprod.com/@thehalocorp.com/pdf/2009-HALO-Curriculu-Guide-Rev-1.6.pdf |archivedate=2011-07-12 }}</ref>
 
HALO hosts a Bicoastal Counter Terrorism summit annually.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gsnmagazine.com/node/20738|title=HALO schedules counter-terrorism summits in San Diego and Fort Lauderdale - Government Security News|website=Gsnmagazine.com|accessdate=2017-06-01}}</ref>
