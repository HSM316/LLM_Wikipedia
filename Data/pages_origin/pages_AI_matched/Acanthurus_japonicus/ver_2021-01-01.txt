'''''Acanthurus japonicus''''' is a [[Tang (fish)|tang]] from the Indo-West [[Pacific]]. It occasionally makes its way into the aquarium trade. It grows to a size of 21&nbsp;cm in length. It is also known as '''Japan surgeonfish''', '''white-faced surgeonfish''', '''gold rim tang''', '''powder brown tang''' and '''white-nose surgeonfish'''.

It is easily found in the shallow water reef area of [[Green Island, Taiwan|Green Island]], a famous diving site located {{convert|33|km||0|abbr=in}} away from the southeast coast of [[Taiwan]].
