'''The 2016 Turkmenistan Higher League ([[Ýokary Liga]]) season''' is the 24rd season of [[Turkmenistan]]'s professional [[association football|football]] league. It runs between March and December 2016. [[Altyn Asyr FK|Altyn Asyr]] are the defending champions from the 2015 campaign.

[[Altyn Asyr FK|Altyn Asyr]] were crowned champions for the third time in their history beating [[Balkan FT|Balkan]] with 23 points. [[Altyn Asyr FK|Altyn Asyr]] also won [[2016 Turkmenistan Cup|Turkmenistan Cup]], meaning that [[Balkan FT|Balkan]] received the second [[AFC Cup]]-ticket

==Teams==
A total of 10 teams will contest the league, including 9 sides from the 2015 season and the champion of the [[Turkmenistan First League|Birinji liga]], [[Köpetdag Aşgabat]].
