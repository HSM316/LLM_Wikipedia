The '''2016–17 UTSA Roadrunners men's basketball team''' represented the [[University of Texas at San Antonio]] during the [[2016–17 NCAA Division I men's basketball season]]. The Roadrunners, led by first-year head coach [[Steve Henson]], played their home games at the [[Convocation Center (University of Texas at San Antonio)|Convocation Center]] and were members of [[Conference USA]]. They finished the season 14–19, 8–10 in [[2016–17 Conference USA men's basketball season|C-USA play]] to finish in the ninth place. They defeated [[2016–17 WKU Hilltoppers basketball team|Western Kentucky]] in the first round of the [[2017 Conference USA Men's Basketball Tournament|C-USA Tournament]] before losing to top-seeded [[2016–17 Middle Tennessee Blue Raiders men's basketball team|Middle Tennessee]].

==Previous season==
The [[2015–16 UTSA Roadrunners men's basketball team|Roadrunners]] finished the season 5–27, 3–15 in [[2015–16 Conference USA men's basketball season|C-USA play]] to finish in last place. They lost in the first round of the [[2016 Conference USA Men's Basketball Tournament|C-USA Tournament]] to [[2015–16 Florida Atlantic Owls men's basketball team|Florida Atlantic]].

On March 10, 2016, after 10 years with the school, head coach [[Brooks Thompson]] was fired.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.espn.com/mens-college-basketball/story/_/id/14946068/brooks-thompson-fired-utsa-roadrunners|title=UTSA fires coach Thompson after 10 seasons|newspaper=ESPN.com|access-date=2017-01-25}}</ref> He finished at UTSA with a record of 130–176. On April 1, the school hired Steve Henson as head coach.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.mysanantonio.com/sports/college_sports/utsa/article/Steve-Henson-hired-as-seventh-men-s-basketball-7222430.php|title=Steve Henson hired as seventh men's basketball coach at UTSA|newspaper=San Antonio Express-News|access-date=2017-01-25}}</ref>
