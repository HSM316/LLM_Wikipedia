'''Alpha Pup Records''' is an American [[independent record label]] and digital distributor based in Los Angeles, California.<ref>{{cite news|first=Chris|last=Martins|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/news/la-ca-la-indie-labels-20100822,0,3901157.story|title=L.A.'s string of indie labels succeeds with a jack-of-all-trades approach (page 1 of 2)|newspaper=[[Los Angeles Times]]|date=22 August 2010|accessdate=10 May 2011|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100918105931/http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/news/la-ca-la-indie-labels-20100822,0,3901157.story|archivedate=18 September 2010}}</ref> It was founded by [[Daddy Kev]] and Danyell Jariel<ref>{{cite web|first=Thomas|last=Palermo|url=https://www.xlr8r.com/news/busy-dogs-alpha-pup-ramps-up-releases|title=Busy Dogs: Alpha Pup Ramps Up Releases|work=[[XLR8R]]|date=23 August 2007|accessdate=6 June 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190528004031/https://www.xlr8r.com/news/busy-dogs-alpha-pup-ramps-up-releases|archivedate=28 May 2019}}</ref> in 2004.<ref>{{cite web|first=Max|last=Bell|url=https://www.laweekly.com/music/when-it-comes-to-la-s-world-renowned-beat-scene-daddy-kev-does-it-all-6878351|title=When It Comes to L.A.'s World-Renowned Beat Scene, Daddy Kev Does It All|work=[[LA Weekly]]|date=4 May 2016|accessdate=28 May 2019|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171004112114/https://www.laweekly.com/music/when-it-comes-to-la-s-world-renowned-beat-scene-daddy-kev-does-it-all-6878351|archivedate=4 October 2017}}</ref> ''[[LA Weekly]]'' placed it at number 2 on the "Top 10 Most Exciting L.A. Indie Labels of 2010" list.<ref>{{cite web|first=Drew|last=Tewksbury|url=http://www.laweekly.com/music/top-10-most-exciting-la-indie-labels-of-2010-2403599|title=Top 10 Most Exciting L.A. Indie Labels of 2010|work=[[LA Weekly]]|date=28 December 2010|accessdate=6 June 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150508063743/http://www.laweekly.com/music/top-10-most-exciting-la-indie-labels-of-2010-2403599|archivedate=8 May 2015}}</ref>

==History==
[[BBC One]] DJ [[Mary Anne Hobbs]] has supported Alpha Pup Records from its inception and comments that "the artists involved with [[Low End Theory]], with the [[Brainfeeder]] label, with Alpha Pup, with Leaving Records all have close ties with the UK".<ref>{{cite news|last=Church|first=Steve|title=Turning it up with Mary Anne Hobbs|url=http://dailyfreepress.com/2011/04/21/turning-it-up-with-mary-anne-hobbs/|accessdate=10 May 2011|newspaper=[[The Daily Free Press]]|date=21 April 2011|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110422221937/http://dailyfreepress.com/2011/04/21/turning-it-up-with-mary-anne-hobbs/|archivedate=22 April 2011|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2014, Chris Ziegler of ''[[LA Weekly]]'' described the label as "a big part of the reason why" "Los Angeles is one of the most vital cities in the world for electronic music right now".<ref>{{cite web|first=Chris|last=Ziegler|url=http://www.laweekly.com/music/the-best-concerts-to-see-in-la-this-week-4442700|title=The Best Concerts to See in L.A. This Week|work=[[LA Weekly]]|date=17 February 2014|accessdate=6 June 2018}}</ref>
