==[[Mamluk dynasty of Iraq|Mamluk]] walis (1704–1831)==

The Mamluks ruled the [[Eyalet|pashaliks]] of [[Baghdad Eyalet|Baghdad]], [[Basra Eyalet|Basrah]], and [[Shahrizor Eyalet|Shahrizor]].<ref>{{cite book|last=Coke|first=Richard|title=Baghdad, The City of Peace|year=1927|publisher=Taylor & Francis|pages=232–233}}</ref> The pashalik of [[Mosul Eyalet|Mosul]] was ruled by the [[Iraqi people|Iraqi]] [[Jalili dynasty]].
