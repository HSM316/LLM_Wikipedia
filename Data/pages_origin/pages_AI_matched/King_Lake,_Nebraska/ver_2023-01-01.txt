'''King Lake''' is an [[unincorporated community]] and [[census-designated place]] in [[Douglas County, Nebraska|Douglas County]], [[Nebraska]], United States.<ref>{{GNIS|830510}}</ref> The population was 280 at the [[2010 United States Census|2010 census]].<ref name="Census 2010">{{cite web| url=http://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_SF1/G001/1600000US3125510| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): King Lake CDP, Nebraska| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=November 3, 2015| archive-url=https://archive.today/20200213044958/http://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_SF1/G001/1600000US3125510| archive-date=February 13, 2020| url-status=dead}}</ref> Of the population, 218 people are male and 62 are female.<ref name="City-Data.com">{{cite web | url=http://www.city-data.com/city/King-Lake-Nebraska.html | title=King Lake, Nebraska | accessdate=19 August 2014}}</ref> The area is {{convert|1.02|sqmi}}. The population density is 125.09 people/sq. mi. The land area is {{convert|0.99|sqmi}}. The water area is {{convert|0.03|sqmi}}.<ref name="Nebraska Gazetteer">{{cite web | url=http://nebraska.hometownlocator.com/ne/douglas/king-lake.cfm | title=King Lake, Nebraska | accessdate=19 August 2014}}</ref>
