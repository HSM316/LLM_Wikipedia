'''Sheikh Abdullah Awad Al Juhany''' ({{lang-ar|عبد الله عواد الجهني}}), is one of the [[Imam]]s of the Grand Mosque [[Masjid Al-Haram|Masjid al-Haram]] in [[Mecca]].<ref name="alim1">{{cite web |last1=News |first1=Arab |title=Sheikh Abdullah Awad Al-Juhani, imam at the Grand Mosque in Makkah |url=https://www.arabnews.com/node/1482046 |website=Arabnews.com |publisher=Arabnews |accessdate=16 September 2021 |ref=Published on 13 April 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Imam-e-Kaaba to give sermon, lead Friday prayer at Faisal Mosque|url=https://www.thenews.com.pk/latest/456960-imam-e-kaaba-to-give-sermon-lead-friday-prayer-at-faisal-mosque|access-date=2021-09-15|website=www.thenews.com.pk|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-04-15|title=Imam-e-Kaaba to visit parliament house today|url=https://nation.com.pk/15-Apr-2019/imam-e-kaaba-dr-abdullah-awad-al-juhany-to-visit-parliament-house-today|access-date=2021-09-15|website=The Nation|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Permana|first=Rakhmad Hidayatulloh|title=Syeikh Baleelah Diserang Saat Khotbah, Ini 11 Imam-Khatib Masjidil Haram|url=https://news.detik.com/berita/d-5578673/syeikh-baleelah-diserang-saat-khotbah-ini-11-imam-khatib-masjidil-haram|access-date=2021-09-15|website=detiknews|language=id-ID}}</ref> He holds a [[Bachelor of Arts|B.A.]] from the faculty of Qur'an at The [[Islamic University of Madinah]], and a [[Doctor of Philosophy|doctorate (Ph.D.)]] from [[Umm al-Qura University]] in [[Mecca]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-04-24|title=2 Billion Muslims follow Sheikh Juhany as their Imam of Ka’aba|url=https://dailytimes.com.pk/381853/2-billion-muslims-follow-sheikh-juhany-as-their-imam-of-kaaba/|access-date=2021-09-15|website=Daily Times|language=en-US}}</ref>

Juhany has led the [[Tarawih|Taraweeh]] prayers during [[Ramadan]] in [[Mecca]] since 2005, and is known for his emotional Quranic recitation style. His voice has been widely recorded and is internationally distributed by various communities.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?75723-Information-On-Some-Of-The-Imams-Of-The-Haramain-Interviews-Profile-Masjid-Haram |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2013-08-25 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130930055925/http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?75723-Information-On-Some-Of-The-Imams-Of-The-Haramain-Interviews-Profile-Masjid-Haram |archivedate=2013-09-30 }}</ref>

== Biography ==
=== Early life ===
Sheikh Al-Juhany was born in Medina holy City of Saudi Arabia on 13 January 1976. He completed the memorization of the Quran at a very young age. At a very young age he entered in the Quran Recitation Competition which was held in Masjid Al Haram every year (and still is) and won the first prize.

=== Personal life ===
He is married, has two sons (Muhammad and Abdul Aziz) and two daughters as well. Both of his sons are young and are memorizing the Holy Quran. He has three brothers and two sisters. He is the youngest son and was born in [[Medina]], [[Saudi Arabia]] in 1976.

=== Academic background ===
As he came from a very Religious background, his parents wanted to him to progress in the field and hence he gained education from [[Umm al-Qura University|Umm Al Qurra University]], and gained a [[Doctor of Philosophy|Ph.D.]] Degree in [[Sharia|Islamic Sharia]] and [[Qiraʼat|Quranic Recitation]]. Thus making him one of the youngest ones to gain a Ph.D. Degree from [[Umm al-Qura University|Umm Al Qura University]].<ref group="note">'''Abdullah Awad al Juhani''' was born into a very religious family. His family encouraged him to pursue his studies in the field of [[Islamic studies|Islamic Studies]] due to which he gained a [[doctorate]] in [[Sharia|Islamic Shari’ah]] and [[Qiraʼat|Qur’anic Recitation]] from [[Umm al-Qura University|Umm al Qura University]], at a very young age.

In 2007, the Sheikh was appointed as the permanent [[Imam]] of [[Masjid al-Haram|Masjid al Haram]]. And since 2008, he has been leading the Taraweeh prayers in [[Mecca|Makkah]] during [[Ramadan]].

Abdullah Awad al Juhani has been the [[Imam]] at [[Quba Mosque|Masjid Al Quba]], Masjid Al Jinn, [[Al-Masjid an-Nabawi|Masjid Al Nabawi]], and [[Masjid al-Qiblatayn|Masjid Al Qiblatain]].</ref>

=== Career ===
He became a TA from the Preparation of Teachers (Teaching School) whilst still in [[Medina]]. He used to supervise the teaching and memorization of the Qur'an in a [[Mosque|Masjid]] in the Western part of [[Medina]]. He led his first [[Salah]] in [[Al-Masjid an-Nabawi]] in the year 1998. He had been appointed to lead salah in one of the biggest Masjids in  [[Medina]], but [[Abdullah of Saudi Arabia|King Abdullah]] (whilst he was still [[Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia]]) appointed him to lead Salah in [[Al-Masjid an-Nabawi]].<ref group="note">Shaikh Al-Juhany Ph.D is one of the Imams of the Grand Mosque (Masjid al-Haram) in Mecca. He holds a bachelors degree from the faculty of Qur'an at The Islamic University of Madinah, and a doctorate (Ph.D) from Umm al-Qura University in Mecca. Juhany has led the Taraweeh prayers during Ramadan in Mecca since 2008, and is known for his emotional Quranic recitation style. His voice has been widely recorded and is internationally distributed by various communities. He graduated from Faculty of Qur'an at The Islamic University of Madinah with a degree in Qur'anic Studies. He was taught by Sheikh Muhammad Ayyub one of the imams of Masjid an-Nabawi. He then went on to obtain his Masters and Doctorate (PhD) in Shariah and Quranic recitations from Umm al-Qura University in Mecca. He led his first Salah in Al-Masjid an-Nabawi in the year 1998. He was appointed as an imam of Masjid Al Haram, Mecca in July 2007.</ref>

He was appointed as an imam of [[Masjid Al Haram]], [[Mecca]] in July 2007. He is considered as the only Imam who has been an Imam in [some] of the biggest masjids of the Muslim world. He is or has been an imam of [[Masjid Al Haram]] in [[Mecca]], Masjid Al Nabawi in [[Medina]], Masjid Quba and Masjid Qiblatain.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?39593-Sheikh-Abdullah-Awwad-Al-Juhany-Imam-Of-Makkah%26highlight%3D |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2013-08-25 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923090940/http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?39593-Sheikh-Abdullah-Awwad-Al-Juhany-Imam-Of-Makkah&highlight= |archivedate=2015-09-23 }}</ref>
