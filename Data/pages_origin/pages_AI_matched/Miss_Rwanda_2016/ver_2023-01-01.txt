'''Miss Rwanda 2016''', the sixth edition of the [[Miss Rwanda]] pageant, was held on February 27, 2016 at Camp Kigali Grounds in the province of [[Kigali]].

The winner, Jolly Mutesi succeeded Doriane Kundwa, [[Miss Rwanda 2015]].<ref>{{cite web|language=en|author=Tashobya, Athan|url=http://www.newtimes.co.rw/section/article/2016-02-28/197492/|title=Jolly Mutesi is crowned Miss Rwanda 2016|work=The News Times|date=February 28, 2016}}.</ref> She entered [[Miss World 2016]] but she did not place. This is the first time Rwanda has competed in the [[Miss World]] contest.<ref>{{cite web|language=en|author=Kantengwa, Sharon|url=http://www.newtimes.co.rw/section/article/2016-12-20/206398/|title=Miss Rwanda misses out on Global crown|work=The New Times Rwanda|date=December 20, 2016}}.</ref> The first runner up, Peace Ndaruhutse Kwizera, won the title of Miss Naiades 2016.<ref>{{cite web|language=en|author=|url=http://www.newtimes.co.rw/section/article/2016-11-14/205337/|title=Kwizera crowned Miss Naiades 2016|work=The New Times Rwanda|date=November 14, 2016}}.</ref>

Miss Heritage Rwanda, Jane Mutoni, has finished 1st runner-up at Miss Heritage Global 2016.<ref>{{cite web|language=fr|author=|url=http://www.fr.igihe.com/divertissement/mutoni-jane-elue-premiere-dauphine-dans-la-miss.html|title=Mutoni Jane élue première dauphine dans la Miss Heritage Global Pageant|work=IGIHE|date=February 17, 2017}}.</ref>
