''' Nahachiv ''' ({{lang-uk|Нага́чів }}) is a village (''selo'') in [[Yavoriv Raion]], [[Lviv Oblast]], in southwest [[Ukraine]]. The village is at a distance of {{convert|70|km|mi}} from the city of [[Lviv]] and {{convert|12|km|mi}} from the city of [[Yavoriv]]. Its population is 3,738. Local government &ndash; Nahachivska Village Council. <ref>[http://rada.info/rada/04373086/ Нагачівська сільська рада] {{uk icon}}</ref>

==History==
The first historical mention of Nahachiv fixed in the 1456, <ref>[http://drymba.net/uk/1012491-selo-nahachiv Село Нагачів: карта вулиць, фото] {{uk icon}}</ref> but there were older &nbsp;- 12&nbsp;- 13th century. <br/>
In territory of village are preserved the ancient fortress mounts 6&nbsp;- 7th century. The oldest findings from the area are Palaeolithic (old stone age) is a stone ax.
