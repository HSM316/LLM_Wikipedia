'''Joanne Hélène Josephine''' "'''Joanneke'''" '''Kruijsen''' (10 January 1969 – 28 May 2016) was a Dutch politician and sustainability researcher. She was a member of the [[Labour Party (Netherlands)|Labour Party]] and served in the [[House of Representatives (Netherlands)|House of Representatives]] between 2003 and 2006.

==Career==
Kruijsen was born on 10 January 1969 in [[Nijmegen]].<ref name=parl>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.parlement.com/id/vge7dtpnavzo/j_h_j_joanneke_kruijsen |title=Dr. J.H.J. (Joanneke) Kruijsen |language=Dutch |publisher=Parlement.com |date= |accessdate=12 June 2016}}</ref> She grew up in [[Berg en Dal (village)|Berg en Dal]].<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.gelderlander.nl/regio/nijmegen-e-o/berg-en-dal/joanneke-komt-voor-de-laatste-keer-thuis-in-berg-en-dal-1.5762523 |title=Joanneke komt voor de laatste keer thuis in Berg en Dal |language=Dutch |publisher=De Gelderlander |date=25 February 2016 |accessdate=12 June 2016}}</ref> She studied public administration and industrial design at [[Delft University of Technology]] between 1987 and 1994. Kruijsen subsequently attended the [[Open University in the Netherlands]] from 1994 to 1995 to study environmental management.<ref name=parl/>

Kruijsen was elected in the [[2003 Dutch general election]] for the Labour Party. She was a member of the House of Representatives between 30 January 2003 and 30 November 2006, not being a candidate in the [[2006 Dutch general election]].<ref name=parl/> During her time in the House she was mainly concerned with environmental policy.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.parlement.com/id/vk4paq09fcr5/nieuws/oud_pvda_tweede_kamerlid_joanneke |title=Oud-PvdA-Tweede Kamerlid Joanneke Kruijsen overleden |language=Dutch |publisher=Parlement.com |date=6 June 2016 |accessdate=12 June 2016}}</ref>

After her time in politics Kruijsen owned her own shop in sustainable clothing. In 2010 she moved to Scotland, where she became a researcher and later fellow at the Center for Understanding Sustainable Practice at [[Robert Gordon University]] in [[Aberdeen]].<ref name=parl/> At the Center she undertook research on behavioural change and sustainable practice.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.rgu.ac.uk/dmstaff/kruijsen-joanneke/ |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151016183652/http://www.rgu.ac.uk/dmstaff/kruijsen-joanneke |title=Profile Dr. Joanneke Kruijsen |publisher=Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences |date= |archive-date=16 October 2015 |accessdate=12 June 2016}}</ref>

She died in [[Huntly]], Scotland on 28 May 2016.<ref name=parl/>
