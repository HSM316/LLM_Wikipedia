'''Al-Sabkhah''' ({{langx|ar|السبخة}})  is a [[Syrian]] [[town]] located in [[Raqqa District]], [[Raqqa Governorate|Raqqa]]. According to the [[Central Bureau of Statistics (Syria)|Syria Central Bureau of Statistics]] (CBS), '''Al-Sabkhah''' had a population of 11,567 in the 2004 census.<ref>{{cite web|title=General Census of Population 2004.|url=https://view.officeapps.live.com/op/view.aspx?src=https://www.humanitarianresponse.info/sites/www.humanitarianresponse.info/files/syr_pop_2004_sycensus_0.xls |access-date= 2017-10-20}}</ref>
