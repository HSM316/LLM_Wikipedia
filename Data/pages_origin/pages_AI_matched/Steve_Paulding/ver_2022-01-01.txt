'''Steven C Paulding''' is a [[Wales|Welsh]] former competitive [[track cyclist]] and [[British Cycling]] track team manager. He lived in Scotland for many years, and has worked for sportscotland, the Scottish Government, Scottish Golf and currently works for British Athletics in Loughborough.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://webcache.googleusercontent.com/search?q=cache:CDHKFB-70swJ:www.scuonline.org/downloads.asp%3FfileID%3D430+%22Steve+Paulding%22&hl=en&ct=clnk&cd=5&gl=uk&client=firefox-a| title=Scottish Sports Hall of Fame}}</ref> He represented Wales in the [[1986 Commonwealth Games|1986]] and [[1990 Commonwealth Games]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.thecgf.com/games/athlete.asp |title=The CGF - Athlete search |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://archive.today/20020810160403/http://www.thecgf.com/games/athlete.asp |archive-date=10 August 2002 |df=dmy-all }}</ref> He now lives in [[Leicestershire]] and married the commonwealth cyclist [[Julie Paulding|Julie Anne Forrester]] in October 2001.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://archive.wirralglobe.co.uk/2002/8/8/4910.html |title=Now for the World! |publisher=Wirral Globe| date=8 August 2002}}</ref>
