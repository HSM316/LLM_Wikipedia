'''E. C. Stearns Bicycle Agency''' was established in 1893 by industrialist [[Edward C. Stearns]], who began business as a hardware manufacturer and branched out into bicycle production from 1893 through 1899.<ref name="agency">{{cite web|url=http://www.thewheelmen.org/sections/bicyclebrands/showresults.asp?whichpage=54&pagesize=50&alphachar=All%20Brands |title=Bicycle Brands Home Page |accessdate=January 7, 2011 |work=|publisher=The Wheelmen, 2010}}</ref>
