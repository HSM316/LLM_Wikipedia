'''José Luis Varela Lagunas''' (born 21 December 1946) is a [[Mexican people|Mexican]] politician formerly affiliated with the [[Citizens' Movement (Mexico)|Citizens' Movement]]. As of 2014 he served as Deputy of the [[LX Legislature of the Mexican Congress]] representing [[Oaxaca]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Perfil del legislador|url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=2300857|publisher=Legislative Information System|accessdate=13 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Renuncia Varela Lagunas al Partido Convergencia|url=http://ciudadania-express.com/2010/06/30/renuncia-varela-lagunas-al-partido-covergencia/|accessdate=13 October 2014|date=30 June 2010}}</ref>
