'''[[Suriname]]''' sent a delegation to compete at the '''[[2008 Summer Paralympics]]''' in [[Beijing]], [[People's Republic of China]]. According to official records,<ref>[http://results.beijing2008.cn/WRMP/ENG/BIO/Athlete/M.shtml Athletes & Teams], Official Website of the 2008 Paralympic Games</ref> the country's only athlete competed in athletics. Suriname did not win a medal at these Games.<ref>[http://results.beijing2008.cn/WRMP/ENG/INF/GL/95A/GL0000000.shtml "Overall Medal Standings"], official website of the 2008 Paralympics</ref>
