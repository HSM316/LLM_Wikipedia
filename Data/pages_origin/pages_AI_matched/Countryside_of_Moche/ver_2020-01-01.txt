The '''Countryside of [[Moche, Trujillo|Moche]]''' is an area of [[Trujillo, Peru|Trujillo]] city inherited from [[Moche (culture)|mochica culture]]. It is located in the [[Moche District]] about 4&nbsp;km south of the [[Historic Centre of Trujillo]]. Today it is a tourist area that presents recreational centers, agricultural areas and alongside this countryside.  The [[Huaca del Sol|Temples of the Sun and Moon]] is located in it. It is part of the [[Moche Valley|Moche]] or Santa Catalina valley and was a place of development in the Moche culture.

==Description==
In the countryside of Moche one can appreciate the lifestyle of [[Moche (culture)|moche]]ros, expressed in the manufacture of [[pottery]], leatherwork, wood and stone carvings, basketry, and others, as in its cuisine or gastronomy, expressed in local dishes like theologian soup, pepián of turkey, and regional as ceviche, goat, and duck stew. One can also appreciate the landscape where traditional growers highlight their crops and gardens.<ref>{{cite web |año = |url = http://huacasdemoche.pe/index.php?menuid=3&submenuid=12|title = (Spanish) La campiña de Moche |editor = |accessdate =29 April 2012}}</ref> The farmers of the countryside of Moche celebrate the festival of San Isidro Labrador.<ref>{{cite web|año = |url = http://www.cholonautas.edu.pe/modulo/upload/JOS%C3%89%20F.%20ELIAS%20MINAYA.pdf|title = (Spanish)San Isidro Labrador y la agricultura en la Campiña de Moche|editor = |accessdate = November 23, 2012}}{{Dead link|date=July 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>
