The '''2020 USL League One season''' will be the leagues second season. The regular season will consist of a 28–game schedule will began on March 27 and will end on October 3. The top six teams will make the playoffs, with the top two teams qualifying for the Semifinals. The First Round will be scheduled for October 9–11, the Semifinals on October 16–18 and the League Final on October 22–25. 9 teams<ref>[[Lansing Ignite]] ceased operations at the end of the 2019 season.</ref> return from the inaugural season, while 3 new teams enter for the first time; MLS reserve teams [[Inter Miami CF|Inter Maimi CF 2]] and [[New England Revolution|New England Revolution II]], and expansion club [[Union Omaha]]. [[North Texas SC]] return as defending champions.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uslleagueone.com/news_article/show/1072826 |title=2020 USL League One Schedule Revealed |publisher=USLLeagueOne.com Staff |date=December 20, 2019 |accessdate=December 20, 2019}}</ref>
