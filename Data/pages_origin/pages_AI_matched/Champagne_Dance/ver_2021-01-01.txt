"'''Champagne Dance'''" is a song by [[UK garage]] crew [[Pay As U Go]]. Released as a [[Single (music)|single]] in late 2001, it charted at number 13 on the [[UK Singles Chart]] in April 2002,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/search/singles/Champagne%20Dance|title=The Official Charts Company for Champagne Dance â€“ Pay As U Go Cartel|publisher=[[Official Charts Company]]|date=|accessdate=2011-10-31|df=dmy-all}}</ref> and remains the group's only charting hit single before disbanding with members going on to join [[Grime (music genre)|grime]] crew [[Roll Deep]].
