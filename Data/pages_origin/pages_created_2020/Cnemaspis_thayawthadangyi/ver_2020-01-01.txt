'''''Cnemaspis thayawthadangyi''''', the '''Thayawthadangyi Islands rock gecko''', is a species of [[Diurnality|diurnal]], rock-dwelling, [[insectivorous]] [[gecko]] endemic to  [[Myanmar]]. It is distributed in the [[Tanintharyi Region]].
