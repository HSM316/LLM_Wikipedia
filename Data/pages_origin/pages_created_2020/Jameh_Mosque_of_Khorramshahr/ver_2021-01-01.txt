The '''Jameh Mosque of Khorramshahr''' (also called Khorramshahr Central Mosque) ({{lang-fa|مسجد جامع خرمشهر}} – ''Masjid-e-Jāmeh Khorramshahr'') is the grand, congregational mosque ([[Jameh Mosque|Jāmeh]]) of [[Khorramshahr]] city, within [[Khorramshahr County]], [[Khuzestan Province]], [[Iran]]. The mosque is one of the historical places of Khorramshahr city. This building is a place where people of the city come there to worship together.<ref name=mjk1>

During [[Iran-Iraq War]], the mosque partially dilapidated. In times of war, this mosque was the center of command and logistics for the city's defenders.<ref name=mjk1/>

Jameh Mosque of Khorramshahr is the thirty thousandth historical monument of Iran, which was registered in the list of national monuments in 2011.<ref name=mjk1/><ref name=mjk3/>

It has two finial with a height of 28 meters from the ground floor and two large and small domes. An air conditioning and central cooling system have been created.<ref name=mjk4/><ref name=mjk5/>
