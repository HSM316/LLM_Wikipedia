{{Weightlifting at the 1964 Summer Olympics}}
The '''men's 67.5&nbsp;kg''' weightlifting competitions at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]] in [[Tokyo]] took place on 13 October at the [[Shibuya Public Hall]]. It was the tenth appearance of the lightweight class.<ref name="SR">{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/summer/1964/WLT/mens-lightweight.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418124707/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/summer/1964/WLT/mens-lightweight.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Weightlifting at the 1964 Tokyo Summer Games: Men's Lightweight |work=Sports Reference |access-date=31 December 2019}}</ref>
