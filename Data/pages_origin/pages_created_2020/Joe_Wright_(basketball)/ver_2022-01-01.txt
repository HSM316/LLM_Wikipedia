'''Joseph Eugene Wright''' (born April 1, 1963) is an American former professional [[basketball]] player. Wright holds the single season scoring record in the [[Úrvalsdeild karla (basketball)|Úrvalsdeild karla]] when he averaged 46.6 points per game during the 1992–1993 season. In 1992 he set the single game scoring record in the [[Korisliiga]] when he scored 79 points.<ref>{{cite web |title=Heildartölfræði einstaklinga á einu tímabili |url=http://gamli.kki.is/tolfraedi_einst_a_timab_medal.asp?Adgerd=NyRodun&Magn=10&RadaEftir=Stig&Timabil=OllTimabil&Lid=OllLid |website=kki.is |publisher=[[Icelandic Basketball Federation]] |accessdate=31 July 2018 |language=Icelandic}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Joe Wright - Tölfræði |url=http://gamli.kki.is/tolfraedi_ferill_leikmanns.asp?Felaganumer=114085 |website=kki.is |publisher=[[Icelandic Basketball Federation]] |accessdate=31 July 2018 |language=Icelandic}}</ref><ref name="Korisliiga-records"/>

==College career==
Wright played college basketball for [[Kansas State Wildcats men's basketball|Kansas State University]]. On January 23, 1985, he made 11 of 11 field goals against [[Oklahoma State Cowboys men's basketball|Oklahoma State]], setting a [[Big 8 Conference]] record.<ref>{{cite web |title=Joe Wright - State Fair Community College |url=https://sfccmoroadrunners.com/information/joewright |website=sfccmoroadrunners.com |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> Wright was named Big 8 Player of the Week in both 1985 and 1986.

==Club career==
Wright started his professional career in [[Austria]] in 1988. He played in [[Germany]] before signing with [[Turun NMKY]] in Finland in 1991.<ref>{{cite news |author1=Skapti Hallgrímsson |title=Ferðast um landið í vor |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=1779532 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=2 February 1993 |page=B2 |language=Icelandic}}</ref>

===Finland===
Wright played [[Turun NMKY]] for one and a half season. In 1991–1992 he led the [[Korisliiga]] in scoring, averaging a league leading 44.1 points per game and finishing second in three point percent with 47,1%<ref>{{cite news |title=Stórskytta til Breiðabliks |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=1778037 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=7 January 1993 |language=Icelandic}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Stigakóngur Finnlands til Blika |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2607440 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=5 January 1993 |language=Icelandic}}</ref><ref name="Korisliiga-records">{{cite web |title=Sarjan historia |url=https://korisliiga.fi/korisliiga/historia/ |website=korisliiga.fi |publisher=[[Korisliiga]] |accessdate=1 January 2020 |language=Finnish}}</ref>

On February 12, 1992, Wright set the Korisliiga single game scoring record when he scored 79 points against [[Pantterit]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Antti Kanervo mässäili divaripelissä: 64 pistettä |url=https://www.is.fi/koripallo/art-2000000006107.html?nomobile=2 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Ilta-Sanomat]] |date=16 March 2008 |language=Finnish}}</ref><ref name="Korisliiga-records"/>

===Iceland===
Wright joined Úrvalsdeild club [[Breiðablik (basketball)|Breiðablik]] in January 1993.<ref>{{cite news |title=Joe Wright mættur til Breiðabliks |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=4070539 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=Tíminn |date=7 January 1993 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> In his first game, he scored 55 points in a 108-110 loss against [[Keflavík men's basketball|Keflavík]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Wright skoraði 55 stig |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2607686 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=11 January 1993 |language=Icelandic}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Joe Wright með 55 stig |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=4070587 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=Tíminn |date=12 January 1993 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> and followed it up by scoring 53 points against [[Haukar men's basketball|Haukar]] in his next game.<ref>{{cite news |title=Joe Wright með annan stórleikinn |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2607815 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=15 January 1993 |page=16 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> On 29 January, Wright led Breiðablik to its second victory of the season by scoring 67 points against [[Njarðvík men's basketball|Njarðvík]],<ref>{{cite news |title=Joe Wright med 67 stig fyrir UBK |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=1779405 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=30 January 1993 |page=47 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> the second highest single game scoring in the Úrvalsdeild karla history.<ref>{{cite news |author1=Óskar Ófeigur Jónsson |title=Met á dag |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2979584 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=21 January 1999 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> In his first five games, he averaged 52.8 points per game.<ref>{{cite news |title=Verður Wright stigahæstur? |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2608519 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=2 February 1993 |page=16 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> In February, he was selected for the Icelandic All-Star game.<ref>{{cite news |title=Stjörnuliðin valin |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=1780125 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=12 February 1993 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> For the season he averaged a league leading 44.1 points per game but was unable to help Breiðablik stave of relegation.

In September 1994, Wright Joined [[Grindavík men's basketball|Grindavík]]<ref>{{cite news |author1=Ægir Már Kárason |title=Joe Wright snýr aftur |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2718499 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=2 September 1994 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> and played with them two games against [[Borås Basket|M7 Basket]] in the [[FIBA Korać Cup]].<ref>{{cite news |author1=Ægir Már Kárason |title=Gott að fara heim með 12 stig |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2718666 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=7 September 1994 |pages=16–17 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> In the two games he scored 33 and 27 points.<ref>{{cite news |author1=Skúli Unnar Sveinsson |title=Grindvíkingar langt frá sínu besta |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=1813253 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=7 September 1994 |page=C4 |language=Icelandic}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author1=Eyjólfur Harðarson |title=Tæpt hjá Grindavík |url=http://timarit.is/view_page_init.jsp?pageId=2718861 |accessdate=31 July 2018 |work=[[Dagblaðið Vísir]] |date=12 September 1994 |page=19 |language=Icelandic}}</ref>
