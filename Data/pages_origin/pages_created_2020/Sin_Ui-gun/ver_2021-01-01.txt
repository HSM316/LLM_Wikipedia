'''Sin Ui-gun''' ({{lang-ko|신의근}}; born 1 October 1958) is a North Korean [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Sin was the head coach of the [[North Korea women's national football team|North Korea women's national team]] at the [[Football at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|2012 Summer Olympics]].
