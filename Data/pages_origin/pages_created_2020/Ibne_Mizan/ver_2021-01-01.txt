'''Ibne Mizan''' (7 October 1930 – 28 March 2017) was a [[Bangladesh]]i film director. He directed many films. He was known for directing folk fantasy films.<ref name=a/><ref name=b/><ref name=c/> His sister [[Mokbula Manzoor]] is an author.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sirajganj.gov.bd/site/page/664d1e7b-1ab0-11e7-8120-286ed488c766/প্রখ্যাত%20ব্যক্তিত্ব
|title=প্রখ্যাত ব্যক্তিত্ব|accessdate=1 January 2020|website=www.sirajganj.gov.bd|language=bn
}}</ref>

==Biography==
Mizan was born on 7 October1930 in [[Sirajganj District|Sirajganj]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://archive1.ittefaq.com.bd/print-edition/entertainment/2017/03/29/185433.html

|date=29 March 2017|accessdate=1 January 2020|website=Ittefaq|language=bn
}}</ref> He was a student of [[Dhaka University]]'s Bangla department.<ref name=a>{{cite web|url=http://web.dailyjanakantha.com/details/article/135213/সিনেমার-ইবনে-মিজান/print/

|date=6 August 2015|accessdate=1 January 2020|website=Janakantha|language=bn
