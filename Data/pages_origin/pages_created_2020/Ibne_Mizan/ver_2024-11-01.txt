'''Ibne Mizan''' (7 October 1930 – 28 March 2017) was a Bangladeshi film director. He directed many films. He was known for directing folk fantasy films.<ref name=a/><ref name=b/><ref name=c/> His sister [[Mokbula Manzoor]] is an author.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sirajganj.gov.bd/site/page/664d1e7b-1ab0-11e7-8120-286ed488c766/প্রখ্যাত%20ব্যক্তিত্ব
|script-title=bn:প্রখ্যাত ব্যক্তিত্ব|access-date=1 January 2020|website=www.sirajganj.gov.bd|language=bn
}}</ref>
