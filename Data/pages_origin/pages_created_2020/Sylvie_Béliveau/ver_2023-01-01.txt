'''Sylvie Béliveau''' (born 8 December 1963) is a Canadian [[Manager (association football)|soccer coach]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Béliveau was the head coach of the [[Canada women's national soccer team|Canada women's national team]] at the [[1995 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
