The '''Alydar Stakes''' is an American [[Thoroughbred horse race]] held annually in early August at [[Saratoga Race Course]] in [[Saratoga Springs, New York]]. Made a [[Graded stakes race|Listed race]] in 2019, it is contested on dirt at a distance of a mile and one-eighth. It is open to four-year-old non-winners in the current year of a Sweepstake other than a State-bred event.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nyra.com/saratoga/racing/stakes-schedule/alydar/ |title=Alydar Stakes |publisher=[[New York Racing Association|NYRA]] |date=2019-08-03 |accessdate=2019-08-03}}</ref> <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thoroughbreddailynews.com/pdf/tdn/tdn190802.pdf |title=North American Racing |publisher=[[Thoroughbred Daily News]], page 26 |date=2019-08-02 |accessdate=2019-08-03}}</ref>
