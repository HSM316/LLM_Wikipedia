'''Ramazan Emirhan Civelek''' (born 5 January 2000) is a [[Turkey|Turkish]] professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|rightback]] for [[Sakaryaspor]] on loan from [[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]].

==Professional career==
On 14 July 2019, Civelek joined [[Kayserispor]] on a season-long loan from [[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnnturk.com/spor/futbol/emirhan-civelek-kayserisporda|title=Emirhan Civelek Kayserispor'da|website=CNN Türk}}</ref> Civelek made his professional debut for Kayserispor in a 6-2 [[Süper Lig]] loss to [[Trabzonspor]] on 28 December 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ca.soccerway.com/matches/2019/12/28/turkey/super-lig/trabzonspor/kayserispor/3081926/|title=Trabzonspor vs. Kayserispor - 28 December 2019 - Soccerway|website=ca.soccerway.com}}</ref>
