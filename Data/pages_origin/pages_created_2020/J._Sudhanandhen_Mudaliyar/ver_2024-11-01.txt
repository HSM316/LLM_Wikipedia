'''J. Sudhanandhen Mudaliyar''' (born 21 September 1944) was an Indian philanthropist, educationalist, and textile merchant.

==Early life==
Sudhanandhen was born into the Sengunthar Kaikola Mudaliyar (Mayan Kothiram) family in 1944. He completed his schooling at Kalaimagal Kalvi Nilayam, Erode. He continued his high school at Sengunthar Higher Secondary School, Erode. He completed his PUC at [[PSG College of Arts and Science]], then he graduated B.Sc. from [[Presidency College, Chennai|The New College]], Madras. He was an adherent follower of [[M. P. Nachimuthu|M.P. Nachimuthu Mudaliar]], a social activist.<ref>{{cite web|title= about the founder|url= https://www.mpnmjec.ac.in/About/aboutFounder.html|website= MPNMJ college|publisher= MPNMJ college|location= Erode district|language= English|access-date= 2020-01-01|archive-date= 2020-01-01|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20200101095239/https://www.mpnmjec.ac.in/About/aboutFounder.html|url-status= dead}}</ref>
