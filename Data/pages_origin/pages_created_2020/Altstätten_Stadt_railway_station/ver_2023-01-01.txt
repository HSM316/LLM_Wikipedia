'''Altstätten Stadt railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Altstätten Stadt}}) is a railway station in [[Altstätten]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is the eastern terminus of the {{RailGauge|1000 mm|allk=on}} [[Altstätten–Gais railway|Altstätten–Gais line]] and is served by local trains only. 

It is located in the center of the town. The railway station [[Altstätten SG railway station|Altstätten SG]] is an intermediate stop on the {{RailGauge|standard|allk=on}} [[Chur–Rorschach railway|Chur–Rorschach line]] with additional local and long-distance services. The stations are approximately {{convert|1.6|km}} apart and linked by bus.
