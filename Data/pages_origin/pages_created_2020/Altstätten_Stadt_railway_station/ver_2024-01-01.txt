'''Altstätten Stadt railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Altstätten Stadt}}) is a railway station in [[Altstätten]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is the eastern terminus of the {{RailGauge|1000 mm|allk=on}} [[Altstätten–Gais railway|Altstätten–Gais line]] and is served by local trains only. 

== Connections ==
The station is located near the center of the town and ca. {{cvt|1.6|km}} to the west of [[Altstätten SG railway station]], which is an intermediate stop on the {{RailGauge|standard|allk=on}} [[Chur–Rorschach railway|Chur–Rorschach line]] with additional local and long-distance services. The two stations are linked by bus lines {{Bahnlinie|S|300|300|white|#d1281e|#d1281e|Rheintalbus bus line 300}} and {{Bahnlinie|S|301|301|white|#933666|#933666|Rheintalbus bus line 301}} of [[:de:Rheintalbus|Rheintalbus]].<ref>{{cite web |title=''Region Altstätten'' [regional bus routes of Altstätten] |url=https://www.rtb.ch/fileadmin/rtb/reisen/liniennetzplaene/2023/Liniennetzplan_Region_Altstaetten.pdf |access-date=2023-12-26}}</ref> 

== Services ==
Altstätten Stadt is served by the S24 of the [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf856">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/856.pdf | title = Gais - Altstätten Stadt  | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=24 October 2019 | accessdate = 1 January 2020}}</ref>

* {{rcb|St. Gallen S-Bahn|24|inline=croute}}: hourly service to [[Gais railway station|Gais]].
