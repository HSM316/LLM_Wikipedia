== Early years and education ==
Afework was supposed to study nursing for two years. But in the middle of her studies, she quit to study Fashion.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbesafrica.com/woman/2015/04/01/singer-seamstress-survivor/|title=Singer. Seamstress. Survivor.|last=Africa|first=Forbes Woman|date=2015-04-01|website=Forbes Africa|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

== Career ==
Afework began her career at age 16 as a model and musician. While in grade 9, she took interest in rap music and released a single titled ''Shalom Africa.'' Aside getting a deal from Yosef Gebre Jossy, a famous recording artiste in Addis Ababa, to record an album, she was also given the opportunity to design clothes for his music video, ''Jossy In Z House.''<ref name=":2" /> Through Google and youtube videos, she learned Fashion. In 2011, she founded ''Mafi Mafi,'' an Ethiopian fashion label. She uses hand-woven fabrics made by women from Ethiopia's rural areas to create clothing and accessories.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.industrieafrica.com/designer/Mafi-Mafi|title=Mafi Mafi - Ethiopia {{!}} Industrie Africa|website=www.industrieafrica.com|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref> She has collaborated with cult UK designer [[Markus Lupfer]] and has exhibited at [[London College of Fashion|London college of fashion]], European Fashion Day in Addis and Africa Fashion Week New York.<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://theculturetrip.com/africa/ethiopia/articles/10-top-fashion-designers-to-know-from-ethiopia/|title=10 Top Fashion Designers to Know from Ethiopia|last=Tafesse|first=Serkalem|website=Culture Trip|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

In 2014, she gave a [[TED (conference)|TED Talk]] about Ancient tradition and modern fashion.<ref>{{Citation|title=Ancient tradition / modern fashion {{!}} Mahlet Afework {{!}} TEDxPlaceDesNations|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-w7dBg9GIL8|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

* 2012 - She won the Origin Africa‘s design award at African Fashion Week New York 2012<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":3" />
