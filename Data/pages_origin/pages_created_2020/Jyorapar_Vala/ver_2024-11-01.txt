'''Jyorapar Vala''' is a village in [[Ujhani]] [[Tehsil]] and [[Budaun district]], [[Uttar Pradesh]], India. [[Budaun railway station]] is 28 KM away from the village.<ref>{{cite web |title=Jyorapar Vala, Ujhani Village information {{!}} Soki.In |url=https://soki.in/jyorapar-vala-ujhani-budaun |website=soki.in}}</ref> The village is administrated by [[Gram Panchayat]]. Its village code is 128527.<ref>{{cite web |title=BhuLekh |url=http://upbhulekh.gov.in/public/village_list_new.jsp?pageId=RGPPR&dcode=149&tcode=00782&tname=%E0%A4%AC%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%82%E0%A4%81&dname=%E0%A4%AC%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%82%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%82 |website=upbhulekh.gov.in}}</ref> According to [[2011 Census of India]] the total population of the village is 4,126, in which 2,190 are males and 1,936 are females.
