'''Chen Kue-sen''' ({{zh|t=陳桂森|s=陈桂森|first=t,s}}; born 30 June 1947) is a Taiwanese [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]]. He competed at the [[1968 Summer Olympics]] and the [[1972 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ch/chen-kue-sen-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418050518/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ch/chen-kue-sen-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Chen Kue-sen Olympic Results |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

==Biography==
Chen is from [[Yanping Township]].<ref name="Economic1967-10-12">{{cite news |date=1967-10-12 |title=舉重賽畢四項 高清發等奪魁 |trans-title=Weightlifting Competition Results: Gao Jing-Fa Wins Four Events |language=zh |newspaper={{ill|Economic Daily News|zh|經濟日報 (臺灣)}} |page=6 }}</ref> He competed in the [[1966 Asian Games]], the [[1968 Summer Olympics]], and the [[1970 Asian Games]].<ref name="UDN1970-12-11">{{cite news |date=1970-12-11 |title=亞運特輯 雛量級舉重 陳桂森出師 |trans-title=Asian Games Special Edition: Featherweight Weightlifting: Chen Kue-sen Takes the Stage |language=zh |newspaper=[[United Daily News]] |page=3 }}</ref> He did high-altitude training at the mountain [[Hehuanshan]] in 1968.<ref name="UDN1968-09-18">{{cite news |date=1968-09-18 |title=臨時槓鈴 |trans-title=Makeshift Barbell |language=zh |newspaper=[[United Daily News]] |page=6 }}</ref> During the Olympics qualification round on 14 August 1968, he lifted a total of {{convert|330|kg|lb}}, which set a [[list of Taiwanese records in athletics|national record]].<ref name="UDN1968-08-15">{{cite news |date=1968-08-15 |title=舉重決選結束 陳桂森創紀錄 |trans-title=Weightlifting Finals Concluded; Chen Kue-sen Sets New Record |language=zh |newspaper=[[United Daily News]] |page=6 }}</ref> By 1970, he was the holder of three national records: as a [[flyweight]], he did a single lift of {{convert|88.4|kg|lb}}; as a [[bantamweight]], he did a single lift of {{convert|110|kg|lb}}; as a bantamweight, he had a total lift of {{convert|330|kg|lb}}.<ref name="UDN1970-12-11"/> Chen qualified for the [[1971 Asian Weightlifting Championships]].<ref name="UDN1970-09-28">{{cite news |date=1970-09-28 |title=亞運舉重決選 兩人達到標準 李秋河抓舉破全國紀錄 羽球單打名次排出 |trans-title=Asian Games Weightlifting Finals: Two Athletes Meet Standards Li Qiu-hua Breaks National Record in Snatch; Badminton Singles Rankings Announced |language=zh |newspaper=[[United Daily News]] |page=6 }}</ref> He served as referee at the championship match of the men's Group A competition of the [[1993 World Weightlifting Championships]].<ref name="Bao1993-11-25">{{cite news |date=1993-11-25 |title=陳桂森執法 有A級水準 |trans-title=Chen Kue-sen's Refereeing Reaches A-Level Standards |language=zh |newspaper=[[Min Sheng Bao]] |page=3 }}</ref> Huang served as a member of the [[Asian Weightlifting Federation]]'s Statistics Committee ({{zh|t=統計委員會}}) in 1994.<ref name="Huang1994-11-13">{{cite news |last=Huang |first=Cheng-fu 黃承富 |date=1994-11-13 |title=體育簡訊 世界杯高球賽 中華隊暫居21名 |trans-title=Sports Brief: In the World Cup Golf Tournament, the Taiwanese team is currently in 21st place |language=zh |newspaper=[[Min Sheng Bao]] |page=4 }}</ref>
