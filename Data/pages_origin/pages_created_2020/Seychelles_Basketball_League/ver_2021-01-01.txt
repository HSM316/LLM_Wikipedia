The '''Seychelles Basketball League (SBL)''' is the premier [[basketball]] league for clubs in [[Seychelles]]. The league consist out of twelve teams. As of December 2019, the defending champions are the Beau Vallon Heat.

The champions of the SBL are eligible to play in the qualifying rounds of the [[Basketball Africa League]] (BAL).
