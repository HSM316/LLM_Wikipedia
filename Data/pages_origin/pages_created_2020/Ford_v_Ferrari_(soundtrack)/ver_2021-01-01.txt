'''''Ford v Ferrari''''' is a 2019 [[Sports film|sports]] [[Action film|action]] [[Drama (film and television)|drama film]] directed by [[James Mangold]] and distributed by [[20th Century Fox]]. Two soundtrack albums for the film were released on November 15, 2019.<ref name="Variety">{{cite magazine |url=https://variety.com/2019/music/news/ford-v-ferrari-soundtrack-matt-damon-exclusive-1203403226/ |title=‘Ford v Ferrari’ Soundtrack Revealed |magazine=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |date=November 13, 2019 |accessdate=January 1, 2020}}</ref><ref name="Deadline">{{cite web |url=https://deadline.com/2019/11/ford-v-ferrari-composers-marco-beltrami-buck-sanders-james-mangold-interview-news-1202788088/ |title=How Composers Marco Beltrami & Buck Sanders Captured Energy & Excitement Of The Racetrack With ‘Ford V Ferrari’ Score |first=Matt |last=Grobar |website=[[Deadline Hollywood]] |date=November 25, 2019 |accessdate=January 1, 2020}}</ref>

'''''Ford v Ferrari: Original Motion Picture Soundtrack''''' is the official soundtrack of ''[[Ford v Ferrari]]'' released by [[Hollywood Records]] on November 15, 2019. The album consists of classic 1950s and 1960s music along with three score tracks by [[Marco Beltrami]] and [[Buck Sanders]].<ref name="Variety"/>
