'''Ümran Zambak''' (born 27 November 2001\0) is a Dutch professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Kayserispor]] .

==Professional career==
On 3 September 2019, Zambak signed his first professional contract with [[Kayserispor]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://kayserispor.org/transferin-son-gununde-2-imza.html|title=Transferin Son Gününde 2 İmza &#124; KAYSERİSPOR.ORG}}</ref> Zambak made his professional debut for Kayserispor in a 6-2 [[Süper Lig]] loss to [[Trabzonspor]] on 28 December 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ca.soccerway.com/matches/2019/12/28/turkey/super-lig/trabzonspor/kayserispor/3081926/|title=Trabzonspor vs. Kayserispor - 28 December 2019 - Soccerway|website=ca.soccerway.com}}</ref>
