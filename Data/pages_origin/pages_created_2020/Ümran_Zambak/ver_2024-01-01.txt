'''Ümran Zambak''' (born 27 November 2000) is a Dutch professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[SV Schermbeck]].<ref>{{cite tweet|number=1308718250335899654|user=MemleketLigleri|title=Yalovaspor, Somaspor'dan Burak Turan ve Kayserispor'dan genç forvet Ümran Zambak ile sözleşme imzaladı.…<!-- full text of tweet that Twitter returned to the bot (excluding links) added by TweetCiteBot. This may be better truncated or may need expanding (TW limits responses to 140 characters) or case changes. --> |date=23 September 2020}}</ref> He has also [[Turkey|Turkish]] nationality.

==Professional career==
On 3 September 2019, Zambak signed his first professional contract with [[Kayserispor]].<ref>{{in lang|tr}} {{Cite web|url=https://kayserispor.org/transferin-son-gununde-2-imza.html|title=Transferin Son Gününde 2 İmza &#124; KAYSERİSPOR.ORG}}</ref> He signed for five years.<ref name="tubantia">{{in lang|nl}} {{Cite web|url=https://www.tubantia.nl/sport-regionaal/droom-komt-uit-voor-almeloer-met-debuut-in-turkse-super-lig~a02db264/|title=Droom komt uit voor Almeloër met debuut in Turkse Süper Lig - 10 January 2020 - TFF|website=www.tubantia.nl}}</ref> In his professional debut game in a [[Turkish Cup]] tie against [[Bayrampaşa SK|Bayrampaşa]] he scored the 1-0.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tff.org/Default.aspx?pageId=528&macId=217744|title=İstikbal Mobilya Kayserispor vs Bayrampaşa - 31 October 2019 - TFF|website=www.tff.org}}</ref> Zambak made his [[Süper Lig]] debut for Kayserispor in a 6-2 loss to [[Trabzonspor]] on 28 December 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ca.soccerway.com/matches/2019/12/28/turkey/super-lig/trabzonspor/kayserispor/3081926/|title=Trabzonspor vs. Kayserispor - 28 December 2019 - Soccerway|website=ca.soccerway.com}}</ref><ref name="tubantia"/>
