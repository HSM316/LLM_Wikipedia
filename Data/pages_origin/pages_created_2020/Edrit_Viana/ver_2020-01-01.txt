'''Edrit Jaqueline Viana Silva''' (born 12 January 1996) is a Uruguayan [[women's association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]]. She has been a member of the [[Uruguay women's national football team|Uruguay women's national team]].

==Club career==
Viana played in Uruguay for [[Colón F.C.|Colón]] and [[Peñarol]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/245/0/|title=Colón Fútbol Club - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/68/4/|title=Club Atlético Peñarol - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/68/1/|title=Club Atlético Peñarol - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/68/5/|title=Club Atlético Peñarol - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
Viana played for Uruguay at senior level in the [[2014 Copa América Femenina]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/con-una-llamativa-ceremonia-se-inauguro-la-vii-copa-america-femenina|title=Con una llamativa ceremonia, se inauguró la VII Copa América Femenina|website=Conmebol|accessdate=30 December 2019|date=11 September 2014|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140914022911/http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/copa-america-femenina-colombia-golea-uruguay-4-0|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/copa-america-femenina-colombia-golea-uruguay-4-0|title=Copa América Femenina: Colombia golea a Uruguay 4-0|website=Conmebol|accessdate=30 December 2019|archive-date=14 September 2014}}</ref>
