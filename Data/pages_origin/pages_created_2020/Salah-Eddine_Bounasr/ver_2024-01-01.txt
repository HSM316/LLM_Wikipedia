'''Salah-Eddine Bounasr''' (born 27 September 1990) is a [[Morocco|Moroccan]] [[Long-distance running|long-distance runner]].

== Career ==

In 2016, he won the [[Guangzhou Marathon]] with a time of 2:11:09.<ref name="guangzhou_win_2016">{{Cite news|url=https://www.worldathletics.org/news/report/guangzhou-marathon-2016-bounasr-kassahun|title=Bounasr and Kassahun triumph in Guangzhou|date=11 December 2016|work=World Athletics|access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>

In 2017, he became the first Moroccan athlete to win the [[Beijing Marathon]].<ref name="bounasr_wins_beijing_2017">{{Cite news|url=https://www.worldathletics.org/news/report/bounasr-beyene-win-beijing-marathon|title=Bounasr and Beyene take top honours in Beijing|date=17 September 2017|work=World Athletics|access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>

In 2019, he won the [[Lake Biwa Marathon]] held in Ōtsu, Shiga, Japan with a new personal best of 2:07:52.<ref name="butterfield_breaks_record_2019">{{Cite news|url=http://www.royalgazette.com/runningtriathlon/article/20190311/butterfield-breaks-his-marathon-record|title=Butterfield breaks his marathon record|last=Wright|first=Stephen|date=11 March 2019|work=The Royal Gazette|access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>
