'''''Goodbye Naples''''' (Italian: '''''Addio, Napoli!''''') is a 1955 Italian [[drama film]] directed by [[Roberto Bianchi Montero]] and starring [[Tamara Lees]], [[Andrea Checchi]] and [[Giorgio De Lullo]].<ref>Chiti & Poppi p.19</ref>

The film's sets were designed by the [[art director]] [[Ivo Battelli]] and [[Alfredo Montori]].
