'''National Tertiary Route 906''', or just '''Route 906''' ({{lang-es|Ruta Nacional Terciaria 906}}, or {{lang|es|Ruta 906}}) is a [[National Road Network of Costa Rica|National Road Route]] of [[Costa Rica]], located in the [[Guanacaste Province|Guanacaste]] province. <ref name='geoportal'>{{cite web |title=GeoPortal |url=https://sig.mopt.go.cr:8084/geo/index.html |publisher=Ministerio de Obras Públicas y Transporte de Costa Rica |access-date=28 May 2020}}</ref> It is a road in the [[Nicoya Peninsula]], it starts and ends at [[National Route 150 (Costa Rica)|Route 150]].

==Description==
Currently a gravel road, Route 906 runs in parallel to Route 150 to the west. Together with [[National Route 929 (Costa Rica)|Route 929]] it is known as {{lang|es|Ruta de la Leche}}, the ''Milk Route''. The route visits the towns of Piave, Corralillo and San Lázaro.<ref>{{cite web |title=MOPT-CONAVI reactivan obras en Ruta de la Leche en Nicoya  MOPT-CONAVI reactivan obras en Ruta de la Leche en Nicoya |url=https://presidencia.go.cr/comunicados/2018/03/mopt-conavi-reactivan-obras-en-ruta-de-la-leche-en-nicoya/ |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>

In [[Guanacaste Province|Guanacaste]] province the route covers [[Nicoya (canton)|Nicoya]] canton ([[San Antonio District, Nicoya|San Antonio]] district).
