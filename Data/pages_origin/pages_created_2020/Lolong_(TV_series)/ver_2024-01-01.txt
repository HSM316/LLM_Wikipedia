'''''Lolong''''' (International title: ''Crocodile Whisperer'') is a 2022 [[Philippine television drama]] action series broadcast by [[GMA Network]]. Directed by Rommel P. Penesa, it stars [[Ruru Madrid]] in the title role. It premiered on July 4, 2022 on the network's Telebabad line up replacing ''[[First Lady (Philippine TV series)|First Lady]]''. The series concluded on September 30, 2022 with a total of 65 episodes. It was replaced by ''[[Maria Clara at Ibarra]]'' in its timeslot.

==Production==
Broadcast journalist [[Jessica Soho]] came up with the series' concept of conflict between humans and a crocodile, which was featured in her show ''[[Kapuso Mo, Jessica Soho]]''.<ref>{{cite news |title=Did the idea for action series 'Lolong' come from Jessica Soho? |url=https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/lifestyle/hobbiesandactivities/788499/did-the-idea-for-action-series-lolong-come-from-jessica-soho/story/ |access-date=June 2, 2021 |work=GMA News |date=June 2, 2021 |language=en}}</ref> ''Lolong'' is named after [[Lolong|a crocodile of the same name]], which was known as the world's largest crocodile in captivity.<ref>{{cite news |title=GMA Network raises the bar with nearly 100 new, cutting-edge programs |url=https://mb.com.ph/2021/04/14/gma-network-raises-the-bar-with-nearly-100-new-cutting-edge-programs/ |access-date=June 3, 2021 |work=Manila Bulletin |date=April 14, 2021}}</ref> A {{convert|6.7|m|ft|sp=us}} long [[animatronic]], enhanced by [[computer-generated imagery]] was used to depict the crocodile "Dakila".<ref>{{cite news |title=WATCH: 'Lolong' teaser introduces its 'highest paid actor,' the crocodile 'Dakila' |url=https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/showbiz/chikaminute/789636/lolong-teaser-introduces-its-highest-paid-actor-the-crocodile-dakila/story/ |access-date=June 3, 2021 |work=GMA News |date=May 31, 2021 |language=en}}</ref>

In October 2020, Filipino actress [[Sanya Lopez]] left the series due to joining the drama series ''[[First Yaya]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/guide/tv/154355/sanya-lopez-lolong-first-yaya |title=Sanya Lopez pulled out of Lolong to replace Marian Rivera in First Yaya |author=Anarcon, James Patrick |date=October 13, 2020 |access-date=May 31, 2021|work=PEP.ph|publisher=Philippine Entertainment Portal, Inc.}}</ref> [[Kim Domingo]] was also initially attached to appear in the series.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/guide/tv/154283/shaira-diaz-lolong |title=Shaira Diaz chosen as Ruru Madrid's leading lady; what happened to Sanya Lopez, Kim Domingo? |author=Anarcon, James Patrick |date=October 8, 2020 |access-date=May 31, 2021}}</ref> Principal photography commenced in July 2021 in [[Quezon]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lionheartv.net/2021/10/10-upcoming-gma-network-shows-already-started-their-lock-in-tapings-this-last-quarter-of-2021/ |title=10 upcoming GMA Network shows already started their lock-in tapings this last quarter of 2021 |author=Gacura, TJ |date=October 3, 2021 |access-date=October 14, 2021}}</ref> It was halted in August 2021 due to the enhanced community quarantine in National Capital Region caused by the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/pepalerts/pep-troika/159855/lolong-voltes-v-legacy-ecq-a4118-20210801 |title=Lolong at Voltes V: Legacy, tigil-taping dahil sa ECQ |date=August 1, 2021 |access-date=August 2, 2021}}</ref>
