'''Eryn Reece''' is an American bartender. She is the bar director for [[Banzabar]] and Freemans Restaurant, both in New York City. In 2013, Reece was named [[Speed Rack]] National Champion. In 2014, ''[[The Daily Meal]]'' named her one of the top 25 bartenders in the United States. Reece has been profiled in, and her work featured in, ''PUNCH'', ''[[The Daily Beast]]'', ''[[Maxim (magazine)|Maxim]]'',<ref name="Maxim1">{{cite web |title=Celebrate Negroni Week With 3 Killer Cocktails |url=https://www.maxim.com/food-drink/3-negroni-recipes-2019-6 |website=Maxim |date=25 June 2019 |accessdate=31 December 2019 |language=en-us}}</ref> [[WPIX]],<ref name="Mannarino">{{cite web |last1=Mannarino |first1=Dan |title=Hidden NY: Lower East Side alley leads to an unexpected surprise |url=https://pix11.com/2019/03/15/banzarbar-the-hidden-bar-at-freemans/ |website=WPIX |accessdate=31 December 2019 |language=en |date=15 March 2019}}</ref> ''[[The Spirits Business]]'',<ref name="Bellwood">{{cite web |last1=Bellwood |first1=Owen |title=High-end bars on pairing cocktails with food |url=https://www.thespiritsbusiness.com/2019/08/high-end-bars-on-pairing-cocktails-with-food/ |website=The Spirits Business |date=30 August 2019 |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> [[Thrillist]],<ref name="Pelz">{{cite web |last1=Pelz |first1=Rachel |title=Take It Easy (Or Drink Even More!) ==Early life and education==

Eryn Reece was born in [[Portland, Oregon]].<ref name="Rothbaum"/> She waitressed when she was a teenager. When she turned 21, she started bartending at Mona's Bistro in [[Seattle, Washington]].<ref name="Medsker">{{cite web |last1=Medsker |first1=Eric |title=Eryn Reece {{!}} Bar Director, Freemans Restaurant and Banzarbar |url=https://punchdrink.com/lookbook/bartender-eryn-reece-freemans-restaurant-banzarbar-nyc/ |website=PUNCH |accessdate=31 December 2019 |language=en}}</ref><ref name="Novak"/> She moved to New York City in 2007 to intern at an [[art gallery]] as part of her undergraduate studies.<ref name="Akkam">{{cite web|last1=Akkam|first1=Alia|date=2013-06-17|title=Raising the Bar: Eryn Reece|url=https://www.liquor.com/articles/raising-the-bar-eryn-reece/#gs.p9ru7w|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180110040001/https://www.liquor.com/articles/raising-the-bar-eryn-reece/|archive-date=2018-01-10|website=Liquor.com|accessdate=}}</ref>

==Career==

In New York City, Reece first bartended at MercBar, followed by Bar Milano.<ref name="Akkam"/> In a 2013 interview, Reece called her time at Bar Milano as "the most eye-opening experience of my life," after working with [[Tony Abou-Ganim]].<ref name="Akkam"/> She also worked at Louis 649, The Hideout, Dressler, Rye House, [[Mayahuel (bar)|Mayahuel]] and [[Death & Co.]]<ref name="Medsker"/><ref name="Akkam"/>  In 2013, Reece was named [[Speed Rack]] National Champion.<ref name="Rothbaum"/> The following year, ''[[The Daily Meal]]'' named her one of the top 25 best bartenders in the United States.<ref name="Novak">{{cite web |last1=Novak |first1=Jess |title=America's 25 Best Bartenders (Slideshow) |url=https://www.thedailymeal.com/drink/americas-25-best-bartenders-slideshow |website=The Daily Meal |accessdate=31 December 2019 |language=en |date=4 March 2014}}</ref>
As of 2019, she serves as bar director at Banzabar and Freemans Restaurant, both in the [[Lower East Side]]. At Banzabar, Reece prepares low alcohol cocktails to be paired with a [[tasting menu]] by chef Humberto Guallpa, as well as serving an Ã  la carte menu featuring beverages of various alcohol levels.<ref name="Medsker"/>

===Style, cocktails and influences===

One of Reece's signature drinks at Banzabar is Shackleton's Urn. The cocktail was inspired by the [[Cobra's Fang]] cocktail. It comprises two types of gin, rum, lime, passionfruit, ginger, cinnamon, and peach. It is served on crushed ice in an urn-shaped container with a lime lit on fire. The container was created specifically for the glass by a company in Portland, Oregon.<ref name="Rothbaum"/>

Reece refers to ''Flavor Bible'' by [[Karen A. Page]] and [[Andrew Dornenburg]] when creating new cocktails.<ref name="Rothbaum">{{cite web |last1=Rothbaum |first1=Noah |title=Johnny Cash, Anthony Bourdain & Tiki: Drinks With Eryn Reece |url=https://www.thedailybeast.com/going-10-rounds-with-top-bartender-eryn-reece-from-new-yorks-banzarbar-which-is-located-above-freemans-restaurant |website=The Daily Beast |accessdate=31 December 2019 |language=en |date=19 March 2019}}</ref> In ''PUNCH'', Reece shared that Tyson Buhler's rye whiskey cocktail, the Warrior Poet, was one of the best drinks she had ever had. The worst drink she has ever had is [[Amaro Sibilla]]. Her favorite bar is [[Good Night Sonny]] and her "go-to" drink at a cocktail bar is a [[Daiquiri]].<ref name="Medsker"/> In 2013, she named Joaquin Simo one of her favorite bartenders.<ref name="Novak"/>

==Personal life==

Reece lives in [[Manhattan]].<ref name="Medsker"/>
