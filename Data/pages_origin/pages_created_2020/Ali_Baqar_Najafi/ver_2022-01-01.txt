'''Ali Baqar Najafi''' (born 15 September 1963) is a Pakistani [[jurist]] and a senior Judge of the [[Lahore High Court]] since 16 April 2012 and member of [[Administration Committee]] of [[Lahore High Court]].

==Early life and education==
Najafi was born on 15 September 1963 in [[Lahore, Punjab]], Pakistan. His father Ali Huzoor Najafi was an advocate  [[Supreme Court of Pakistan]]. He completed his basic education from Muslim Model High School located in [[Urdu Bazaar (Lahore)|Urdu Bazaar, Lahore]]. He completed his [[LLB]] from University Law College [[University of the Punjab|Punjab University]], Lahore in 1989.<ref name="LHC">{{cite web|url=http://data.lhc.gov.pk/judges/sitting_judges|title=Hon'ble Sitting Judges - Lahore High Court|website=data.lhc.gov.pk|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

== Family life ==
He hails from a noble family, his father was a senior [[Advocate]] [[Supreme Court of Pakistan]], who enjoyed a high value and respect in legal fraternity and was an active [[Rotarian]] and member of [[Lahore Gymkhana]].

==Career==
In 1989, he started his legal career as an advocate under the mentorship of his father in Lahore. He registered as an advocate of High Courts in 1990. He continued on to become the advocate of Supreme Court of Pakistan.<ref name="LHC"/> He attended and read papers in many international conferences and seminars etc.

He was a faculty at Punjab University Law College, Lahore for twelve years. He also taught in private [[law college]]s for eighteen years. He also served as internal and external examiner for [[University of the Punjab]].<ref name="LHC"/>
