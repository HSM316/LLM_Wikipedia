==International career==
Andrei Sepci played at four games at international level for [[Romania national football team|Romania]], including three clean sheets in the successful [[1933 Balkan Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://eu-football.info/_player.php?id=19001|title=Andrei Sepci|publisher=European Football|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>
