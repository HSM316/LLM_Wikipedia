The '''2020 Men's EuroHockey Club Trophy II''' will be the 44th edition of the [[EuroHockey Club Trophy]], Europe's tertiary men's club [[field hockey]] tournament organized by the [[European Hockey Federation]] and the first edition since it was renamed from the EuroHockey Club Trophy.<ref>{{cite web |title=2018 EHF Announces Changes to EuroHockey Club Events |url=https://eurohockey.org/2018-ehf-announces-changes-to-eurohockey-club-events/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=2 November 2018}}</ref> It will be held in [[Lisburn]], Northern Ireland from 28 to 31 May 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=2020 EuroHockey Club Championships Venues and Teams Announced- Updated |url=https://eurohockey.org/2020-eurohockey-club-championships-venues-and-teams-announced/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=29 October 2019}}</ref>
