The '''2020 Men's EuroHockey Club Trophy II''' would have been the first edition of the [[EuroHockey Club Trophy I]], Europe's tertiary men's club [[field hockey]] tournament organized by the [[European Hockey Federation]].<ref>{{cite web |title=2018 EHF Announces Changes to EuroHockey Club Events |url=https://eurohockey.org/2018-ehf-announces-changes-to-eurohockey-club-events/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=2 November 2018}}</ref> It would be held in [[Lisburn]], Northern Ireland from 28 to 31 May 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=2020 EuroHockey Club Championships Venues and Teams Announced- Updated |url=https://eurohockey.org/2020-eurohockey-club-championships-venues-and-teams-announced/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=29 October 2019}}</ref>

The tournament was canceled on 23 March 2020 due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{cite web |title=EHF cancels all EuroHockey Club Trophy and Challenge events, 2020 |url=https://eurohockey.org/2020/03/24/ehf-cancels-all-eurohockey-club-trophy-and-challenge-events-2020/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=25 March 2020 |date=24 March 2020}}</ref>
