'''Meggen Zentrum railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Meggen Zentrum}}) is a railway station in the municipality of [[Meggen]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Lucerne|Lucerne]]. It is an intermediate stop on the standard gauge [[Lucerne–Immensee railway|Lucerne–Immensee line]] of [[Swiss Federal Railways]].

== Services ==
The following services stop at Meggen Zentrum:<ref name="fpf601">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/601.pdf | title = Luzern - Rotkreuz - Arth-Goldau - Erstfeld | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=5 November 2019 | accessdate = 31 December 2019}}</ref>

* [[Voralpen Express]]: hourly service between [[Lucerne railway station|Lucerne]] and [[St. Gallen railway station|St. Gallen]].
* [[Lucerne S-Bahn]] {{rcb|Lucerne S-Bahn|3|inline=croute}}: hourly service between Lucerne and [[Brunnen railway station|Brunnen]].
