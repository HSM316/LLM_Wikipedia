'''Meggen Zentrum railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Meggen Zentrum}}) is a railway station in the municipality of [[Meggen]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Lucerne|Lucerne]]. It is an intermediate stop on the standard gauge [[Lucerne–Immensee railway|Lucerne–Immensee line]] of [[Swiss Federal Railways]].<ref name="saw">{{Eisenbahnatlas Schweiz | page = 22}}</ref>

== Services ==
{{As of|alt=As of the December 2020 timetable change|2020|12|post=,}} the following services stop at Meggen Zentrum:<ref name="fpf601">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/601.pdf | title = Luzern - Küssnacht am Rigi - Arth-Goldau - Göschenen | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=3 November 2020 | accessdate = 1 January 2021}}</ref>

* [[InterRegio|{{Bahnlinie|S|S|IR|white|#F53327|#F53327}}]] ''[[Voralpen-Express]]'': hourly service between [[Lucerne railway station|Lucerne]] and [[St. Gallen railway station|St. Gallen]].
* [[Lucerne S-Bahn]] {{rcb|Lucerne S-Bahn|3|inline=croute}}: hourly service between Lucerne and [[Brunnen railway station|Brunnen]].
