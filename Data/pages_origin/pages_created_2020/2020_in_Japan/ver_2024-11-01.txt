Events in the year '''2020 in Japan'''.

The first year was largely defined by [[COVID-19 pandemic in Japan|COVID-19 pandemic]] that caused the [[Economic impact of the COVID-19 pandemic#Japan|national economy to go into recession]], and would continue until October 1, 2021 (when the fourth [[state of emergency]] ends). In addition to various historical events, such as the postponement of [[2020 Summer Olympics|Tokyo Olympics]] (until 2021) and with [[Shinzo Abe#Retirement|the end]] of [[Shinzo Abe]] era.
