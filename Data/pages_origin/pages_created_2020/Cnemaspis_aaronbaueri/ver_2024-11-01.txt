'''''Cnemaspis aaronbaueri''''' is a species of [[Diurnality|diurnal]], non rock-dwelling, [[insectivorous]] [[gecko]] endemic to  the [[Western Ghats]] of [[South India]]. It is distributed only in the [[Agasthyamalai]] hill range of [[Kerala]].
