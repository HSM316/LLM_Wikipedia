'''Herisau railway station''' ({{langx|de|Bahnhof Herisau}}) is a railway station in [[Herisau]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Appenzell Ausserrhoden]]. It is an intermediate station on the {{Track gauge|sg|allk=on}} [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway|Bodensee–Toggenburg line]] of [[Südostbahn]] and the {{Track gauge|1000mm|allk=on}} [[Gossau–Wasserauen railway|Gossau–Wasserauen line]] of [[Appenzell Railways]]. Both companies have separate tracks and facilities, separated by Bahnhofplatz.

== Layout ==
Herisau serves both the east–west {{Track gauge|sg|allk=on}} [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway|Bodensee–Toggenburg line]] of [[Südostbahn]] (SOB) and the north–south {{Track gauge|1000mm|allk=on}} [[Gossau–Wasserauen railway|Gossau–Wasserauen line]] of [[Appenzell Railways]] (AB).<ref name="saw">{{Eisenbahnatlas Schweiz | page = 14}}</ref> SOB has two platforms serving three tracks ({{Abbr|Nos.|Numbers}} 1–3) on the north side of Bahnhofplatz. AB is similarly situated, with two platforms serving tracks 11–13 south of Bahnhofplatz.{{sfn|Herisau|2017|p=14}}

A major project is underway to improve access to the station and redevelop parts of the site. This project will shift the Appenzell Railways tracks and platforms a short distance to the south, permitting the construction of a new bus loading area off Bahnhofplatz. Besides affording greater capacity, this loading area will be handicap-accessible. Traffic congestion will be further reduced by the construction of a [[roundabout]] at the intersection of Bahnhofplatz and Bahnhofstrasse, just west of the station. Construction work began in January 2021; it is estimated that the entire project will be complete by 2028.<ref>{{Cite news |last=Pagani |first=Alessia |date=13 January 2021 |title=Neugestaltung - Schritt für Schritt zum neuen Bahnhof – in Herisau haben die ersten Arbeiten begonnen |language=de |work=[[St. Galler Tagblatt|Tagblatt]] |url=https://www.tagblatt.ch/ostschweiz/appenzellerland/neugestaltung-schritt-fuer-schritt-zum-neuen-bahnhof-in-herisau-haben-die-ersten-arbeiten-begonnen-ld.2085185 |access-date=18 April 2021}}</ref>

== Services ==
{{As of|alt=As of the December 2023 timetable change|2023|12|post=,}} the following services stop at Herisau (most regional services are part of the [[Bodensee S-Bahn]]):<ref name="fpf870">{{cite web | url = https://www.xn--v-info-vxa.ch/sites/default/files/fap/2024/pdf/870.pdf | title = St. Gallen - Wattwil - Rapperswil | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=15 September 2023 | accessdate = 16 December 2023 | lang=de}}</ref><ref name="fpf854">{{cite web | url = https://www.öv-info.ch/sites/default/files/fap/2024/pdf/854.pdf | title = Gossau SG - Herisau - Appenzell - Wasserauen | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=15 September 2023 | accessdate = 16 December 2023}}</ref>

* [[InterRegio|{{Bahnlinie|S|S|IR|white|#F53327|#F53327}}]] ''[[Voralpen-Express]]'': hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg line between [[Lucerne railway station|Lucerne]] and [[St. Gallen railway station|St. Gallen]].
* [[RegioExpress]] {{ric|THURBO|RE1}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg line to [[Konstanz station|Konstanz]].
* [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:
** {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|2}} / {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|4}}: half-hourly service between {{rws|Wattwil}} and {{rws|Altstätten SG}} via {{rws|St. Gallen}} and hourly service to {{rws|Rapperswil}}, {{rws|Sargans}}, and {{rws|Nesslau-Neu St. Johann}}.
** {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|23}}: half-hourly service over the Gossau–Wasserauen line between [[Gossau SG railway station|Gossau SG]] and [[Wasserauen railway station|Wasserauen]].
** {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|81}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg line to [[St. Gallen railway station|St. Gallen]].

The S2 and S4 combine for half-hourly service between {{rws|Wattwil}} and Altstätten SG; the various services on the Bodensee–Toggenburg line provide one train approximately every 11 minutes between Herisau and St. Gallen.
