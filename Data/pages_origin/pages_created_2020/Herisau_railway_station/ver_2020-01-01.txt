'''Herisau railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Herisau}}) is a railway station in [[Herisau]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Appenzell Ausserrhoden|Appenzell Ausserrhoden]]. It is an intermediate station on the {{Track gauge|sg|allk=on}} [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]] and {{Track gauge|1000mm|allk=on}} [[Gossau–Wasserauen railway]]. It served by local and long-distance trains.

== Services ==
=== Long-distance ===
Herisau is served by the [[Voralpen Express]], which runs hourly between [[Lucerne railway station|Lucerne]] and [[St. Gallen railway station|St. Gallen]].<ref name="fpf870">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/870.pdf | title = Romanshorn - St. Gallen - Wattwil - Rapperswil | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=4 December 2019 | accessdate = 30 December 2019}}</ref>

=== Local ===
Herisau is served by five services of the [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf870" /><ref name="fpf854">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/854.pdf | title = Gossau SG - Herisau - Appenzell - Wasserauen | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=3 October 2019 | accessdate = 30 December 2019}}</ref>

* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|2}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway between [[Nesslau-Neu St. Johann railway station|Nesslau-Neu St. Johann]] and [[Altstätten SG railway station|Altstätten SG]].
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|4}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway via [[Sargans railway station|Sargans]] (circular operation).
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|23}}: half-hourly service over the Gossau–Wasserauen railway between [[Gossau SG railway station|Gossau SG]] and [[Wasserauen railway station|Wasserauen]].
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|81}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway to [[St. Gallen railway station|St. Gallen]].
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|RE}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway to [[Konstanz station|Konstanz]].
