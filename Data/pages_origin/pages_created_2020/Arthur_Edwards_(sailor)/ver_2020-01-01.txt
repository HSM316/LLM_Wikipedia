{{short description|British sailor}}
'''Arthur Edwards''' was a British [[sailor]] and [[merchant]] Who was sent to the [[Tahmasp I|Persian royal court]] (Tahmasp I) in 1566. He worked for [[Muscovy Company]]. Shah tahmasb gave him permission to trade in Persia (Iran).
