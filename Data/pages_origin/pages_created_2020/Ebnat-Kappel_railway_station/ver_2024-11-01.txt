'''Ebnat-Kappel railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Ebnat-Kappel}}) is a railway station in [[Ebnat-Kappel]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is an intermediate station on the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]] and is served by local trains only.

== Services ==
Ebnat-Kappel is served by the S2 of the [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf853">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/853.pdf | title = Wil SG - Nesslau-Neu St. Johann | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=27 September 2019 | accessdate = 30 December 2019}}</ref>

* {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|2}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway between [[Nesslau-Neu St. Johann railway station|Nesslau-Neu St. Johann]] and [[Altstätten SG railway station|Altstätten SG]].
