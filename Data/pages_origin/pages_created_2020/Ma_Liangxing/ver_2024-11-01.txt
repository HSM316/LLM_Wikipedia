'''Ma Liangxing''' ({{zh|c=马良行|p=Mǎ Liángxíng}}; born 29 November 1957) is a Chinese [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Ma was the head coach of the [[China women's national football team|China women's national team]] at the [[2003 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
