'''North Platte Public Schools (NPPS)''' is a public school district in [[North Platte, Nebraska|North Platte]] ([[Lincoln County, Nebraska|Lincoln County]]), [[Nebraska]], [[United States]].

== Facilities ==
Schools currently in operation by NPPS are shown in the following list.<ref name="nppsd-website">{{Citation|url=https://www.nppsd.org|title=North Platte Public Schools|accessdate=January 1, 2020}}</ref>

* '''High schools (1):''' [[North Platte High School (Nebraska)|North Platte High School]]
* '''Middle schools (2):''' Adams Middle School and Madison Middle School
* '''Elementary schools (9) :''' Buffalo Elementary, Cody Elementary, Eisenhower Elementary, Jefferson Elementary, Lake Maloney Elementary, Lincoln Elementary, McDonald Elementary, Osgood Elementary, and Washington Elementary

NPPS also operates its main office building (McKinley Education Center), and a maintenance and bus barn building.<ref name="nppsd-website" />

Schools formerly in use but have since been closed include Roosevelt, Cleveland, and Taft schools.<ref name="np-voters-approve-school-bond-issue">{{Citation|newspaper=North Platte Telegraph|page=1|location=North Platte, NE|date=October 22, 1975|title=NP Voters Approve School Bond Issue}}</ref>
