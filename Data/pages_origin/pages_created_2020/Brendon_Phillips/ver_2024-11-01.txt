'''Brendon Ulysses Phillips''' (born 16 July 1954) is a Jamaican former professional [[association football|footballer]] who played in the [[English Football League|Football League]] for [[Mansfield Town F.C.|Mansfield Town]] and [[Peterborough United F.C.|Peterborough United]].<ref name="ENFA"/><ref>{{Hugman|15697}}</ref>

==Management career==
===Halesowen Town===
Phillips managed [[Halesowen Town F.C.|Halesowen Town]] between 2001 and 2004.

Phillips managed [[Coalville Town F.C.|Coalville Town]] between 2006 and 2007.
