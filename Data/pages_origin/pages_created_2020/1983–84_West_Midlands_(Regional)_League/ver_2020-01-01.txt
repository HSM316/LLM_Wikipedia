The '''1983–84 West Midlands (Regional) League season''' was the 84th in the history of the [[West Midlands (Regional) League]], an English [[association football]] competition for semi-professional and amateur teams based in the [[West Midlands (county)|West Midlands county]], [[Shropshire]], [[Herefordshire]], [[Worcestershire]] and southern [[Staffordshire]].

The Premier Division featured 17 clubs which competed in the division last season, along with three new clubs:
*[[Chasetown F.C.|Chasetown]], promoted from Division One
*[[Cradley Town F.C.|Cradley Town]], transferred from the [[Midland Combination]]
*[[GKN Sankeys F.C.|GKN Sankeys]], promoted from Division One

Also, Bilston was renamed [[Bilston Town F.C.|Bilston Town]].
