'''Dina Al-Erhayem''' (2 October 1975 – 29 December 2019)<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ekstrabladet.dk/flash/dkkendte/dina-er-doed-familien-i-dyb-sorg/7936771|title=Dina er død: Familien i dyb sorg|first=Rådhuspladsen 37 1785 København V.|last=Telefon: 33111313|website=ekstrabladet.dk}}</ref> was a Danish actress with an Iraqi background.

== Early life ==
Al-Erhayem was raised in [[Gilleleje]] as a child of a Danish mother and an Iraqi father and had three older siblings.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dr.dk/nyheder/kultur/tv-vaert-dina-al-erhayem-er-doed-hun-fortjener-en-hyldest-hun-var-saa-fuld-af|title=Tv-vært Dina Al-Erhayem er død: 'Hun fortjener en hyldest – hun var så fuld af kærlighed' |date=30 December 2019 }}</ref>

== Career ==
Al-Erhayem graduated as an actor from [[Aarhus Theatre]] in 2003. She was a student on DR2's weekly faith magazine ''Univers'' (Universe),<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://fyens.dk/artikel/alle-b%C3%A6rer-ondskaben-inde-i-sig-2009-2-5|title=Alle bærer ondskaben inde i sig|first=Marianne|last=Koch|date=5 February 2009|website=fyens.dk}}</ref> and in 2014 hosted the ''Dinas Talkshow'' on [[dk4]]. In December 2014, [[Ole Stephensen]] joined as host, after which the title became ''Dinas og Oles Talkshow''.

In addition to her work as an actor and talk show host, Al-Erhayem was a singer; she released a CD titled ''Se min kjole''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/Dina-Se-Min-Kjole-10-Erotiske-Sange/release/5266487|title=Dina* - Se Min Kjole - 10 Erotiske Sange|website=Discogs}}</ref> (Look at My Dress). She also appeared as a stand-up comedian. Al-Erhayem's film debut was the short film ''Istedgade'' (2006) directed by Birgitte Stærmose.

Shortly before her death, Al-Erhayem talked about her religious development from atheist and Buddhist to Christian.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kristeligt-dagblad.dk/kirke-tro/tv-vaert-jeg-tog-alle-mine-boeger-om-spirituel-vaekst-og-smed-dem-ud|title=Tv-vært: Jeg tog alle mine bøger om spirituel vækst og smed dem ud - Kristeligt Dagblad}}</ref> She committed suicide on December 29, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Schauser |first=Søren |date=2019-12-30 |title=»Hun havde desværre opgivet livet« |url=https://www.berlingske.dk/content/item/1431874 |access-date=2024-01-10 |website=Berlingske.dk |language=da}}</ref>
