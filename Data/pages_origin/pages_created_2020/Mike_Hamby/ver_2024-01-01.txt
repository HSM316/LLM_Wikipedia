'''Mike Hamby''' (born November 12, 1962) is a former [[American football]] [[defensive end]]. He played for the [[Buffalo Bills]] in 1986.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/H/HambMi20.htm |title=Mike Hamby Stats |publisher=Pro-Football-Reference.com |date= |access-date=January 1, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nfl.com/player/mikehamby/2515684/profile |title=Mike Hamby, DE |work=Nfl.com |date= |access-date=January 1, 2020}}</ref> He was drafted by the Bills in the sixth round of the 1985 NFL Draft.<ref>{{Cite web |title=1985 NFL Draft Listing |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/years/1985/draft.htm |access-date=October 5, 2023 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com |language=en}}</ref>
