'''Mansfield Zoo''' is a zoo situated in [[Mansfield, Victoria|Mansfield]], Victoria, Australia.<ref name=ZAA>{{cite web|url=https://www.zooaquarium.org.au/public/Locate/Public/Locate.aspx |title=ZAA Accredited Member Location Map |accessdate=2020-01-01 |publisher=[[Zoo and Aquarium Association]]}}</ref> It features a large collection of exotic and native animals, many of which can be hand-fed by visitors.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mansfieldzoo.com.au/ |title=Mansfield Zoo |accessdate=1 January 2020 |publisher=Mansfield Zoo}}</ref>

Species include:

* [[African lion]] (including several [[White lion|white-coated]] [[Southern African lion]]s)
* [[Agile wallaby]]
* [[Alpaca]]
* [[American alligator]]
* [[American bison]]
* [[Arabian camel]]
* [[Bare-nosed wombat]]
* [[Blackbuck]]
* [[Blue peafowl]]
* [[Common ostrich]]
* [[Dingo]]
* [[Eastern blue-tongue lizard]]
* [[Eastern grey kangaroo]]
* [[Eastern water dragon]]
* [[Emu]]
* [[European fallow deer]]
* [[Galah]]
* [[Guinea pig]]
* [[Helmeted guinea fowl]]
* [[Hog deer]]
* [[Llama]]
* [[Long-billed corella]]
* [[Maned wolf]]
* [[Masked owl]]
* [[Meerkat]]
* [[Red deer]]
* [[Red kangaroo]] (albino)
* [[Rhesus macaque]]
* [[Rusa deer]]
* [[Sambar deer]]
* [[Sulphur-crested cockatoo]]
* [[Swamp wallaby]]
* [[Common brushtail possum|Tasmanian brushtail possum]] (golden-coated morph)
* [[Texas longhorn|Texas longhorn cattle]]
*[[Varied white-fronted capuchin]]
* [[Water buffalo]]
* [[White-tufted marmoset]]
