'''Parijat Kusum Chakma''' (1946 – 28 March 1998) was a [[Bangladesh]]i  academic and politician from [[Rangamati District|Rangamati]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Nationalist Party]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Chakma was born on 1946 in [[Rangamati District|Rangamati]].<ref name=a/> He graduated in 1969. He was a teacher of Shah High School and Rupkari High School. He was the headmaster of Kachalong High School too.<ref name=a/>

Chakma was elected as a member of [[Rangamati Hill District Council]] in 1989. He was elected as a chairman of [[Rangamati Hill District Council]] in 1992.<ref name=a/> He was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Rangamati in the [[February 1996 Bangladeshi general election|Sixth General Election of Bangladesh]].<ref name="feb1996results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/6th%20Parliament%20.pdf |title=List of 6th Parliament Members |format=PDF|website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=8 October 2015}}</ref>

Chakma died on 28 March 1998 at the age of 51.<ref name=a>{{cite web |url=http://www.rangamati.gov.bd/site/page/40244fd1-2144-11e7-8f57-286ed488c766/বিখ্যাত%20ব্যক্তিত্ব
