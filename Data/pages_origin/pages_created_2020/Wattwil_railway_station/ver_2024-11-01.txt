'''Wattwil railway station''' ({{langx|de|Bahnhof Wattwil}}) is a railway station in [[Wattwil]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is an intermediate station on the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]], the southern terminus of the [[Wil–Ebnat-Kappel railway]], and the eastern terminus of the [[Uznach–Wattwil railway]] (the latter includes the {{convert|8.6|km}} long [[Ricken Tunnel]]). It is served by local and long-distance trains.

{{As of|alt=As of the December 2023 timetable change|2023|12|post=,}} the following services stop at Wattwil:<ref name="fpf870">{{cite web | url = https://www.xn--v-info-vxa.ch/sites/default/files/fap/2024/pdf/870.pdf | title = St. Gallen - Wattwil - Rapperswil | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=15 September 2023 | accessdate = 16 December 2023 | lang=de}}</ref>

* [[InterRegio|{{Bahnlinie|S|S|IR|white|#F53327|#F53327}}]] ''[[Voralpen-Express]]'': hourly service between {{rws|Lucerne}} and {{rws|St. Gallen}} (via {{rws|Arth-Goldau}} and {{rws|Rapperswil}}).
* [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:
** {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|2}} / {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|4}}: half-hourly service to {{rws|Altstätten SG}} via St. Gallen and hourly service to {{rws|Rapperswil}}, {{rws|Sargans}}, and {{rws|Nesslau-Neu St. Johann}}.
** {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|9}}: half-hourly service between Wattwil and {{rws|Wil SG}}.
