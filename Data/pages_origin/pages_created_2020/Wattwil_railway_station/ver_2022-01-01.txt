'''Wattwil railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Wattwil}}) is a railway station in [[Wattwil]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is an intermediate station on the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]], the southern terminus of the [[Wil–Ebnat-Kappel railway]], and the eastern terminus of the [[Uznach–Wattwil railway]]. It is served by local and long-distance trains.

== Services ==
=== Long-distance ===
Wattwil is served by the [[Voralpen Express]], which runs hourly between [[Lucerne railway station|Lucerne]] and [[St. Gallen railway station|St. Gallen]].<ref name="fpf870">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/870.pdf | title = Romanshorn - St. Gallen - Wattwil - Rapperswil | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=4 December 2019 | accessdate = 30 December 2019}}</ref>

=== Local ===
Wattwil is served by three services of the [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf870" />

* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|2}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway between [[Nesslau-Neu St. Johann railway station|Nesslau-Neu St. Johann]] and [[Altstätten SG railway station|Altstätten SG]].
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|4}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway via [[Sargans railway station|Sargans]] (circular operation).
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|9}}: half-hourly service over the Wil–Ebnat-Kappel railway to [[Wil railway station|Wil]].
