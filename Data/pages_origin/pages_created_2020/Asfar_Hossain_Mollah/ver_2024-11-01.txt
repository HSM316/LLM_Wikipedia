'''Asfar Hossain Mollah''' ({{circa|1953}} – 13 May 2016) was a Bangladeshi physician and politician from [[Gazipur District|Gazipur]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Awami League]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]]. He was a member of [[Bangladesh Medical Association]].<ref name=a/>

==Biography==
Mollah was the son of Hafiz Uddin Mollah and Begum Shahida Mollah.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.risingbd.com/fascinating-world/news/160304/সাবেক-সাংসদ-ডা-আসফার-হোসেন-আর-নেই|script-title=bn:সাবেক সাংসদ ডা. আসফার হোসেন আর নেই|date=13 May 2016|access-date=1 January 2020|work=risingbd.com|language=bn
}}</ref><ref name=b/> He was the vice president of [[Dhaka Medical College|Dhaka Medical College Central Students' Union]] and presidium member of [[Jubo League]].<ref name=a>{{cite news |url=https://samakal.com/todays-print-edition/tp-khobor/article/1605212048/সাবেক-সাংসদ-আসফার-হোসেন-মোল্লার-ইন্তেকাল
|script-title=bn:সাবেক সাংসদ আসফার হোসেন মোল্লার ইন্তেকাল
|date=14 May 2016|access-date=1 January 2020|newspaper=Samakal|language=bn

He was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from [[Gazipur-3]] in 1991.<ref name="1991results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/5th.pdf |title=List of 5th Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad|format=PDF |language=bn |access-date=9 April 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180917232851/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/5th.pdf |archive-date=17 September 2018}}</ref>
