'''Yuri Vladimirovich Bystritsky''' ({{lang-ru|Юрий Владимирович Быстрицкий}}; born 31 July 1944) is a Russian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Bystritsky was the head coach of the [[Russia women's national football team|Russia women's national team]] at the [[1999 FIFA Women's World Cup]] and [[2003 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
