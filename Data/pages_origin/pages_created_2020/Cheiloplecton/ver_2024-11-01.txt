'''''Cheiloplecton''''' is a genus of [[fern]]s in the subfamily [[Cheilanthoideae]] of the family [[Pteridaceae]] with a single species '''''Cheiloplecton rigidum''''',<ref name=PPGI/> native to [[Mexico]], [[Guatemala]], [[El Salvador]] and [[Honduras]].<ref name=CFLW/>
