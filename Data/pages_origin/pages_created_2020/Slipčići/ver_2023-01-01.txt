'''Slipčići''' is a [[village]] in the [[Municipalities of Bosnia and Herzegovina|City]] of [[Mostar]], [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]].<ref>Official results from the book: Ethnic composition of Bosnia-Herzegovina population, by municipalities and settlements, 1991. census, Zavod za statistiku Bosne i Hercegovine - Bilten no.234, Sarajevo 1991.</ref>

== Demographics ==
According to the 2013 census, its population was 207.<ref>{{cite web|title=Naseljena Mjesta 1991/2013|url=http://www.statistika.ba/?show=12&id=11410|publisher=[[Statistical Office of Bosnia and Herzegovina]]|accessdate=October 9, 2021|language=Croatian}}</ref>
