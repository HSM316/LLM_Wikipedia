The '''men's [[4 × 400 metres relay]]''' event at the '''[[Athletics at the 1989 Summer Universiade|1989 Summer Universiade]]''' was held at the [[Wedaustadion]] in [[Duisburg]] with the final on 29 and 30 August 1989.
