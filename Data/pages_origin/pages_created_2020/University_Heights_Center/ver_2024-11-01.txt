The '''University Heights Center''' is a former school building in the [[University District, Seattle|University District]] of [[Seattle]], Washington, United States. It is located at [[The Ave|University Way]] and 50th Street.

The building was opened in 1903 for University Heights Elementary School.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Long |first1=Priscilla |title=University Heights Elementary School opens in the University District in September 1903. |url=https://www.historylink.org/File/3381 |website=[[HistoryLink]] |date=June 18, 2001 |accessdate=December 31, 2019}}</ref>

The building is alleged to be haunted.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Rice |first1=Jacob |title=Ghost Hunt - University Heights Center in Seattle |url=https://www.ghostlyactivities.com/ghost-hunt-university-heights-center-seattle/ |website=Ghostly Activities |date=August 29, 2016 |accessdate=December 31, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Lloyd |first1=Sarah Anne |title=17 of Seattle's most haunted places |url=https://seattle.curbed.com/maps/seattle-haunted-places-ghosts-paranormal |website=Curbed Seattle |language=en |date=October 24, 2017 |accessdate=December 31, 2019}}</ref>

In 2011 [[Historic Seattle]] awarded the organization a preservation award for care in restoring the windows.<ref>{{cite web |author1=Historic Seattle |title=2011 Awards |url=https://historicseattle.org/events-2/2011-awards/ |website=[[Historic Seattle]] |date=May 10, 2011 |accessdate=December 31, 2019}}</ref>

In 1991 a [[p-patch]] was established at the building.<ref>{{cite web |title=University Heights - Neighborhoods |url=https://www.seattle.gov/neighborhoods/programs-and-services/p-patch-community-gardening/p-patch-list/university-heights |website=www.seattle.gov |accessdate=December 31, 2019}}</ref>
