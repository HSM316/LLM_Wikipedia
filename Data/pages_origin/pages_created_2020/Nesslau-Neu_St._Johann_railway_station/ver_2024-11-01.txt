'''Nesslau-Neu St. Johann railway station''' ({{langx|de|Bahnhof Nesslau-Neu St. Johann}}) is a railway station in [[Nesslau]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is the southern terminus of the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]] and is served by local trains only.

== Services ==
Nesslau-Neu St. Johann is served by the S2 regional train of [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf853">{{cite web |url=https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2024/853.pdf |title=Wil SG–Nesslau-Neu St. Johann |publisher=Bundesamt für Verkehr |date=2023-09-15 |accessdate=2023-12-23}}</ref>

* {{Rail icon|St. Gallen S-Bahn|2}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway to {{rws|Altstätten SG}} via {{rws|Wattwil}} and {{rws|St. Gallen}}.

[[PostAuto Schweiz]] bus service 790 calls in front of the station building.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/80.790.pdf |title=PostAuto Schweiz timetable |publisher=Bundesamt für Verkehr |date=2023}}</ref> 
Towards south, it serves the [[valley station]] (in Unterwasser) of the [[Iltiosbahn]] to Iltios, from where a cable car continues to [[Chäserrugg]] mountain ([[Churfirsten]]).
