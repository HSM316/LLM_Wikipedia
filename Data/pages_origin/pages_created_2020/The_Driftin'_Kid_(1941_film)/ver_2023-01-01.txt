'''''The Driftin' Kid''''' is a 1941 American [[Western (genre)|Western]] film directed by [[Robert Emmett Tansey]] and written by Robert Emmett Tansey and [[Frances Kavanaugh]]. The film stars [[Tom Keene (actor)|Tom Keene]], [[Betty Miles]], [[Frank Yaconelli]], [[Glenn Strange]], [[Stanley Price]] and Fred Hoose. The film was released on October 17, 1941, by [[Monogram Pictures]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/73660/The-Driftin-Kid/ |title=The Driftin' Kid (1941) - Overview |publisher=TCM.com |date= |accessdate=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Sandra Brennan |url=https://www.allmovie.com/movie/the-driftin-kid-v90034 |title=The Driftin' Kid (1941) - Robert Emmett Tansey |publisher=AllMovie |date= |accessdate=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://catalog.afi.com/Film/26682-THE-DRIFTINKID |title=The Driftin' Kid |publisher=Catalog.afi.com |date= |accessdate=2019-12-31}}</ref>
