'''''Iceman''''' ({{lang-de|Der Mann aus dem Eis}}) is a 2017 German-Italian-Austrian adventure film directed by Felix Randau.<ref>{{cite news |last=Kiang |first=Jessica |url=https://variety.com/2017/film/reviews/iceman-review-1202523730// |title=Locarno Film Review: ‘Iceman’ |publisher=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |date=13 August 2017 |accessdate=31 December 2019 }}</ref> It is a fictional story about the life of [[Ötzi]], a [[natural mummy]] of a man found in 1991 in the [[Ötztal Alps]].<ref name="guard">{{cite news |last=Connolly |first=Kate |url=https://www.theguardian.com/science/2017/dec/03/otzi-iceman-mummy-brought-to-life-on-film |title=Iceman the movie: stone age survivor Ötzi is brought back to life |work=[[The Guardian]] |date=3 December 2017 |accessdate=31 December 2019 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/iceman-der-mann-aus-dem-eis-1028784|title='Iceman' ('Der Mann aus dem Eis'): Film Review {{!}} Locarno 2017|website=The Hollywood Reporter|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/reviews/iceman-film-review-aardman-early-man-rhaetic-language-a8463011.html|title=Iceman film review: Drama plays like an X-rated version of Aardman cartoon Early Man|date=2018-07-25|website=The Independent|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>
