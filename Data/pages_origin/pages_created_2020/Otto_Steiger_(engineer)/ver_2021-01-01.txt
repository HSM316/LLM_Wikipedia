{{short description|Swiss engineer (1858-1923), designer of the Millionaire calculator}}
'''Otto Steiger''' (1858-1923) was Swiss engineer. Steiger came from [[St. Gallen]] and lived in [[Munich]].
 
Steiger is known for the invention and construction of the ''[[The Millionaire (calculator)|Millionaire]]'' mechanical calculator, the first commercially successful direct multiplication calculator (German patent 1892).
