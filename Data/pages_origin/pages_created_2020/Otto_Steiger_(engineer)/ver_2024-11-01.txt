{{short description|Swiss engineer (1858-1923), designer of the Millionaire calculator}}
'''Otto Steiger''' (1858-1923) was Swiss engineer. Steiger came from [[St. Gallen]] and lived in [[Munich]].
 
Steiger is known for the invention and construction of the ''[[The Millionaire (calculator)|Millionaire]]'' mechanical calculator, the first commercially successful direct multiplication calculator (German patent 1892).<ref>{{Cite book |last=Arbel |first=Alexander |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UiCODwAAQBAJ |title=Routes to the Information Revolution |date=2019-03-18 |publisher=Cambridge Scholars Publishing |isbn=978-1-5275-3150-5 |pages=38 |language=en}}</ref>
