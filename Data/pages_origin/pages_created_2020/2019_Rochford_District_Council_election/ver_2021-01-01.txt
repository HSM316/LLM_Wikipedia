
The '''2019 Rochford District Council election''' took place on 2 May 2019 to elect members of [[Rochford District Council]] in England. This was on the same day as other [[2019 United Kingdom local elections|local elections]].<ref>https://www.andrewteale.me.uk/leap/results/2019/206/</ref>
