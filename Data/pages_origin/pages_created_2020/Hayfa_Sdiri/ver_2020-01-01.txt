'''Hayfa Sdiri''' ({{Lang-ar|هيفاء سديري}} ''Hayfā’ Sdīrī'', born 1997) is a [[Tunisian people|Tunisian]] [[entrepreneur]], activist and [[blogger]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/unvolunteers/videos/416966565635688/|title=Meet Hayfa Sdiri, UN Volunteer with UNDP Tunisia|via=www.facebook.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://icawomen.com/%d9%87%d9%8a%d9%81%d8%a7%d8%a1-%d8%b3%d8%af%d9%8a%d8%b1%d9%8a|title=هيفاء سديري}}</ref>

In 2016, she founded Entr@crush, an online platform for future entrepreneurs.

Sdiri is currently studying Applied Sciences, Business Administration and Management at [[Paris Dauphine University]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://womenpreneur-initiative.com/inspire/hayfa-sdiri/|title=HAYFA SDIRI|date=February 12, 2018|website=Womenpreneur Initiative}}</ref>

In 2019, she was listed among the BBC's [[100 Women (BBC)|100 Women]], a list of 100 inspiring and influential women.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://news-tunisia.tunisienumerique.com/tunisia-hayfa-sdiri-makes-the-list-of-bbc-100-women-2019/|title=Tunisia-Hayfa Sdiri makes the list of BBC 100 Women 2019|first=Rim|last=Hana|date=October 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tuniscope.com/article/186575/culture/who/hayfa-sdiri-243716|title=Hayfa Sdiri, une Tunisienne parmi les 100 femmes les plus inspirantes au monde|date=December 31, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.espacemanager.com/la-jeune-volontaire-tunisienne-hayfa-sdiri-parmi-les-100-femmes-les-plus-influentes-au-monde.html|title=La Jeune Volontaire Tunisienne Hayfa Sdiri parmi les 100 femmes les plus influentes au monde|website=Espace Manager}}</ref>
