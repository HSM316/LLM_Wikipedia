'''''The Clancy Name''''' is a 1908 play by Irish playwright [[Lennox Robinson]]. It was his first produced play. It was produced under the name SL Robinson.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.irishplayography.com/play.aspx?playid=32055|title=Irish Playography|last=|first=|date=|website=www.irishplayography.com|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

== Production history ==
The play was first produced by the [[Abbey Theatre]] in 1908.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.irishplayography.com/play.aspx?playid=32055|title=Irish Playography|last=|first=|date=|website=www.irishplayography.com|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

The play was produced by the Abbey Theatre in December 1942 for 13 performances.
