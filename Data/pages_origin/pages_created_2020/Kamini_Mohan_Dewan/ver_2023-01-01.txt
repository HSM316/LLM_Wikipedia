'''Kamini Mohan Dewan''' (1890 – 1976) was a Bangladeshi politician from [[Rangamati District|Rangamati]]. He was elected as a member of the [[East Bengal Legislative Assembly]] in 1954.<ref name=a/> His son  [[Binoy Kumar Dewan]] served as the [[Ministry of Local Government, Rural Development and Co-operatives|state minister of the Ministry of Local Government, Rural Development and Co-operatives]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mzamin.com/details.php?mzamin=NzY3Mzg=

}}</ref>

==Biography==
Dewan was born on 1890.<ref name=a/> He founded Parbatya Chattagram Janasamiti in 1920.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://bn.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=%E0%A6%AA%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%B0%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%AC%E0%A6%A4%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%AF_%E0%A6%9A%E0%A6%9F%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%9F%E0%A6%97%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%B0%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%AE_%E0%A6%9C%E0%A6%A8%E0%A6%B8%E0%A6%82%E0%A6%B9%E0%A6%A4%E0%A6%BF_%E0%A6%B8%E0%A6%AE%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%A4%E0%A6%BF
|title=পার্বত্য চট্টগ্রাম জনসংহতি সমিতি
|website=Banglapedia|language=bn |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> Later, he established Hill Tracts People Organization in 1950.<ref name=a/> He was elected as a member of the [[East Bengal Legislative Assembly]] in 1954.

Dewan died in 1976.<ref name=a>{{cite web |url=http://www.rangamati.gov.bd/site/page/40244fd1-2144-11e7-8f57-286ed488c766/বিখ্যাত%20ব্যক্তিত্ব

|website=www.rangamati.gov.bd|language=bn |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> He wrote an autobiography titled ''Parbotya Chattagramer Din Seboker Jibon Kahini''.
