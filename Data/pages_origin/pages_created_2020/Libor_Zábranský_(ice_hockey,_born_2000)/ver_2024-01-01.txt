'''Libor Zábranský Jr.''' (born 26 May 2000) is a Czech [[ice hockey]] [[defenceman]] currently playing for [[Porin Ässät (men's ice hockey)|Porin Ässät]] of the [[Liiga]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Libor Zábranský at eliteprospects.com |url=https://www.eliteprospects.com/player/247245/libor-zábranský}}</ref> He is the son of the Czech former professional ice hockey defenceman [[Libor Zábranský (ice hockey, born 1973)|Libor Zábranský Sr.]]

==Playing career==
In August 2017, he was the captain of [[Czech Republic men's national under-18 ice hockey team|men's national under-18 team]] in the [[2017 Ivan Hlinka Memorial Tournament|Ivan Hlinka Memorial Tournament]]. The national team got into the finals where they lost to [[Canada men's national under-18 ice hockey team|Canada]] 4–1. Zábranský himself, however, could not play the final match due to injury.<ref>{{cite web
