'''Natalia Martyasheva''' or '''Natalya Martyasheva''' (6 January 1988 - 8 June 2011) was a [[Russian people|Russian]] [[para table tennis]] player who was born with [[cerebral palsy]]. She is a Paralympic champion in [[table tennis at the Summer Paralympics|table tennis]] in 2008 and a triple European silver medalist.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.paralympic.org/natalia-martyasheva|title=Natalia Martyasheva - IPC Profile|last=|first=|date=31 December 2019|website=International Paralympic Committee|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://stats.ipttc.org/en/profiles/2797|title=Natalya Martyasheva - IPTTF Profile|last=|first=|date=31 December 2019|website=International Para Table Tennis Federation|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref>
