'''Siraj Haider''' (6 February 1948 – 11 January 2018) was a [[Bangladesh]]i actor and director. He acted in over 400 films.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/online/entertainment/2018/01/11/588288/৪০০-চলচ্চিত্রে-অভিনয়-করেছিলেন-সিরাজ-হায়দার|script-title=bn:৪০০ চলচ্চিত্রে অভিনয় করেছিলেন সিরাজ হায়দার|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Kaler Kantho|language=bn
}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.jugantor.com/entertainment/5639/চলে-গেলেন-সিরাজ-হায়দার
|script-title=bn:চলে গেলেন সিরাজ হায়দার
|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Jugantor|language=bn
}}</ref> He also directed two films. He acted in television, stage, radio and jatra too.<ref name=aa>{{cite news |url=https://www.channelionline.com/চলে-গেলেন-চলচ্চিত্র-অভিন/
|script-title=bn:চলে গেলেন চলচ্চিত্র অভিনেতা সিরাজ হায়দার

==Biography==
Haider was born on 6 February 1948 in [[Bikrampur]], [[Munshiganj District|Munshiganj]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.daily-sun.com/post/281320/2018/01/11/Actor-Siraj-Haider-dies
|title=Actor Siraj Haider dies
|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Daily Sun
}}</ref> He made his acting career debut with a drama titled ''Tipu Sultan'' in 1962.<ref name=bb>{{cite news |url=https://m.banglanews24.com/entertainment/news/bd/629870.details
|script-title=bn:অভিনেতা সিরাজ হায়দার আর নেই
|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Banglanews24.com|language=bn
}}</ref> He was in class nine at that time.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://archive1.ittefaq.com.bd/entertainment/2018/01/11/143023.html
|script-title=bn:চলে গেলেন অভিনেতা-নির্মাতা সিরাজ হায়দার
|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Ittefaq|language=bn
}}</ref>

Haider made his acting career debut in [[Dhallywood]] with ''Sukher Songsar''.<ref name=aaa>{{cite news |url=https://www.samakal.com/entertainment/article/1801590/%EF%BB%BF%E0%A6%85%E0%A6%AD%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%A8%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%A4%E0%A6%BE-%E0%A6%B8%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%B0%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%9C-%E0%A6%B9%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%AF%E0%A6%BC%E0%A6%A6%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%B0-%E0%A6%86%E0%A6%B0-%E0%A6%A8%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%87
|script-title=bn:অভিনেতা সিরাজ হায়দার আর নেই
|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Samakal|language=bn
}}</ref> Besides films he also involved in acting in television, stage and jatra. He directed many stage dramas too.<ref name=hh/> He founded Rangana Natyagoshthi in 1976.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.prothomalo.com/entertainment/article/1406246/চলে-গেলেন-অভিনেতা-সিরাজ-হায়দার
|script-title=bn:চলে গেলেন অভিনেতা সিরাজ হায়দার
|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Prothom Alo|language=bn
}}</ref>

Haider also directed two films.<ref name=cc>{{cite news |url=https://www.ekushey-tv.com/অভিনেতা-সিরাজ-হায়দার-মারা-গেছেন/23991

}}</ref> The name of these two films are ''Adom Byapari'' and ''Sukh''. Of them ''Adom Bypari'' is an unreleaded film.<ref name=hh>{{cite news |url=https://www.bd-pratidin.com/entertainment/2018/01/11/296609
|script-title=bn:অভিনেতা সিরাজ হায়দার আর নেই
|date=11 January 2018|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Bangladesh Pratidin|language=bn
}}</ref> [[Mujib Pardeshi]] made his acting career debut with ''Sukh''.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://samakal.com/todays-print-edition/tp-others/article/1701266243/%E0%A6%86%E0%A6%AC%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%B0%E0%A6%93-%E0%A6%9A%E0%A6%B2%E0%A6%9A%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%9A%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%A4%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%B0%E0%A7%87-%E0%A6%AE%E0%A7%81%E0%A6%9C%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%AC-%E0%A6%AA%E0%A6%B0%E0%A6%A6%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%B6%E0%A7%80
|script-title=bn:আবারও চলচ্চিত্রে মুজিব পরদেশী
|date=29 January 2017|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Samakal|language=bn
}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://archive1.ittefaq.com.bd/print-edition/anondo-binodon/2017/02/09/174634.html
|script-title=bn:তিনি মুজিব পরদেশী|date=9 February 2017
|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Ittefaq|language=bn
}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.dailyinqilab.com/article/63558/%E0%A6%A8%E0%A6%A4%E0%A7%81%E0%A6%A8-%E0%A6%95%E0%A6%B0%E0%A7%87-%E0%A6%AB%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%B0%E0%A7%87-%E0%A6%86%E0%A6%B8%E0%A6%9B%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%A8-%E0%A6%B8%E0%A6%99%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%97%E0%A7%80%E0%A6%A4%E0%A6%B6%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%B2%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%AA%E0%A7%80-%E0%A6%AE%E0%A7%81%E0%A6%9C%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%AC-%E0%A6%AA%E0%A6%B0%E0%A6%A6%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%B6%E0%A7%80
|script-title=bn:নতুন করে ফিরে আসছেন সঙ্গীতশিল্পী মুজিব পরদেশী|date=11 February 2017
|access-date=31 December 2019|work=Inqilab|language=bn
}}</ref>

Haider was married to Mina Haider.<ref name=aa/> They had two sons and one daughter.<ref name=bb/>

Haider died on 11 January 2018 at his own home in Kalyanpur, [[Dhaka]] at the age of 69.<ref name=aaa/><ref name=cc/><ref name=p/>
