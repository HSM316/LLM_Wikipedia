'''1,2-Dibromobenzene''' is an [[organobromine compound]] with the formula C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>4</sub>Br<sub>2</sub>.  It is one of three isomers, the others being 1,3- and 1,4-dibromobenzene.  It is a colorless liquid, although impure samples appear yellowish.  The compound is a precursor to many 1,2-disubstituted derivatives of [[benzene]]. For example, it is a precursor to [[phthalonitrile|1,2-dicyanobenzene]]<ref>{{Ullmann|author=LÃ¶bbert, Gerd|title=Phthalocyanines|year=2000|doi=10.1002/14356007.a20_213}}</ref> and dithioethers.<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.15227/orgsyn.042.0022|title=1,2-BIS(n-BUTYLTHIO)BENZENE|journal=Organic Syntheses|year=1962|volume=42|page=22}}</ref>
