'''1,2-Dibromobenzene''' is an [[organobromine compound]] with the formula C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>4</sub>Br<sub>2</sub>.  It is one of three isomers, the others being 1,3- and 1,4-dibromobenzene.  It is a colorless liquid, although impure samples appear yellowish.  The compound is a precursor to many 1,2-disubstituted derivatives of [[benzene]].
