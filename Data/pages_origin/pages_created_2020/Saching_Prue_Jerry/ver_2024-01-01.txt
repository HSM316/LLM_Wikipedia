'''Sacing Prue Jerry''' is a Bangladeshi politician from [[Bandarban District|Bandarban]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Nationalist Party]]. He is a former member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]]. He is the son of [[Aung Shwe Prue Chowdhury]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/print-edition/campus/2012/08/09/276586 |script-title=bn:বোমাং রাজা অং শৈ প্রু চৌধুরী আর নেই |date=9 August 2012 |newspaper=Kaler Kantho |language=bn |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref><ref name=a>{{cite news |url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/print-edition/2nd-rajdhani/2018/12/19/716114 |script-title=bn:বান্দরবানে বীর বাহাদুর ও সাচিং প্রু টেনশনে |date=19 December 2018 |access-date=1 January 2019 |work=Kaler Kantho |language=bn}}</ref> His aunt [[Ma Mya Ching]]  is a former member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/print-edition/priyo-desh/2017/05/18/498413 |title=বিএনপির দুই পক্ষ মুখোমুখি |date=18 May 2017 |access-date=1 January 2019 |work=Kaler Kantho |language=bn}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.prothomalo.com/bangladesh/article/1566317/কেন্দ্রের-দিকে-তাকিয়ে-মামি-ভাগনে |script-title=bn:কেন্দ্রের দিকে তাকিয়ে মামি-ভাগনে |date=23 November 2018 |access-date=1 January 2019 |work=Prothom Alo |language=bn}}</ref>

==Biography==
Jerry is a prince of [[Bohmong Circle]].<ref name=a/><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.jugantor.com/eleventh-parliament-election/122408/ |script-title=bn:দেশের শেষ আসনে বীরের সঙ্গে লড়ছেন রাজপুত্র |date=15 December 2018 |access-date=1 January 2019 |work=Jugantor |language=bn}}</ref> He was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Bandarban in the [[February 1996 Bangladeshi general election|Sixth General Election of Bangladesh]].<ref name="feb1996results">{{cite web |format=PDF |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/6th%20Parliament%20.pdf |title=List of 6th Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=8 October 2015}}</ref>  He also served as the chairman of [[Bandarban Sadar Upazila]] and [[Bandarban Hill District Council]].<ref>{{cite news |url=http://mini.thesangbad.net/news/lastpage/বান্দরবানে%2Bধানের%2Bশীষের%2Bএকক%2Bপ্রার্থী%2Bসাচিং%2Bপ্রু-40423/ |script-title=bn:বান্দরবানে ধানের শীষের একক প্রার্থী সাচিং প্রু |date=28 November 2018 |access-date=1 January 2019 |work=The Sangbad |language=bn}}</ref>
