'''Osman Can Çötür''' (born 13 September 2001) is a Turkish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]].

==Professional career==
Çötür made his professional debut for [[Kayserispor]] in a 6–2 [[Süper Lig]] loss to [[Trabzonspor]] on 28 December 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ca.soccerway.com/matches/2019/12/28/turkey/super-lig/trabzonspor/kayserispor/3081926/|title=Trabzonspor vs. Kayserispor - 28 December 2019 - Soccerway|website=ca.soccerway.com}}</ref>
