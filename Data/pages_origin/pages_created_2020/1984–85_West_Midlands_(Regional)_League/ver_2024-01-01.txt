The '''1984–85 West Midlands (Regional) League season''' was the 85th in the history of the [[West Midlands (Regional) League]], an English [[association football]] competition for semi-professional and amateur teams based in the [[West Midlands (county)|West Midlands county]], [[Shropshire]], [[Herefordshire]], [[Worcestershire]] and southern [[Staffordshire]].

*[[Tamworth F.C.|Tamworth]], relegated from the [[1983–84 Southern Football League#Midland Division|Southern League]]
*[[Tipton Town F.C.|Tipton Town]], promoted from Division One
