The '''Botswana Basketball League (BBL)''' is the premier [[basketball]] league for clubs in [[Botswana]]. The league consist out of twelve teams. As of December 2019, the defending champions are [[Dolphins BC|Dolphins]].

The champions of the BBL are able to play in the qualifying rounds of the [[Basketball Africa League]] (BAL).
