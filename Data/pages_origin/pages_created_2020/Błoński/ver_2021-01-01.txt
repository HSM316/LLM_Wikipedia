'''Błoński''', fem. '''Błońska''' is a Polish-language [[toponymic surname]] associated with the city of [[Błonie]]. The East-Slavic variant of the surnam is [[Blonsky]]. 

Notable people with this surname include:

*[[Jan Błoński]] (1931 – 2009), Polish historian, literary critic, publicist and translator
