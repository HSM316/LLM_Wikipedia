'''Charles John Boyle''' ({{Circa|1877}} – April 21, 1947)<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.newspapers.com/clip/5185922/charles_j_boyle_obituary/|title=Charles J Boyle obituary|website=newspapers.com|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> was an American [[college football]] player and coach. Boyle grew up in [[Worcester, Massachusetts]] and played football at [[Dartmouth College]] from 1896 to 1900 before graduating in 1901.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=XpQXAAAAYAAJ&dq=boyle+wentworth+dartmouth&pg=PA349|title=Athletics at Dartmouth|first1=Horace Gibson|last1=Pender|first2=Raymond Francis|last2=McPartlin|date=June 4, 1923|publisher=Pub. under the direction of Dartmouth College Athletic Council|via=Google Books}}</ref>  He was an assistant football coach at Dartmouth for a couple years and was the head football coach at [[Ohio Wesleyan University]] from 1901 to 1902 and [[Western Reserve University]] in [[Cleveland]] in 1903.<ref>{{cite news |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |title=Reserve Gets a Coach at Last, Charles J. Boyle Engaged to Take Charge of the Football Squad |url=https://infoweb-newsbank-com.eresources.cuyahogalibrary.org/resources/doc/nb/image/v2%3A122AFBBA107AC9E4%40EANX-NB-125B3E57DD36A350%402416167-125ADAE07721A9A4%405-1381C51A8BE24CF9%40Reserve%2BGets%2BA%2BCoach%2BAt%2BLast.%2BCharles%2BJ.%2BBoyle%2BEngaged%2Bto%2BTake%2BCharge%2Bof%2Bthe?p=EANX-NB&hlterms=Boyle |newspaper=[[Plain Dealer]] |location=[[Cleveland, Ohio]] |date=February 21, 1903 |page=6}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://case.edu/its/archives/Seasons/wfoot1903.htm|title=WRU Football 1903/04|website=case.edu}}</ref>
