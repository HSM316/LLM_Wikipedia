'''Charles J. Boyle''' (c. 1877 – April 21, 1947) was an [[American football]] player and coach. 

Boyle played college football for [[Dartmouth College|Dartmouth]] in [[1896 Dartmouth football team|1896]], [[1897 Dartmouth football team|1897]], [[1898 Dartmouth football team|1898]], [[1899 Dartmouth football team|1899]], and [[1900 Dartmouth football team|1900]], graduating in 1901.<ref>https://books.google.com/books?id=XpQXAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA349&lpg=PA349&dq=boyle+wentworth+dartmouth&source=bl&ots=d8UIxFLSvz&sig=ACfU3U1vJ2l43fUJFzCMT3zxMe0n-BnEXA&hl=en&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjzh_ny6t7mAhWEY98KHb6dCnwQ6AEwA3oECAgQAQ#v=onepage&q=boyle%20wentworth%20dartmouth&f=false</ref>  He was an assistant football coach at Dartmouth for a couple years and was the head football coach at [[Western Reserve University]] in [[Cleveland, Ohio]] in 1903.  Boyle achieved a 3–6 record.<ref>https://case.edu/its/archives/Seasons/wfoot1903.htm</ref>
