'''Yesica Luciana Hernández Ferrando''' (born 16 September 1988) is a Uruguayan [[association football|football]] [[Manager (association football)|manager]] and former player, who played as a [[midfielder]]. She has been a member of the [[Uruguay women's national football team|Uruguay women's national team]].

==Early life==
Hernández hails from [[Ombúes de Lavalle]].<ref name=YH/>

==College career==
Hernández attended the [[Missouri Valley College]] in the United States.<ref name=YH/>

==Club career==
Hernández played in Uruguay for [[Colón F.C.|Colón]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/245/0/|title=Colón Fútbol Club - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|access-date=31 December 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
Hernández played for Uruguay at senior level in the [[2014 Copa América Femenina]].<ref>{{Cite web|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140914022911/http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/copa-america-femenina-colombia-golea-uruguay-4-0|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/copa-america-femenina-colombia-golea-uruguay-4-0|title=Copa América Femenina: Colombia golea a Uruguay 4-0|website=Conmebol|access-date=31 December 2019|archive-date=14 September 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140917021753/http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/uruguay-levanta-cabeza-ante-peru-2-1|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/uruguay-levanta-cabeza-ante-peru-2-1|title=Uruguay levanta cabeza ante Perú (2-1)|language=es|website=Conmebol|access-date=31 December 2019|archive-date=17 September 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/ecuador-clasifica-los-juegos-panamericanos-y-la-fase-final-de-la-copa-america-femenina|title=Ecuador clasifica a los Juegos Panamericanos y a la Fase Final de la Copa América Femenina|website=Conmebol|access-date=31 December 2019|date=19 September 2014|language=es}}</ref>
