{{Nihongo|'''Minamoto no Yoshitsune'''|源義経}} is a 1966 Japanese television series. It is the 4th [[NHK]] [[taiga drama]].<ref name="NHK">{{cite web|url=https://www6.nhk.or.jp/drama/pastprog/detail.html?i=taiga04 |publisher=NHK|title=　大河ドラマ　源義経 | accessdate= March 19, 2017}}</ref><ref>日本放送協会（編）  20世紀放送史 上  日本放送出版協会 2001 P.495</ref>

==Story==
Taikōki deals with the [[Kamakura period]]. Based on [[Genzō Murakami]]'s novels "Minamoto no Yoshitsune".<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.tvdrama-db.com/drama_info/p/id-10711   |publisher=DDB|title=大河ドラマ　源義経 | accessdate= March 19, 2017 }}</ref>

The story chronicles the life of [[Minamoto no Yoshitsune]].<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www2.nhk.or.jp/archives/tv60bin/detail/index.cgi?das_id=D0009010028_00000|publisher=NHK|title=源義経 |accessdate= March 19, 2017 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.amazon.co.jp/NHK%E5%A4%A7%E6%B2%B3%E3%83%89%E3%83%A9%E3%83%9E%E7%B7%8F%E9%9B%86%E7%B7%A8-%E6%BA%90%E7%BE%A9%E7%B5%8C-2%E6%9E%9A%E7%B5%84-DVD-%E5%B0%BE%E4%B8%8A%E8%8F%8A%E4%B9%8B%E5%8A%A9/dp/B0006FGWQU  |publisher=アマゾン|title=NHK大河ドラマ総集編 源義経 2枚組| accessdate= March 19, 2017 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=源義経とは|work=kotobank|url=https://kotobank.jp/word/%E6%BA%90%E7%BE%A9%E7%B5%8C-139234|accessdate=30 December 2020}}</ref>
