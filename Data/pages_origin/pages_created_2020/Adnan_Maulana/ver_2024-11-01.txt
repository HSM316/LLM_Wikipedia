'''Adnan Maulana''' (born 23 October 1999) is an Indonesian [[badminton]] player affiliated with Jaya Raya Jakarta club.<ref>{{cite web |title=Pemain: Adnan Maulana |url=https://badmintonindonesia.org/app/profile/aktif.aspx?id=000014469 |publisher=Badminton Association of Indonesia |access-date=10 January 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201031075532/https://badmintonindonesia.org/app/profile/aktif.aspx?id=000014469 |url-status=live |archive-date=31 October 2020 |language=id}}</ref> He won a bronze medal in the mixed doubles at the [[Badminton at the 2021 SEA Games|2021 SEA Games]] partnered with [[Mychelle Crhystine Bandaso]].<ref name="seai22">{{cite web |last=Arif |first=Fauzi Handoko |title=Hasil Bulu Tangkis SEA Games 2021 - Adnan/Mychelle Takluk, Indonesia Tak Capai Target Emas |url=https://www.bolasport.com/read/313291361/hasil-bulu-tangkis-sea-games-2021-adnanmychelle-takluk-indonesia-tak-capai-target-emas |publisher=Bola Sport |date=21 May 2022 |access-date=28 July 2022 |language=id |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220728080803/https://www.bolasport.com/read/313291361/hasil-bulu-tangkis-sea-games-2021-adnanmychelle-takluk-indonesia-tak-capai-target-emas |url-status=live |archive-date=28 July 2022}}</ref>

== Career ==
In 2019, Maulana won his first senior international title at the 2019 [[Iran Fajr International]] in the men's doubles event partnering Ghifari Anandaffa Prihardika.<ref name="irn19">{{cite web |last=Tegar |first=Bimo |title=(Iran Fajr International Challenge) Adnan/Ghifari Sumbang Gelar Juara untuk Indonesia |url=https://www.djarumbadminton.com/internasional/berita/read/iran-fajr-international-challenge-adnanghifari-sumbang-gelar-juara-untuk-indonesia/ |publisher=Djarum Badminton |date=2 July 2019 |access-date=28 July 2022 |language=id |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220728085008/https://www.djarumbadminton.com/internasional/berita/read/iran-fajr-international-challenge-adnanghifari-sumbang-gelar-juara-untuk-indonesia/ |url-status=live |archive-date=28 July 2022}}</ref> He then won his World Tour title at the [[2019 Russian Open (badminton)|Russian Open]] in the mixed doubles with Mychelle Crhystine Bandaso. He and Bandaso beating host pair [[Evgenij Dremin]] and [[Evgenia Dimova]] in the final in straight games.<ref name="rus19">{{cite web |last=Tegar |first=Bimo |title=(Russia Open) Adnan/Mychelle Juara, Indonesia Borong Tiga Gelar |url=https://www.djarumbadminton.com/internasional/berita/read/russia-open-adnanmychelle-juara-indonesia-borong-tiga-gelar/ |publisher=Djarum Badminton |date=22 July 2019 |access-date=28 July 2022 |language=id |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220728082323/https://www.djarumbadminton.com/internasional/berita/read/russia-open-adnanmychelle-juara-indonesia-borong-tiga-gelar/ |url-status=live |archive-date=28 July 2022}}</ref> Maulana and Bandaso also finished as runners-up at the World Tour Super 100 in [[2019 Hyderabad Open (badminton)|Hyderabad]] and [[2019 Indonesia Masters Super 100|Indonesia]].<ref name="hyd19">{{cite web |last=Tan |first=Ming Wai |title=Pang Ron-Yee See finally smash down first international title |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/sport/badminton/2019/08/12/pang-ron-yee-see-finally-smash-down-first-international-title |work=The Star |date=29 August 2019 |access-date=28 July 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220728091259/https://www.thestar.com.my/sport/badminton/2019/08/12/pang-ron-yee-see-finally-smash-down-first-international-title |url-status=live |archive-date=28 July 2022}}</ref><ref name="inass19">{{cite web |last1=Laksamana |first1=Nugyasa |last2=Gonsaga |first2=Aloysius |title=Indonesia Masters 2019, Adnan/Mychelle Hanya Jadi Runner-up |url=https://bola.kompas.com/read/2019/10/06/19550028/indonesia-masters-2019-adnan-mychelle-hanya-jadi-runner-up?page=all |publisher=Kompas |date=6 October 2019 |access-date=28 July 2022 |language=id |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220728094650/https://bola.kompas.com/read/2019/10/06/19550028/indonesia-masters-2019-adnan-mychelle-hanya-jadi-runner-up?page=all |url-status=live |archive-date=28 July 2022}}</ref>

In 2022, Maulana competed with new partner [[Indah Cahya Sari Jamil]] and reached the finals of [[Indonesia International]].<ref name="iic23">{{cite web |title=Rekap Final Indonesia International Challenge 2022, Tuan Rumah Raih 3 Gelar |url=https://skor.id/post/rekap-final-indonesia-international-challenge-2022-tuan-rumah-raih-3-gelar-01433565 |publisher=Skor |date=2 October 2022 |access-date=2 October 2022 |language=id}}</ref>

=== 2023 ===
In February, Maulana started the season with new partner [[Nita Violina Marwah]] playing in mixed doubles, and reach the semi-finals of [[Iran Fajr International]].

In March, Maulana and Marwah reached the finals of [[Thailand International (badminton)|Thailand International]].<ref name="tic23">{{cite web |last=Pradipta |first=Benediktus Agya |title=Hasil Thailand International Challenge: Indonesia Tanpa Gelar, Adnan/Nita Kalah di Final |url=https://www.kompas.com/badminton/read/2023/03/12/18170308/hasil-thailand-international-challenge-indonesia-tanpa-gelar-adnan-nita |publisher=Kompas |date=13 March 2023 |access-date=18 March 2023 |language=id}}</ref> In the end of March, they competed in the European tour at the [[2023 Spain Masters|Spain Masters]], but had to lose in the second round from Danish pair [[Mathias Thyrri]] and [[Amalie Magelund]].<ref name="esp23">{{Cite web |last= Annas |first=Wahid Fahrur |title=Hasil Spain Mastes 2023 - Adnan/Nita Gagal Jumpai Amri/Winny Usai Tersingkir Dramatis |url=https://www.bolasport.com/read/313745341/hasil-spain-mastes-2023-adnannita-gagal-jumpai-amriwinny-usai-tersingkir-dramatis |website=BolaSport |date=31 March 2023 |access-date=9 April 2023 |language=id}}</ref> In the next tour, they lost in the semi-finals of [[2023 Orléans Masters|Orléans Masters]] in France from Chinese Taipei pair [[Ye Hong-wei]] and [[Lee Chia-hsin]].<ref name="orl23">{{Cite web |last=Zilky |first=Ahmad |title=Hasil Orleans Masters 2023: Adnan/Nita Gugur, All Indonesian Final Pupus |url=https://www.kompas.com/badminton/read/2023/04/08/18014688/hasil-orleans-masters-2023-adnan-nita-gugur-all-indonesian-final-pupus |website=Kompas |date=8 April 2023 |access-date=9 April 2023 |language=id}}</ref>

In May, Maulana alongside the Indonesian team competed at the [[2023 Sudirman Cup]] in [[Suzhou]], China. He played a match in the group stage, won against [[Adam Dong]] and [[Josephine Wu]] of Canada. Indonesia advanced to the knockout stage but lost at the quarterfinals against China.<ref name="sc23">{{cite news |last=Rusdi K |first=Putra |title=Hasil Lengkap China Vs Indonesia di Perempatfinal Piala Sudirman 2023 |url=https://sport.detik.com/raket/d-6728841/hasil-lengkap-china-vs-indonesia-di-perempatfinal-piala-sudirman-2023 |website=Detik Sport |date=19 May 2023 |access-date=19 May 2023 |language=id}}</ref> In the next tournament, they lost in qualifying rounds of [[2023 Malaysia Masters|Malaysia Masters]] from Malaysian pair [[Chan Peng Soon]] and [[Cheah Yee See]].<ref name="masm23">{{cite news |last=Khoirul Huda |first=Andhika |title=Hasil Malaysia Masters 2023: Adnan Maulana/Nita Violina Gugur di Kualifikasi Usai Dihajar Wakil Tuan Rumah |url=https://sports.okezone.com/read/2023/05/23/40/2818406/hasil-malaysia-masters-2023-adnan-maulana-nita-violina-gugur-di-kualifikasi-usai-dihajar-wakil-tuan-rumah?page=1 |website=Okezone Sports |date=23 May 2023 |access-date=23 May 2023 |language=id}}</ref> In the next tour, they competed in the [[2023 Thailand Open (badminton)|Thailand Open]], but lost in the second round from 1st seed and eventual finalist Thai pair [[Dechapol Puavaranukroh]] and [[Sapsiree Taerattanachai]].<ref name="tha23">{{cite web |last=Atmoko |first=Ervan Yudhi Tri |title=Hasil Thailand Open 2023: Adnan/Nita Kalah dari Bass/Popor, Ganda Campuran Indonesia Habis |url=https://www.kompas.com/sports/read/2023/06/01/12471308/hasil-thailand-open-2023-adnan-nita-kalah-dari-bass-popor-ganda-campuran?page=all |publisher=Kompas |date=1 June 2023 |access-date=3 June 2023 |language=id}}</ref>

In June, Maulana and Marwah competed at the home tournament, [[2023 Indonesia Open|Indonesia Open]], but lost in the first round from 1st seed CHinese player [[Zheng Siwei]] and [[Huang Yaqiong]].<ref name="ina23">{{Cite web |last=Annas |first=Wahid Fahrur |title=Hasil Indonesia Open 2023 - Adnan/Nita Diterkam Pasangan No 1 yang Menggila usai Disetrap |url=https://www.bolasport.com/read/313810877/hasil-indonesia-open-2023-adnannita-diterkam-pasangan-no-1-yang-menggila-usai-disetrap |website=BolaSport |date=13 June 2023 |access-date=13 June 2023 |language=id}}</ref> In the next tour, they competed in the [[2023 Taipei Open|Taipei Open]], but lost in the first round from Thai pair [[Ruttanapak Oupthong]] and [[Jhenicha Sudjaipraparat]].<ref name="tpe23">{{cite web |last=Atmoko |first=Ervan Yudhi Tri |title=Hasil Taipei Open 2023: Febriana/Amalia ke 16 Besar, 2 Wakil Indonesia Gugur |url=https://www.kompas.com/sports/read/2023/06/21/11205088/hasil-taipei-open-2023-febriana-amalia-ke-16-besar-2-wakil-indonesia-gugur |publisher=Kompas |date=21 June 2023 |access-date=21 June 2023 |language=id}}</ref>

In early August, Maulana and Marwah competed at the [[2023 Australian Open (badminton)|Australian Open]], but had to lose in the second round from 2nd seed Chinese pair [[Feng Yanzhe]] and [[Huang Dongping]] in straight games.<ref name="aus23">{{cite web |last=Annas |first=Wahid Fahrur |title=Hasil Australian Open 2023 - Juara Indonesia Masters Terlalu Tangguh bagi Adnan/Nita |url=https://www.bolasport.com/read/313855069/hasil-australian-open-2023-juara-indonesia-masters-terlalu-tangguh-bagi-adnannita |publisher=BolaSport |date=3 August 2023 |access-date=3 August 2023 |language=id}}</ref>

=== BWF World Tour (2 titles, 3 runners-up) ===
The BWF World Tour, which was announced on 19 March 2017 and implemented in 2018,<ref>{{cite web |last=Alleyne |first=Gayle |title=BWF Launches New Events Structure |url=http://bwfbadminton.com/news-single/2017/03/19/bwf-launches-new-event-structure/ |publisher=[[Badminton World Federation]] |date=19 March 2017 |access-date=29 November 2017 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201164159/http://bwfbadminton.com/news-single/2017/03/19/bwf-launches-new-event-structure/ |archive-date=1 December 2017}}</ref> is a series of elite badminton tournaments sanctioned by the [[Badminton World Federation]] (BWF). The BWF World Tour is divided into levels of World Tour Finals, Super&nbsp;1000, Super&nbsp;750, Super&nbsp;500, Super&nbsp;300 (part of the HSBC World Tour), and the BWF Tour Super&nbsp;100.<ref>{{cite web |last=Sukumar |first=Dev |title=Action-Packed Season Ahead! |url=http://bwfworldtour.com/news-single/2018/01/10/action-packed-season-ahead/ |publisher=Badminton World Federation |date=10 January 2018 |access-date=15 January 2018 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180113162925/http://bwfworldtour.com/news-single/2018/01/10/action-packed-season-ahead/ |archive-date=13 January 2018}}</ref>
