'''''Ormopteris''''' is a genus of [[fern]]s in the subfamily [[Cheilanthoideae]] of the family [[Pteridaceae]].<ref name=PPGI/> Species are native to [[Brazil]] and [[Venezuela]].<ref name=CFLW/>

==Species==
{{As of|2019|December}}, the ''Checklist of Ferns and Lycophytes of the World'' recognized the following species:<ref name=CFLW/>
*''[[Ormopteris crenata]]'' <small>(R.M.Tryon) Barbará</small>
*''[[Ormopteris cymbiformis]]'' <small>(J.Prado) Barbará</small>
*''[[Ormopteris flavescens]]'' <small>(Fée) Barbará</small>
*''[[Ormopteris gleichenioides]]'' <small>(Gardner) J.Sm.</small>
*''[[Ormopteris pinnata]]'' <small>(Kaulf.) Lellinger</small>
*''[[Ormopteris riedelii]]'' <small>(Baker) Barbará</small>
