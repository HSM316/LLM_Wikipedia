'''Ruiz Pineda''' is a [[Caracas Metro]] station on Line 2. It was opened on 4 October 1987 as part of the inaugural section of Line 2 from [[La Paz station (Caracas)|La Paz]] to [[Las Adjuntas station|Las Adjuntas]] and [[Zoológico station|Zoológico]], on the branch leading to Las Adjuntas. The station is between [[Mamera station|Mamera]] and Las Adjuntas.<ref name="urbanrail">{{cite web |last1=Schwandl |first1=Robert |title=Caracas |url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/cara/caracas.htm |website=www.urbanrail.net}}</ref>
