'''Sadiq Mahmud Khurram''' (born 8 January 1973) is a Pakistani [[jurist]] who has been Justice of the [[Lahore High Court]] since 23 October 2018.

==Career==
Khurram was inducted into Lahore High Court (LHC) as an additional justice on 23 October 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://data.lhc.gov.pk/judges/sitting_judges/40|title=Hon'ble Sitting Judges - Lahore High Court|author=|date=|website=data.lhc.gov.pk|accessdate=30 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pakistantoday.com.pk/2018/10/22/justice-anwaarul-haq-appointed-as-lhc-chief-justice/amp/|title=Justice Anwaarul Haq appointed as LHC chief justice &#124; Pakistan Today|website=www.pakistantoday.com.pk}}</ref> He became permanent Justice of the LHC on 18 October 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pakistantoday.com.pk/2019/10/18/six-newly-confirmed-lhc-judges-take-oath/amp/|title=Six newly-confirmed LHC judges take oath|website=Pakistan Today}}</ref>
