'''Matt Monger''' (born November 15, 1961) is a former [[American football]] [[linebacker]]. He played for the [[New York Jets]] from 1985 to 1987 and for the [[Buffalo Bills]] from 1989 to 1990.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/M/MongMa20.htm |title=Matt Monger Stats |publisher=Pro-Football-Reference.com |date= |access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nfl.com/player/mattmonger/2521431/profile |title=Matt Monger, LB |work=Nfl.com |date= |access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>
