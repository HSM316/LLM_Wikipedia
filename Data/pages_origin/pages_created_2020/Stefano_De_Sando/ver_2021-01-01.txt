'''Giuseppe "Stefano" De Sando''' (born July 31, 1954) is an [[Italy|Italian]] [[actor]] and [[voice actor]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.antoniogenna.net/doppiaggio/voci/vocisdes.htm |title=Stefano De Sando’s dubbing contributions|publisher=Antoniogenna.net |date= |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>

==Biography==
Born in Pizzo, De Sando began his acting career during the mid-1970s where he was mentored on stage by [[Vittorio Gassman]] and also made several appearances on film and television.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mymovies.it/biografia/?a=91064 |title=Stefano De Sando|author= |publisher=MYmovies |date= |accessdate= 31 December 2019}}</ref> He is best known as a voice dubber and he is the official Italian voice of [[Robert De Niro]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.madmass.it/the-irishman-doppiaggio-italiano-netflix-giancarlo-giannini-leo-gullotta-stefano-de-sando/|title= The Irishman doppiaggio italiano: Netflix arruola Giancarlo Giannini, Leo Gullotta e Stefano De Sando |date=23 October 2019|publisher=madmass.it|language=it|accessdate=10 March 2020}}</ref> He took on the role since [[Ferruccio Amendola]]’s death in 2001. Other actors De Sando dubs includes [[James Gandolfini]], [[Ciarán Hinds]], [[Ben Kingsley]], [[John Goodman]] and many more.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jInOzCffOfI Intervista a STEFANO DE SANDO (2012) | enciclopediadeldoppiaggio.it]</ref>

Some of De Sando’s popular character dubbing roles include [[Walter White (Breaking Bad)|Walter White]] (portrayed by [[Bryan Cranston]]) in ''[[Breaking Bad]]'', [[Tony Soprano]] (portrayed by James Gandolfini) in ''[[The Sopranos]]'' and [[Théoden]] (portrayed by [[Bernard Hill]]) in ''[[The Lord of the Rings (film series)|The Lord of the Rings]]'' film franchise. His animated roles include Don Lino in ''[[Shark Tale]]'', Clayton in ''[[Tarzan (1999 film)|Tarzan]]'' and Buck Strickland in ''[[King of the Hill]]''.
