'''Giuseppe "Stefano" De Sando''' (born 31 July 1954) is an Italian actor and voice actor.<ref name=mondo>{{cite web|url=https://www.antoniogenna.net/doppiaggio/voci/vocisdes.htm|title=Stefano De Sando's voice acting and dubbing contributions|website=Il mondo dei doppiatori, antoniogenna.net |date= |accessdate=31 December 2019|language=it}}</ref>

== Biography ==
Born in [[Pizzo Calabro]], De Sando began his acting career during the mid-1970s, when he was mentored on stage by [[Vittorio Gassman]].<ref name=Elle>{{cite web|author=Lucia Resta|url=https://www.elle.com/it/showbiz/a37044616/stefano-de-sando-biografia-carriera/|title=Stefano De Sando: scopri tutta la biografia e la carriera|website=elle.com|date=17 July 2021 }}</ref><ref name=interview>{{cite web|url=https://andrnido.wixsite.com/spray-news/post/la-voce-di-de-niro-de-sando-ricorda-gigi-proietti-con-un-video-inedito-di-nun-je-d%C3%A0-retta-romae|title=La voce di De Niro De Sando ricorda Gigi Proietti con un video inedito di Nun je dà retta Roma|author=Eduardo Sirignano|date=11 October 2021|language=it}}</ref> He later made several appearances on film and television, acting in [[Alberto Negrin]]'s [[docudrama]] ''[[Mussolini and I]]''<ref name=Elle /> and working multiple times with director [[Lamberto Bava]]. In 2007 he played the role of Apuleius in [[Giulio Base]]'s ''[[Imperium: Pompeii]]'' (2007).<ref name=rp>{{cite web|url=https://temi.repubblica.it/iniziative-shakespeare-collection-2012/2012/10/20/stefano-de-sando/|title=Stefano De Sando - The Shakespeare Collection 2012|website=[[la Repubblica|repubblica.it]]|language=it}}</ref> De Sando is well known as a voice dubber as he is the official Italian voice of [[Robert De Niro]],<ref name=rp /><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.madmass.it/the-irishman-doppiaggio-italiano-netflix-giancarlo-giannini-leo-gullotta-stefano-de-sando/|title= The Irishman doppiaggio italiano: Netflix arruola Giancarlo Giannini, Leo Gullotta e Stefano De Sando |date=23 October 2019|publisher=madmass.it|language=it|access-date=10 March 2020}}</ref> dubbing over him for the first time in ''[[The Mission (1986 film)|The Mission]]'',<ref name=interview /> later taking on the role definitively since [[Ferruccio Amendola]]’s death in 2001.<ref name=Elle /> Other actors De Sando dubs include [[James Gandolfini]], [[Ciarán Hinds]], [[Ben Kingsley]], [[John Goodman]] and many more.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jInOzCffOfI Intervista a STEFANO DE SANDO (2012) | enciclopediadeldoppiaggio.it]</ref>

Some of De Sando’s popular character dubbing roles include [[Walter White (Breaking Bad)|Walter White]] (portrayed by [[Bryan Cranston]]) in ''[[Breaking Bad]]'', [[Tony Soprano]] (portrayed by James Gandolfini) in ''[[The Sopranos]]'' and [[Théoden]] (portrayed by [[Bernard Hill]]) in ''[[The Lord of the Rings (film series)|The Lord of the Rings]]'' film franchise. His animated dubbing roles include Clayton in ''[[Tarzan (1999 film)|Tarzan]]'', Don Lino in ''[[Shark Tale]]'', Buck Strickland in ''[[King of the Hill]]'' and various characters in ''[[The Simpsons]]''.
