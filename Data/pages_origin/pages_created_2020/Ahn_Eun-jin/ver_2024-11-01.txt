'''Ahn Eun-jin''' ({{Korean|hangul=안은진}}; born May 6, 1991) is a South Korean actress. She is best known for her roles in ''[[Hospital Playlist]]'' (2020–2021) and ''[[The Good Bad Mother]]'' (2023), which gained her international recognition, as well as ''[[My Dearest]]'' (2023). She received a [[Baeksang Arts Award for Best Supporting Actress – Film|Best Supporting Actress]] nomination at the [[59th Baeksang Arts Awards]] for her role in the film ''[[The Night Owl (2022 film)|The Night Owl]]'' (2022).<ref>{{cite web|title='헤어질 결심' 대상→더 글로리' 3관왕, 이변 없는 수상[백상예술대상①]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=609&aid=0000718413|website=Newsen|last=Park|first=Soo-in|access-date=April 29, 2023|archive-date=November 12, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201112002154/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=609&aid=0000718413|url-status=live}}</ref>

Ahn Eun-jin is a member of the class of 2010 at [[Korea National University of Arts]], a group that include fellow actors and actresses such as [[Kim Go-eun]], [[Lee Yoo-young]], [[Lim Ji-yeon]], [[Park So-dam]], [[Lee Sang-yi (actor)|Lee Sang-yi]], and [[Kim Sung-cheol]], who are actively engaged in both the film and television industries.<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|title=[인터뷰] '나쁜엄마' 안은진 "난 사랑받으며 무럭무럭 자라나는 배우"|url=https://www.mk.co.kr/star/broadcasting-service/view/2023/07/515138/|website=[[Maeil Business Newspaper]]|language=ko|date=July 7, 2023|access-date=December 11, 2023|archive-date=December 11, 2023|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231211024742/https://www.mk.co.kr/star/broadcasting-service/view/2023/07/515138/|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
In 2010, Ahn enrolled at the [[Korea National University of Arts]]. She debuted in 2012 in the musical ''The Sorrows of Young Werther''.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title=≪백뉴스≫ 뮤지컬 배우 안은진, 무대를 넘어 브라운관으로...이매진아시아와 전속 계약 체결|url=https://www.100news.kr/1175|website=백뉴스|date=April 10, 2018|access-date=December 31, 2023|archive-date=March 5, 2024|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240305165836/http://www.100news.kr/1175|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2015, while she was still in college, Ahn performed in a play directed by her professor Lee Sang-woo, titled ''The Story of Tail Cotton'', to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the Chaimu Troupe. Ahn believed her performance was lacking at the time, but fellow actor [[Lee Joong-ok]] reassured her years later, saying that she had done well. Following their discussion, Ahn said she realized that the performance had marked a turning point in her career as she was able to overcome the difficulties despite being disappointed with her work.<ref>{{Cite web|title=씨네플레이|url=https://blog.naver.com/cine_play/221996140791|website=[[Naver Blog]]|access-date=December 11, 2023|archive-date=December 11, 2023|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231211023928/https://blog.naver.com/cine_play/221996140791|url-status=live}}</ref>{{Unreliable source?|reason=Per [[WP:KO/RS#UR]]|date=April 2024}}<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title=배우 안은진, 극단 차이무의 20주년 기념 공연 '꼬리솜이야기' 캐스팅|url=https://www.newsculture.press/news/articleView.html?idxno=48776|website=뉴스컬처 (NEWSCULTURE)|language=ko|date=November 6, 2015|access-date=December 11, 2023|archive-date=December 11, 2023|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231211023932/https://www.newsculture.press/news/articleView.html?idxno=48776|url-status=live}}</ref>

In 2015, Ahn also appeared in director Park Hee-soon's musical production ''Infinite Energy'', focused on a group of young people gathered in the boarding house of an eccentric inventor to create an infinite energy device. Ahn made her debut in the role of Kim Sol on September 4, 2015,  and her performance received positive reviews, praising her versatile acting, comedic scenes, and her clear vocals. The musical ran until January 3, 2016, at Daehangno TOM 1 Theater.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|title=안은진, 뮤지컬 '무한동력' 4차원 캐릭터 매력 발산 [스포츠투데이]|url=http://stoo.com/article.php?aid=18755627938|website=stoo|language=ko|access-date=December 31, 2023|archive-date=December 31, 2023|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231231070733/http://stoo.com/article.php?aid=18755627938|url-status=live}}</ref>

In April 2018, Imagine Asia announced that Ahn had signed an exclusive contract with the agency.<ref name=":2" /> In August 2019, Ahn signed an exclusive contract with Big Boss Entertainment.<ref>{{Cite web|title=[공식] '타인은 지옥이다' 안은진, 빅보스엔터테인먼트와 전속계약 체결|url=https://www.sedaily.com/NewsView/1VN58RI5O5/GL0101|website={{ill|The Seoul Economic Daily|ko|서울경제}}|language=ko|date=August 28, 2019|access-date=December 31, 2023|archive-date=December 31, 2023|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231231032835/https://www.sedaily.com/NewsView/1VN58RI5O5/GL0101|url-status=live}}</ref> Ahn auditioned for an unspecified role in a new [[medical drama]] by writer [[Lee Woo-jung]] and director [[Shin Won-ho (director)|Shin Won-ho]]. Months later, Ahn was asked to audition again and subsequently cast in the role of Chu Min-ha. Ahn said she felt lucky to be considered for the role at the last minute, having lost hope to get it after waiting such a long time.<ref name=":1" /> In August 2021, Ahn Eun-jin signed an exclusive contract with United Artist Agency (UAA).<ref name=":3" />
