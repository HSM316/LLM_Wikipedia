'''Michael Andrew Whitt''' is an American banker, politician, and a [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] member of the [[Alabama House of Representatives]], representing District 6.<ref name=bama>{{cite web|url=https://www.bamapolitics.com/alabama/alabama-government-officials/profiles/andy-whitt/|title=Andy Whitt|website=Bama Politics}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Alabama Legislature |url=http://www.legislature.state.al.us/aliswww/ISD/ALRepresentative.aspx?NAME=Whitt&OID_SPONSOR=100486&OID_PERSON=8490&SESSNAME=Regular%20Session%202020 |website=legislature.state.al.us}}</ref> Whitt is chair of the Economic Development and Tourism committee and the vice chair of the house financial services committee.<ref>{{cite web |title=Alabama Legislature - House Financial Services Committee |url=http://www.legislature.state.al.us/aliswww/ISD/HseCommittee.aspx?OID_ORGANIZATION=2948&COMMITTEE=Financial%20Services |website=legislature.state.al.us}}</ref> 
Rep. Whitt is also a member of the Alabama Commission on the Evaluation of Services.

==Personal life==
Whitt is an eighth generation Alabamian and a lifelong resident of Madison County, Al. He is a 1993 graduate of Sparkman High School, attended Calhoun Community College and [[Louisiana State University]] (LSU).<ref name=bama/> He was the vice president of the [[Alabama National BanCorporation|First American Bank]] from 1996 to 2008, and has been the senior vice president and Madison County Executive of the First National Bank since 2008.<ref>{{cite web |title=New Member Profile: Rep. Andy Whitt |url=https://www.aldailynews.com/new-member-profile-rep-andy-whitt/ |website=Alabama Daily News |language=en |date=January 29, 2019}}</ref> Whitt is married to his high school sweetheart, Jennifer Hilliard Whitt. They have 2 children.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Parker |first1=Gregg |title=Whitt Enters Race for State House, District 6 |url=https://themadisonrecord.com/2018/01/03/whitt-enters-race-for-state-house-district-6/ |website=The Madison Record |date=January 3, 2018}}</ref>
