'''Jumping Park''' (점핑파크) is the [[ski jumping hill]]s in the [[Muju Resort]]. It is located in [[Muju]], [[South Korea]]. The ski jumping hills consist of a large hill with a [[Construction point|K-point]] of 120, a normal hill with a K-point of 90, and two training hills. It hosted the [[ski jumping at the 1997 Winter Universiade]]. The latest events at the venue was the summer ski jumping held in September 2008.<ref>{{cite news |title=무주서 전국스키 점프대회 |url=https://sports.news.naver.com/general/news/read.nhn?oid=001&aid=0002278859 |publisher=Yonhap News Agency |date=23 September 2008 |language=Korean}}</ref>
