'''''Owe My Love''''' is an upcoming [[Philippine television drama]] romantic comedy series to be broadcast by [[GMA Network]]. It stars [[Lovi Poe]] and [[Benjamin Alves]]. It is set to premiere on February 15, 2021 on the network's Telebabad line up.

==Production==
Principal photography was halted in March 2020 due to the [[enhanced community quarantine in Luzon]] caused by the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.interaksyon.com/trends-spotlights/2020/03/17/164497/abscbn-gma-top-rating-shows-comeback-covid19-quarantine/ |title=Top-rating ABS-CBN, GMA shows make TV comeback as quarantine halts regular programs |author=Adel, Rosette |date=March 17, 2020 |accessdate=May 17, 2020}}</ref> Filming was continued in November 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/pepalerts/cabinet-files/153703/coco-martin-dingdong-dantes-lovi-poe |title=Mga teleserye nina Coco, Dingdong, at Lovi balik-taping na |author=Gabinete, Jojo |date=September 7, 2020 |accessdate=October 24, 2020}}</ref>

==Controversy==
Actress-singer Mystica complained about the production's mistreatment towards her. She was later fired and replaced by Patani Dano in the role in November 2020.<ref name="Mystica">{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/pepalerts/cabinet-files/155200/jervy-dano-mystica |title=Former Survivor castaway Jervy "Patani" Daño, kinuhang kapalit ni Mystica sa Owe My Love |author=Gabinete, Jojo |accessdate=November 29, 2020}}</ref>
