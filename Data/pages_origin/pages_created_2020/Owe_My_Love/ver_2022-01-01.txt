'''''Owe My Love''''' is a 2021 [[Philippine television drama]] romantic comedy series broadcast by [[GMA Network]]. Directed by Rember Gelera and Ray Gibraltar, it stars [[Lovi Poe]] and [[Benjamin Alves]]. It premiered on February 15, 2021 on the network's Telebabad line up. The series concluded on June 4, 2021 with a total of 76 episodes.

The series is streaming online on [[YouTube]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gmanetwork.com/fullepisodes/home/owe_my_love |title=Owe My Love - Home - Full Episodes |access-date=September 25, 2021}}</ref>

==Production==
Principal photography commenced in March 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gmanetwork.com/entertainment/tv/owe_my_love/77279/benjamin-alves-looks-back-at-first-owe-my-love-taping-day-with-lovi-poe/story |title=Benjamin Alves looks back at first 'Owe My Love' taping day with Lovi Poe |author=Sun, Cherry |date=May 28, 2021 |access-date=July 11, 2021}}</ref> It was halted in the same month due to the [[enhanced community quarantine in Luzon]] caused by the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.interaksyon.com/trends-spotlights/2020/03/17/164497/abscbn-gma-top-rating-shows-comeback-covid19-quarantine/ |title=Top-rating ABS-CBN, GMA shows make TV comeback as quarantine halts regular programs |author=Adel, Rosette |date=March 17, 2020 |access-date=May 17, 2020}}</ref> Filming was continued in November 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/pepalerts/cabinet-files/153703/coco-martin-dingdong-dantes-lovi-poe |title=Mga teleserye nina Coco, Dingdong, at Lovi balik-taping na |author=Gabinete, Jojo |date=September 7, 2020 |access-date=October 24, 2020}}</ref>

==Ratings==
According to [[AGB Nielsen Philippines]]' Nationwide Urban Television Audience Measurement People in television homes, the pilot episode of ''Owe My Love'' earned an 11.5% rating.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lionheartv.net/2021/02/benjamin-alves-and-lovi-poes-owe-my-love-receives-highest-ratings-so-far/ |title=Benjamin Alves and Lovi Poe’s ‘Owe My Love’ receives highest ratings so far |author=Mataro, RJ |access-date=July 29, 2021}}</ref> While the final episode scored a 9.1% rating.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lionheartv.net/2021/06/how-did-owe-my-love-finale-fare-in-tv-ratings/ |title=How did ‘Owe My Love’ finale fare in TV ratings? |author=Mia, Ron |access-date=July 29, 2021}}</ref>

==Controversy==
Actress-singer Mystica complained about the production's mistreatment towards her. She was later fired and replaced by Patani Dano in the role in November 2020.<ref name="Mystica">{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/pepalerts/cabinet-files/155200/jervy-dano-mystica |title=Former Survivor castaway Jervy "Patani" Daño, kinuhang kapalit ni Mystica sa Owe My Love |author=Gabinete, Jojo |access-date=November 29, 2020}}</ref>
