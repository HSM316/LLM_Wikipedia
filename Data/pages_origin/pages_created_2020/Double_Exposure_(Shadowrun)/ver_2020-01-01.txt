'''''Double Exposure''''' is a 1994 role-playing game adventure for ''[[Shadowrun]]'' published by [[FASA]].

==Plot summary==
''Double Exposure'' is an adventure in which a megacorporation becomes involved with a charity in Seattle.<ref>https://index.rpg.net/display-entry.phtml?mainid=4940</ref>
