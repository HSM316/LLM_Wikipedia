'''''Don't Take Your Laser to Town''''' is a 1988 role-playing game adventure for ''[[Paranoia (role-playing game)|Paranoia]]'' published by [[West End Games]].

==Plot summary==
''Don't Take Your Laser to Town'' is a scenario for Yellow Clearance troubleshooters set in WST Sector, where outlaw Black Bot is terrorizing the townsfolk.<ref name="HW"/>

==Publication history==
''Don't Take Your Laser to Town'' was written by [[Malcolm Mouris]] and [[Vern C. Hargett]], and was published by [[West End Games]] in 1988 as a 32-page book.<ref name="HW">{{cite book|last=Schick |first=Lawrence|title=Heroic Worlds: A History and Guide to Role-Playing Games|publisher=Prometheus Books |year=1991|isbn=0-87975-653-5 |page=329}}</ref>

According to Shannon Appelcline, "Though some post-Rolston releases like the pre-crash ''Don't Take Your Laser to Town'' (1988) and the time travel ''Alice through the Mirrorshades'' did well commercially, the uneven humor of the early '90s and the metaplot issues would eventually doom the line."<ref name="designers80s">{{Cite book|author=Shannon Appelcline|title=Designers & Dragons: The '80s|publisher=[[Evil Hat Productions]]|year=2014| isbn=978-1-61317-081-6}}</ref>{{rp|255}}
