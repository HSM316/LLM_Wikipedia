'''''Reencuentro''''' is the thirteenth studio album by Salvadoran singer [[Álvaro Torres]], released on March 7, 1995 through [[EMI Latin]]. It was produced by Álvaro Torres and Víctor Torres,<ref name=":1">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com.sv/books?id=3QsEAAAAMBAJ&pg=PT57&dq=%22reencuentro%22+%22alvaro+torres%22+%22released%22&hl=es&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwibutaioKfmAhXxxlkKHVOMDXgQ6AEIJzAA#v=onepage&q=%22reencuentro%22%20%22alvaro%20torres%22%20%22released%22&f=false|title=Billboard|last=Inc|first=Nielsen Business Media|date=1995-03-11|publisher=Nielsen Business Media, Inc.|language=en}}</ref> recorded in 1994<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.qobuz.com/gb-en/album/lo-esencial-de-alvaro-torres/0094635440157|title=Album Lo Esencial De..., Alvaro Torres {{!}} Qobuz: download and streaming in high quality|website=Qobuz|language=en-GB|access-date=2019-12-08}}</ref> in Entourage Studios, [[North Hollywood, Los Angeles|North Hollywood, CA]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/reencuentro-mw0000123218|title=Reencuentro - Alvaro Torres {{!}} Songs, Reviews, Credits|last=|first=|date=|website=[[AllMusic]]|language=en-us|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-27}}</ref>

The opening track, "[[Reencuentro (Álvaro Torres song)|Reencuentro]]", which was sung featuring Puerto Rican-Colombian band [[Barrio Boyzz]], was a success in Latin America and the United States, reaching the position 11 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Latin Songs|Hot Latin Tracks]] chart and number 3 in [[Latin Pop Airplay]] chart.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/alvaro-torres/chart-history/HTL|title=Alvaro Torres (Hot Latin Tracks)|last=|first=|date=|website=[[Billboard magazine|Billboard]]|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-08}}</ref><ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/alvaro-torres/chart-history/LPO|title=Alvaro Torres (Latin Pop Airplay)|last=|first=|date=|website=[[Billboard magazine|Billboard]]|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-08}}</ref>
