'''Ildefonso Lee''' (born 12 March 1936) is a Panamanian [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.olympedia.org/athletes/56470 |title=Ildefonso Lee |work=Olympedia |access-date=15 June 2020}}</ref> He competed at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]], the [[1968 Summer Olympics]] and the [[1972 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/le/ildefonso-lee-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418091457/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/le/ildefonso-lee-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Ildefonso Lee Olympic Results |access-date=30 December 2019}}</ref>
