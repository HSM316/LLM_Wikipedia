===Entrepreneur===
As an entrepreneur, Young is recognized for his work in [[health care]],<ref name="news-technically"/> [[software]]<ref name="news-founder-peabody"/> and [[e-commerce]].<ref name="news-biz-journal"/>  Young served as a judge advisor for the US [[Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services]] (CMS)’ Artificial Intelligence (AI) Health Outcomes Challenge.<ref>{{cite web
|title=CMS Artificial Intelligence (AI) Health Outcomes Challenge
|url=https://innovation.cms.gov/initiatives/artificial-intelligence-health-outcomes-challenge/
|quote=Partnering with the American Academy of Family Physicians the CMS AI Health Outcomes Challenge will engage with innovators [including Young] to harness AI solutions to predict health outcomes.
|website=CMS.GOV
}}</ref> As founder of [[Intertwine Systems]],<ref name="news-founder-peabody"/> Young worked with [[Johns Hopkins University|Johns Hopkins]] researchers to deliver cardiac monitoring software to patients in [[Trinidad and Tobago]],<ref>{{cite web
|title=Trinidad and Tobago Health Sciences Initiative
|url=https://www.hopkinsmedicine.org/international/international_affiliations/latin_america_caribbean/trinidad_tobago_health_sciences_initiative.html
|website=Johns Hopkins Medicine
}}</ref><ref>{{cite news
|title=Healing Hearts on an Island Nation
|url=https://www.hopkinsmedicine.org/news/publications/hopkins_medicine_magazine/archives/springsummer_2013/healing_hearts_on_an_island_nation_

}}</ref> and was named a 'Mobile Entrepreneur to Watch' by the [[Baltimore Business Journal]].<ref name="news-biz-journal"/> Young was a partner and Chief Technical Advisor for [[SailTime]],<ref>{{cite news
