"'''Tere Mere Beech Mein'''" ({{translation|Between You and Me}}) is a song from the 1981 Hindi film ''[[Ek Duuje Ke Liye]]'', starring [[Kamal Haasan]] and [[Rati Agnihotri]].<ref name="Premchand2018">{{cite book|author=Manek Premchand|title=Yesterday's Melodies Today's Memories|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ZJCBDwAAQBAJ&pg=PT93|date=27 December 2018|publisher=Notion Press|isbn=978-1-64429-877-0|pages=93–}}</ref> The song was composed by [[Laxmikant–Pyarelal]]. [[Anand Bakshi]] was the [[lyricist]] of this song and he won a [[Filmfare Award for Best Lyricist]] award for penning it. The song was sung by [[Lata Mangeshkar]] and [[S. P. Balasubrahmanyam]].<ref name="10 Love duets that became anthems for debutant stars">{{cite web |title=10 Love duets that became anthems for debutant stars |url=https://www.bollywoodhungama.com/news/features/10-love-duets-became-anthems-debutant-stars/ |website=[[Bollywood Hungama]] |accessdate=1 January 2020 |language=en |date=29 August 2019 |archive-date=4 December 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191204190623/https://www.bollywoodhungama.com/news/features/10-love-duets-became-anthems-debutant-stars/ |url-status=live }}</ref> [[Britney Spears]]' 2003 smash hit single [[Toxic (song)|Toxic]] samples some of the song.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://uproxx.com/music/britney-spears-toxic-samples-bollywood-song/|title=Does Britney Spears 'Toxic' Owe Its Unshakeable Melody To This Bollywood Song?|publisher=[[Uproxx]]|author=Morgan, Chris|date=21 June 2016|accessdate=10 June 2017|archive-date=26 August 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170826155658/http://uproxx.com/music/britney-spears-toxic-samples-bollywood-song/|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Theme ==
In the song, the lyricist claims that he has a long-standing relationship and a deep bond with his lover. Neither of them is able to understand the reason or the depth of the relationship. Each of them remains sleepless at night, pining for the other. If one of them is in trouble, the other one feels troubled as well.
