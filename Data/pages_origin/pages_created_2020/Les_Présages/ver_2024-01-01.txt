'''''Les Présages''''' is a [[ballet]] [[Choreography|choreographed]] by [[Léonide Massine]] to music from [[Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky|Tchaikovsky]]'s [[Symphony No. 5 (Tchaikovsky)|Symphony No. 5]], with sets and costumes by [[André Masson]]. The premiere was performed on 13 April 1933 at the [[Opéra de Monte Carlo]] by the [[Ballet Russe de Monte-Carlo|Ballets Russes de Monte-Carlo]].<ref>Norton, p.154</ref>

The ballet was Massine's first experiment with a plotless work based on symphonic music.<ref>[https://bolshoimoscow.com/?perfomance=1733&page=catalog BolshoiMoscow.com: extract from Massine's autobiography, ''My Life in Ballet'']</ref>
