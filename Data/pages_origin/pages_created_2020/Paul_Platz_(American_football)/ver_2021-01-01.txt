'''Paul Frederick Platz''' (July 24, 1920 – June 15, 1991) was an [[American football]] player and coach. He served as the head football coach at the [[University of San Diego]] in 1959, compiling a record of 1–5.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://usdtoreros.com/sports/2018/6/1/2008-football-media-guide.aspx|title=2008 Football Media Guide|year=2008|publisher=[[San Diego Toreros]]|access-date=January 1, 2020}}</ref> Platz played [[college football]] at [[California Polytechnic State University]] in [[San Luis Obispo, California]] in 1940.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://digitalcommons.calpoly.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?referer=https://www.google.com/&httpsredir=1&article=1335&context=studentnewspaper|title=Student Newspaper|publisher=[[Cal Poly SLO]]|access-date=January 1, 2020}}</ref> He later coach at [[Poway High School]] in [[San Diego County, California]] from 1961 to 1967.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://maxpreps.com/local/team/records/coaches.aspx?gendersport=boys,football&schoolid=9adef103-5c38-4dc2-a6c4-b36ea4f5400c|title=Paul Platz|publisher=[[MaxPreps.com]]|access-date=January 1, 2020}}</ref>
