'''Ruhul Amin''' (14 March 1940 – 7 January 2013) was a Bangladeshi film director.

==Biography==
Amin was born on 14 March 1940 in [[Dhaka]].<ref name=a/> His ancestors' home is situated at Pubail in [[Gazipur District|Gazipur]].<ref name=c/>

Amin's first direction, ''Nijere Haraye Khuji'' was released in 1972.<ref name=b/> He also directed films like ''Gangchil'' and ''Beiman'' which were selected for preservation in [[Bangladesh Film Archive]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ofc.bfa.gov.bd/websites/viewPage/447
