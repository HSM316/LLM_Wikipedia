The '''North American Association of Indian Students''' (NAAIS) is a [[nonprofit]] and non-partisan [[Indian Americans|Indian-American]] organization that functions as an umbrella group to cater to Indian and Indian-American students studying in the United States and Canada.<ref>https://www.guidestar.org/profile/84-4455237</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.naaindian.org/|title=NAAIS|website=www.naaindian.org|access-date=2019-12-30}}</ref> With over 200,000 Indian students and over 500,000 Indian-American students studying in US institutions, young Indians are one of the largest identity groups within the United States, behind China.<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Other One Percent|last=Chakravorty|first=Sanjoy|last2=Kapur|first2=Devesh|last3=Singh|first3=Nirvikar|date=2017-01-26|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-064874-9|doi = 10.1093/acprof:oso/9780190648749.001.0001}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theprint.in/india/education/21-fewer-indian-students-went-abroad-last-year-as-us-figures-see-biggest-drop/326433/|title=21% fewer Indian students went abroad last year as US figures saw biggest drop|last=Sharma|first=Kritika|date=2019-11-26|website=ThePrint|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theprint.in/india/governance/no-let-up-in-number-of-indians-going-to-study-in-the-us/148859/|title=India holds on to its position as one of the top two countries that send students to US|last=Sharma|first=Kritika|date=2018-11-13|website=ThePrint|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-30}}</ref> The organization is aiming to connect the variety of Indian groups on college and university campuses.

== Programs and Initiatives ==

=== Radio Mirchi x NAAIS Helpline ===
In early 2020, NAAIS partnered with [[Radio Mirchi]] to set up a phone helpline for Indian students impacted by the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic. The helpline has supported students in answering questions about visa status, travel restrictions, and university closures.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/hyderabad/coronavirus-indian-embassy-consulates-in-us-reach-out-to-local-telugu-associations/articleshow/74693512.cms|title=Coronavirus: Indian embassy, consulates in US reach out to local Telugu associations {{!}} Hyderabad News - Times of India|last=Mar 18|first=TNN {{!}}|last2=2020|website=The Times of India|language=en|access-date=2020-04-09|last3=Ist|first3=17:31}}</ref>

=== The Desi Vote===
The organization is conducting an outreach and voter registration for the 2020 elections to get more Indians to vote.<ref>https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/hyderabad/indian-americans-seek-to-play-increased-role-in-us-politics/articleshow/74700661.cms</ref>

=== Mentorship Program ===
The NAAIS mentorship program is a student-led initiative that aims to connect Indian-origin/Indian-American/Indian undergraduate students with experienced Indian alumni mentors. The program encourages support of Indian individuals from underrepresented backgrounds including but not limited to non-Hindu, LGBTQIA+, low-income, non-professional through mentorship.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.naaindian.org/work/chotto-motto-hy23k|title=Community|website=North American Association of Indian Students|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-11}}</ref>

=== The India Initiative ===
NAAIS is in the works of organizing an annual conference to help form stronger, lasting bonds between Indian-origin students.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.naaindian.org/work/events|title=Events/Lectures/Discussions|website=North American Association of Indian Students|language=en-US|access-date=2020-04-09}}</ref>
