The '''North American Association of Indian Students''' (NAAIS) is a [[nonprofit]] and non-partisan [[Indian Americans|Indian-American]] organization that functions as an umbrella group to cater to Indian and Indian-American students studying in the United States.<ref>{{Cite news|title='Living in two realities': Indian diaspora reckons with crisis abroad|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/health/2021/05/01/india-diaspora-covid-response/|access-date=2021-11-26|newspaper=Washington Post|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|last=Callimachi|first=Rukmini|date=2021-06-03|title=Colleges Say Students Must Get a Covid Vaccine. But No, Not That One.|language=en-US|work=The New York Times|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2021/06/03/us/coronavirus-vaccine-college-students.html|access-date=2021-11-26|issn=0362-4331}}</ref> The organization has 23,000 members{{cn|date=May 2023}} and aims to connect the variety of Indian groups on college and university campuses to create better resources of Indian and Indian-American students.

With over 200,000 Indian students and over 500,000 Indian-American students studying in US institutions, young Indians are one of the largest identity groups within the United States, second only to the Chinese.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theprint.in/india/education/21-fewer-indian-students-went-abroad-last-year-as-us-figures-see-biggest-drop/326433/|title=21% fewer Indian students went abroad last year as US figures saw biggest drop|last=Sharma|first=Kritika|date=2019-11-26|website=ThePrint|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-30}}</ref>

== Activities ==

=== Mentorship program ===
The NAAIS mentorship program is a student-led initiative that aims to connect Indian-origin/Indian-American/Indian undergraduate students with experienced Indian alumni mentors. The program encourages support of Indian individuals from underrepresented backgrounds including but not limited to LGBTQIA+, low-income, and non-professional students through mentorship.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.naaindian.org/work/chotto-motto-hy23k|title=Community|website=North American Association of Indian Students|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-11}} {{dead link|date=November 2021}}</ref>

=== Workshops ===
NAAIS runs a workshop series to encourage bonding between Indian-origin students.<ref>{{Cite web |last= |first= |title=North American Association of Indian Students |url=https://naais.org/tour |access-date=2022-01-10 |website=naais.org |language=en}}</ref>{{bsn|date=May 2023}}
