'''''100% Tierra Caliente''''' ({{trans|100% Hot Land }}) is a studio album by [[regional Mexican]] band, [[Beto y sus Canarios]], released on January 27, 2004. This album includes the hit single "[[Está Llorando Mi Corazón]]," which won the award for Regional Mexican Airplay of the Year by a Male Group at the [[2005 Latin Billboard Music Awards]].<ref>{{cite news|url= https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/63104/2005-billboard-latin-music-awards-winners|title=2005 Billboard Latin Music Awards Winners|work=Billboard|publisher=Nielsen Business Media|date=April 29, 2005|accessdate=April 29, 2017|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180614085947/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/63104/2005-billboard-latin-music-awards-winners|archivedate=June 14, 2018}}</ref>

It comprises fifteen tracks with different rhythms, such as [[ranchera]]s, [[cumbia]]s,  and [[Sentimental ballad|ballad]]s.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.chron.com/entertainment/music/article/Groundbreaking-work-in-tierra-caliente-sound-1913082.php|title = Groundbreaking work in tierra caliente sound|date = 17 July 2005}}</ref>
The album sold nearly 200,000 copies.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=rRQEAAAAMBAJ&q=100%25+Tierra+Caliente&pg=PA34|title = Billboard|date = 23 July 2005}}</ref>
