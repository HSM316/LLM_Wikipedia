The '''KBF Premier League''' is the premier [[basketball]] league for clubs in [[Kenya]]. The league consist out of twelve teams. The most successful team in the history of the league is [[KPA (basketball)|KPA]] with five national championships.

The champions of the KBF Premier League are eligible to play in the qualifying rounds of the [[Basketball Africa League]] (BAL).
