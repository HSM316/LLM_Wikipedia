'''André Rives''' (6 January 1924 - 11 March 2017<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ffr13.fr/actualite-international/andre-rives-vient-de-nous-quitter/|title=André RIVES vient de nous quitter...|date=2017-03-12|website=Fédération Française de Rugby à XIII|language=fr-FR|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>) was a French rugby league footballer who played as a {{rlp|fb}}.<ref>{{cite web|title=André Rives|url=http://www.rugbyleagueproject.org/players/andre-rives/summary.html|work=rugbyleagueproject.org|publisher=Shawn Dollin, Andrew Ferguson and Bill Bates|accessdate=31 August 2013}}</ref>

==Career==
Rives played for two clubs in his entire career, [[Albi XIII|Albi]] and [[Saint-Gaudens Bears|Saint-Gaudens]]. With the former, he won two French champion titles, with the latter, he won a French championship final. His club performances brought him into the [[France national rugby league team|France national team]] between 1957 and 1959, notably taking part at the [[1957 Rugby League World Cup]] and succeeding his position to [[Puig Aubert]].
