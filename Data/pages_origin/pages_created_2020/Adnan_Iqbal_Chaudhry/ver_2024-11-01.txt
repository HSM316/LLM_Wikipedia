'''Adnan Iqbal Chaudhry''' (born 15 December 1971) is a Pakistani [[jurist]] who has been Justice of the [[Sindh High Court]] since 6 February 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://shc.gov.pk/sitting_judges.php|title=Welcome to High Court of Sindh|website=shc.gov.pk|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Chaudhry was born on 15 December 1971.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://sindhhighcourt.gov.pk/Justice_Mr_Adnan_Iqbal.php|title=Welcome to High Court of Sindh|website=sindhhighcourt.gov.pk}}</ref> He earned his LL.B. in 1997 from [[Sindh Muslim Law College]] and simultaneously pursued studies in [[business administration]] at [[Adamson University]]'s Karachi Campus.<ref name="auto"/>

==Career==
Chaudhry was admitted to the Sindh Bar Council as an advocate in 1998.<ref name="auto"/> He was admitted as an advocate of the [[Sindh High Court]] in the same year, and as an advocate of the [[Supreme Court of Pakistan]] in 2012.<ref name="auto"/> Prior to his judicial career, he was a partner at a law firm in Karachi. He was appointed as a judge of the Sindh High Court on February 6, 2018.<ref name="auto"/>
