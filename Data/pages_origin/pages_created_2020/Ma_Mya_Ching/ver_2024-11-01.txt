'''Ma Mya Ching''' is a Bangladeshi politician from [[Bandarban District|Bandarban]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Nationalist Party]]. She is a former member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

Marma is the district BNP president in Bandarban.<ref name=":2">{{Cite news|date=13 October 2018|script-title=bn:বান্দরবানে আ.লীগের বীর বাহাদুর, দ্বন্দ্বে বিএনপি|work=[[Jagonews24.com]] |language=bn |url=https://www.jagonews24.com/country/news/456973|access-date=10 October 2020}}</ref> She was a member of parliament nominated by the Bangladesh Nationalist Party from 30 women's seats in the Fifth and Sixth National Assemblies.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|script-title=bn:৫ম জাতীয় সংসদে নির্বাচিত মাননীয় সংসদ-সদস্যদের নামের তালিকা|url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/5th.pdf|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180917232851/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/5th.pdf|archive-date=17 September 2018|website=[[Jatiya Sangshad]]|publisher=[[Government of Bangladesh]]|language=bn }}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|script-title=bn:৬ষ্ঠ জাতীয় সংসদে নির্বাচিত মাননীয় সংসদ-সদস্যদের নামের তালিকা|url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/6th%20Parliament%20.pdf|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180915084544/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/6th%20Parliament%20.pdf|archive-date=15 September 2018|website=[[Jatiya Sangshad]]|publisher=[[Government of Bangladesh]]|language=bn }}</ref>
