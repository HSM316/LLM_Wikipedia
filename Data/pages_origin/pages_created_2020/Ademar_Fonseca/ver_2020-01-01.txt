'''Ademar Fonseca Nogueira Júnior''' (17 April 1963 – 8 June 2017), commonly known as '''Dema''', was a Brazilian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Fonseca was the head coach of the [[Brazil women's national football team|Brazil women's national team]] at the [[1995 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
