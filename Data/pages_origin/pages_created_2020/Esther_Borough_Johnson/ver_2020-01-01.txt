'''Esther Harriet Borough Johnson''' (1866-1958, {{nee}} '''George''') was an English painter.<ref name=suffolk />

Her works are held in collections including [[Brighton and Hove Museums and Art Galleries]], [[Bushey Museum |Bushey Museum and Art Gallery]] and [[The Box, Plymouth]].<ref name="artuk" />

She wrote ''The technique of flower painting in oil, water-colour, and pastel'', published by Pitman (1931).<ref name="jisc-technique" />
