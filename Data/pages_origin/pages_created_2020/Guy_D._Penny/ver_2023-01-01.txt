'''Guy D. Penny''' (May 13, 1928 – April 8, 2019) was an [[American football]] coach and player. He served as the head football coach at [[Morehead State University]] in [[Morehead, Kentucky]] from 1959 to 1967, compiling a record of 38–40–2.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://msueagles.com/sports/2009/9/22/FB_0922090039.aspx|title=Football Media Guide|year=2009|publisher=[[Morehead State Eagles]]|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref>

Penny played [[college football]] at the [[University of Mississippi]] and later served as a [[physical education]] faculty member at [[Middle Tennessee State University]] from 1970 to 1993.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://woodfinchapel.com/obituary/guy-d-penny|title=Guy D. Penny|publisher=woodfinchapel.com|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref>
