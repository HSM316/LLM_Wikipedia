'''Balvinder Sopal''' (born 4 December 1978) is an English actress, whose acting career has spanned for over two decades. She rose to prominence for her portrayal as [[Suki Panesar]] in the [[BBC One|BBC]] soap opera ''[[EastEnders]]'' since January 2020. Her most notable storyline includes her on-off affair with [[Eve Unwin]]. Sopal has also appeared in other British soap operas including ''[[Emmerdale]]'', ''[[Coronation Street]]'', ''[[Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors]]'' and ''[[Hollyoaks]]''.

==Career==
Sopal made her professional acting debut as Bhelua in ''[[The Beautiful Violin]]'' at [[Oldham Coliseum Theatre]] in 2001.<ref>[http://www.brad.ac.uk/theatre/find-out-about-us/whos-who/Iain_Bloomfield/ Iain Bloomfield] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110613101751/http://www.brad.ac.uk/theatre/find-out-about-us/whos-who/Iain_Bloomfield/ |date=13 June 2011}}</ref> She then went on to appear in stage productions such as ''[[Absent Friends (play)|Absent Friends]]'', ''[[Relatively Speaking (Ayckbourn play)|Relatively Speaking]]'' and ''The Tales of the Harrow''.<ref name="Spotlight">{{Cite web|url=https://www.spotlight.com/7819-9088-6740|title=Spotlight: Balvinder Sopal|website=Spotlight|access-date=22 December 2019|archive-date=22 December 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191222201742/https://www.spotlight.com/7819-9088-6740|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="Profiling">{{Cite news|url=http://www.dickandlottie.com/profiling-balvinder-sopal/|title=Profiling: Balvinder Sopal|website=Dick and Lottie|access-date=22 December 2019|archive-date=22 December 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191222202537/http://www.dickandlottie.com/profiling-balvinder-sopal/|url-status=live}}</ref> Sopal made her onscreen debut in a 2001 episode of the BBC series ''Kidhaar!'' as Reena directed by Kate Rowland.<ref name="Spotlight" /> In 2008, she appeared in the [[BBC One|BBC]] film ''[[White Girl (2008 film)|White Girl]]'' as Fatima.<ref name="Profiling" />

From 2009 to 2018, Sopal made numerous appearances in four [[Soap operas#United Kingdom|British soap operas]]; ''[[Emmerdale]]'', ''[[Coronation Street]]'', ''[[Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors]]'' and ''[[Hollyoaks]]''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.radiotimes.com/news/tv/2019-12-22/eastenders-casts-balvinder-sopal-as-suki-panesar/amp/|title=EastEnders casts Balvinder Sopal as Suki Panesar|work=[[Radio Times]]|access-date=29 December 2019|archive-date=22 December 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191222154829/https://www.radiotimes.com/news/tv/2019-12-22/eastenders-casts-balvinder-sopal-as-suki-panesar/amp/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.whatsontv.co.uk/news/eastenders-announces-powerful-ruthless-and-machiavellian-new-character-arriving-in-the-new-year-605011/amp/|title=EastEnders announces 'powerful, ruthless and Machiavellian' new character arriving in the New Year|last=Slade|first=Alison|date=22 December 2019|access-date=29 December 2019|archive-date=22 December 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191222075503/https://www.whatsontv.co.uk/news/eastenders-announces-powerful-ruthless-and-machiavellian-new-character-arriving-in-the-new-year-605011/amp/|url-status=live}}</ref> In December 2019, Sopal was cast in her first regular role in the [[BBC One|BBC]] soap opera ''[[EastEnders]]'' as [[Suki Panesar]].<ref name="Casting">{{Cite news|url=https://www.digitalspy.com/soaps/eastenders/a30304983/eastenders-balvinder-sopal-suki-panesar/|title=Former Coronation Street star Balvinder Sopal joins EastEnders as the Panesars' mum|last=Edwards|first=Chris|work=[[Digital Spy]]|date=22 December 2019|access-date=29 December 2019|archive-date=25 December 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191225210439/https://www.digitalspy.com/soaps/eastenders/a30304983/eastenders-balvinder-sopal-suki-panesar/|url-status=live}}</ref> Her first scene aired on 27 January 2020.<ref name="Casting" />
