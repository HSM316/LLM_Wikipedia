'''Sadık Pekünlü''' (21 July 1938 &ndash; 14 July 1973) was a Turkish [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]]. He competed in the [[Weightlifting at the 1964 Summer Olympics – Men's 90 kg|men's middle heavyweight event]] at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pe/sadik-pekunlu-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418122346/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pe/sadik-pekunlu-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Sadık Pekünlü Olympic Results |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>
