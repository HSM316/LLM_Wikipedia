'''Pedro Serrano''' (9 November 1931 &ndash; 10 November 2017) was a Puerto Rican [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.copur.pr/fallece-pesista-pedro-serrano/ |title=Fallece el pesista Pedro Serrano |work=copur |access-date=14 October 2020}}</ref> He competed at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]] and the [[1968 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/se/pedro-serrano-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418105241/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/se/pedro-serrano-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Pedro Serrano Olympic Results |access-date=30 December 2019}}</ref>
