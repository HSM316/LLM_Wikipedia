'''Aung Shwe Prue Chowdhury''' (1 August 1914 – 8 August 2012) was a [[Bangladesh]]i from [[Bandarban District|Bandarban]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Nationalist Party]]. He was the 15th King of [[Bohmong Circle]].<ref name=a/>

==Biography==
Chowdhury was born on 1 August 1914.<ref name=b/> He was elected as a member of the [[East Pakistan Legislative Assembly]] in 1970.<ref name=c/> He was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Chittagong Hill Tracts-2 in 1979.<ref name="1979results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/2nd.pdf |title=List of 2nd Parliament Members  |website=Jatiya Sangsad |format=PDF|language=bn |access-date=13 August 2014}}</ref> He was also appointed as the state minister of the [[Ministry of Food (Bangladesh)|Ministry of Food]].<ref name=d>{{cite web|url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/print-edition/2nd-rajdhani/2018/12/19/716114|title=বান্দরবানে বীর বাহাদুর ও সাচিং প্রু টেনশনে|date=19 December 2018|accessdate=1 January 2019|website=Kaler Kantho|language=bn
}}</ref>

Chowdhury was appointed as the King of [[Bohmong Circle]] on 19 November 1998.<ref name=c/> He remained as the King of [[Bohmong Circle]] till his death.
