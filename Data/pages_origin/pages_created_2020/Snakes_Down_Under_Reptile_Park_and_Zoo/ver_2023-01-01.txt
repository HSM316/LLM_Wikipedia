'''Snakes Downunder Reptile Park and Zoo''' is a zoo situated in [[Childers, Queensland|Childers]], Queensland, Australia.<ref name=ZAA>{{cite web|url=https://www.zooaquarium.org.au/public/Locate/Public/Locate.aspx |title=ZAA Accredited Member Location Map |accessdate=1 January 2020 |publisher=[[Zoo and Aquarium Association]]}}</ref> The zoo features Australian reptiles, amphibians and marsupials as well as [[meerkats]].
<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.snakesdownunder.com/ |title=Snakes Downunder Reptile Park & Zoo|accessdate=1 January 2020 |publisher=Snakes Downunder Reptile Park and Zoo}}</ref>

===Other animals===
Their amphibian species are the [[Australian green tree frog]] and [[white-lipped tree frog]].

Their bird species are [[emu]] and [[hyacinth macaw]].

Their mammal species are [[red kangaroo]], [[eastern grey kangaroo]], [[Lumholtz's tree kangaroo]], [[koala]] and [[meerkat]].
