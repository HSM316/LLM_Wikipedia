'''Jannatul Ferdous''' ({{circa|1938}} – 1 February 2012) was a [[Bangladesh]]i teacher and politician from [[Pabna District|Pabna]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Awami League]]. She was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Ferdous was an assistant teacher of [[Pabna Government Girls' High School]].<ref name=a/> Later, she joined Pabna Government Women's College and worked there from 1974 to 1995.<ref name=b/> She was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Reserved Women's Seat-5 in the [[June 1996 Bangladeshi general election|Seventh Jatiya Sangsad Election]].<ref name="1996results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/index.php/en/mps/members-of-parliament/former-mp-s/list-of-7th-parliament-members-bangla |format=PDF|title=List of 7th Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=6 March 2018}}</ref>

Ferdous was married to [[Amjad Hossain (lawyer)|Amjad Hossain]].<ref name=a/> He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] too. They had two daughters and one son.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://m.prothomalo.com/bangladesh/article/483880/আমজাদ-হোসেন
|title=আমজাদ হোসেন
|website=Prothom Alo|language=bn|date=23 March 2015|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>
