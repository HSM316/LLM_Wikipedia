'''Jannatul Ferdous''' ({{circa|1938}} – 1 February 2012) was a Bangladeshi teacher and politician from [[Pabna District|Pabna]] belonging to the [[Bangladesh Awami League]]. She was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Ferdous was an assistant teacher of [[Pabna Government Girls' High School]].<ref name=a/> Later, she joined Pabna Government Women's College and worked there from 1974 to 1995.<ref name=b/> She was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Reserved Women's Seat-5 in the [[June 1996 Bangladeshi general election]].<ref name="1996results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/index.php/en/mps/members-of-parliament/former-mp-s/list-of-7th-parliament-members-bangla |format=PDF|title=List of 7th Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=6 March 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924064449/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/7th%20Parliament%20.pdf |archive-date=24 September 2015}}</ref>

Ferdous was married to [[Amjad Hossain (lawyer)|Amjad Hossain]].<ref name=a/> He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] too. They had two daughters and one son.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.prothomalo.com/bangladesh/%E0%A6%86%E0%A6%AE%E0%A6%9C%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%A6-%E0%A6%B9%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%B8%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%A8 |script-title=bn:আমজাদ হোসেন |work=Prothom Alo|language=bn|date=23 March 2015|access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>

Ferdous died on 1 February  2012 at the age of 74.<ref name=a>{{cite news |url=https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/85808.details|script-title=bn:সাবেক সাংসদ অধ্যাপিকা জান্নাতুল ফেরদৌস আর নেই|work=Banglanews24.com|language=bn|date=2 February 2012|access-date=1 January 2020|archive-date=1 January 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101070905/https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/85808.details|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name=b>{{cite news |url=https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/85894.details|script-title=bn:সাবেক সাংসদ জান্নাতুল ফেরদৌসের দাফন সম্পন্ন|work=Banglanews24.com|language=bn|date=2 February 2012|access-date=1 January 2020|archive-date=1 January 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101065508/https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/85894.details|url-status=dead}}</ref>
