'''Chong Tsu-pin''' ({{zh|c=張子濱|p=Zhāng Zǐbīn}}) is a Taiwanese [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Chong was the head coach of the [[Chinese Taipei women's national football team|Chinese Taipei women's national team]] at the [[1991 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
