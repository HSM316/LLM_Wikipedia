'''Víctor Ángel Pagán''' (born 20 February 1939) is a Puerto Rican [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]]. He competed at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]], the [[1968 Summer Olympics]] and the [[1972 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pa/victor-angel-pagan-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418092911/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pa/victor-angel-pagan-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Víctor Ángel Pagán Olympic Results |access-date=31 December 2019}}</ref>
