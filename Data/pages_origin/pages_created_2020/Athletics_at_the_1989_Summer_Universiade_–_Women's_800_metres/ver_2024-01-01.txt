Universiade: Ein Hauch von Olympia im Ruhrevier |journal=Leichtathletik |date=7 September 1989 |volume=36/1989 |pages=1134–1139 |trans-title=XV. Universiade: A touch of the Olympics in the Ruhrevier |publisher=Bartels & Wernitz |language=German |issn=0343-5369}}</ref><ref>[https://www.tilastopaja.eu/db/results.php?Season=1989&CID=12982645 Results at Tilastopaja]</ref>
