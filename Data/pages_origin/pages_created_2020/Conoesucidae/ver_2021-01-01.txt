'''Conoesucidae''' is a family of caddisflies in the order [[Trichoptera]]. There are about 12 genera and more than 40 described species in Conoesucidae.<ref name=itis/><ref name=gbif/><ref name=Morse2019/>

==Genera==
These 12 genera belong to the family Conoesucidae:
{{Div col|colwidth=22em}}
* ''[[Beraeoptera]]'' <small>Mosely, 1953</small>
* ''[[Coenoria]]'' <small>Mosely, 1953</small>
* ''[[Confluens (caddisfly)|Confluens]]'' <small>Wise, 1962</small>
* ''[[Conoesucus]]'' <small>Mosely, 1936</small>
* ''[[Costora]]'' <small>Mosely, 1936</small>
* ''[[Hampa (caddisfly)|Hampa]]'' <small>Mosely, 1953</small>
* ''[[Lingora]]'' <small>Mosely, 1936</small>
* ''[[Matasia]]'' <small>Mosely, 1936</small>
* ''[[Olinga (caddisfly)|Olinga]]'' <small>McLachlan, 1894</small>
* ''[[Periwinkla]]'' <small>McFarlane, 1973</small>
* ''[[Pycnocentria]]'' <small>McLachlan, 1866</small>
* ''[[Pycnocentrodes]]'' <small>Tillyard, 1924</small>
{{Div col end}}
