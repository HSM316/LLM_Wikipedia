'''Neil Turnbull''' (born 21 July 1959) is an English-Canadian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Turnbull was the head coach of the [[Canada women's national soccer team|Canada women's national team]] at the [[1999 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
