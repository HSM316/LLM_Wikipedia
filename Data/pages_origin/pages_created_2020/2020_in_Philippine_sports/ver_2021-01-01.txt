===Other events===
* [[March 8]] - After one year of broadcasts, [[5 Plus]] rebrands as [[One Sports (TV channel)|One Sports]]. In addition of transferring the name from cable to free-to-air, its cable and satellite counterpart has been also rebranded to [[One Sports+]].

* [[March 12]] 
** The major sports leagues across the country announce a temporary suspension of play in an attempt to slow the spread of the coronavirus. the [[Philippine Basketball Association]], the [[PBA Developmental League]], and the [[Philippine Super Liga]] announces an indefinite suspension of league activity, while the [[Maharlika Pilipinas Basketball League]] and the [[National Basketball League (Philippines)|National Basketball League]] announced that will suspend the remainder of the playoffs. In addition, the [[National Collegiate Athletic Association]] and the [[University Athletic Association of the Philippines]] games are canceled. The suspension of the four leagues adds a large schedule void for [[5 (TV network)|5]], [[One Sports (Philippines)|One Sports]], [[One Sports+]], [[PBA Rush]], [[S+A]], [[Liga (TV channel)|Liga]] and [[Solar Sports]].
** The major sports leagues across the country announce a temporary suspension or termination of play in an attempt to slow the spread of the coronavirus.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.spin.ph/life/guide/list-of-sports-events-suspended-coronavirus-a1374-20200312 | title=Here's a list of sports events suspended due to coronavirus | publisher=Spin.PH | date=March 12, 2020 | accessdate=March 12, 2020}}</ref>

* [[March 30]] — [[One Sports (Philippines)|One Sports]] temporary suspends broadcast due to limitations brought about by the Enhanced Community Quarantine in Luzon, with [[5 (TV channel)|5]] taking over some of its archived programming.
