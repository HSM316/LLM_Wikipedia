'''Ma Yuanan''' ({{zh|c=马元安|p=Mǎ Yuánān}}; born 9 April 1945) is a Chinese former [[Association football|footballer]] and [[Manager (association football)|manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Ma played football before retiring in 1975, and began coaching afterwards. He was the head coach of the [[China women's national football team|China women's national team]] from 1992 until 2001.<ref name="resume"/> He led the team at four major international tournaments, the [[1995 FIFA Women's World Cup]], [[Football at the 1996 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|1996 Summer Olympics]], [[1999 FIFA Women's World Cup]] and [[Football at the 2000 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|2000 Summer Olympics]].
