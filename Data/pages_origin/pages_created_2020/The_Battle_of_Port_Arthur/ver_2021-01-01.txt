{{Nihongo|'''''The Battle of Port Arthur'''''|二百三高地| Ni hyaku san kochi  |lead=yes}} is a 1980 Japanese war film directed by [[Toshio Masuda (director)|Toshio Masuda]].<ref>{{cite web|URL=https://kotobank.jp/word/%E4%BA%8C%E7%99%BE%E4%B8%89%E9%AB%98%E5%9C%B0-592596|title=二百三高地とは|publisher=ことバンク|accessdate=29 December 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|URL= https://www.amazon.co.jp/%E4%BA%8C%E7%99%BE%E4%B8%89%E9%AB%98%E5%9C%B0-DVD-%E4%BB%B2%E4%BB%A3%E9%81%94%E7%9F%A2/dp/B0000TCN88 |title=二百三高地 |publisher=アマゾン|accessdate=19 March 2017}}</ref><ref name="Eirin">{{cite web|URL=http://db.eiren.org/contents/03000001003.html|title=二百三高地|publisher=映倫|accessdate=19 March 2017}}</ref> The Japanese title "Ni hyaku san kochi" means [[203 Hill]].<ref>{{cite web|URL=https://www.japanese-cinema-db.jp/Details?id=13371|title=二百三高地|publisher=日本映画情報|accessdate=19 March 2017}}</ref> The film depicts the fiercest battles in [[Siege of Port Arthur]] during the [[Russo-Japanese War]] 1904 - 1905.<ref name="Eirin"/><ref>{{cite web|URL=https://eiga.com/movie/38463/ |title=二百三高地 |publisher=映画|accessdate=19 March 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|URL=https://www.cinematoday.jp/news/N0117883|title=公開から40年『二百三高地』丸の内TOEIでデジタル上映決定|publisher=シネマトゥディ|accessdate=29 December 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|URL=https://realsound.jp/movie/2020/08/post-599089.html|title=仲代達矢主演『二百三高地』丸の内TOEIで限定公開へ　40年の時を経て初のデジタル上映|publisher=リアルサウンド|accessdate=29 December 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|URL=https://thetv.jp/program/0000019263/1/|title=二百三高地|publisher=ザテレビジョン|accessdate=29 December 2020}}</ref>
