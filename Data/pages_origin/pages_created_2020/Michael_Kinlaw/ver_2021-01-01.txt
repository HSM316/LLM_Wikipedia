'''Michael Wayne Kinlaw''' (born 1973) is an [[United States|American]] [[mortgage broker]] and politician from [[Texas]] who was a [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] candidate for the [[2016 United States presidential election]]. He is currently living in [[Colorado Springs, Colorado]]. He was also the founder of the [[Houston]]-based company Manhattan Mortgage, running it from 2002 to its closure in 2008.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://progressinlending.com/2015/05/04/executive-spotlight-michael-kinlaw-mortgage-broker-turned-presidential-candidate/|title=progressinlending.com {{!}} Executive Spotlight: Michael Kinlaw, Mortgage Broker Turned Presidential Candidate|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.politico.com/story/2013/11/2016-election-fringe-hopefuls-100473.html|title=2016 already here for fringe hopefuls|last=Gavin|first=Patrick|website=POLITICO|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ballotpedia.org/Michael_Wayne_Kinlaw|title=Michael Wayne Kinlaw|website=Ballotpedia|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>
