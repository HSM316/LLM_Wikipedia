The '''World Athletics Continental Tour''' is an annual series of [[track and field]] athletic competitions, recognised by [[World Athletics]] (formerly known as the IAAF). The Tour forms the second tier of international one-day meetings after the [[Diamond League]].<ref name="worldathletics">[https://www.worldathletics.org/competitions/world-athletics-continental-tour/news/world-athletics-continental-tour-launch-2020 "World Athletics Continental Tour to launch in 2020 "]. ''[[World Athletics]]''. 23 November 2019. Retrieved 21 April 2021.</ref><ref>[https://www.athleticsweekly.com/athletics-news/triple-jump-and-200m-cut-from-2020-diamond-league-1039926366/ "Triple jump and 200m cut from 2020 Diamond League"]. ''[[Athletics Weekly]]''. Retrieved 21 April 2021.</ref> The inaugural season was in [[2020 World Athletics Continental Tour|2020]].
