'''Doug Stewart''' is a game designer who edited the ''[[Monstrous Manual]]'', a compilation of monsters from the 2nd edition of the [[role-playing game]] ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]''. He was also a designer for RPG rulebooks ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Basic Set#1994 revision|The Classic Dungeons & Dragons Game]]'', ''[[Encyclopedia Magica]]'', and ''[[Player's Secrets of Stjordvik]]''.<ref name=RPGGEEK>{{cite web | author=<!--not listed--> | title = Doug Stewart RPG Designer | url = https://rpggeek.com/rpgdesigner/16169/doug-stewart | publisher = BoardGameGeek | website = RPGGeek | accessdate=13 March 2020 }}</ref> He is credited as a designer on the adventure supplements ''Children of the Night: Ghosts and The Naval Architect's Manual'', as well as the adventure paths Castle Spulzeer and Missions of State.<ref name=RPGGEEK/>
