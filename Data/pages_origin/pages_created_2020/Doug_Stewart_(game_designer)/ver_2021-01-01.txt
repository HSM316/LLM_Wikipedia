'''Doug Stewart''' is a game designer who edited the 2nd edition ''[[Monstrous Manual]]'', a compilation of monsters from the [[role-playing game]] ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Doug_Stewart|title=Doug Stewart|website=Forgotten Realms Wiki|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> He was also a designer for RPG rulebooks [[Dungeons & Dragons Basic Set#1994 revision|The Classic Dungeons & Dragons Game]], [[Encyclopedia Magica]], and [[Player's Secrets of Stjordvik]].<ref name=RPGGEEK>{{cite web | author=<!--not listed--> | title = Doug Stewart RPG Designer | url = https://rpggeek.com/rpgdesigner/16169/doug-stewart | publisher = BoardGameGeek | website = RPGGeek | accessdate=13 March 2020 }}</ref> He is credited as a designer on the adventure supplements Children of the Night: Ghosts and The Naval Architect's Manual, as well as the adventure paths Castle Spulzeer and Missions of State.<ref name=RPGGEEK/>
