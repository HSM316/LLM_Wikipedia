'''Carl Enow Ngachu''' (born 21 February 1975) is a Cameroonian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Enow was the head coach of the [[Cameroon women's national football team|Cameroon women's national team]] at the [[Football at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|2012 Summer Olympics]] and [[2015 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
