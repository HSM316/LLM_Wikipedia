'''Artigas''' is a [[Caracas Metro]] station on Line 2. It was opened on 6 November 1988 as part of the extension of the line from La Paz to [[El Silencio station|El Silencio]]. The station is between [[Maternidad station|Maternidad]] and [[La Paz station (Caracas)|La Paz]].<ref name="urbanrail">{{cite web |last1=Schwandl |first1=Robert |title=Caracas |url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/cara/caracas.htm |website=www.urbanrail.net}}</ref>
