'''Kutrai''' is a village in Jagat [[Block (district subdivision)|block]], [[Budaun district]], [[Uttar Pradesh]], India. Its village code is 128433.<ref>{{cite web |title=Uttar Pradesh Village List |url=http://upbhulekh.gov.in/public/village_list_new.jsp?pageId=RGPPR&dcode=149&tcode=00782&tname=%E0%A4%AC%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%82%E0%A4%81&dname=%E0%A4%AC%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%82%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%82 |website=upbhulekh.gov.in}}</ref> [[Budaun railway station]] is 11&nbsp;km away from the village.<ref>{{cite web |title=Kutrai, Jagat Village information {{!}} Soki.In |url=https://soki.in/kutrai-jagat-budaun |website=soki.in}}</ref> According to [[2011 Census of India]] population of the village is 2,207, in which 1,210 are males and 997 are females.
