'''Jean-René Fournier''' (born 18 December 1957) is a Swiss politician. Notably, he was a member of the [[Council of States (Switzerland)|Council of States]] from 2007 to 2019, serving as the President of the Council from 2018 to 2019. Previously, he was a member of the [[Grand Council of Valais]] from 1985 to 1997 and a Councillor of State of Valais from 1997 to 2009.<ref name=Bio>{{cite web |url= https://www.parlament.ch/fr/biografie/jean-rené-fournier/3915 |title=Jean-René Fournier |publisher=Parliament of Switzerland |accessdate=2019-12-30}}</ref> He was president of the cantonal executive from 2000 to 2001 and from 2004 to 2005.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.vs.ch/web/communication/detail?groupId=529400&articleId=4452498 |title= Election de Jean-René Fournier à la présidence du Conseil des Etats - Le Valais accueillera le président élu le mercredi 28 novembre 2018 |publisher=Canton of Valais |date=2018-11-26}}</ref>

Fournier was elected as the President of the Council of States for the 2018–2019 term with 44 out of 45 votes. He was the fourth Valais representative to be elected to the post.<ref name=Election>{{cite news |url= https://www.lenouvelliste.ch/articles/suisse/le-pdc-valaisan-jean-rene-fournier-est-elu-president-du-conseil-des-etats-802648 |title= Le PDC valaisan Jean-René Fournier est élu président du Conseil des Etats |language=French |newspaper=Le Nouvelliste |date=2018-11-26}}</ref>

Fournier did not run for re-election in [[2019 Swiss federal election|2019]]. He was succeeded by [[Marianne Maret]].<ref name=PDC>{{cite news |url= https://www.letemps.ch/suisse/valais-citadelle-pdc-reste-imprenable-0 |title= En Valais, la citadelle PDC reste imprenable |language=French |newspaper=Le Temps |date=2019-11-30}}</ref>
