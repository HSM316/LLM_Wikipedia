'''Crocodylus Park''' is a zoo situated in [[Knuckey Lagoon, Northern Territory]], Australia.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.crocodyluspark.com.au/|title=Crocodylus Park|access-date=2023-01-01}}</ref> It specialises in the conservation of [[saltwater crocodile|saltwater]] and [[freshwater crocodile]]s and features a comprehensive crocodile museum. The park also has big cats, monkeys, birds, turtles and snakes.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.crocodyluspark.com.au/about-us.html |title=About Us|accessdate=1 January 2020 |publisher=Crocodylus Park}}</ref>
