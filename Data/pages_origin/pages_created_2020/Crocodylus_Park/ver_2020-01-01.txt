'''Crocodylus Park''' is a zoo situated in [[Berrimah, Northern Territory|Berrimah]], Northern Territory, Australia.<ref name=ZAA>{{cite web|url=https://www.zooaquarium.org.au/public/Locate/Public/Locate.aspx |title=ZAA Accredited Member Location Map |accessdate=1 January 2020 |publisher=[[Zoo and Aquarium Association]]}}</ref> It specialises in the conservation of  saltwater and freshwater crocodiles and features a comprehensive crocodile museum. The park also has big cats, monkeys, birds, turtles and snakes.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.crocodyluspark.com.au/about-us.html |title=About Us|accessdate=1 January 2020 |publisher=[[Crocodylus Park]]}}</ref>
