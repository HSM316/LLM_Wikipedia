'''Meggen railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Meggen}}) is a railway station in the municipality of [[Meggen]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Lucerne|Lucerne]]. It is an intermediate stop on the standard gauge [[Lucerne–Immensee railway|Lucerne–Immensee line]] of [[Swiss Federal Railways]].
