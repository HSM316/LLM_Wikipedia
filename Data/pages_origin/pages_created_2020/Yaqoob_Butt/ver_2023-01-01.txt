'''Yaqoob Ijaz Butt''' (born 9 September 1988) is a [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]].<ref>{{Soccerway|yaqoob-ejaz-butt/288965|accessdate=21 June 2020}}</ref> Born in Denmark, he represents Pakistan at the international level.<ref>{{NFT player|id=51200|access-date=21 June 2020}}</ref> His brother, [[Yousuf Butt]], is also a footballer and has played alongside him for [[Pakistan national football team|Pakistan]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.footballpakistan.com/2014/06/mujahid-backs-home-grown-option-of-shamlan-the-news/|title=Mujahid backs home-grown option of Shamlan|website=FootballPakistan.com|publisher=FootballPakistan.Com|access-date=21 June 2020|date=12 June 2014|quote= Both Yousuf and his brother Yaqoob have already played for Pakistan.}}</ref>
