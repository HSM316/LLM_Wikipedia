The '''2007 Western Kentucky Hilltoppers football team''' represented [[Western Kentucky University]] (WKU) during the [[2007 NCAA Division I FCS football season]]. The team's head coach was [[David Elson]]. It was the team's first and only season as an [[NCAA Division I FCS independent schools|NCAA Division I FCS independent]] team as they made the transition from the [[Gateway Football Conference|Gateway Conference]]<ref>[http://www.mvc.org/football/records.pdf 2019 MVC Football Records, retrieved 30 April 2020]</ref> of [[Division I-AA]] (now FCS) to the [[Sun Belt Conference]] of the [[NCAA Division I FBS|FBS]].<ref>[https://www.bgdailynews.com/sports/wkus-move-to-i-a-tops-2006-stories/article_84ecd7b8-86ea-590c-8787-530ff758f5b6.html WKU's move to I-A tops 2006 stories, By OJ STAPLETON and MICHEAL COMPTON, The Daily News, Jan 1, 2007, retrieved 30 April 2020]</ref> The Hilltoppers played their home games at [[Houchens Industries–L. T. Smith Stadium]] in [[Bowling Green, Kentucky]].
