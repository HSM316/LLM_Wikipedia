'''Carlo Facchin''' (27 August 1938 – 27 November 2022)<ref>[https://www.siracusanews.it/calcio-siracusa-piange-la-scomparsa-di-mister-carlo-facchin/ Calcio, Siracusa piange la scomparsa di mister Carlo Facchin] {{in lang|it}}</ref> was an Italian [[association football|football]] player and manager.<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Facchin was the head coach of the [[Italy women's national football team|Italy women's national team]] at the [[1999 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
