'''Julián Eduardo Palacios''' (born 4 February 1999) is an Argentine professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Super League Greece|Greek Super League]] club [[Asteras Tripolis F.C.|Asteras Tripolis]], on loan from [[San Lorenzo de Almagro|San Lorenzo]]. He is the brother of fellow footballer [[Matías Palacios]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Matías Palacios, el pibe de 16 años que debutó ante Patronato|url=http://ar.marca.com/claro/futbol/san-lorenzo/2018/09/22/5ba581f0e5fdea784f8b4602.html|lang=pt-br|accessdate=30 December 2019|work=Marca|date=21 September 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191230201906/http://ar.marca.com/claro/futbol/san-lorenzo/2018/09/22/5ba581f0e5fdea784f8b4602.html|archive-date=30 December 2019|url-status=dead}}</ref>
