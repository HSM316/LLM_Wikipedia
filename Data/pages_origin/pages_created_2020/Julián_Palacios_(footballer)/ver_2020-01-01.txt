'''Julián Palacios''' (born 4 February 1999) is an [[Argentina|Argentine]] [[Association football|footballer]] currently playing as a [[midfielder]] for [[San Lorenzo de Almagro|San Lorenzo]] of the [[Argentine Primera División]]. He is the brother of fellow footballer [[Matías Palacios]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Matías Palacios, el pibe de 16 años que debutó ante Patronato|url=http://ar.marca.com/claro/futbol/san-lorenzo/2018/09/22/5ba581f0e5fdea784f8b4602.html|accessdate=30 December 2019|work=Marca|date=21 September 2018}}</ref>
