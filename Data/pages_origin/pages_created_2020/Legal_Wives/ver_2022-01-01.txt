'''''Legal Wives''''' is a 2021 [[Philippine television drama]] romance series broadcast by [[GMA Network]]. Directed by Zig Dulay, it stars [[Dennis Trillo]], [[Alice Dixson]], [[Andrea Torres]] and [[Bianca Umali]]. It premiered on July 26, 2021 on the network's Telebabad line up. The series concluded on November 12, 2021 with a total of 80 episodes. It was replaced by ''[[I Left My Heart in Sorsogon]]'' in its timeslot.

==Production==
''Legal Wives'' features a story of a [[Maranao people|Maranao]] Muslim royalty and his relationship with his three wives.<ref name=kabit/><ref name=threeleading/>

Actress [[Megan Young]] was initially hired for the role of Diane San Luis. In November 2020, Young left the series due to the production's lock-in filming. She was replaced by [[Andrea Torres]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://journal.com.ph/entertainment/showbiz/megan-opts-to-be-with-mikael-than-work/ |title=Megan opts to be with Mikael than work |author=Bautista, Mario |date=November 11, 2020 |access-date=February 26, 2020|work=Journal Online}}</ref> Principal photography commenced on December 1, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gmanetwork.com/entertainment/showbiznews/news/13329/sneak-peek-unang-araw-ng-legal-wives-lock-in-taping/photo |title=SNEAK PEEK: Unang araw ng 'Legal Wives' lock-in taping |trans-title=SNEAK PEEK: First day of "Legal Wives"' lock-in taping|author=Ruiz, Marah |date=December 3, 2020 |access-date=February 26, 2021|work=GMA News|language=tl}}</ref> In May 2021, actress [[Cherie Gil]] left the series due to personal reasons.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/pepalerts/pep-troika/cherie-gil-leaves-show-a4118-20210508 |title=Cherie Gil in-announce ang pag-alis sa isang teleserye |date=May 8, 2021 |access-date=June 16, 2021}}</ref>

==Ratings==
According to [[AGB Nielsen Philippines]]' Nationwide Urban Television Audience Measurement People in television homes, the pilot episode of ''Legal Wives'' earned an 11.9% rating.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lionheartv.net/2021/07/newest-kapuso-series-legal-wives-off-to-a-great-start/ |title=Newest Kapuso series, ‘Legal Wives,’ off to a great start! |author=Gacura, TJ |access-date=July 30, 2021}}</ref> While the final episode scored a 15.7% rating.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lionheartv.net/2021/11/legal-wives-finale-week-performs-well-in-ratings-remains-as-the-most-watched-drama-nationwide/ |title=‘Legal Wives’ finale week performs well in ratings, remains as the most-watched drama nationwide |access-date=November 17, 2021}}</ref>
