'''Tuomas Turriago''' (born 1979) is a [[Finland|Finnish]] [[composer]], [[pianist]] and [[Conducting|conductor]]. Since 2004 Turriago has served as senior lecturer in [[accompaniment]] at [[Tampere University of Applied Sciences]].<ref name="FG">[[Fennica Gehrman]], [https://www.fennicagehrman.fi/composers-works/turriago-tuomas/ Tuomas Turriago], 2018.</ref><ref>Ruissalo, P., [https://www.tamperelainen.fi/artikkeli/730369-1960-luvun-huuruinen-hippimusikaali-tulee-tampereelle-hair-valtaa-teatterimontun "1960-luvun huuruinen hippimusikaali tulee Tampereelle!—HAIR valtaa Teatterimontun tammikuussa"], ''Tamperelainen'', December 19, 2018.</ref> He is recognized as the first Finnish composer to record his entire piano oeuvre.<ref>Anon., [https://www.aamulehti.fi/a/43d61240-0d93-4c7c-b42d-e8253905a5ff "Tamperelainen säveltäjä teki historiaa levyttämällä kaikki pianokappaleensa—Tuomas Turriagon musiikissa ilo, värit ja rytmit kietoutuvat ihastuttavasti toisiinsa"], ''[[Aamulehti]]'', December 20, 2019.</ref><ref>Pilfink Records, [https://pilfink.fi/en/tuote/tuomas-turriago/ Tuomas Turriago: ''Complete Piano Solo Works''], 2019.</ref>

Tuomas Turriago studied piano under the tutelage of Carlos Turriago at the Central Finland Conservatory and under [[Erik T. Tawaststjerna]] at the [[Sibelius Academy]]. He was awarded first prize at the Kili Piano Competition in Sweden in 1992. Turriago has performed in Finland as a pianist for the [[Tampere Philharmonic Orchestra|Tampere Philharmonic]], TampereRaw, [[St. Michel Strings]] and the [[Jyväskylä Sinfonia]].<ref name="FG" /> He performs at music festivals in Europe, the United States, [[Colombia]] and Asia.

Tuomas Turriago is a founding member and conductor of the Tampere Chamber Opera Association. He has conducted the [[Vaasa]], [[Seinäjoki]] and [[Mikkeli]] City Orchestras, and TampereRaw, Tampere Chamber Orchestra and the Brass Band of the Tampere Philharmonic.

As of Autumn 2010 Tuomas Turriago is a member of the Finnish Composers' Association.

Since 2011 Tuomas Turriago has served as the pianist of [[Astor Piazzolla]]'s In Time Quintet. He has recorded for Alba Records and Pilfink Records, including an LP in collaboration with trumpeter Jouko Harjanne featuring trumpet sonatas by [[Harri Wessman]], Lasse Eerola, Arttu Sipilä and Turriago.<ref>[[:fi:Fuga|Fuga Oy]], [https://www.fuga.fi/?sivu=detail&id=6420617451080 ''Finnish Trumpet Sonatas''], 2012.</ref>
