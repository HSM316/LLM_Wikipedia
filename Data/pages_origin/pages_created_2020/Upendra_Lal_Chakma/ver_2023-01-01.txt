'''Upendra Lal Chakma''' ({{circa|1925}} – 18 July 2005) was a Bangladeshi  politician from [[Khagrachhari District|Khagrachhari]] belonging to [[Jatiya Samajtantrik Dal-JSD]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]]. He was an adviser to the [[President of Bangladesh]] too.<ref name=a/>

==Biography==
Chakma was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Chittagong Hill Tracts-1 in 1979 as a [[Jatiya Samajtantrik Dal-JSD]] candidate.<ref name="1979results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/2nd.pdf |title=List of 2nd Parliament Members  |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=13 August 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://samakal.com/todays-print-edition/coming-election/article/18112112/সরব-আওয়ামী-লীগবিএনপি-কোণঠাসা-আঞ্চলিক-দল
|title=সরব আওয়ামী লীগ-বিএনপি কোণঠাসা আঞ্চলিক দল
|newspaper=Samakal|language=bn|date=11 November 2018 |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> He also served as an adviser to the [[President of Bangladesh]].

Chakma died of cardiac arrest  at Khagrachhari Sadar Hospital on 18 July 2005 at the age of 80.<ref name=a>{{cite web |url=http://archive.thedailystar.net/2005/07/19/d50719061178.htm
|title=Upendra Lal Chakma passes away
|website=The Daily Star|date=19 July 2005 |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>
