'''Isam Faiz''' ({{lang-ar|عصام فايز}}; born 6 March 2000) is a [[footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[UAE Pro League]] club [[Ajman Club|Ajman]] and the [[United Arab Emirates national football team|United Arab Emirates national team]].<ref name="agleague">{{cite web|url=https://www.uaefa.ae/#/profile/76406|title=Isam Faiz|publisher=UAE Football Association|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> Born in Morocco, he acquired [[Emirati nationality law|Emirati citizenship]] in 2023.

==International career==
Faiz made his debut for the senior [[United Arab Emirates national football team|United Arab Emirates national team]] on 26 March 2024 in a [[2026 FIFA World Cup qualification – AFC second round|World Cup qualifier]] against Yemen.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=ESPN|url=https://www.espn.co.uk/football/match/_/gameId/687470/united-arab-emirates-yemen|title=Yemen v UAE game report|date=26 March 2024}}</ref>
