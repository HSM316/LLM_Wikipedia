'''Isam Faiz''' ([[Arabic]]:عصام فايز) (born 6 March 2000) is a Emirati- Born [[Morocco|Moroccan]] [[football (soccer)|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Ajman Club]].<ref name="agleague">{{cite web|url=https://www.uaefa.ae/#/profile/76406|title=Isam Faiz|publisher=UAE Football Association|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

Born in Brazil, Caio acquired [[Emirati nationality law|Emirati citizenship]] in 2023.
