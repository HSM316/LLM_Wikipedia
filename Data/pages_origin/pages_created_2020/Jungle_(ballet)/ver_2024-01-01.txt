'''''Jungle''''' is a [[ballet]] composed by the [[Dutch people|Dutch]] composer [[Henk Badings]] in 1959.<ref>Leo Samama, p.164</ref> It was choreographed by [[Rudi van Dantzig]] for the [[Dutch National Ballet]], with sets and costumes by [[Toer van Schayk]], and premiered on 20 December 1961 in Amsterdam.<ref>Rudi van Dantzig (unpaginated)</ref>
