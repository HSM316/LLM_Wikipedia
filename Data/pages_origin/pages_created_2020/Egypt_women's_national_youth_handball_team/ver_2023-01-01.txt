The '''Egypt women's national youth handball team''' represents [[Egypt]] in Women's Youth [[Handball]]. It is administered  by the [[Egyptian Handball Federation]]. It is the most successful team in the continent, holding [[African Women's Youth Handball Championship|4 continental wins]] followed by [[Angola women's youth national handball team|Angola]] with 3 continental wins.
