'''Back to Black''' was a band formed in [[Minneapolis]] around 1970. They changed their name to The Family, a name later adopted by [[Prince (musician)|Prince]] for [[The Family (band)|The Family]]. They were the house band at The Way community center, and performed at the [[Phyllis Wheatley Community Center]] (both now demolished). Prince considered them a lifelong influence.
