'''National Tertiary Route 929''', is a road in the [[Nicoya Peninsula]] located in the [[Guanacaste province]] of [[Costa Rica]], this route starts at [[National Route 906 (Costa Rica)|Route 906]] and ends at Puerto Humo, on the shores of Tempisque river.

==Description==

Currently a gravel road, this is a small road that connects [[National Route 150 (Costa Rica)|Route 150]] through Route 906 to the Tempisque river. Together with Route 906 it is known as {{lang|es|Ruta de la Leche}}, the ''Milk Route''. The route visits the towns of Pozo de Agua, Puerto Humo and the Tempisque river.<ref>{{cite web |title=MOPT-CONAVI reactivan obras en Ruta de la Leche en Nicoya  MOPT-CONAVI reactivan obras en Ruta de la Leche en Nicoya |url=https://presidencia.go.cr/comunicados/2018/03/mopt-conavi-reactivan-obras-en-ruta-de-la-leche-en-nicoya/ |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

==History==

In late 2019 a pilot program with a new and cheaper asphalt paving procedure, using recycled materials, was put in place with initial tests over this gravel road. <ref>{{cite news |last1=Recio |first1=Patricia |title=MOPT asfalta vías de lastre con técnicas de bajo costo |url=https://www.nacion.com/el-pais/infraestructura/mopt-asfalta-vias-de-lastre-con-tecnicas-de-bajo/LTBLB3VJ5NEOXIOCZULWODMFDU/story/ |accessdate=1 January 2020 |date=27 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Recio |first1=Patricia |title=MOPT pone a trabajar personal y maquinaria ociosos |url=https://www.nacion.com/el-pais/infraestructura/mopt-pone-a-trabajar-personal-y-maquinaria-ociosos/FLIV5K36IRE57BPQCPVRQLZZVU/story/ |accessdate=1 January 2020 |date=7 December 2019}}</ref>
