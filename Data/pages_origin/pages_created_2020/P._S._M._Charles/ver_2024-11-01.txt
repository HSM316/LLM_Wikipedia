'''Prince Sarojini Manmatharajah Charles''' is a [[Sri Lanka|Sri Lankan]] civil servant who has served as the [[Governor of the Northern Province|Governor]] of the [[Northern Province, Sri Lanka|Northern Province]] of Sri Lanka on two occasions.

Charles is a Catholic and the daughter of a school principal.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Daniel |first1=Shannine |title=An Interview With Sarojini Charles, The DG Of Sri Lanka Customs |url=https://roar.media/english/life/culture-identities/an-interview-with-sarojini-charles-the-dg-of-sri-lanka-customs/ |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=Roar Media |date=4 February 2018 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref> She has a degree from the [[University of Jaffna]] and masters degrees in disaster management and business administration from the [[University of Peradeniya]] and [[Rajarata University of Sri Lanka]].<ref name="DM260917">{{cite news |title=Batticaloa DS appointed Customs DG |url=http://www.dailymirror.lk/article/Batticaloa-DS-appointed-Customs-DG-137324.html |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[The Daily Mirror (Sri Lanka)|The Daily Mirror]] |date=26 September 2017 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref><ref name="TI270917">{{cite news |title=Charles new DG Customs |url=http://www.island.lk/index.php?page_cat=article-details&page=article-details&code_title=172364 |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[The Island (Sri Lanka)|The Island]] |date=27 September 2017 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref>

Charles was Additional District Secretary (AGA) for [[Vavuniya District]] before becoming [[District Secretary]] (GA) for the district in October. She was appointed District Secretary for [[Batticaloa District]] in May 2012.<ref>{{cite news |title=Govt. reshuffles GAs |url=http://www.island.lk/index.php?page_cat=article-details&page=article-details&code_title=51600 |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[The Island (Sri Lanka)|The Island]] |date=11 May 2012 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Jaffna GA transferred to Presidential Secretariat - report |url=http://www.colombopage.com/archive_12/May10_1336634114CH.php |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=Colombo Page |date=10 May 2012 |location=Indianapolis, U.S.A.}}</ref>

Charles was appointed Director General of [[Sri Lanka Customs]] in September 2017.<ref>{{cite news |title=Batticaloa Government Agent P.S.M. Charles appointed Customs Director General |url=http://www.dailynews.lk/2017/09/26/local/129438/batticaloa-government-agent-psm-charles-appointed-customs-director-general |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[Daily News (Sri Lanka)|Daily News]] |date=26 September 2017 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Karunaratna |first1=Sanchith |title=Cabinet appoints new Director General of Customs |url=http://www.adaderana.lk/news/43245/cabinet-appoints-new-director-general-of-customs- |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[Ada Derana]] |date=26 September 2017 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref> She was removed from the post in January 2019, allegedly after refusing to give in to government pressure in respect of investigations into 143 suspicious [[cargo container]]s.<ref>{{cite news |title=Protests intensify over Customs head appointment |url=http://www.ft.lk/business/Protests-intensify-over-Customs-head-appointment/34-672078 |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[Daily FT]] |date=1 February 2019 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Charles’ Removal Linked To Probe On 143 Containers: Mangala’s Coordinating Secretary, Finance Min. Secretary Have Given Unlawful Orders: Sirisena Stirring Up Issues Behind The Scenes |url=https://www.colombotelegraph.com/index.php/charles-removal-linked-to-probe-on-143-containers-mangalas-coordinating-secretary-finance-min-secretary-have-given-unlawful-orders-sirisena-stirring-up-issues-behind-the-scenes/ |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=Colombo Telegraph |date=2 February 2019}}</ref> She was re-instated swiftly following [[industrial action]] by customs workers.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Bandara |first1=Kelum |title=P.S.M.Charles reinstated as Customs DG |url=http://www.dailymirror.lk/161985/P-S-M-Charles-reinstated-as-Customs-DG |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[The Daily Mirror (Sri Lanka)|The Daily Mirror]] |date=5 February 2019 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=P.S.M. Charles reinstated as Custom’s Director General |url=https://www.newsfirst.lk/2019/02/05/p-s-m-charles-reinstated-as-customs-director-general/ |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[News First]] |date=5 February 2019 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref>

Charles was appointed [[Permanent secretary|secretary]] of the [[Ministry of Healthcare and Indigenous Medical Services (Sri Lanka)|Ministry of Healthcare and Indigenous Medical Services]] in November 2019.<ref>{{cite news |title=Twenty new Ministry Secretaries appointed |url=http://island.lk/index.php?page_cat=article-details&page=article-details&code_title=214641 |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=[[The Island (Sri Lanka)|The Island]] |date=1 December 2019 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=President appoints secretaries to 20 ministries |url=http://www.colombopage.com/archive_19B/Nov27_1574864100CH.php |accessdate=21 December 2019 |work=Colombo Page |date=27 November 2019 |location=Indianapolis, U.S.A.}}</ref> She was sworn in as [[Governor of the Northern Province|Governor]] of the [[Northern Province, Sri Lanka|Northern Province]] on 30 December 2019.<ref>{{cite news |title=P.S.M. Charles appointed NP Governor |url=http://www.dailymirror.lk/top_story/P-S-M-Charles-appointed-NP-Governor/155-180391 |accessdate=30 December 2019 |work=[[The Daily Mirror (Sri Lanka)|The Daily Mirror]] |date=30 December 2019 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Top civil servant Charles appointed Governor NP |url=http://www.island.lk/index.php?page_cat=article-details&page=article-details&code_title=216285 |accessdate=30 December 2019 |work=[[The Island (Sri Lanka)|The Island]] |date=31 December 2019 |location=Colombo, Sri Lanka}}</ref> In October 2021, [[Jeevan Thiagarajah]] replaced her as the new governor of Northern Province.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jeevan Thiagarajah sworn in as Northern Province Governor - Latest News {{!}} Daily Mirror|url=https://www.dailymirror.lk/latest_news/Jeevan-Thiagarajah-sworn-in-as-Northern-Province-Governor/342-222270|access-date=2021-10-13|website=www.dailymirror.lk|language=English}}</ref> She was reappointed in May 2023 as governor of Northern Province, replacing Thiagarajah.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailymirror.lk/breaking_news/Three-provinces-get-new-governors/108-259369|title=Three provinces get new governors|website=www.dailymirror.lk|access-date=2023-05-18}}</ref>

Upon [[Anura Kumara Dissanayake]] taking office as President; Charles resigned. This led to the appointment of  Nagalingam Vethanayahan, a former government agent for Jaffna as Governor of Northen Province.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Media Release – Northern Provincial Council, Sri Lanka |url=https://np.gov.lk/media-release-2/ |access-date=2024-10-02 |language=en-US}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web |date=2024-09-25 |title=President appoints new Provincial Governors (Full list) |url=https://www.newswire.lk/2024/09/25/prent-appoints-new-provisional-governors/ |access-date=2024-10-02 |website=Newswire |language=en-US}}</ref>
