The [[Pakistan national cricket team|Pakistan cricket team]] is scheduled to tour New Zealand in December 2020 and January 2021 to play two [[Test cricket|Test]] and three [[Twenty20 International]] (T20I) matches.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thenational.ae/sport/cricket/complete-schedule-of-pakistan-cricket-team-in-2020-including-asia-cup-and-t20-world-cup-1.957244 |title=Complete schedule of Pakistan cricket team in 2020 including Asia Cup and T20 World Cup |work=The National |accessdate=30 December 2019}}</ref> The Test series will form part of the inaugural [[2019–21 ICC World Test Championship]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.icc-cricket.com/news/742337 |title=Schedule for inaugural World Test Championship announced |work=International Cricket Council |accessdate=11 January 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://icc-static-files.s3.amazonaws.com/ICC/document/2018/06/20/6dc2c8d4-e1a5-4dec-94b4-7121fab3cd7f/ICC_Tours.pdf |title=Men's Future Tours Programme |accessdate=11 October 2019 |work=International Cricket Council}}</ref>
