'''Giovanni Lorenzo Forcieri ''' (born 24 March 1949) is an Italian politician who was elected to the [[Senate of the Republic (Italy)|Italian Senate]] in the 1992, 1994, 1996 and [[2001 Italian general election|2001 general election]] representing left-wing parties.

He is the president of the [[Distretto Ligure delle Tecnologie Marine]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.ilsecoloxix.it/la-spezia/2019/12/15/news/spezia-ha-inaugurato-il-polo-universitario-nell-ex-ospedale-militare-storia-e-futuro-1.38214085 |date=15 December 2019|access-date=1 January 2020|title=Spezia ha inaugurato il polo universitario. Nell’ex ospedale militare storia e futuro|language=Italian |first=Marco|last=Toracca|publisher=[[Il Secolo XIX]]}}</ref>
He was also the president of the ''[[Port of La Spezia|autorità portuale]]'' of La Spezia.<ref name="ANSA">{{cite web|url=http://www.ansa.it/liguria/notizie/2018/12/10/corruzione-a-giudizio-forcieri_588fe4da-424b-4903-8d72-cf0a262f609e.html|publisher=[[Agenzia Nazionale Stampa Associata]]|accessdate=15 February 2015|title=Corruzione, a giudizio Forcieri|date=12 December 2018|archivedate=12 April 2019|archiveurl=https://archive.ph/20190412191750/http://www.ansa.it/liguria/notizie/2018/12/10/corruzione-a-giudizio-forcieri_588fe4da-424b-4903-8d72-cf0a262f609e.html}}</ref>
