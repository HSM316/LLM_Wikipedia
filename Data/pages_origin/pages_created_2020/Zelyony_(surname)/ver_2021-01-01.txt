'''Zelyony''', feminine: '''Zelyonaya''' {{lang-ru|Зелёный, Зелёная}}, meaning "green")  is a Russian-language surname.  The Russian name is often printed without diacritics and may be occasionally transliterated as "Zeleny". Notable people with this surname include:

*[[Lev Zelyony]], Soviet and Russian physicist
*[[Rina Zelyonaya]], Soviet actress
* Vsevolod Zelyony,  native name of [[Vsevolods Zeļonijs]], Latvian judoka
