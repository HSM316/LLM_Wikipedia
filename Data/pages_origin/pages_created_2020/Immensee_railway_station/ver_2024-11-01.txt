'''Immensee railway station''' ({{langx|de|Bahnhof Immensee}}) is a railway station in the municipality of [[Küssnacht]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It located at the junction of three standard gauge lines of [[Swiss Federal Railways]]: the [[Gotthard railway|Gotthard]], [[Lucerne–Immensee railway|Lucerne–Immensee]], and [[Rupperswil–Immensee railway|Rapperswil–Immensee]].<ref name="saw">{{Eisenbahnatlas Schweiz | page = 22}}</ref>

== Layout ==
Immensee has three platforms serving four tracks, numbered 1–4. The platform nearest the station building ({{Langx|de-CH|Hausperron}}) is {{convert|70|m}} long. Next to that platform is a {{convert|119|m|adj=on}} intermediate platform ({{Langx|de-CH|Zwischenperron}}), serving one track. Finally, there is a {{convert|199|m|adj=on}} [[island platform]] serving two tracks.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Stop: platform length (body) |url=https://data.sbb.ch/explore/dataset/perron/table/?q=5003 |access-date=2023-08-11 |website=data.sbb.ch |language=en-US}}</ref>
