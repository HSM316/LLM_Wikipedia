'''Immensee railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Immensee}}) is a railway station in the municipality of [[Küssnacht]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It located at the junction of three standard gauge lines of [[Swiss Federal Railways]]: the [[Gotthard railway|Gotthard]], [[Lucerne–Immensee railway|Lucerne–Immensee]], and [[Rupperswil–Immensee railway|Rupperswil–Immensee]].

== Services ==
The following services stop at Immensee::<ref name="fpf601">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/601.pdf | title = Luzern - Rotkreuz - Arth-Goldau - Erstfeld | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=5 November 2019 | accessdate = 31 December 2019}}</ref>

* [[Zug Stadtbahn]] {{rcb|Zug Stadtbahn|2 South|inline=croute}}: peak-hour service over the [[Rupperswil–Immensee railway|Rupperswil–Immensee line]] to [[Rotkreuz railway station|Rotkrezu]] and the [[Gotthard railway|Gotthard line]] to [[Flüelen railway station|Flüelen]].
* [[Lucerne S-Bahn]] {{rcb|Lucerne S-Bahn|3|inline=croute}}: hourly service over the [[Lucerne–Immensee railway|Lucerne–Immensee line]] to {{rws|Luzern}} and the Gotthard line to [[Brunnen railway station|Brunnen]].
