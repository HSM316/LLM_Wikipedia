'''Immensee railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Immensee}}) is a railway station in [[Küssnacht]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It sits at the junction of three railway lines: the [[Gotthard railway]], [[Lucerne–Immensee railway]], and the [[Aargau Southern Railway]]. It is served by local trains only.

== Services ==
Immensee is served by the S2 of the [[Zug Stadtbahn]] and S3 of the [[Lucerne S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf601">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/601.pdf | title = Luzern - Rotkreuz - Arth-Goldau - Erstfeld | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=5 November 2019 | accessdate = 31 December 2019}}</ref>

* {{rcb|Zug Stadtbahn|2 South|inline=croute}}: peak-hour service over the [[Aargau Southern Railway]] to [[Rotkreuz railway station|Rotkrezu]] and the [[Gotthard railway]] to [[Flüelen railway station|Flüelen]].
* {{rcb|Lucerne S-Bahn|3|inline=croute}}: hourly service over the [[Lucerne–Immensee railway]] to [[Luzern railway station|Luzern]] and the Gotthard railway to [[Brunnen railway station|Brunnen]].
