'''Freedom''' is the twelfth single by Australian rock band [[Sherbet (band)|Sherbet]], released in 1975.<ref>[http://www.45cat.com/record/k5834 45cat - Sherbet - Freedom]</ref>  It was released as the first and final single from the album [[In Concert (Sherbet album)|In Concert]] and it was written by [[Garth Porter]] and [[Clive Shakespeare]].<ref>[https://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Sherbet&titel=Freedom&cat=s Australian Charts - Sherbet - Freedom]</ref> The song reached number 52  on the [[Kent Music Report]].
