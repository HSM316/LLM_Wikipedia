Season '''1890–91''' was the eighteenth [[Football in Scotland|Scottish football]] season in which [[Kilmarnock F.C.|Kilmarnock]] competed at a national level, entering the inaugural [[Scottish Cup]].

== Overview ==
Kilmarnock were knocked put in the first round in a 6–2 at home against [[Annbank F.C.|Annbank]] after a 4–4 draw.
