'''''Into the Badlands''''' is a 1996 role-playing game supplement for ''[[Heavy Gear]]'' published by [[Dream Pod 9]].

==Contents==
''Into the Badlands'' provides more in-depth background for ''Heavy Gear'''s gameworld - the balkanized planet, Terra Nova.<ref name="Arcane8"/>

==Reception==
James Swallow reviewed ''Into the Badlands'' for ''Arcane'' magazine, rating it an 8 out of 10 overall.<ref name="Arcane8">{{cite journal|last=Swallow|first=James|date=July 1996|title=Games Reviews|journal=Arcane|publisher=[[Future plc|Future Publishing]]|issue=8|pages=69}}</ref> Swallow comments that "For ''Heavy Gear'' players on the lookout for fresh challenges, ''Into the Badlands'' holds more than enough for a dozen campaigns."<ref name="Arcane8"/>
