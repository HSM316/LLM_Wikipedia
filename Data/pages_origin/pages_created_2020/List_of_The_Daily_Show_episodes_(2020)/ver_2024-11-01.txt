This is a list of episodes for ''[[The Daily Show|The Daily Show with Trevor Noah]]'' in 2020.<ref name=showsite>[https://web.archive.org/web/20150818212743/http://www.cc.com/shows/the-daily-show-with-trevor-noah ''The Daily Show with Trevor Noah''] at cc.com.</ref>

Due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic|coronavirus pandemic]], Noah began filming episodes from his home for the show's [[YouTube]] channel. On March 23, episodes—now titled ''The Daily Social Distancing Show with Trevor Noah''—began to air on television.<ref>{{cite news|author=Bruce Haring, Nellie Andreeva|date=March 18, 2020|title=Stephen Colbert, Trevor Noah, Jimmy Fallon, Jimmy Kimmel, Samantha Bee Keep The Late Night Trains Running With Videos|work=Deadline Hollywood|url=https://deadline.com/2020/03/jimmy-fallon-jimmy-kimmel-samantha-bee-post-youtube-bits-1202887318/|url-status=live|access-date=March 19, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160924190214/https://deadline.com/2020/03/jimmy-fallon-jimmy-kimmel-samantha-bee-post-youtube-bits-1202887318/|archive-date=September 24, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|title='The Daily Social Distancing Show with Trevor Noah' To Air On Comedy Central During Coronavirus Pandemic|url=https://deadline.com/2020/03/the-daily-social-distancing-show-with-trevor-noah-comedy-central-coronavirus-pandemic-1202889491/|access-date=March 21, 2020|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=March 21, 2020|url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160924190214/https://deadline.com/2020/03/the-daily-social-distancing-show-with-trevor-noah-comedy-central-coronavirus-pandemic-1202889491/|archive-date=September 24, 2016}}</ref> On April 27, episodes were expanded to 45 minutes.<ref>{{cite news|first=Brian|last=Steinberg|title=Trevor Noah's 'Daily Show' Will Expand to 45 Minutes|url=https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/daily-show-trevor-noah-expand-45-minutes-1234590702/|accessdate=April 27, 2020|work=Variety|date=April 27, 2020|url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160924190214/https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/daily-show-trevor-noah-expand-45-minutes-1234590702/|archive-date=September 24, 2016}}</ref>
