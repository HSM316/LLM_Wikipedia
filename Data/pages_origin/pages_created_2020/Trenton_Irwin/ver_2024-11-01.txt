'''Trenton Irwin''' (born December 10, 1995<ref>{{Cite web |title=Trenton Irwin |url=http://www.nfl.com/players/trentonirwin/profile?id=IRW629310 |access-date=January 1, 2020 |website=NFL.com |language=en}}</ref>) is an American professional [[American football|football]] [[wide receiver]] for the [[Cincinnati Bengals]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). He appeared on the 2005 Bravo series "Sports Kids Moms & Dads" with his sister Alyssa and father Craig.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Klein |first=Gary |date=June 1, 2005 |title=From actor to AFC championship: SoCal's Trenton Irwin on big stage with Bengals |url=https://dothaneagle.com/ap/sports/from-actor-to-afc-championship-socal-s-trenton-irwin-on-big-stage-with-bengals/article_e2d41889-330e-5641-b980-34984593c2a2.html |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Dothan Eagle |language=en}}</ref> He played [[college football]] at [[Stanford Cardinal football|Stanford]].

==Early life==
Irwin attended Rosemont Middle School, and afterward Irwin attended and played [[high school football]] at [[William S. Hart High School]] in [[Newhall, California]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Klein |first=Gary |date=January 28, 2023 |title=From actor to AFC championship: SoCal's Trenton Irwin on big stage with Bengals |url=https://www.latimes.com/sports/story/2023-01-27/trent-irwin-childhood-actor-santa-clarita-hart-high-bengals-receiver |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Los Angeles Times |language=en-US}}</ref>

== College career ==
Irwin played [[college football]] at [[Stanford Cardinal football|Stanford]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Trenton Irwin College Stats, School, Draft, Gamelog, Splits |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/cfb/players/trenton-irwin-1.html |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=College Football at Sports-Reference.com |language=en}}</ref> He finished his college career with 152 [[football catch|receptions]] for 1,738 receiving yards and five [[touchdowns]], while also returning 20 punts for 230 yards.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |title=Irwin Signed By The Dolphins |url=https://247sports.com/college/stanford/Article/Irwin-Signed-By-The-Dolphins-131618852/ |access-date=January 1, 2020 |website=TheBootleg.com |language=en-US}}</ref> He also had at least one reception in 40 consecutive games.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Dolphins rookie Irwin, former child actor, looks to tackle the spotlight |url=https://wsvn.com/sports/dolphins-rookie-irwin-former-child-actor-looks-to-tackle-the-spotlight/ |access-date=January 1, 2020 |language=en}}</ref>

=== Miami Dolphins ===
Irwin went undrafted in the [[2019 NFL draft]]. He signed with the [[Miami Dolphins]] in April 2019.<ref name=":0" /> He was released from the Dolphins on August 31, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Poupart |first=Alain |date=August 31, 2019 |title=Roster Breakdown: Initial Dolphins 53-Player Roster |url=https://www.miamidolphins.com/news/roster-breakdown-initial-dolphins-53-player-roster?campaign=sf:fanshare:facebook |access-date=January 1, 2020 |website=Miami Dolphins |language=en-US}}</ref>

=== Cincinnati Bengals ===
====2019====
Irwin was signed to the [[practice squad]] of the [[Cincinnati Bengals]] on October 3, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Marcum |first=Jason |date=October 3, 2019 |title=Bengals sign Trenton Irwin to practice squad |url=https://www.cincyjungle.com/2019/10/3/20897456/bengals-news-trenton-irwin-practice-squad-miami-dolphins-stanford-cardinal |access-date=January 1, 2020 |website=Cincy Jungle |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Roling |first=Chris |date=October 4, 2019 |title=Bengals add Trenton Irwin to fill Stanley Morgan's practice squad spot |url=https://bengalswire.usatoday.com/2019/10/04/bengals-sign-trenton-irwin-stanley-morgans-practice-squad-free-agent/ |access-date=January 1, 2020 |website=Bengals Wire |language=en}}</ref> He was promoted to the active roster on December 27, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Sheeran |first=John |date=December 27, 2019 |title=Bengals place William Jackson on IR; sign Jordan Franks and Trenton Irwin from practice squad |url=https://www.cincyjungle.com/2019/12/27/21039505/bengals-place-william-jackson-on-ir-sign-jordan-franks-and-trenton-irwin-from-practice-squad |access-date=January 1, 2020 |website=Cincy Jungle |language=en}}</ref>

==== 2020 ====
On September 5, 2020, Irwin was waived by the Bengals and signed to the practice squad the next day.<ref>{{Cite web |date=September 6, 2020 |title=Bengals Establish Practice Squad |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-establish-practice-squad-x0373 |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com |archive-date=September 29, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200929052540/https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-establish-practice-squad-x0373 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

In Week 16 against the [[Houston Texans]], Irwin was elevated to the active roster, and made the first reception of his career, a 5-yard pass from quarterback [[Brandon Allen (American football)|Brandon Allen]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Cincinnati Bengals at Houston Texans - December 27th, 2020 |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/boxscores/202012270htx.htm |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com |language=en}}</ref> He reverted to the practice squad after the game.<ref>{{Cite web |date=December 26, 2020 |title=Bengals Make Roster Moves Ahead Of Week 16 |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-make-roster-moves-ahead-of-week-16 |access-date=February 8, 2021 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref>

==== 2021 ====
On September 1, 2021, Irwin was waived by the Bengals and re-signed to the practice squad.<ref>{{Cite web |date=September 1, 2021 |title=Bengals Make Player Moves, Sign 15 To The Practice Squad |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-make-player-moves-sign-15-to-the-practice-squad |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref> He was elevated to the active roster on September 15, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web |date=September 15, 2021 |title=Bengals Make Roster Moves Ahead Of Week 2 At Chicago |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-make-roster-moves-ahead-of-week-2-at-chicago |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref>

In Week 4, Irwin caught his only reception of 2021 for a 25-yard gain against the [[Jacksonville Jaguars]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Jacksonville Jaguars at Cincinnati Bengals - September 30th, 2021 |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/boxscores/202109300cin.htm |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com |language=en}}</ref>

==== 2022 ====
On March 21, 2022, Irwin re-signed with the Bengals.<ref>{{Cite web |date=March 21, 2022 |title=Bengals Roster Moves |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-roster-moves-x3824 |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref> He was waived on August 30, 2022, and signed to the practice squad the next day.<ref>{{Cite web |date=August 30, 2022 |title=Roster Moves: Bengals Reduce Roster To 53 Players |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-53-man-roster-cuts-2022 |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=August 31, 2022 |title=Bengals Roster Moves: Practice Squad Announced |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-roster-moves-practice-squad-2022-season-brandon-allen |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref>

In Week 11, against the [[Pittsburgh Steelers]], Irwin caught his first professional touchdown from quarterback [[Joe Burrow]] on a goal-line pass.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Hobson |first=Geoff |date=November 20, 2022 |title=Bengals Upend Steelers With Second Half Onslaught |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-throttle-steelers-with-second-half-onslaught |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref> On November 22, 2022, Irwin was signed to the active roster.<ref>{{Cite web |date=November 22, 2022 |title=Bengals Roster Moves: Trenton Irwin Signed To Active Roster, Prince Waived |url=https://www.bengals.com/news/bengals-roster-moves-trenton-irwin-signed-isaiah-prince-waived |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Bengals.com}}</ref> Irwin caught his second career touchdown pass in Week 14 against the [[Cleveland Browns]], when the Bengals called a [[Flea flicker]] that left him open in the backfield for a 35-yard touchdown.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Heltman |first=Russ |date=December 11, 2022 |title=Watch: Trenton Irwin Catches Second NFL TD, Bengals Take 20-3 Lead Over Cleveland |url=https://www.si.com/nfl/bengals/gameday/watch-trenton-irwin-catches-second-nfl-td-bengals-take-20-3-lead-over-cleveland |access-date=December 11, 2022 |website=Sports Illustrated |language=en}}</ref>

In Week 16 against the [[New England Patriots]], Irwin played his most impressive professional outing to date. Out of four targets, he netted three receptions for 45 yards and two touchdowns. He narrowly missed a pass in the end zone that could have resulted in a third touchdown, which would have given him a perfect reception percentage in the 22–18 victory.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Beighle |first=Nathan |date=December 24, 2022 |title=3 winners and 2 losers from Cincinnati's win at New England |url=https://www.cincyjungle.com/2022/12/24/23525518/bengals-vs-patriots-tee-higgins-trenton-irwin-3-winners-2-losers-evan-mcpherson |access-date=December 25, 2022 |website=Cincy Jungle |language=en}}</ref> Irwin played in nine games in the 2022 regular season. He finished with 15 receptions for 231 receiving yards and four receiving touchdowns.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Trenton Irwin 2022 Game Log |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/I/IrwiTr00/gamelog/2022/ |access-date=February 11, 2023 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com |language=en}}</ref>

==== 2023 ====
Irwin made his first catch of the season in Week 4 against the [[Tennessee Titans]], pulling in one reception for 17 yards. The following week against the [[Arizona Cardinals]], Irwin was named a starting wide receiver due to [[Tee Higgins]] being ruled inactive for a rib injury. He took advantage of this opportunity by pulling in 8 catches for 60 yards. Irwin scored his first touchdown of the season in Week 10 against the Houston Texans. In addition to his lone touchdown, he finished the season with 25 receptions for 316 yards, both career bests for him.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Trenton Irwin 2023 Game Log |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/I/IrwiTr00/gamelog/2023/ |access-date=2024-07-30 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com |language=en}}</ref>

====2024====
Irwin re-signed with the Bengals on March 19, 2024.<ref>{{cite web|title=Bengals Re-Sign Trenton Irwin|url=https://www.bengals.com/news/trenton-irwin-re-signed-free-agency-2024|website=Bengals.com|date=March 19, 2024}}</ref> He competed with [[Andrei Iosivas]], [[Charlie Jones (American football, born 1998)|Charlie Jones]], and [[Jermaine Burton]] for the Bengals' third starting wide receiver position in the preseason, with Iosivas ultimately winning the job, and Irwin being named the fourth wide receiver on the depth chart to begin the year.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Garrison |first=Drew |date=2024-09-03 |title=Bengals release Week 1 depth chart |url=https://www.cincyjungle.com/2024/9/3/24234950/bengals-depth-chart-vs-patriots-nfl-week-1 |access-date=2024-10-19 |website=Cincy Jungle |language=en}}</ref>
