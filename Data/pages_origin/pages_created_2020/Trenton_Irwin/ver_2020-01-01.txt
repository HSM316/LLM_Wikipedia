'''Trenton Irwin''' (born December 10, 1995<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nfl.com/players/trentonirwin/profile?id=IRW629310|title=Trenton Irwin|website=NFL.com|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>) is an [[American football]] [[wide receiver]] who is currently a member of the [[Cincinnati Bengals]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). 

== College career ==
Irwin played [[college football]] at [[Stanford Cardinal football|Stanford]]. He finished his college career with 152 receptions for 1,178 receiving yards and five touchdowns.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://247sports.com/college/stanford/Article/Irwin-Signed-By-The-Dolphins-131618852/|title=Irwin Signed By The Dolphins|website=TheBootleg.com|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> He also had at least one reception in 40 consecutive games.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://wsvn.com/sports/dolphins-rookie-irwin-former-child-actor-looks-to-tackle-the-spotlight/|title=Dolphins rookie Irwin, former child actor, looks to tackle the spotlight|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

== Professional career ==

=== Miami Dolphins ===
Irwin went undrafted in the [[2019 NFL Draft]]. He signed with the Miami Dolphins in April 2019.<ref name=":0" /> He was released from the Dolphins on August 31, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.miamidolphins.com/news/roster-breakdown-initial-dolphins-53-player-roster?campaign=sf:fanshare:facebook|title=Roster Breakdown: Initial Dolphins 53-Player Roster|website=www.miamidolphins.com|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

=== Cincinnati Bengals ===
Irwin was signed to the Cincinnati Bengals practice squad on October 3, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cincyjungle.com/2019/10/3/20897456/bengals-news-trenton-irwin-practice-squad-miami-dolphins-stanford-cardinal|title=Bengals sign Trenton Irwin to practice squad|last=Marcum|first=Jason|date=2019-10-03|website=Cincy Jungle|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://bengalswire.usatoday.com/2019/10/04/bengals-sign-trenton-irwin-stanley-morgans-practice-squad-free-agent/|title=Bengals add Trenton Irwin to fill Stanley Morgan's practice squad spot|date=2019-10-04|website=Bengals Wire|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> He was promoted to the active roster on December 27, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cincyjungle.com/2019/12/27/21039505/bengals-place-william-jackson-on-ir-sign-jordan-franks-and-trenton-irwin-from-practice-squad|title=Bengals place William Jackson on IR; sign Jordan Franks and Trenton Irwin from practice squad|last=Sheeran|first=John|date=2019-12-27|website=Cincy Jungle|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>
