'''Weimar. Lexikon zur Stadtgeschichte''' is the title of an [[Encyclopedia]] on the history of the German city of [[Weimar]]. The [[non-fiction]] book was first published in 1993 and improved in 1997 by [[Böhlau Verlag]] in a second edition. It was edited by [[Gitta Günther]], who worked as a Weimar city archivist from 1959 to 2001, together with the musicologist [[Wolfram Huschke (musicologist)|Wolfram Huschke]] and the geologist [[Walter Steiner (geologist)|Walter Steiner]]. The book with numerous illustrations and more than 500 pages was last published under the {{ISBN|3-7400-0807-5}}.<ref>Vergleiche Belegexemplar {{{DNB|930433416}} at the [[Deutsche Nationalbibliothek]].</ref>
