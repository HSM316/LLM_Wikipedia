'''Bruno Nhavene''' (born February 21, 2002) is a [[Mozambique|Mozambican]] junior [[tennis]] player.

Nhavene has a career high [[Association of Tennis Professionals|ATP]] doubles ranking of 1418 achieved on 2 December 2019.<ref>{{cite web|title=Bruno Nhavene|url=https://www.atptour.com/en/players/bruno-nhavene/n0cm/overview|publisher=[[Association of Tennis Professionals|ATP]]|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> Nhavene has a career high [[International Tennis Federation|ITF]] juniors ranking of 168 achieved on 9 March 2020.<ref>{{cite web|title=Bruno Nhavene Junior profile at the ITF|url=https://www.itftennis.com/en/players/bruno-nhavene/800512474/moz/jt/s/overview|publisher=[[International Tennis Federation|ITF]]|accessdate=1 February 2021}}</ref>

Nhavene has represented [[Mozambique Davis Cup team|Mozambique]] at [[Davis Cup]], where he has a win-loss record of 10–8.<ref>{{cite web|title=Bruno Nhavene|url=https://www.daviscup.com/en/players/player.aspx?id=800512474|publisher=[[Davis Cup]]|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> Nhavene made his [[Davis Cup]] debut for [[Mozambique Davis Cup team|Mozambique]] in 2018, while the team was competing in the [[2018 Davis Cup Africa Zone Group III|Africa Zone Group III]], when he was 16 years and 118 days old.
