'''Jørgen Hvidemose''' (born 12 May 1940) is a Danish [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Hvidemose was the head coach of the [[Denmark women's national football team|Denmark women's national team]] at the [[1999 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
