'''Ishmael Pole''' (born 25 January 1993) is a [[Papua New Guinea]]n [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[Gulf Komara]].

==Career==
After starting out as a striker, Pole later transitioned to goalkeeping.<ref>{{Cite news |last=Harry |first=Priscilla |date=26 January 2016 |title=Pole eyes 2016 Nations Cup this year |pages=27 |work=Papua New Guinea Post-Courier}}</ref>

Pole spent eight years at Hekari United in the [[Papua New Guinea National Soccer League]] and [[Papua New Guinea National Premier League]]. In April 2022, he joined [[Gulf Komara]].<ref>{{Cite news |last=Tambua |first=Parker |date=20 April 2022 |title=Pole joins Gulf Komara|work=The National|location=Port Moresby |url=https://www.thenational.com.pg/pole-joins-gulf-komara/ |access-date=2023-06-12}}</ref> Pole joined Lautoka FC in mid-2022 on loan during qualification for the [[OFC Champions League]].<ref>{{Cite news |date=21 June 2022 |title=Pole fractures finger in Fijian league playoff for Lautoka |work=The National |location=Port Moresby|url=https://www.thenational.com.pg/pole-fractures-finger-in-fijian-league-playoff-for-lautoka/ |access-date=2023-06-12}}</ref>
