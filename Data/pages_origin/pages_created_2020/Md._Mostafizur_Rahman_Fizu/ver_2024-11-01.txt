'''Md. Mostafizur Rahman Fizu''' (died 2006) was a Bangladeshi politician from [[Dinajpur District, Bangladesh|Dinajpur]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Awami League]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Fizu was a cofounder of [[Swapnapuri]] which was established in 1989.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bhorerkagoj.com/print-edition/2018/06/30/199016.php |script-title=bn:বিনোদন কেন্দ্র 'স্বপ্নপুরী' জনপ্রিয় হয়ে উঠছে |work=Bhorer Kagoj |language=bn |accessdate=1 January 2020 |date=30 June 2018 |archive-date=1 January 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101070409/https://www.bhorerkagoj.com/print-edition/2018/06/30/199016.php |url-status=dead}}</ref> He was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from [[Dinajpur-6]] in the [[June 1996 Bangladeshi general election]].<ref name="1996results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/7th%20Parliament%20.pdf |title=List of 7th Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=6 March 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924064449/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/7th%20Parliament%20.pdf |archive-date=24 September 2015}}</ref> His son [[Shibli Sadique]] is the MP of this constituency as of 2019.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bd-pratidin.com/national/2019/06/09/430238 |script-title=bn:বিয়ে করলেন এমপি শিবলী সাদিক |work=Bangladesh Pratidin |language=bn |date=9 June 2019 |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>

Fizu died in 2006.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/online/entertainment/2016/11/28/434644 |script-title=bn:'শেষ মুহূর্তেও চেয়েছিলাম যাতে ডিভোর্স না হয়' |work=Kaler Kantho |language=bn |date=28 November 2016 |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>
