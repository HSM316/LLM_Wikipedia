'''Md. Mostafizur Rahman Fizu''' was a Bangladeshi  politician from [[Dinajpur District, Bangladesh|Dinajpur]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Awami League]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Fizu was a cofounder of [[Swapnapuri]] which was established in 1989.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bhorerkagoj.com/print-edition/2018/06/30/199016.php|title=বিনোদন কেন্দ্র 'স্বপ্নপুরী' জনপ্রিয় হয়ে উঠছে|website=Bhorer Kagoj|language=bn|accessdate=1 January 2020|date=30 June 2018|archive-date=1 January 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101070409/https://www.bhorerkagoj.com/print-edition/2018/06/30/199016.php|url-status=dead}}</ref> He was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from [[Dinajpur-6]] in the [[June 1996 Bangladeshi general election|Seventh Jatiya Sangsad Election]].<ref name="1996results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/index.php/en/mps/members-of-parliament/former-mp-s/list-of-7th-parliament-members-bangla |format=PDF|title=List of 7th Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=6 March 2018}}</ref> His son [[Shibli Sadique]] is the current MP of this constituency.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bd-pratidin.com/national/2019/06/09/430238
|title=বিয়ে করলেন এমপি শিবলী সাদিক
|website=Bangladesh Pratidin|language=bn|date=9 June 2019 |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

Fizu died in 2006.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/online/entertainment/2016/11/28/434644
