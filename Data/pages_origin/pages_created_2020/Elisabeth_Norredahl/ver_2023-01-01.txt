'''Anna Margareta Elisabet "Bettan" Norredahl''' (née '''Hansson'''; born 2 October 1967 in [[Östersund]]) is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] female [[curling|curler]].<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20190909173724/http://www.curling.se/folja/landslagsspelaregenomtidernadamer#H Landslagsspelare genom tiderna – Damer - Svenska Curlingförbundet]{{in lang|sv}} (Swedish female national curlers; look at "Hansson, Elisabeth") (web archive)</ref>

In 2013 she was inducted into the [[Swedish Curling Hall of Fame]].
