'''''Trachypteris''''' is a small genus of [[fern]]s in the subfamily [[Cheilanthoideae]] of the family [[Pteridaceae]].<ref name=PPGI/> Three species are native to the tropical South America, one (''[[Trachypteris drakeana]]'') to [[Madagascar]].<ref name=CFLW/>

==Species==
{{As of|2021|December}}, the ''Checklist of Ferns and Lycophytes of the World'' recognized the following species:<ref name=CFLW/>
*''[[Trachypteris drakeana]]'' <small>C.Chr.</small>
*''[[Trachypteris gilliana]]'' <small>(Baker) Svenson</small>
*''[[Trachypteris induta]]'' <small>(Maxon) R.M.Tryon & A.F.Tryon</small>
*''[[Trachypteris pinnata]]'' <small>(Hook.f.) C.Chr.</small>
