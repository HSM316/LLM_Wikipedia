The '''Masik River''' is a river on [[Banks Island]], in Canada's [[Northwest Territories]].<ref>"[http://www4.rncan.gc.ca/search-place-names/unique/LAPBQ Masik River]". ''Canadian Geographical Names Data Base''. Natural Resources Canada. Retrieved 7 March 2020.</ref>

The river is named after the polar researcher August Masik (1887–1976).<ref>Härm, Tiiu (2010). ''Jää voolab: Eesti polaarränduri August Masiku maailm''. Tallinn: Eesti Raamat.</ref>
