The '''Masik River''' is a river on [[Banks Island]], in Canada's [[Northwest Territories]].  It is a part of the [[Arctic Ocean]] watershed.<ref>"[http://www4.rncan.gc.ca/search-place-names/unique/LAPBQ Masik River]". ''Canadian Geographical Names Data Base''. Natural Resources Canada. Retrieved 7 March 2020.</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://geonames.nga.mil/namesgaz/|website=GeoNames Search|title=Masik River|access-date=July 21, 2021}}</ref>

The river is named after the polar researcher August Masik (1887–1976).<ref>Härm, Tiiu (2010). ''Jää voolab: Eesti polaarränduri August Masiku maailm''. Tallinn: Eesti Raamat.</ref>
