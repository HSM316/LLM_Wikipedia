'''Santa Lucia''' is a [[Gothic architecture|gothic]]-style [[Roman Catholic]] church located at Piazza Umberto I in the hamlet of Capradosso, a frazione of the town of [[Rotella, Marche|Rotella]], in the province of Ascoli Piceno, region of [[Marche]], Italy. It is also known as the ''Santa Lucia a Capradosso''.

== History and description ==
This church houses a Gothic [[reliquary]] from the 15th century, attributed to Alessandro Battista da Marcuccio, a pupil of [[Pietro Vannini]]. The interior is decorated with polychrome baroque stucco decorations. The crypt is dedicated to St Pontico. A canvas in a lunette, depicting the Madonna of the Rosary above donors of the Gabrielli family is attributed to a follower of [[Simone de Magistris]].<ref>[http://www.comunerotella.net/c044065/zf/index.php/servizi-aggiuntivi/index/index/idtesto/9 Commune of Rotella, short description of the church].</ref>
