'''James "J. C." Hassenauer''' (born September 15, 1995) is an [[American football]] [[Center (gridiron football)|center]] for the [[New York Giants]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). He played [[college football]] at [[Alabama Crimson Tide football|Alabama]].

===Atlanta Falcons===
After playing four years at [[Alabama Crimson Tide football|Alabama]], Hassenauer was signed by the [[Atlanta Falcons]] as an undrafted free agent on April 30, 2018.<ref>{{Cite web |date=April 30, 2018 |title=Five Former Alabama Football Players Sign as Free Agents Following 2018 NFL Draft |url=https://rolltide.com/news/2018/4/30/five-former-alabama-football-players-sign-as-free-agents-following-2018-nfl-draft.aspx |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=Rolltide.com}}</ref> He was waived on September 1 and signed to the practice squad the next day, where he spent most of the season.<ref>{{Cite web |last=McFadden |first=Will |date=September 1, 2018 |title=Falcons 2018 roster: Atlanta announces roster cuts, trims active roster down to 52 |url=https://www.atlantafalcons.com/news/falcons-2018-roster-atlanta-announces-roster-cuts-trims-active-roster-down-to-52 |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=AtlantaFalcons.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=McFadden |first=Will |date=September 2, 2018 |title=Falcons sign 10 players to practice squad |url=https://www.atlantafalcons.com/news/falcons-sign-10-players-to-practice-squad |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180904121013/https://www.atlantafalcons.com/news/falcons-sign-10-players-to-practice-squad |archive-date=September 4, 2018 |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=AtlantaFalcons.com}}</ref>

===Birmingham Iron===
After the season ended, Hassenauer signed with the [[Birmingham Iron]] of the [[Alliance of American Football]], where he played eight games.

===Pittsburgh Steelers===
On April 5, 2019, Hassenauer signed with the [[Pittsburgh Steelers]],<ref>{{Cite web |last=Varley |first=Teresa |date=April 5, 2019 |title=Steelers sign Hassenauer, Tocho |url=https://www.steelers.com/news/steelers-sign-hassenauer-tocho |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190405231814/https://www.steelers.com/news/steelers-sign-hassenauer-tocho |archive-date=April 5, 2019 |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=Steelers.com}}</ref> but was waived on August 31.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Varley |first=Teresa |date=August 31, 2019 |title=Steelers make roster cuts |url=https://www.steelers.com/news/steelers-make-roster-cuts |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=Steelers.com}}</ref> On November 20, he was signed to the Steelers' practice squad,<ref>{{Cite web |last=Bryan |first=Dave |date=August 31, 2019 |title=Steelers Sign C J.C. Hassenauer To Practice Squad, Release TE Kevin Rader |url=https://steelersdepot.com/2019/11/steelers-sign-c-j-c-hassenauer-to-practice-squad-release-te-kevin-rader/ |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=SteelersDepot.com}}</ref> and was promoted to the active roster on December 24.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Kozora |first=Alex |date=August 31, 2019 |title=Steelers Place QB Mason Rudolph On IR |url=https://steelersdepot.com/2019/12/steelers-place-qb-mason-rudolph-on-ir/ |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=SteelersDepot.com}}</ref>

On December 2, 2020, Hassenauer made his first NFL start in a 19–14 win over the [[Baltimore Ravens]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Adamski |first=Chris |date=December 2, 2020 |title=J.C. Hassenauer latest 'next man up' contributing to Steelers |url=https://triblive.com/sports/j-c-hassenauer-latest-nest-man-up-contributing-to-steelers/ |access-date=December 2, 2020 |website=TribLive.com}}</ref>

Hassenauer signed a one-year contract extension with the Steelers on March 8, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Varley |first=Teresa |date=March 8, 2021 |title=Hassenauer signed to a one-year contract |url=https://www.steelers.com/news/hassenauer-signed-to-a-one-year-contract |access-date=March 8, 2021 |website=Steelers.com}}</ref> He was placed on injured reserve on November 27, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Varley |first=Teresa |date=November 27, 2021 |title=Steelers make moves, place two on IR |url=https://www.steelers.com/news/steelers-make-moves-place-two-on-ir |access-date=November 13, 2023 |website=Steelers.com}}</ref> He was activated on December 25.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Bryan |first=Dave |date=December 25, 2021 |title=Steelers Activate G/C J.C. Hassenauer From IR; Signed Two Punters; Elevated Several From PS |url=https://steelersdepot.com/2021/12/steelers-activate-g-c-j-c-hassenauer-from-ir-signed-two-punters-elevated-several-from-ps/ |access-date=February 2, 2023 |website=Steelers Depot |language=en-US}}</ref>

===New York Giants===
On April 7, 2023, Hassenauer was signed by the New York Giants.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Salomone |first=Dan |date=April 7, 2023 |title=Giants sign offensive lineman J.C. Hassenauer |url=https://www.giants.com/news/reports-giants-agree-to-terms-with-ol-j-c-hassenauer-alabama-football |access-date=April 8, 2023 |website=Giants.com}}</ref> On August 1, 2023, Hassenauer was placed on injured reserve with a torn tricep.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Salomone |first=Dan |date=August 1, 2023 |title=Giants sign center Sean Harlow; J.C. Hassenauer to IR |url=https://www.giants.com/news/j-c-hassenauer-injury-sean-harlow-roster-move-signed-alabama-oregon-state |access-date=November 13, 2023 |website=Giants.com}}</ref>
