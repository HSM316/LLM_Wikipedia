'''James "J. C." Hassenauer''' (born September 15, 1995) is an [[American football]] [[Center (gridiron football)|center]] for the [[Pittsburgh Steelers]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). He played [[college football]] at [[Alabama Crimson Tide football|Alabama]].

===Atlanta Falcons===
After playing four years at [[Alabama Crimson Tide football|Alabama]], Hassenauer was signed by the [[Atlanta Falcons]] as an undrafted free agent on April 30, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|title=Five Former Alabama Football Players Sign as Free Agents Following 2018 NFL Draft|url=https://rolltide.com/news/2018/4/30/five-former-alabama-football-players-sign-as-free-agents-following-2018-nfl-draft.aspx|website=Rolltide.com|date=April 30, 2018|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> He was waived on September 1 and signed to the practice squad the next day, where he spent most of the season.<ref>{{cite web|first=Will|last=McFadden|title=Falcons 2018 roster: Atlanta announces roster cuts, trims active roster down to 52|url=https://www.atlantafalcons.com/news/falcons-2018-roster-atlanta-announces-roster-cuts-trims-active-roster-down-to-52|website=AtlantaFalcons.com|date=September 1, 2018|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Will|last=McFadden|title=Falcons sign 10 players to practice squad|url=https://www.atlantafalcons.com/news/falcons-sign-10-players-to-practice-squad|website=AtlantaFalcons.com|date=September 2, 2018|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref>

===Birmingham Iron===
After the season ended, Hassenauer signed with the [[Birmingham Iron]] of the [[Alliance of American Football]], where he played eight games.<ref>{{cite news|first=Mark|last=Inabinett|url=https://www.al.com/sports/2019/01/birmingham-iron-sets-roster-for-inaugural-season.html|title=Birmingham Iron sets roster for inaugural season|newspaper=[[The Birmingham News]]|date=January 30, 2019|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref>

===Pittsburgh Steelers===
On April 5, 2019, Hassenauer signed with the [[Pittsburgh Steelers]],<ref>{{cite web|first=Teresa|last=Varley|title=Steelers sign Hassenauer, Tocho|url=https://www.steelers.com/news/steelers-sign-hassenauer-tocho|website=Steelers.com|date=April 5, 2019|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> but was waived on August 31.<ref>{{cite web|first=Teresa|last=Varley|title=Steelers make roster cuts|url=https://www.steelers.com/news/steelers-make-roster-cuts|website=Steelers.com|date=August 31, 2019|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> On November 20, he was signed to the Steelers' practice squad,<ref>{{cite web|first=Dave|last=Bryan|title=Steelers Sign C J.C. Hassenauer To Practice Squad, Release TE Kevin Rader|url=https://steelersdepot.com/2019/11/steelers-sign-c-j-c-hassenauer-to-practice-squad-release-te-kevin-rader/|website=SteelersDepot.com|date=August 31, 2019|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> and was promoted to the active roster on December 24.<ref>{{cite web|first=Alex|last=Kozora|title=Steelers Place QB Mason Rudolph On IR|url=https://steelersdepot.com/2019/12/steelers-place-qb-mason-rudolph-on-ir/|website=SteelersDepot.com|date=August 31, 2019|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref>

On December 2, 2020, Hassenauer made his first NFL start in a 19–14 win over the [[Baltimore Ravens]].<ref>{{cite web|first=Chris|last=Adamski|title=J.C. Hassenauer latest 'next man up' contributing to Steelers|url=https://triblive.com/sports/j-c-hassenauer-latest-nest-man-up-contributing-to-steelers/|website=TribLive.com|date=December 2, 2020|access-date=December 2, 2020}}</ref>

Hassenauer signed a one-year contract extension with the Steelers on March 8, 2021.<ref>{{cite news |author=Varley, Teresa |title=Hassenauer signed to a one-year contract |url=https://www.steelers.com/news/hassenauer-signed-to-a-one-year-contract |website=Steelers.com |date=March 8, 2021 |access-date=March 8, 2021}}</ref> He was placed on injured reserve on November 27, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Varley, Teresa|date=November 27, 2021|title=Steelers make moves, place two on IR|url=https://www.steelers.com/news/steelers-make-moves-place-two-on-ir|website=Steelers.com}}</ref> He was activated on December 25.
