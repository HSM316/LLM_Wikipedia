'''Chaithoai Roaza''' (16 December 1930 – 9 January 1994) was a [[Bangladesh]]i politician from [[Rangamati District|Rangamati]].  He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Roaza was born on 16 December 1930 in [[Kawkhali Upazila, Rangamati|Kawkhali]], [[Rangamati District|Rangamati]].<ref name=a/> He provided financial help and arms to the freedom fighters of [[Chittagong]] during the [[Liberation War of Bangladesh]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://kawkhali.rangamati.gov.bd/site/page/f842322c-2144-11e7-8f57-286ed488c766/%E0%A6%AC%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%96%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%AF%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%A4%20%E0%A6%AC%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%AF%E0%A6%95%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%A4%E0%A6%BF%E0%A6%A4%E0%A7%8D%E0%A6%AC
|title=বিখ্যাত ব্যক্তিত্ব
|website=www.kawkhali.rangamati.gov.bd
|language=bn |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> He was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Chittagong Hill Tracts-2 as an [[Independent politician|independent]] candidate in 1973.<ref name="1973results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/1st.pdf |title=List of 1st Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=13 August 2014|format=PDF}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://samakal.com/todays-print-edition/tp-priyo-chittagong/article/18126047/রাঙামাটিতে-দুই-তালুকদারের-লড়াই
