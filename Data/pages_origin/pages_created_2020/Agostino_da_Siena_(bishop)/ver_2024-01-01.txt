'''Agostino da Siena, [[Order of Friars Minor|O.F.M.]]''' (died 1484) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as [[Bishop of Montemarano]] (1477–1484).

==Biography==
Agostino da Siena was ordained a priest in the [[Order of Friars Minor]].<ref name=HierarchiaII-Montemarano/><ref name=CathHierAgoSiena/>
On 24 Jan 1477, he was appointed during the papacy of [[Pope Sixtus IV]] as [[Bishop of Montemarano]].<ref name=HierarchiaII-Montemarano/><ref name=CathHierAgoSiena/>
He served as Bishop of Montemarano until his death in 1484.<ref name=HierarchiaII-Montemarano/><ref name=CathHierAgoSiena/>
