'''Schachen (Herisau) railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Schachen (Herisau)}}) is a railway station in [[Herisau]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Appenzell Ausserrhoden|Appenzell Ausserrhoden]]. It is an intermediate station on the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]] and is served by local trains only.

== Services ==
Schachen (Herisau) is served by two services of the [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf870">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/870.pdf | title = Romanshorn - St. Gallen - Wattwil - Rapperswil | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=4 December 2019 | accessdate = 30 December 2019}}</ref>

* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|2}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway between [[Nesslau-Neu St. Johann railway station|Nesslau-Neu St. Johann]] and [[Altstätten SG railway station|Altstätten SG]].
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|4}}: hourly service over the Bodensee–Toggenburg railway via [[Sargans railway station|Sargans]] (circular operation).
