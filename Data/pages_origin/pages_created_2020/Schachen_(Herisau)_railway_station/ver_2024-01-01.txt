'''Schachen (Herisau) railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Schachen (Herisau)}}) is a railway station in [[Herisau]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Appenzell Ausserrhoden|Appenzell Ausserrhoden]]. It is an intermediate station on the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]] and is served as a [[request stop]] by local trains only.
