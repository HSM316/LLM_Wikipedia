'''Zachary Merton Hospital''' is a health facility at Glenville Road, [[Rustington]], [[West Sussex]], England. It is managed by the [[Sussex Community NHS Foundation Trust]].

==History==
The facility, which was financed by a trust fund established by [[Zachary Merton]], was opened as a convalescent home in April 1937.<ref name=hh>{{cite web|url=https://historic-hospitals.com/english-hospitals-rchme-survey/sussex-west-sussex/|title=Zachary Merton Hospital|publisher=Historic Hospitals|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> It became a maternity hospital in 1939<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.littlehamptongazette.co.uk/lifestyle/exhibition-tells-story-of-rustington-community-hospital-1-7944269|title=Exhibition tells story of Rustington community hospital|publisher=Littlehampton Gazette|date=4 May 2017|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> and, after joining the [[National Health Service]] as the Zachary Merton Maternity Home in 1948,<ref name=na>{{cite web|url=http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/hospitalrecords/details.asp?id=2301|title=Zachary Merton Hospital, Littlehampton|publisher=National Archives|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> it became a community hospital in 1979.<ref name=hh/>
