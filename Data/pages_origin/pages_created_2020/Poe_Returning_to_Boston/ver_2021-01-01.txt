'''Poe Returning to Boston''' is a statue of [[Edgar Allan Poe]] in [[Boston, Massachusetts]]. It was created by the american sculptor Stefanie Rocknak.<ref name=Selyee/> The statue is located at the corner of Boylston and Charles streets at Edgar Allan Poe Square.<ref name=Lewis/>

It is accompanied by an [[The Raven|oversized flying raven]]; his suitcase lid has fallen open, leaving a "paper trail" of literary works embedded in the sidewalk behind him.<ref name="Fox2013"/><ref name="Kaiser"/><ref name="PFOB2"/><ref name=Lewis/> The public unveiling on October 5, 2014 was attended by former US poet laureate [[Robert Pinsky]].<ref name="Lee"/>
