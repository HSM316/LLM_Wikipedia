During the 1989–90 season, '''[[Leeds United F.C.|Leeds United A.F.C.]]''' competed in the [[Football League Second Division]].

== Season summary ==
In the 1989–90 season, Leeds were promoted to the [[Football League First Division|First Division]], having finished as champions of the [[Football League Second Division|Second Division]] on 85 points.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rsssf.com/engpaul/FLA/1989-90.html|title=RSSSF - England - Season 1989/90|website=www.rsssf.com|access-date=2019-12-30}}</ref>
