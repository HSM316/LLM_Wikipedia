The '''2020 NCAA Division I Outdoor Track and Field Championships''' was the 79th [[NCAA Division I Men's Outdoor Track and Field Championships]] and the 39th [[NCAA Division I Women's Outdoor Track and Field Championships]] held at [[Mike A. Myers Stadium]] in [[Austin, Texas]] on the campus of the [[University of Texas at Austin]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ustfccca.org/ustfccca-calendar/future-collegiate-championships|title=Future Collegiate Championships|work=[[USTFCCCA]]}}</ref> In total, forty-two different men's and women's [[track and field]] events was contested from Wednesday, June 10 to Saturday, June 13, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ncaa.com/championships/trackfield-outdoor/d1 |title=2020 NCAA Division I Outdoor Track and Field Championships |work=[[National Collegiate Athletic Association]]}}</ref>
