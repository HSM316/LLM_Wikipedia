The '''Home Technology Association''' is an American industry organization for home technology integration dealerships.

==Certification==
The Homes Technology Association runs the HTA Certified [[certification program]] began in 2017. Certification is applied for on an annual basis,<ref name=Rest>{{Cite web|url=https://restechtoday.com/home-technology-association/|title=HTA Gains Ground in Home Tech Professional Certification Initiative|date=July 9, 2019}}</ref> and indicates the top 10% to 15% of dealers within a particular market.<ref name=Resid>{{Cite web|url=https://www.residentialsystems.com/news/home-technology-association-looks-to-certify-top-integrators|title=Home Technology Association Looks to Certify Top Integrators|date=August 31, 2017|website=Residential Systems}}</ref> In addition to running the certification process,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dealerscope.com/article/in-first-full-year-hta-reports-strong-growth-and-engagement/|title=In First Full Year, HTA Reports Strong Growth and Engagement|first=Rob|last=Stott|website=Dealerscope}}</ref> the program also administers the "Best in the US" awards for the home technology installation industry.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cepro.com/audio-video/hta_launches_best_in_the_u-s-_awards_program/|title=HTA Launches 'Best in the U.S.' Awards Program|date=August 29, 2018|website=CEPRO}}</ref>

HTA certification is provided by the Home Technology Association to home technology integration dealers, and is based upon installation experience and business practices,<ref>https://www.connecteddesign.com/article/hta-summit-signals-strength/</ref> including a history of technical competence, marketplace reputation, and quality of aftercare service and support. Additional criteria for certification includes a five year absence of [[bankruptcy]], proper [[liability insurance]], and others.<ref name=Rest /> Certified dealers are provided with resources such as a budgeting tool for dealers to set transparent pricing,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cepro.com/news/home-technology-association-summit/|title=HTA Summit Focuses on Customer Experience|date=October 4, 2019|website=CEPRO}}</ref> in addition to other onboarding tools.<ref name=Rest />
