'''Bogomil Petrov''' ({{lang-bg|Богомил Петров}}, born 20 November 1939) is a Bulgarian [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]]. He competed in the [[Weightlifting at the 1964 Summer Olympics – Men's 67.5 kg|men's lightweight event]] at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pe/bogomil-petrov-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418122326/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pe/bogomil-petrov-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Bogomil Petrov Olympic Results |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>
