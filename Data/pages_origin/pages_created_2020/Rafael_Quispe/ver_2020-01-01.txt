'''Rafael Arcangel Quispe Flores''' is an indigenous Bolivian community leader, politician and current head of the Fund for Indigenous Development after appointment by [[Jeanine Añez]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.paginasiete.bo/nacional/2019/11/26/anez-posesiona-quispe-como-director-del-fondioc-238561.html|title=Añez posesiona a Quispe como director del Fondioc - Diario Pagina Siete|website=www.paginasiete.bo}}</ref> He was born in Ayllu Sicuypata on 24 Octobert 1969<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=http://www.diputados.bo/legisladores/rafael-arcangel-quispe-flores|title=Rafael Arcangel Quispe Flores|date=June 27, 2017|website=Cámara de Diputados}}</ref> in the department of [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz]]. 

He is a [[mallku]] (traditional leader) and became leader of [[National Council of Ayllus and Markas of Qullasuyu|National Council of Ayllus and Markas of Cullasuyu]] (CONAMAQ), a body of traditional leaders representing collective land-holdings ([[Ayllu|ayllus]]) and regions (markas) from throughout the [[Aymara people|Aymara]] realm ([[Collasuyu|Cullasuyu]]).<ref name="auto2">{{Cite web|url=https://nacla.org/article/beyond-extraction-interview-rafael-quispe|title=Beyond Extraction: An Interview With Rafael Quispe|website=NACLA}}</ref> In March 2014, he reported that the then Executive Secretary of the Federation of Peasant Women Bartolina Sisa de La Paz, Felipa Huanca, was part of the mulimillion dollar embezzlement of the liquidated Indigenous Fund.<ref name="auto1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.paginasiete.bo/nacional/2019/3/29/rafael-quispe-el-lado-humoristico-polemico-de-la-politica-213521.html|title=Rafael Quispe, el lado humorístico y polémico de la política - Diario Pagina Siete|website=www.paginasiete.bo}}</ref> He is currently a member of the Chamber of Deputies for UD (Unidad Demócrata).<ref name="auto"/> He has been a vocal critic of the [[Evo Morales]] government for many years.<ref name="auto2"/> 

He is also well known for his humorous and polemic style of politics, pulling many stunts, including giving "a "basic level" [[Aymara language]] translation dictionary to President Evo Morales when the debate about the authorities speaking his native language was in vogue"<ref name="auto1"/> Although Morales is ethnically Aymara, he does not speak the language.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.elalteno.com.bo/ciudad/20181212/quispe-quiere-frenar-evo-porque-no-habla-aymara|title=Quispe quiere frenar a Evo porque “no habla aymara” &#124; El Alteño|website=www.elalteno.com.bo}}</ref> In the run-up to the 2019 election, he delivered a tray of eggs to Vice President Linera to boost Linera's brainpower if he were to take up the challenge of facing Quispe in a debate.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lostiempos.com/actualidad/pais/20190618/diputado-rafael-quispe-reitera-pedido-debate-alvaro-garcia-linera|title=El diputado Rafael Quispe reitera pedido de debate a Álvaro García Linera|date=June 18, 2019|website=Los Tiempos}}</ref>
