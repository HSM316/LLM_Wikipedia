'''Machiel Kiel''' (born February 28, 1938) is a Dutch professor of [[art history]]. Narrow specialist in [[Ottoman architecture]] in the [[Balkans]].

For more than half a century after the Second World War, he worked on the grounds, restorations and with the [[Ottoman archives]] in [[Sofia]], [[Istanbul]] and [[Ankara]]. <ref>[http://www.turkishculture.org/whoiswho/academics/machiel-kiel-265.htm Prof. Dr. Machiel Kiel]</ref>

He personally researched the Ottoman architecture of the [[Old Bazaar, Skopje]] before the [[1963 Skopje earthquake]].
