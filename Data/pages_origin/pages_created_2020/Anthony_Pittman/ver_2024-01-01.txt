'''Anthony Pittman''' (born November 24, 1996<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nfl.com/players/anthonypittman/profile?id=PIT087988|title=Anthony Pittman|website=NFL.com|language=en|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref>) is an [[American football]] [[linebacker]] for the [[Detroit Lions]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). He played [[college football]] at [[Wayne State Warriors football|Wayne State]].

== College career ==
Pittman prepped at [[Birmingham Groves High School]] where he was all league and district as a senior. Lightly recruited he played [[college football]] at [[Wayne State Warriors football|Wayne State]]. At Wayne State, Pittman was a two-year team captain and two-year all-[[Great Lakes Intercollegiate Athletic Conference]] performer.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Schoch |first=Matthew |date=October 18, 2019 |title=Wayne State, Groves LB Anthony Pittman surviving numbers game with Lions |url=https://www.detroitnews.com/story/sports/nfl/lions/2019/10/18/wayne-state-groves-lb-anthony-pittman-surviving-numbers-game-lions/4020872002/ |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=Detroit News |language=en}}</ref> During his college career, Pittman recorded 181 career tackles.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Kirven |first=Jeremiah |date=August 7, 2019 |title=Detroit Lions' Anthony Pittman (Wayne State) eager to do what it takes to make team |url=https://www.freep.com/story/sports/nfl/lions/2019/08/07/detroit-lions-anthony-pittman-wayne-state/1931134001/ |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=Detroit Free Press |language=en}}</ref> Pittman also saw action on special teams for the Warriors.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Schoch |first=Matthew |date=June 5, 2019 |title=Wayne State's Anthony Pittman could carry on Michigan tradition with Lions |url=https://www.detroitnews.com/story/sports/nfl/lions/2019/06/05/anthony-pittman-could-carry-michigan-tradition-detroit-lions/1349647001/ |access-date=December 30, 2019 |website=Detroit News |language=en}}</ref>

Pittman went undrafted in the [[2019 NFL Draft]]. He then signed with the Detroit Lions.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theoaklandpress.com/sports/birmingham-s-anthony-pittman-learns-from-best-at-detroit-lions/article_a1a468da-b3d2-11e9-8a55-e7e0b9284fac.html|title=Birmingham's Anthony Pittman learns from best at Detroit Lions training camp|last=Pasche|first=Paula|website=The Oakland Press|language=en|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref>

On December 4, 2019, Pittman was released from the Lions practice squad.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lionswire.usatoday.com/2019/12/04/lions-sign-te-cole-herdman-to-practice-squad-release-lb-anthony-pittman/|title=Lions sign TE Cole Herdman to practice squad, release LB Anthony Pittman|date=December 4, 2019|website=Lions Wire|language=en|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> He was resigned to the practice squad on December 9, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://971theticket.radio.com/articles/press-release/lions-sign-free-agent-lb-anthony-pittman-to-practice-squad|title=Lions Sign Free Agent LB Anthony Pittman To Practice Squad|date=December 9, 2019|website=97.1 The Ticket|language=en|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> Pittman was promoted to the active roster on December 27, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.detroitnews.com/story/sports/nfl/lions/2019/12/27/detroit-lions-promote-wayne-states-anthony-pittman-off-practice-squad/2757008001/|title=Lions promote Wayne State's Anthony Pittman off practice squad|last=Rogers|first=Justin|website=Detroit News|language=en|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-sign-lb-anthony-pittman-to-active-roster|title=Lions sign LB Anthony Pittman to active roster|website=www.detroitlions.com|language=en-US|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref>

On September 5, 2020, Pittman was waived by the Lions and signed to the practice squad the next day.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lions announce roster moves|url=https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-announce-roster-moves-x4330|website=DetroitLions.com|date=September 5, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Lions establish practice squad|url=https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-establish-practice-squad|website=DetroitLions.com|date=September 6, 2020}}</ref> He was placed on the practice squad/[[COVID-19]] list by the team on December 22, 2020,<ref>{{cite news |title=Anthony Pittman: Placed on COVID-19 list |url=https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/football/news/anthony-pittman-placed-on-covid-19-list/ |website=CBSSports.com |date=December 22, 2020 |access-date=February 5, 2021}}</ref> and restored to the practice squad on January 2, 2021.<ref>{{cite news |title=Anthony Pittman: Restored to practice squad |url=https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/football/news/anthony-pittman-restored-to-practice-squad/ |website=CBSSports.com |date=January 2, 2021 |access-date=February 5, 2021}}</ref> He signed a reserve/future contract on January 5, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lions announce Reserve/Future signings|url=https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-announce-reserve-future-signings-bolden-kennedy-pittman|website=DetroitLions.com|date=January 5, 2021|access-date=January 12, 2021|archive-date=January 15, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210115134547/https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-announce-reserve-future-signings-bolden-kennedy-pittman|url-status=dead}}</ref>

On August 30, 2022, Pittman was waived by the Lions and signed to the practice squad the next day.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lions announce roster moves|url=https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-announce-roster-moves-x0035|website=DetroitLions.com|date=August 30, 2022|access-date=September 3, 2022|archive-date=January 2, 2023|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230102104838/https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-announce-roster-moves-x0035|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Lions announce roster moves|url=https://www.detroitlions.com/news/lions-announce-roster-moves-x6174|website=DetroitLions.com|date=August 31, 2022}}</ref> He was promoted to the active roster on September 27.
