'''Wesley Person Jr.''' (born August 1, 1995) is an American professional [[basketball]].

Selected by the [[Maine Red Claws]] in the 2nd Round of the [[2019 NBA G League draft]] out of [[Troy University]], Person played previously for the Roseto Sharks of the Italian A2 Basketball League. 

==NBA G League career==

===Maine Red Claws (2019)===
Despite being picked by the Maine Red Claws with the 4th pick in the 2nd Round of the 2019 NBA G League Draft, Person Jr. was waived by Maine following training camp in November of that year.

==Personal==
Born in [[Brantley, Alabama]], Wesley Person Jr. is the son of former NBA player [[Wesley Person]], and nephew or former NBA player [[Chuck Person]]<ref name=chuckperson>[http://sports.espn.go.com/los-angeles/nba/columns/story?id=5278158 2010 NBA Playoffs: Los Angeles Lakers' Chuck Person is more than just a mentor - ESPN Los Angeles]</ref>.  Person played college basketball at [[Troy University]]<ref>http://www.troytrojans.com/roster.aspx?rp_id=4447</ref> and in 2019 was selected by the [[Maine Red Claws]] in the [[2019 NBA G League Draft]].<ref>{{cite web|first1=Evans|last1=Boston|title=2019 NBA G League Draft RED CLAWS DRAFT FOUR, ANNOUNCE TRAINING CAMP ROSTER|url=https://maine.gleague.nba.com/news/2019-draft|date=October 26, 2019|website=NBA.com|accessdate=October 26, 2019}}</ref> Wesley's nephew, Adrian Person, is a former nationally ranked JUCO basketball player who averaged a national best 31 points per game at [[Southern Union State Community College]] in Wadley, Alabama in 1997.<ref>[http://www.news-gazette.com/sports/illini-sports/mens-basketball/1997-11-05/tate-juco-guard-real-deal-coach-says.html Tate: Juco guard real deal, coach says]</ref>
