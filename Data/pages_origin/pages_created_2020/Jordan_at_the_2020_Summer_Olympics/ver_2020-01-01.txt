'''[[Jordan]]''' is scheduled to compete at the '''[[2020 Summer Olympics]]''' in [[Tokyo]] from 24 July to 9 August 2020. It will be the nation's eleventh consecutive appearance at the Summer Olympics.

==Equestrian==
{{main article|Equestrian at the 2020 Summer Olympics|Equestrian at the 2020 Summer Olympics – Qualification}}
Jordan entered one jumping rider into the Olympic competition by finishing in the top two, outside the group selection, of the individual FEI Olympic Rankings for Group F (Africa and Middle East), marking the country's recurrence to the sport after an eight-year absence.
