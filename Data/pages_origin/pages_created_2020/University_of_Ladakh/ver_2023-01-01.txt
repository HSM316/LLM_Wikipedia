'''University of Ladakh''' ('''UOL''') or '''Ladakh University''', is a public  university located in Indian [[Union territory]] of [[Ladakh]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.financialexpress.com/education-2/ladakh-to-get-its-first-ever-university-sac-approves-jammu-and-kashmir-university-of-ladakh-bill-2018/1417157/|title=Ladakh to get its first ever university! SAC approves ‘Jammu and Kashmir University of Ladakh Bill-2018’|date=2018-12-18|website=The Financial Express|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indiatoday.in/education-today/news/story/ladakh-is-getting-its-first-ever-university-html-1411229-2018-12-17|title=Ladakh is getting its first ever university, approval passed by J&K govt|last= Web Desk|first=India Today|website=India Today|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>  It was established on 16 December 2018 by ''The University of Ladakh Act, 2018 (Governor Act No. LVI of 2018)'' of [[Government of Jammu and Kashmir]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://jklaw.nic.in/pdf/LVI.pdf|title=The University of Ladakh Act,2018|website=Department of Law, Justice & Parliamentary Affairs Govt of J&K}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailyexcelsior.com/university-of-ladakh-convenes-maiden-syndicate-meeting/|title=University of Ladakh convenes maiden Syndicate meeting|last=Excelsior|first=Daily|date=2019-11-26|website=Daily Excelsior|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://uol.ac.in/about|title=HISTORY PERSPECTIVE|website=uol.ac.in|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref> It is a cluster university comprising degree colleges of [[Leh]], [[Kargil]], [[Nubra Valley]], [[Zanskar]], [[Dras]] and [[Khalatse]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/politics-and-nation/pm-modi-inaugurates-first-ever-university-in-ladakh/articleshow/67816373.cms|title=PM Narendra Modi inaugurates first-ever university in Ladakh|date=2019-02-03|work=The Economic Times|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

==History==

University of Ladakh had entered into Memorandum of Understanding(MoU) with Delhi University to promote scientific and academic collaboration between two Universities.<ref name="lad uni ref6">{{cite news |title=Delhi University Signs Agreement With University Of Ladakh |url=https://www.ndtv.com/education/delhi-university-signs-agreement-university-of-ladakh |access-date=13 October 2021 |publisher=NDTV.com |date=5 August 2021 |language=en}}</ref> The understanding will help in organising academic workshops,conferences, seminars and cultural exchanges of mutual interest, organising sporting events in mutual places and also exchange of expertise in revising the existing curricular topics so that they can meet the current demands of employment and industry.

University of Ladakh has got Dr Surinder Kumar Mehta as its Vice-Chancellor for a period of three years.<ref name="lad uni ref12">{{cite news |title=Dr Mehta is new VC of Ladakh University |url=https://www.dailyexcelsior.com/dr-mehta-is-new-vc-of-ladakh-university/ |access-date=13 October 2021 |publisher=Jammu Kashmir Latest News {{!}} Tourism {{!}} Breaking News J&K |date=8 August 2021}}</ref>
