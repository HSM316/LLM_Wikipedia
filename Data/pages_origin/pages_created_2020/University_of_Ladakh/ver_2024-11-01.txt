'''University of Ladakh''' ('''UOL'''), aka '''Ladakh University''', is a public university located in [[Leh]] in [[Leh district]] of [[India|Indian]] [[Union territory]] of [[Ladakh]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.financialexpress.com/education-2/ladakh-to-get-its-first-ever-university-sac-approves-jammu-and-kashmir-university-of-ladakh-bill-2018/1417157/ |title=Ladakh to get its first ever university! SAC approves ‘Jammu and Kashmir University of Ladakh Bill-2018’ |date=2018-12-18 |website=The Financial Express |language=en-US |access-date=2019-12-31 |author=FE Online }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.indiatoday.in/education-today/news/story/ladakh-is-getting-its-first-ever-university-html-1411229-2018-12-17 |title=Ladakh is getting its first ever university, approval passed by J&K govt |author=India Today Web Desk |work=India Today |language=en |access-date=2019-12-31 |date=2018-12-26 }}</ref>

{{stack|[[File:Kargil Campus.jpg|thumb|alt=Kargil Campus|Kargil campus.]] }}

UOL is a cluster university comprising the degree colleges of [[Leh]], [[Kargil]], [[Nubra Valley]], [[Zanskar]], [[Dras]], and [[Khalatse]].<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/politics-and-nation/pm-modi-inaugurates-first-ever-university-in-ladakh/articleshow/67816373.cms |title=PM Narendra Modi inaugurates first-ever university in Ladakh |date=2019-02-03 |work=The Economic Times |access-date=2019-12-31 |author=PTI }}</ref>

==History==
On 16 December 2018, UOL was established by ''The University of Ladakh Act, 2018 (Governor Act No. LVI of 2018)'' of [[Government of Jammu and Kashmir]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://jklaw.nic.in/pdf/LVI.pdf |title=The University of Ladakh Act, 2018 |website=Department of Law, Justice & Parliamentary Affairs Govt of J&K }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.dailyexcelsior.com/university-of-ladakh-convenes-maiden-syndicate-meeting/|title=University of Ladakh convenes maiden Syndicate meeting |author=Daily Excelsior |date=2019-11-26 |work=Daily Excelsior |language=en-US |access-date=2019-12-31 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://uol.ac.in/about |title=HISTORY PERSPECTIVE |website=uol.ac.in |access-date=2019-12-31 }}</ref>

On 5 August 2021, University of Ladakh entered into a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with [[Delhi University]] to promote scientific and academic collaboration between the two universities. The understanding was expected to help in organising academic workshops, conferences, seminars, and cultural exchanges of mutual interest, organising sporting events in mutual places, and facilitating exchanges of expertise in revising the existing curricular topics so that they can meet the current demands of employment and industry.<ref name="lad uni ref6">{{cite news |title=Delhi University Signs Agreement With University Of Ladakh |url=https://www.ndtv.com/education/delhi-university-signs-agreement-university-of-ladakh |author=Press Trust of India |access-date=2021-10-13 |publisher=NDTV.com |date=2021-08-05 |language=en}}</ref>

On 8 August 2021, University of Ladakh appointed Dr Surinder Kumar Mehta as its Vice-Chancellor for a period of three years.<ref name="lad uni ref12">{{cite news |title=Dr Mehta is new VC of Ladakh University |url=https://www.dailyexcelsior.com/dr-mehta-is-new-vc-of-ladakh-university/ |access-date=2021-10-13 |publisher=Daily Excelsior |author=Daily Excelsior |date=2021-08-09 }}</ref>
