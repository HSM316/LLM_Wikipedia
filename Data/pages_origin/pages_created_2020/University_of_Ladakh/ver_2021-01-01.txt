'''University of Ladakh''' or '''Ladakh University''', is a public university located in [[Ladakh]], [[India]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.financialexpress.com/education-2/ladakh-to-get-its-first-ever-university-sac-approves-jammu-and-kashmir-university-of-ladakh-bill-2018/1417157/|title=Ladakh to get its first ever university! SAC approves ‘Jammu and Kashmir University of Ladakh Bill-2018’|date=2018-12-18|website=The Financial Express|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indiatoday.in/education-today/news/story/ladakh-is-getting-its-first-ever-university-html-1411229-2018-12-17|title=Ladakh is getting its first ever university, approval passed by J&K govt|last= Web Desk|first=India Today|website=India Today|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

==About University==
It was established on 16 December 2018 by ''The University of Ladakh Act, 2018 (Governor Act No. LVI of 2018)'' of erstwhile [[Government of Jammu and Kashmir]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://jklaw.nic.in/pdf/LVI.pdf|title=The University of Ladakh Act,2018|website=Department of Law, Justice & Parliamentary Affairs Govt of J&K}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailyexcelsior.com/university-of-ladakh-convenes-maiden-syndicate-meeting/|title=University of Ladakh convenes maiden Syndicate meeting|last=Excelsior|first=Daily|date=2019-11-26|website=Daily Excelsior|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://universityofladakh.org.in/history.php|title=HISTORY PRESPECTIVE|website=universityofladakh.org.in|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref> It is a cluster university comprising degree colleges of [[Leh]], [[Kargil]], [[Nubra Valley]], [[Zanskar]], [[Dras]] and [[Khalatse]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/politics-and-nation/pm-modi-inaugurates-first-ever-university-in-ladakh/articleshow/67816373.cms|title=PM Narendra Modi inaugurates first-ever university in Ladakh|date=2019-02-03|work=The Economic Times|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>
