'''Stancho Penchev''' ({{lang-bg|Станчо Пенчев}}, born 9 May 1940) is a Bulgarian [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]]. He competed at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]] as a light heavyweight  and the [[1972 Summer Olympics]] as a heavyweight.<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pe/stancho-penchev-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418122146/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pe/stancho-penchev-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Stancho Penchev Olympic Results |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> He was ranked number one in Bulgaria for his weight class in 1963, 1966, 1969, 1971, 1973 and 1974.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Stancho Penchev, Olympic Lifters Profiles @ Lift Up |url=http://www.chidlovski.net/liftup/l_athleteStatsResult.asp?a_id=2417 |access-date=2022-04-23 |website=www.chidlovski.net}}</ref>
