'''James Frederick Vennings''' ({{Failed verification span|text=born 20 November 2000|date=December 2023}}) is an English professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[National League (division)|National League]] club [[Braintree Town F.C.|Braintree Town]].<ref name="soccerway">{{soccerway|591456}}</ref>

==Career==
Vennings started his career at local grassroots side, Our Lady Youth, before joining [[Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal]] at the age of nine. Following two years in North London, he signed for [[Charlton Athletic F.C.|Charlton Athletic]].<ref>{{Cite news |title=Academy Day At home with James Vennings and family |url=https://www.charltonafc.com/news/view/5e46ef8dd2f5e/academy-day-at-home-with-james-vennings-and-family |publisher=Charlton Athletic FC |date=14 February 2020 |accessdate=6 June 2024}}</ref> On 30 November 2019, he made his debut for Charlton during a 3–1 home defeat to [[Sheffield Wednesday F.C.|Sheffield Wednesday]], replacing fellow academy graduate, [[Albie Morgan]] with less than 30 minutes to play.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Charlton Athletic vs. Sheffield Wednesday |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/11/30/england/championship/charlton-athletic-football-club/sheffield-wednesday-fc/3035915/ |publisher=Soccerway |date=30 November 2019 |accessdate=6 June 2024}}</ref> During his time at [[The Valley (stadium)|The Valley]], Vennings also enjoyed brief loan spells in the [[National League (division)|National League]] with [[Aldershot Town F.C.|Aldershot Town]]<ref>{{Cite news |title=LOAN WATCH Vennings joins Aouachria at Aldershot Town |url=https://www.charltonafc.com/news/view/6158d4c2b72f5/loan-watch-vennings-joins-aouachria-at-aldershot-town#:~:text=James%20Vennings%2C%20who%20joined%20National,weather%20conditions%20six%20minutes%20later. |publisher=Charlton Athletic FC |date=4 October 2021 |accessdate=6 June 2024}}</ref> and [[Bromley F.C.|Bromley]]<ref>{{Cite news |title=Ravens sign James Vennings on loan from Charlton Athletic |url=https://www.bromleyfc.co.uk/news/first-team/ravens-sign-james-vennings-on-loan-from-charlton-athletic/ |publisher=Bromley FC |date=15 March 2022 |accessdate=6 June 2024}}</ref> before leaving the club in June 2022 upon the expiry of his contract.

Ahead of the 2022–23 campaign, Vennings returned to Bromley on a permanent deal following his loan spell.<ref>{{Cite news |title=James Vennings returns to Hayes Lane |url=https://www.bromleyfc.co.uk/news/first-team/james-vennings-returns-to-hayes-lane/ |publisher=Bromley FC |date=1 July 2022 |accessdate=6 June 2024}}</ref> During his two years at the club, he went onto feature over 60 times, scoring just once. Following the club's promotion to [[EFL League Two|League Two]], he left in June 2024 at the end of his contract.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Bromley FC Released and Retained List |url=https://www.bromleyfc.co.uk/news/first-team/bromley-fc-released-and-retained-list-2024-25/#:~:text=Bromley%20Football%20Club%20can%20confirm,and%20extended%20for%202024%2F25. |publisher=Bromley FC |date=31 May 2024 |accessdate=6 June 2024}}</ref>

On 5 June 2024, it was announced that Vennings would join newly-promoted National League side, [[Braintree Town F.C.|Braintree Town]] ahead of the 2024–25 campaign.<ref>{{Cite news |title=National League new boys Braintree Town sign Kyrell Lisbie |url=https://www.braintreeandwithamtimes.co.uk/sport/24369264.ex-colchester-striker-kevin-lisbies-son-kyrell-joins-braintree/ |publisher=Braintree & Witham Times |first=Jonathan |last=Waldron |date=5 June 2024 |accessdate=6 June 2024}}</ref>
