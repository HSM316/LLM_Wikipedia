'''''The Summer Girl''''' is a [[lost film|lost]]<ref>[http://memory.loc.gov/diglib/ihas/loc.mbrs.sfdb.9617/default.html The Library of Congress/FIAF American Silent Feature Film Survival Catalog:''The Summer Girl'']</ref> 1916 [[silent film|silent]] film comedy drama directed by [[Edwin August]] and starring [[Mollie King (actress)|Mollie King]]. It was produced by [[Peerless Pictures Studios]] and distributed by [[World Pictures]].<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20150928194335/http://www.afi.com/members/catalog/DetailView.aspx?s=&Movie=14368 The AFI Catalog of Feature Films:''The Summer Girl'']</ref><ref>''Pictorial History of the Silent Screen'', p. 112 c.1953 by Daniel Blum {{ISBN|0-399-50667-5}}</ref>
