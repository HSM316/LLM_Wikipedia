'''Mike France''' (born October 22, 1962) is an American politician who has served in the [[Connecticut House of Representatives]] from the 42nd district since 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theday.com/article/20161016/NWS01/161019414 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161221231807/https://www.theday.com/article/20161016/NWS01/161019414 |url-status=dead |archive-date=2016-12-21 |title=Mike France is running unopposed for state representative from the 42nd District |publisher=Theday.com |date=2016-10-16 |accessdate=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theday.com/article/20181026/NWS01/181029453 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190624170144/https://www.theday.com/article/20181026/NWS01/181029453 |url-status=dead |archive-date=2019-06-24 |title=France faces challenge from Schwebel for 42nd House District seat |publisher=Theday.com |date=2018-10-26 |accessdate=2020-01-01}}</ref> France will be seeking the [[2022 United States House of Representatives elections in Connecticut|2022]] Republican nomination to unseat incumbent Democrat [[Joe Courtney (politician)|Joe Courtney]] in [[Connecticut's 2nd congressional district]].<ref>{{cite web |title=State Rep.
