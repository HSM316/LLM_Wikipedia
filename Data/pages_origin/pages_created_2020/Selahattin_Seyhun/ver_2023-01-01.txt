'''Selahattin Seyhun''' (born 28 June 1999) is a Turkish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[Çatalcaspor]] on loan from [[Bucaspor 1928]].

==Professional career==
On 5 December 2019, Seyhun signed his first professional contract with [[Kayserispor]] for 5 years.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sabah.com.tr/spor/futbol/2019/12/05/kayserispor-selahattin-seyhun-ile-5-yillik-sozlesme-imzaladi|title=Kayserispor, Selahattin Seyhun ile 5 yıllık sözleşme imzaladı|website=Sabah}}</ref> Seyhun made his professional debut for Kayserispor in a 6–2 [[Süper Lig]] loss to [[Trabzonspor]] on 28 December 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ca.soccerway.com/matches/2019/12/28/turkey/super-lig/trabzonspor/kayserispor/3081926/|title=Trabzonspor vs. Kayserispor - 28 December 2019 - Soccerway|website=ca.soccerway.com}}</ref>
