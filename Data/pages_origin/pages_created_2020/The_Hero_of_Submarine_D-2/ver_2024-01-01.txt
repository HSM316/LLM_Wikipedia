'''''The Hero of Submarine D-2''''' is a [[lost film|lost]]<ref>[http://memory.loc.gov/diglib/ihas/loc.mbrs.sfdb.6123/default.html The Library of Congress/FIAF American Silent Feature Film Survival Catalog:''The Hero of Submarine D-2'']</ref> 1916 silent film adventure war film directed by [[Paul Scardon]] and starring [[Charles Richman (actor)|Charles Richman]]. It was produced by the [[Vitagraph Company of America]] and released by [[V-L-S-E]] Incorporated.<ref>[http://silentera.com/PSFL/data/H/HeroOfSubmarineDTwo1916.html ''The Hero of Submarine D-2'' at silentera.com]</ref><ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20170712161323/http://www.afi.com/members/catalog/DetailView.aspx?s=&Movie=14125 The AFI Catalog of Feature Films:''The Hero of Submarine D-2''](Wayback)</ref><ref>''Pictorial History of the Silent Screen'', p.114 c.1953 by Daniel Blum {{ISBN|0-399-50667-5}}</ref>
