'''Fabián Felipe Taborda Torres''' (born 19 September 1978) is a Colombian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{Soccerway}}</ref>

==Career==
Taborda was the head coach of the [[Colombia women's national football team|Colombia women's national team]] at the [[2015 FIFA Women's World Cup]] and [[Football at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|2016 Summer Olympics]].
