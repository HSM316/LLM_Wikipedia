The '''Minister of Marine''' in [[New Zealand]] was a former [[Cabinet members|cabinet member]] appointed by the Prime Minister to be responsible for New Zealand's marine transport, aquaculture and fishing industries and in charge of the [[New Zealand Marine Department]]. The portfolio was abolished in 1972 with responsibilities split between the [[Minister of Transport (New Zealand)|Minister of Transport]] (marine transport) and [[Minister of Agriculture (New Zealand)|Minister of Agriculture]] (aquaculture and fishing). Similar duties are performed today by the Minister of Transport and [[Minister of Fisheries (New Zealand)|Minister of Fisheries]].
