'''Traian Georgescu''' (20 March 1931 – 15 May 2008) was a [[Romania]]n football [[Defender (association football)|defender]] who spent his entire career at [[FC Universitatea Cluj|Universitatea Cluj]]. He was [[FC Universitatea Cluj|"U" Cluj]]'s captain when the club won the [[1964–65 Cupa României|1964–65]] [[Cupa României]].<ref name=cluj/>

Georgescu also worked as a [[surgeon]] and when his team colleague [[Remus Câmpeanu]] was diagnosed with [[appendicitis]], Georgescu was the one who operated him at Câmpeanu's request.<ref name=romaniadevarati/> He died in 2008 a few years after being diagnosed with [[Alzheimer's disease]].<ref name=romaniadevarati/>
