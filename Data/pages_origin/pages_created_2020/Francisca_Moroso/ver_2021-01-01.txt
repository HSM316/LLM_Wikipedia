'''Francisca Moroso Velasco''' (born 20 March 1993) is a Chilean [[women's association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]]. She has been a member of the [[Chile women's national football team|Chile women's national team]].

==Club career==
After spending her first years playing in Chile, in 2018, Moroso moved for signing with Spanish team [[CFF Cáceres]].

On 20 September 2019, Moroso agreed terms with [[Segunda División Pro (women)|Segunda División]] team [[Sporting de Gijón (women)|Sporting de Gijón]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.publimetro.cl/cl/grafico-chile/2019/09/20/francisca-moroso-nueva-jugadora-sporting-gijon.html|publisher=Publímetro|language=es|date=20 September 2019|title=Francisca Moroso cambia de club en España y se convirtió en nueva jugadora del Real Sporting de Gijón}}</ref> However, on 8 November 2019, the club and the player parted ways due to personal reasons.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.realsporting.com/noticia/acuerdo-para-la-baja-de-fran-moroso|publisher=[[Real Sporting]]|accessdate=11 November 2019|title=Acuerdo para la baja de Fran Moroso|date=8 November 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
Moroso has represented Chile at the [[2010 FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup]].<ref>{{FIFA player|321177}}</ref> At senior level, she played the [[2013 International Women's Football Tournament of Brasília]], the [[Football at the 2014 South American Games|2014 South American Games]] and the [[2014 Copa América Femenina]].<ref name=Roja/>
