'''Yahia Nader Moustafa Elsharif''' ([[Arabic]]:يحيى نادر مصطفى الشريف) (born 11 September 1998) is an Emirati [[football (soccer)|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder (association football)#Defensive midfielder|defensive midfielder]] or a [[Defender (association football)#Centre-back|centre back]] for [[Al Ain FC|Al Ain]].<ref name="agleague">{{cite web|url=https://www.uaefa.ae/#/profile/28638|title=Yahia Nader|author=XS Studios|publisher=www.uaefa.ae|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kooora.com/?player=144769|title=Yahia Nader|website=www.kooora.com}}</ref>

==Career==
Yahia Nader is a football player with Egyptian origins born in the [[United Arab Emirates]]. He played with [[Al Ain FC|Al Ain]] in juniors and participated in the first team in 2018 after allowing those born in [[United Arab Emirates]] to participate in the [[UAE Pro League]]. He was granted Emirati citizenship in 2019 and was chosen to participate with the Olympic team in the [[2020 AFC U-23 Championship]] .<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/Alwsl_123/status/1211990522170159104|title=UAE Olympic team list in preparation for the Asian Cup scheduled in Thailand|website=twitter}}</ref>

He receives his First International cap with Emirates "A selection" when coming in as substitute of [[Abdullah Ramadan (footballer)|Abdullah Ramadan]] at the 75th minute of the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – AFC fourth round|World Cup 2022 AFC Playoff]] against [[Australia men's national soccer team|Australia]] in June 2022.
