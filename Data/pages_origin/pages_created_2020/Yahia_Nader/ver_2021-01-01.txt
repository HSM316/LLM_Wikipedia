'''Yahia Nader''' ([[Arabic]]:يحيى نادر) (born 11 September 1998) is an [[United Arab Emirates|Emirati]] [[football (soccer)|footballer]]. He currently plays as a [[Midfielder]] for [[Al Ain FC|Al Ain]] .<ref name="agleague">{{cite web|url=https://www.uaefa.ae/#/profile/28638|title=Yahia Nader|author=XS Studios|publisher=www.uaefa.ae|accessdate=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kooora.com/?player=144769|title=Yahia Nader|website=www.kooora.com}}</ref>

==Career==
Yahia Nader is an [[Egypt]]ian player born in the [[United Arab Emirates]]. He played with [[Al Ain FC|Al Ain]] in juniors and participated in the first team in 2018 after allowing the born in [[United Arab Emirates]] to participate in the [[UAE Pro League]]. He was granted Emirati citizenship in 2019 and was chosen he was chosen to participate with the Olympic team to participate in [[2020 AFC U-23 Championship]] .<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/Alwsl_123/status/1211990522170159104|title=UAE Olympic team list in preparation for the Asian Cup scheduled in Thailand|website=twitter}}</ref>
