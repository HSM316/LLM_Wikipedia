'''Yahia Nader''' ([[Arabic]]:يحيى نادر) (born 11 September 1998) is an [[Egypt]]ian-born [[United Arab Emirates|Emirati]] [[football (soccer)|footballer]]. He currently plays as a [[Midfielder]] for [[Al Ain FC|Al Ain]] .<ref name="agleague">{{cite web|url=https://www.uaefa.ae/#/profile/28638|title=Yahia Nader|author=XS Studios|publisher=www.uaefa.ae|accessdate=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kooora.com/?player=144769|title=Yahia Nader|website=www.kooora.com}}</ref>

==Career==
Yahia Nader is an [[Egypt]]ian player born in the [[United Arab Emirates]]. He played with [[Al Ain FC|Al Ain]] in juniors and participated in the first team in 2018 after allowing the birth of children to participate in the [[UAE Pro League]]. He was granted Emirati citizenship in 2019 and was chosen to participate with the Olympic team to participate in [[2020 AFC U-23 Championship]] .<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://arabia.eurosport.com/article/%D9%83%D8%B1%D8%A9-%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%82%D8%AF%D9%85/%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%AF%D9%88%D8%B1%D9%8A-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%A5%D9%85%D8%A7%D8%B1%D8%A7%D8%AA%D9%8A/%D9%85%D8%B5%D8%B1%D9%8A%D8%A7%D9%86-%D9%8A%D8%B4%D8%A7%D8%B1%D9%83%D8%A7%D9%86-%D9%81%D9%8A-%D9%81%D9%88%D8%B2-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%A3%D9%88%D9%84%D9%85%D8%A8%D9%8A-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%A5%D9%85%D8%A7%D8%B1%D8%A7%D8%AA%D9%8A-%D8%B9%D9%84%D9%89-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%A3%D8%B2%D8%B1%D9%82-%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%83%D9%88%D9%8A%D8%AA%D9%8A|title=Two Egyptians participate in the victory of the UAE Olympic team|website=eurosport}}</ref>
