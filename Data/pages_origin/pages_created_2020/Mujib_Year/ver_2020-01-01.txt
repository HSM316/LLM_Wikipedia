The government of [[Bangladesh]] has announced the commemoration of the '''''year''''' as the '''''Mujib Year''''' on the occasion of the birth anniversary of the founding leader of the country, [[Sheikh Mujibur Rahman]] . <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.somoynews.tv/pages/details/119490|title=২০২০-২১ সালকে মুজিববর্ষ ঘোষণা|last=webdesk@somoynews.tv|website=somoynews.tv|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.jugantor.com/todays-paper/first-page/67201/২০২০-২১-সাল-হবে-মুজিববর্ষ--প্রধানমন্ত্রী|title=২০২০-২১ সাল হবে মুজিববর্ষ : প্রধানমন্ত্রী|website=Jugantor|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> This year will be celebrated from March 17, 2020 to March 26, 2021. ''The father of the nation'' and the famed leader of ''Bangabandhu,'' was born on March 17, 1920 AD in East Bengal (now in Tungipara village of [[Gopalganj District, Bangladesh|Gopalganj district]] of Bangladesh). Again, on March 26, 2021, Bangladesh will mark the centenary of its independence. The announcement of the year is particularly important as Sheikh Mujibur Rahman is actively involved in the country's independence struggle .

== Global Celebration ==
The UN General Assembly, UNESCO, has decided to jointly celebrate the Mujib Year with Bangladesh at the UNESCO 40th General Assembly. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dhakatribune.com/bangladesh/dhaka/2019/11/27/unesco-to-celebrate-mujib-year-with-bangladesh|title=Unesco to celebrate Mujib Year with Bangladesh|date=2019-11-27|website=Dhaka Tribune|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://unesdoc.unesco.org/ark:/48223/pf0000371424_eng|website=unesdoc.unesco.org|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>The decision was made in the presence of all UNESCO members on November 12-27 in Paris this year, held on November 25.
