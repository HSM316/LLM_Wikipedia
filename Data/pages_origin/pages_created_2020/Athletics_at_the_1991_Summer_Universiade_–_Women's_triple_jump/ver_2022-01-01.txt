{{AthleticsAt1991Universiade}}
The '''women's [[triple jump]]''' event at the '''[[Athletics at the 1991 Summer Universiade|1991 Summer Universiade]]''' was held at the [[Don Valley Stadium]] in [[Sheffield]] on 20 July 1991.<ref>{{cite news |title=Results |url=https://www.newspapers.com/clip/41351117/south_florida_sun_sentinel/ |publisher=[[South Florida Sun Sentinel]] |date=21 July 1991 |page=41}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author1=Mel Watman |title=Women's day at Universiade |journal=Athletics Today |date=25 July 1991 |volume=5 |issue=32 |pages=17–20 |publisher=Athletics Today Publications Limited |location=Surrey |issn=0269-1302}}</ref> It was the first time that this event was contested by women at the Games.
