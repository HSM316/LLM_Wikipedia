'''''Mr. Simpatia''''' is the second [[studio album]] by Italian rapper [[Fabri Fibra]]. It was released on 1 September 2004 by Vibrarecords.

== In popular culture ==
The Mr. Simpatia album is considered to be one of the albums that made history of Italian hip hop,<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-02-21|title=Fabri Fibra, il suo Mr. Simpatia è disco d'oro dopo 14 anni: "Quell'album ha cambiato la storia del rap italiano, non ci sono cazzi"|url=https://www.ilfattoquotidiano.it/2018/02/21/fabri-fibra-il-suo-mr-simpatia-e-disco-doro-dopo-14-anni-quellalbum-ha-cambiato-la-storia-del-rap-italiano-non-ci-sono-cazzi/4177950/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-14|website=[[Il Fatto Quotidiano]]|language=it-IT}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Bettinelli|first=Daniele|date=2018-02-27|title=Mr. Simpatia è il disco cult del rap italiano|url=https://medium.com/@danielebettinelli/mr-simpatia-%C3%A8-il-disco-cult-del-rap-italiano-ace575aacf5d|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-14|website=Medium|language=it}}</ref> that's why also the rapper [[Madman (rapper)|Madman]] in his first album ''[[Escape from Heart]]'' cited it, in the verse contained in the track ''Trash Music'': 

''E non so niente a memoria, nemmeno l'Ave Maria'' – ''And I don't know anything by heart, not even the [[Ave Maria]]''

''Ma so qualcosa di storia tipo Mr. Simpatia'' – ''But I know something about history, like Mr. Simpatia''

In this verse, Madman says that like a Christian knows by heart the Ave Maria and other prayers, MadMan knows by heart some records that have made the history of [[Italian rap]], which for him is religion.<ref>{{Cite web|title=E non so niente a memoria, nemmeno l'Ave Maria / Ma so qualcosa di storia tipo Mr. Simpatia|url=https://genius.com/5061517/Madman-trash-music/E-non-so-niente-a-memoria-nemmeno-lave-maria-ma-so-qualcosa-di-storia-tipo-mr-simpatia|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-14|website=[[Genius (website)|Genius]]|language=en}}</ref>
