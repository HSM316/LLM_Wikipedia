'''Studio Mao''', is an American [[independent film]] production company.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/film/reviews/driveways-review-1203157241/|title=Film Review: ‘Driveways’|last=Debruge|first=Peter|date=2019-03-07|website=Variety|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> It was founded October 23, 2006 in the [[New York (state)|State of New York]], [[United States]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pro.imdb.com/company/co0217114/|title=Studio Mao - Client & Contact Info {{!}} IMDbPro|website=pro.imdb.com|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> In 2018 an office in the Voentorg building at 10, Vozdvizhenka Street was opened in Moscow, Russia.

Studio Mao produced the short drama film [[Skin (2018 short film)|''Skin'']] by director Guy Nattiv and producer [[Jaime Ray Newman]] in association with New Native Pictures.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cinemotionlab.com/novosti/04382-obladatel_oskara_prodyuser_stiven_mao_my_v_poiske_silnyh_istoriy/|title=Обладатель Оскара, продюсер Стивен Мао: мы в поиске сильных историй|website=CINEMOTION|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> The film was acquired by [[Fox Searchlight Pictures|Fox Searchlight]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/02/skin-fox-searchlight-oscar-nominated-short-film-acquired-1202552542/|title=Oscar-Nominated Short Film ‘Skin’ Acquired By Fox Searchlight|last=Evans|first=Greg|date=2019-02-08|website=Deadline|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.yahoo.com/entertainment/oscar-nominated-short-film-skin-142415221.html|title=Oscar-Nominated Short Film ‘Skin’ Acquired By Fox Searchlight|website=www.yahoo.com|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> and won an Oscar ® in the category of [[Academy Award for Best Live Action Short Film|Best Live Action Short]] at the 91st [[Academy Awards]].<ref>{{Citation|title="Skin" wins Best Live Action Short Film|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x-fsRO3CbW8|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

== Upcoming Projects ==
Studio Mao produced and is packaging the documentary film ''Solo'' directed by Olga Pankratova filmed in Russia, France, Germany and Poland. The film follows six dancers in the genres of Classical Ballet, Vogue, Contemporary, experimental hip-hop, pole dance, and Krump. The Solo world premiere in Moscow, Russia was February 14, 2022 at the Oktober Cinema.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vokrug.tv/article/show/16451018751/|title=Film Review: ‘Solo’|last=Solovova|first=Mila|date=2022-02-17|website=Vokrug|language=ru|access-date=2022-02-19}}</ref> Russia World Vision acquired Russia/CIS rights in advance of the premiere.
