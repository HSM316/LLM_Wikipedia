'''Studio Mao''', is an American [[independent film]] production company<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/film/reviews/driveways-review-1203157241/|title=Film Review: ‘Driveways’|last=Debruge|first=Peter|last2=Debruge|first2=Peter|date=2019-03-07|website=Variety|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>. It was founded October 23, 2006 in the [[New York (state)|State of New York]], [[United States]] where it is currently headquartered<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pro.imdb.com/company/co0217114/|title=Studio Mao - Client & Contact Info {{!}} IMDbPro|website=pro.imdb.com|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>.

Studio Mao produced the short drama film [[Skin (2018 short film)|Skin]] by director Guy Nattiv and producer [[Jaime Ray Newman]] in association with New Native Pictures<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cinemotionlab.com/novosti/04382-obladatel_oskara_prodyuser_stiven_mao_my_v_poiske_silnyh_istoriy/|title=Обладатель Оскара, продюсер Стивен Мао: мы в поиске сильных историй|website=CINEMOTION|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>. The film was acquired by [[Fox Searchlight Pictures|Fox Searchlight]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/02/skin-fox-searchlight-oscar-nominated-short-film-acquired-1202552542/|title=Oscar-Nominated Short Film ‘Skin’ Acquired By Fox Searchlight|last=Evans|first=Greg|last2=Evans|first2=Greg|date=2019-02-08|website=Deadline|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.yahoo.com/entertainment/oscar-nominated-short-film-skin-142415221.html|title=Oscar-Nominated Short Film ‘Skin’ Acquired By Fox Searchlight|website=www.yahoo.com|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> and won an Oscar ® in the category of [[Academy Award for Best Live Action Short Film|Best Live Action Short]] at the 91st [[Academy Awards]]<ref>{{Citation|title="Skin" wins Best Live Action Short Film|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x-fsRO3CbW8|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>.
