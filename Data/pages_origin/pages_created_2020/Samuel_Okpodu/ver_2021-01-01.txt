'''Samuel Okpodu''' (born 7 October 1962) is a Nigerian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Okpodu was the head coach of the [[Nigeria women's national football team|Nigeria women's national team]] at the [[2003 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
