The '''2020 Just World Indoor Bowls Championship''' will be held at [[Potters Resort|Potters Leisure Resort]], [[Hopton-on-Sea]], [[Great Yarmouth]], [[England]], from 9–26 January 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://worldbowlstour.tv/the-just-2020-world-indoor-championships/|title=THE ‘JUST’ 2020 WORLD INDOOR CHAMPIONSHIPS|publisher=World Bowls}}</ref> The event is organised by the [[World Bowls Tour]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://worldbowlstour.tv/just-2020-open-singles-draw/|title=Draw and results|publisher=World Bowls}}</ref>
