'''''Rock'n'Roll Baby''''' is the fifth studio release by Australian hard rock band, [[Wolfmother]].<ref name="Astell" />

== History ==
The album was self-released and distributed by [[DistroKid]] on 29 December 2019.<ref>{{cite web |title=Wolfmother Surprise Releases New Album "Rock 'N' Roll Baby" |url=https://www.ghostcultmag.com/wolfmother-surprise-releases-new-album-rock-n-roll-baby/ |website=Ghost Cult Magazine |access-date=5 October 2021 |date=2 January 2020}}</ref> Two singles were released with the album, "Freedom Is Mine" and "Higher". The album was released with no advance notice and was recorded at a studio owned by [[Dave Grohl]], with primitive production.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Tobey |first=Josh |date=2020-05-16 |title=Wolfmother - Rock'n'roll Baby (2019) |url=https://wanderingthroughsound.com/wolfmother-rock-n-roll-baby-2019/ |access-date=2022-08-14 |website=Wandering Through Sound |language=en-US}}</ref><ref name="Astell">{{Cite web |last=Astell |first=Hal C. F. |date=13 January 2020 |title=Wolfmother - Rock 'n' Roll Baby (2019) |url=https://www.apocalypselatermusic.com/2020/01/wolfmother-rock-n-roll-baby-2019.html |access-date=2022-08-14 |website=Apocalypse Later}}</ref>
