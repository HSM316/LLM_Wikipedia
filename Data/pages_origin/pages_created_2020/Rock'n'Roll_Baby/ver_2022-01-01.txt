'''''Rock'n'Roll Baby''''' is the fifth studio album by Australian hard rock band, [[Wolfmother]]. The album was self-released and distributed by [[DistroKid]] on 29 December 2019.<ref>{{cite web |title=Wolfmother Surprise Releases New Album "Rock ‘N’ Roll Baby" |url=https://www.ghostcultmag.com/wolfmother-surprise-releases-new-album-rock-n-roll-baby/ |website=Ghost Cult Magazine |access-date=5 October 2021 |date=2 January 2020}}</ref> Two singles were released with the album, "Freedom Is Mine" and "Higher".
