'''''All Mad About Him''''' (French: '''''Toutes folles de lui''''') is a 1967 French-Italian [[comedy film]] directed by [[Norbert Carbonnaux]] and starring [[Robert Hirsch (actor)|Robert Hirsch]], [[Sophie Desmarets]] and Maria Latour.<ref>Cowie & Elley p.136</ref>

The film's sets were designed by the [[art director]]s [[Georges Wakhévitch]]. It was shot in [[Eastmancolor]]. Future star [[Edwige Fenech]] made her screen debut in the film.
