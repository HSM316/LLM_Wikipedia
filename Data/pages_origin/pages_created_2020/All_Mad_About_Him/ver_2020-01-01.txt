'''''All Mad About Him''''' (French: '''''Toutes folles de lui''''') is a 1967 French-Italian [[comedy film]] directed by [[Norbert Carbonnaux]] and starring [[Robert Hirsch]], [[Sophie Desmarets]] and [[Maria Latour]].<ref>Cowie & Elley p.136</ref>

The film's sets were designed by the [[art director]]s [[Georges Wakhévitch]].
