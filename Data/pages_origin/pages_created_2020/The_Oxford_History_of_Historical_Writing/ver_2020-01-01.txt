'''''The Oxford History of Historical Writing''''' is a five volume multi-authored history of [[Historiography|historical writing]] published by [[Oxford University Press]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://global.oup.com/academic/content/series/o/oxford-history-of-historical-writing-ohhw|title=Oxford History of Historical Writing - Oxford University Press|website=global.oup.com}}</ref> under the general editorship of [[Daniel Woolf]].
