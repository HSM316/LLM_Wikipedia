'''Sven Enar Edberg''' (9 July 1936 &ndash; 9 February 2013) was a Swedish weightlifter. He won a bronze medal at the 1958 European Championships<ref>[http://www.chidlovski.net/liftup/l_athleteStatsResult.asp?a_id=988 Enar Edberg]. chidlovski.net</ref> and placed 20th at the [[Weightlifting at the 1964 Summer Olympics – Men's 60 kg|1964 Olympics]], in the featherweight event.<ref name="SportsRef"/>
