The '''Maracaibo Open Invitational''' was a golf tournament in Venezuela played from 1960 to 1974. It was part of the Caribbean Tour.<ref name="philpga">{{Cite web|url=http://philadelphia.pga.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/1960-1969-Section-History1217.pdf|title=A Chronicle of the Philadelphia Section PGA and its Members, 1960–1969|last=Trenham|first=Peter|publisher=Philadelphia PGA|url-status=live}}</ref> The event was played at Maracaibo Country Club in [[Maracaibo, Venezuela]].<ref name=si>{{Cite magazine |url=https://www.si.com/vault/1962/02/12/591303/golf-events |title=Golf Events |magazine=Sports Illustrated |date=12 February 1962 |access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref> In the 1970s the event was sponsored by Ford.<ref name=graham>{{Cite web |url=https://www.pgatour.com/players/player.01436.david-graham.html |title=David Graham – Profile |publisher=PGA Tour |access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>
