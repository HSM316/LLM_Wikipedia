'''''Rhizomnium''''' is a [[genus]] of [[moss]]es in the family [[Mniaceae]]<ref name=fna/> commonly referred to as '''leafy mosses'''.<ref name=pnw1994/> They grow nearly worldwide, mostly in the northern hemisphere.<ref name=fna/>

==Species==
* ''[[Rhizomnium andrewsianum]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium appalachianum]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium glabrescens]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium gracile]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium horikawae]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium magnifolium]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium nudum]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium pseudopunctatum]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium punctatum]]''
* ''[[Rhizomnium striatulum]]''
