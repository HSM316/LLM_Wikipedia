'''''Merry Christmas Mr. Mo''''' ({{Korean|hangul=메리 크리스마스 미스터 모}}) is a 2016 South Korean comedy drama film. Shot in black and white, it was written and directed by first-time director [[Lim Dae-hyung]] and stars [[Gi Ju-bong]], Oh Jung-hwan, [[Go Won-hee]] and [[Jeon Yeo-been]].

==Plot==
Widower and barber Mo Geum-san ([[Gi Ju-bong]]) is terminally ill. His dying wish is to make a short film directed by his estranged son, Stephen (Oh Jung-hwan).<ref>{{cite web|first=Richard|last=Kuipers|title=Busan Film Review: ''Merry Christmas Mr. Mo''|url=https://variety.com/2016/film/reviews/merry-christmas-mr-mo-review-1201883148/|website=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|accessdate=31 December 2019|date=10 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Jason|last=Bechervaise|title=''Merry Christmas Mr Mo'': Busan Review|url=https://www.screendaily.com/reviews/merry-christmas-mr-mo-busan-review/5110254.article|website=[[Screen International]]|accessdate=31 December 2019|date=12 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Merry Christmas Mr. Mo (2017)|url=http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/eng/films/index/filmsView.jsp?movieCd=20160465|website=[[Korean Film Council]]|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Panos|last=Kotzathanasis|title=[Guest Film Review] ''Merry Christmas Mr. Mo''|url=https://www.hancinema.net/guest-film-review-merry-christmas-mr-mo-111301.html|website=[[Hancinema]]|accessdate=31 December 2019|date=21 October 2017}}</ref>
