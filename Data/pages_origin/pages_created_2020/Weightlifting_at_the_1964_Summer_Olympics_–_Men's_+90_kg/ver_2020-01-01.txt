{{Weightlifting at the 1964 Summer Olympics}}
The '''men's +90&nbsp;kg''' weightlifting competitions at the [[1964 Summer Olympics]] in [[Tokyo]] took place on 18 October at the [[Shibuya Public Hall]]. It was the tenth appearance of the heavyweight class.<ref name="SR">{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/summer/1964/WLT/mens-heavyweight.html |title=Weightlifting at the 1964 Tokyo Summer Games: Men's Middle-Heavyweight |work=Sports Reference |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>
