'''''A Public Reading of an Unproduced Screenplay About the Death of Walt Disney''''' is a 2013 play by [[Lucas Hnath]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sohorep.org/walt-disney-2|title=A Public Reading of an Unproduced Screenplay About the Death of Walt Disney – Soho Rep.|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

== Synopsis ==
The show is in the form of a screenplay written and performed by Walt Disney about himself and his death. It's about his last days on earth and a city he's going to build that's going to change the world. It's about his brother. It's about everyone who loves him so much, and it's about how sad they're going to be when he's gone.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dramatists.com/cgi-bin/db/single.asp?key=4794|title=Dramatists Play Service, Inc.|website=www.dramatists.com|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

== Production History ==
The play premiered at the Soho Rep in 2013. It was directed by Sarah Benson.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sohorep.org/walt-disney-2|title=A Public Reading of an Unproduced Screenplay About the Death of Walt Disney – Soho Rep.|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

The Wilbury Theatre Group produced the New England premiere of the play in November 2014. The production was directed by Brien Lang.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thewilburygroup.org/a-public-reading-of-an-unproduced-screenplay-about-the-death-of-walt-disney.html|title=The Wilbury Group|last=|first=|date=|website=thewilburygroup.org|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

The play was produced by MKA Theatre in Australia in 2018. The production was directed by Tobias Manderson-Galvin and starred a real life family.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mka.org.au/disney/|title=A Public Reading of an Unproduced Screenplay About the Death of Walt Disney|language=en-AU|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>
