'''''A Public Reading of an Unproduced Screenplay About the Death of Walt Disney''''' is a 2013 play by [[Lucas Hnath]].<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://sohorep.org/walt-disney-2|title=A Public Reading of an Unproduced Screenplay About the Death of Walt Disney – Soho Rep.|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>[https://www.nytimes.com/2013/05/11/theater/reviews/death-of-walt-disney-at-soho-rep.html ‘Public Reading About Death of Walt Disney,’ at Soho Rep - The New York Times]</ref>

== Synopsis ==
The show is in the form of a screenplay written and performed by [[Walt Disney]] about himself and his death. It's about his last days on earth and [[EPCOT (concept)|a city he's going to build that's going to change the world]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dramatists.com/cgi-bin/db/single.asp?key=4794|title=Dramatists Play Service, Inc.|website=www.dramatists.com|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

== Production History ==
The play premiered at the [[Soho Repertory Theatre|Soho Rep]] in 2013. It was directed by Sarah Benson.<ref name="auto"/>

The [[Wilbury Theatre Group]] produced the New England premiere of the play in November 2014. The production was directed by Brien Lang.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thewilburygroup.org/a-public-reading-of-an-unproduced-screenplay-about-the-death-of-walt-disney.html|title=The Wilbury Group|last=|first=|date=|website=thewilburygroup.org|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181203003515/http://thewilburygroup.org:80/a-public-reading-of-an-unproduced-screenplay-about-the-death-of-walt-disney.html |archive-date=2018-12-03 |access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

The play was produced by [[MKA: Theatre of New Writing|MKA Theatre]] in Australia in 2018. The production was directed by [[Tobias Manderson-Galvin]] and starred a real life family.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mka.org.au/disney/|title=A Public Reading of an Unproduced Screenplay About the Death of Walt Disney|language=en-AU|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>
