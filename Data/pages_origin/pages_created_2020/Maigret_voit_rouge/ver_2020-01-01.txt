'''''Maigret voit rouge''''' (English:'''''Maigret Sees Red''''') is a 1963 [[Cinema of France|French]]-[[Cinema of Italy|Italian]] [[crime film]] directed by Gilles Grangier and starring [[Jean Gabin]], [[Françoise Fabian]] and [[Roland Armontel]]. It is Gabin's third appearance as [[Belgium|Belgian]] writer [[Georges Simenon]]'s fictional detective [[Jules Maigret]].<ref name="hardy">{{cite book|last=Hardy|first=Phil|authorlink=Phil Hardy (journalist)|title=The BFI Companion to Crime|year=1997|page=217|publisher=[[University of California Press]]|ISBN=9780520215382}}</ref>
