'''Maqbool Ahmed''' ({{lang-ur|{{nastaliq|مقبول احمد}}}}; born 9 December 1992) is a Pakistani [[wicketkeeper]]-[[batsman]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Maqbool Ahmed profile|url=https://www.pcb.com.pk/player-detail.php?action=view_profile&player_id=24937&note=&pg=872&ipp=25&player_name=&father_name=&district=&region=&school=&address=&club=|work=Pakistan Cricket Board|access-date=30 December 2019}}</ref> He plays for [[Southern Punjab cricket team (Pakistan)|Southern Punjab]] in domestic cricket. He has played a total of 33 [[first-class cricket|first-class]], 29 [[List A cricket|List A]] and 2 [[Twenty20|T20]] games for [[Multan cricket team|Multan]], [[State Bank of Pakistan cricket team|State Bank of Pakistan]] and [[Sui Southern Gas Company cricket team|Sui Southern Gas Company]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/512/512880/f_Batting_by_Team.html|title=First-class batting and fielding for each team by Maqbool Ahmed|work=CricketArchive|access-date=2020-01-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/512/512880/a_Batting_by_Team.html|title=List A  batting and fielding for each team by Maqbool Ahmed|work=CricketArchive|access-date=2020-01-17}}</ref>
