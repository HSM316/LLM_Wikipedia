'''Albert Baró''' (born 29 April or 24 September 1996) is a Spanish actor,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm3131330/|title=Albert Baró|accessdate=4 January 2016|website=[[IMDb]]}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{cite web|title=Albert Baró: "La política es muy difícil de entender"|website=fotogramas.es|url=http://www.fotogramas.es/El-Relevo/2012/Albert-Baro-La-politica-es-muy-dificil-de-entender|date=24 October 2016|accessdate=15 October 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref> best known for having starred in the film ''[[The Wild Ones (film)|The Wild Ones]]'', and the series ''[[Merlí (TV series)|Merlí]]'' and ''[[Argentina, tierra de amor y venganza]]''.

== Career ==
Coming from a family with tradition in the theater, he trained in Horitzó Teatre de [[Sant Esteve de Palautordera]], with which he has made several theatrical productions such as ''Los Pastorets'', ''Abrazo-Sant Esteve Tsunami'' and ''[[Grease (musical)|Grease]]''.<ref name=":1">{{cite web|title=Albert Baró, de Sant Esteve, triomfa a la sèrie ‘Merlí’ de TV3|url=https://el9nou.cat/valles-oriental/cultura-i-gent/albert-baro-de-sant-esteve-triomfa-a-la-serie-merli-de-tv3/|website=el9nou.cat|language=Catalan|date=30 November 2015|accessdate=15 October 2019}}</ref> With twelve years he obtained his first professional work performing in Johan during the ninth and part of the tenth season of the series, ''[[El Cor de la Ciutat]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Quién es Albert Baró, el español que se luce en “Argentina, tierra de amor y venganza”|url=https://www.clarin.com/espectaculos/tv/albert-baro-espanol-luce-argentina-tierra-amor-venganza_0_22o6KOx5Q.html|website=clarin.com|date=14 March 2019|accessdate=15 October 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref> In 2009, he participated in the television version of Les veus del Pamano, a novel by [[Jaume Cabré]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Los fantasmas de la adolescencia en 'El primer día'|url=https://www.elmundo.es/elmundo/2012/03/02/ocio/1330697577.html|website=elmundo.es|date=5 March 2012|accessdate=15 October 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref> In 2012, he debuted on the big screen playing Gabi, one of the protagonists of ''[[The Wild Ones (film)|The Wild Ones]]'' and in 2014 he appeared in Asmodexia, by director Marc Carreté.<ref name=":1" /> On the other hand, in the theater he has participated in the play ''Els nostres tigres beuen llet'' by [[Albert Espinosa]], which was performed at the [[National Theatre of Catalonia]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Els nostres tigres beuen llet|website=nuvol.com|url = http://www.nuvol.com/opinio/els-nostres-tigres-beuen-llet-dalbert-espinosa/|date=21 December 2012|accessdate=15 October 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref><ref name=":0" />

In 2015, he joined the cast of the ''[[Merlí (TV series)|Merlí]]'' series. He appeared in the video clip of "[[The Less I Know the Better]]" by the Australian group [[Tame Impala]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Este video de Tame Impala es protagonizado por actores de Merlí y quizás no te habías dado cuenta|url=https://indiehoy.com/videos/este-video-tame-impala-protagonizado-actores-merli-quizas-no-te-habias-dado-cuenta/|website=indiehoy.com|date=14 August 2018|accessdate=15 October 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref> Between 2015 and 2016, he conducted an interpretation course at Laura Jou School and participated in the play, ''Citizenship'', which premiered in March in [[Barcelona, Spain]].<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ccma.cat/tv3/merli/examen-final-al-joan-de-merli/noticia/2702093/|title=Examen final al Joan de "Merlí"|accessdate=4 January 2016|date=11 December 2015|language=Spanish}}</ref> During 2018, he participated in the [[Televisión Española]] series ''[[Servir y proteger]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Albert Baró y Diego Dominguez preparan nueva serie en Argentina|url=https://www.diezminutos.es/telenovela/a1999358/albert-baro-diego-dominguez-argentina-tierra-de-amor-y-venganza/|website=diezminutos.es|date=27 November 2018|accessdate=15 October 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref> In 2019 he played Bruno, a Catalan fleeing the Civil War in [[El Trece]] telenovela ''[[Argentina, tierra de amor y venganza]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Albert Baró: el alumno de “Merlí” que Adrián Suar contrató para su tira de época|work=Clarín|url=https://www.clarin.com/espectaculos/tv/albert-baro-alumno-merli-adrian-suar-contrato-tira-epoca_0_0mMx6qIWo.html|date=7 November 2018|accessdate=15 October 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref>
