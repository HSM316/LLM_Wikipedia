'''''Spergo parunculis''''' is a [[species]] of [[sea snail]], a marine [[gastropod]] [[mollusk]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Raphitomidae]].<ref name="WoRMS">MolluscaBase (2019). MolluscaBase. Spergo parunculis Stahlschmidt, Chino & Fraussen, 2015. Accessed through: World Register of Marine Species at: http://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails&id=850511 on 2020-01-01</ref>

==Description==
The shell reaches a length of 84&nbsp;mm.
{{Expand section|date=January 2020}}

==Distribution==
This marine species occurs in the [[Mozambique Channel]].
