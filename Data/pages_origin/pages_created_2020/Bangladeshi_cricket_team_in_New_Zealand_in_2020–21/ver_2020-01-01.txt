The [[Bangladesh national cricket team|Bangladesh cricket team]] is scheduled to tour New Zealand in October 2020 to play three [[Twenty20 International]] (T20I) matches.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.icc-cricket.com/news/742337 |title=Schedule for inaugural World Test Championship announced |work=International Cricket Council |accessdate=11 January 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://icc-static-files.s3.amazonaws.com/ICC/document/2018/06/20/6dc2c8d4-e1a5-4dec-94b4-7121fab3cd7f/ICC_Tours.pdf |title=Men's Future Tours Programme |accessdate=11 October 2019 |work=International Cricket Council}}</ref> The matches are scheduled to take place ahead of the [[2020 ICC Men's T20 World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thenational.ae/sport/cricket/full-schedule-of-bangladesh-cricket-team-in-2020-including-test-series-against-australia-with-shakib-al-hasan-banned-1.958164 |title=Full schedule of Bangladesh cricket team in 2020 including Test series against Australia with Shakib Al Hasan banned |work=The National |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>
