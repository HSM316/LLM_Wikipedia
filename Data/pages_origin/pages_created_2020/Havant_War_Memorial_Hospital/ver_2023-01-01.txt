'''Havant War Memorial Hospital''' was a health facility in Crossway in [[Havant]], [[Hampshire]], England. It was managed by [[Southern Health NHS Foundation Trust]].

==History==
The foundation stone for the facility was laid by Major General [[J. E. B. Seely, 1st Baron Mottistone|John Seely]], [[Lord Lieutenant of Hampshire]], in January 1928.<ref name=history>{{cite web|url=http://thespring.co.uk/media/2995/15-havant-war-memorial-hospital-plus-idh.pdf|first=Dr. Giles |last=Maskell|date= 1 September 1971|pages=36â€“54 |title=The History of Havant War Memorial Hospital|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> The building, which was designed by Vernon-Inkpen and Rogers, was opened in July 1929.<ref name=history/> A fine frieze of [[Wedgwood]] tiles depicting [[nursery rhyme]]s was added to the children's ward in 1935.<ref>Cousins R & Rogers P. ''Bygone Havant.'' Phillimore & Co Ltd, Chichester, 1993.</ref> The hospital joined the [[National Health Service]] in 1948 and a casualty department was added in June 1957.<ref name=history/> After services transferred to modern facilities such as [[Fareham Community Hospital]], Havant War Memorial Hospital closed in September 2011 and was subsequently converted for use as a care home.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.portsmouth.co.uk/health/havant-s-hospital-set-to-become-new-care-home-1-3696813|title=Havant's hospital set to become new care home|date=4 April 2012|publisher=Portsmouth News|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>

===Replacement===
An 80 bed replacement hospital at the old Oak park school site was agreed and planning permission accepted by [[Havant Borough Council]] in 2010 but in the event was shelved, the site remaining empty, though a small ''Oak Park Clinic''{{Efn|{{As of|2021}} ''Oak Park clinic'' is under the stewardship of [[Portsmouth Hospitals University NHS Trust]]}} day services scheme did go ahead to provide day services.<ref name="AB">{{Cite web|author1=HCS Editor|last2=Buckley|first2=Ann|date=2021|title=Oak Park Hospital site update|url=https://havantcivicsociety.uk/2021/05/05/oak-park-hospital-site-update/|website=Havant Civic Society}}</ref>
