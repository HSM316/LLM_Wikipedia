'''''Tumi O Tumi''''' is a 2020 [[Bengali language|Bengali]] [[Drama Film|drama film]] directed by Arunima Dey.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://m.dailyhunt.in/news/india/english/the+times+of+bengal+english-epaper-timbanen/tumi+o+tumi+coming+soon-newsid-107968246|title=Tumi O Tumi Coming soon - The Times Of Bengal English|website=Dailyhunt|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref> This film was released on 17 January 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://in.bookmyshow.com/kolkata/movies/tumi-o-tumi/ET00119947?fbrefresh=1|title=Tumi O Tumi Movie (2020) {{!}} Reviews, Cast & Release Date in Kolkata|last=BookMyShow|website=BookMyShow|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/bengali/movie-details/tumi-o-tumi/movieshow/73155966.cms|title=Tumi O Tumi|work=Times of India|access-date=27 July 2020}}</ref>

==Plot==
The film revolves around the conflict between different stages of a woman's life in a male dominated society. The story is told from the perspective of a writer who narrates her own story. It states the life of three struggling women Gunjan, Sindur and Ashalata who are from three different generations and portrays the real meaning of women empowerment in Indian society.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/bengali/movies/news/soumitra-chatterjee-and-lily-chakraborty-to-team-up-once-again/articleshow/69218299.cms|title=Soumitra Chatterjee and Lily Chakraborty to team up once again - Times of India|website=The Times of India|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>
