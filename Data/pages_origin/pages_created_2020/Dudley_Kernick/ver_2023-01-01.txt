'''Dudley Henry John Kernick''' (24 August 1921 – 15 December 2019) was an English professional footballer who played as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]]. He played in the English football league for [[Torquay United F.C.|Torquay United]], [[Northampton Town F.C.|Northampton Town]] and [[Birmingham City F.C.|Birmingham City]].<ref>[http://grecianarchive.exeter.ac.uk/items/show/1491 The Grecian Archive: Dudley Kernick]</ref><ref>{{Hugman|10924}}</ref>

Kernick later retired to [[Florida]], but moved back to [[Nuneaton]] in his final years. He died in December 2019 at the age of 98.<ref>[https://www.stokesentinel.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/stoke-city-commercial-dudley-kernick-3654035 Ex-Stoke City commercial manager Dudley Kernick dies at 98]</ref>
