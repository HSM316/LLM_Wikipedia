This is a season-by-season list of records compiled by [[Yale Bulldogs men's ice hockey|Yale]] in men's [[ice hockey]]. The Yale men's team is the oldest active ice hockey team in [[North America]], predating all current professional and amateur clubs.

[[Yale University]] has won one [[List of NCAA Division I men's ice hockey champions|NCAA championship]] in its history, coming in [[2013 NCAA Division I Men's Ice Hockey Tournament|2013]].
