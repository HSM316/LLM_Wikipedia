'''''The Feast of Life''''' is a 1916 [[silent film|silent]] film drama directed by [[Albert Capellani]] and starring [[Clara Kimball Young]]. It was distributed by the World Film Company.<ref>[http://web.archive.org/web/20170710054144/http://www.afi.com/members/catalog/DetailView.aspx?s=&Movie=14090 The AFI Catalog of Feature Films:''The Feast of Life''](Wayback)</ref>


==Cast==
*[[Clara Kimball Young]] - Aurora Fernandez
*Mrs. E.M. Kimball - Senora Fernandez(*Pauline Kimball)
*[[Edward Kimball]] - Father Venture
*[[Doris Kenyon]] - Celida
*[[Robert Frazer]] - Pedro

==Preservation status==
The [[Library of Congress]] website lists no holding for the film. However Greta de Groats Clara Kimball Young webpage states the film is held at Narodni Filmovy Archive, Czech Republic. <ref>[http://memory.loc.gov/diglib/ihas/loc.mbrs.sfdb.5172/default.html The Library of Congress/FIAF American Silent Feature Film Survival Catalog:''The Feast of Life'']</ref><ref>[http://web.stanford.edu/~gdegroat/CKY/reviews/fol.htm ''The Clara Kimball Young Page'']; by Greta deGroat(Stanford Univ.)</ref>

Some of the film was shot in [[Cuba]].
