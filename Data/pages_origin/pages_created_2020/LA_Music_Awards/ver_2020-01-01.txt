The '''LA Music Awards''' is an awards program originally held in the city of [[Los Angeles, California]] to celebrate new artists in the Los Angeles music scene.  In 2015 the Awards held their 25th anniversary with a program in located at the [[Avalon Theater]] in Los Angeles.<ref name=MC>{{cite web|url=https://www.musicconnection.com/win-recording-contract-valued-100000/|work=Music Connection|date=28 August 2015|title=Win a Recording Contract Valued at $100,000}}</ref>  The international online competition allows fans to vote their favorite musical artist to the top of his/ her category.<ref name=MC/>

The program, which also includes the '''F.A.M.E.''' Awards (film, art, music, and entertainment) has since been relocated to the city of [[Las Vegas]], [[Nevada]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Say goodbye to Hollywood: F.A.M.E. awards find home in Las Vegas|url=https://www.reviewjournal.com/entertainment/entertainment-columns/kats/say-goodbye-to-hollywood-f-a-m-e-awards-find-home-in-las-vegas/|work=Las Vegas Review-Journal|author=John Katsilometes|date=3 April 2018}}</ref>  The show's producer, [[Al Bowman]], was is also the shows's founder.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vegasnews.com/159563/the-las-vegas-fame-awards-honor-olivia-newton-john-murray-sawchuck-marty-allen-anita-mann-and-others-at-the-hard-rock-live-in-las-vegas.html|title=The Las Vegas Fame Awards honor Olivia Newton-John, Murray SawChuck, Marty Allen, Anita Mann and others at the Hard Rock Live in Las Vegas|author=VegasNews.com|date=25 March 2017}}</ref>
