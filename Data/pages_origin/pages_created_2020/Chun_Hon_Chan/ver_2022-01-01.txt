'''Chun Hon Chan''' (21 July 1935 &ndash; 3 March 2004) was a Canadian [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]].<ref>Obituaries, ''La Journal de Montreal'', March 6, 2004</ref> He competed at the [[1968 Summer Olympics]] and the [[1972 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ch/chun-hon-chan-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418065237/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ch/chun-hon-chan-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Chun Hon Chan Olympic Results |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>
