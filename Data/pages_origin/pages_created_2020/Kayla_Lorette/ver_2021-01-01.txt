'''Kayla Lorette''' is a [[Canadians|Canadian]] actress from [[Ladysmith, British Columbia|Ladysmith]], [[British Columbia]].<ref name=ladysmith>Cole Schisler, [https://www.ladysmithchronicle.com/entertainment/ladysmiths-kayla-lorette-to-release-new-series-on-crave-new-years-day/ "Ladysmith’s Kayla Lorette to release new series on Crave New Year’s Day"]. ''[[Ladysmith-Chemainus Chronicle]]'', December 27, 2019.</ref> She is most noted for her performances in the film ''[[When the Storm Fades]]'', for which she won the [[Vancouver Film Critics Circle Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Canadian Film]] in 2019,<ref>[https://www.thestar.com/entertainment/movies/2019/01/08/haida-language-mystery-thriller-edge-of-the-knife-named-top-canadian-feature-film.html "Vancouver Film Critics Circle names Edge of the Knife top Canadian feature film"]. ''[[Toronto Star]]'', January 8, 2019.</ref> the television special ''[[The Second City Project]]'', for which she was a [[Canadian Screen Award]] nominee for Best Performance in a Variety or Sketch Comedy Program or Series at the [[4th Canadian Screen Awards]] in 2016,<ref>Brent Furdyk, [https://etcanada.com/news/37268/2016-canadian-screen-awards-nominees-announced/ "2016 Canadian Screen Awards Nominees Announced"]. ''[[ET Canada]]'', January 19, 2016.</ref> and the web series ''[[Space Riders: Division Earth]]'', for which she was a Canadian Screen Award nominee for Best Lead Performance in a Web Program or Series at the [[7th Canadian Screen Awards]] in 2019.

Her other credits have included the web series ''[[Everyone's Famous]]'', ''[[Gary and His Demons]]'' and ''[[New Eden]]'',<ref>Norman Wilner, [https://nowtoronto.com/movies/features/new-eden-crave-kayla-lorette-evany-rosen/ "Crave's New Eden explores why women are drawn to true crime"]. ''[[Now (newspaper)|Now]]'', January 1, 2020.</ref> the films ''[[She Stoops to Conquer (2015 film)|She Stoops to Conquer]]'',<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.cinefilles.ca/2015/09/12/tiff-15-stepping-into-another-skin-interview-with-kayla-lorette-from-she-stoops-to-conquer/|title = TIFF '15: Stepping into another skin with Kayla Lorette of 'She Stoops to Conquer'|last = Clarke|first = Amanda|date = September 12, 2015|work = Cinefilles|access-date = January 21, 2016}}</ref> ''[[When the Storm Fades]]'' and ''[[Filth City]]'', and stage-based [[improvisational comedy]] as part of the duo [[The Sufferettes]].

In 2020, she narrated a portion of the [[8th Canadian Screen Awards]] along with Evany Rosen.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.academy.ca/virtualpresentations/#:~:text=Narrators%20will%20include%20Eric%20McCormack,%2C%20Twitter%2C%20and%20YouTube%20channels.|title= Virtual Presentations, Hosts|website=academy.ca|access-date=15 June 2020}}</ref>
