== GDP of districts of Telangana ==
Gross District Domestic Product of Telangana at current prices from 2018–19 to 2020–21 (in INR Crore).<ref>https://www.telangana.gov.in/PDFDocuments/Telangana-Socio-Economic-Outlook-2023.pdf</ref>

Details of 2018–19 are those of Third Revised Estimates (TRE), 2019–20 are of Second Revised Estimates (SRE), 2020–21 are of First Revised Estimates (FRE) according to [https://www.telangana.gov.in/PDFDocuments/Telangana-Socio-Economic-Outlook-2023.pdf Telangana Socio Economic Outlook 2023].

* The tabulated figures provided below are outdated yet the only official information provided by the [[Government of India]]. This data was collected before the [[Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act, 2014|bifurcation of the state]] of [[Andhra Pradesh]] into the new state of Telangana with 10 districts. 
* The 10 districts below were further carved out in October 2018 to form [[List of districts of Telangana|33 new districts of Telangana]].
