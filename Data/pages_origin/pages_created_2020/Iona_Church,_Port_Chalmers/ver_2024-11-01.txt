'''Iona Church''' is an historic church in [[Port Chalmers]], [[New Zealand]]. The church building is listed as a Category I Historic Place.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.heritage.org.nz/the-list/details/7165|title=Search the List {{!}} Iona Church {{!}} Heritage New Zealand|website=www.heritage.org.nz|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

== History ==
Iona church was designed to replace an earlier wooden church. The new stone church was designed by [[Nathaniel Wales (architect)|Nathaniel Wales]]. The foundation stone was laid in 1871 and the building was opened on 7 January 1872. As a result of a growing congregation an additional gallery was added between 1882 and 1883. The new gallery became the main church and the original church building became the church hall.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite book|url=http://nzetc.victoria.ac.nz/tm/scholarly/tei-Cyc04Cycl-t1-body1-d3-d1-d20.html|title=The Cyclopedia of New Zealand|last=Cyclopedia Company Limited|first=|publisher=The Cyclopedia Company|year=1905|isbn=|location=Christchurch|pages=}}</ref>
