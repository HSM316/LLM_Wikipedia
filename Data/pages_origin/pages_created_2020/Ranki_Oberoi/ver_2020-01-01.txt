'''Ranki Oberoi''' is a Dutch Paralympic athlete competing in [[T20 (classification)|T20]]-classification [[long jump]] and [[triple jump]] events.<ref name="paralympic_bio">{{Cite web|url=https://www.paralympic.org/ranki-oberoi|title=Ranki Oberoi|website=[[International Paralympic Committee]]|url-status=live|access-date=30 December 2019}}</ref> At the [[2019 World Para Athletics Championships]] he won the gold medal in the [[2019 World Para Athletics Championships – Men's long jump|men's long jump T20]] event and he qualified to represent the [[Netherlands at the 2020 Summer Paralympics]].<ref name="bolt_inspired_oberoi_2019">{{Cite news|url=https://www.paralympic.org/news/bolt-inspired-oberoi-now-looks-himself|title=Bolt-inspired Oberoi now looks to himself|date=13 November 2019|work=[[International Paralympic Committee]]|access-date=30 December 2019|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="para_atleet_oberoi_wk_2019">{{Cite news|url=https://nos.nl/l/2310246|title=Para-atleet Oberoi springt naar goud: 'Ik ben gewoon wereldkampioen!'|date=13 November 2019|work=[[Nederlandse Omroep Stichting|NOS]]|access-date=30 December 2019|url-status=live|language=Dutch}}</ref>

He competed at the [[2015 IPC Athletics World Championships|2015 World Championships]] where he won the bronze medal in the [[2015 IPC Athletics World Championships – Men's triple jump|men's triple jump T20]] event and also at the [[2017 World Para Athletics Championships|2017 World Championships]] where he finished in 4th place in the [[2017 World Para Athletics Championships – Men's long jump|men's long jump T20]] event. At the [[2018 World Para Athletics European Championships|2018 European Championships]] he won the bronze medal in the [[2018 World Para Athletics European Championships – Men's long jump|men's long jump T20]] event.<ref name="para_atleet_oberoi_wk_2019"/>

He also represented the [[Netherlands at the 2016 Summer Paralympics]] and he finished in 4th place in the [[Athletics at the 2016 Summer Paralympics – Men's long jump|men's long jump T20]] event.<ref name="paralympic_bio"/>
