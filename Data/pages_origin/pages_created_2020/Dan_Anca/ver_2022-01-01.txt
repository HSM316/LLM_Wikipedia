'''Dan Sabin Anca''' (7 January 1947 – 20 October 2005) was a [[Romania]]n football [[midfielder]] and [[Manager (association football)|manager]] for [[FC Universitatea Cluj|Universitatea Cluj]].<ref name=RS/><ref name=NFT/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.stiridecluj.ro/sport/6-ani-de-la-moartea-lui-dan-anca-suporterii-plang|title=6 ani de la moartea lui Dan Anca! Suporterii plang|publisher=Stiridecluj.ro|language=Romanian |trans-title=6 years since Dan Anca died! The fans still crying| date=20 October 2011 |accessdate= 31 December 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
Dan Anca appeared at international level in 7 matches for [[Romania national football team|Romania]], making his debut in a friendly which ended 1–1 against [[England national football team|England]] played on the [[Wembley Stadium]] from [[London]].<ref name=EU>{{cite web|url=https://eu-football.info/_player.php?id=455|title=Dan Anca|publisher=European Football|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://eu-football.info/_match.php?id=14047|title=England - Romania 1:1|publisher=European Football|accessdate=8 June 2020}}</ref> He also played two games at the [[UEFA Euro 1972 qualifying|Euro 1972 qualifiers]] and one at the [[1974 FIFA World Cup qualification – UEFA Group 4|1974 World Cup qualifiers]].<ref name=EU/>
