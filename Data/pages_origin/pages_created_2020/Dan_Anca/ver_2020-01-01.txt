'''Dan Sabin Anca''' (7 January 1947 – 20 October 2005) was a [[Romania]]n football [[midfielder]] and [[Manager (association football)|manager]] for [[FC Universitatea Cluj|Universitatea Cluj]].<ref name=RS/><ref name=NFT/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.stiridecluj.ro/sport/6-ani-de-la-moartea-lui-dan-anca-suporterii-plang|title=6 ani de la moartea lui Dan Anca!
