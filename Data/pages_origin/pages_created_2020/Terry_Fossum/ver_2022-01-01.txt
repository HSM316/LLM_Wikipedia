'''Terry Fossum''' is an American businessperson and philanthropist. He is best known as the winner of the [[Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox]] reality television series ''[[Kicking & Screaming (TV series)|Kicking and Screaming]]''.

==Early life==
Terry L. Fossum was born in [[Mission, Texas]] but raised in [[McAllen, Texas]] as one of three brothers, during which time he joined the [[Boy Scouts of America]], where he became an Eagle Scout. He earned a degree in mechanical engineering from [[Texas A&M University]] while serving in the [[Corps of Cadets]], following which Fossum became an officer in the [[U.S. Air Force]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.themonitor.com/2017/09/08/camp-perry-to-receive-6-1-million-overhaul/|title=Camp Perry to receive $6.1 million overhaul|first=Travis|last=Whitehead|date=September 8, 2017}}</ref><ref name=Thorn>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bonnercountydailybee.com/local_news/20190426/been_there_survived_that_speaker_inspires_business_crowd|title=Been there, survived that: Speaker inspires business crowd|first=KAYE THORNBRUGH Hagadone News|last=Network|website=www.bonnercountydailybee.com}}</ref><ref name=Winch>{{Cite web|url=https://www.winchesterstar.com/news/clarke/philanthropist-fossum-speaks-at-friends-of-scouting-dinner/article_406b6197-dcd9-561c-a6f5-ed5ef7a591c5.html|title=Philanthropist Fossum speaks at 2018 Friends of Scouting Dinner|first=JOSH JANNEY &#124; The Winchester|last=Star|website=The Winchester Star}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://today.tamu.edu/2017/05/15/fossum-86-wins-kicking-and-screaming-takes-home-250k/|title=Fossum '86 Wins 'Kicking And Screaming,' Takes Home $250k|date=May 15, 2017|website=Texas A&M Today}}</ref>  He became the Officer of the Year for the [[Fairchild Air Force Base]]<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.chattanoogan.com/2018/2/9/363060/Community-Leaders-To-Support-Scouts.aspx|title=Community Leaders To Support Scouts At Annual Luncheon|website=www.chattanoogan.com}}</ref> and was the Executive Officer for a Group of Nuclear B-52 Bombers.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2017/apr/15/local-scoutmaster-nearing-finish-line-on-kicking-a/|title=Local Scoutmaster Terry Fossum nearing finish line on "Kicking and Screaming" reality TV show &#124; The Spokesman-Review|website=www.spokesman.com}}</ref> After the Air Force Fossum founded a direct sales company, and became a travelling public speaker.<ref name=Thorn />  He also founded the Terry L. Fossum Learning Center in [[Rwanda]], the Terry L. Fossum Scholarships for Underprivileged Youth in McAllen, and providing agricultural training for [[Malawi, Africa]].<ref name="auto"/> He has been named the [[Strategic Air Command]] Humanitarian of the Year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.spokanenorthrotary.org/Stories/kicking-and-screaming-leadership-terry-fossum|title="Kicking and Screaming" & Leadership -- Terry Fossum|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-04-06}}</ref>

==Television==
In 2017 Fossum appeared on the Fox reality television series ''Kicking and Screaming'', a show that paired survival experts with “wilderness impaired partners”.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.brownsvilleherald.com/news/valley/mcallen-native-cast-in-survivor--like-reality-show-premiering/article_ff9d8e16-047b-11e7-8f36-7b7881e43a14.html|title=McAllen native cast in 'Survivor'-like reality show premiering Thursday on Fox|website=Brownsville Herald}}</ref> He and his partner Natalie Casanova won the competition, splitting the $500,000 grand prize.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2017/apr/27/spokane-scoutmaster-wins-survivalist-reality-tv-sh/|title=Spokane Scoutmaster wins $250,000 grand prize on survivalist reality TV show &#124; The Spokesman-Review|website=www.spokesman.com}}</ref> Fossum was also the producer for the film ''Tiger Lily Is My Little Sister'', in which he also acted, and the film ''Role Prey''. Both films centered on the problem of human trafficking.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.entertainmentspokane.com/currentIssue.asp |title=Human trafficking movie to be filmed on Kalispel Reservation |publisher=Entertainment Spokane |date=January 29, 2020}}</ref>

==Volunteering==
Fossum has continued to be involved in the Boy Scouts of America, first as a scoutmaster, and eventually as the vice president for programs for the Washington State's Inland Northwest Council,<ref name=Winch /> as a resident of [[Spokane Valley, Washington]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.themonitor.com/2017/05/02/mcallen-native-wins-250000-in-survivalist-reality-show/|title=McAllen native wins $250,000 in survivalist reality show|first=Special to The|last=Monitor|date=May 2, 2017}}</ref>
