'''Terry Fossum''' is an American actor and businessperson. He is best known as the winner of the 2017 [[reality television]] series ''[[Kicking & Screaming (TV series)|Kicking and Screaming]]''. Fossum is an author and does business training and speaking engagements.

== Biography ==
Fossum was born in [[Mission, Texas]] but raised in [[McAllen, Texas]] as one of three brothers, during which time he joined the [[Boy Scouts of America]], where he became an Eagle Scout. He earned a degree in mechanical engineering from [[Texas A&M University]] while serving in the [[Corps of Cadets]], following which Fossum became an officer in the [[U.S. Air Force]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://today.tamu.edu/2017/05/15/fossum-86-wins-kicking-and-screaming-takes-home-250k/|title=Fossum '86 Wins 'Kicking And Screaming,' Takes Home $250k|date=May 15, 2017|website=Texas A&M Today}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2018-05-04 |title=The Bay Area Review May 4, 2018 by The Bay Area Review - Issuu |url=https://issuu.com/thebayareareview/docs/may-04-2018 |access-date=2023-06-09 |website=issuu.com |language=en}}</ref><ref name="Winch" /><ref>{{Cite web |last=Peshek |first=Sam |date=2017-05-15 |title=Fossum '86 Wins 'Kicking And Screaming,' Takes Home $250k |url=https://today.tamu.edu/2017/05/15/fossum-86-wins-kicking-and-screaming-takes-home-250k/ |access-date=2023-06-22 |website=Texas A&M Today |language=en-US}}</ref> He also became the Officer of the Year for the [[Fairchild Air Force Base]]<ref>{{Cite web |date=2018-02-09 |title=Community Leaders To Support Scouts At Annual Luncheon |url=https://www.chattanoogan.com/2018/2/9/363060/Community-Leaders-To-Support-Scouts.aspx |access-date=2023-06-22 |website=www.chattanoogan.com |language=en}}</ref> and was the Executive Officer for a Group of Nuclear B-52 Bombers.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Local Scoutmaster Terry Fossum nearing finish line on "Kicking and Screaming" reality TV show {{!}} The Spokesman-Review |url=https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2017/apr/15/local-scoutmaster-nearing-finish-line-on-kicking-a/ |access-date=2023-06-22 |website=www.spokesman.com}}</ref>

After the Air Force Fossum founded sales company and the ''Terry L. Fossum Learning Center'' in [[Rwanda]]. he also started the ''Terry L. Fossum Scholarships for Underprivileged Youth'' in McAllen, Texas; providing agricultural training for [[Malawi]], Africa in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2018-05-04 |title=The Bay Area Review May 4, 2018 by The Bay Area Review - Issuu |url=https://issuu.com/thebayareareview/docs/may-04-2018 |access-date=2023-06-09 |website=issuu.com |language=en}}</ref>

== Television ==
In 2017, Fossum appeared on Fox reality television series ''[[Kicking & Screaming (TV series)|Kicking and Screaming]]'', a show that paired survival experts with “wilderness impaired partners”.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.brownsvilleherald.com/news/valley/mcallen-native-cast-in-survivor--like-reality-show-premiering/article_ff9d8e16-047b-11e7-8f36-7b7881e43a14.html|title=McAllen native cast in 'Survivor'-like reality show premiering Thursday on Fox|website=Brownsville Herald|access-date=2019-12-31|archive-date=2019-12-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191231075404/https://www.brownsvilleherald.com/news/valley/mcallen-native-cast-in-survivor--like-reality-show-premiering/article_ff9d8e16-047b-11e7-8f36-7b7881e43a14.html|url-status=dead}}</ref> He and his partner Natalie Casanova <ref>{{Cite web |date=2017-04-15 |title=Local Scoutmaster Terry Fossum nearing finish line on "Kicking and Screaming" reality TV show |url=https://apnews.com/article/reality-tv-spokane-archive-cf981f385ae0458eaa167f0f32fd50ba |access-date=2023-06-22 |website=AP NEWS |language=en}}</ref> won the competition, splitting the $500,000 grand prize.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2017/apr/27/spokane-scoutmaster-wins-survivalist-reality-tv-sh/|title=Spokane Scoutmaster wins $250,000 grand prize on survivalist reality TV show &#124; The Spokesman-Review|website=www.spokesman.com}}</ref> This then led to several million dollars in donations for the Scouts following the series airing.<ref name="Spoke">{{Cite web |title=Spokane Valley's Terry Fossum, winner of 'Kicking and Screaming' reality show, writes best seller {{!}} The Spokesman-Review |url=https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2022/nov/13/spokane-valleys-terry-fossum-winner-of-kicking-and/?fbclid=IwAR12ShC2pSIlCqNmtQjZh_5BBxjP5wEeet2uJUQdFHSjhsOLk64vRd5_1x0 |access-date=2023-03-28 |website=www.spokesman.com}}</ref> Fossum was also the producer for the film Tiger Lily Is My Little Sister, in which he also acted, and the film Role Prey. Both films centered on the problem of human trafficking.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Entertainment Spokane! from Ed Clark |url=http://www.entertainmentspokane.com/currentIssue.asp |access-date=2023-06-22 |website=www.entertainmentspokane.com}}</ref>

In 2021, Fossum received the ''Best Supporting Actor and Fan Favorite'' award at the Christian Film Festival for his role in the movie ''Agape''.<ref name="Spoke" /> Also in 2021, Fossum published a self-help book titled ''The Oxcart Technique: Blueprint for Success''. {{Citation needed|date=June 2023}}

Fossum has continued to be involved in the Boy Scouts of America, first as a [[Scout leader|scoutmaster]], and eventually as the vice president for programs for the Washington State's Inland Northwest Council.<ref name="Winch">{{Cite web|url=https://www.winchesterstar.com/news/clarke/philanthropist-fossum-speaks-at-friends-of-scouting-dinner/article_406b6197-dcd9-561c-a6f5-ed5ef7a591c5.html|title=Philanthropist Fossum speaks at 2018 Friends of Scouting Dinner|first=JOSH JANNEY &#124; The Winchester|last=Star|website=The Winchester Star|date=26 January 2018 }}</ref> Fossum was married before but then divorced. Today he is married to a woman who has three sons, whose scoutmaster he became. {{Citation needed|date=June 2023}}
