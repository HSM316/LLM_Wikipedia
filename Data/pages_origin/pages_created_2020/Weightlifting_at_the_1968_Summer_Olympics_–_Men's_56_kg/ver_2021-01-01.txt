{{Weightlifting at the 1968 Summer Olympics}}
The '''men's 56&nbsp;kg''' weightlifting competitions at the [[1968 Summer Olympics]] in [[Mexico City]] took place on 13 October at the [[Teatro de los Insurgentes]]. It was the sixth appearance of the bantamweight class.<ref name="SR">{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/summer/1968/WLT/mens-bantamweight.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418020703/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/summer/1968/WLT/mens-bantamweight.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Weightlifting at the 1968 Mexico City Summer Games: Men's Bantamweight |work=Sports Reference |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>
