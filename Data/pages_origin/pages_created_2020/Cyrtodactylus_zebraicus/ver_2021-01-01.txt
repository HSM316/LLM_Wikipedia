'''''Cyrtodactylus zebraicus''''' is a [[species]] of [[Gekkonidae|gecko]] that is found in Thailand. It was originally [[species description|described]] as [[subspecies]] of ''[[Cyrtodactylus peguensis]]'' but appears to be more closely related to ''[[Cyrtodactylus oldhami]]'' instead.<ref>{{NRDB species |genus= Cyrtodactylus |species= zebraicus |accessdate=22 December 2019}}</ref>
