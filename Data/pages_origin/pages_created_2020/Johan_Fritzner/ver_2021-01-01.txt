'''Johan Fritzner''' (9 April 1812 - 10 December 1893) was a [[Norway|Norwegian]] priest and [[List of lexicographers|lexicographer]]. He is known for his magnum opus ''[[Ordbog over det gamle norske Sprog]]'', a major dictionary of [[Old Norse]] vocabulary.<ref>{{Citation|last=Halvorsen|first=Eyvind Fjeld|title=Johan Fritzner|date=2014-09-28|url=http://nbl.snl.no/Johan_Fritzner|work=Norsk biografisk leksikon|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190430052627/https://nbl.snl.no/Johan_Fritzner|language=no|access-date=2019-12-31|archive-date=30 April 2019}}</ref>
