'''''Tragic Ballad''''' (Italian: '''''Ballata tragica''''') is a 1954 Italian [[crime film|crime]] [[Musical film|musical]] [[melodrama film]] directed by [[Luigi Capuano]] and starring [[Teddy Reno]], [[Beniamino Maggio]] and [[Nando Bruno]].<ref>Chiti & Poppi p.54</ref>

The film's sets were designed by the [[art director]] [[Alfredo Montori]].
