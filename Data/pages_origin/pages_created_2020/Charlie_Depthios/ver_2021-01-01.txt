'''Charlie Depthios''' (2 February 1940 &ndash; 4 September 1999) was an Indonesian [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]]. He competed at the [[1968 Summer Olympics]] and the [[1972 Summer Olympics]].<ref name="SportsRef">{{cite Sports-Reference |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/de/charlie-depthios-1.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418062336/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/de/charlie-depthios-1.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=18 April 2020 |title=Charlie Depthios Olympic Results |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

His son [[Enosh Depthios]] is also an Olympic weightlifter.
