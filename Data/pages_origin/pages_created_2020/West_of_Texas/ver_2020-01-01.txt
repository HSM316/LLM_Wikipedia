'''''West of Texas''''' is a 1943 American [[Western (genre)|Western]] film written and directed by [[Oliver Drake (filmmaker)|Oliver Drake]]. The film stars [[Dave O'Brien (actor)|Dave O'Brien]], [[James Newill]], [[Guy Wilkerson]], Frances Gladwin, Henry Hall and Marilyn Hare. The film was released on May 10, 1943, by [[Producers Releasing Corporation]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/95341/West-of-Texas/ |title=West of Texas (1943) - Overview |publisher=TCM.com |date= |accessdate=2019-12-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Hans J.
