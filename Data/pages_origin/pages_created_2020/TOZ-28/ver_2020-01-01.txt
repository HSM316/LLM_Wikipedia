The '''TOZ-28''' (''ТОЗ-28'') is a Soviet hunting [[combination gun]]<ref name=autogenerated1 /><ref name=autogenerated2 /><ref name=autogenerated3>А. Соколов. Старые модели. ТОЗ-28 // журнал «Охота и охотничье хозяйство», № 12, декабрь 1991. стр.27</ref>. 

== History ==
The gun was designed by G. P. Chetverikova (''Г. П. Четверикова'') in early 1960s<ref name=autogenerated2 />. It was shown at [[VDNKh (Russia)|VDNKh exhibition]] in [[Moscow]].

Since 1963 began the production of this shotgun by [[Tula Arms Plant]]<ref name=autogenerated3 /><ref name=autogenerated2 />.

== Design ==
TOZ-28 is a combination gun, with one rifled barrel under two other<ref name=autogenerated1 /><ref name=autogenerated2 />.
* smoothbore barrels are chambered for standard 20/70 mm R shotgun shells<ref name=autogenerated3 /><ref name=autogenerated2 />.
* a rifled barrel was chambered for [[7.62×38mmR]] round<ref name=autogenerated1 /><ref name=autogenerated2 /> or special 6.5×38mmR round<ref name=autogenerated3 /><ref name=autogenerated2 />

It is equipped with [[Extractor (firearms)|extractor]]<ref name=autogenerated2 />.

All guns have a birch [[Stock (firearms)|shoulder stock]] (with or without cheekpiece) and fore-end<ref name=autogenerated1 /><ref name=autogenerated3 /><ref name=autogenerated2 />.
