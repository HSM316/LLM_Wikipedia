'''AAC Technologies Holdings, Inc.''' ({{zh|s=瑞声科技控股有限公司|t=瑞聲科技控股有限公司}}), or '''AAC Technologies''' in short form, is a [[civilian-run enterprise]] founded in 1993 and [[headquartered]] in [[Shenzhen]], [[PR China]]. It engages in the manufacture and distribution of [[miniaturized]] [[acoustics|acoustic]] components.<ref name="profile"/>

AAC Technologies designs, develops and manufactures a broad range of [[miniaturized]] components that include [[Loudspeaker|speakers]], [[receiver (radio)|receiver]]s and [[microphones]] in the acoustic segment. It produces these components for mobile devices such as [[smartphones]], [[tablet computer|tablets]], [[wearables]], [[ultrabooks]], [[Laptop|notebooks]] and [[e-readers]].<ref>[https://www.bloomberg.com/profile/company/2018:HK AAC Technologies Holdings Inc]</ref>

AAC Technologies is one of the main suppliers of [[Apple Inc.]]<ref>[https://www.ft.com/content/24230ede-3966-11e9-b72b-2c7f526ca5d0 Apple supplier AAC Technologies tumbles on profit warning]</ref>
