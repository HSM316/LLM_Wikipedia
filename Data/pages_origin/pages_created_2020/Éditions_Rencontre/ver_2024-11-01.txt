'''Éditions Rencontre''' (Meeting Editions) was a [[left wing]] [[publishing house]] in [[Francophone]] [[Switzerland]] founded in [[Lausanne]] in 1950. In 1970 they sold 6 million books globally to the Francophone world.<ref name="Martin">{{cite news |last1=Martin |first1=Isabelle |title=Les Editions Rencontre, de l'ascension à la chute |url=https://www.letemps.ch/culture/editions-rencontre-lascension-chute |issue=24 December 2004 |publisher=[[Le Temps]] |date=2004 |location=Lausanne |language=fr}}</ref>

Éditions Rencontre was founded as a [[co-operative society]] on 16 June 1950.<ref name="Vallotton">{{cite book |last1=Vallotton |first1=François |title=Les Editions Rencontre: 1950-1971 |date=2004 |publisher=Editions d'en bas |location=Lausanne |isbn=978-2-8290-0312-7 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=swUbCmpfYvoC&pg=PA16 |language=fr}}</ref> It was founded with the aim of establishing ''La revue Rencontre'', a [[youth culture]] magazine despite the founders having no experience in the publishing world. However it became one of the most important [[book sales club|book clubs]] in [[Europe]].<ref name="Vallotton"/> The six founding members were:<ref name="Vallotton"/><ref name="Catinchi">{{cite news |last1=Catinchi |first1=Philippe-Jean |title=Mort de l'écrivain suisse Yves Velan |url=https://www.lemonde.fr/disparitions/article/2017/05/12/mort-de-l-ecrivain-suisse-yves-velan_5126905_3382.html |agency=Le Monde |issue=12 May 2017 |date=2017 |language=fr}}</ref>
* [[Henri Debluë]]
* [[Yves Velan]]
* [[Jean-Pierre Schlunegger]]
* [[Michel Dentan]]
* Georges Wagen
* Jean Messner

However, they soon involved [[Pierre Balthasar de Muralt]], who soon became the principal person running the company, particularly after the review and the publishing house split.<ref name="Vallotton"/>

In 1953 they started producing the "Great Novels" series, under the direction of [[Louis Guilloux]].<ref name="Vallotton"/>

By 1968 the company employed 550 people in Lausanne.<ref name="Catinchi"/>
