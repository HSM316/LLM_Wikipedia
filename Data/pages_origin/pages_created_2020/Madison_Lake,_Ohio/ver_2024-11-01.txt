'''Madison Lake''' is an [[Unincorporated area|unincorporated community]] in [[Union Township, Madison County, Ohio|Union Township]], [[Madison County, Ohio|Madison County]], [[Ohio]], United States. It is located east of [[London, Ohio|London]] around the shore of [[Madison Lake (Ohio)|Madison Lake]], for which it was named.<ref>Rand McNally, ''The Road Atlas '06'', Chicago: Rand McNally, 2006, 78.</ref>
