'''Merlischachen railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Merlischachen}}) is a railway station in the municipality of [[Küssnacht]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It is an intermediate stop on the standard gauge [[Lucerne–Immensee railway|Lucerne–Immensee line]] of [[Swiss Federal Railways]].<ref name="saw">{{Eisenbahnatlas Schweiz | page = 22}}</ref>
