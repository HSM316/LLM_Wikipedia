'''Merlischachen railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Merlischachen}}) is a railway station in [[Küssnacht]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It is an intermediate stop on the [[Lucerne–Immensee railway]] and is served by local trains only.

== Services ==
Merlischachen is served by the S3 of the [[Lucerne S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf601">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/601.pdf | title = Luzern - Rotkreuz - Arth-Goldau - Erstfeld | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=5 November 2019 | accessdate = 31 December 2019}}</ref>

* {{rcb|Lucerne S-Bahn|3|inline=croute}}: hourly service between [[Luzern railway station|Luzern]] and [[Brunnen railway station|Brunnen]].
