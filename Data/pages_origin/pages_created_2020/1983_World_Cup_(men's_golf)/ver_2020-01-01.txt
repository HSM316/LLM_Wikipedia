The '''1983 World Cup''' took place 11–14 December 1983 at the Pondok Indah Golf Club in Jakarta, Indonesia. It was the 30th [[World Cup (men's golf)|World Cup]] event. The tournament was a 72-hole stroke play team event with 32 teams (of wich Germany withdraw from the competition). Each team consisted of two players from a country. The combined score of each team determined the team results. The United States team of [[Rex Caldwell]] and [[John Cook (golfer)|John Cook]] won by seven strokes over the Australia team of [[Terry Gale]] and [[Wayne Grady]] and Canada team of [[Dave Barr (golfer)|Dave Barr]] and [[Jerry Anderson (golfer)|Jerry Anderson]] The individual competition for The International Trophy, was won by [[Dave Barr (golfer)|Dave Barr]] three strokes ahead of [[Rex Caldwell]].
