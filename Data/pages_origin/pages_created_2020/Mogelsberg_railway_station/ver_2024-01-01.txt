'''Mogelsberg railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Mogelsberg}}) is a railway station in [[Mogelsberg]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is an intermediate station on the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]] and is served as a [[request stop]] by local trains only.
