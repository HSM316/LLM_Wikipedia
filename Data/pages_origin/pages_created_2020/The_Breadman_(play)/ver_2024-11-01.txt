'''''The Bread Man''''' is a 1990 play by the Irish playwright [[Frank McGuinness]].

== Synopsis ==
The Bread Man is set in Donegal in 1970. It follows a man, Sinner Courtney who is coming to terms with the death of his father who delivered bread.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.irishplayography.com/play.aspx?playid=30213|title=Irish Playography|last=|first=|date=|website=www.irishplayography.com|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

== Production history ==
The play premiered at the [[Gate Theatre]] on October 2, 1990. The production was directed by [[Andy Hinds]] and designed by Francis O'Connor.<ref name=":0" />

In 2019 the Alingham Arts Festival commissioned a new production of the play directed by Keith Robinson.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.allinghamfestival.com/full-programme/2019/11/6/frank-mc-guinnesss-the-bread-man-preview-performance|title='The Bread Man' by Frank Mc Guinness (Preview)|website=Allingham Festival|language=en-GB|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>
