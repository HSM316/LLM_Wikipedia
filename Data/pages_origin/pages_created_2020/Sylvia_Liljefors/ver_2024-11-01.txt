'''Sylvia Marianne Malmberg Liljefors'''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.merinfo.se/person/Stockholm/Sylvia-Marianne-Liljefors-1944/bkout-w510 |title=Sylvia Marianne Malmberg Liljefors (Stockholm, 75 år) - Merinfo.se |lang=sv |access-date=December 31, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191231083046/https://www.merinfo.se/person/Stockholm/Sylvia-Marianne-Liljefors-1944/bkout-w510 |archive-date=December 31, 2019 }}</ref> (born 9 November 1944; also known as '''Sylvia Malmberg''' and '''Sylvia Malmberg-Liljefors''') is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] [[curling|curler]].<ref>[http://www.curling.se/folja/landslagsspelaregenomtidernadamer#M Landslagsspelare genom tiderna – Damer - Svenska Curlingförbundet]{{in lang|sv}} (Swedish female national curlers; look at "Malmberg, Sylvia")</ref>

In 2005 she was inducted into the [[Swedish Curling Hall of Fame]].
