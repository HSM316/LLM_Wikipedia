The nominations for the '''19th Vancouver Film Critics Circle Awards''', honoring the best in filmmaking in [[2019 in film|2019]], were announced on December 16, 2019.<ref name=mack>Adrian Mack, [https://www.straight.com/movies/1337036/marriage-story-dominates-vancouver-film-critics-circle-noms "Marriage Story dominates Vancouver Film Critics Circle noms"]. ''[[The Georgia Straight]]'', December 13, 2019.</ref> Winners in the international categories were announced on December 16,<ref name=vlessing>Etan Vlessing, [https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/parasite-named-best-picture-by-vancouver-film-critics-circle-1262939 "'Parasite' Named Best Picture by Vancouver Film Critics Circle"]. ''[[The Hollywood Reporter]]'', December 16, 2019.</ref> and winners in the Canadian film categories were announced on January 6, 2020.<ref name=cdnwinners>[https://www.tricitynews.com/vancouver-film-critics-award-the-body-remembers-when-the-world-broke-open-1.24047775 "Vancouver film critics award 'The Body Remembers When the World Broke Open'"]. ''Tri-City News'', January 7, 2020.</ref>
