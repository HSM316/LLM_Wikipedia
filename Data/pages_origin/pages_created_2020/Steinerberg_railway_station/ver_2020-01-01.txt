'''Steinerberg railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Steinerberg}}) is a railway station in [[Steinerberg]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It is an intermediate stop on the [[Pfäffikon–Arth-Goldau railway]] and is served by local trains only.

== Services ==
Steinerberg is served by the S31 of the [[Lucerne S-Bahn]]:<ref name="fpf670">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/670.pdf | title = Rapperswil - Pfäffikon SZ - (Einsiedeln) - Arth-Goldau | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=27 November 2019 | accessdate = 31 December 2019}}</ref>

* {{rcb|Lucerne S-Bahn|31|inline=croute}}: hourly service between [[Arth-Goldau railway station|Arth-Goldau]] and [[Biberbrugg railway station|Biberbrugg]].
