'''Joseph Mkhonzana Mkhonza''' (born 2 September 1953) is a South African [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

He is the father of former South African international football player [[Siphiwe Mkhonza]]

==Career==
Mkhonza was the head coach of the [[South Africa women's national football team|South Africa women's national team]] at the [[Football at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|2012 Summer Olympics]].
