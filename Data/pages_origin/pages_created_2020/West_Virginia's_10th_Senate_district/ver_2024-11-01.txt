'''[[West Virginia]]'s 10th Senate district''' is one of 17 districts in the [[West Virginia Senate]]. It is currently represented by and [[Republican Party (United States)|Republicans]] [[Jack Woodrum]] and Vince Deeds.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.wvlegislature.gov/Senate1/lawmaker.cfm?member=Senator%20Baldwin|title=Stephen Baldwin (D - Greenbrier, 10)|publisher=West Virginia State Senate|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wvlegislature.gov/senate1/lawmaker.cfm?member=Senator%20Woodrum|title=Jack David Woodrum (R - Summers, 10)|publisher=West Virginia State Senate|access-date=May 30, 2022}}</ref> All districts in the West Virginia Senate elect two members to staggered four-year terms.

==Geography==
District 10 covers all of [[Fayette County, West Virginia|Fayette]], [[Greenbrier County, West Virginia|Greenbrier]], [[Monroe County, West Virginia|Monroe]], and [[Summers County, West Virginia|Summers Counties]] in [[Southern West Virginia]]. Communities within the district include [[Alderson, West Virginia|Alderson]], [[Hinton, West Virginia|Hinton]], [[Lewisburg, West Virginia|Lewisburg]], [[Ronceverte, West Virginia|Ronceverte]], [[White Sulphur Springs, West Virginia|White Sulphur Springs]], [[Rainelle, West Virginia|Rainelle]], [[Fairlea, West Virginia|Fairlea]], [[Montgomery, West Virginia|Montgomery]], [[Mount Hope, West Virginia|Mount Hope]], [[Oak Hill, West Virginia|Oak Hill]], [[Ansted, West Virginia|Ansted]], and [[Fayetteville, West Virginia|Fayetteville]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.wvlegislature.gov/Districts/maps.cfm|title=Senate District Maps - 2010 Plan|publisher=West Virginia Legislature|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref>

The district is located entirely within [[West Virginia's 3rd congressional district]], and overlaps with the 28th, 32nd, 41st, and 42nd districts of the [[West Virginia House of Delegates]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailykos.com/stories/2019/7/30/1848730/-How-do-counties-House-districts-and-legislative-districts-all-overlap-These-new-tools-show-you|author=David Jarman|title=How do counties, House districts, and legislative districts all overlap?|publisher=Daily Kos|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref> It borders the state of [[Virginia]].<ref name="Census"/>
