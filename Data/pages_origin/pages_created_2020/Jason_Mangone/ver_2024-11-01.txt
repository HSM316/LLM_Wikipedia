'''Jason Mangone''' is an American [[college football]] coach. He is the head football coach for [[SUNY Brockport]], a position he has held since 2013. Mangone played [[college football]] as a [[quarterback]], first at [[Alfred University]] in [[Alfred (village), New York|Alfred, New York]], before transferring to Brockport. He became an assistant coach at Brockport in 1999 and was promoted to [[offensive coordinator]] in 2004.<ref>{{cite news |last=Perez |first=A.J. |title=Jason Mangone has helped build The College at Brockport up half his life |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/ncaaf/2017/12/28/jason-mangone-afca-division-iii-coach-year/989256001/ |newspaper=[[USA Today]] |date=December 28, 2017  |access-date=December 31, 2019 }}</ref>

Mangone is a native of [[Lancaster, New York]] and now resides in [[Henrietta, New York]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://gobrockport.com/sports/football/roster/coaches/jason-mangone/1004|title = Jason Mangone - Football Coach}}</ref>
