'''Chalpi''' is a village in [[Quijos]] canton, [[Napo Province|Napo]] Province in [[Ecuador]].

==Climate==
Chalpi has a [[Subtropical highland climate]] (Cfb) with cool weather year-round and moderately heavy rainfall.
