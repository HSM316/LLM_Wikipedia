'''Slavica Nakov-Dimovska''' (born 29 July 1985) is a Macedonian [[basketball]] player and a former member of the [[North Macedonia women's national basketball team|Macedonian national basketball team]]. She won the [[Úrvalsdeild kvenna (basketball)|Icelandic championship]] with [[Haukar women's basketball|Haukar]] in 2009 when she was named the [[Úrvalsdeild Women's Foreign Player of the Year|Úrvalsdeild Foreign Player of the Year]] and the [[Úrvalsdeild Women's Playoffs MVP|Úrvalsdeild Playoffs MVP]]. In 2010, she was named the Macedonian Women's Basketball Player of the Year.<ref>{{cite news |author1=Óskar Ófeigur Jónsson |title=Slavica valin körfuboltakona ársins í Makedóníu |url=https://www.visir.is/g/2011911670091 |accessdate=1 January 2020 |work=[[Vísir.is]] |date=23 January 2011 |language=Icelandic}}</ref>

==Career==
From 2005 to 2007, Dimovska played for Triglias in [[Greece]]. During the 2006–2007 season, she finished 5th in scoring in the league with 22.6 points per game.<ref name="frettabladid-2007-09-18"/>

In 2007, Dimovska joined [[Úrvalsdeild kvenna (basketball)|Úrvalsdeild kvenna]] club [[Fjölnir women's basketball|Fjölnir]].<ref name="frettabladid-2007-09-18">{{cite news |title=Makedónísk landsliðskona til Fjölnis |url=https://timarit.is/page/3973661 |accessdate=1 January 2020 |work=[[Fréttablaðið]] |date=18 September 2007 |page=42 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> For the season she averaged 24.1 points, 7.3 rebounds and 4.3 assists.<ref>{{cite web |title=Iceland Express-deild kvenna. - Fjölnir |url=http://gamli.kki.is/mot/1500002685_FJOLNIR_____.htm |website=kki.is |publisher=[[Icelandic Basketball Association]] |accessdate=1 January 2020 |language=Icelandic}}</ref>

Following the season, Dimovska signed with rival Úrvalsdeild club [[Haukar women's basketball|Haukar]]. On 19 November 2008, she scored a season high 38 points, including the game winning three pointer at the buzzer, against [[Hamar women's basketball|Hamar]].<ref>{{cite news |author1=Skúli Unnar Sveinsson |title=Slavica tryggði Haukum efsta sætið |url=https://timarit.is/page/4204431 |accessdate=1 January 2020 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=20 November 2008 |page=C2 |language=Icelandic}}</ref> On 11 January 2009, she scored 37 points in an unexpected 65-93 loss against [[KR women's basketball|KR]] in the [[Icelandic Women's Basketball Cup|Icelandic Cup]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Ótrúlega stór sigur KR á Haukum |url=https://timarit.is/page/5245383 |accessdate=1 January 2020 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=12 January 2009 |page=B3 |language=Icelandic}}</ref>

Dimovska spent the 2009-10 season with [[ŽKK Vardar Mladinec|Vardar Mladinec]] where she led the team to the [[First Women's Basketball League of Macedonia|Macedonian championship]].<ref name="frettabladid-2010-08-19">{{cite news |title=Á leið til Hamars |url=https://timarit.is/page/5082537 |accessdate=1 January 2020 |work=[[Fréttablaðið]] |date=19 August 2010 |page=44 |language=Icelandic}}</ref>

In 2010, she returned to Iceland and signed with [[Hamar women's basketball|Hamar]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Dimovska fer í Hamar |url=https://timarit.is/page/5336285 |accessdate=1 January 2020 |work=[[Morgunblaðið]] |date=21 August 2010 |page=C3 |language=Icelandic}}</ref>

In 2012, she won the Macedonian championship and Macedonian Cup with Vigor Skopje.<ref>{{cite news |title=Славица Наков: Мојот живот во моментов е 300 на саат |url=http://majkaidete.mk/%D1%81%D0%BB%D0%B0%D0%B2%D0%B8%D1%86%D0%B0-%D0%BD%D0%B0%D0%BA%D0%BE%D0%B2-%D0%BC%D0%BE%D1%98%D0%BE%D1%82-%D0%B6%D0%B8%D0%B2%D0%BE%D1%82-%D0%B2%D0%BE-%D0%BC%D0%BE%D0%BC%D0%B5%D0%BD%D1%82%D0%BE%D0%B2/ |accessdate=1 January 2020 |work=majkaidete.mk |date=13 December 2018 |language=Macedonian}}</ref>

In 2019, she led [[ŽKK Vardar Mladinec|Vardar Mladinec]] to the Macedonian championship and the Macedonian Cup. For her efforts in the playoffs, she was named the Playoffs MVP.<ref>{{cite news |title=НАЈГОЛЕМИОТ ИГРАЧ ПОСЛЕДНИВЕ ДВЕ ДЕЦЕНИИ: Со една реченица, кажа се! |url=http://basketball.mk/%d0%bd%d0%b0%d1%98%d0%b3%d0%be%d0%bb%d0%b5%d0%bc%d0%b8%d0%be%d1%82-%d0%b8%d0%b3%d1%80%d0%b0%d1%87-%d0%bf%d0%be%d1%81%d0%bb%d0%b5%d0%b4%d0%bd%d0%b8%d0%b2%d0%b5-%d0%b4%d0%b2%d0%b5-%d0%b4%d0%b5%d1%86/ |accessdate=2 January 2020 |work=basketball.mk |date=30 May 2019 |language=Macedonian}}</ref>
