{{Nihongo|'''''Bakumatsu'''''|幕末|Bakumatsu|lead=yes}} is a 1970 Japanese jidaigeki film directed by [[Daisuke Itō (film director)|Daisuke Itō]]. It is based on [[Ryōtarō Shiba]]'s novel of the same title.<ref>{{cite web|URL=http://db.eiren.org/contents/02019700008.html|title=幕末|publisher=映倫|accessdate=19 March 2017}}</ref>

The film depicts chronicles the life of [[Sakamoto Ryōma]] and people around him.<ref>{{cite web|URL=https://www.japanese-cinema-db.jp/Details?id=11267|title=幕末|publisher=日本映画情報|accessdate=19 March 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|URL=https://eiga.com/movie/38710/ |title=幕末 |publisher=映画|accessdate=19 March 2017}}</ref>
