'''Shahidul Amin''' ({{circa|1936}} – 6 August 2017) was a [[Bangladesh]]i director, playwright, actor and screenplay writer.

==Biography==
Amin directed ''Ramer Sumoti'' was released in 1985. He was the screenplay writer of that film too. This film won [[10th Bangladesh National Film Awards|National Film Award]] in two categories.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fdc.gov.bd/site/page/f3bb17fe-d9ad-4b61-bd8a-5824262691ad/-

|accessdate=6 August 2019|website=Bangladesh Film Development Corporation|language=bn|format=PDF}}</ref> This film was the first film of [[Sadek Bachchu]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/online/entertainment/2018/02/22/605356
