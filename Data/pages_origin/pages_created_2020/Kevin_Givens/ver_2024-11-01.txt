'''Kevin Givens''' (born March 1, 1997) is an American professional [[American football|football]] [[defensive tackle]] for the [[San Francisco 49ers]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). He played [[college football]] at [[Penn State Nittany Lions football|Penn State]].

==Early life==
Givens was born in [[Newark, New Jersey]] and grew up in [[Altoona, Pennsylvania]] and attended [[Altoona Area High School]], where he played [[defensive end]] and [[Fullback (gridiron football)|fullback]] on the football team.<ref name="molds" /> As a senior he was named first-team All-State on defense and rushed for 317 yards and six [[touchdowns]] on offense.<ref>{{Cite web |date=August 2, 2018 |title=Only Kevin Givens Until Penn State Football |url=https://www.blackshoediaries.com/2018/8/2/17641378/only-kevin-givens-until-penn-state-football-30-days-until-2018-nittany-lions-season-defensive-tackle |access-date=May 3, 2020 |website=BlackShoeDiaries.com |publisher=[[SB Nation]]}}</ref>

==College career==
Givens was a member of the [[Penn State Nittany Lions football|Penn State Nittany Lions]] for five seasons, [[redshirt (college sports)|redshirting]] his true freshman season. He was named the [[Big Ten Conference]] All-Freshman team after making 26 tackles, seven tackles for loss and 4.5 sacks in his first season.<ref name="molds">{{Cite web |last=McGonigal |first=John |date=May 27, 2017 |title=Altoona support molds Penn State DT Kevin Givens into complete student-athlete |url=https://www.centredaily.com/sports/college/penn-state-university/psu-football/article153062199.html |access-date=April 1, 2020 |website=[[Centre Daily Times]]}}</ref> As a redshirt sophomore, Givens recorded 23 tackles with four tackles for loss, 3.5 sacks, a forced fumble and a fumble recovery.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Rudel |first=Neil |date=November 18, 2018 |title=Quietly, Givens doing his share on defense |url=https://www.altoonamirror.com/sports/psu/2018/11/quietly-givens-doing-his-share-on-defense/ |access-date=April 1, 2020 |website=[[Altoona Mirror]]}}</ref> Givens finished his redshirt junior season with 33 tackles, 10.5 tackles for loss and five sacks. After the season Givens, who had already completed his undergraduate degree, announced that he would forgo his final season in order to enter the [[2019 NFL draft]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Slanina |first=Jared |date=April 24, 2019 |title=So You Drafted Penn State DT Kevin Givens... |url=https://www.blackshoediaries.com/2019/4/24/18511140/penn-state-nittany-lions-football-kevin-givens-2019-nfl-draft-profile-defensive-tackle |access-date=April 1, 2020 |website=BlackShoeDiaries.com |publisher=[[SB Nation]]}}</ref>

Givens signed with the [[San Francisco 49ers]] as an [[undrafted free agent]] on April 28, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Weider |first=Collin |date=April 28, 2019 |title=Penn State Football: Kevin Givens signs as undrafted free agent |url=https://victorybellrings.com/2019/04/28/penn-state-football-kevin-givens-signs-undrafted-free-agent/ |access-date=December 31, 2019 |website=VictoryBellRings.com |publisher=[[FanSided]]}}</ref> He was waived at the end of [[training camp (NFL)|training camp]] during final roster cuts, but was re-signed to the team's [[practice squad]] on September 1, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Rudel |first=Neil |date=September 1, 2019 |title=Givens lands on practice squad |url=https://www.altoonamirror.com/sports/local-sports/2019/09/givens-lands-on-practice-squad/ |access-date=December 31, 2019 |website=Altoona Mirror}}</ref> The 49ers promoted Givens to the active roster on December 28.<ref>{{Cite web |date=December 28, 2019 |title=49ers promote defensive lineman to replace Jullian Taylor |url=http://www.knbr.com/2019/12/28/49ers-promote-defensive-lineman-to-replace-jullian-taylor/ |access-date=December 31, 2019 |website=KNBR.com}}</ref> Givens reached [[Super Bowl LIV]] with the 49ers, who were defeated by the [[Kansas City Chiefs]] by a score of 31–20.

In Week 7 against the [[New England Patriots]] in 2020, Givens recorded his first career sack on [[Jarrett Stidham]] late in the fourth quarter during the 33–6 win.<ref>{{Cite web |title=San Francisco 49ers at New England Patriots – October 25th, 2020 |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/boxscores/202010250nwe.htm |access-date=October 29, 2020 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com}}</ref> Givens was placed on a roster exemption on December 12, 2020,<ref>{{Cite news |date=December 12, 2020 |title=49ers Announce Roster Moves |work=49ers.com |url=https://www.49ers.com/news/49ers-announce-roster-moves-2020-week-14 |access-date=January 27, 2021}}</ref> and was reinstated to the active roster five days later.<ref>{{Cite news |date=December 17, 2020 |title=49ers' Kevin Givens: Has exemption lifted |work=CBSSports.com |url=https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/football/news/49ers-kevin-givens-has-exemption-lifted/ |access-date=January 27, 2021}}</ref>

On September 25, 2021, Givens was placed on [[injured reserve]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=September 25, 2021 |title=49ers Announce Roster Moves Ahead of #GBvsSF |url=https://www.49ers.com/news/49ers-packers-roster-moves-kerryon-johnson-running-back-hasty-injured-reserve |website=49ers.com}}</ref> He was activated on October 30.<ref>{{Cite web |date=October 30, 2021 |title=Two 49ers Downgraded to OUT vs. Bears; Other Roster Moves |url=https://www.49ers.com/news/two-49ers-downgraded-to-out-vs-bears-other-roster-moves |website=49ers.com}}</ref>

On March 10, 2022, Givens re-signed with the 49ers.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Alper |first=Josh |date=March 10, 2022 |title=49ers re-sign JaMycal Hasty, Kevin Givens and three others |url=https://profootballtalk.nbcsports.com/2022/03/10/49ers-re-sign-jamycal-hasty-kevin-givens-and-three-others/ |access-date=February 2, 2023 |website=ProFootballTalk}}</ref>

Over the course of the 2022 and 2023 seasons, Givens appeared in 30 regular season games, starting 12 of them. During that period, he appeared in six playoff games, including a Super Bowl appearance.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Kevin Givens 2022 Game Log |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/G/GiveKe01/gamelog/2022/ |access-date=February 25, 2024 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Kevin Givens 2023 Game Log |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/G/GiveKe01/gamelog/2023/ |access-date=February 25, 2024 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Super Bowl LVIII – San Francisco 49ers vs. Kansas City Chiefs – February 11th, 2024 |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/boxscores/202402110kan.htm |access-date=February 25, 2024 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com}}</ref>

On March 14, 2024, Givens re-signed with the 49ers.<ref>{{cite web|title=49ers Acquire DL in Trade with Texans; Sign Three Free Agents|url=https://www.49ers.com/news/nfl-free-agency-collins-elliott-gross-matos-givens-texans-defensive-line|website=49ers.com|date=March 14, 2024|access-date=March 14, 2024}}</ref>
