'''Kevin Givens''' (born March 1, 1997) is an [[American football]] [[defensive tackle]] for the [[San Francisco 49ers]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). He played [[college football]] at [[Penn State Nittany Lions football|Penn State]].

==Professional career==
Givens signed with the [[San Francisco 49ers]] as an [[undrafted free agent]] on April 28, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://victorybellrings.com/2019/04/28/penn-state-football-kevin-givens-signs-undrafted-free-agent/|title=Penn State Football: Kevin Givens signs as undrafted free agent|first=Collin|last=Weider|website=VictoryBellRings.com|publisher=[[FanSided]]|date=April 28, 2019|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref> He was waived at the end of training camp during final roster cuts, but was re-signed to the team's practice squad on September 1, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.altoonamirror.com/sports/local-sports/2019/09/givens-lands-on-practice-squad/|title=Givens lands on practice squad|first=Neil|last=Rudel|work=Altoona Mirror|date=September 1, 2019|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref> The 49ers promoted Givens to the active roster on December 28.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.knbr.com/2019/12/28/49ers-promote-defensive-lineman-to-replace-jullian-taylor/|title=49ers promote defensive lineman to replace Jullian Taylor|website=KNBR.com|date=December 28, 2019|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref>
