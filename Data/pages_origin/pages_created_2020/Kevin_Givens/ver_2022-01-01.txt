'''Kevin Givens''' (born March 1, 1997) is an [[American football]] [[defensive tackle]] for the [[San Francisco 49ers]] of the [[National Football League]] (NFL). He played [[college football]] at [[Penn State Nittany Lions football|Penn State]].

==Early life and high school==
Givens was born in [[Newark, New Jersey]] and grew up in [[Altoona, Pennsylvania]] and attended [[Altoona Area High School]], where he played defensive end and fullback on the football team.<ref name="molds"/> As a senior he was named first team All-State on defense and rushed for 317 yards and six touchdowns on offense.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blackshoediaries.com/2018/8/2/17641378/only-kevin-givens-until-penn-state-football-30-days-until-2018-nittany-lions-season-defensive-tackle|title=Only Kevin Givens Until Penn State Football|website=BlackShoeDiaries.com|publisher=[[SB Nation]]|date=August 2, 2018|access-date=May 3, 2020}}</ref>

==College career==
Givens was a member of the [[Penn State Nittany Lions football|Penn State Nittany Lions]] for five seasons, [[redshirt (college sports)|redshirting]] his true freshman season. He was named the [[Big Ten Conference]] All-Freshman team after making 26 tackles, seven tackles for loss and 4.5 sacks in his first season.<ref name="molds">{{cite web|url=https://www.centredaily.com/sports/college/penn-state-university/psu-football/article153062199.html|title=Altoona support molds Penn State DT Kevin Givens into complete student-athlete|first=John|last=McGonigal|work=[[Centre Daily Times]]|date=May 27, 2017|access-date=April 1, 2020}}</ref> As a redshirt sophomore, Givens recorded 23 tackles with four tackles for loss, 3.5 sacks, a forced fumble and a fumble recovery.<ref>{{cite web|last=Rudel|first=Neil|date=November 18, 2018|title=Quietly, Givens doing his share on defense|url=https://www.altoonamirror.com/sports/psu/2018/11/quietly-givens-doing-his-share-on-defense/|url-status=live|access-date=April 1, 2020|work=[[Altoona Mirror]]}}</ref> Givens finished his redshirt junior season with 33 tackles, 10.5 tackles for loss and five sacks. After the season Givens, who had already completed his undergraduate degree, announced that he would forgo his final season in order to enter the [[2019 NFL Draft]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blackshoediaries.com/2019/4/24/18511140/penn-state-nittany-lions-football-kevin-givens-2019-nfl-draft-profile-defensive-tackle|title=So You Drafted Penn State DT Kevin Givens...|first=Jared|last=Slanina|website=BlackShoeDiaries.com|publisher=[[SB Nation]]|date=April 24, 2019|access-date=April 1, 2020}}</ref>

==Professional career==
Givens signed with the [[San Francisco 49ers]] as an [[undrafted free agent]] on April 28, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://victorybellrings.com/2019/04/28/penn-state-football-kevin-givens-signs-undrafted-free-agent/|title=Penn State Football: Kevin Givens signs as undrafted free agent|first=Collin|last=Weider|website=VictoryBellRings.com|publisher=[[FanSided]]|date=April 28, 2019|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref> He was waived at the end of training camp during final roster cuts, but was re-signed to the team's practice squad on September 1, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.altoonamirror.com/sports/local-sports/2019/09/givens-lands-on-practice-squad/|title=Givens lands on practice squad|first=Neil|last=Rudel|work=Altoona Mirror|date=September 1, 2019|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref> The 49ers promoted Givens to the active roster on December 28.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.knbr.com/2019/12/28/49ers-promote-defensive-lineman-to-replace-jullian-taylor/|title=49ers promote defensive lineman to replace Jullian Taylor|website=KNBR.com|date=December 28, 2019|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref> Givens reached [[Super Bowl LIV]] with the 49ers, but were defeated by the [[2019 Kansas City Chiefs season|Kansas City Chiefs]] by a score of 31–20.

In Week 7 against the [[2020 New England Patriots season|New England Patriots]] in 2020, Givens recorded his first career sack on [[Jarrett Stidham]] late in the fourth quarter during the 33–6 win.<ref>{{Cite web |title=San Francisco 49ers at New England Patriots – October 25th, 2020 |url=https://www.pro-football-reference.com/boxscores/202010250nwe.htm |access-date=October 29, 2020 |website=Pro-Football-Reference.com |language=en}}</ref> Givens was placed on a roster exemption on December 12, 2020,<ref>{{cite news |title=49ers Announce Roster Moves |url=https://www.49ers.com/news/49ers-announce-roster-moves-2020-week-14 |website=49ers.com |date=December 12, 2020 |access-date=January 27, 2021}}</ref> and was reinstated to the active roster five days later.<ref>{{cite news |title=49ers' Kevin Givens: Has exemption lifted |url=https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/football/news/49ers-kevin-givens-has-exemption-lifted/ |website=CBSSports.com |date=December 17, 2020 |access-date=January 27, 2021}}</ref>

On September 25, 2021, Givens was placed on injured reserve.<ref>{{cite web|title=49ers Announce Roster Moves Ahead of #GBvsSF|url=https://www.49ers.com/news/49ers-packers-roster-moves-kerryon-johnson-running-back-hasty-injured-reserve|website=49ers.com|date=September 25, 2021}}</ref> He was activated on October 30.<ref>{{cite web|title=Two 49ers Downgraded to OUT vs. Bears; Other Roster Moves|url=https://www.49ers.com/news/two-49ers-downgraded-to-out-vs-bears-other-roster-moves|website=49ers.com|date=October 30, 2021}}</ref>
