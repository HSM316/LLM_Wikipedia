The '''1981–82 West Midlands (Regional) League season''' was the 82nd in the history of the [[West Midlands (Regional) League]], an English [[association football]] competition for semi-professional and amateur teams based in the [[West Midlands (county)|West Midlands county]], [[Shropshire]], [[Herefordshire]], [[Worcestershire]] and southern [[Staffordshire]].
