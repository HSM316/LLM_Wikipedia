'''Silvery Moon''' is a song by Australian rock band [[Sherbet (band)|Sherbet]], released in August 1974 as the third and final single from the band's third studio album, ''[[Slipstream (Sherbet album)|Slipstream]]''. The song reached number 5 on the [[Kent Music Report]]<ref>[http://www.australianmusicdatabase.com/recordings/silvery-moon-by-sherbet Australian Music Database - Sherbet - Silvery Moon]</ref>

The song was written by [[Garth Porter]] and [[Clive Shakespeare]].<ref>[https://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Sherbet&titel=Slipstream&cat=s Australian Charts - Sherbet - Silvery Moon]</ref>
