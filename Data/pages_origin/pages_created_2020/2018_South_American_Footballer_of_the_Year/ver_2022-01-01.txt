[[File:Emelec 0 River 1 (Gonzalo Nicolás Martínez).jpg|thumb|[[Pity Martínez]], 2018 South American Footballer of the Year]]
The '''2018 [[South American Footballer of the Year]]''' award (Spanish: '''''Rey del Fútbol de América'''''), given to the best football player in South America by Uruguayan newspaper [[El País (Uruguay)|El País]] through voting by journalists across the continent, was awarded to Argentine midfielder [[Pity Martínez|Gonzalo "Pity" Martínez]] of [[Club Atlético River Plate|River Plate]] on December 31, 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.infocancha.com/contenido/noticia/2/11978/sensacional-temporada-con-river-convirtio-a-pity-martinez-en-el-rey-de-america.html|title=SENSACIONAL TEMPORADA CON RIVER CONVIRTIÓ A "PITY" MARTÍNEZ EN EL 'REY DE AMÉRICA' {{!}} INFOCancha Información Deportiva|website=www.infocancha.com|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

The award is part of the paper's "El Mejor de América" (''The Best of America'') awards, which also presents the awards for South American Coach of the Year (''Entrenador del año en Sudamérica'') and the Best XI (''Equipo Ideal''), composed of the best eleven players at their positions.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lostiempos.com/deportes/entretiempo/20181231/escobar-villegas-son-elegidos-mejores-del-futbol-boliviano|title=Escobar y Villegas son elegidos los mejores del fútbol boliviano|date=2018-12-31|website=Los Tiempos|language=es|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> [[Marcelo Gallardo]] of [[Club Atlético River Plate|River Plate]] was named [[South American Coach of the Year|Coach of the Year]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://globoesporte.globo.com/futebol/futebol-internacional/noticia/pity-martinez-e-eleito-o-rei-da-america-em-tradicional-eleicao-de-jornal-uruguaio.ghtml|title=Pity Martínez é eleito o Rei da América; Brasil tem dois representantes da seleção de 2018|website=Globoesporte|language=pt-br|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>
