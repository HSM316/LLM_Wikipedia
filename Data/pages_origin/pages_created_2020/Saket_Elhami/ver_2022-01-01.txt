'''Saket Elhami''' ({{lang-fa|ساکت الهامی}}) is an [[Iran]]ian [[football (soccer)|football]] manager who currently manages [[Nassaji Mazandaran|Nassaji]] in [[Persian Gulf Pro League]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.tasnimnews.com/fa/news/1398/10/08/2169928 |title=ابقای الهامی در تراکتور قطعی شد/ ادامه کار سهرابی به‌عنوان سرپرست}}</ref>

He played as a player for [[Pas F.C.|Pas]], [[Esteghlal Ahvaz F.C.|Esteghlal Ahvaz]], and [[Tractor S.C.|Tractor]]. He also was [[Firouz Karimi]]'s assistant in [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] between 2007 and 2008 years.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.farsnews.com/printnews/8609110370 |title=ساكت الهامي دستيار فيروز كريمي مي‌شود |date=2007-12-02 |publisher=[[Fars News Agency]] |trans-title=Saket Elhami becomes Firouz Karimi's assistant |access-date=2020-01-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.varzesh3.com/news/1660341 |title=ساکت الهامی رسما سرمربی تراکتور شد}}</ref>
