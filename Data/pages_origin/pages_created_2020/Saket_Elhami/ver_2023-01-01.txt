'''Saket Elhami''' ({{lang-fa|ساکت الهامی}}) is an Iranian [[football (soccer)|football]] manager who currently manages [[Havadar S.C.|Havadar ]] in [[Persian Gulf Pro League]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.tasnimnews.com/fa/news/1398/10/08/2169928 |title=ابقای الهامی در تراکتور قطعی شد/ ادامه کار سهرابی به‌عنوان سرپرست}}</ref>

He played as a player for [[Pas F.C.|Pas]], [[Esteghlal Ahvaz F.C.|Esteghlal Ahvaz]], and [[Tractor S.C.|Tractor]].
