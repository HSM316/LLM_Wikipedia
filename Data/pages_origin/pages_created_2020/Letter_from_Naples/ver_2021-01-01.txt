'''''Letter from Naples''''' (Italian: '''''Lettera napoletana''''') is a 1954 Italian [[musical film|musical]] [[melodrama film]] directed by [[Giorgio Pastina]] and starring [[Giacomo Rondinella]], [[Virna Lisi]] and [[Otello Toso]].<ref>Chiti & Poppi p.207</ref>

The film's sets were designed by the [[art director]] [[Alfredo Montori]]. It was shot [[location shooting|on location]] in [[Naples]].
