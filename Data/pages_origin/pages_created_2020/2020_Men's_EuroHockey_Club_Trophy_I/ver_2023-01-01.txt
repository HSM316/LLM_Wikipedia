The '''2020 Men's EuroHockey Club Trophy I''' would have been the 44th edition of the [[EuroHockey Club Trophy I]], Europe's secondary men's club [[field hockey]] tournament organized by the [[European Hockey Federation]] and the first edition since it was renamed from the EuroHockey Club Trophy.<ref>{{cite web |title=2018 EHF Announces Changes to EuroHockey Club Events |url=https://eurohockey.org/2018-ehf-announces-changes-to-eurohockey-club-events/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=2 November 2018 |archive-date=31 December 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191231120647/https://eurohockey.org/2018-ehf-announces-changes-to-eurohockey-club-events/ |url-status=dead }}</ref> It would be held in [[Vienna]], Austria from 29 May to 1 June 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=2020 EuroHockey Club Championships Venues and Teams Announced- Updated |url=https://eurohockey.org/2020-eurohockey-club-championships-venues-and-teams-announced/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=29 October 2019 |archive-date=19 October 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191019105557/https://eurohockey.org/2020-eurohockey-club-championships-venues-and-teams-announced/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>

The tournament was canceled on 23 March 2020 due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{cite web |title=EHF cancels all EuroHockey Club Trophy and Challenge events, 2020 |url=https://eurohockey.org/2020/03/24/ehf-cancels-all-eurohockey-club-trophy-and-challenge-events-2020/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=25 March 2020 |date=24 March 2020}}</ref>
