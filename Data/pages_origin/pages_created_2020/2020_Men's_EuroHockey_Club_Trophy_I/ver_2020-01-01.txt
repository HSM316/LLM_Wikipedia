The '''2020 Men's EuroHockey Club Trophy I''' will be the first edition of the [[EuroHockey Club Trophy I]], Europe's secondary men's club [[field hockey]] tournament organized by the [[European Hockey Federation]].<ref>{{cite web |title=2018 EHF Announces Changes to EuroHockey Club Events |url=https://eurohockey.org/2018-ehf-announces-changes-to-eurohockey-club-events/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=2 November 2018}}</ref> It will be held in [[Vienna]], Austria from 29 May to 1 June 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=2020 EuroHockey Club Championships Venues and Teams Announced- Updated |url=https://eurohockey.org/2020-eurohockey-club-championships-venues-and-teams-announced/ |website=eurohockey.org |publisher=[[European Hockey Federation]] |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=29 October 2019}}</ref>
