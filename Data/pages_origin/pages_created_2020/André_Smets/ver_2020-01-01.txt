'''André Smets''' (5 July 1943 – 30 December 2019) was a Belgian politician, who served as Mayor of [[Herve]] from 1985 to 2010.

Smets professionally became a teacher and school director.

In 1970 he was elected to Herve as a municipal councillor for the then PSC and from 1977 to 1985 there were ships. In 1985 he became mayor of the municipality and remained until the end of 2010, when he left politics.

He also served from 1999 to 2003 in the Chamber of Representatives for the Verviers constituency. He mainly focused on traffic, infrastructure and government companies.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sudinfo.be/id158976/article/2019-12-30/andre-smets-ex-bourgmestre-de-herve-est-decede-durant-la-nuit|title=André Smets, ex-bourgmestre de Herve, est décédé durant la nuit|language=fr|date=2019-12-30|website=sudinfo.be}}</ref>
