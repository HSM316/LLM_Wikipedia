The [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']] [[Latin Pop Airplay]] is a chart that ranks the best-performing Spanish-language [[Latin pop|Pop music]] [[single (music)|singles]] of the United States. Published by ''Billboard'' magazine, the data are compiled by [[Nielsen SoundScan]] based collectively on each single's weekly [[airplay]].
