'''Praveen Pardeshi''' is an [[Indian Administrative Service]] (IAS) officer, currently serving as the [[Municipal Commissioner of Mumbai]]. He succeeded [[Ajoy Mehta]] and took office in May 2019. Prior to that he served as Additional Chief Secretary to then Chief Minister [[Devendra Fadnavis]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Ramaswamy |first1=Radhika |title=Praveen Pardeshi Replaces Ajoy Mehta as Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation Chief |url=https://www.news18.com/news/india/praveen-pardeshi-to-replace-ajoy-mehta-as-brihanmumbai-municipal-corporation-chief-2136467.html |accessdate=31 December 2019 |work=News18 |date=11 May 2019}}</ref>
