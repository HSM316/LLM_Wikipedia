==Biography==
Ece Çeşmioğlu was born on November 26, 1990 in Istanbul and is from Istanbul.  She graduated from the Theater Department of Mimar Sinan University State Conservatory, which is her childhood dream.  Ece Çeşmioğlu then drew attention with his performances in the series ar Trailing ”, er A Time That Will Pass”, “Summer Story”, “Kalbim Fire Place” and “Magnificent Century Kösem”.
