'''Ece Çeşmioğlu Ölmez''' (born 26 November 1990) is a Turkish actress.

==Biography==
Ece Çeşmioğlu Ölmez was born on 26 November 1990 in Istanbul. She graduated from the Theater Department of [[Mimar Sinan University]] State Conservatory. She started her acting career at the age of 12 in Çocuklar Duymasın.<ref name=sozcu>{{cite web |title= Ece Çeşmioğlu kimdir? Ece Çeşmioğlu nereli ve kaç yaşında |url=https://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/magazin-haberleri/ece-çeşmioğlu-kimdir-oub4/|website= sozcu.com.tr |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> Ece then drew attention with her performances in various TV series. In 2017, she played the character of Zeynep in the series ''Yüz Yüze''.<ref name=sozcu/><ref name=ahaber>{{cite web |title= Ece Çeşmioğlu kimdir? Taner Ölmez sevgilisi Ece Çeşmioğlu kimdir, kaç yaşında?. |url=https://www.ahaber.com.tr|website= ahaber.com.tr |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> (English: Face to Face).
