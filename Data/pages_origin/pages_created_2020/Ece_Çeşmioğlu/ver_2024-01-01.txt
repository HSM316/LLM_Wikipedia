'''Ece Çeşmioğlu Ölmez''' (born 26 November 1990) is a Turkish actress. She is best known for hit family comedy series ''İki Aile'', period series ''Öyle Bir Geçer Zaman Ki'', ''[[Muhteşem Yüzyıl: Kösem]]'' and military series ''Söz''.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/gundem/ece-cesmioglu-kimdir-nereli-kac-yasinda-41841838 | title=Ece Çeşmioğlu kimdir nereli kaç yaşında? Ece Çeşmioğlu hangi dizide oynadı? }}</ref>

==Biography==
Ece Çeşmioğlu Ölmez was born on 26 November 1990 in Istanbul. Her family is from [[Istanbul]], [[Turkey]] and from [[Yugoslavia]]. She graduated from the Theater Department of [[Mimar Sinan University]] State Conservatory. She started her acting career at the age of 12 in hit comedy family series ''Çocuklar Duymasın''.<ref name=sozcu>{{cite web |title= Ece Çeşmioğlu kimdir? Ece Çeşmioğlu nereli ve kaç yaşında |url=https://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/magazin-haberleri/ece-çeşmioğlu-kimdir-oub4/|website= sozcu.com.tr |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> Ece then drew attention with her performances in various TV series. With her husband [[Taner Ölmez]], she played the character of Zeynep in the series ''Yüz Yüze'' and as Atike Sultan in ''[[Muhteşem Yüzyıl: Kösem]]''.<ref name=sozcu/><ref name=ahaber>{{cite web |title= Ece Çeşmioğlu kimdir? Taner Ölmez sevgilisi Ece Çeşmioğlu kimdir, kaç yaşında?. |url=https://www.ahaber.com.tr|website= ahaber.com.tr |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> (English: Face to Face).
