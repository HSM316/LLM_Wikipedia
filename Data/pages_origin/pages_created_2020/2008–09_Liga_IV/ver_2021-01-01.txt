The '''2008–09 Liga IV''' was the 67th season of the [[Liga IV]], the fourth tier of the [[Romanian football league system]]. The champions of each county association play against one from a neighboring county in a play-off match played on a neutral venue. The winners of the play-off matches promoted to [[Liga III]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://liga2.prosport.ro/special/21-de-echipe-au-promovat-in-liga-3-4565909|date=June 17, 2009|title=21 de echipe au promovat în Liga 3|website=liga2.prosport.ro}}{{in lang|ro}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://adevarul.ro/locale/ramnicu-valcea/ramnicu-valcea-ghecon-lapusata-promovat-liga-iii-a-1_50bd53b47c42d5a663ca6909/index.html|date=June 18, 2009|title=Râmnicu Vâlcea: Ghecon Lăpuşata a promovat în liga a III-a|website=adevarul.ro}}{{in lang|ro}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://liga2.prosport.ro/liga-3/minaur-zlatna-promoveaza-in-dauna-berzoviei-4572378|date=June 18, 2009|title=Minaur Zlatna promovează în dauna Berzoviei|website=liga2.prosport.ro}}{{in lang|ro}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://liga2.prosport.ro/liga-3/vise-naruite-pentru-retezatul-hateg-4572719|date=June 18, 2009|title=Vise năruite pentru Retezatul Haţeg|website=liga2.prosport.ro}}{{in lang|ro}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://liga2.prosport.ro/special/a-treia-promovare-a-petrolului-teleajen-in-c-4572290|date=June 18, 2009|title=A treia promovare a Petrolului Teleajen în „C”|website=liga2.prosport.ro}}{{in lang|ro}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://turdanews.net/articole/sport/4196-baraj-live-luceafrul-oradea-fc-arieul-ii-0-0|date=June 17, 2009|title=Baraj FINAL: Luceafărul Oradea - FC Arieşul II 2-1|website=turdanews.net}}{{in lang|ro}}</ref>
