The '''2008–09 Liga IV''' was the 67th season of the [[Liga IV]], the fourth tier of the [[Romanian football league system]]. The champions of each county association play against one from a neighboring county in a play-off match played on a neutral venue. The winners of the play-off matches promoted to [[2009–10 Liga III|Liga III]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://liga2.prosport.ro/special/21-de-echipe-au-promovat-in-liga-3-4565909|title=21 de echipe au promovat în Liga 3|trans-title=21 teams promoted to Liga 3|publisher=liga2.prosport.ro|access-date=1 January 2020|archive-date=1 January 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101153505/http://liga2.prosport.ro/special/21-de-echipe-au-promovat-in-liga-3-4565909|url-status=live}}{{in lang|ro}}</ref>{{TOC right|limit=3}}
