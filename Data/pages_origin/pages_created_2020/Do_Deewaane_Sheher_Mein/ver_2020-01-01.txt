"'''Do Deewaane Sheher Mein'''" is an Indian [[Hindi]] song from the [[Bollywood]] film ''[[Gharaonda]]''. (1977). The film was directed by Bhimsain Khurana. The lyrics of the song was written by [[Gulzar]], and the music was composed by [[Jaidev]].<ref name="Anantharaman2008">{{cite book|author=Ganesh Anantharaman|title=Bollywood Melodies: A History|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=KS3ZDgAAQBAJ&pg=PT170|date=14 February 2008|publisher=Random House Publishers India Pvt. Limited|isbn=978-93-86495-31-0|pages=170–}}</ref> In 1977 Gulzar won his first [[Filmfare Award for Best Lyricist]] for this song.<ref name="Premchand2018">{{cite book|author=Manek Premchand|title=Yesterday’s Melodies Today’s Memories|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ZJCBDwAAQBAJ&pg=PT512|date=27 December 2018|publisher=Notion Press|isbn=978-1-64429-877-0|pages=512–}}</ref> The song was sung by [[Bhupinder Singh (musician)|Bhupinder Singh]] and [[Runa Laila]].<ref name="Filmfare Beyond borders Runa Laila">{{cite web |title=Beyond borders Runa Laila |url=https://www.filmfare.com/interviews/beyond-borders-runa-laila-1479.html |website=filmfare.com |accessdate=30 December 2019 |language=en}}</ref> The song has a reprise "Ek Akela Is Sheher Mein".<ref name="Bollywood Retrospect">{{cite news |last1=Arunachalam |first1=Param |title=Bollywood Retrospect - Top 10 songs by music composer Jaidev |url=https://www.dnaindia.com/entertainment/report-bollywood-retrospect-top-10-songs-by-music-composer-jaidev-2109897 |work=DNA India |date=1 August 2015 |language=en}}</ref>
