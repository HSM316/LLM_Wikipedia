'''Lichtensteig railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Lichtensteig}}) is a railway station in [[Lichtensteig]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It sits at the junction of the [[Bodensee–Toggenburg railway]] and [[Wil–Ebnat-Kappel railway]] and is served by local trains only. It is an example of a [[Keilbahnhof]].
