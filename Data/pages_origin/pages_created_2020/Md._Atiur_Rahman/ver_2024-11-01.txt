'''Md. Atiur Rahman''' ({{circa|1939}} – 13 November 2013) was a Bangladeshi academic and politician from [[Dinajpur District, Bangladesh|Dinajpur]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Nationalist Party]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Atiur Rahman received a postgraduate degree in English from [[Rajshahi University]] in 1962.<ref name=a>{{cite web |url=http://www.dailysangram.com/post/132535-সাবেক-এমপি--আতিউর-রহমান--আর-নেই
|script-title=bn:সাবেক এমপি আতিউর রহমান আর নেই
|website=Sangram|language=bn |accessdate=1 January 2020|date=22 November 2013}}</ref> He was the principal of Dinajpur Government Women's College, Syedpur Cantonment Public College, [[Jalalabad Cantonment Public School and College|Jalalabad Cantonment Public College]] and [[Bogra Cantonment Public School and College|Bogra Cantonment Public College]]. In 1978 he resigned from his job and joined politics.<ref name=a/>

Atiur Rahman was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Dinajpur-11 in the [[1979 Bangladeshi general election|second general election of Bangladesh]].<ref name="1979results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/2nd.pdf |title=List of 2nd Parliament Members  |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=13 August 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180904090815/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/2nd.pdf |archive-date=4 September 2018}}</ref> Later, he was elected as a member of the Jatiya Sangsad from [[Dinajpur-6]] in the [[February 1996 Bangladeshi general election|sixth Jatiya Sangsad election]].<ref name="feb1996results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/6th%20Parliament%20.pdf |title=List of 6th Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=8 October 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180915084544/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp-bangla/6th%20Parliament%20.pdf |archive-date=15 September 2018}}</ref>

Atiur Rahman died on 13 November 2013 at the age of 74.<ref name=a/><ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.risingbd.com/fascinating-world/news/24292/দিনাজপুরে-বিএনপির-সাবেক-সাংসদের-মৃত্যু-হরতাল-শিথীল
|script-title=bn:দিনাজপুরে বিএনপির সাবেক সাংসদের মৃত্যু, হরতাল শিথীল
|work=risingbd.com|language=bn|date=13 November 2013 |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>
