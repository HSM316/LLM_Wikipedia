'''Harry Ossip Meerson''' (1910 or 1911 - 1991) was a French photographer.

Harry Ossip Meerson was born in [[Warsaw]], Poland in either 1910 or 1911.<ref name="Curiator">{{cite web |title=Harry Ossip Meerson |url=https://curiator.com/art/harry-ossip-meerson |website=Curiator |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>

His brother [[Lazare Meerson]] became a film director in Hollywood.<ref name="Curiator"/>
