'''Amjad Hossain''' ({{circa|1935}} – 22 March 2015) was a Bangladeshi lawyer and politician from [[Pabna District|Pabna]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Awami League]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Hossain was an organizer of the [[Liberation War of Bangladesh]].<ref name=a/> After liberation he was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Pabna-12 in 1973.<ref name="1973results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/1st.pdf |title=List of 1st Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=13 August 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180909153327/http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/1st.pdf |archive-date=9 September 2018}}</ref>

Hossain was married to [[Jannatul Ferdous (politician)|Jannatul Ferdous]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/85808.details |title=সাবেক সাংসদ অধ্যাপিকা জান্নাতুল ফেরদৌস আর নেই |work=Banglanews24.com |language=bn |date=2 February 2012 |access-date=1 January 2020 |archive-date=1 January 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101070905/https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/85808.details |url-status=dead}}</ref> She was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] too. They had two daughters and one son.<ref>{{cite news |script-title=bn:আমজাদ হোসেন |url=https://www.prothomalo.com/bangladesh/%E0%A6%86%E0%A6%AE%E0%A6%9C%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%A6-%E0%A6%B9%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%BE%E0%A6%B8%E0%A7%87%E0%A6%A8 |work=Prothom Alo |language=bn |date=23 March 2015 |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>

Hossain died on 22 March 2015 at the age of 80.<ref name=a>{{cite news |url=https://samakal.com/bibidh/article/1503126188/পাবনার-সাবেক-এমপি-আমজাদ-হোসেনের-মৃত্যু |script-title=bn:পাবনার সাবেক এমপি আমজাদ হোসেনের মৃত্যু |work=Samakal |language=bn |date=22 March 2015 |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/378858.details |script-title=bn:পাবনার সাবেক এমপি আমজাদ হোসেন আর নেই |work=Banglanews24.com |language=bn |date=22 March 2015 |access-date=1 January 2020 |archive-date=1 January 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101065401/https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/378858.details |url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.risingbd.com/fascinating-world/news/98979/পাবনায়_মুক্তিযুদ্ধের_সংগঠক_আমজাদ_হোসেনের_মৃত্যু |script-title=bn:পাবনায় মুক্তিযুদ্ধের সংগঠক আমজাদ হোসেনের মৃত্যু |work=risingbd.com |language=bn |date=22 March 2015 |access-date=1 January 2020}}</ref>
