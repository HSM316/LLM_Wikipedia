'''Amjad Hossain''' ({{circa|1935}} – 22 March 2015) was a Bangladeshi lawyer and politician from [[Pabna District|Pabna]] belonging to [[Bangladesh Awami League]]. He was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]].

==Biography==
Hossain was an organizer of the [[Liberation War of Bangladesh]].<ref name=a/> After liberation he was elected as a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] from Pabna-12 in 1973.<ref name="1973results">{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.bd/images/pdf/formermp/1st.pdf |title=List of 1st Parliament Members |website=Jatiya Sangsad |language=bn |access-date=13 August 2014}}</ref>

Hossain was married to [[Jannatul Ferdous (politician)|Jannatul Ferdous]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://m.banglanews24.com/national/news/bd/85808.details

|website=Banglanews24.com|language=bn|date=2 February 2012 |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> She was a member of the [[Jatiya Sangsad]] too. They had two daughters and one son.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://m.prothomalo.com/bangladesh/article/483880/আমজাদ-হোসেন
