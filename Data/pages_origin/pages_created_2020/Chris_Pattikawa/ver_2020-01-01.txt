'''Chris Pattikawa''' (1 October 1940 â€“ 1 January 2020) was an Indonesian film director and producer. Born in [[Ambon]], in 1974 Pattikawa led the film company P.T. Aurea Aristo Film. Actress Rina Hassim is his wife, and has a son who became a singer Jean Pattikawa. Chris is a contemporary of composer Enteng Tanamal and director Nico Pelamonia and Jopijaya Burnama.
