'''''Cnemaspis koynaensis''''', the '''Koyna dwarf gecko''', is a species of [[Diurnality|diurnal]], rock-dwelling, [[insectivorous]] [[gecko]] endemic to  [[India]]. It is distributed in [[Maharashtra]].
