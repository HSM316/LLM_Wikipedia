'''''Cypraeovula cruickshanki''''' is a [[species]] of [[sea snail]], a [[cowry]], a marine [[gastropod]] [[mollusk]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Cypraeidae]], the cowries.

==Description==
This globular species attains a size of 30 mm.

==Distribution==
Durban, at 430 m. depth.
