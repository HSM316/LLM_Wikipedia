'''Woodview Park''' is an [[Unincorporated area|unincorporated community]] in [[Paris Township, Union County, Ohio|Paris Township]], [[Union County, Ohio|Union County]], [[Ohio]], United States. It is located at the intersection of [[Ohio State Route 4]] and Country Home Road, just north of [[Marysville, Ohio|Marysville]].<ref>Rand McNally, ''The Road Atlas '06,'' Chicago, 2006, p. 78</ref>
