'''PRUride Philippines''' is an annual professional [[road bicycle racing]] [[race stage|stage race]] held in the [[Philippines]] since 2019. The race is part of the [[UCI Asia Tour]] and was classified by the [[International Cycling Union]] (UCI) as a 2.2 category race.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.procyclingstats.com/race/pruride-ph/2019/gc | title=PRUride Philippines | work=ProCyclingStats | accessdate=30 December 2019}}</ref>
