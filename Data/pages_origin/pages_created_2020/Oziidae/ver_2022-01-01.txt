'''Oziidae''' is a family of crabs in the order [[Decapoda]]. There are about 7 genera and more than 30 described species in Oziidae.<ref name=gbif/>

==Genera==
These seven genera belong to the family Oziidae:
* ''[[Baptozius]]'' <small>Alcock, 1898</small>
* ''[[Bountiana]]'' <small>N.g.Davie, 2000</small>
* ''[[Epixanthoides]]'' <small>Balss, 1935</small>
* ''[[Epixanthus]]'' <small>Heller, 1861</small>
* ''[[Eupilumnus]]'' <small>Kossmann, 1877</small>
* ''[[Lydia (crab)|Lydia]]'' <small>Gistel, 1848</small>
* ''[[Ozius]]'' <small>Milne-Edwards, 1834</small>
