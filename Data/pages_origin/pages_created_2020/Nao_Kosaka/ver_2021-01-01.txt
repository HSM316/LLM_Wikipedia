{{nihongo|'''Nao Kosaka'''|小坂菜緒|Kosaka Nao|born September 7, 2002}} is a Japanese idol, model, actress and member of the female Japanese idol group [[Hinatazaka46]]. She is an exclusive model for the female fashion magazine [[Seventeen (Japanese magazine)|Seventeen]].<ref name="Seventeen">{{ cite web | url=https://mdpr.jp/news/detail/1767968 | title=けやき坂46小坂菜緒「Seventeen」専属モデル抜てき＜コメント到着＞ | work=モデルプレス | language=ja | date=May 22, 2018 | accessdate=December 31, 2019 }}</ref>

==Career==
On August 13, 2017, Kosaka passed the auditions for new members of Keyakizaka46's subgroup Hiragana Keyakizaka46.<ref>{{ cite web | url=https://mdpr.jp/music/detail/1707341 | title=けやき坂46、新メンバー9名決定＜プロフィール＞ | work=モデルプレス　| language=Japanese | date=August 15, 2017 | accessdate=March 5, 2020 }}</ref>

On May 19, 2018, at the GirlsAward 2018 SPRING／SUMMER show, Kosaka had her [[Runway (fashion)|runway]] debut.<ref>{{ cite web | url=https://mdpr.jp/news/detail/1767468 | title=けやき坂46小坂菜緒、ステージでも輝く圧倒的美貌 初ランウェイに歓声飛ぶ＜GirlsAward 2018 S／S ＞ | work=モデルプレス | language=Japanese | date=May 19, 2018 | accessdate=March 4, 2020 }}</ref> May 21, 2018, ''[[Seventeen (Japanese magazine)|Seventeen]]'' announced that she will be one of the magazine's exclusive model.<ref name="Seventeen" />
