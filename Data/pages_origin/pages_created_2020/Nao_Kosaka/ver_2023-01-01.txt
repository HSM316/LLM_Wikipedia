{{nihongo|'''Nao Kosaka'''|小坂菜緒|Kosaka Nao|lead=yes|born September 7, 2002}} is a Japanese singer, model, and actress. She is a member of the Japanese female idol group [[Hinatazaka46]] and an exclusive model for the women's fashion magazine [[Seventeen (Japanese magazine)|''Seventeen'']].<ref name="Seventeen">{{ cite web | url=https://mdpr.jp/news/detail/1767968 | title=けやき坂46小坂菜緒「Seventeen」専属モデル抜てき＜コメント到着＞ | work=モデルプレス | language=ja | date=May 22, 2018 | access-date=December 31, 2019 }}</ref>

==Career==
On August 13, 2017, Kosaka passed the auditions for new members of [[Keyakizaka46]]'s subgroup Hiragana Keyakizaka46 and entered the group as a second generation member.<ref>{{ cite web | url=https://mdpr.jp/music/detail/1707341 | title=けやき坂46、新メンバー9名決定＜プロフィール＞ | work=モデルプレス| language=Japanese | date=August 15, 2017 | access-date=March 5, 2020 }}</ref> She made her debut in the song "No War in the Future", a [[B-side]] to "[[Kaze ni Fukarete mo]]".<ref>{{cite web |title=欅坂46 5th single: 風に吹かれても |url=https://www.keyakizaka46.com/s/k46o/page/5th_single |website=欅坂46公式サイト |access-date=29 January 2021 |language=ja}}</ref> On May 19, 2018, Kosaka made her [[Runway (fashion)|runway]] debut at the GirlsAward 2018 SPRING/SUMMER show.<ref>{{ cite web | url=https://mdpr.jp/news/detail/1767468 | title=けやき坂46小坂菜緒、ステージでも輝く圧倒的美貌 初ランウェイに歓声飛ぶ＜GirlsAward 2018 S／S ＞ | work=モデルプレス | language=Japanese | date=May 19, 2018 | access-date=March 4, 2020 }}</ref> ''[[Seventeen (Japanese magazine)|Seventeen]]'' announced two days later that she would be one of the magazine's [[exclusive model]]s.<ref name="Seventeen" />

After Hiragana Keyakizaka46 was renamed to Hinatazaka46 in February 2019,<ref>{{cite news |title=Hiragana Keyaki Changes Name to Hinatazaka46, Debut Single Due in March |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8498732/hiragana-keyaki-changes-name-hinatazaka46-debut-single-japan-arena-concert-Keyakizaka46 |access-date=29 January 2021 |agency=Billboard |date=16 February 2019}}</ref> Kosaka was selected to hold the center (lead performer) position in the title song of their debut single, "[[Kyun (song)|Kyun]]".<ref>{{cite news |title=日向坂デビューシングルセンターは2期生の小坂菜緒 |url=https://www.nikkansports.com/entertainment/news/201903040000023.html |access-date=29 January 2021 |agency=Nikkan Sports |date=4 March 219 |language=ja}}</ref> She continued to hold the position until the fourth single title song, "[[Sonna Koto Nai yo]]".<ref>{{cite news |title=日向坂46新曲センターは4作連続で小坂菜緒 東村芽依&金村美玖が初の1列目 |url=https://www.oricon.co.jp/news/2153077/full/ |access-date=29 January 2021 |agency=Oricon |date=13 January 2020 |language=ja}}</ref>

Kosaka was cast as the main character in the 2019 mystery film {{Nihongo|''{{ill|The Japanese Doll|ja|恐怖人形}}''|恐怖人形|Kyōfu Ningyō}}.<ref>{{cite news |title=日向坂小坂菜緒「恐怖人形」サプライズ舞台あいさつ |url=https://www.nikkansports.com/entertainment/news/201909230000228.html |access-date=29 January 2021 |agency=Nikkan Sports |date=23 September 2019 |language=ja}}</ref> She would also appear in ''[[Jump!! The Heroes Behind the Gold]]'', a 2021 film about [[ski jumping at the 1998 Winter Olympics]],<ref>{{cite news |title=眞栄田郷敦：日向坂46小坂菜緒とテストジャンパーに 田中圭主演映画「ヒノマルソウル」出演 |url=https://mantan-web.jp/article/20200217dog00m200048000c.html |access-date=29 January 2021 |agency=Mantan Web |date=18 February 2020 |language=ja}}</ref> in which her character is based on ski jumper {{Interlanguage link|Yoshiko Yoshiizumi|ja|吉泉賀子}};<ref>{{Cite web|title=日向坂46小坂菜緒、ジャンプ競技を間近で初観戦「今から興奮しています」 - モデルプレス|url=https://mdpr.jp/news/detail/2338201|access-date=2021-01-29|website=モデルプレス - ライフスタイル・ファッションエンタメニュース|language=ja}}</ref> ''Hominis'' commented that despite performing alongside more experienced cast members, she maintained a unique presence by acting "from the heart".<ref>{{Cite web |title=日向坂46のセンター、小坂菜緒の「女優としての才能が光る瞬間」 |url=https://hominis.media/category/idol/post9000/ |access-date=2022-06-08 |website=Hominis |publisher=[[SKY Perfect JSAT]] |language=ja}}</ref>

Kosaka's solo photobook, produced by the ''Seventeen'' magazine editorial staff, was released on June 29.<ref>{{Cite web|title=日向坂小坂菜緒が初ソロ写真集「子供から大人へ成長していく姿」初の水着も - 坂道 : 日刊スポーツ|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/entertainment/news/202104140000670.html|access-date=2021-04-23|website=nikkansports.com}}</ref> The book placed first in [[Oricon]]'s 2021 photobook sales ranking with 166,876 copies sold.<ref>{{Cite web |title=【年間本ランキング】『鬼滅の刃』最終巻が500万部越えで史上初の快挙 総合1位は『スマホ脳』、現代人への課題を提示 11ページ目 |url=https://www.oricon.co.jp/special/57840/11/ |access-date=2022-06-23 |website=ORICON NEWS}}</ref> As a [[dinosaur]] enthusiast, she was also appointed in the same month the ambassador for Sony's DinoScience exhibition, which would be held from July to September at the [[Pacifico Yokohama]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=ソニー損保の自動車保険と「DinoScience 恐竜科学博」コラボCMに日向坂46 小坂菜緒さんを起用|url=https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000251.000001545.html|access-date=2021-11-18|website=プレスリリース・ニュースリリース配信シェアNo.1｜PR TIMES}}</ref> However, she went into hiatus on June 26 for health reasons<ref>{{Cite web|title=Hinatazaka46's Kosaka Nao to go on temporary hiatus due to poor health|url=https://www.tokyohive.com/article/2021/06/hinatazaka46s-kosaka-nao-to-go-on-temporary-hiatus-due-to-poor-health|access-date=2021-10-05|website=tokyohive}}</ref> and did not participate in the rest of the event; she also did not appear in Hinatazaka46's sixth single, "[[Tteka]]". Kosaka announced her resumption of activities on March 4, 2022<ref>{{Cite web |title=日向坂46小坂菜緒が活動再開へ「少しずつのスタートとなりますが」９カ月ぶりブログ更新 - 坂道 : 日刊スポーツ |url=https://www.nikkansports.com/entertainment/news/202203040000266.html |access-date=2022-03-07 |website=nikkansports.com |language=ja}}</ref> and also returned to the center position for Hinatazaka46's seventh single title track "[[Boku Nanka]]", despite her "mixed feelings" about the appointment.<ref>{{Cite web |title=日向坂46 小坂菜緒、復帰後に改めて感じるセンターとしての魅力 自身も輝きながら周囲の魅力を引き立てる存在感 |trans-title=Hinatazaka46 Nao Kosaka's renewed appeal as the center after her comeback, a shining presence who enhances the charm of those around her |url=https://realsound.jp/2022/06/post-1047787.html |access-date=2022-06-23 |website=Real Sound｜リアルサウンド |language=ja}}</ref>

In a late 2021 survey by {{Ill|Talent Power Ranking|ja|タレントパワーランキング}}, Kosaka was ranked the seventh most popular teen actress in Japan (under twenty years old as of February 2022) based on public recognition and interest.<ref>{{cite news |date=April 14, 2022 |title=今話題の若手女優は誰？10代の人気女優ランキング |language=ja |trans-title=Who is the hottest young actress now? Ranking of popular teenage actresses |work=Talent Power Ranking |url=https://tpranking.com/10s-actress}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Kosaka played [[volleyball]] in middle school and occasionally demonstrates her skill in her appearances. She has an older brother who is a [[Keyakizaka46]] fan.<ref>{{Cite episode|title=Introducing the 2nd Generation Hiragana Members!|episode-link=|series=Keyakitte, Kakenai?|series-link=Keyakitte, Kakenai?|network=[[TV Tokyo]]|date=October 12, 2017|season=|number=100|time=12:45}}</ref>

Kosaka's main interests include dinosaurs and [[reptile]]s.<ref>{{Cite web|title=日向坂46、初収録から涙…全力バラエティに挑む！＜HINABINGO！＞ - モデルプレス|url=https://mdpr.jp/news/detail/1832590|access-date=2021-02-11|website=モデルプレス - ライフスタイル・ファッションエンタメニュース|language=ja}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Saito|first=Kyoko|author-link=Kyōko Saitō|date=14 August 2018|title=小坂菜緒の実態|trans-title=The Truth About Nao Kosaka|url=https://www.hinatazaka46.com/s/official/diary/detail/25997?ima=0000&cd=member|url-status=live|access-date=2021-03-03|website=日向坂46公式サイト}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Interview: Kosaka Nao|url=https://www.frontage.jp/dino-science/en/interview02/|url-status=live|website=DinoScience}}</ref> She also enjoys [[Owarai|comedy shows]] and named [[Kazuya Kojima]] of Unjash and [[Audrey (owarai)|Audrey]] as her favorite comedians.<ref>{{Cite web|title=日向坂46小坂菜緒、好きな男性芸能人告白「嘘じゃないです」 - モデルプレス|url=https://mdpr.jp/news/2267384|access-date=2021-02-11|website=モデルプレス - ライフスタイル・ファッションエンタメニュース|language=ja}}</ref>
