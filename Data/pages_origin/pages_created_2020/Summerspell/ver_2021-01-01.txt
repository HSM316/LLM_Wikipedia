'''''Summerspell''''' is an independent film written and directed by [[Lina Shanklin]]. It premiered at the U.S. Film Festival (the precursor to [[Sundance Film Festival|Sundance]]) on April 1, 1983.

== Plot ==
The film follows as the members of a large extended family gather on the Fourth of July for a not-so-happy reunion on their California ranch right after World War II.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.newspapers.com/image/161449031/?terms=summerspell+%22lina+shanklin%22|title=Eloquent Without Words|last=Thomas|first=Kevin|date=23 Sep 1999|website=The Los Angeles Times|language=en|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

== Cast ==
