'''Zakho International Stadium''' ([[Arabic language|Arabic]]: ملعب زاخو الدولي, [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]: یاریگەها نێودەولەتیا زاخۆ) is a multi-purpose stadium in [[Zakho]], [[Iraq]]. It is used mostly for football matches and serves as the home stadium of [[Zakho FC]] which plays in the [[Iraqi Premier League]]. The stadium holds 25,000 people.<ref>[https://www.goalzz.com/?showstadiums=1&stadium=5639 Zakho International Stadium], GOALZZ.com</ref> It has 20 entrance and exit gates. The roof consists of 12,000 m{{nbsp}}<sup>2</sup> of polycarbonate architectural panels shaded in red and white depicting the colors of the local team.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.palramprojects.com/projects-in.aspx?num=67|title=ROOF // MULTIWALL POLYCARBONATE SYSTEM|website=palramprojects.com|access-date=2020-08-31}}</ref> Construction took 3 years (from 2012 to 2015) for a total cost of $20 million.<ref>[https://rudaw.net/arabic/sports/030620151 Inauguration of Zakho Stadium], rudaw.net</ref>

The opening ceremony took place on Wednesday 3 June 2015 between [[Zakho FC]] and the [[Iraq national football team]].<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jmeCD7scva8 Iraq 2–0 Zakho FC], youtube.com</ref> The match ended in a 2–0 victory for the [[Iraq national football team]], scored by captain [[Younis Mahmoud]] and [[Hussein Ali Wahid]].<ref>[https://www.azzaman.com/%D9%81%D9%88%D8%B2-%D9%85%D8%B9%D9%86%D9%88%D9%8A-%D9%84%D9%84%D9%85%D9%86%D8%AA%D8%AE%D8%A8-%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%88%D8%B7%D9%86%D9%8A-%D9%81%D9%8A-%D8%A5%D9%81%D8%AA%D8%AA%D8%A7%D8%AD-%D9%85%D9%84%D8%B9/ فوز معنوي للمنتخب الوطني في إفتتاح ملعب زاخو الجديد], azzaman.com</ref><ref>[https://www.alsumaria.tv/news/136126/ المنتخب الوطني يفوز على زاخو بملعبه الجديد], alsumaria.tv</ref>

The inauguration aroused large official and popular attendance, including the presence of the former Minister of Youth and Sport [[Abdul-Hussein Abtaan]], the head of the [[National Olympic Committee of Iraq]] [[Raad Hammoudi]] and the former Iraqi national team player, [[Hussein Saeed]].<ref>[https://www.baghdad-times.net/2015/06/03/%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%AF-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%AD%D8%B3%D9%8A%D9%86-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%B7%D8%A7%D9%86-%D9%8A%D8%AD%D8%B6%D8%B1-%D8%A7%D9%81%D8%AA%D8%AA%D8%A7%D8%AD-%D9%85%D9%84%D8%B9%D8%A8-%D8%B2%D8%A7/ عبد الحسين العبطان يحضر افتتاح ملعب زاخو في دهوك], baghdad-times.net</ref>
