'''Horace G.''' “'''Pappy'''” '''Owens''' (born October 9, 1961) is an American [[basketball]] coach and former player who is an assistant coach for the [[Delaware State Hornets men's basketball|Delaware State Hornets men's team]].

Born in [[Philadelphia, Pennsylvania]], he played for [[Murrell Dobbins Vocational School|Dobbins Technical High School]] and was a [[McDonald's All-American Game|McDonald's All-American]] in 1979.<ref name=tpi>{{cite web |last1=Silary |first1=Ted |title=Horace Owens fondly remembers McDonald's All American Game |url=https://www.inquirer.com/philly/hp/sports/20090401_High_Schools_-_Horace_Owens_fondly_remembers_McDonald_s_All_American_Game.html |website=The Philadelphia Inquirer |accessdate=31 December 2019 |date=1 April 2009}}</ref> Owens played collegiately for the [[Rhode Island Rams men's basketball|Rhode Island Rams]], where he ranks seventh on the school's all-time scoring list.<ref name=lsb>{{cite web |title=Horace Owens |url=https://goexplorers.com/sports/mens-basketball/roster/coaches/horace-owens/1479 |website=La Salle University Athletics |accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> He was an honorable mention [[1983 NCAA Men's Basketball All-Americans|All-American]] and first team All-[[Atlantic 10 Conference|Atlantic 10]] selection during his senior season.<ref name=lsb /> Owens was drafted by the [[New Jersey Nets]] as the 44th overall pick of the [[1983 NBA draft]]. His rights were traded to the [[Philadelphia 76ers]] during training camp that year and he was one of the team's last preseason cuts.<ref name=tpi /> Owens never played in the [[National Basketball Association]] (NBA).

Owens worked as a juvenile probation officer for the City of Philadelphia for fourteen years.<ref name=lsb /> In 2004, he joined the [[La Salle Explorers men's basketball|La Salle Explorers]] as an assistant coach and served that role for fourteen years.<ref name=lsb /> In 2018, he shifted roles to become a special assistant to head coach [[Ashley Howard (basketball)|Ashley Howard]].<ref name=lsb /> Owens joined the [[Delaware State Hornets men's basketball|Delaware State Hornets]] as an assistant coach in July 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Horace Owens |url=https://dsuhornets.com/sports/mens-basketball/roster/coaches/horace-owens/553 |website=Delaware State University Athletics |access-date=September 1, 2021}}</ref>

Owens was inducted into the Philadelphia Black Basketball Hall of Fame in 2019.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Hunt |first1=Donald |title=Philadelphia Black Basketball Hall of Fame immortalizes 25 more outstanding contributors and legends |url=https://www.phillytrib.com/sports/philadelphia-black-basketball-hall-of-fame-immortalizes-25-more-outstanding-contributors-and-legends/article_d157fcfd-b4c6-5f5c-ac1f-2fd44afc631d.html |website=The Philadelphia Tribune |access-date=September 1, 2021 |date=February 18, 2019}}</ref>
