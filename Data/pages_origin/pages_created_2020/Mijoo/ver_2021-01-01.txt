'''Lee Mi-joo''' ({{lang-ko|이미주}}; born September 23, 1994), better known by the mononym '''Mijoo''', is a South Korean singer and dancer.<ref name="bio">{{cite web |title= [더스타프로필] 이미주 "베이비소울, 러블리즈 '힐링요정' 서열 8위" 이유는? |url= http://thestar.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2015/12/15/2015121503809.html |website= 조선일보 |date= 2015-12-18|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170810131718/http://thestar.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2015/12/15/2015121503809.html |archive-date=2017-08-10|url-status= dead }}</ref> She debuted as a member of South Korean [[girl group]] [[Lovelyz]] under [[Woollim Entertainment]] in 2014.

==Early life==
Mijoo was born on September 23, 1994 in [[Okcheon County]], [[North Chungcheong Province]], South Korea.<ref name="bio"></ref>

==Career==
===2014–2017: Debut with ''Girls' Invasion'' and career beginnings===
{{Main|Lovelyz}}
Mijoo debuted with Lovelyz, which was first announced on November 5, 2014 by Woolim Entertainment.<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.newspim.com/view.jsp?newsId=20141103000080&fromurl=na|title = 울림 첫 걸그룹 ′러블리즈′ 데뷔 임박, 10일 음원 선공개로 ′출사표′|last = Yang|first = Jinyeong|date = 2014-11-03}}</ref> Lovelyz released a pre-release digital single, "Good Night Like Yesterday", on November 5, 2014. Their debut showcase was held on November 12 at the K-ART Hall in Olympic Park, and their debut stage was on the next day on [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]] ''[[M Countdown]]''.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Hong|first1=Grace Danbi|title=Lovelyz Wishes You a 'Good Night Like Yesterday' in Debut MV|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/80323/video-lovelyz-wishes-you-a-good-night-like-yesterday-in-debut-mv|accessdate=November 25, 2014|work=[[CJ E&M]] enewsWorld|date=November 10, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141122181207/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/80323/video-lovelyz-wishes-you-a-good-night-like-yesterday-in-debut-mv|archive-date=November 22, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> The group's debut studio album ''[[Girls' Invasion]]'' was officially released on November 17, along with its lead single titled "Candy Jelly Love".<ref>{{cite web|title = Lovelyz drops debut album amid controversy|url = http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20141117000884|date = 2014-11-17}}</ref>

Mijoo made a cameo in ''[[The Gentlemen of Wolgyesu Tailor Shop]]'' in 2016. She participated in the [[dance]] survival show ''[[Hit the Stage]]''. She placed 6th by the end of episode 8 and was eventually eliminated. She was also a host in ''[[Inkigayo]]'' alongside bandmate [[Kei (singer)|Kei]] and [[BTS (band)|BTS]] members [[Kim Seok-jin|Jin]] and [[RM (rapper)|RM]].<ref name="inkigayo"></ref> Mijoo was cast in the television series ''[[:ko:나는 취준생이다|I'm a Job Seeker]]'', playing the role as Na Young-hee.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/213/0000903314|title= [T포토] 나는 취준생이다 "모든 분들을 응원합니다!"|date=August 30, 2016|work=Naver|access-date=January 1, 2019|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/421/0002240610|title= 러블리즈 이미주, 웹드라마 '나는 취준생이다' 출연 |date=August 30, 2016|work=Naver|access-date=January 1, 2019|language=ko}}</ref>

===2018–present: Solo activities===
Mijoo was part of the cast of ''[[Dunia: Into a New World]]'' alongside [[Yunho]], [[DinDin]], [[Sam Okyere]], [[Kwon Hyun-bin]] and many more.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://m.news1.kr/articles/?3353775#_enliple|title= [단독]'두니아' 2차 촬영 나간다…30일 출국 |date=June 25, 2018|website=News1|access-date=January 3, 2020}}</ref> She was also part of ''My Mad Beauty 2'' with Lee Jin-yi, [[Kim Hyo-yeon|Hyoyeon]] and [[Park Na-rae]].<ref>{{cite news |title=[마이 매드 뷰티 2] 출연진 소개 |url=http://tv.jtbc.joins.com/cast/pr10010946 |accessdate=January 1, 2020 |work=JTBC |language=ko}}</ref> She is part of the third season of the series alongside Park Na-rae, [[Choi Yoo-jung (singer)|Choi Yoo-jung]] and [[Han Hye-jin (model)|Han Hye-jin]].<ref>{{Cite web|url= https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/144/0000597446 |title= '뷰티만 있니?'…'마매뷰3' 한혜진·박나래, 웃음 더한다 [스경X현장] |date=January 26, 2019|website=Naver|access-date=January 1, 2020}}</ref> She is also cast in the television show ''In-laws In Practice'' (2018), paired with [[Kwon Hyuk-soo (actor)|Kwon Hyuk-soo]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/311/0000911170|title= [종합] '아찔한 사돈연습' 권혁수♥미주, 콩트 부부 신혼집 입주|date=October 19, 2018|website=Naver|access-date=January 3, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/009/0004264882|title= '안녕하세요' 러블리즈 미주, "가상남편 권혁수.. 배려해줄 때 심쿵해"|date=December 3, 2018|website=Naver|access-date=January 3, 2020}}</ref> and ''Shopping Cart Saviour'' (2019) together with [[Haha (entertainer)|Haha]] and more.<ref name="shop"></ref>
