'''Lee Mi-joo''' ({{lang-ko|이미주}}, born September 23, 1994), better known by the mononym '''Mijoo''', is a South Korean singer and dancer. She debuted as a member of South Korean [[girl group]] [[Lovelyz]] under [[Woollim Entertainment]] in 2014.
