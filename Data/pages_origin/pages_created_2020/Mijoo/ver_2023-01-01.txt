'''Lee Seung-ah''' ({{Korean|hangul=이승아}}; born '''Lee Mi-joo''' ({{Korean|hangul=이미주}}) on September 23, 1994), known as '''Lee Mi-joo''', is a South Korean singer, dancer and entertainer. She debuted as a member of South Korean [[girl group]] [[Lovelyz]] under [[Woollim Entertainment]] in November 2014. Apart from her group's activities, Lee has starred in various [[Korean variety show|Korean variety shows]] such as, ''[[Hit The Stage]]'' (2016), ''[[Dunia: Into a New World]]'' (2018), ''My Mad Beauty'' (2018–2019), ''Shopping Cart Savior'' (2019), ''[[Sixth Sense (TV series)|Sixth Sense]]'' (2020–2022), ''[[Hangout with Yoo]]'' (2021) and season 2 of ''[[Learn Way (Season 2)|Learn Way]]'' (2021–2022). Lee made her acting debut with ''I'm a Job Seeker'' (2016).

On November 16, 2021, she left [[Woollim Entertainment]] after deciding not to renew her contract, and later joined [[Antenna (record label)|Antenna]] for her future solo activities.<ref name="Antenna">{{cite web|title=유재석 라인 탄 러블리즈 이미주, 안테나와 전속계약 [공식]|trans-title=Yoo Jae-seok, Line Tan Lovelyz's Mi-joo, exclusive contract with Antenna [Official]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=015&aid=0004629531|via=Naver|publisher=Hankyung|language=ko|author=Kim Soo-young|date=November 17, 2021|access-date=November 17, 2021|archive-date=November 17, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211117031052/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=015&aid=0004629531|url-status=live}}</ref>
