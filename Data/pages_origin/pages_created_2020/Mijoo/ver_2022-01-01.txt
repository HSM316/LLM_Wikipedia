'''Lee Seung-ah''' ({{Korean|hangul=이승아}}; born '''Lee Mi-joo''' ({{Korean|hangul=이미주}}) on September 23, 1994), known as '''Lee Mi-joo''', is a South Korean singer, dancer and entertainer. She debuted as a member of South Korean [[girl group]] [[Lovelyz]] under [[Woollim Entertainment]] in November 2014. Apart from her group's activities, Lee has starred in various [[Korean variety show|Korean variety shows]] such as, ''[[Hit The Stage]]'' (2016), ''[[Dunia: Into a New World]]'' (2018), ''My Mad Beauty'' (2018-2019), ''Shopping Cart Savior'' (2019), ''[[Sixth Sense (TV series)|Sixth Sense]]'' (2020-2021), ''[[Hangout with Yoo]]'' (2021). Lee also made her acting debut wih starred Naver TV's ''I'm a Job Seeker'' (2016).

On November 16, 2021, she left [[Woollim Entertainment]] after deciding not to renew her contract, following Lovelyz's disbandment<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=382&aid=0000941930|title=러블리즈 해체…울림 “16일 계약만료, 각자 새출발” (공식) [전문]|last=Hong|first=Seyoung|date=November 1, 2021|access-date=November 1, 2021|work=Sports Donga|publisher=Naver|language=ko|archive-date=November 1, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211101092517/https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=382&aid=0000941930|url-status=live}}</ref> and later joined [[Antenna (record label)|Antenna]] for her future solo activities.<ref>{{cite web|title=유재석 라인 탄 러블리즈 이미주, 안테나와 전속계약 [공식]|trans-title=Yoo Jae-seok, Line Tan Lovelyz's Mi-joo, exclusive contract with Antenna [Official]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=015&aid=0004629531|via=Naver|publisher=Hankyung|language=ko|author=Kim Soo-young|date=November 17, 2021|access-date=November 17, 2021|archive-date=November 17, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211117031052/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=015&aid=0004629531|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
===2014–2017: Debut with ''Girls' Invasion'' and career beginnings===
{{Main|Lovelyz}}
Mijoo debuted with Lovelyz, which was first announced on November 5, 2014 by Woollim Entertainment.<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.newspim.com/view.jsp?newsId=20141103000080&fromurl=na|title = 울림 첫 걸그룹 ′러블리즈′ 데뷔 임박, 10일 음원 선공개로 ′출사표′|last = Yang|first = Jinyeong|date = 2014-11-03|access-date = January 1, 2020|archive-date = October 21, 2020|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20201021171302/http://www.newspim.com/view.jsp?newsId=20141103000080&fromurl=na|url-status = live}}</ref> Lovelyz released a pre-release digital single, "Good Night Like Yesterday", on November 5, 2014. Their debut showcase was held on November 12 at the K-ART Hall in Olympic Park, and their debut stage was on the next day on [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]] ''[[M Countdown]]''.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Hong|first1=Grace Danbi|title=Lovelyz Wishes You a 'Good Night Like Yesterday' in Debut MV|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/80323/video-lovelyz-wishes-you-a-good-night-like-yesterday-in-debut-mv|accessdate=November 25, 2014|work=[[CJ E&M]] enewsWorld|date=November 10, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141122181207/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/80323/video-lovelyz-wishes-you-a-good-night-like-yesterday-in-debut-mv|archive-date=November 22, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> The group's debut studio album ''[[Girls' Invasion]]'' was officially released on November 17, along with its lead single titled "Candy Jelly Love".<ref>{{cite web|title = Lovelyz drops debut album amid controversy|url = http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20141117000884|date = 2014-11-17|access-date = January 1, 2020|archive-date = October 22, 2020|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20201022213214/http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20141117000884|url-status = live}}</ref>

Mijoo made a cameo in ''[[The Gentlemen of Wolgyesu Tailor Shop]]'' in 2016. She participated in the [[dance]] survival show ''[[Hit the Stage]]''. She placed 6th by the end of episode 8 and was eventually eliminated. She was also a host in ''[[Inkigayo]]'' alongside bandmate [[Kei (singer)|Kei]] and [[BTS (band)|BTS]] members [[Kim Seok-jin|Jin]] and [[RM (rapper)|RM]].<ref name="inkigayo" /> Mijoo was cast in the television series ''[[:ko:나는 취준생이다|I'm a Job Seeker]]'', playing the role as Na Young-hee.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/213/0000903314|title=[T포토] 나는 취준생이다 "모든 분들을 응원합니다!"|date=August 30, 2016|work=Naver|access-date=January 1, 2019|language=ko|archive-date=January 1, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101153329/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/213/0000903314|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/421/0002240610|title=러블리즈 이미주, 웹드라마 '나는 취준생이다' 출연|date=August 30, 2016|work=Naver|access-date=January 1, 2019|language=ko|archive-date=January 1, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200101153420/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/421/0002240610|url-status=live}}</ref>
