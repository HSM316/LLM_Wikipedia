'''''Dirty Secrets of the Black Hand''''' is a 1994 role-playing game supplement for ''[[Vampire: The Masquerade]]'' published by [[White Wolf Publishing]].

==Contents==
''Dirty Secrets of the Black Hand'' explores the secrets of the Black Hand, and its history and purposes, the oldest covenant of vampires in the [[World of Darkness]].<ref>https://index.rpg.net/display-entry.phtml?mainid=4018</ref>
