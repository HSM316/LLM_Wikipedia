'''''Dirty Secrets of the Black Hand''''' is a supplement  published by [[White Wolf Publishing]] in 1994 for the modern-day horror role-playing game ''[[Vampire: The Masquerade]]''.

==Contents==
''Dirty Secrets of the Black Hand'', by Steven C. Brown, explores the Black Hand, the oldest vampire covenant, and its history and goals.{{cn|date=June 2020}} The book also details new spells and powers.<ref name=dragon223 />

==Reception==
In the November 1995 edition of ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon]]'' (Issue #223), [[Rick Swan]] commented, "Grisly and humorless, ''Black Hand'' is the role-playing equivalent of a splatter movie — hard-core players should take that as a recommendation."<ref name=dragon223>{{cite journal|last=Swan|first=Rick|authorlink=Rick Swan| date=November 1995 |title=Roleplaying Reviews|journal=[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon]]|publisher=[[TSR (company)|TSR, Inc.]]|issue=223|pages=46}}</ref>
