'''Oko Aryee''' (born 18 January 1941) was a Ghanaian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}[[Oko Aryee]]</ref> He died on 12 August 2024 due to a brief illness.{{Citation needed|date=September 2024}}

==Career==
Aryee was the head coach of the [[Ghana women's national football team|Ghana women's national team]] at the [[2003 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
