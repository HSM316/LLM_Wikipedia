'''Elston Ridgle''' (born August 24, 1963) is a former [[American football]] [[defensive end]]. He played for the [[San Francisco 49ers]] in 1987, the [[Buffalo Bills]] and [[Seattle Seahawks]] in 1989, the [[Arizona Cardinals|Phoenix Cardinals]] in 1990 and the [[Cincinnati Bengals]] in 1992.<ref>https://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/R/RidgEl20.htm</ref><ref>http://www.nfl.com/player/elstonridgle/2502663/profile</ref>
