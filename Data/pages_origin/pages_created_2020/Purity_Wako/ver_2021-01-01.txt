{{short description|Ugandan life coach}}
'''Purity Kategaya Wako''' is a [[Ugandan people|Ugandan]] [[life coach]], [[nutritionist]] and activist.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvULVvzw2FY|title=AUTHENTIC CHAT WITH PRAISE ATWINE hosting PURITY WAKO AND GLORIA AKETCH|via=www.youtube.com}}</ref>

Wako was born in [[Ibanda]] and studied at [[Makerere University Business School]]. She currently lives in [[Kampala]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/purity.wako|title=Purity Wako|website=www.facebook.com}}</ref>

As a ''senga'', she advises new wives on how to please their husbands, but expands the role, wanting Ugandan women to have equal rights in marriage.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.scoopnest.com/user/BBCAfrica/1184469378768744448-purity-wako-is-a-life-coach-from-ugandashe-aims-to-help-ugandan-women-feel-empowered-in-their-relati|title=Purity Wako life coach Uganda aims Ugandan women feel empowered relationships calling women legal rights marriage vision future led women|website=Scoopnest}}</ref><ref>{{Cite tweet|user=bbcafrica|number=1184469378768744448|title=Purity Wako is a life coach from Uganda. She aims to help Ugandan women feel empowered in their relationships and is calling for all women to have legal rights in marriage. Here's her vision of a future led by women: pic.twitter.com/l1mQyQZYRT|author=BBC News Africa|date=October 16, 2019}}</ref> She founded her own company, KweraBITS, in 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.linkedin.com/checkpoint/challengesV2/AQEnYMHych_PfwAAAW9jCFBtI9KqEdcIXkamOt7CIpPxBkxd_u1bbMN6svkHFxmqnCl6o9LBflHmZ5zhxJV8Bld-xcYb0mspKg|title=Security Verification &#124; LinkedIn|website=www.linkedin.com}}</ref>

In 2019, she was listed among the BBC's [[100 Women (BBC)|100 Women]], a list of 100 inspiring and influential women.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.africaglobalradio.com/bbc-100-women-2019-19/|title=BBC 100 Women 2019|website=Africa Global Radio|date=December 3, 2019}}</ref>
