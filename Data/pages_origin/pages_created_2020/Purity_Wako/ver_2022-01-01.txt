{{short description|Ugandan life coach}}
'''Purity Kategaya Wako''' is a [[Ugandan people|Ugandan]] transformational coach, [[nutritionist]] and activist. Also a member of International coach federation and the of C.E.O KweraBits.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvULVvzw2FY|title=AUTHENTIC CHAT WITH PRAISE ATWINE hosting PURITY WAKO AND GLORIA AKETCH|via=www.youtube.com}}</ref>

Purity was born in [[Ibanda]] She currently lives in [[Kampala]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/purity.wako|title=Purity Wako|website=www.facebook.com}}</ref>

As a ''modern senga'', she advises women on relationships, but expands the role, wanting African women to have equal rights in marriage.<<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.scoopnest.com/user/BBCAfrica/1184469378768744448-purity-wako-is-a-life-coach-from-ugandashe-aims-to-help-ugandan-women-feel-empowered-in-their-relati|title=Purity Wako is a life coach from Uganda|website=Scoopnest}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/bbcafrica/status/1184469378768744448?lang=en|title=Purity Wako is a life coach from Uganda|first=BBC News|last=Africa|date=October 16, 2019}}</ref> She founded her own company, KweraBITS for women's health, beauty and counselling, in 2013 and she has transformed thousands of lives especially women.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.linkedin.com/checkpoint/challengesV2/AQEnYMHych_PfwAAAW9jCFBtI9KqEdcIXkamOt7CIpPxBkxd_u1bbMN6svkHFxmqnCl6o9LBflHmZ5zhxJV8Bld-xcYb0mspKg|title=Security Verification &#124; LinkedIn|website=www.linkedin.com}}</ref>

In 2019, she was listed among the BBC's [[100 Women (BBC)|100 Women]], a list of 100 inspiring and influential women.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.africaglobalradio.com/bbc-100-women-2019-19/|title=BBC 100 Women 2019|website=Africa Global Radio|date=December 3, 2019}}</ref>
