{{short description|Ugandan life coach}}
'''Purity Kategaya Wako''' is a [[Ugandan people|Ugandan]] [[life coach]], [[nutritionist]] and activist.<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvULVvzw2FY</ref>

Wako was born in [[Ibanda]] and studied at [[Makerere University Business School]]. She currently lives in [[Kampala]].<ref>https://www.facebook.com/purity.wako/about?section=overview&lst=100000334765112%3A100000016944489%3A1577913990</ref>

As a ''senga'', she advises new wives on how to please their husbands, but expands the role, wanting Ugandan women to have equal rights in marriage.<ref>https://www.scoopnest.com/user/BBCAfrica/1184469378768744448-purity-wako-is-a-life-coach-from-ugandashe-aims-to-help-ugandan-women-feel-empowered-in-their-relati</ref><ref>https://twitter.com/bbcafrica/status/1184469378768744448?lang=en</ref> She founded her own company, KweraBITS, in 2013.<ref>https://www.linkedin.com/in/purity-wako-5973b98a/?originalSubdomain=ug</ref>

In 2019, she was listed among the BBC's [[100 Women (BBC)|100 Women]], a list of 100 inspiring and influential women.<ref>http://www.africaglobalradio.com/bbc-100-women-2019-19/</ref>
