'''Syed Arshad Ali''' (born 10 April 1971) is a Pakistani [[jurist]] who has been Justice of the [[Peshawar High Court]] since 16 June 2017.

==Early life and education==
Ali was born in [[Swabi District]] on 10 April 1971. He got his [[LLB]] from Khyber Law College associated with [[University of Peshawar]] in 1993.<ref name="PHC">{{cite web|url=https://www.peshawarhighcourt.gov.pk/image_bank/Downloads/profile%20of%20Mr.%20Justice%20Arshad%20Ali.pdf|title=Mr. Justice Arshad Ali|website=www.peshawarhighcourt.gov.pk|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref><ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1340021|title=Three PHC additional judges take oath|first=Bureau|last=Report|date=June 17, 2017|website=DAWN.COM}}</ref>

==Judicial career==
He registered as an advocate of High Court in 1996. Until 2007, he worked with "Afridi, Shah and Minallah", a law firm located in [[Peshawar]]. Later, he started his own firm in Peshawar and named it "Syed Arshad Ali and Associates". In 2012, he started practicing as an advocate of Supreme Court. He became [[Supreme Court Bar Association of Pakistan|Supreme Court Bar Association]]'s member for life. He remained member Peshawar High Court Bar Association and member of Peshawar Bar Association, District Courts. As an advocate, he specialized representing banks, hospitals, and universities. He joined Peshawar High Court (PHC) as an additional judge on 16 June 2017.<ref name="PHC"/><ref name="auto"/> He became permanent member of the PHC bench on 31 May 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pakistantoday.com.pk/2019/05/31/three-phc-judges-take-oath/amp/|title=Three PHC judges take oath &#124; Pakistan Today|website=www.pakistantoday.com.pk}}</ref>
