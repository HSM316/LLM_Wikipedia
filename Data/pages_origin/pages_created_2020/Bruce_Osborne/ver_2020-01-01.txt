'''Bruce H. Osborne''' (August 27, 1924 – November 23, 2008) was an [[American football]] player and coach. He served as the first head football coach at [[Southern Utah University]] from 1963 to 1964.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://issuu.com/suuathletics/docs/2012suufbmediaguide/51|title=Football Media Guide|year=2012|publisher=[[Southern Utah Thunderbirds]]|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref> He played college football at [[Utah State University]] and [[Brigham Young University]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.legacy.com/obituaries/saltlaketribune/obituary.aspx?n=bruce-h-osborne&pid=120684663|title=Bruce H. Osborne|publisher=legacy.com|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref>
