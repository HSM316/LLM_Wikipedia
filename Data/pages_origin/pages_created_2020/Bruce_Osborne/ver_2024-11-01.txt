'''Bruce H. Osborne''' (August 27, 1924 – November 23, 2008) was an [[American football]] player and coach. He was the first head football coach at [[Southern Utah University]], serving from 1963 to 1964 and compiling a record of 8–5–1.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://issuu.com/suuathletics/docs/2012suufbmediaguide/51|title=Football Media Guide|year=2012|publisher=[[Southern Utah Thunderbirds]]|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref> Osborne played [[college football]] at [[Utah State University]] and [[Brigham Young University]] (BYU).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.legacy.com/obituaries/saltlaketribune/obituary.aspx?n=bruce-h-osborne&pid=120684663|title=Bruce H. Osborne|publisher=legacy.com|access-date=December 31, 2019}}</ref>

Osborne was a member of [[the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints]]. He served in the United States Marine Corps in World War II in the South Pacific.<ref>[https://www.legacy.com/us/obituaries/saltlaketribune/name/bruce-osborne-obituary?pid=120684663 ''Salt Lake Tribune'' obituary of Osborne]</ref>
