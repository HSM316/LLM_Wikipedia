'''Dimitrios Bakochristos''' is a Greek Paralympic powerlifter.<ref name="paralympic_bio">{{Cite web|url=https://www.paralympic.org/dimitrios-bakochristos|title=Dimitrios Bakochristos|website=paralympic.org|url-status=live|access-date=1 January 2020|publisher=[[International Paralympic Committee]]}}</ref> He represented [[Greece at the 2016 Summer Paralympics]] and he won the bronze medal in the [[Powerlifting at the 2016 Summer Paralympics – Men's 54 kg|men's 54 kg]] event.<ref name="paralympic_bio"/> Bakochristos lifted 162 kg, the same as [[Bruno Carra]] who represented Brazil, and Bakochristos won the medal as his body weight was lower.<ref name="mexico_city_2017_top_lifters">{{Cite news|url=https://www.paralympic.org/news/mexico-city-2017-top-lifters-americas|title=Mexico City 2017: Top lifters from the Americas|date=24 August 2017|work=[[International Paralympic Committee]]|access-date=1 January 2020|url-status=live}}</ref>

At the [[2019 World Para Powerlifting Championships]] he won the bronze medal in the men's 54 kg event.<ref name="nur_sultan_sherif_osman_2019">{{Cite news|url=https://www.paralympic.org/news/nur-sultan-2019-sherif-osman-enhances-his-legend|title=Nur-Sultan 2019: Sherif Osman enhances his legend|date=14 July 2019|work=[[International Paralympic Committee]]|access-date=1 January 2020|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="rahman_to_watch_world_para_2019">{{Cite news|url=https://www.insidethegames.biz/articles/1081983/world-para-powerlifting-champs-starting|title=Rahman among ones to watch at World Para Powerlifting Championships|last=Etchells|first=Daniel|date=12 July 2019|work=InsideTheGames.biz|access-date=1 January 2020|url-status=live}}</ref>
