'''Agha Faisal''' (born 8 November 1972) is a Pakistani [[jurist]] who has been Justice of the [[Sindh High Court]] since 6 February 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1387760|title=SHC judges sworn in|first=The Newspaper's Staff|last=Reporter|date=February 7, 2018|website=DAWN.COM}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Agha was born on 8 November 1972 in [[Karachi]].<ref name="auto"/> He holds a B.Sc. in [[finance]] from [[Miami University]] and an LLB (Hons) from the [[University of Buckingham]].<ref name="auto"/>

==Career==
Agha was called to the Bar of England and Wales by [[Lincoln's Inn]] and began his legal practice in 1998.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://sindhhighcourt.gov.pk/Justice_Mr_Agha_Faisal.php|title=Welcome to High Court of Sindh|website=sindhhighcourt.gov.pk}}</ref> Prior to his judicial appointment, he taught as an lecturer at [[Sindh Muslim Law College]].<ref name="auto"/> He was admitted to the Supreme Court bar in 2012.<ref name="auto"/>

Agha was appointed to as a justice of the [[Sindh High Court]] on February 6, 2018.<ref name="auto"/> As a jurist, he has served on the Senate Tribunal Sindh for the 2021 elections and the Sindh Subordinate Judiciary Service Tribunal.<ref name="auto"/> He has also been part of the High Court's Selection Committee for Promotion for Grade 16 and above, and committees focused on development, building maintenance, and horticulture.<ref name="auto"/>
