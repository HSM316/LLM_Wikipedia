Events from [[2020]] in the [[European Union]].

== Events ==

=== January ===

* 31 January – The [[United Kingdom]] and [[Gibraltar]] left the [[European Union]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Brexit: UK leaves the European Union |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-51333314 |work=BBC News |publisher=BBC |date=31 January 2019}}</ref>
=== November ===

* 17 November – [[Hungary]] and [[Poland]] veto the seven-year EU budget, over attempts to link funding to respect for rule of law. They both said that linking funding to rule of law will mean a loss to their nation's sovereignty.<ref>https://www.theguardian.com/world/2020/nov/16/eu-hungary-veto-budget-viktor-orban</ref><ref>https://www.aa.com.tr/en/economy/hungary-poland-veto-eu-budget-deal/2045854</ref>
=== December ===

* 17 December – [[Court of Justice of the European Union|The European Court of Justice]] rules that EU states can ban kosher and halal ritual slaughter. <ref>https://www.politico.eu/article/eu-states-can-ban-kosher-and-halal-ritual-slaughter-court-rules</ref> <ref>https://www.i24news.tv/en/news/international/europe/1608201127-european-court-of-justice-upholds-ban-on-kosher-halal-slaughter-in-belgium</ref>
