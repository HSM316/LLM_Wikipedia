'''Wolfgang Winkler''' (2 March 1943 – 7 December 2019) was a German actor. Born in [[Görlitz]], he was best known for starring in films such as ''[[The Rabbit Is Me]]'', ''[[Das Mädchen auf dem Brett]]'' and ''[[I Was Nineteen]]'', as well as playing  Hauptkommissar Herbert Schneider in the television series [[Polizeiruf 110]].<ref>[https://www.mz-web.de/halle-saale/wolfgang-winkler---76--hallescher-polizeiruf-star-ist-gestorben-33582700 Wolfgang Winkler (†76) Hallescher Polizeiruf-Star ist gestorben] {{in lang|de}}</ref>
