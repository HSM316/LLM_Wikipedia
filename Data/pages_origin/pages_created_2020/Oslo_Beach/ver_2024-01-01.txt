'''Oslo Beach''' is a small coastal suburb located along the [[South Coast (KwaZulu-Natal)|South Coast]] of [[KwaZulu-Natal]], [[South Africa]] and forms part of the coastal town of [[Port Shepstone]].

== Geography ==
Oslo Beach lies just 3 kilometres (1.9 mi) south of the Port Shepstone [[central business district]] (CBD) and lies nestled between [[Port Shepstone]] to the north and [[Shelly Beach, South Africa|Shelly Beach]] to the north.

== Facilities ==
Oslo Beach is host to two schools, Creston College <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.curro.co.za/ |title=Independent School Network |website=www.curro.co.za |access-date=2020-01-01}}{{failed verification|date=June 2022}}</ref> and Suid-Natal Primere Skool<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.africanadvice.com/1363266/Schools/Kwazulu_Natal/Suid-natal_Primere_Skool/|title=üòÄüò°üòá Suid-natal Primere Skool, Schools, Kwazulu Natal, ...039 682 6|website=www.africanadvice.com|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> which is an [[Afrikaans]] school. Most schools in the [[KwaZulu-Natal South Coast|Lower South Coast]] are in Port Shepstone and Margate.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.southcoastdirectory.co.za/home/education/primary-schools|title=Primary Schools - South Coast Directory|website=www.southcoastdirectory.co.za|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.southcoastdirectory.co.za/home/education/high-schools|title=High Schools - South Coast Directory|website=www.southcoastdirectory.co.za|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

Oslo Beach is largely a residential area with no retail facilities present. However, the nearest malls to Oslo Beach are in close proximity,  either in the Port Shepstone CBD or Shelly Beach.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.southcoastdirectory.co.za/home/shopping-centres|title=Shopping Centres - South Coast Directory|website=www.southcoastdirectory.co.za|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

In terms of medical facilities, the nearest hospitals are in the Port Shepstone CBD including the Port Shepstone Regional Hospital and Hibiscus Hospital Port Shepstone.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.southcoastdirectory.co.za/home/health-and-fitness/hospitals|title=Hospitals - South Coast Directory|website=www.southcoastdirectory.co.za|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>
