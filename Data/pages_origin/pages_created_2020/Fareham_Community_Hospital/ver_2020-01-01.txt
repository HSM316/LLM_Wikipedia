'''Fareham Community Hospital''' is a healthcare facility at Brook Lane in [[Sarisbury]], [[Hampshire]], England. It is managed by the [[Southern Health NHS Foundation Trust]].

==History==
The facility was procured under a [[Private Finance Initiative]] contract in 2007.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/13844527.jeremy-hunt-will-visit-fareham-community-hospital-after-mp-suella-fernandes-asked-him-to-take-action-over-service-provision/|title=Jeremy Hunt will visit Fareham Community Hospital after MP Suella Fernandes asked him to take action over service provision|publisher=Southern Daily Echo|date=13 October 2015|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> It was built by [[Morgan Sindall Group]] at a cost of Â£10 million and opened in 2010.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.heart.co.uk/southcoast/news/local/new-community-hospital-opens/|title=New Community Hospital Opens|date=24 May 2010|publisher=Heart FM|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref> In 2018 it was estimated that its rooms were only being used 40% of each day and, as such, the hospital was not being used to its full potential.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.portsmouth.co.uk/news/now-is-the-time-to-get-more-use-out-of-fareham-community-hospital-1-8619863|title='Now is the time to get more use out of Fareham Community Hospital'|publisher=Portsmouth News|date=31 August 2018|accessdate=31 December 2019}}</ref>
