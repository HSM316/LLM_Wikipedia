'''''Paragymnopteris''''' is a genus of [[fern]]s in the subfamily [[Cheilanthoideae]] of the family [[Pteridaceae]].<ref name=PPGI/> Species of ''Paragymnopteris'' are native to the [[Old World]].<ref name=CFLW/>

==Species==
{{As of|2020|January}}, the ''Checklist of Ferns and Lycophytes of the World'' recognized the following species:<ref name=CFLW/>
*''[[Paragymnopteris bipinnata]]'' <small>(Christ) K.H.Shing</small>
*''[[Paragymnopteris borealisinensis]]'' <small>(Kitag.) comb. ined.</small>
*''[[Paragymnopteris delavayi]]'' <small>(Baker) K.H.Shing</small>
*''[[Paragymnopteris marantae]]'' <small>(L.) K.H.Shing</small>
*''[[Paragymnopteris sargentii]]'' <small>(Christ) K.H.Shing</small>
*''[[Paragymnopteris vestita]]'' <small>(Wall. ex C.Presl) K.H.Shing</small>
