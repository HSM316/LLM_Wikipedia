'''Alexandra Eala''' (born 23 May 2005) is a [[Philippines|Filipino]] [[tennis]] player.

Eala has a career high [[International Tennis Federation|ITF]] junior combined ranking of 11 achieved on 09 December 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.itftennis.com/en/players/alexandra-eala/800511432/phi/jt/s/overview#pprofile-info-tabs|title=Alexandra Eala|first=|last=|date=1 January 2020|publisher=|via=www.itftennis.com}}</ref>
