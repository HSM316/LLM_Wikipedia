'''Cecilia Domeniguini Coccolo''' (born 22 September 1991) is a Uruguayan [[women's association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for Peñarol Colonia. She has been a member of the [[Uruguay women's national football team|Uruguay women's national team]].

==Club career==
Domeniguini played in Uruguay for [[Colón F.C.|Colón]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/245/0/|title=Colón Fútbol Club - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=30 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/245/4/|title=Colón Fútbol Club - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=30 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/245/1/|title=Colón Fútbol Club - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=30 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/historico-planteles/245/5/|title=Colón Fútbol Club - Femenino|website=AUF|language=es|accessdate=30 December 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
Domeniguini played for Uruguay at senior level in the [[2014 Copa América Femenina]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/con-una-llamativa-ceremonia-se-inauguro-la-vii-copa-america-femenina|title=Con una llamativa ceremonia, se inauguró la VII Copa América Femenina|website=Conmebol|accessdate=30 December 2019|date=11 September 2014|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140914022911/http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/copa-america-femenina-colombia-golea-uruguay-4-0|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/copa-america-femenina-colombia-golea-uruguay-4-0|title=Copa América Femenina: Colombia golea a Uruguay 4-0|website=Conmebol|accessdate=30 December 2019|archive-date=14 September 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140917021753/http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/uruguay-levanta-cabeza-ante-peru-2-1|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/uruguay-levanta-cabeza-ante-peru-2-1|title=Uruguay levanta cabeza ante Perú (2-1)|language=es|website=Conmebol|accessdate=30 December 2019|archive-date=17 September 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/content/ecuador-clasifica-los-juegos-panamericanos-y-la-fase-final-de-la-copa-america-femenina|title=Ecuador clasifica a los Juegos Panamericanos y a la Fase Final de la Copa América Femenina|website=Conmebol|accessdate=30 December 2019|date=19 September 2014|language=es}}</ref>
