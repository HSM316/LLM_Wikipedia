The '''2019–20 FAO Women's League''' was the 7th edition of the [[FAO Women's League]]. [[Sports Authority of India|SAI-STC Cuttack]] were the defending champions. The FAO Women's League (FWL) is organised every year by the [[Football Association of Odisha]] (FAO), the official football governing body of [[Odisha]], [[India]]. The regular season started on 20 February 2019 and ended on 9 March 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://orisports.com/NewsDetails.aspx?nId=NTc5NQ==|title=FAO Women League: SAI Training Centre crowned champs|work=Orisports|date=28 August 2011|accessdate=8 May 2019}}</ref> Sangeeta Sharma, Joint Secretary ([[Football Association of Odisha|FAO]]), and Geetanjali Khuntia (Ex-Indian International) were the chief guests in the inauguration ceremony.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/permalink.php?story_fbid=2757384300990838&id=225325417530085&__tn__=-R|title=FAO Women's League 2019|work=[[Football Association of Odisha]]|date=27 November 2019|accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref> On 30 December 2019, [[East Coast Railway zone|East Coast Railway]] were crowned the champions, garnering fifteen points from eight matches.
