'''''Apple Penne''''' ({{translation|Apple girl}}) is a 2013 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film directed by [[R. K. Kalaimani]]. The film stars [[Vatsan Chakravarthy|Vatsan]] and [[Iswarya Menon|Aishwarya Menon]], while [[Roja Selvamani|Roja]] plays a pivotal role.

== Cast ==
*[[Vatsan Chakravarthy|Vatsan]] as James
*[[Iswarya Menon|Aishwarya Menon]] as Komalavalli 
*[[Roja Selvamani|Roja]] as Hamsavalli
*[[Thambi Ramaiah]] as Constable Arumugam
*[[Suresh (actor, born 1963)|Suresh]]
*Deva
*Suchitra
*KG Pandian
