'''''Apple Penne''''' (English: Apple Girl) is a 2013 Tamil romantic drama film directed by [[R. K. Kalaimani]]. The film stars [[Vatsan Chakravarthy|Vatsan]] and [[Iswarya Menon|Aishwarya Menon]] in the lead roles while [[Roja (actress)|Roja]] plays a pivotal role.

*[[Thambi Ramaiah]] as Constable Arumugam
