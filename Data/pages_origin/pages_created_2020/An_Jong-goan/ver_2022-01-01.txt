'''An Jong-goan''' ({{lang-ko|안종관}}; born 30 August 1966) is a South Korean [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
An was the head coach of the [[South Korea women's national football team|South Korea women's national team]] at the [[2003 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
