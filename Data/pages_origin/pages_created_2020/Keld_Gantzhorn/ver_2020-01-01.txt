'''Keld Gantzhorn Knudsen''' (born 3 October 1954) is a Danish former [[Association football|footballer]] and [[Manager (association football)|manager]].<ref>{{Soccerway}}</ref>

==Career==
Gantzhorn played for [[Odense Boldklub|Odense]], and appeared for the team in the [[1978–79 European Cup]] on 27 September 1978 against Bulgarian club [[FC Lokomotiv 1929 Sofia|Lokomotiv Sofia]], which finished as a 1–2 away loss.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/champions-league-1978-1979-1-runde-lokomotiv-sofia-odense-bk/ |title=Champions League 1978/1979 » 1. Round » Lokomotiv Sofia – Odense BK 2:1 |website=WorldFootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |access-date=31 December 2019}}</ref> He later began coaching, and was the manager of the [[Denmark women's national football team|Denmark women's national team]] from 1988 until 1996. He coached the team at the [[1991 FIFA Women's World Cup]], [[1995 FIFA Women's World Cup]] and [[Football at the 1996 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|1996 Summer Olympics]].

==Personal life==
Gantzhorn was born in {{ill|Gamby|da}} in the [[Nordfyn Municipality]]. He studied to become a [[teacher]], and currently works at the Hjalleseskolen in [[Odense]]. He lives in the {{ill|lt=Dalum|Dalum, Odense|da}} district of Odense with his wife Rita, and has four children.<ref>{{cite magazine |last=Kaarsted |first=Hanne |url=http://dalumhjallese.livebook.dk/dalumhjallese_DHA180209AA/DHA180209AA.pdf |title=Lokal profil |trans-title=Local profile |magazine=Dalum-Hjallese Avis |volume=2 |number=8 |date=18 February 2009 |access-date=31 December 2019 |format=PDF |page=11 |language=Danish |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191231150235/http://dalumhjallese.livebook.dk/dalumhjallese_DHA180209AA/DHA180209AA.pdf |archive-date=31 December 2019}}</ref>
