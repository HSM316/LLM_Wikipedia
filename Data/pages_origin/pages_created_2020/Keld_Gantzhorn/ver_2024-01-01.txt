'''Keld Gantzhorn Knudsen''' (born 3 October 1954) is a Danish former [[Association football|footballer]] and [[Manager (association football)|manager]].<ref>{{Soccerway|keld-gantzhorn-knudsen/727293|Keld Gantzhorn Knudsen}}</ref><ref>{{Soccerway coach|keld-gantzhorn/292373|Keld Gantzhorn}}</ref>

==Career==
Gantzhorn played for [[Odense Boldklub|Odense]], and appeared for the team in the [[1978–79 European Cup]] on 27 September 1978 against Bulgarian club [[FC Lokomotiv 1929 Sofia|Lokomotiv Sofia]], which finished as a 1–2 away loss.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/champions-league-1978-1979-1-runde-lokomotiv-sofia-odense-bk/ |title=Champions League 1978/1979 » 1. Round » Lokomotiv Sofia – Odense BK 2:1 |website=WorldFootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |access-date=31 December 2019}}</ref> He later began coaching, and was the manager of the [[Denmark women's national football team|Denmark women's national team]] from 1988 until 1996. He coached the team at the [[1991 FIFA Women's World Cup]], [[1995 FIFA Women's World Cup]] and [[Football at the 1996 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament|1996 Summer Olympics]].

Under the leadership of Keld Gantzhorn, the team won a bronze medal during the European Championships in Denmark in 1991 by winning 2–1 over Italy in the match of 3–4. place, after losing to Norway in the semifinals after rematch and penalty shootout.
Likewise, the team won a bronze medal at the European Championships in Italy in 1993 by beating the defending European champions Germany 3–1. Here too, Denmark had lost the semifinal to Norway 1–0.

In addition to the national coaching job for women, Gantzhorn coached male teams from OB (U-18), Od. KFUM, Næsby and Dalum.

==Personal life==
Gantzhorn was born in {{ill|Gamby|da}} in the [[Nordfyn Municipality]]. He studied to become a [[teacher]], and currently works at the Hjalleseskolen in [[Odense]]. He lives in the {{ill|lt=Dalum|Dalum, Odense|da}} district of Odense with his wife Rita, and has four children.<ref>{{cite magazine |last=Kaarsted |first=Hanne |url=http://dalumhjallese.livebook.dk/dalumhjallese_DHA180209AA/DHA180209AA.pdf |title=Lokal profil |trans-title=Local profile |magazine=Dalum-Hjallese Avis |volume=2 |number=8 |date=18 February 2009 |access-date=31 December 2019 |format=PDF |page=11 |language=Danish |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191231150235/http://dalumhjallese.livebook.dk/dalumhjallese_DHA180209AA/DHA180209AA.pdf |archive-date=31 December 2019}}</ref> He was diagnosed with [[Parkinson's disease]] in 2012.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Nielsen |first1=Steffen |title=– Mange vil nok kalde mig jubelidiot: Ti år med parkinson har ikke slået Keld ud - nu har han fået en pris for sit arbejde for ligestillede |url=https://fyens.dk/navne/mange-vil-nok-kalde-mig-jubelidiot-ti-aar-med-parkinson-har-ikke-slaaet-keld-ud-nu-har-han-faaet-en-pris-for-sit-arbejde-for-ligestillede |access-date=8 October 2023 |work=Fyens Stiftstidende |date=17 December 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231008185020/https://fyens.dk/navne/mange-vil-nok-kalde-mig-jubelidiot-ti-aar-med-parkinson-har-ikke-slaaet-keld-ud-nu-har-han-faaet-en-pris-for-sit-arbejde-for-ligestillede |archive-date=8 October 2023 |language=da}}</ref>
