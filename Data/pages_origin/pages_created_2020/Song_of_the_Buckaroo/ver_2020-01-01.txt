'''''Song of the Buckaroo''''' is a 1938 American [[Western (genre)|Western]] film directed by [[Albert Herman]] and written by [[John Rathmell]]. The film stars [[Tex Ritter]], [[Jinx Falkenburg]], Mary Ruth, [[Tom London]], Frank LaRue and [[Charles King (character actor)|Charles King]]. The film was released on December 7, 1938, by [[Monogram Pictures]].<ref>http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/90855/Song-of-the-Buckaroo/</ref><ref>https://www.allmovie.com/movie/song-of-the-buckaroo-v111044</ref><ref>https://catalog.afi.com/Film/5472-SONG-OFTHEBUCKAROO</ref>
