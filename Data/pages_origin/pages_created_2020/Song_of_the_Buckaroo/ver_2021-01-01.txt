'''''Song of the Buckaroo''''' is a 1938 American [[Western (genre)|Western]] film directed by [[Albert Herman]] and written by John Rathmell. The film stars [[Tex Ritter]], [[Jinx Falkenburg]], Mary Ruth, [[Tom London]], Frank LaRue and [[Charles King (character actor)|Charles King]]. The film was released on December 7, 1938, by [[Monogram Pictures]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/90855/Song-of-the-Buckaroo/ |title=Song of the Buckaroo (1938) - Overview |publisher=TCM.com |date= |accessdate=2020-01-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Hans J. ==Plot==
In order to escape the law, a bandit takes the identity of a dead man, when is dead alter ego gets elected as mayor he decides to change his outlaw ways.
