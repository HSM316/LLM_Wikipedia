'''Ivan Andreyevich Yakimushkin''' ({{lang-ru|Иван Андреевич Якимушкин}}; born 17 June 1996) is a Russian [[Cross-country skiing (sport)|cross-country skier]].

==Career==
Ivan Yakimushkin was born 17 June 1996 to a sportive family. He started cross-country skiing from the age of eight, his first coach was Galina Kabanova. His father Andrey Vladimirovich replaced her when Ivan was 12. Yakimushkin first trained in [[Murom]], then in [[Tyumen]].

At the [[2015 Nordic Junior World Ski Championships]] in [[Almaty]], Yakimushkin won silver in 10&nbsp;km classical/freestyle skiathlon and gold as part of the relay team. A year later he won another gold medal, now in 15&nbsp;km freestyle. His relay team finished second.

He debuted in the [[2017–18 FIS Cross-Country World Cup|2017–18 FIS World Cup season]], in the sprint freestyle Stage World Cup in [[Lenzerheide]], being part of the [[2017–18 Tour de Ski]]. In the overall standings he was placed 21.

Yakimushkin won his first national championships title in [[Syktyvkar]], in 15&nbsp;km freestyle.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://teamrussia.pro/sportsman/yakimushkin-ivan-andreevich/|title=Якимушкин Иван Андреевич|publisher=Team Russia|language=ru|accessdate=4 March 2019}}</ref>

At the [[2019 Winter Universiade]] in [[Krasnoyarsk]], Yakimushkin won two gold medals, in 10&nbsp;km classical and 10&nbsp;km freestyle pursuit.

Yakimushkin has made a major breakthrough in [[2020–21 FIS Cross-Country World Cup|2020-2021]]. After scoring three individual podiums and showing solid performance in the [[2021 Tour de Ski|Tour de Ski]] (4th place) he finished second in both overall and distance standings of the World Cup only behind his teammate [[Alexander Bolshunov]]. 
 
In an interview to the Russian Olympic Channel in February 2021 Yakimushkin said he has no special pre-race rituals or habits, underlined key role of his family in his athletic upbringing and career, and named [[Alexey Prokurorov]] as the only sportsperson he could call a kind of a childhood idol.<ref>[https://olympics.com/ru/news/%D0%B8%D0%B2%D0%B0%D0%BD-%D1%8F%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%BC%D1%83%D1%88%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%BD-%D0%BB%D1%8B%D0%B6%D0%B8-%D0%BF%D0%B5%D1%80%D0%B5%D0%B4-%D0%B3%D0%BE%D0%BD%D0%BA%D0%BE%D0%B8-%D0%BD%D0%B5-%D1%86%D0%B5%D0%BB%D1%83%D1%8E Иван Якимушкин: «Лыжи перед гонкой не целую»]</ref>
  
Yakimushkin is a student in the Oil Faculty at the Tyumen Industrial University.
