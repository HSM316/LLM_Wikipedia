The 2020 cycling season for {{ct|BOH|2020}} will begin in January at the [[2020 Tour Down Under|Tour Down Under]] in Australia.

As a [[UCI WorldTeam]], they are automatically invited and obliged to send a squad to every event in the [[2020 UCI World Tour|UCI World Tour]].
