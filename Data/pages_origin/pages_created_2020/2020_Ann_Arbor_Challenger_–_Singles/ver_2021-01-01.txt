This was the first edition of the tournament.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.clickondetroit.com/all-about-ann-arbor/2019/11/22/mens-professional-tennis-tournament-coming-to-ann-arbor-in-january/|title=Men’s professional tennis tournament coming to Ann Arbor in January|first=Meredith|last=Bruckner|date=November 22, 2019|website=WDIV}}</ref>

[[Ulises Blanch]] won the title after defeating [[Roberto Cid Subervi]] 3–6, 6–4, 6–2 in the final.
