'''Sergio Guenza''' (born 9 January 1933) is an Italian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Guenza was the head coach of the [[Italy women's national football team|Italy women's national team]] at the [[1991 FIFA Women's World Cup]].
