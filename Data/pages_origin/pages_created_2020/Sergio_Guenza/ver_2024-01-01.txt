'''Sergio Guenza''' (9 January 1933 – 6 April 2020) was an Italian [[Manager (association football)|football manager]].<ref>{{WorldFootball.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Guenza was the head coach of the [[Italy women's national football team|Italy women's national team]] in 1979–1981, 1989–1993 and 1995–1997,<ref>[https://www.footballdatabase.eu/en/player/details/410557-sergio-guenza Footballdatabase]</ref> including at the [[1991 FIFA Women's World Cup]].

==Personal life==
Guenza died on 6 April 2020 at the age of 87.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://sport.sky.it/calcio/femminile/2020/04/06/morto-sergio-guenza |title=Morto Sergio Guenza: ha allenato la Lazio e la Nazionale femminile |trans-title=Sergio Guenza is dead: he coached Lazio and the women's national team |work=[[Sky Sport (Italy)|Sky Sport]] |date=6 April 2020 |access-date=6 April 2020 |language=it}}</ref>
