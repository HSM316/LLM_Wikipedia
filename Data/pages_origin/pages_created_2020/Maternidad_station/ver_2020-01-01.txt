'''Maternidad''' is a [[Caracas Metro]] station on Line 2. It was opened on 6 November 1988 as part of the extension of the line from [[La Paz station|La Paz]] to [[El Silencio station|El Silencio]]. The station is between [[Capuchinos station|Capuchinos]] and [[Artigas station|Artigas]].<ref name="urbanrail">{{cite web |last1=Schwandl |first1=Robert |title=Caracas |url=http://www.urbanrail.net/am/cara/caracas.htm |publisher=urbanrail}}</ref>
