'''Zdeňka Padevětová''' (born 12 December 1958) represented [[Czechoslovakia]] in [[archery]].

== Life ==

She was born in [[Helsinki]].

Padevětová competed at the [[1980 Summer Olympic Games]] in the [[Archery at the 1980 Summer Olympics – Women's individual|women's individual]] event and finished fourth with a score of 2405 points.<ref>{{cite web |title=Zdenka PadevÄtovÃ¡ |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pa/zdenka-padevetova-1.html |website=www.sports-reference.com |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>

Padevětová is a member of the Czech Archery Hall of Fame.<ref>{{cite web |title=Zdenka Rousková-Padevětová |url=https://www.olympic.cz/sportovec/1639--zdenka-rouskova-padevetova |website=www.olympic.cz |accessdate=1 January 2020}}</ref>
