'''Primrose Mary O. Archer''' is an English fashion model.

== Early life ==
Archer was born in [[Worcestershire]], England—the eighth of ten children—and raised on a farm.<ref name=VogueUK>{{cite web|url= https://www.vogue.co.uk/article/primrose-archer-british-vogue-december-2018-cover-interview|title= From Farm To Vogue Cover: Meet Primrose Archer|work=British Vogue}}</ref> She and her siblings appeared in ''[[British Vogue]]'' in a 2005 editorial photographed by [[Tim Walker]] when she was a child.<ref name=VogueUK/><ref>{{cite web|url= https://fashionmagazine.com/fashion/new-models-2019/|title= These Are the New Models to Keep Your Eye on This Year|work=Fashion Canada}}</ref> She was educated at [[The Bewdley School]].<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.worcesternews.co.uk/news/14958896.sixteen-year-old-model-talks-about-getting-scouted-for-one-of-the-uks-biggest-modelling-agencies/ |title= Sixteen year-old model talks about getting scouted for one of the UK's biggest modelling agencies|work=Worcester News}}</ref>

== Career ==
Archer was discovered at Birmingham's [[Clothes Show Live]].<ref name=VogueUK/> In December 2018, she appeared on the cover of ''British Vogue'', hand-chosen by editor-in-chief [[Edward Enninful]], for one of the month's four covers; models like [[Stella Tennant]] and [[Adut Akech]] appeared on other covers.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.vogue.co.uk/article/stella-tennant-adut-akech-saffron-vadher-primrose-archer-december-cover-british-vogue-2018|title=4 Exciting Faces Cover Edward Enninful's One-Year Anniversary Issue Of British Vogue|work=British Vogue}}</ref> The cover was photographed by [[Steven Meisel]].

Archer has modelled for [[Anna Sui]] and [[Marc Jacobs]] campaigns,<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.thelovemagazine.co.uk/article/primrose-archer-high-kicks-her-way-to-high-fashion-in-the-new-marc-jacobs-campaign|title=PRIMROSE ARCHER HIGH KICKS HER WAY TO HIGH FASHION IN THE NEW MARC JACOBS CAMPAIGN|work=Love Magazine}}</ref> while walking the runway for [[Michael Kors]], [[Valentino (fashion house)|Valentino]], [[Max Mara]], Matty Bovan, [[Burberry]], [[Simone Rocha]], [[Coach New York]], and [[Missoni]].<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.vogue.com/vogueworld/article/vogue-top-14-models-fall-2019|title=Top 14 Models of Fall 2019|work=Vogue}}</ref>
