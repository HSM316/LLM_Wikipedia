'''''Oeosporangium''''' is a  genus of [[fern]]s in the subfamily [[Cheilanthoideae]] of the family [[Pteridaceae]].<ref name=PPGI/> The genus has not always been recognized. In the [[Pteridophyte Phylogeny Group]] classification of 2016 (PPG I) it was included in ''Allosorus''<ref name=PPGI/> (a genus since subsumed into ''[[Aleuritopteris]]''). It was accepted by the ''Checklist of the Ferns and Lycophytes of the World'' {{As of|2020|January|lc=yes}}.<ref name=CFLW/> As [[Circumscription (taxonomy)|circumscribed]] there, the genus is native to the [[Old World]].<ref name=CFLW/>
