The '''1901–02 Yale Bulldogs men's ice hockey season''' was the seventh season of play for the program.

==Season==
For the fourth consecutive season Yale was the Intercollegiate Hockey Association champion. They finished undefeated in league play and defeated [[Harvard Crimson men's ice hockey|Harvard]] in the championship series.

The team did not have a coach, however, C.H. Baxter served as team manager.
