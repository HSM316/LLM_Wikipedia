'''Shafey Kidwai''' (born 8 April 1960) is an Indian academic, columnist and author. He is the head of the Department of Mass Communications at [[Aligarh Muslim University]]. He writes a weekly column for [[The Hindu]]. Kidwai has produced twelve books in Urdu and English. His 2017 work ''Sawaneh-e-Sir Syed: Ek Bazdeed'' - a biography of [[Syed Ahmad Khan|Sir Syed]] won the 2019 [[List of Sahitya Akademi Award winners for Urdu| Sahitya Akademi Award for Urdu]]. A previous work Urdu Literature and Journalism: Critical Perspective was published by Cambidge University Press.
