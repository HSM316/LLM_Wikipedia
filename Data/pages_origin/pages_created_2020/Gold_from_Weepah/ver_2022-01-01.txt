'''''Gold from Weepah''''' is a 1927 American [[silent film|silent]] [[Western (genre)|Western]] film directed by [[William Bertram (actor)|William Bertram]] and starring [[Bill Cody (actor)|Bill Cody]], [[Doris Dawson]] and [[Dick La Reno]].<ref>Langman p.178</ref>

* [[Doris Dawson]] as Elsie Blaine
