'''Stewart Brydon''' (born 1967) is a Scottish former racing cyclist, a multiple British national champion on the track in the sprint (British Track Sprint champion six times consecutively ), twice tandem champion with [[Eddie Alexander]] and a winner of the Keirin and team sprint titles. He represented Scotland at three [[Commonwealth Games]] in 1986, 1990 and 1994.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thecgf.com/results/athletes/44867|title=Commonwealth Games results|website=Commonwealth Games Federation}}</ref>
