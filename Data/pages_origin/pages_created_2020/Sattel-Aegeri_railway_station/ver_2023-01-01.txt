'''Sattel-Aegeri railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Sattel-Aegeri}}) is a railway station in [[Sattel, Switzerland|Sattel]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It is an intermediate stop on the standard gauge [[Pfäffikon–Arth-Goldau railway|Pfäffikon–Arth-Goldau line]] of [[Südostbahn]].

== Services ==
The following services stop at Sattel-Aegeri:<ref name="fpf670">{{cite web | url = https://www.fahrplanfelder.ch/fileadmin/fap_pdf_fields/2020/670.pdf | title = Rapperswil - Pfäffikon SZ - (Einsiedeln) - Arth-Goldau | publisher = Bundesamt für Verkehr | date=27 November 2019 | accessdate = 31 December 2019}}</ref>
