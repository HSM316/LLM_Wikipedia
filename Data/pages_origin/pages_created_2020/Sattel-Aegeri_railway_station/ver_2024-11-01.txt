'''Sattel-Aegeri railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof Sattel-Aegeri}}) was a railway station in [[Sattel, Switzerland|Sattel]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]]. It was an intermediate stop on the standard gauge [[Pfäffikon–Arth-Goldau railway|Pfäffikon–Arth-Goldau line]] of [[Südostbahn]]. The station closed with the timetable change on 10 December 2023 in favor of a new station, {{rws|Sattel}}, located near the town center.<ref>{{Cite news |date=17 March 2023 |title=Die SOB erstellt in Sattel eine moderne Haltestelle |language=de |work=Nau |url=https://www.nau.ch/ort/schwyz/die-sob-erstellt-in-sattel-eine-moderne-haltestelle-66451419 |access-date=2023-11-30}}</ref>
