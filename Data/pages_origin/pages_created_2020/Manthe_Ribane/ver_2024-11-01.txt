'''Manthe Ribane''' is an artist, performer, and musician based in South Africa and the Creative Director at 113 Studios. ''OkayAfrica'' described her as a "master of [[Afrofuturism]]" in her style and aesthetic.{{r|OA 2019}}

''Dazed'' wrote that, of the many performance mediums for which Ribane is known, her dance was her "most imaginative and magnetic".<ref name=Dazed100>{{Cite web |last1=Alemoru |first1=Kemi |title=Manthe Ribane â€“ Dazed 100 |work=[[Dazed (magazine)|Dazed]] |date=2018-04-24 |url=https://www.dazeddigital.com/projects/article/39817/1/manthe-ribane-musician-biography-dazed-100-2018-profile |language=en |accessdate=2019-12-31 |df=mdy-all }}</ref> Well-known in South Africa's dance scene, she has mastered styles including [[pantsula]], [[crumping]], and [[voguing]]. She danced for [[Die Antwoord]] on tour.{{r|Dazed100}} In 2019, she choreographed ''La Maison Noir: The Gift And The Curse'', a visual novel by [[Petite Noir]].{{r|OA 2019}}

As a musician, Ribane has collaborated with rapper [[Spoek Mathambo]]. With [[Hyperdub]] producer [[Okzharp]], she released a [[gqom]] EP in 2016{{r|Dazed100}} and a debut [[R&B]]-influenced album, ''Closer Apart'', in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web |last1=Cardew |first1=Ben |title=Okzharp & Manthe Ribane: Closer Apart |work=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] |date=2018-07-14 |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/okzharp-and-manthe-ribane-closer-apart/ |language=en |accessdate=2019-12-31 |df=mdy-all }}</ref>{{r|OA 2019}}
