The novel '''''The Letter Bearer''''' was critically acclaimed when it was published in 2014.<ref name=theguardian2014-05-10/><ref name=independent2014-04-02/>

Author [[Robert Allison (author)|Robert Allison]] was nominated for, or won several prestigious awards, including the [[McKitterick Prize]].<ref name=theguardian2015-06-26/>  The novel was shortlisted for the 2014 [[Desmond Elliott Prize]].
