The novel '''''The Letter Bearer''''' was critically acclaimed when it was published in 2014.<ref name=theguardian2014-05-10/>

Author [[Robert Allison]] was nominated for, or won several prestigiuous awards, including the [[McKitterick prize]].<ref name=theguardian2015-06-26/>  The novel was shortlisted for the 2014 [[Desmond Elliott Prize]].
