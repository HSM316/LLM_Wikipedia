'''Armando Junior Quitirna''' (born 25 April 2000) is a Guinea-Bissau professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Waterford F.C.|Waterford]] in the [[League of Ireland Premier Division]].<ref>{{soccerway|624099}}</ref>

==Career==

===Charlton Athletic===
Quitirna made two appearances for [[Charlton Athletic F.C.|Charlton Athletic]], both in cup games before it was announced on the 18th May 2021 that he would leave the club at the end of his contract.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cafc.co.uk/news/view/609d02c1c601c/squad-update-following-completion-of-the-202021-season|title=Squad update following completion of the 2020/21 season|website=www.cafc.co.uk}}</ref>

===Waterford===
It was announced that had Quitirna made the move to [[League of Ireland Premier Division]] side [[Waterford F.C.|Waterford]] on the 9th July 2021, signing until the end of the season in November.<ref>https://waterfordfc.ie/blues-sign-nolan-quitirna-milambo-wordsworth/</ref> On the same day, he made his debut for the club, scoring a penalty in a 1–0 win away to [[Finn Harps F.C.|Finn Harps]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.independent.ie/sport/soccer/league-of-ireland/junior-quitirna-strikes-to-give-waterford-lifeline-at-bottom-of-premier-division-40635541.html|title = Junior Quitirna strikes to give Waterford lifeline at bottom of Premier Division}}</ref> On the 30th July 2021, he came off the bench away to [[Drogheda United F.C.|Drogheda United]] in the 75th minute and scored the winning goal 8 minutes later in a 2–1 win for his side.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.irishexaminer.com/sport/soccer/arid-40349732.html|title = Super-sub Junior Quitirna seals Waterford win away to Drogheda United|date = 30 July 2021}}</ref> He made a total of 20 appearances in all competitions for the club, scoring 6 goals<ref>https://int.soccerway.com/players/junior-quitirna/667885/</ref> as they were relegated to the [[League of Ireland First Division]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.the42.ie/ucd-waterford-report-5613512-Nov2021/|title=Joy for UCD as they beat beleagured Waterford to seal Premier Division status|first=Paul|last=Fennessy|website=The42}}</ref>
