'''Lucien Stephen Kempf Sr.''' (December 26, 1905 – September 10, 1998) was an [[American football]] coach. He served as the first head football coach in the history of [[Wagner College]] in [[Staten Island, New York]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://issuu.com/greenandwhite/docs/2019_fb_media_guide_-_final_issuu|title=2019 Football Media Guide|publisher=[[Wagner Seahawks]]|access-date=December 30, 2019}}</ref>
