'''The Sölvesborg Bridge''' is a {{Nowrap|760 meter}} long walk- and biking bridge over the [[Sölvesborg]] Bay Area in [[Blekinge]]. The bridge was officially opened on 25 May 2013.<ref>{{cite web|title=Nu är Sölvesborgsbron invigd|url=http://www.ljusarkitektur.com/en/nu-ar-solvesborgsbron-invigd/|accessdate=2019-05-20}}</ref>

The bridge was constructed by the local company ''Stål och Rörmontage''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Stål & Rörmontage - Pressarkiv, Bro över Sölvesborgsviken|url=http://www.srmab.com/2012-2.html|publisher=srmab.com|date=2013-08-22|accessdate=2013-07-10}}</ref>

In 2016, the bridge was named one of the eleven most spectacular bridges in the world by [[CNN]].<ref>{{cite web|title=A bridge too far? 11 spectacular new bridges that break the mold|url=http://edition.cnn.com/2016/04/27/architecture/spectacular-new-bridges-that-break-the-mold/index.html?sr=twCNN042716spectacular-new-bridges-that-break-the-mold1137AMStoryLink&linkId=23907862|publisher=CNN|accessdate=29 April 2016}}</ref>
