'''Andrew Feldherr''' is professor of classics at [[Princeton University]] from where he also earned his bachelor's degree. He received his PhD from the University of California at Berkeley.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://classics.princeton.edu/people/faculty/core/andrew-feldherr|title=Andrew Feldherr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://classics.princeton.edu/sites/default/files/cv/Feldherr_CV_2016.pdf |title=Archived copy |access-date=2019-12-31 |archive-date=2020-03-17 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200317100419/https://classics.princeton.edu/sites/default/files/cv/Feldherr_CV_2016.pdf |url-status=dead }}</ref>
