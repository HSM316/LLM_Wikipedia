'''Andrew Feldherr''' is professor of classics at [[Princeton University]] from where he also earned his bachelor's degree. He received his PhD from the University of California at Berkeley.<ref>https://classics.princeton.edu/people/faculty/core/andrew-feldherr</ref><ref>https://classics.princeton.edu/sites/default/files/cv/Feldherr_CV_2016.pdf</ref>

==Selected publications==
* ''Spectacle and Society in Livy's History''. University of California Press, 1998.<ref>https://doi.org/10.1086/449493</ref><ref>https://www.jstor.org/stable/1561781</ref>
* ''The Cambridge Companion to the Roman Historians''. (Cambridge Companions to Literature) Cambridge University Press, 2009.<ref>https://doi.org/10.1017/CCOL9780521854535</ref>
* ''Playing Gods: Ovid's Metamorphoses and the Politics of Fiction''. Princeton University Press, 2010.<ref>http://bmcr.brynmawr.edu/2010/2010-12-74.html</ref>
* ''The Oxford History of Historical Writing: Volume 1: Beginnings to AD 600''. Oxford University Press, 2011. (Editor with [[Grant Hardy]])<ref>https://doi.org/10.1017/S0009840X13002886</ref>
