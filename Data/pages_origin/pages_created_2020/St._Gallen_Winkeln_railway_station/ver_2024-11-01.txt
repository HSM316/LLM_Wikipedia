'''St. Gallen Winkeln railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof St. Gallen Winkeln}}) is a railway station in the Winkeln neighborhood of [[St. Gallen]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is an intermediate stop on the [[St. Gallen–Winterthur railway line|St. Gallen–Winterthur line]].<ref name="saw">{{Eisenbahnatlas Schweiz | page = 14}}</ref>
