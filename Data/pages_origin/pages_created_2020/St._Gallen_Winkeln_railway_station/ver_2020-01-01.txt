'''St. Gallen Winkeln railway station''' ({{lang-de|Bahnhof St. Gallen Winkeln}}) is a railway station in the Winkeln neighborhood of [[St. Gallen]], in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Canton of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]]. It is an intermediate station on the [[St. Gallen–Winterthur railway]] and is served by local trains only.

== Services ==
St. Gallen Winkeln is served by two services of the [[St. Gallen S-Bahn]]:<ref name="abfahrt">{{cite web | title=Abfahrt: Bahnhof St. Gallen Winkeln | language=de | date=15 December 2019 | accessdate=30 December 2019 | url=https://www.sbb.ch/content/dam/internet/sbb/all/pdf/fahrplan/gedruckte-fahrplaene/fahrplaene_2020/ab-an-taschenfahrplaene/SGWI_191215_ab_6300.pdf.sbbdownload.pdf | work=[[Swiss Federal Railways]]}}</ref>

* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|1}}: half-hourly service to St. Gallen and [[Wil railway station|Wil]]
* {{S-Bahn-St.-Gallen|5}}: half-hourly service to St. Gallen and [[Weinfelden railway station|Weinfelden]].
