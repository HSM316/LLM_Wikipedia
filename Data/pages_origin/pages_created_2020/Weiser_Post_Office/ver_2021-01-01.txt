The '''Weiser Post Office''', at Main and W. 1st Sts. in [[Weiser, Idaho]], was built in 1932.  It was listed on the [[National Register of Historic Places]] in 1982.<ref name=nris/>

It was designed by architects [[Tourtellotte & Hummel]] in [[Georgian Revival architecture|Georgian Revival]] style.  Its main section is two stories tall, and it has a rear [[ell (architecture)|ell]] at the center rear.  It has a flat roof behind a [[parapet]] that has sections of enclosed [[balustrades]].<ref name="nrhpdoc">{{cite web|url={{NRHP url|id=82000383}}|title=Idaho State Historical Society Inventory for Group Nomination of Tourtellotte and Hummel Architecture: Weiser Post Office |publisher=[[National Park Service]]|author= |date= |accessdate=December 30, 2019}} With {{NRHP url|id=82000383|photos=y|title=accompanying two photos from 1980}}</ref>
