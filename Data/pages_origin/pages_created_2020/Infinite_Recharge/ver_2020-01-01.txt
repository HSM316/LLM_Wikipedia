'''Infinite Recharge''', stylized as '''INFINITE RECHARGE''', is the [[FIRST Robotics Competition]] game for the 2020 season.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.firstinspires.org/robotics/frc/game-and-season|title=FIRST Robotics Competition Game and Season Info|last=|first=|date=2019-04-20|website=FIRSTinspires|language=en|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref> As the rules are yet to be revealed, it is unknown what the game will involve. The game is teased to center around a theme of energy and citybuilding and is in partnership with [[Star Wars: Force for Change]]. Additionally, this will be the first season without an enforced six-week build period, with teams able to work on their robots until the date of their first event.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.firstinspires.org/robotics/frc/blog/2020-sbd-july-dlc|title=Stop Build Day Retirement -- Update and a new DLC pack!|date=2019-07-09|website=FIRST|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref>

== Kickoff ==
The season's kickoff event will take place on January 4, 2020. The event will be livestreamed starting at 10:00 AM Eastern Time.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.firstinspires.org/robotics/frc/kickoff|title=Kickoff|date=2015-11-25|website=FIRST|language=en|access-date=2019-12-31}}</ref>

== Events ==
The competition season for Infinite Recharge is divided into seven weeks, with many events occurring simultaneously during each week. After Week 7, teams that have qualified compete in the [[FIRST Championship]], held over two weeks in [[Houston]] and [[Detroit]]. Only regional, district championship, and world championship events are shown.
