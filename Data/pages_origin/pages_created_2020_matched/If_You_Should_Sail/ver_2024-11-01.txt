"'''If You Should Sail'''" is a song by American duo [[Nielsen Pearson]]. It was released as a single in 1980 from their [[Nielsen Pearson (album)|self-titled album]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/song/if-you-should-sail-mt0030094098|title=Nielsen Pearson â€“ If You Should Sail|website=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=December 19, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://noted.blogs.com/westcoastmusic/2006/06/nielsenpearson_.html|title=Nielsen-Pearson "If You Should Sail" video|website=West Coast Music France|accessdate=December 19, 2019}}</ref>

The song peaked at No. 38 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] and No. 35 on the [[Adult Contemporary (chart)|Adult Contemporary]] chart, becoming the duo's only top 40 hit.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.popredux80.com/2013/04/if-you-should-sail-by-nielsenpearson.html|title="If You Should Sail" by Nielsen/Pearson|website=PopRedux80|accessdate=December 19, 2019}}</ref>
