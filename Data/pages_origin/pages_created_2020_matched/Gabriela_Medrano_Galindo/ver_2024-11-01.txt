'''Gabriela Medrano Galindo''' (born 27 June 1983) is a Mexican politician affiliated with the [[Ecologist Green Party of Mexico|PVEM]]. She served as federal deputy of the [[LXII Legislature of the Mexican Congress]] representing [[Quintana Roo]], and previously served as a local deputy in the XIII Legislature of the [[Congress of Quintana Roo]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Perfil del legislador|url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=9216882|publisher=Legislative Information System|access-date=3 March 2023|language=es}}</ref>
