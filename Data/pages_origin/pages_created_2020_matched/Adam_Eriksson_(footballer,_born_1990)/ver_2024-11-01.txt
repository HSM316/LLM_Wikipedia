'''Adam Lars Eriksson''' (born 13 July 1990) is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Falkenbergs FF]] as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]].<ref>{{Soccerway|127390}}</ref>

He signed for [[Helsingborgs IF]] from [[Falkenbergs FF]] before the start of the [[2016 Allsvenskan|2016]] season.<ref>{{cite web|title=Adam Eriksson|url=https://svenskfotboll.se/superettan/person/?playerid=7492&instant=1|publisher=Svenskfotboll|accessdate=20 September 2014}}</ref>
