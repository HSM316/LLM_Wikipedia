'''Michael Francis Gonzalez''' (born March 8, 1988), also known by his stage name '''Mike Del Rio''', is a [[musical artist]], [[Record producer|producer]], and [[songwriter]] from [[New York, NY]] now based in Los Angeles, CA.<ref>[http://www.bmi.com/news/entry/539985 BMI website]. Accessed January 23, 2010.</ref> He is the co creator and lead member of the alternative group '''[[Powers (duo)|POWERS]]''' along with songwriter, vocalist and instrumentalist Crista Ru signed to [[Republic Records]]. As a record producer, Del Rio's eclectic style and musicality has led him to work with an array of artist such as [[Kylie Minogue]], [[Selena Gomez]], [[Eminem]], [[Christina Aguilera]], [[CeeLo Green]], [[Cheryl Cole]], [[The Knocks]], [[Skylar Grey]], [[Jamie N Commons]], and [[X Ambassadors]]. Most recently Del Rio executive produced recording artist [[LP (singer)|LP]] latest album ''[[Lost on You (album)|Lost on You]]'' containing the global hit title track "[[Lost on You]]". He is signed to [[Alex da Kid|Alex Da Kid]]'s publishing company [[Kidinakorner|KidinaKorner]] distributed through [[Universal Music Group]].

==POWERS==
Together with [[Crista Ru]], he also is part of the alternative duo [[Powers (duo)|POWERS]], which they started in late 2013.
