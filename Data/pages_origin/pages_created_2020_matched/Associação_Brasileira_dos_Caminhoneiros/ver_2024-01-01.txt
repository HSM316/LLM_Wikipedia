'''Associação Brasileira dos Caminhoneiros''' ('''ABCAM''', Brazilian Association of Truck Drivers) is an association of [[Freelancer|freelance]] [[truck driver]]s in [[Brazil]]. It was founded by [[José da Fonseca Lopes]] in 1983.<ref>{{cite web |title=Associação Brasileira dos Caminhoneiros - Abcam |url=http://www.abcam.org.br/index.php/pt/abcam |website=Associação Brasileira dos Caminhoneiros |accessdate=1 June 2018 |language=pt-br |date=15 April 2015}}</ref>  It represents around 500,000 independent truck drivers.<ref>{{cite web |title=Caminhoneiros autônomos dizem que aumento de impostos torna inviável profissão |url=http://agenciabrasil.ebc.com.br/geral/noticia/2017-08/caminhoneiros-autonomos-dizem-que-aumento-de-impostos-torna-inviavel-profissao |website=Agência Brasil |accessdate=1 June 2018 |language=pt-br |date=10 August 2017}}</ref>

It played a lead role in organizing the [[2018 Brazil truck drivers' strike]],<ref>{{cite web |title=Key Brazil truckers association Abcam rejects government accord to... |url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-brazil-transport-abcam/key-brazil-truckers-association-abcam-rejects-government-accord-to-end-protest-idUSKCN1IQ2FY?il=0 |publisher=Reuters}}</ref> issuing an ultimatum in early May that led to nationwide disruptions on May 20.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Albuquerque |first1=Felipe |title=Caminhoneiros dão ultimato por diesel mais barato e ameaçam greve |url=http://www.gazetadopovo.com.br/politica/republica/ameaca-de-greve-caminhoneiros-dao-ultimato-por-reducao-do-preco-do-diesel-cbvtuujo39mopsvaguljez1rr |website=Gazeta do Povo |accessdate=1 June 2018 |language=pt-BR}}</ref>
