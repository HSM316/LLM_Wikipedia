'''De Borgia Schoolhouse''' is a two-story wood frame school located in [[De Borgia, Montana]], [[United States]] which was listed on the [[National Register of Historic Places]] on December 27, 1979.  Constructed in 1908, the schoolhouse was the first two-story building built in the West End of [[Mineral County, Montana|Mineral County]] and is the only building now standing in De Borgia to survive the [[Great Fire of 1910]].  The school closed in 1956, becoming a community center for the town. <ref>{{NRISref|2010a}}</ref><ref name="NRHP_Placard">{{citation |publisher= [[Montana Historical Society]] - Montana History Wiki |place= Helena, Mont. |url= http://montanahistorywiki.pbworks.com/w/page/21639763/National%20Register%20-%20Mineral%20County |title= National Register of Historic Places Sign Texts - Mineral County |accessdate= November 23, 2013 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20131203013625/http://montanahistorywiki.pbworks.com/w/page/21639763/National%20Register%20-%20Mineral%20County |archive-date= December 3, 2013 |url-status= dead }}</ref>
