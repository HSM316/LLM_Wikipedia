'''State Route&nbsp;131''' ('''SR&nbsp;131''') is a {{convert|58+1/2|mi|km|adj=mid|-long}} [[state route]] in the U.S. state of [[Maine]]. Its southern terminus is in the [[St. George, Maine|St. George]] community of [[Port Clyde, Maine|Port Clyde]], at a dead end near the Port Clyde Harbor. Its northern terminus is in [[Swanville, Maine|Swanville]], at the intersection with [[Maine State Route 141|SR&nbsp;141]].<ref name=GM>{{google maps |url=https://www.google.com/maps/dir/43.9260093,-69.2588506/44.5219984,-69.0003781/@44.232796,-69.4213325,10z/am=t/data=!3m1!4b1!4m14!4m13!1m10!3m4!1m2!1d-69.282334!2d44.2310027!3s0x4cadde0d43f908dd:0x76196971cd24f86e!3m4!1m2!1d-69.1497722!2d44.4526512!3s0x4cae12da7a177669:0x1f2bafee8acd8f7c!1m0!3e0?hl=en-US |accessdate=August 2, 2017}}</ref>
