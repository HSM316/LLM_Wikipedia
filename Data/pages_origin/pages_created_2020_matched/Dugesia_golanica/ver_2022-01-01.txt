'''''Dugesia golanica''''' is a species of [[dugesiid]] [[triclad]] that inhabits freshwater bodies of Israel. The species is named after the [[Golan Heights]].<ref name="Bromley90">Heather J. Bromley & Mario Benazzi, 1990. New planarians (Turbellaria, Tricladida) from Israel and Lebanon. Israel Journal of Zoology, Vol. 37, pp. 139-150.</ref>
