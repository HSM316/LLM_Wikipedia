{{Main|2016 Fed Cup Americas Zone}}

'''Group A''' of the '''2016 Fed Cup Americas Zone Group II''' was one of two pools in the Americas zone of the [[2016 Fed Cup]]. Four teams competed in a round robin competition, with the top two teams proceeding to the [[2016 Fed Cup Americas Zone Group II – Play-offs|play-offs]] where they played for promotion to [[2017 Fed Cup Americas Zone#Group I|Group I]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fedcup.com/en/draws-results/group-ii/americas/2016.aspx|title=2016 Fed Cup Americas Zone Group II|work=fedcup.com}}</ref>
