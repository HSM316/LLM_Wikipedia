''' Riyadh Nouri''' (born 1 July 1951) is a former [[Iraqi people|Iraqi]] [[association football|football]] [[midfielder]] who played for [[Iraq national football team|Iraq]] in the [[1972 AFC Asian Cup]] and [[1974 FIFA World Cup qualification]]. He played for the national team between 1970 and 1977.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rsssf.org/miscellaneous/iraq-recintlp.html|title=Iraq – Record International Players|website=[[RSSSF]]}}</ref>

On 11 March 1973, Riyadh scored Iraq's first ever [[World Cup qualification]] goal against [[Australia men's national soccer team|Australia]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://iraqsport.com/cgi-bin/db/db.cgi?db=players&uid=default&view_records=1&ID=*&nh=27 |title=Players DataBase: Search Results |website=iraqsport.com |access-date=12 January 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20020820235250/http://iraqsport.com/cgi-bin/db/db.cgi?db=players&uid=default&view_records=1&ID=*&nh=27 |archive-date=20 August 2002 |url-status=dead}}</ref>
