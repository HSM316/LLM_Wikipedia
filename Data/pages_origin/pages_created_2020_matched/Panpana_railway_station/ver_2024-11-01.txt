'''Panpana railway station''' is a railway station on [[Kharagpur–Puri line]], part of the [[Howrah–Chennai main line]] under [[Kharagpur railway division]] of [[South Eastern Railway zone]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.totaltraininfo.com/station/PNPN/|title=PNPN / Panpana Railway Station {{!}} Train Arrival / Departure Timings at Panpana|website=www.totaltraininfo.com|access-date=2019-10-31}}</ref> It is situated at Begunia, Panpana in [[Balasore district]] in the Indian state of [[Odisha]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ndtv.com/indian-railway/panpana-pnpn-station|title=Panpana Railway Station (PNPN) : Station Code, Time Table, Map, Enquiry|publisher=NDTV|access-date=2019-10-31}}</ref>

==History==
In between 1893 and 1896 the [[East Coast State Railway]] constructed Howrah–Chennai main line. Kharagpur–Puri branch was finally opened for public in 1901.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ser.indianrailways.gov.in/view_section.jsp?lang=0&id=0,1|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130401151628/http://www.ser.indianrailways.gov.in/view_section.jsp?lang=0&id=0,1|url-status=dead|archive-date=2013-04-01|title=South Eastern Railway|date=2013-04-01|access-date=2019-10-31}}</ref> The route was electrified in several phases. In 2005, Howrah–Chennai route was completely electrified.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.irfca.org/faq/faq-history7.html|title=[IRFCA] Indian Railways FAQ: IR History: Part 7|publisher=IRFCA|access-date=2019-10-31}}</ref>
