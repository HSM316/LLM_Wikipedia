== Early career ==
Hawke was a teacher at St Cuthbert's College, [[Epsom (New Zealand electorate)|Epsom]].<ref>{{Cite news|title = CYF accused in Russian adoption muddle|url = http://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=10453442|newspaper = New Zealand Herald|date = 2007-07-24|access-date = 2015-12-08|issn = 1170-0777|language = en-NZ|first = Simon|last = O'Rourke}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title = Shakeup for adoption law viewed as well overdue|url = http://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=170455|newspaper = New Zealand Herald|date = 2001-01-30|access-date = 2015-12-08|issn = 1170-0777|language = en-NZ}}</ref> After adopting her children from Russia she then went onto volunteering at ICANZ.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Investitures at Government House Auckland March 2014 {{!}} The Governor-General of New Zealand Te Kawana Tianara o Aotearoa|url = http://gg.govt.nz/content/investitures-government-house-auckland-march-2014|website = gg.govt.nz|accessdate = 2015-12-08}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Mrs Wendy Hawke, ONZM {{!}} The Governor-General of New Zealand Te Kawana Tianara o Aotearoa|url = http://gg.govt.nz/content/mrs-wendy-hawke-onzm|website = gg.govt.nz|accessdate = 2015-12-08}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = New Year Honours List 2014 {{!}} DPMC|url = http://www.dpmc.govt.nz/node/1260|website = www.dpmc.govt.nz|accessdate = 2015-12-08}}</ref> In 1995, Hawke then became the executive director.<ref name=":0" />

== Personal life ==
Hawke and her husband David were the first New Zealand couple to adopt from Russia in 1992. Hawke has five children. Four adopted from Russia and one son by birth.<ref>{{Cite web |last=ABADIA |first=KARINA |date=2014-01-09 |title=Adoption advocate honoured |url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/auckland/local-news/east-bays-courier/9591967/Adoption-advocate-honoured |access-date=2023-04-25 |website=Stuff |language=en}}</ref> Her husband died in 2013 after a long illness.<ref>{{Cite web |title=David HAWKE Obituary (2013) - The New Zealand Herald |url=https://notices.nzherald.co.nz/nz/obituaries/nzherald-nz/name/david-hawke-obituary?id=41837776 |access-date=2023-04-25 |website=Legacy.com}}</ref>
