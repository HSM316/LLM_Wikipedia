'''Dianne Lesley Hollister''' (born 9 December 1947) is a former [[Australia]]n politician.

==Early life==
Born in [[Devonport, Tasmania|Devonport]], [[Tasmania]], she started her career as a teacher.

==Political career==
In 1989, she was elected to the [[Tasmanian House of Assembly]] for [[Division of Braddon (state)|Braddon]] as an [[Independent (politician)|Independent Green]]. In 1992, the five Independent Greens formed the [[Tasmanian Greens]]. Hollister held her seat until she was defeated in 1998.<ref name=tas>{{cite web
