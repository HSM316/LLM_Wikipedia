'''Aniezo''' is a hamlet in the municipality of [[Cabezón de Liébana]], ([[Cantabria]], Spain). The 2009 census showed Aniezo to have a population of 33 inhabitants ([[National Institute of Statistics (Spain)|INE]]). It is located at an altitude of 683  [[metre]]s above sea level, and 6.6&nbsp;km from the municipal capital, [[Cabezón de Liébana]].

According to tradition, Saint [[Beatus of Liebana]] was born here. The fiesta of 'La Santuca' is celebrated on 2 May every year. It is a celebration that has taken place since the 15th century. A wooden carving of the Virgin Mary is carried in a procession down the mountain to the Monastery [[Santo Toribio de Liébana]] which is 15&nbsp;km away. It is known as the longest procession in Spain.<ref>[Fiestas in Cantabria|http://www.casas.co.uk/blog/?p=622]</ref>
