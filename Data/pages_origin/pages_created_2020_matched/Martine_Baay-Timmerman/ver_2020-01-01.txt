'''Martine Baay-Timmerman''' (born 16 January 1958) is a Dutch politician. She was member of the [[House of Representatives (Netherlands)|House of Representatives of the Netherlands]] for [[50PLUS]] between 29 October 2013 and 10 September 2014. She left the House of Representatives due to health issues and was replaced by [[Henk Krol]]. Since 28 March 2017 Baay-Timmerman is a member of the [[Senate (Netherlands)|Senate]].

==Career==
Baay-Timmerman studied law at the [[Vrije Universiteit]] in Amsterdam, she later worked as a lawyer for twelve years.<ref name=volkskrant>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.volkskrant.nl/vk/nl/14229/Henk-Krol-verlaat-50Plus/article/detail/3522977/2013/10/07/Baay-definitief-in-Kamer-voor-50Plus.dhtml |title=Baay definitief in Kamer voor 50Plus |language=Dutch |publisher=Volkskrant |date=7 October 2013 |accessdate=31 October 2013}}</ref>

Since early 2009 Baay-Timmerman has been involved with 50PLUS, she was a secretary for the party between November 2011 and November 2012. She was third on the candidate list of 50PLUS for the [[2012 Dutch general election|2012 elections]].<ref name=volkskrant/>

When House of Representatives member Henk Krol resigned on 4 October 2013, Baay-Timmerman, as first candidate on the list, was likely to become his successor. She entered the House on 29 October 2013.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.tweedekamer.nl/nieuws/kamernieuws/newspage2373_martine_baaij.jsp#0 |title=Beëdiging nieuw Kamerlid Martine Baay (50PLUS) |language=Dutch |publisher=Tweede Kamer |date= |accessdate=31 October 2013}}</ref> On 10 September 2014 she went on sick leave and was replaced by Henk Krol.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.parlement.com/id/vjn2hw86id91/nieuws/krol_50plus_en_leenders_pvda_keren_terug |title=Krol (50PLUS) en Leenders (PvdA) keren terug in Tweede Kamer |language=Dutch |publisher=Parlement.com |date=10 September 2014 |accessdate=5 October 2014}}</ref> In December 2014 it became clear that Baay-Timmerman would not return do the House of Representatives as she needed more time to recuperate from breast cancer.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.nrc.nl/nieuws/2014/12/12/martine-baay-uit-kamer-50-plus-vraagt-henk-krol-te-blijven/ |title=Henk Krol blijft 50PLUS-Kamerlid, na definitief vertrek Martine Baay |language=Dutch |publisher=[[NRC Handelsblad|NRC]] |date=12 December 2014 |accessdate=3 February 2015}}</ref>

On 28 March 2017 Baay-Timmerman became a member of the [[Senate (Netherlands)|Senate]], she took over the seat from [[Martin van Rooijen]] who became member of the House.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.parlement.com/id/vkcwi4hnvqxz/nieuws/vijf_eerste_kamerleden_beedigd |title=Vijf Eerste Kamerleden beëdigd |language=Dutch |publisher=Parlement.com |date= |accessdate=7 April 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.parlement.com/id/vj2uugfz90wj/m_h_h_martine_baay_timmerman |title=Mr. M.H.H. (Martine) Baay-Timmerman |language=Dutch |publisher=Parlement.com |date= |accessdate=7 April 2017}}</ref>
