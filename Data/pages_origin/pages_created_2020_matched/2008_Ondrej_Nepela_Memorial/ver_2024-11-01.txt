The '''2008 [[Ondrej Nepela Memorial]]''' ({{lang-sk|Memoriál Ondreja Nepelu}}) was the 16th edition of an annual senior-level international [[figure skating competition]] held in [[Bratislava]], [[Slovakia]]. The competition was held between November 22 and 23, 2008 at the [[Ondrej Nepela Ice Rink]]. Skaters competed in the disciplines of [[single skating|men's singles, ladies' singles]], and [[ice dancing]]. The [[compulsory dance]] was the Paso Doble.
