The '''2006 Kremlin Cup''' was a [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Tennis court#Indoor courts|indoor carpet courts]]. It was the 16th edition of the [[Kremlin Cup]], and was part of the [[ATP International Series|International Series]] of the [[2005 ATP Tour]], and of the [[WTA Tier I Events|Tier I Series]] of the [[2005 WTA Tour]]. It took place at the [[Olympic Stadium (Moscow arena)|Olympic Stadium]] in [[Moscow]], [[Russia]], from 10 October through 16 October 2005.

==Finals==
===Men's Singles===
{{main|2005 Kremlin Cup – Men's Singles}}
{{flagicon|RUS}} [[Igor Andreev]] defeated {{flagicon|GER}} [[Nicolas Kiefer]], 5–7, 7–6<sup>(7–3)</sup>, 6–2
*It was Igor Andreev's 3rd title of the year, and his 3rd overall.

===Women's Singles===
{{main|2005 Kremlin Cup – Women's Singles}}
{{flagicon|FRA}} [[Mary Pierce]]  defeated {{flagicon|ITA}} [[Francesca Schiavone]], 6–4, 6–3
*It was Mary Pierce's 2nd title of the year, and her 18th and final career title overall. It was her 2nd Tier I title of the year and her 5th overall.

===Men's Doubles===
{{main|2005 Kremlin Cup – Men's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|BLR}} [[Max Mirnyi]] / {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Mikhail Youzhny]] defeated {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Igor Andreev]] / {{flagicon|RUS}} [[Nikolay Davydenko]], 5–1, 5–1
* It was Mirnyi's 5th title of the year and the 25th of his career. It was Youzhny's 1st title of the year and the 1st of his career.

===Women's Doubles===
{{main|2005 Kremlin Cup – Women's Doubles}}
{{flagicon|USA}} [[Lisa Raymond]] / {{flagicon|AUS}} [[Samantha Stosur]] defeated {{flagicon|ZIM}} [[Cara Black]] / {{flagicon|AUS}} [[Rennae Stubbs]], 6–2, 6–4
* It was Raymond's 5th title of the year and the 49th of his career. It was Stosur's 6th title of the year and the 6th of his career.
