'''Felipe Ochagavía Eguiguren''', (born 9 September 1993) is a [[Chilean people|Chilean]] retired [[association football|footballer]] that who played as [[goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]].

==Career==

===Youth career===

Ochagavía started his career at [[Primera División de Chile]] club [[O'Higgins F.C.|O'Higgins]]. He progressed from the under categories club all the way to the senior team.

===O'Higgins===
Ochagavía won the [[2013–14 Chilean Primera División season|Apertura 2013-14]] with [[O'Higgins F.C.|O'Higgins]]. In the tournament, he didn't play in any game.<ref>http://eltipografo.cl/2013/12/el-cielo-es-celeste/</ref>

In 2014, he won the [[Supercopa de Chile]] against [[Deportes Iquique]]. In that match, he was in the bench as the second goalkeeper.<ref>http://www.anfp.cl/noticia/20957/ohiggins-gano-la-supercopa-al-vencer-a-deportes-iquique</ref>

He participated with the club in the [[2014 Copa Libertadores]] where they faced [[Deportivo Cali]], [[Cerro Porteño]] and [[Club Atlético Lanús|Lanús]], being third and being eliminated in the group stage. 
<ref>http://www.cooperativa.cl/noticias/deportes/copa-libertadores/ohiggins/oa-higgins-se-quedo-en-blanco-con-lanus-y-fue-eliminado-de-la-copa-libertadores/2014-04-08/235905.html</ref>
