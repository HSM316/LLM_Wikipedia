'''Marcos Nogueira Eberlin''' (born 4 March 1959<ref name="urlAcademia Brasileira de Ciências">{{cite web |url=http://www.abc.org.br/resultadoPT.php3?codigo=meberlin |title=Academia Brasileira de Ciências |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160620214532/http://www.abc.org.br/resultadoPT.php3?codigo=meberlin |archive-date=2016-06-20 |url-status=dead }}</ref>) is a Brazilian [[chemist]] and former professor at the Institute of Chemistry of the [[University of Campinas]]. He is a member of the [[Brazilian Academy of Sciences]] and received the Brazilian [[National Order of Scientific Merit]] in 2005 and the [[International Mass Spectrometry Foundation#Awards|Thomson Medal]] in 2016.<ref name="urlAcademia Brasileira de Ciências"/>

Eberlin discovered the Eberlin Reaction during his work on [[Gas-phase ion chemistry|gas phase ion]] chemistry, and he and his research group introduced EASI<ref name="pmid18331004">{{cite journal |vauthors=Haddad R, Milagre HM, Catharino RR, Eberlin MN |title=Easy ambient sonic-spray ionization mass spectrometry combined with thin-layer chromatography |journal=Anal. Chem. |volume=80 |issue=8 |pages=2744–50 |year=2008 |pmid=18331004 |doi=10.1021/ac702216q }}</ref> (Easy Ambient Sonic-spray Ionization), an [[Ionization|ionization technique]] used in [[mass spectrometry]].

Eberlin is an advocate of the pseudoscience [[intelligent design]] in Brazil,<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Schall|first=Brunah|last2=Fernandes|first2=Victor|last3=Castelfranchi|first3=Yurij|date=2019|title="Não estou aqui para discutir aspectos religiosos": a defesa do criacionismo com argumentos tecnocientíficos|url=http://www.scielo.br/scielo.php?script=sci_arttext&pid=S0100-85872019000300197&tlng=pt|journal=Religião & Sociedade|language=pt|volume=39|issue=3|pages=197–220|doi=10.1590/0100-85872019v39n3cap09|issn=1984-0438|doi-access=free}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Silva|first=Heslley Machado|date=2020-03-22|title=Ark of Absurdities: Creationism Comes to Brazil.|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/342366162_Ark_of_Absurdities_Creationism_Comes_to_Brazil|journal=Skeptic|volume=25|issue=2|pages=26–31|issn=1063-9330}}</ref> on which he also lectures and he has signed the [[A Scientific Dissent From Darwinism|Dissent From Darwinism]] statement.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.discovery.org/scripts/viewDB/filesDB-download.php?command=download&id=660|title=A Scientific Dissent from Darwinism (List)}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dissentfromdarwin.org/index.php|title=A Scientific Dissent from Darwinism|access-date=2016-09-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151023065934/http://dissentfromdarwin.org/index.php|archive-date=2015-10-23|url-status=dead}}</ref> He is a [[Creationism|creationist]] also, and has said that [[Evolution|evolution theory]] is a [[fallacy]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://saude.estadao.com.br/noticias/geral,biologos-querem-reforcar-ensino-da-evolucao,866626|title=Biólogos querem reforçar ensino da evolução - Saúde - Estadão|newspaper=Estadão|access-date=2016-11-04}}</ref>
