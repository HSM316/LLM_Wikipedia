
The '''World Trade Center Tacoma''' (WTCTA) is an [[office building]] and [[trade center]] in downtown [[Tacoma, Washington]]. The World Trade Center is affiliated with the [[World Trade Center Association]] and is considered the only full-service center in the [[Pacific Northwest]].<ref name="Tribune">{{cite news |last=Martin |first=Kate |date=March 10, 2017 |title=Chamber takes reins at World Trade Center Tacoma |url=http://www.thenewstribune.com/news/business/article137851983.html |work=[[The News Tribune]] |location=Tacoma, Washington |accessdate=July 1, 2017}}</ref>

The World Trade Center Association of Tacoma was established in 1970 and was renamed to the World Trade Center Tacoma after licensing was approved by the World Trade Center Association.<ref>{{cite news |last=Davis |first=Brett |date=June 13, 2002 |title=Tacoma celebrates first annual World Trade Center Association Day |url=http://www.tacomadailyindex.com/blog/tacoma-celebrates-first-annual-world-trade-center-association-day/101096/ |work=[[Tacoma Daily Index]] |accessdate=July 1, 2017}}</ref> The organization was originally headquartered on [[Port of Tacoma]] property, but moved in 2003 to the Rust Building in downtown Tacoma.<ref>{{cite news |date=February 3, 2003 |title=Port of Tacoma buys World Trade Center Tacoma building |url=http://www.bizjournals.com/seattle/stories/2003/02/03/daily8.html |work=[[Puget Sound Business Journal]] |accessdate=July 1, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Davis |first=Brett |date=April 3, 2003 |title=WTC Tacoma, EDB, Chamber sign new agreement |url=http://www.tacomadailyindex.com/blog/wtc-tacoma-edb-chamber-sign-new-agreement/167091/ |work=Tacoma Daily Index |accessdate=July 1, 2017}}</ref>

Since 2017, the trade center has been managed by the [[Tacoma-Pierce County Chamber of Commerce]].<ref name="Tribune"/>
