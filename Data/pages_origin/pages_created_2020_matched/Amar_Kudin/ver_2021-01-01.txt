'''Amar Kudin''' (born 9 August 1992) is an [[Italy|Italian]] [[rugby union]] player who plays as a [[Hooker (rugby union)|Hooker]]. He currently competes for [[Fiamme Oro Rugby|Fiamme Oro]] in the [[Top12]].<ref>https://www.rugbymercato.it/prendono-forma-le-fiamme-di-casellato-in-arrivo-kudin-e-fragnito/</ref> <ref>http://www.fiammeororugby.it/news/player/amar-kudin/</ref>

Born in [[Croatia]], he moved to Italy at a young age, spending most of his time at local rugby youths. Amar spent a couple years with [[Benetton Rugby|Treviso Youth]] before joining Rugby San Dona on a professional contract. After a few successful seasons, he returned to his old club after Treviso's clearing out of half the team.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.rugbymercato.it/2014/08/26/il-benetton-pesca-ancora-in-casa-mogliano-ecco-amar-kudin// |title=The Benetton fishing still at home Mogliano here Amar Kudin, 26 August 2014 |access-date=13 November 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141113150944/http://www.rugbymercato.it/2014/08/26/il-benetton-pesca-ancora-in-casa-mogliano-ecco-amar-kudin/ |archive-date=13 November 2014 |url-status=dead }}</ref> In [[2014â€“15 Pro12]] season, he played for [[Benetton Rugby|Benetton]].<ref>[http://www.pro12rugby.com/teams/benetton/squad.php?player=99833&includeref=dynamic//''Guinness Pro 12'', Amar Kudin, September, 2014]</ref>

In 2011, Kudin was named in the [[Italy national under-20 rugby union team|Italy Under 20]] squad.
