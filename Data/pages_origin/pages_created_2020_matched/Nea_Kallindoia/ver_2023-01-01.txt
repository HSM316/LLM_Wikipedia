'''Nea Kallindoia''' ({{lang-el|Νέα Καλλίνδοια}}) is a [[Communities and Municipalities of Greece|community]] of the [[Lagkadas]] municipality.<ref name="Kallikratis">{{Cite web|url=http://www.et.gr/idocs-nph/search/pdfViewerForm.html?args=5C7QrtC22wGYK2xFpSwMnXdtvSoClrL81-32jgAMSfbnMRVjyfnPUeJInJ48_97uHrMts-zFzeyCiBSQOpYnT00MHhcXFRTsb2fGphpq4MKX2ZkaHobySNnvZCNHXvYVvlf80XevW0Q.|title=ΦΕΚ B 1292/2010, Kallikratis reform municipalities|language=el|publisher=[[Government Gazette (Greece)|Government Gazette]]}}</ref> Before the 2011 local government reform it was part of the municipality of [[Kallindoia]], of which it was a municipal district.<ref name="Kallikratis"/> The 2011 census recorded 563 inhabitants in the community.<ref name="census11"/> The community of Nea Kallindoia covers an area of 29.418&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>.<ref name=stat01>{{cite web|url=http://dlib.statistics.gr/Book/GRESYE_02_0101_00098%20.pdf|publisher=National Statistical Service of Greece|title=Population & housing census 2001 (incl. area and average elevation)|language=el}}</ref>

==Administrative division==
The community of Nea Kallindoia consists of two separate settlements:<ref name="census11"/> 
*[[Kalamoto]] (population 501)
*[[Mesokomo]] (population 62)
The aforementioned population figures are as of 2011.<ref name="census11"/>
