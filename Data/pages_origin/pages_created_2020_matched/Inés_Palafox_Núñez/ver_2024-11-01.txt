'''José Inés Palafox Núñez''' (14 September 1960 – 6 July 2018)<ref name=fallece/> was a Mexican politician from the [[National Action Party (Mexico)|National Action Party]] (PAN). From 2006 to 2009 he served in the [[Chamber of Deputies (Mexico)|Chamber of Deputies]] representing [[Sonora]]'s [[First federal electoral district of Sonora|first district]] during the [[LX Legislature of the Mexican Congress|60th Congress]].<ref name="sil">{{cite web |title=Perfil: Dip. José Inés Palafox Núñez, LX Legislatura |url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=2300698 |publisher=[[Secretariat of the Interior|SEGOB]] |website=Sistema de Información Legislativa (SIL) |accessdate=4 July 2024}}</ref>

Born in [[Zacatecas]], he moved to [[San Luis Río Colorado]] as a child.<ref name=fallece/> He served as a local deputy in the LV Legislature of the [[Congress of Sonora]] from 1997 to 2000 before becoming [[municipal president]] of San Luis Río Colorado.<ref name=sil/>
