'''José Inés Palafox Núñez''' (born 14 September 1960) is a [[Mexican people|Mexican]] politician from the [[National Action Party (Mexico)|National Action Party]]. From 2006 to 2009 he served as Deputy of the [[LX Legislature of the Mexican Congress]] representing [[Sonora]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Perfil del legislador|url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=2300698|publisher=Legislative Information System|accessdate=17 October 2014}}</ref>
