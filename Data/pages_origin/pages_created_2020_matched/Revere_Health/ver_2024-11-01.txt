'''Revere Health''' is the largest independent multispecialty physician group in [[Utah]] and southeastern [[Nevada]], [[United States]].<ref name="fierce">{{cite web|url=https://www.fiercehealthcare.com/hospitals/hospital-impact-2-keys-to-revere-health-s-value-based-care-model|title=Hospital Impact: 2 keys to Revere Health's value-based shift|last=Argule|first=Lexi|website=fiercehealthcare.com|publisher=Questex, LLC|date=22 Dec 2016|access-date=8 Oct 2018}}</ref>

==Description==
The company employs 175 physicians and 130 advanced practitioners<ref name="Redgrave">{{cite news|url=https://www.ksl.com/?sid=36077909|title=Revere Health|last=Redgrave|first=Chris|date=29 July 2015|publisher=KSL.com|access-date=19 Dec 2017}}</ref> at over 100 locations throughout Utah, as well as northern [[Arizona]] and southeastern [[Nevada]].

==History==
Founded in Provo in 1960, the company was formerly known as '''Central Utah Clinic'''.  However, in the early 2010s it began expanding into northern and southern Utah. With its wider service area it became prudent to change the identity of the company from its regional name. In [[Rebranding|rebranded]] itself as Revere Health in July 2015.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.heraldextra.com/business/local/property-solutions-changes-name-to-entrata-central-utah-clinic-now/article_201e776c-0d4a-5282-be20-c03151bcd8fc.html|title=Property Solutions changes name to Entrata; Central Utah Clinic now Revere Health|last=Neely|first=Karissa|newspaper=[[Daily Herald (Utah)|Daily Herald]]|publisher=[[Ogden Newspapers]]|location=Provo, Utah|date=8 Jul 2015|access-date=8 Oct 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://reverehealth.com/rebrand-page/|title=Central Utah Clinic Is Now Revere Health|website=reverehealth.com|publisher=Revere Health|access-date=8 Oct 2018}}</ref>

In 2012, Revere Health became the first healthcare organization in Utah to be [[Accreditation|accredited]] as an [[accountable care organization]].<ref name="fierce"/>

In 2021 it was revealed that the group was impacted by a phishing attack the lead to the exposure of around 12,000 patients.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Coble|first=Sarah|date=2021-08-25|title=Revere Health Data Breach Impacts Cardiology Patients|url=https://www.infosecurity-magazine.com/news/revere-health-breach-impacts/|access-date=2021-09-10|website=Infosecurity Magazine}}</ref>
