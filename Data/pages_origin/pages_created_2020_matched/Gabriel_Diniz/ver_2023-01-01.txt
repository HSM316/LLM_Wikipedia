'''José Gabriel de Souza Diniz'''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.acritica.com/channels/cotidiano/news/gabriel-diniz-aviao-com-cantor-do-sucesso-jeniffer-cai-em-sergipe|title=Gabriel Diniz: avião com cantor do sucesso 'Jenifer' cai em Sergipe {{!}} Cotidiano|website=A Crítica|language=en}}</ref> (18 October 1990 – 27 May 2019), better known as '''Gabriel Diniz''', was a Brazilian singer-songwriter.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://heloisatolipan.com.br/musica/o-nome-dele-e-gabriel-diniz-conheca-o-cantor-por-tras-do-hit-jenifer/|title=O nome dele é Gabriel Diniz: Conheça o cantor por trás do hit Jenifer|website=Heloisa Tolipan|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ombrelo.com.br/musica/gabriel-diniz-conheca-cantor-musica-jenifer/|title=Gabriel Diniz: Conheça o cantor de "Jenifer", hit do verão 2019|date=2019-01-18|website=Ombrelo|language=pt-BR}}</ref>

==Career==
Born in [[Campo Grande]], [[Mato Grosso do Sul]], he released three studio albums during his short career. In 2017, he released the song titled "Acabou Acabou", a collaboration with [[Wesley Safadão]].<ref name=UOL>{{Cite news|url=https://entretenimento.uol.com.br/noticias/redacao/2019/05/27/gabriel-diniz-do-hit-jenifer-se-envolve-em-acidente-de-aviao-em-sergipe.htm|title=Gabriel Diniz, do hit "Jenifer", morre em acidente de avião em Sergipe|publisher=Universo Online|access-date=27 May 2019|date=27 May 2019|language=pt}}</ref>

Diniz became nationally known by the single "Jenifer", a song that was the most played on the radio and music streaming sites of [[Brazil]], becoming the hit of the summer of 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://g1.globo.com/fantastico/noticia/2019/01/13/musica-jenifer-sucesso-de-gabriel-diniz-foi-composta-por-oito-pessoas.ghtml|title=Música 'Jenifer', sucesso de Gabriel Diniz, foi composta por oito pessoas|website=G1|language=pt-br}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vejasp.abril.com.br/cultura-lazer/jenifer-cache-gabriel-diniz/|title=Cachê do cantor Gabriel Diniz vai às alturas com sucesso de 'Jenifer'|website=VEJA SÃO PAULO|language=pt-BR}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://f5.folha.uol.com.br/voceviu/2019/01/sao-paulo-e-cidade-com-mais-jenifers-no-tinder-hit-do-verao-alavanca-buscas-pelo-aplicativo.shtml|title=São Paulo é cidade com mais Jenifers no Tinder; hit do verão alavanca buscas pelo aplicativo|date=2019-01-15|website=F5|language=pt-BR}}</ref>

Although born in Campo Grande, he moved with his family to [[João Pessoa, Paraíba|João Pessoa, in Paraíba]](northeast region) where he was raised. Gabriel Diniz started his career as a musician singing at some parties at his college, where he studied electrical engineering. His first steps started with his friends, who created a garage band, attracting businessmen from the city. He went through bands like Campim com Mel and Cavaleiros do Forró, and later joined his main band: Forró na Farra, between 2010 and 2011.

In 2013, he changed the name of his band to his own name: Gabriel Diniz. He innovated by joining two musical genres typical of brazil's northeast region: [[Forró]] and [[:pt:Pagode baiano|swingueira]] (pagode baiano), highlighting percussion.

== Death ==
On 27 May 2019, Diniz died in a plane crash after a concert in [[Sergipe]] in [[Estância, Sergipe|Estância]], [[Brazil]] on his way to surprise his girlfriend for her birthday party.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.otempo.com.br/brasil/avi%C3%A3o-onde-estava-o-cantor-gabriel-diniz-do-hit-jenifer-cai-em-sergipe-1.2186993|title=Avião onde estava o cantor cai em Sergipe|date=May 27, 2019|publisher=O Tempo|access-date=May 27, 2019}}</ref> The aircraft was a [[Piper PA-28 Cherokee]] manufactured in 1974 [[aircraft registration|registered]] PT-KLO.<ref name=G1>{{cite web|url=https://g1.globo.com/se/sergipe/noticia/2019/05/27/corpo-de-bombeiros-e-acionado-para-atender-acidente-envolvendo-queda-de-bimotor.ghtml|title=Gabriel Diniz, cantor de ´Jenifer´, morre aos 28 anos em queda de avião em Sergipe|date=May 27, 2019|access-date=May 27, 2019|website=[[G1 (website)|G1]]|language=pt-br}}</ref>
