'''Jessica Matten''' is a Canadian actress and [[film producer]].

== Early life and education ==
Matten was born in [[Edmonton]], [[Alberta]].<ref name="griwkowsky">{{cite news |last1=Griwkowsky |first1=Fish |title=Edmonton-born actress brings edge to fur-trade TV drama Frontier |url=https://edmontonjournal.com/entertainment/television/edmonton-born-actress-brings-edge-to-fur-trade-history-drama-frontier |access-date=3 August 2022 |work=[[Edmonton Journal]] |date=2016-11-04 |language=en-CA}}</ref> She is of Red River [[Métis]] and [[Cree#In Canada|Cree]] heritage through her mother.<ref name="griwkowsky" /><ref name="puckett">{{cite news |last1=Puckett-Pope |first1=Lauren |title=Jessica Matten of 'Dark Winds' is Ready to Lead the First Indigenous Superhero Movie |url=https://www.elle.com/culture/movies-tv/a40433343/jessica-matten-dark-winds-superhero-interview/ |access-date=3 August 2022 |work=ELLE |date=27 June 2022 |language=en-us}}</ref> 

From the age of six, she assisted with her mother's First Nations modeling agency around Canada. She also worked for her father in marketing for several years before becoming an actor.<ref name="Larue 2011">{{cite web | last=Larue | first=Frank | title=Jessica Yee: Positive Beauty | website=First Nations Drum Newspaper | date=2011-03-16 | url=http://www.firstnationsdrum.com/2011/03/jessica-yee-positive-beauty/ | access-date=2022-07-11}}</ref>

Matten graduated from the [[University of Alberta]] with a science degree in [[human ecology]].<ref name="griwkowsky" />

== Career ==
Matten appeared in five international/Native People’s television series in eight years including Discovery Canada and [[Netflix]] series ''[[Frontier (2016 TV series)|Frontier]]'' starring [[Jason Momoa]] and CW Network and [[Canadian Broadcasting Corporation|CBC]] drama series ''[[Burden of Truth (TV series)|Burden of Truth]]''. In 2020, Matten began starring in ''[[Tribal (TV series)|Tribal]]'' on the [[APTN (Canada)|APTN]] Network.  She plays the newly appointed chief of an [[indigenous police in Canada|indigenous police]] force in [[Alberta]], Canada. She was also an associate producer on the series.<ref>{{cite web|accessdate=November 26, 2023|url=https://cowgirlmagazine.com/jessica-matten/|website=Cowgirlmagazine|title=Jessica Matten - The Dark Winds Series Actress is Leading the Generation Next Indigenous Movement|first=Wendy|last= Wilkinson|date=March 15, 2023}}</ref> 

She also previously starred in the short film ''A Red Girl’s Reasoning'' which received an award at the [[imagineNATIVE Film and Media Arts Festival|Imagine NATIVE Film Festival]]. In 2020, Matten appeared in the horror movie ''[[The Empty Man (film)|The Empty Man]]'' for Disney/20th Century Fox studios. <ref name="Deadline">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2022/09/dark-winds-jessica-matten-signs-m88-1235123222/amp/|website=Deadline Hollywood|accessdate=26 November 2023|title= 'Dark Winds' Star Jessica Matten Signs With M88|first=Rosy|last=Cordero|date=September 20, 2022}}</ref>

In 2022, Matten began co-starring in the TV crime drama series ''[[Dark Winds]]''. She plays Sergeant Bernadette Manuelito, the only female police officer on the Navajo reservation.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Gomez |first1=Adrian |title=Breaking boundaries: Jessica Matten returns as Sgt. Bernadette Manuelito in 'Dark Winds' |url=https://www.abqjournal.com/breaking-boundaries-jessica-matten-returns-as-sgt-bernadette-manuelito-in-dark-winds/article_acfa77d8-2994-11ee-b0d6-9be78ec16435.html |access-date=10 November 2023 |work=Albuquerque Journal |date=28 July 2023 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Miller |first1=Stuart |title='Dark Winds' actress Jessica Matten is 'all about representation' |url=https://www.bostonglobe.com/2023/07/26/arts/dark-winds-actress-jessica-matten-is-all-about-representation/ |access-date=10 November 2023 |work=The Boston Globe |date=26 July 2023}}</ref>

==Other activities ==
Matten is a co-founder of The Counting Coup Indigenous Film Academy (“CCIFA”), an educational environment for creative work, where emerging and aspiring First Nations, Metis and Inuit artists can gain expertise and experience in film.<ref name="Deadline"/><ref>{{cite news |last1=Trembath |first1=Terri |title='Dark Winds' actor helps First Nations students get into film industry through college course |url=https://www.cbc.ca/amp/1.6562981 |access-date=26 November 2023 |work=CBC News |date=26 August 2022}}</ref> She is also the President of 7 Forward Entertainment, an Indigenous-owned production company based on the West Coast of Canada.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://wwd.com/eye/people/dark-winds-jessica-matten-empower-women-1235303550/|website=wwd.com|accessdate=26 November 2023|first=Booth|last=Moore|title= ‘Dark Winds’ Jessica Matten: “My Entire Life’s Purpose Is to Empower Women’}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Matten is a direct descendant of [[Cuthbert Grant]], the first rebel [[Métis people (Canada)|Métis]] leader, known for the Battle of the Seven Oaks in Canada.<ref name="Deadline"/>
