The [[New England Black Wolves]] are a [[indoor lacrosse|lacrosse]] team based in [[Uncasville, Connecticut]] playing in the [[National Lacrosse League]] (NLL). The [[2018 NLL season|2018 season]] will be the team's 4th season in the league.
