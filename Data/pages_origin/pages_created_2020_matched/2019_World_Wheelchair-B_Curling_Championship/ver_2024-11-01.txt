The '''2019 [[World Wheelchair Curling Championship|World Wheelchair-B Curling Championship]]''' were held from November 27 to December 2 in [[Lohja]], [[Finland]]. The championship was used to qualify three teams for the [[2020 World Wheelchair Curling Championship]] in [[Wetzikon]], [[Switzerland]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://worldcurling.org/events/wwhbcc2019/|title=World Wheelchair-B Curling Championship 2019|website=World Curling Federation|access-date=November 26, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://worldcurling.org/2019/11/wwhbcc2019-preview/|title=World Wheelchair-B Curling Championship 2019 set for Lohja, Finland|website=World Curling Federation|last=Gray|first=Richard|date=November 25, 2019|access-date=November 26, 2019}}</ref>

In the final, Canada defeated Sweden by a score of 6-1. This meant Canada, Sweden and bronze medal winners Czech Republic all qualified for the 2020 World Championships.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://worldcurling.org/2019/12/wwhbcc2019-canada-gold/|title=Canada win gold at the World Wheelchair-B Curling Championship 2019|website=World Curling Federation|access-date=December 2, 2019}}</ref>
