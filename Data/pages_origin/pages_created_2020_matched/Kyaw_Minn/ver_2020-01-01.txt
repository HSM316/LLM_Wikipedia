'''Kyaw Minn''' ({{lang-my|ကျော်မင်း}}, also spelt '''Kyaw Min'''; born 20 June 1961) is a [[Burma|Burmese]] politician who currently serves as a [[Pyithu Hluttaw]] member of parliament for [[Letpadan Township]]. He is a member of the [[National League for Democracy]]. In the [[2015 Myanmar general election]], he contested the Letpadan Township constituency for a seat in the [[Pyithu Hluttaw]] MP, the country's [[lower house]].<ref name="Pyidaungsu Hluttaw">{{cite web|url= https://www.pyidaungsu.hluttaw.mm/members/56cc363f3f609e2b2c8b45ec |title=MP profile|work=[[Pyidaungsu Hluttaw]]|date=|accessdate=13 August 2018|language=my}}</ref>

== Early life and education ==
Kyaw Minn was born on 20 June 1961 in Kannarsu Village, [[Letpadan Township]], [[Bago Region]]. He graduated with [[B.Sc]] (Maths) from [[Yangon University]]. His former work is millers.<ref name="Pyidaungsu Hluttaw"/>

== Political career==
He is a member of the [[National League for Democracy]] [[Party]], he was elected as a [[Pyithu Hluttaw]] MP elected representative from Letpantan
parliamentary constituency.<ref name="Pyidaungsu Hluttaw"/>
