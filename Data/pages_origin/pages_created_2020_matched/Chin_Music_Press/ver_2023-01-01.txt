'''Chin Music Press''' is a [[Book Publishing|book publishing]] company known for its high-quality editions.<ref name="Boing-accolades">{{cite web|last1=Marshall|first1=Colin|title=Here's the rare American publisher who understands the printed book|url=http://boingboing.net/2015/02/11/heres-the-rare-american-publ.html|website=Boing-Boing|date=11 February 2015|accessdate=18 April 2016}}</ref>

==History==
The Press was founded in Seattle in 2002 by Bruce Rutledge and Yuko Enomoto.<ref name="Japan-Times">{{cite web|last1=Kosaka|first1=Kris|title=Small presses fill a niche in books about Japan|url=http://www.japantimes.co.jp/culture/2014/05/24/books/small-presses-fill-niche-books-japan/|website=The Japan Times|date=24 May 2014|accessdate=18 April 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=About Chin Music Press|url=http://chinmusicpress.com/about.php|website=Chin Music Press|accessdate=14 April 2016}}</ref> They began by publishing books on contemporary Japan, but have expanded to include books on [[New Orleans]], China, and Korea.<ref>{{cite web|title=Welcome to Chin Music Press|url=http://chinmusicpress.com/index.php|website=Chin Music Press|accessdate=14 April 2016}}</ref>

After being located in various locations around Seattle for many years, in 2014 the company opened a store in Seattle's [[Pike Place Market]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Indie Publisher Opens Book Store in Seattle|url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/by-topic/industry-news/bookselling/article/63346-indie-publisher-opens-bookstore-in-seattle.html|website=Publishers Weekly|accessdate=14 April 2016}}</ref><ref name="Sunbreak-pike-store">{{cite web|title=A look inside Chin Music Press, Seattle's newest bookstore|url=http://thesunbreak.com/2014/07/22/a-look-inside-chin-music-press-seattles-newest-bookstore/|website=The Sun Break|accessdate=18 April 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160206212822/http://thesunbreak.com/2014/07/22/a-look-inside-chin-music-press-seattles-newest-bookstore/|archive-date=6 February 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="Examiner-pike-store">{{cite web|last1=Pai|first1=Shin Yu|title=Japanese-inspired independent publisher Chin Music opens showroom in Pike Place Market|url=http://www.iexaminer.org/2014/10/japanese-inspired-independent-publisher-chin-music-opens-showroom-in-pike-place-market/|website=The International Examiner|accessdate=18 April 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160514025328/http://www.iexaminer.org/2014/10/japanese-inspired-independent-publisher-chin-music-opens-showroom-in-pike-place-market/|archive-date=14 May 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>
