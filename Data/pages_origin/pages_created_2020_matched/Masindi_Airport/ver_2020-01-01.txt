'''Masindi Airport''' {{airport codes|KCU|HUMI}} is an [[airport]] in [[Uganda]].

==Location==
Masindi Airport is in the Kyema suburb of the town of [[Masindi]], [[Masindi District]], [[Bunyoro sub-region]], in the [[Western Region, Uganda|Western Region]]. It is approximately {{convert|199|km|0}}, by air, north-west of [[Entebbe International Airport]], the country's largest civilian and military airport.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://distancecalculator.globefeed.com/Uganda_Distance_Result.asp?fromplace=Entebbe%20Airport%20()%20&toplace=Masindi%20(Masindi)&fromlat=0.042386&tolat=1.6744444&fromlng=32.443503&tolng=31.715 | title=Flight Distance Between Entebbe Airport And Masindi Airport With Map | work=Distance Calculator | publisher=Globefeed.com | accessdate=2 August 2011 | quote=198.72 km }}</ref>
This is approximately {{convert|10|km|0}}, by road, directly north of the central business district of the town.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://distancecalculator.globefeed.com/Uganda_Distance_Result.asp?%20fromplace=Masindi%20(Masindi)&toplace=Kyema%20(Masindi)%20&fromlat=1.6744444&tolat=1.7352778&fromlng=31.715&tolng=31.7486111|title=Distance between ,  and Kyema (Masindi)  (Uganda)|website=distancecalculator.globefeed.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-08-29}}</ref>

The Masindi [[non-directional beacon]] (ident: '''MS''') is within the town, approximately {{convert|6|km}} south of the Runway 01 threshold.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://skyvector.com/?ll=1.69694,31.718&chart=301&zoom=1&chart=301&zoom=1|title=SkyVector: Flight Planning / Aeronautical Charts|website=skyvector.com|access-date=2018-08-29}}</ref>
