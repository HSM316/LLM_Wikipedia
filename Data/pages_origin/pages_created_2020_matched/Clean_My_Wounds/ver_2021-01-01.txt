"'''Clean My Wounds'''" is a song by American [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band [[Corrosion of Conformity]]. It was released as the third single from the band's fourth studio album ''[[Deliverance (Corrosion of Conformity album)|Deliverance]]''. Written by lead vocalist/guitarist [[Pepper Keenan]], "Clean My Wounds" was a hit on rock radio, peaking at No. 19 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks|Mainstream Rock]] chart, the band's highest charting single along with "[[Albatross (Corrosion of Conformity song)|Albatross]]" (which also peaked at No. 19).<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Prometheus Global Media]]|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/corrosion-of-conformity/chart-history/hot-mainstream-rock-tracks/song/110371|title=Corrosion of Conformity Clean My Wounds Chart History|accessdate=April 2, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Prometheus Global Media]]|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/corrosion-of-conformity/chart-history/hot-mainstream-rock-tracks|title=Corrosion of Conformity Chart History|accessdate=April 2, 2018}}</ref>

The [[B-side]] "Big Problems" originally appeared on [[Clerks: Music from the Motion Picture|the soundtrack]] for the 1994 film ''[[Clerks (1994 film)|Clerks]]''.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/clerks-mw0000119180|title=Clerks - Original Soundtrack|accessdate=April 2, 2018}}</ref>

==Live performances==
The song has been played live 239 times, making it the band's second most performed song; only "Vote with a Bullet" has been played live more with 271 performances.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Setlist.fm|url=https://www.setlist.fm/stats/songs/corrosion-of-conformity-63d68e9b.html?song=Clean+My+Wounds|title=Clean My Wounds by Corrosion of Conformity Song Statistics|accessdate=April 2, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=Setlist.fm|url=https://www.setlist.fm/stats/songs/corrosion-of-conformity-63d68e9b.html?song=Vote+With+a+Bullet|title=Vote With a Bullet by Corrosion of Conformity Song Statistics|accessdate=April 2, 2018}}</ref> A live version of "Clean My Wounds" appears on the 2001 release ''[[Live Volume]]''.
