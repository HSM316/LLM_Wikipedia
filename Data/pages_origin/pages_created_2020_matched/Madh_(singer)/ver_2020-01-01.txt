'''Marco Cappai''' (born 13 November 1993 in [[Carbonia, Sardinia|Carbonia]], Sardinia), known by his stage name '''Madh''', meaning "''my advice doesn't help''",<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.davidemaggio.it/archives/107681/x-factor-8-madh | work = davidemaggio.it | language = Italian | accessdate = 23 October 2014 | title = X Factor, Madh | author = Davide Maggio}}</ref> is an [[Italians|Italian]] singer-songwriter. He rose to fame in 2014, at the eighth season of the Italian edition of ''[[X Factor]]''. His debut [[single (music)|single]], "Sayonara", was performed for the first time during the semi-finals of the competition. Also included in the [[extended play]] with the same name, the single reached the second spot of the [[Italian Singles Chart]], and it was certified platinum by the [[Federation of the Italian Music Industry]].<ref name=CertificationsSingles>{{cite certification|region=Italy|type=single|artist=Madh|accessdate=20 December 2014}}</ref><ref name=SinglesChart>{{cite web|url=http://www.fimi.it/classifiche_digital.php|publisher=[[Federation of the Italian Music Industry]]|language=Italian|accessdate=20 December 2014|archivedate=20 December 2014|title=Top Digital - Classifica settimanale WK 50 (dal 08-12-2014 al 14-12-2014)|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141220123009/http://www.fimi.it/classifiche_digital.php}}</ref>

==Early life==
Marco Cappai was born on 13 November 1993 in [[Carbonia, Sardinia|Carbonia]], a ''[[comune]]'' in the former [[province of Carbonia-Iglesias]] (now [[province of South Sardinia]]), in [[Sardinia]], Italy, where he also spent childhood.

==Music career==

===2011–2014===
In 2011, Madh began creating songs with his brother. In 2012, he met some music publishers and record producers, and decided to found an [[independent label]], the ''SEMS Music''. He started self-producing [[video clips]] and uploading them on [[YouTube]] and on other various platforms. At the start of his musical career, he performed in clubs, bars and disco clubs in [[Sardinia]]. He won the first prize of a traditional Sardinian contest "''Corrida Sulcis''".<ref name="X Factor Italia 8">{{cite web | url = http://xfactor.sky.it/2014/08/31/madh-2/ | work = [[Sky Italia]] | language = Italian | accessdate = 31 August 2014 | title = Cartà d'identità Madh | author = X Factor Italia 8}}</ref>

==Musical style and influences==
His passion and love for music have been influenced by the [[Eastern culture]] and [[Eastern philosophy|philosophy]]. Five of the most important songs of his life are: "[[Love Is A Losing Game]]" and "[[You Know I'm No Good]]" ([[Amy Winehouse]]), "[[Redemption Song]]" ([[Bob Marley]]), "[[Ready or Not (Fugees song)|Ready Or Not]]" ([[Fugees]]), Exodus ([[M.I.A. (artist)|MIA]]).<ref name="X Factor Italia 8"/>
