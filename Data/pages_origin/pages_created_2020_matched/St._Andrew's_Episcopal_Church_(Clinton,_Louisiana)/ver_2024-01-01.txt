'''St. Andrew's Episcopal Church''' is a historic [[Carpenter Gothic]] [[Episcopal Church (United States)|Episcopal Church]]  built in 1871 and located at the corner of Church Street and St. Andrew's in [[Clinton, Louisiana]].<ref name=ladoc>{{cite web|url=https://www.crt.state.la.us/dataprojects/hp/nhl/attachments/Parish19/Scans/19018001.pdf|title=St. Andrew's Episcopal Church|publisher=State of Louisiana's Division of Historic Preservation |author= |date=|accessdate=May 22, 2018}} with [https://www.crt.state.la.us/dataprojects/hp/nhl/view.asp?ID=289 three photos and a map]</ref><ref name=nrhpdoc>{{cite web|url={{NRHP url|id=84001282}}|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination Form: St. Andrew's Episcopal Church|publisher=[[National Park Service]]|author=State of Louisiana's Division of Historic Preservation|date=January 1984|accessdate=May 22, 2018}} With {{NRHP url|id=84001282|photos=y|title=six photos from 1983}}.</ref>

Parts of the 1958 movie, ''[[The Long, Hot Summer]]'', which starred [[Paul Newman]], [[Lee Remick]] and [[Joanne Woodward]], were filmed at the church.<ref>[https://www.amazon.com/The-Long-Hot-Summer/dp/B000UL45BO Amazon.com Theatricl release Information on The Long, Hot Summer]</ref> St. Andrew's Episcopal Church is still an active parish in the [[Episcopal Diocese of Louisiana]].<ref>''Episcopal Church Annual, 2004'', Harrisburg: Morehouse Publishing, p. 237</ref>

The church was listed on the [[National Register of Historic Places]] on June 21, 1984.<ref name="nris"/>

The congregation meets at 9 A.M. each Sunday for Holy Eucharist three Sundays a month and Morning Prayer on the other Sunday(s). Coffee and fellowship follow immediately in the parish hall. All are welcome.

The church is located at 11015 Church St., Clinton, LA 70722. The phone number is (225) 683-5498.
