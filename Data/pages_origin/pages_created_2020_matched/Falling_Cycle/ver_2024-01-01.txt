'''Falling Cycle''' was a [[Christian metal]]/[[Christian hardcore]]<ref name=BK>{{cite web|url=http://bkmusic777.blogspot.com/2015/07/falling-cycle-conflict-2002.html?m=1|title=Falling Cycle - ''The Conflict''|publisher=BKMusic777|date=July 20, 2015|accessdate=October 13, 2015}}</ref> band from [[Riverside, California|Riverside]], [[California]]. They have been compared to [[Darkest Hour (band)|Darkest Hour]] and [[No Innocent Victim]].<ref name=AllMusic>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/the-conflict-mw0000223409|title=Falling Cycle - ''The Conflict''|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|first=Kurt|last=Morris|accessdate=October 13, 2015}}</ref> Guitarist Sean Durham joined [[Sinai Beach]] soon after the band broke-up.

==Musical style==
The band originally played [[hardcore punk]],<ref name=BK/> but mainly played [[metalcore]]<ref name=ChristCore/> and [[death metal]].<ref name=CrossRhythms/> Along with elements on [[speed metal]],<ref name=AllMusic/> [[grind metal]],<ref name=AllMusic/> and [[melodic death metal]].<ref name=Metallum>{{cite web|url=http://www.metal-archives.com/bands/Falling_Cycle/60169|title=Falling Cycle|publisher=[[Encyclopedia Metallum]]|author=TheStormIRide|date=March 12, 2006|accessdate=October 14, 2015}}</ref>
