'''Luigi Druetti''' (1853–1919)<ref>[http://www.worldstatesmen.org/Libya.htm Libya]</ref> was an Italian general. He was the governor of Tripolitania from November 1914 to February 1915.

At the beginning of World War I, he commanded the 5th division of corpus III.<ref>Compiled from information in L’Esercito italiano nella grande guerra, Vol I-bis, pp. 75–104</ref>
