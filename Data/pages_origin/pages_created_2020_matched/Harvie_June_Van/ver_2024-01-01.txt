'''Harvie June Van''' (née Vanderpool; born March 2, 1940) is a retired country music singer. She was born in Monterey, Tennessee.<ref name=hillybilly>

</ref> She came from a family of musicians, and her father had a local radio show in Ohio.<ref name=hillybilly2 />

==Personal life==

She married her manager [[Bob Ferguson (music)|Bob Ferguson]], who would later become a [[RCA Records]] producer and executive. They would adopt two [[Indigenous peoples of the Americas|Native American]] children. Van would later divorce him. Today she lives in the Nashville area.
