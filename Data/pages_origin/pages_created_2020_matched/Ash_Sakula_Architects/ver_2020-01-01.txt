'''Ash Sakula Architects''' is a small architecture practice based in [[London]], founded in 1996.<ref name="Britbest">[http://www.bestarchitects.co.uk/ash-sakula-architects.asp Ash Sakula Architects] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120425231000/http://www.bestarchitects.co.uk/ash-sakula-architects.asp |date=2012-04-25 }}, Britains Best Architects. Retrieved 2011-11-16.</ref>. The practice is known for projects that are community-focused, environmentally sustainable and materials-driven.

Ash Sakula was formed by architects Cany Ash and Robert Sakula. Ash previously worked for the [[Greater London Council]] and Burrell Foley Fischer, and in Berlin and New York. She is an external examiner for Cambridge University.<ref>[http://women-in-architecture.com/index.php?id=31&tx_wiaprofiles_pi1%5Bprofile%5D=66&tx_wiaprofiles_pi1%5Baction%5D=show&tx_wiaprofiles_pi1%5Bcontroller%5D=Profile&cHash=5b11386b3e65ee48a5d09d1b0d0d2821 Ash Sakula] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120425231000/http://women-in-architecture.com/index.php?id=31&tx_wiaprofiles_pi1%5Bprofile%5D=66&tx_wiaprofiles_pi1%5Baction%5D=show&tx_wiaprofiles_pi1%5Bcontroller%5D=Profile&cHash=5b11386b3e65ee48a5d09d1b0d0d2821 |date=2012-04-25 }}, Women in Architecture. Retrieved 2011-11-16.</ref> Sakula graduated from Liverpool University before working for [[Clough Williams-Ellis|Sir Clough Williams-Ellis]], [[David Lea (architect)|David Lea]] and [[DEGW|DEGW Architects]].<ref name="SAWSA Sakula">[http://www.cardiff.ac.uk/archi/sawsa/2010-11/logo%20dec.pdf Design Circle, RSAW South - Robert Sakula 10 December]{{dead link|date=October 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, SAWSA webpages, Cardiff University, 2010. Retrieved 2011-11-16.</ref>
