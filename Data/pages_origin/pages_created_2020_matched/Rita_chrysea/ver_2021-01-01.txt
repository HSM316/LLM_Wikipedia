'''''Rita chrysea''''', or the '''Mahanadi rita''', is a species of [[bagrid catfish]] [[endemism|endemic]] to [[India]] where it inhabits the [[Mahanadi River]] system in [[Orissa, India|Orissa]] and [[Madhya Pradesh]]. It is found in [[river]]s and large [[stream]]s. Spawning occurs during the [[monsoon]] months. It grows to a length of 19.5&nbsp;cm and is [[commercial fishery|commercially fished]] for human consumption.
