'''José Luis Varela Lagunas''' (born 21 December 1946) is a Mexican politician formerly affiliated with the [[Citizens' Movement (Mexico)|Convergencia]]. 

In the [[2006 Mexican general election|2006 general election]] he was elected to the [[Chamber of Deputies (Mexico)|Chamber of Deputies]] to represent the [[Eighth federal electoral district of Oaxaca|eighth district]] of [[Oaxaca]] during the [[LX Legislature of the Mexican Congress|60th Congress]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Perfil: Dip. Tomás José Luis Varela Lagunas, LX Legislatura |url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=2300857 |publisher=[[Secretariat of the Interior|SEGOB]] |website=Sistema de Información Legislativa (SIL) |accessdate=1 August 2024}}</ref>

He resigned his party membership in June 2010.<ref>{{cite news|title=Renuncia Varela Lagunas al Partido Convergencia|url=http://ciudadania-express.com/2010/06/30/renuncia-varela-lagunas-al-partido-covergencia/|accessdate=13 October 2014|date=30 June 2010}}</ref>
