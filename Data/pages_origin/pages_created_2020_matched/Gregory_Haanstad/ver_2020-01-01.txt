'''Gregory Haanstad''' is an American attorney and the former [[United States Attorney]] for the [[Eastern District of Wisconsin]].  He was appointed to the post on an acting basis on August 1, 2015.<ref name=DiedrichMasonic>{{cite news|last1=Diedrich|first1=John|title=FBI thwarts mass shooting at Milwaukee Masonic center|url=http://www.jsonline.com/news/crime/fbi-thwarts-mass-shooting-at-milwaukee-masonic-temple-b99659005z1-366609371.html|accessdate=27 January 2016|publisher=Milwaukee Journal Sentinel|date=26 January 2016}}</ref>  He had served as first assistant to U.S. attorney [[James Santelle]] since 2010.<ref name=SpivakSantelle>{{cite news|last1=Spivak|first1=Cary|title=James Santelle serves last day as U.S. attorney|url=http://www.jsonline.com/news/statepolitics/james-santelle-serves-last-day-as-us-attorney-b99548610z1-320398652.html|accessdate=27 January 2016|publisher=Journal Sentinel|date=1 August 2015}}</ref>

Haanstadt graduated from the [[University of Wisconsin]] Law School.<ref name=DOJbio>{{cite news|title=Gregory Haanstadt|url=http://www.justice.gov/usao-edwi/meet-us-attorney|accessdate=27 January 2016|publisher=Department of Justice}}</ref>
