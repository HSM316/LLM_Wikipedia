'''Joana Robles Partida''' (born 26 July 1994) is a Mexican [[women's association football|footballer]] who plays as an [[Midfielder#Attacking midfielder|attacking midfielder]] for [[Atlético San Luis (women)|Atlético San Luis]]  and the [[Mexico women's national football team|Mexico women's national team]].

==International career==
Robles made her senior debut for Mexico on 27 February 2019 in a [[Exhibition game|friendly match]] against [[Italy women's national soccer team|Italy]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://deportes.televisa.com/futbol/aqui-esta-el-tri/pierde-mexico-femenil-copa-chipre/ |title=Pierde México Femenil en Copa Chipre |publisher=Televisa Deportes |accessdate=1 March 2019 |language=Spanish |archive-date=2 March 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190302024859/https://deportes.televisa.com/futbol/aqui-esta-el-tri/pierde-mexico-femenil-copa-chipre/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>
