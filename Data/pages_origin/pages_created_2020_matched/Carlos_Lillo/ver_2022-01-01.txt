'''Carlos Lillo''' (born October 2, 1915, date of death unknown) was a [[Chile]]an [[Boxing|boxer]], who competed in the [[1936 Summer Olympics]].

In 1936, he was eliminated in the quarter-finals of the [[Boxing at the 1936 Summer Olympics - Men's lightweight|lightweight class]], after losing his fight to the upcoming silver medalist [[Nikolai Stepulov]]. Due to a scoring miscalculation, [[Thomas Hamilton-Brown]] was eliminated until the error was discovered several days later.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ca.sports.yahoo.com/olympics/news?slug=ys-forbesolyblunders020310&prov=yhoo&type=lgns|title=Olympics on Yahoo!
