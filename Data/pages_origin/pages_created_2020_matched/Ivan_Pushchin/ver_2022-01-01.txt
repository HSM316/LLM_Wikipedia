'''Ivan Ivanovich Pushchin'''  ([[Russian language|Russian]]: Ива́н Ива́нович Пу́щин); 4 (15)  May 1798, [[Moscow]] – 3 (15) April 1859, the estate Marino, Bronnitsy district of [[Moscow Governorate]] (now the [[Ramensky District]] of [[Moscow Oblast]]), buried in [[Bronnitsy]], near the city's cathedral) was a [[Decembrist]],<ref name=pushin>[http://decemb.hobby.ru/index.shtml?alphavit/alf_p#i_puschin Музей декабристов]</ref> Collegiate assessor<ref>[http://slovari.yandex.ru/dict/revoluc/article/re1/re1-0694.htm Пущин Иван Иванович — Революционеры — Яндекс.Словари<!-- Заголовок добавлен ботом -->]</ref> and poet.
