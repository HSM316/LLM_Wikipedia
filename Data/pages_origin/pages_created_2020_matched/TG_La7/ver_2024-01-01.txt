'''''TG La7''''' (''TeleGiornale La7'') is the brand for Italian TV channel [[La7]]'s [[news program]]mes. They are shown domestically on [[La7]] several times throughout the day. It was launched in 2001.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ilmessaggero.it/articolo.php?id=108983&sez=HOME_INITALIA|title=La 7, comincia l<nowiki>'</nowiki>era di Mentana da venerdì dirigerà il telegiornale|publisher=[[Il Messaggero]]|language=it|date=July 1, 2010|accessdate=January 19, 2021|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924050216/http://www.ilmessaggero.it/articolo.php?id=108983&sez=HOME_INITALIA|archivedate=September 24, 2015}}</ref>

==Programme format==
The programme is generally presented by a single [[News presenter|newsreader]] but with additional newsreaders for the sports.

==Editions and Presenters==

=== TG LA7 07:30 (''TG LA7 7.30 a.m.'')===

* Edgardo Gulotta
* Flavia Fratello
* Fabio Angelicchio
* Emanuela Garulli

=== TG LA7 13:30 (''TG LA7 1.30 p.m.'')===

* Cristina Fantoni
* Luca Speciale
* Adriana Bellini
* Bianca Caterina Bizzarri

=== TG LA7 ore 20:00 (''TG LA7 8 p.m.'')===

* [[Enrico Mentana]]
* Francesca Fanuele (week-end)
* Paolo Celata (week-end)

=== TG LA7 Notte (''TG LA7 12 p.m.'')===

* Paola Mascioli
* Edoardo Soldati
* Andrea Prandi
* Paolo Stella
* Franco Rina

When there is a very important fact, this edition is almost always conducted by [[Enrico Mentana]].
