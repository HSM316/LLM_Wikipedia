'''Tatyana Nikolayevna  Moskalkova''' ({{lang-ru|Татья́на Никола́евна Москалькóва}}; born May 30, 1955, [[Vitebsk]], [[Byelorussian SSR]], [[USSR]]<ref>[http://oficery.ru/node/15930  Женщины-Офицеры России]</ref>) is a Russian lawyer, teacher, and politician. She has been [[Commissioner for Human Rights (Russia)|Russia's Commissioner for Human Rights]] since 22 April 2016,<ref name="К23">[http://kommersant.ru/doc/2972168 Татьяна Москалькова указала основные права]</ref> succeeding [[Ella Pamfilova]],<ref>''Irina Nagornykh",  Natalia Kurchenkova''. [http://www.kommersant.ru/doc/2946253 На пост омбудсмена открывают очередь] // [[Kommersant]]</ref> and is the Deputy of the [[State Duma]] of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation V and VI convocations.<ref name="К23"/>

Moskalkova holds a [[Doctor of Law]] and [[PhD]], and is an [[Honoured Lawyer of Russia]].<ref>[http://echo.msk.ru/news/1752960-echo.htmlГенерал-майор МВД в запасе Татьяна Москалькова стала новым уполномоченным по правам человека в России] // [[Echo of Moscow]]</ref> She is a retired Major-General in the police.<ref>[http://www.personbio.com/view_post.php?id_info=6009 Татьяна Москалькова. Биография]</ref>

Moskalkova is known for her pro-Kremlin stance.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-04-29|title=Lithuania receives a complaint from Moscow about January 13th case convict: his life is in danger|url=https://www.15min.lt/naujiena/aktualu/lietuva/lietuvai-skundas-is-maskvos-del-nuteistojo-sausio-13-osios-byloje-jo-gyvybei-esa-gresia-pavojus-56-1494054|url-status=live}}</ref> She has condemned the [[Pussy Riot]] movement as "an attack against morality" and is critical towards the West.
