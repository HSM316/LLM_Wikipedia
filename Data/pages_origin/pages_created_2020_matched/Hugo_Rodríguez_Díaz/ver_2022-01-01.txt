'''Hugo Rodríguez Díaz''' (born 15 August 1957) is a Mexican politician affiliated with the [[Institutional Revolutionary Party]]. As of 2014 he served as Deputy of the [[LIX Legislature of the Mexican Congress]] representing [[Jalisco]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Perfil del legislador|url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=569454|publisher=Legislative Information System|accessdate=30 May 2014}}</ref>
