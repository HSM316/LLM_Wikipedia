'''Eternal Flame''' is a Dutch [[hard rock]] band founded in 1987 in [[Enschede]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://erh.courant.nu/issue/HNB/1988-09-29/edition/0/page/3|title=Eternal Flame|date=29 September 1988|publisher=Holtens Nieuwsblad|pages=3|language=nl|via=Erfgoed Rijssen-Holten|access-date=2019-05-23|quote=De Enschedese hard rock band Eternal Flame […] Gerrit "Casse" Tijhof (31) koos De Wippert voor première van zijn nieuwste band […] Andre Romita (20, guitaar), Roberto Buiting (20, drummer), Harry Punt (23, bassist)}}</ref>

== History ==
The band was founded in 1987 by Gerrit Tijhof (1956, [[Rijssen]]) and André Romita.<ref name=":0" /><ref name="kazzshow-rockyears">{{Cite web |title=The Rock Years |url=https://kazzshow.net/history/the-rock-years/ |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180316214228/https://kazzshow.net/history/the-rock-years/ |archive-date=2018-03-16 |access-date=2018-07-02 |website=KAZZ Show |language=en}}</ref> By late 1987 the initial line up was complete, with the addition of Robert Buitink (drums) and Harry Pünt (bass).<ref name=":0" />

The band premiered in concert at De Wippert ([[Laren, Gelderland|Laren]]) in September 1988.<ref name=":0" />

In 1993, Harry Pünt left the band, replaced by Aldo Quattrocchi. Later that year, the band was split up.<ref name="kazzshow-rockyears" />

=== Millstone ===
In 1991, the band released their second album, a [[split album]] with three other artists. Production was assisted by Gerrit Tijhof (Eternal Flame). The band released three songs on this album. Released by ''Metal Mustang Media'' in [[Hengelo]], a label from SMN (Stichting Muziek Nieuws).<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thecorroseum.org/comps/harderthanamillstone.html|title=Harder Than A Millstone (1991 CD)|date=29 Aug 2018|website=thecorroseum.org|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180902225245/http://www.thecorroseum.org/comps/harderthanamillstone.html|archive-date=2018-09-02|url-status=live|access-date=2018-09-02}}</ref>
