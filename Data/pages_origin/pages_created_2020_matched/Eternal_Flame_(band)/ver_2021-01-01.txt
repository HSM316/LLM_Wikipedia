'''Eternal Flame''' is a [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[hard rock]] band founded in 1987 in [[Enschede]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://erh.courant.nu/issue/HNB/1988-09-29/edition/0/page/3|title=Eternal Flame|date=29 September 1988|publisher=Holtens Nieuwsblad|pages=3|language=nl|via=Erfgoed Rijssen-Holten|access-date=2019-05-23|quote=De Enschedese hard rock band Eternal Flame […] Gerrit "Casse" Tijhof (31) koos De Wippert voor première van zijn nieuwste band […] Andre Romita (20, guitaar), Roberto Buiting (20, drummer), Harry Punt (23, bassist)}}</ref>

== History ==
The idea for the band came from Gerrit Tijhof (aka ''KAZZ'') in late 1986.<ref name="kazzshow-rockyears">{{Cite web|url=https://kazzshow.net/history/early-years/|title=Early Years|website=kazzshow.net|language=en|access-date=2018-07-02}}</ref> In 1987 the band was founded in Enschede by Gerrit Tijhof (1956, [[Rijssen]]) and André Romita.<ref name="kazzshow-rockyears" /> By late 1987 the initial line up completed with the addition of Robert Buitink (drums) and Harry Pünt (bass).<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.metal-archives.com/bands/Eternal_Flame/3540299960#band_tab_members|title=Eternal Flame|website=Metal-Archives.com|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150524013003/https://www.metal-archives.com/bands/Eternal_Flame/3540299960#band_tab_members|archive-date=2015-05-24|url-status=live|access-date=2018-07-02}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.metal-archives.com/albums/Eternal_Flame/Stalingrad/#album_tabs_lineup|title=Stalingrad (Eternal Flame) - Lineup|website=www.metal-archives.com|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170217002126/https://www.metal-archives.com/albums/Eternal_Flame/Stalingrad/#album_tabs_lineup|archive-date=2017-02-17|url-status=live|access-date=2018-07-02}}</ref>

The band premiered in concert at De Wippert ([[Laren, Gelderland|Laren]]) in September 1988.<ref name=":0" />

In 1993, Harry Pünt left the band, replaced by Aldo Quattrocchi. Later that year, the band was split up.<ref name="kazzshow-rockyears" />

=== Millstone ===
In 1991, the band released their second album, a [[split album]] with three other artists. Production was assisted by Gerrit Tijhof (Eternal Flame). The band released three songs on this album. Released by ''Metal Mustang Media'' in [[Hengelo]], a label from SMN (Stichting Muziek Nieuws). <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thecorroseum.org/comps/harderthanamillstone.html|title=Harder Than A Millstone (1991 CD)|date=29 Aug 2018|website=thecorroseum.org|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180902225245/http://www.thecorroseum.org/comps/harderthanamillstone.html|archive-date=2018-09-02|url-status=live|access-date=2018-09-02}}</ref>
