'''Shu Jianming''' ({{zh|c=舒建明}}; born 16 June 1990 in [[Xichang]],<ref>{{cite web |url=http://lsxb.ls666.com/html/2016-07/26/content_260466.htm |title=Liangshan Radio and Television (2016-7-26) |website=ls666.com}}</ref> [[Sichuan]])<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rio2016.com/en/athlete/jianming-shu |title=Shu Jianming |publisher=Rio 2016 |accessdate=8 August 2016 |archive-date=6 August 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160806043857/https://www.rio2016.com/en/athlete/jianming-shu |url-status=dead }}</ref> is a Chinese [[Slalom canoeing|slalom canoeist]] who has competed since 2008.<ref>{{cite web|title=Overview of athlete's results|website=CanoeSlalom.net|url=http://www.canoeslalom.net/int/index/index_83.html#12429|accessdate=29 August 2016|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140502035735/http://www.canoeslalom.net/int/index/index_83.html#12429|archivedate=2 May 2014}}</ref> He finished 14th in the [[Canoeing at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Men's slalom C-1|C1 event]] at the [[2016 Summer Olympics]] in [[Rio de Janeiro]].
