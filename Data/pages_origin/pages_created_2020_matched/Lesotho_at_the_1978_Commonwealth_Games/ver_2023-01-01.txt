'''[[Lesotho]]''' competed in the '''[[1978 Commonwealth Games]]''' in [[Edmonton|Edmonton, Alberta]], Canada from August 3 to August 12, 1978. Their team consisted of eight athletes competing in track events.<ref name="official">{{cite web |url=http://www.thecgf.com/games/intro.asp?yr=1978 |title=1978 Commonwealth Games |website=thecgf.com |publisher= |accessdate=11 September 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170414194244/http://thecgf.com/games/intro.asp?yr=1978 |archive-date=14 April 2017 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
