'''Helmen Kütt''' (born 28 July 1961) is an Estonian Social Democratic Party politician. She was the Minister of Social Protection in [[Taavi Rõivas' cabinet|Taavi Rõivas´cabinet]] between 26 March 2014 and 30 March 2015. In the parliament Kütt represents the [[Electoral districts of Estonia|electoral district]] of Järva- and Viljandimaa .<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.riigikogu.ee/?page=ikaart&op=ems&pid=1318946|title= RIIGIKOGU LIIGE : HELMEN KÜTT|accessdate=8 June 2014|publisher=Parliament of Estonia|language=Estonian}}</ref>

Before entering into national politics, Kütt worked as the head of a Social Commity in Viljandi.

== Minister of Social Protection ==
As Andrus Ansip resigned Taavi Rõivas became the new Prime Minister of Estonia and he formed the coalition with the Social Democratic Party (Andrus Ansip´s III government was a coalition between conservative [[Isamaa ja Res Publica Liit|IRL]] and classical liberal [[Reform Party (Estonia)|Reform Party]]). As a result of coalition negotiations Minister of Social Affairs was torn apart into two separate ministers: Minister of Social Protection and Minister of Health and Labour.
