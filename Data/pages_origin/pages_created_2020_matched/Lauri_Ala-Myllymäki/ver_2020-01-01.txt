'''Lauri Ala-Myllymäki''' (born 4 June 1997 in [[Pirkkala]]) is a [[Finland|Finnish]] [[association football|football]] [[midfielder]] playing for [[Finland|Finnish]] [[Veikkausliiga]] club [[FC Ilves]] <ref>{{Soccerway|lauri-ala-myllymaki/296459|accessdate=3 July 2019}}</ref>. He has played for Finland in various youth national teams and is regarded as one of the most promising Finnish players in his age group.
