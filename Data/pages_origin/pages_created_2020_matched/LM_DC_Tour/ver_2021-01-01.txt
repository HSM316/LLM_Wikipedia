The '''LM/DC Tour'''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://dcmetrotheaterarts.com/2018/06/04/review-lea-michele-darren-criss-at-the-kennedy-center/ |title=Review: ‘Lea Michele & Darren Criss – the LM/DC Tour’ at the Kennedy Center |last1=Skow |first1=Em |date=June 4, 2018 |website=DC Metro Theater Arts |access-date=December 11, 2019 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20191211135214/https://dcmetrotheaterarts.com/2018/06/04/review-lea-michele-darren-criss-at-the-kennedy-center/ |archive-date=December 11, 2019 |url-status=live }}</ref> is a co-headlining concert tour by American singers [[Lea Michele]] and [[Darren Criss]]. It is Michele's second headlining tour, promoting her second studio album ''[[Places (Lea Michele album)|Places]]'' (2017), and Criss' second concert tour, promoting his third extended play ''Homework'' (2016). The co-headlining tour began on May 30, 2018, in [[Nashville, Tennessee|Nashville]] and concluded on December 7, 2018 in [[Birmingham]].

==Background==
On April 9, 2018, Michele and Criss announced on ''[[The Ellen DeGeneres Show]]'' that they would go on tour together.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/broadway/8299788/lea-michele-darren-criss-tour|title=Lea Michele and Darren Criss Are Heading on Tour Together|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|publisher=[[Prometheus Global Media]]|date=April 9, 2018|access-date=April 9, 2018|first=Shanté|last=Honeycutt}}</ref>

In September 2018, further dates were announced including [[Las Vegas]], [[Salt Lake City]], [[Phoenix, Arizona|Phoenix]] and multiple cities in [[California]].<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.playbill.com/article/lea-michele-and-darren-criss-add-west-coast-dates-to-concert-tour |title=Lea Michele and Darren Criss Add West Coast Dates to Concert Tour |last=Fierberg |first=Ruthie |date=September 12, 2018 |work=Playbill |access-date=September 22, 2018}}</ref> The tour will then expand to [[Dublin]] and the [[United Kingdom]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.irishexaminer.com/breakingnews/entertainment/former-glee-stars-lea-michele-and-darren-criss-to-tour-the-uk-and-ireland-869509.html |title=Former Glee stars Lea Michele and Darren Criss to tour the UK and Ireland |date=September 17, 2018 |work=[[Irish Examiner]] |access-date=September 22, 2018}}</ref>
