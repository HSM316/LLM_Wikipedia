'''''Karokia''''' is a genus of spur-throated grasshoppers in the family [[Acrididae]]. There are at least 2 described species in ''Karokia''.<ref name="OSF">{{cite web|title=genus ''Karokia'' Rehn, 1964|url=http://orthoptera.speciesfile.org/Common/basic/Taxa.aspx?TaxonNameID=1110758|work=orthoptera.speciesfile.org|accessdate=2020-04-02}}</ref><ref name=bugref>{{cite web|url=https://bugguide.net/node/view/361890|title=''Karokia'' Genus Information|website=BugGuide.net|accessdate=2018-01-27}}</ref><ref name=itis>{{cite web|url=https://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN&search_value=657720|title=''Karokia'' Report|website=Integrated Taxonomic Information System|accessdate=2018-01-27}}</ref>

==Species==
* ''[[Karokia blanci]]'' <small>(Rehn, 1964)</small>
* ''[[Karokia memorialis]]'' <small>Gurney & Buxton, 1968</small>
