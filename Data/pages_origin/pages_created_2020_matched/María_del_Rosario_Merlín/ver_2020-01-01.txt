'''María del Rosario Merlín García''' (born 1 November 1964) is a [[Mexican people|Mexican]] politician affiliated with the [[Party of the Democratic Revolution|PRD]]. She currently serves as Deputy of the [[LXII Legislature of the Mexican Congress]] representing [[Guerrero]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Perfil del legislador|url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=9216558|publisher=Legislative Information System|accessdate=1 November 2013}}</ref>
