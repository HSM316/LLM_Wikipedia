{{nihongo4|"'''Nami Nori Pirates'''"|波乗りパイレーツ|Nami Nori Pairētsu|lit. "Surfing Pirates"}} is the 13th single by [[Pink Lady (band)|Pink Lady]]. It was released on 5 July 1979, with a peak position on the [[Oricon]] charts of #4,<ref name="JPNO">{{cite web|url=http://www.pinkladyamerica.com/pinkdiscography.html|title= Pink Lady singles|accessdate=2009-08-08}}</ref> and also a #4 position on the Japanese [[Billboard Hot 100|Music Labo]] chart.<ref name="BillboardAugust1979">{{cite web |url=https://books.google.nl/books?id=fiQEAAAAMBAJ&pg=PT71&dq|title=Billboard Magazine, August 11, 1979 |work=Billboard}}</ref>

The B-side, an alternate version of the song used on their ''[[Magical Musical Tour]]'' studio album, featured [[The Beach Boys]] as backing vocals.<ref>{{cite book  | last = | first =| title =音楽CD検定公式ガイドブック下巻  | publisher = レコード検定協議会  | year = | page = 136| isbn = }}</ref>

The single was also featured on the Japanese music program ''The Best Ten'', where it peaked at #7.<ref>{{cite book  | last =Yamada  | first =Osamuji| title =The Best Ten  | publisher = ソニー・マガジンズ  | year = 2008  | page =  | isbn = 4789733726}}</ref>

It sold approximately 550,000 copies.<ref name="JPN">{{cite web|url=http://pinklady.gooside.com/Dg.html|title= Pink Lady single sales|accessdate=2012-05-28}}</ref>

== Track listing (7" vinyl) ==
All tracks composed by [[Shunichi Tokura]], lyrics written by [[Yū Aku]]. B-side arranged by Paul Fauerso.
