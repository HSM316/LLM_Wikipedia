'''Elena Victorovna Zhosul''' ({{lang-ru|link=no|Еле́на Ви́кторовна Жо́сул}}; born September 16, 1980, [[Chișinău]], [[Moldavian Soviet Socialist Republic]], [[USSR]]) is a Russian journalist, television presenter, [[politologist]] and [[Media studies|media expert]].{{sfn|Khroul|2018|p=51}}

She is a presenter of social and political talk show ''Do Samoj Suti'' ({{trans|To the Core}}; {{lang-ru|До самой сути}}) and talk show for [[Eastern Orthodox Church|Orthodox]] women ''Zhenskaja Polovina'' ({{trans|Women Moiety}}; {{lang-ru|Женская половина}}) on the first Russian public Orthodox TV channel ''[[Spas (TV channel)|"Spas"]]'' (since 2018). From 2005 to 2010 Zhosul was a correspondent and columnist for the news agency ''[[Interfax]]-Religion''.

She has been a member of the Council of Chairman of the [[:ru:Синодальный отдел по взаимоотношениям Церкви с обществом и средствами массовой информации|Department for Church's Society and Mass Mediа Relations]] ([[Russian Orthodox Church]]), a member of the Russian Orthodox Church [[:ru:Межсоборное присутствие|Inter-Council Presence]] Commission on information activities of the Church and media relations (since 2015), and a Head of the Center for Media Communications of the [[Russian Orthodox University of Saint John the Divine]] (since 2016).

She was a candidate of Political Sciences, and from 2010 to 2016 was a Docent and Head of the Department of Journalism and Public Relations of the History and Philology Faculty of Russian Orthodox University of St. John the Divine.

She is a regular expert in various discussion platforms on religious and ethnic issues,<ref>Александров Г. [https://aif.ru/society/law/1384485 Уехать, чтобы вернуться.] // [[Аргументы и факты|Аргументы и факты — СтоЛичность]]. — November 18, 2014. — № 11 (60). — С. 4</ref> expert of the non-profit research service ''[[:ru:Среда (исследовательская служба)|Sreda]]'',<ref>[http://sreda.org/expert/elena-zhosul Елена Жосул] // Некоммерческая исследовательская служба [[:ru:Среда (исследовательская служба)|"Среда"]]</ref> and an expert of the federal news agency [[:ru:Regions|Regions.ru]].<ref>[https://regions.ru/expert/870/ Елена Жосул] // [[:ru:Regions.ru|Regions.ru]]</ref>
