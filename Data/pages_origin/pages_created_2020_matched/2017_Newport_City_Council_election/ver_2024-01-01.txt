2017 Elections to [[Newport City Council]] were held on 3 May 2017<ref>[http://www.assembly.wales/Research%20Documents/Local%20Election%20Results%202017%20-%20Research%20paper-17052017-234009/12-020-English.pdf Local Election Results 2017 - National Assembly for Wales], Welsh Assembly, 2017</ref> along with elections to the other 21 local authorities in Wales, community council elections in Wales and [[2017 United Kingdom local elections]].

Councillors elected in this election would serve a five-year term due to the new Welsh Government policy.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Drakeford|first1=Mark|title=Written Statement - Local Elections and Electoral Arrangements|url=https://gov.wales/written-statement-local-elections-and-electoral-arrangements|access-date=2019-05-03}}</ref>

The previous election took place on [[2012 Newport City Council election|3 May 2012]].

The next full election took place [[2022 Newport City Council election|in May 2022]].
