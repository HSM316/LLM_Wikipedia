'''Abdullah Awad Al Juhany''' ({{lang-ar|عبد الله عواد الجهني}}), is one of the nine [[Imam]]s of the Grand Mosque [[Masjid Al-Haram|Masjid al-Haram]] in [[Mecca]].<ref name="alim1">{{cite web |title=Sheikh Abdullah Awad Al-Juhani, imam at the Grand Mosque in Makkah |url=https://www.arabnews.com/node/1482046 |website=Arabnews.com |date=13 April 2019 |publisher=Arabnews |accessdate=16 September 2021 |ref=Published on 13 April 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Imam-e-Kaaba to give sermon, lead Friday prayer at Faisal Mosque|url=https://www.thenews.com.pk/latest/456960-imam-e-kaaba-to-give-sermon-lead-friday-prayer-at-faisal-mosque|access-date=2021-09-15|website=www.thenews.com.pk|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-04-15|title=Imam-e-Kaaba to visit parliament house today|url=https://nation.com.pk/15-Apr-2019/imam-e-kaaba-dr-abdullah-awad-al-juhany-to-visit-parliament-house-today|access-date=2021-09-15|website=The Nation|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Permana|first=Rakhmad Hidayatulloh|title=Syeikh Baleelah Diserang Saat Khotbah, Ini 11 Imam-Khatib Masjidil Haram|url=https://news.detik.com/berita/d-5578673/syeikh-baleelah-diserang-saat-khotbah-ini-11-imam-khatib-masjidil-haram|access-date=2021-09-15|website=detiknews|language=id-ID}}</ref> He holds a [[Bachelor of Arts|B.A.]] from the faculty of Qur'an at the [[Islamic University of Madinah]], and a [[Doctor of Philosophy|doctorate (Ph.D.)]] from [[Umm al-Qura University]] in [[Mecca]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-04-24|title=2 Billion Muslims follow Sheikh Juhany as their Imam of Ka'aba|url=https://dailytimes.com.pk/381853/2-billion-muslims-follow-sheikh-juhany-as-their-imam-of-kaaba/|access-date=2021-09-15|website=Daily Times|language=en-US}}</ref>

Juhany has led the [[Tarawih|Taraweeh]] prayers during [[Ramadan]] in [[Mecca]] since 2005. His voice has been widely recorded and is internationally distributed by various communities.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?75723-Information-On-Some-Of-The-Imams-Of-The-Haramain-Interviews-Profile-Masjid-Haram |title=Information on Some of the Imams of the Haramain &#124; Interviews &#124; Profile&#124; Masjid Haram |accessdate=2013-08-25 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130930055925/http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?75723-Information-On-Some-Of-The-Imams-Of-The-Haramain-Interviews-Profile-Masjid-Haram |archivedate=2013-09-30 }}</ref>

== Career ==

He was appointed as an imam of [[Masjid Al Haram]], [[Mecca]] in July 2007. He is or has been an imam of [[Masjid Al Haram]] in [[Mecca]], [[Al-Masjid an-Nabawi|Masjid Al Nabawi]] in [[Medina]], [[Quba Mosque|Masjid Quba]] and [[Masjid al-Qiblatayn|Masjid Qiblatain]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?39593-Sheikh-Abdullah-Awwad-Al-Juhany-Imam-Of-Makkah%26highlight%3D |title=Sheikh Abdullah 'Awwad Al-Juhany - Imam of Makkah |accessdate=2013-08-25 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923090940/http://www.sunniforum.com/forum/showthread.php?39593-Sheikh-Abdullah-Awwad-Al-Juhany-Imam-Of-Makkah&highlight= |archivedate=2015-09-23 }}</ref>
