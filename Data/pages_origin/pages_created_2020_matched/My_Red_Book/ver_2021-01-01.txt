'''''My Red Book''''' is [[Rick Astley]]'s unreleased seventh album. This album was originally planned to release in January 2013.<ref>http://www.radiocremebrulee.com/80sartistreturns/rick-astley-my-red-book-album-review</ref> This unreleased album contains the singles "[[Lights Out (Rick Astley song)|Lights Out]]", and "Superman".

"I Like the Sun" and a re-recording of "Let It Rain" later appeared on Astley's 2016 album ''[[50 (album)|50]]'',<ref>{{cite web|last1=Daw|first1=Robbie|title=Rick Astley's Still Got It: Watch The Video For His Comeback Single "Keep Singing" {{!}} Idolator|url=http://www.idolator.com/7629413/rick-astley-listen-keep-singing|website=Idolator.com|accessdate=7 June 2016|date=8 April 2016}}</ref> with "A Letter" and a remix of "Sailing" appearing on the US edition.
