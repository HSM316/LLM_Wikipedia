'''Ashley Chastain''' is an American softball coach who is the current head coach at [[Charlotte 49ers softball|Charlotte]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Chastain Named 49ers Head Softball Coach |url=https://charlotte49ers.com/news/2019/6/6/chastain-named-49ers-head-softball-coach.aspx |website=Charlotte49ers.com |publisher=UNC Charlotte |accessdate=8 June 2019}}</ref>

==Coaching career==
===College of Charleston (asst.)===
On October 22, 2013, Chastain was announced as assistant coach of the College of Charleston softball program.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ashley Chastain Completes CofC Softball Staff |url=https://cofcsports.com/news/2013/10/22/209289457.aspx |website=CofCSports.com |publisher=COLLEGE OF CHARLESTON |accessdate=8 June 2019}}</ref>
===Michigan State (asst.)===
On July 20, 2016, Chastain was announced as the new pitching coach of the Michigan State softball program.<ref>{{cite web |title=Chastain Joins Softball Staff |url=https://msuspartans.com/news/2016/7/20/Chastain_Joins_Softball_Staff.aspx |website=MSUSpartans.com |publisher=Michigan State Spartan Athletics |accessdate=8 June 2019}}</ref>
===Ole Miss (asst.)===
On November 1, 2017, Chastain was announced as the new pitching coach of the Ole Miss softball program.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ole Miss Softball Adds Ashley Chastain as Pitching Coach |url=https://olemisssports.com/news/2017/11/1/Ole_Miss_Softball_Adds_Ashley_Chastain_as_Pitching_Coach.aspx |website=OleMissSports.com |publisher=University of Mississippi |accessdate=8 June 2019}}</ref>
===Charlotte===
On June 6, 2019, Chastain was announced as the new head coach of the Charlotte softball program.<ref>{{cite web |title=Chastain Named 49ers Head Softball Coach |url=https://charlotte49ers.com/news/2019/6/6/chastain-named-49ers-head-softball-coach.aspx |website=Charlotte49ers.com |publisher=UNC Charlotte |accessdate=8 June 2019}}</ref>
