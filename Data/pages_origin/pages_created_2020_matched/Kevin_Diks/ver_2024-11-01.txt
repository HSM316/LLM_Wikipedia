'''Kevin Diks Bakarbessy''' (born 6 October 1996) is an [[Indonesia national football team|Indonesian]] professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for [[Danish Superliga]] club [[F.C. Copenhagen|Copenhagen]].

==Club career==
===Vitesse===
[[File:Kevin Diks Vitesse 28062015.png|left|thumb|233x233px|Diks playing for [[SBV Vitesse|Vitesse]] in 2015]]
Born  in [[Apeldoorn]], Diks joined [[SBV Vitesse|Vitesse]]'s youth setup in 2005, aged nine, after playing for VIOS Vaassen and hometown's [[AGOVV Apeldoorn]]. In the 2014 summer he was called to the main squad for the pre-season, also appearing in a 1–3 defeat against [[Chelsea F.C.|Chelsea]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vitesse.nl/nl/nieuws/detail/3889/verwijderen|title = Vitesse nieuws: Chelsea FC wint aantrekkelijk duel vs Vitesse: 1-3}}</ref> On 24 August 2014, after profiting from first-choice [[Wallace Oliveira dos Santos|Wallace]]'s injury, Diks made his first-team – and [[Eredivisie]] – debut, starting in a 1–2 away loss against [[PEC Zwolle]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vitesse.nl/vitessenl/nieuws/detail/4622|title=Vitesse na rust ten onder in Zwolle: 2–1|trans-title=Vitesse stays behind Zwolle after half-time: 2–1|publisher=Vitesse's official website|date=24 August 2014|accessdate=25 August 2014}}</ref> On 8 January of the following year, after being already a permanent part of the main squad, he renewed his link until 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vi.nl/nieuws/vitesse-legt-doorgebroken-diks-drie-jaar-langer-vast.htm|title=Vitesse legt doorgebroken Diks drie jaar langer vast|trans-title=Vitesse secures breakthrough Diks for three more years|publisher=[[Voetbal International]]|language=nl|date=8 January 2015|accessdate=10 March 2015}}</ref>

===Fiorentina===
On 6 July 2016, Diks joined [[Serie A]] side [[ACF Fiorentina|Fiorentina]] on a five-year deal after impressing at Vitesse.<ref>{{Cite news |title=KEVIN DIKS' A PLAYER OF FIORENTINA |url=http://it.violachannel.tv/vc13-dettaglio-breaking/items/id-06-07-2016_12-59-54_kevin-diks-e-un-giocatore-della-fiorentin.html |publisher=ACF Fiorentina |date=6 July 2016 |accessdate=6 July 2016}}</ref> He later requested number 34 shirt in tribute to [[Abdelhak Nouri]], who collapsed and suffered brain damage, ending his career.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/bbcthree/article/abbf8564-5453-4f69-9a08-5d85630a5268|title=The touching reason players are wearing the number 34|date=8 January 2019|accessdate=9 January 2019|author=Ciaran Varley|publisher=BBC}}</ref>

On 23 October 2016, three months after signing for the Italian side, Diks finally made his long-awaited debut in a 5–3 away victory against [[Cagliari Calcio|Cagliari]], replacing [[Cristian Tello]] in the 88th minute.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Cagliari vs. Fiorentina |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2016/10/23/italy/serie-a/cagliari-calcio/acf-fiorentina/2305834/ |publisher=Soccerway |date=23 October 2016 |accessdate=23 October 2016}}</ref>

====Vitesse (loan)====
On 31 January 2017, Diks returned to Vitesse on loan for the remainder of the [[2016–17 Vitesse season|2016–17]] campaign, after failing to make his breakthrough whilst in Italy.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Diks back on old nest |url=https://www.vitesse.nl/nl/nieuws/detail/6225/diks-terug-op-oude-nest |publisher=Vitesse Official Site |date=31 January 2017 |accessdate=31 January 2017}}</ref> On 11 February 2017, Diks made his Vitesse return in their 2–0 home defeat against [[Willem II (football club)|Willem II]], playing the full 90 minutes.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Vitesse vs. Willem II |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2017/02/11/netherlands/eredivisie/sbv-vitesse/sbv-willem-ii-tilburg/2240466/ |publisher=Soccerway |date=11 February 2017 |accessdate=7 March 2017}}</ref> On 5 April 2017, Diks got sent off for a second bookable offence during Vitesse's 1–0 away victory against [[Heracles Almelo]].<ref>{{Cite news |title=Heracles vs. Vitesse |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2017/04/05/netherlands/eredivisie/stichting-heracles-almelo/sbv-vitesse/2240521/ |publisher=Soccerway |date=5 April 2017 |accessdate=5 April 2017}}</ref>

He came off the bench as Vitesse won the final of the [[KNVB Beker]] 2–0 against [[AZ Alkmaar]] on 30 April 2017 to help the club, three-time runners up, to the title for the first time in its 125-year history.<ref name="reuters">{{cite news |last1=Said |first1=Nick |title=Vitesse claim first major trophy with Cup triumph |url=https://www.reuters.com/article/uk-soccer-dutch-aza-vit/vitesse-claim-first-major-trophy-with-cup-triumph-idUKKBN17W0PR |accessdate=1 February 2019 |work=Reuters |date=30 April 2017}}</ref><ref name="espn">{{cite web |title=AZ Alkmaar vs. Vitesse Arnhem - Football Match Line-Ups - April 30, 2017 - ESPN |url=http://www.espn.co.uk/football/lineups?gameId=475656 |website=ESPN |accessdate=1 February 2019}}</ref>

====Feyenoord (loan)====
On 4 July 2017, Diks joined Feyenoord on a season-long loan.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Feyenoord take Diks on loan |url=http://www.football-italia.net/105211/feyenoord-take-diks-loan |publisher=Football Italia |date=4 July 2017 |accessdate=4 July 2017}}</ref>

On 22 April 2018, he played as Feyenoord won the 2017–18 [[KNVB Cup]] final 3–0 against [[AZ Alkmaar]].<ref name="ad">{{cite news |last1=Gouka |first1=Mikos |title=Feyenoord wint KNVB-beker mede dankzij prachtgoal Van Persie |url=https://www.ad.nl/dossier-feyenoord-pakt-beker/feyenoord-wint-knvb-beker-mede-dankzij-prachtgoal-van-persie~aa5bde0b/ |accessdate=1 February 2019 |work=Allgemeen Dagblad |date=22 April 2018 |language=Dutch}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2018/04/22/netherlands/knvb-beker/stichting-az/feyenoord-rotterdam-nv/2727912/|title = AZ vs. Feyenoord - 22 April 2018 - Soccerway}}</ref>

====Empoli (loan)====
On 31 January 2019, Diks joined [[Serie A]] side [[Empoli F.C.|Empoli]] on loan until 30 June 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.tuttomercatoweb.com/serie-a/ufficiale-fiorentina-ecco-rasmussen-resta-a-empoli-va-anche-diks-1208798|access-date=31 January 2019|title=UFFICIALE: Fiorentina, ecco Rasmussen. Resta a Empoli, va anche Diks|language=Italian}}</ref>

====AGF (loan)====
On transfer deadline day, 2 September 2019, Diks was loaned out to [[Danish Superliga]] club [[Aarhus Gymnastikforening|AGF]] for the 2019–20 season.<ref>[https://www.agf.dk/klubben/nyhedsarkiv/2019/september/agf-henter-hollandsk-forsvarsspiller-i-serie-a/ AGF HENTER HOLLANDSK FORSVARSSPILLER I SERIE A] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190902191301/https://www.agf.dk/klubben/nyhedsarkiv/2019/september/agf-henter-hollandsk-forsvarsspiller-i-serie-a/ |date=2 September 2019 }}, agf.dk, 2 September 2019</ref> On 31 August 2020, the loan was renewed for the [[ 2020–21 Aarhus Gymnastikforening season|2020–21 season]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Aarhus Gymnastikforening|AGF]]|url=https://www.agf.dk/klubben/nyhedsarkiv/2020/august/kevin-diks-vender-tilbage/|title=KEVIN DIKS VENDER TILBAGE|date=31 August 2020|language=Danish}}</ref>

===FC Copenhagen===
On 5 July 2021, Diks returned to Denmark, signing a four-year deal with [[F.C. Copenhagen]].<ref>[https://www.fck.dk/nyhed/fc-koebenhavn-henter-kevin-diks F.C. KØBENHAVN HENTER KEVIN DIKS], fck.dk, 5 July 2021</ref>

==International career==
On 9 October 2014, Diks made his [[Netherlands national under-19 football team|Netherlands U19]] debut in a 7–0 victory against [[Andorra national under-19 football team|Andorra U19]]'s, playing the first half before being replaced by [[Leeroy Owusu]]. Diks went on to make three more appearances before receiving a call-up to the [[Netherlands national under-20 football team|under-20]] squad. Diks has also represented at [[Netherlands national under-21 football team|under-21]] squad. In 2018, Diks was called up to the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands senior team]] for a taining camp, but did not play any matches for the senior team.

In October 2024, Diks opted to represent Indonesia. As he met with [[Erick Thohir]], and the latter welcomed him to the [[Indonesia national football team|Indonesia national team]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bolasport.com/read/314165898/kevin-diks-datang-pssi-buka-suara-sulitnya-cari-penyerang-keturunan-untuk-timnas-indonesia|title=Kevin Diks Datang, PSSI Buka Suara Sulitnya Cari Penyerang Keturunan untuk Timnas Indonesia|website=Bola Sport|date=15 October 2024|language=id}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Diks is the younger brother of [[Jamarro Diks|Jamarro]], who had a spell in the Slovakian league. He is of [[Indonesians|Indonesian]] descent through his mother,<ref>{{Cite web|title=Kevin Diks mother's name Natasja Bakarbessy|url=https://www.libero.id/detail/11372/kisah-natasja-diks-bakarbessy-ibu-calon-pemain-naturalisasi-indonesia-keturunan-maluku.html}}</ref> who is from [[Maluku (province)|Maluku]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fiorentinanews.com/diks-il-fantasma-della-rosa-viola-lolandese-non-si-vede-mai-e-corvino/|title=Diks, il fantasma della rosa viola. L'olandese non si vede mai e Corvino…|last=Razzolini|first=Filippo|website=Fiorentinanews.com|date=26 December 2018|language=it-IT|access-date=1 February 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indosport.com/sepakbola/20180904/5-pemain-keturunan-indonesia-yang-memiliki-harga-tinggi/kevin-diks|title=5 Pemain Keturunan Indonesia yang Memiliki Harga Tinggi|last=INDOSPORT.com|date=4 September 2018|website=INDOSPORT.com|language=en|access-date=1 February 2019}}</ref>
