{{nihongo|'''Kaito Nakamura'''|中村 嘉惟人|Nakamura Kaito|born April 5, 1998 in [[Nagoya]], [[Aichi Prefecture]]}} is a Japanese actor and model affiliated with [[Oscar Promotion]].<ref name="Oscar Promotion">{{cite web|url=http://catalog.oscarpro.co.jp/PcSearch/talent_detail/Talent/talent_search_result_list/1000007150/|title=Official profile at Oscar Promotion|accessdate=2015-11-01}}</ref> He played the role of [[List of Shuriken Sentai Ninninger characters#Nagi Matsuo|Nagi Matsuo/Ki Ninger]] in the 2015 [[Super Sentai]] TV series ''[[Shuriken Sentai Ninninger]]''.<ref name="Smart the TV">{{cite web|url=http://thetv.jp/news_detail/53758/|title=""忍びなれども忍ばない!"今度のスーパー戦隊シリーズは忍者ヒーロー「手裏剣戦隊ニンニンジャー」". Smart the TV (December 26, 2014)|accessdate=2015-06-11}}</ref>

==Biography==
From 2010 to 2011, Nakamura made regular appearances in the [[NHK Educational TV]] series ''Tensai TV-kun MAX''.<ref name="Oscar Promotion"/> He was an exclusive model for the fashion magazine ''[[Nicola (magazine)#Nico-petit|Nico☆Petit]]'' in 2012,<ref name="Oscar Promotion"/> and for ''[[Pichi Lemon]]'' from 2013 to 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pichilemon.net/posts/66350|title=Official profile at ''Pichi Lemon''|accessdate=2015-11-01}}</ref> In 2015, Nakamura made his acting debut with the role of [[List of Shuriken Sentai Ninninger characters#Nagi Matsuo|Nagi Matsuo/Ki Ninger]] in the 39th [[Super Sentai]] TV series ''[[Shuriken Sentai Ninninger]]''.<ref name="Smart the TV"/>
