'''Alfredo Jacobo Rodríguez''' (born April 7, 1982)<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20200418031744/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ja/alfredo-jacobo-1.html Jacobo's entry] from www.sports-reference.com; retrieved 2009-06-29.</ref> is an Olympic breaststroke [[Swimming (sport)|swimmer]] from Mexico.

==Career==
Alfredo is the older brother of fellow Mexican swimmer, [[Alejandro Jacobo]]. He represented Mexico at the [[Mexico at the 2000 Summer Olympics|2000 Olympics]].<ref>[http://www.aggieathletics.com/sports/m-swim/mtt/jacobo_alejandro00.html Alejandro Jacobo's bio]{{Dead link|date=October 2018 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} from the Texas A&M Athletics website; retrieved 2009-06-29.</ref>

Jacobo also swam for Mexico at the [[Mexico at the 2003 Pan American Games|2003]] and [[Mexico at the 2007 Pan American Games|2007 Pan American Games]].

As of March 2008, he holds the [[List of Mexican records in swimming|Mexican Records]] in the long-course (50m) 50 breaststroke; and the short-course (25m) 50, 100 and 200 breaststrokes.

Alfredo, and his brother Alejandro, both competed/attended college and swam in the USA at [[Texas A&M University]].
