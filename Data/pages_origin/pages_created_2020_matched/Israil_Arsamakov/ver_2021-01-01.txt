'''Israil Magomedgireyevich Arsamakov''' ({{lang-ru|Исраил Магомедгиреевич Арсамаков}}, (born February 8, 1962 in [[Grozny]], [[Chechen–Ingush ASSR]]) is a former [[Ingush people|Ingush]] and [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] [[Olympic weightlifting|weightlifter]] and Olympic champion who competed for the [[Soviet Union]].

Arsamakov won a gold medal at the [[1988 Summer Olympics]] in [[Seoul]].<ref name=db-ol1988-weightlifting>[http://www.databaseolympics.com/games/gamessport.htm?g=22&sp=WEI "1988 Summer Olympics - Seoul, South Korea - Weightlifting"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080531095529/http://www.databaseolympics.com/games/gamessport.htm?g=22&sp=WEI |date=2008-05-31 }} - ''databaseOlympics.com'' (Retrieved on February 23, 2008)</ref>
