'''Caitlin Maureen McHugh''' (born May 13, 1986) is an American actress, writer, and model.

==Education==
Born in 1986 in [[Santa Monica]], [[California]] to Leilani (née Empeno) and Timothy McHugh, she attended [[Flintridge Sacred Heart Academy]] in [[La Cañada Flintridge]], California, graduating in 2004.<ref name="imdb.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm2444118/bio|title=Caitlin McHugh|website=IMDb}}</ref> She studied contemporary dance at the [[University of North Carolina School of the Arts]] from 2004-2006.{{cn|date=December 2019}} She is of [[Irish Americans|Irish]] and [[Filipino Americans|Filipino]] ancestry.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rmh-newyork.org/Media/Default/ronaldreports/Ronald%20Report%20Archive%20-%20July%202012%20-%20Ronald%20McDonald%20House%20New%20York.html |title=Wednesday Night Team Luau |first=Megs |last=DiDario |work=The Ronald Report |publisher=[[Ronald McDonald House Charities|Ronald McDonald House New York]] |date=July 2012 |accessdate=2018-01-10}}</ref>

==Career==
She first began to find bit parts in film and television in 2007, with a minor role in ''[[I Am Legend (film)|I Am Legend]]'', and earned nine more credits over the next six years.  In 2014, she was cast in a recurring role in the TV series ''[[The Vampire Diaries]]''. In 2017, she co-wrote the romantic comedy short film "Ingenue-ish" with fellow actor [[John Stamos]], who also co-starred with her.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt7267974/reference|title=Ingenue-ish (2017)|publisher=|via=www.imdb.com}}</ref>

McHugh is signed with Wilhelmina Models.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wilhelmina.com/los-angeles/models/direct/women/585506/caitlin-mchugh|title=Wilhelmina International - caitlin mchugh los angeles direct portfolio|first=cDs|last=Global|website=www.wilhelmina.com}}</ref>  In 2016, she appeared in television commercials for [[Colgate toothpaste]] and the following year, for the [[Buick]] "Encore".

==Personal life==
McHugh married restaurateur Massimo Lusardi on June 3, 2011.{{cn|date=December 2019}} They separated a few months later and divorced in 2014.<ref>https://starcasm.net/caitlin-mchughs-age/</ref>

On October 23, 2017, McHugh announced her engagement to actor [[John Stamos]] after a year of dating.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/john-stamos-and-zack-butler-are-engaged |title=John Stamos and Caitlin McHugh Are Engaged |first=Kathy |last=Campbell |work=[[Us Weekly]] |date=2017-10-23 |accessdate=2018-01-10}}</ref> In December 2017, the couple announced they were expecting their first child, due in the spring of 2018.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://people.com/babies/john-stamos-fiancee-caitlin-mchugh-pregnant-expecting-first-child/ |title=John Stamos to Finally Be a Father at 54: 'I've Been Practicing for a Long Time' |first=Jess |last=Cagle |magazine=[[People (magazine)|People]] | date=2017-12-13 |accessdate=2018-01-10}}</ref>  Stamos and McHugh married in February 2018.<ref>{{cite news |url= http://m.eonline.com/news/911419/john-stamos-and-caitlin-mchugh-are-married |title=John Stamos and Caitlin McHugh Are Married |website=ENews Online |date=2018-02-03 |accessdate=2018-02-03}}</ref> On April 15, 2018, Stamos announced the birth of their first son, Billy, named after John's father.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BhnqDhqhKPg/|title=John Stamos on Instagram: "From now on, the best part of me will always be my wife and my son.
