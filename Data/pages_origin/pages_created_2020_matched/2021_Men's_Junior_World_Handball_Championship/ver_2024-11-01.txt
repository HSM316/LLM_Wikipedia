The 2021 '''[[IHF Men's Junior World Championship]]''' would have been the 23rd edition of the tournament, to be held in [[Budapest]], Hungary in 2021. It would have been second time that Hungary hosted the championship after [[2005 Men's Junior World Handball Championship|2005]].

On 22 February 2021, the tournament was cancelled due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ihf.info/media-center/news/key-decisions-made-ihf-events-ihf-council-meeting|title=Key decisions made on IHF events at IHF Council meeting|date=22 February 2021|work=ihf.info}}</ref>

==Bidding process==
[[Hellenic Handball Federation|Greece]], [[Hungarian Handball Federation|Hungary]] and [[Romanian Handball Federation|Romania]] were bidding to host the 2021 Men's Junior World Championship. The championship was awarded during the [[IHF Congress|36th IHF Congress]] at [[Antalya]], [[Turkey]] on Saturday, 11 November 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://archive.ihf.info/en-us/mediacentre/news/newsdetails.aspx?ID=5335|title=Dr Hassan Moustafa re-elected IHF President|date=11 November 2017|publisher=[[International Handball Federation]]}}</ref>

Greece was the host of the [[1991 Men's Junior World Handball Championship]] and the [[2011 Men's Junior World Handball Championship]]. Greece was awarded with the [[2021 Men's Youth World Handball Championship]].

Hungary hosted the [[2005 Men's Junior World Handball Championship]] and was host of the [[1982 World Women's Handball Championship]]. Hungary was also co-host of [[1995 World Women's Handball Championship]].

Romania hosted the [[1962 World Women's Handball Championship]], [[1977 Women's Junior World Handball Championship]] and also awarded with [[2020 Women's Junior World Handball Championship]].
