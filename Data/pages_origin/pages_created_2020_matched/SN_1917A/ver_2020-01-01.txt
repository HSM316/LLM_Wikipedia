'''SN 1917A''' is a supernova that occurred in the [[NGC 6946|Fireworks Galaxy]] on 19 July 1917. Discovered by [[George Willis Ritchey]], it reached a peak magnitude of 14.6 mag and is 37"W and 105"S of the core of the Fireworks Galaxy<ref name=seds />
