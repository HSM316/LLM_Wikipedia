'''Apishapa River''' is a {{convert|139|mi|km|adj=mid|-long}}<ref name=NHD>U.S. Geological Survey. National Hydrography Dataset high-resolution flowline data. [http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/ The National Map] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120329155652/http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/ |date=2012-03-29 }}, accessed March 31, 2011</ref> [[tributary]] of the [[Arkansas River]] that flows from a source near [[West Spanish Peak]] in southern [[Colorado]].  It joins the [[Arkansas River|Arkansas]] east of [[Fowler, Colorado]]. A U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) station, #07119500, located along this river near Fowler measures the river's discharge.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://waterdata.usgs.gov/co/nwis/uv/?site_no=07119500|title=USGS Current Conditions for USGS 07119500 APISHAPA RIVER NEAR FOWLER, CO}}</ref>

The level and salinity levels of the Apishapa River are monitored by gaging stations installed at three locations along the headwaters of the river in 2007.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.apishapawatershed.org/ |title=Archived copy |access-date=2022-06-24 |archive-date=2018-12-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181223082417/http://apishapawatershed.org/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>
