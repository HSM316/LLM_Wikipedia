'''Robin Lee "Rob" Hicklin''' (August 6, 1937 – September 22, 2006) was an [[American football]] coach.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cstv.com/sports/m-footbl/stories/092606afe.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190106153253/http://www.cstv.com/sports/m-footbl/stories/092606afe.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=January 6, 2019|title=Robin Hicklin|publisher=[[CBS Sports Network]]|access-date=December 30, 2018}}</ref> He served as the head football coach at [[Missouri Western University]] in [[Saint Joseph, Missouri]] from 1974 to 1985 and [[Southern Arkansas University]] from 1986 to 1989.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kidwellgarber.com/obituaries/Robin-RobLee-Hicklin-731/#!/Obituary|title=Robin "Rob" Lee Hicklin|publisher=lodwellgarber.com|access-date=December 30, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://gogriffons.com/hof.aspx?hof=30&mobile=skip|title=Rob Hicklin|publisher=[[Missouri Western Griffons]]|access-date=December 30, 2018}}</ref>
