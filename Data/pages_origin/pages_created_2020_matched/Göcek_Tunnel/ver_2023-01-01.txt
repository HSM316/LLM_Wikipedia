'''Göcek Tüneli''' ({{lang-tr|Göcek Tüneli}}) is a road tunnel in [[Dalaman]], [[Muğla]]. The tunnel goes through [[State road D.400 (Turkey)|D400]] road. It takes its name from [[Göcek, Fethiye|Göcek]]. It was opened on 9 September 2006, by the president of the term [[Recep Tayyip Erdoğan]].<ref name="haberler.com"/>
