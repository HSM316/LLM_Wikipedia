The '''1990 [[Welsh Professional Championship|Senator Windows Welsh Professional Championship]]''' was a professional non-[[Snooker world rankings|ranking]] snooker tournament, which took place in February 1990.<ref>{{citeweb|title=Welsh Professional Championship|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120216155654/http://www.cajt.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/welshpro.html|publisher=Chris Turner's Snooker Archive|accessdate=21 February 2018}}</ref>

[[Darren Morgan]] won the tournament defeating [[Doug Mountjoy]] 9–7 in the final.<ref name="Cuesport">{{cite book|last=Hayton|first=Eric|title=Cuesport Book of Professional Snooker|page=158}}</ref>
