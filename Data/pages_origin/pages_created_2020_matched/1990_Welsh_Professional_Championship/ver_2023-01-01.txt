The '''1990 [[Welsh Professional Championship|Senator Windows Welsh Professional Championship]]''' was a professional non-[[Snooker world rankings|ranking]] snooker tournament, which took place between 12 and 17 February 1990 at the [[Newport Centre (Wales)|Newport Centre]] in [[Newport, Wales|Newport]], [[Wales]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Newbury is seeded fourth |url=https://www.britishnewspaperarchive.co.uk/viewer/bl/0003773/19900111/031/0031|url-access=subscription|work=Neath Guardian|date=11 January 1990|page=31}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Welsh Professional Championship|url=http://www.cajt.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/welshpro.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120216155654/http://www.cajt.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/welshpro.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=16 February 2012|publisher=Chris Turner's Snooker Archive|accessdate=21 February 2018}}</ref>

[[Darren Morgan]] won the tournament defeating [[Doug Mountjoy]] 9–7 in the final.<ref name="Cuesport">{{cite book|last=Hayton|first=Eric|title=Cuesport Book of Professional Snooker|page=158}}</ref>
