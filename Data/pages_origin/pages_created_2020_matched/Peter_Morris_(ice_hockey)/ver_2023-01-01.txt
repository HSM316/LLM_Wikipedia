'''Peter Morris''' (born June 29, 1955) is a Canadian former professional [[ice hockey]] player who played in the [[World Hockey Association]] (WHA).<ref name="hdc">{{cite web|title=1975 NHL Amateur Draft -- Peter Morris|url=http://hockeydraftcentral.com/1975/75103.html|publisher=Hockey Draft Central|accessdate=March 27, 2014}}</ref> Drafted in the sixth round of the [[1975 NHL Amateur Draft]] by the [[Pittsburgh Penguins]], Morris opted to play in the WHA after being selected by the [[Edmonton Oilers]] in the second round of the [[1975 WHA Amateur Draft]].<ref name="hdc"/> He played parts of two WHA seasons for the Oilers.<ref name="ep">{{eliteprospects|264161|Peter Morris}}. Retrieved March 27, 2014.</ref> His father, [[Frank Morris (Canadian football)|Frank Morris]], played in the [[Canadian Football League]].<ref name="hdc"/>
