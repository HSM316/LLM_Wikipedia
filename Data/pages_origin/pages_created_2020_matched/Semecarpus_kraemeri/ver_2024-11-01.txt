'''''Semecarpus kraemeri''''', or '''Chuuk poisontree''',<ref>{{Cite web |title=Mindat.org |url=https://www.mindat.org/taxon-3660017.html |access-date=2023-01-13 |website=www.mindat.org}}</ref> is a species of [[plant]] in the family [[Anacardiaceae]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Semecarpus kraemeri {{!}} International Plant Names Index |url=https://www.ipni.org/n/71247-1 |access-date=2023-01-13 |website=www.ipni.org}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Mindat.org |url=https://www.mindat.org/taxon-3660017.html |access-date=2023-01-13 |website=www.mindat.org}}</ref> It is [[Endemism|endemic]] to the island of [[Chuuk State|Chuuk]] within the [[Federated States of Micronesia]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Chuuk Islands {{!}} islands, Micronesia {{!}} Britannica |url=https://www.britannica.com/place/Chuuk-Islands |access-date=2023-01-13 |website=www.britannica.com |language=en}}</ref>  A fellow endemic, the [[Faichuk white-eye|great Truk white-eye]] is thought to depend on this plant for survival.
