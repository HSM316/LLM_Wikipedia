'''105.3 Super Tunog Pinoy''' (DZCT 105.3 MHz) is an [[FM station]] owned and operated by [[DCG Radio-TV Network]]. Its studios and transmitter are located at 1022 DCG Tower 1, Maharlika Hi-Way, Brgy. Isabang, [[Tayabas]]<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://storage.googleapis.com/request-attachments/AV4y9qh3QvqYRdiDILKyN9nVIEnGnFovM9uuKB8iRaRW7PRrO633PGaXjSEULkqvEmjorBtVejJ00OBQf6uq5cH0nMZNFTt12UrY/FM%20STATIONS_2019.pdf | title = 2019 NTC FM Stations | website = [[Freedom of Information Order (Philippines)|foi.gov.ph]] | access-date = February 7, 2020}}</ref>

==History==
*2009 - '''Radio City''' was inaugurated. It was formerly owned by [[Kaissar Broadcasting Network]] and operated by Southern Tagalog Sweet Life.<ref>{{Citation | url = https://psa.gov.ph/sites/default/files/2011PY_Communications.pdf |title = TABLE 20.7a | journal = 2011 Philippine Yearbook | pages = 18â€“45 | publisher = [[Philippine Statistics Authority]] | access-date = February 7, 2020}}</ref>
*September 2014 - Katigbak Enterprises acquired the station and became a relay of [[DWLA|Retro 105.9]] in Manila.<ref>{{Cite web | url = https://balitangkamhantik.net/katotohanan-sa-isyu-ng-radiocity/ | title = Ang Katotohanan sa Isyu ng Radiocity | website = balitangkamhantik.net | date = November 18, 2014 | access-date = February 7, 2020}}</ref> Radio City moved to [[DWNG|97.5]] a week later.<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://ibalita.blogspot.com/2014/09/re-launching-ng-975-radio-city-naging.html | title = Re-launching ng 97.5 Radio City Naging Matagumpay | website = balita.blogspot.com | date = September 23, 2014 | access-date = February 7, 2020}}</ref>
*June 2017 - It went off the air.
*January 2018 - '''Super Tunog Pinoy''' was launched with an [[Original Pilipino Music|all-OPM]] format.
