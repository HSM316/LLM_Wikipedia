'''Jean-Paul Comart''' (born 27 September 1953) is a [[Belgium|Belgian]] [[actor]] best known for his appearances in French film in the 1980s. He has appeared in films, TV and in the theatre.

Since 2000, Comart has mostly appeared on television, playing Inspector Miller in the series ''[[Trois femmes flics]]'' amongst other roles.
