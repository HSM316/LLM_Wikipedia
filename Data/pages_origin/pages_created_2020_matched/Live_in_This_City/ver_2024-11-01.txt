"'''Live In This City'''" is an [[electropop]]/[[New wave music|new wave]] song performed by [[Canada|Canadian]] band [[Dragonette]]. The song was written and produced by Dragonette for their third studio album ''[[Bodyparts (album)|Bodyparts]]'' (2012). It was released as the album's second single in August 2012.

==Music video==
The official music video for the track premiered September 17, 2012, on Dragonette's official [[YouTube]] channel.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ca_W2FajtUQ |title=Dragonette - Live In This City (Official Video) |publisher=YouTube |date=2012-09-17 |accessdate=2013-07-24}}</ref>
