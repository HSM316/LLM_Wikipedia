'''''Glyphis hastalis''''' is a species of extinct [[river shark]] from the United Kingdom.<ref>{{cite web|title=Fossilworks: Glyphis hastalis|url=http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=210102|website=fossilworks.org|accessdate=13 April 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=United States National Museum|title=Proceedings of the United States National Museum|page=20|url=https://repository.si.edu/bitstream/handle/10088/16497/USNMP-99_3235_1949.pdf?sequence=1&isAllowed=y|accessdate=13 April 2018}}</ref> Modern relatives include the [[Ganges shark]] and [[speartooth shark]].
