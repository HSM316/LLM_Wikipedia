'''Alexandra Grace Brooks''' (born 19 January 1995) is an English [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[Blackburn Rovers L.F.C.|Blackburn Rovers]] in the [[FA Women's Championship]].

==Club career==
Having come through their academy system, Alexandra Brooks made her first team debut for [[Manchester City W.F.C.|Manchester City]] when she replaced [[Karen Bardsley]] in an [[FA WSL Cup]] match against [[Durham W.F.C.|Durham]] on 13 July 2014.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mcfc.co.uk/News/Match-reports/2014/July/Durham-Women-v-Manchester-City-Women-13-July-2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140808205741/http://www.mcfc.co.uk/News/Match-reports/2014/July/Durham-Women-v-Manchester-City-Women-13-July-2014 |title=Durham Women 0 City 3 |date=13 July 2014 |archive-date=8 August 2014 |accessdate=27 January 2019 }}</ref> She would further occasional appearances for the next season and a half before a loan move to [[Everton L.F.C.|Everton]] for the duration of the 2016 season plus the half-length 2017 [[FA WSL Spring Series]] campaign. The following year she departed City for Sheffield United.

On 26 January 2019, Brooks moved back to the highest division of English football with a transfer to [[Birmingham City W.F.C.|Birmingham City]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bcfc.com/news/articles/2019/blues-women-bring-in-new-goalkeeper |title=BLUES WOMEN BRING IN NEW GOALKEEPER |work=Birmingham City F.C. official website |date=26 January 2019 |accessdate=27 January 2019 }}</ref>
