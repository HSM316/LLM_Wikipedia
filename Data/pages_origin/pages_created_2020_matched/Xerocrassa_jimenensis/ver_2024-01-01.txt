'''''Xerocrassa jimenensis''''' is a [[species]] of air-breathing [[land snail]], a [[pulmonate]] [[gastropod]] [[mollusk]] in the family [[Geomitridae]].<ref name=WoRMS>MolluscaBase eds. (2022). MolluscaBase. Xerocrassa jimenensis (Puente & Arrébola, 1996). Accessed through: World Register of Marine Species at: https://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails&id=1050700 on 2022-08-08</ref> 

==Distribution==
[[File:Xerocrassa-jimenensis-map-eur-nm-moll.jpg|thumb|left|Distribution]]
This species is [[endemism|endemic]] to the provinces of [[Province of Cádiz|Cádiz]], [[Province of Málaga|Málaga]] and [[Province of Seville|Seville]] in [[Andalusia]], southern [[Spain]].<ref name="iucn status 12 November 2021" /><ref name=AnimalBase>{{cite web| url=http://www.animalbase.uni-goettingen.de/zooweb/servlet/AnimalBase/home/species?id=3772| author=Welter Schultes, F.| title=Species summary for ''Xerocrassa jimenensis''| work=[[AnimalBase]]| publisher=[[Göttingen State and University Library|SUB Göttingen]]| access-date=27 July 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.faunaeur.org/full_results.php?id=429164| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924144530/http://www.faunaeur.org/full_results.php?id=429164| url-status=dead| archive-date=September 24, 2015| title=Taxon Details: ''Xerocrassa jimenensis'' (Puente & Arrebola, 1996)| publisher=[[Fauna Europaea]]| access-date=30 July 2014}}</ref>
