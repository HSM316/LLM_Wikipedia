'''Carl Eric Bulow''' was a male [[Swedes|Swedish]] international [[table tennis]] player.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tabletennis.guide/profile.php?name=carl-eric-bulow-130406|title=Profile|publisher=Table Tennis Guide}}</ref>

==Table tennis career==
He won a [[silver medal]] at the [[1930 World Table Tennis Championships]] in the men's team event.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sports123.com/tte/index.html|title=Table Tennis World Championship medal winners|publisher=Sports123|access-date=2018-03-08|archive-date=2018-09-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180922055935/http://sports123.com/tte/index.html|url-status=dead}}</ref>
