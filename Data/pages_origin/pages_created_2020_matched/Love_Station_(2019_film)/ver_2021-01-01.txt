'''Love Station''' is a 2019 Nepalese [[Drama (film and television)|drama]] [[Romance film|romance]] film directed by [[Ujwal Ghimire]]. The film is produced by Gobinda Shahi, Krishu Shahi, and Kabir Bikram Shahi under the banner of Kafiya Films.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.moviemandu.com/pradeep-khadka-to-star-in-love-station-makers-locked-the-release-date/|title=Pradeep Khadka To Star In LOVE STATION, Makers Locked The Release Date|language=en-US|access-date=2019-03-19}}</ref> The film stars [[Pradeep Khadka]], and [[Jassita Gurung]] in the lead roles alongside Ramesh Budhathoki, Siru Bista, Rabi Dangol and Priya Rizal in the supporting roles.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://kathmandupost.ekantipur.com/news/2018-09-12/veteran-director-ujwal-ghimires-dream-project-to-begin-filming.html|title=Veteran director Ujwal Ghimire’s ‘dream project’ to begin filming|website=kathmandupost.ekantipur.com|language=English|access-date=2019-03-19}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://myrepublica.nagariknetwork.com/news/49560/|title=Pradeep Khadka and Jassita Gurung confirmed for ‘Love Station’|last=Republica|website=My City|language=en|access-date=2019-03-19}}</ref> The film is scheduled to release on 5 April 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://myrepublica.nagariknetwork.com/news/60583/|title=Pradeep and Jassita romancing in 'Love Station'|last=Republica|website=My City|language=en|access-date=2019-03-19}}</ref>
