'''Kartal Özmızrak''' (born 29 August 1995) is a Turkish professional [[basketball]] player for [[Bahçeşehir Koleji S.K.|Bahçeşehir Koleji]] of the [[Basketbol Süper Ligi]] (BSL) and [[Basketball Champions League]].

==Professional career==
Kartal was a product of [[Beşiktaş J.K. (men's basketball)|Beşiktaş]] youth system and he also played there between 2011 and 2015. During this period, he has been loaned to [[Edirne Basket|Eskişehir Basket]] for 2014–15 season.

On December 8, 2015, he has signed with [[Darüşşafaka S.K.|Darüşşafaka]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.haberturk.com/spor/basketbol/haber/1164058-kartal-ozmizrak-darussafakali-oldu|title=Kartal Özmızrak Darüşşafakalı oldu|last=|first=|date=December 8, 2015|website=|publisher=haberturk|language=tr|access-date=July 1, 2021}}</ref> and then loaned to [[İstanbul Büyükşehir Belediyespor (basketball)|İstanbul BB]] for two seasons.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.eurohoops.net/tr/tbl-tr/310368/kartal-oezmizrak-ibbye-kiralandi/|title=Kartal Özmızrak İBB'ye Kiralandı|last=|first=|date=September 9, 2016|website=|publisher=Eurohoops|language=tr|access-date=July 1, 2021}}</ref>

On July 6, 2020, he has signed with [[Obradoiro CAB|Obradoiro]] of the [[Liga ACB]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sportando.basketball/en/obradoiro-puts-kartal-ozmizrak-at-point-guard/|title=Obradoiro puts Kartal Ozmizrak at point guard|last=|first=|date=July 6, 2020|website=|publisher=Sportando|language=En|access-date=July 1, 2021}}</ref>

On July 6, 2021, he has signed with [[Bahçeşehir Koleji S.K.|Bahçeşehir Koleji]] of the Turkish [[Basketbol Süper Ligi]] (BSL).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://basketservisi.com/2021/07/06/resmi-bahcesehir-kartali-acikladi/|title=RESMİ: Bahçeşehir, Kartal’ı Açıkladı|last=|first=|date=July 6, 2021|website=|publisher=basketservisi|language=En|access-date=July 6, 2021}}</ref>
