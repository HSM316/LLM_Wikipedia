'''Kartal Özmızrak''' (born 29 August 1995) is a Turkish professional [[basketball]] player for [[Darüşşafaka S.K.|Darüşşafaka]] of the Turkish [[Basketbol Süper Ligi]] (BSL) and the [[EuroLeague]].
