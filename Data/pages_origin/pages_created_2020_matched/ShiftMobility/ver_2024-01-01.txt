'''ShiftMobility''', stylized as '''SHIFTMobility''', is a mobile platform for the [[automotive industry]]. It is the first platform to connect parts distributors, manufacturers, repair shops and car drivers through a fully integrated [[mobile application]].<ref name="wsj">{{cite web| url=https://blogs.wsj.com/venturecapital/2015/07/07/shiftmobility-raises-2-million-to-make-cars-smarter-about-repairs/| title=ShiftMobility Raises $2 Million to Make Cars Smarter About Repairs| last=Gage| first=Deborah| date=7 July 2015| publisher=Wall Street Journal| access-date=21 September 2015}}</ref><ref name="creator">{{cite web| url=https://creator.wework.com/foundedbywomen/shiftmobility-one-app-slashes-repair-time-getting-cars-in-road-ready-condition-faster-than-ever/| title=ShiftMobility: Getting Cars Road-Ready Faster Than Ever| last=Sullivan| first=Mark| date=14 July 2015| publisher=Creator Magazine| access-date=21 September 2015}}</ref>

==Service==
According to its founders, ShiftMobility is one of the first companies connecting aftermarket manufacturers, distributors, and repair shops with each other and clients on a single platform.<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.motownindia.com/Interview-Videos/17/2015-ACMA-Automechanika-New-Delhi-Feb-26-to-March-1-2015#.VdIqiflVikp| title=Interview Video with 2015 ACMA Automechanika New Delhi| date=1 March 2015| publisher=Motown| access-date=21 September 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.motownindia.com/Interviews/102/Pavana-Jain-P-Tharyan-CEO-Co-founder-ShiftMobility#.VdIsYPlVikq| title=Interview with Pavana Jain| date=29 March 2015| publisher=Motown India| access-date=21 September 2015}}</ref> ShopLite is ShiftMobility's mobile app that manages parts sources for independent repair centers by connecting part manufacturer's distributor inventory and price lists to repair centers allowing immediate response to car owner's service needs. The company combined industry research and input from automotive shop owners to design the fully integrated application.<ref>{{cite journal| title=Apps Available to Manage Service and Parts for Automotive Independents| date=March 2015| journal=The Cooling Journal| volume=58| issue=3| page=17| url=http://narsa.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/March_April-CJ.pdf}}{{dead link|date=May 2018 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>  ShopLite includes services such as license plate and VIN scan abilities, an OEM library with over 30 car manufacturers, and a searchable network of local OE and aftermarket distributors inventories.<ref>{{cite web| url=http://partsandpeople.com/national-mechanical/shiftmobility-offers-all-one-platform |title=SHIFTMobility Offers All-In-One Platform |author=Shane Rohleder |date=October 1, 2015 |publisher=Parts and People| accessdate=October 23, 2015}}</ref>
