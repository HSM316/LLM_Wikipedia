'''Kristin Viktoria Amparo Sundberg''' (born 1 December 1983) is a Swedish singer. She attended [[Adolf Fredrik's Music School]] in [[Stockholm]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://beingblogged.se/2015/02/en-intervju-med-kristin-amparo-infor-melodifestivalen |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150522063208/http://beingblogged.se/2015/02/en-intervju-med-kristin-amparo-infor-melodifestivalen |title=En intervju med Kristin Amparo inför Melodifestivalen |publisher=Being Blogged |author=Johan Alexed |language=Swedish |date=20 February 2015 |archivedate=2015-05-22}}</ref> She participated in season 1 of the Swedish ''[[X Factor (Swedish TV series)|X Factor]]'' in 2012, which was broadcast on [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]], where she made it to the judge's houses stage before being eliminated.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nyheter24.se/noje/kandissverige/724811-hon-far-staende-ovationer-av-x-factor-juryn|title=Hon rör Marie Serneholt till tårar|publisher=|accessdate=13 February 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150214013147/http://nyheter24.se/noje/kandissverige/724811-hon-far-staende-ovationer-av-x-factor-juryn|archive-date=14 February 2015|url-status=live}}</ref> Amparo participated in [[Melodifestivalen 2015]] with the song "I See You" in the third semi-final.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.svt.se/melodifestivalen/artister/2015/kristin-amparo-i-see-you |title=Kristin Amparo – I See You |work=svt.se |accessdate=13 February 2015 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150214013324/http://www.svt.se/melodifestivalen/artister/2015/kristin-amparo-i-see-you |archivedate=14 February 2015 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.svd.se/kultur/3878584.svd|title=Kristin Amparo går sina drömmars väg|author=Stockholm TT|work=SvD.se|accessdate=13 February 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150506184039/http://www.svd.se/kultur/3878584.svd|archive-date=6 May 2015|url-status=live}}</ref> She made it to the "Second Chance" round but failed to make it to the Melodifestivalen final.
