'''Ronald Victor Thomas''' (7 August 1888 – 30 December 1936) was an Australian [[tennis]] player.<ref name="Sports Reference">[https://web.archive.org/web/20200418071117/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/th/ronald-thomas-1.html Ronald Thomas' profile at Sports Reference.com]</ref>

Thomas won two doubles titles at the Australasian Championships (now the [[Australian Open]]), in [[1919 Australasian Championships|1919]] and [[1920 Australasian Championships|1920]], and one doubles title at the [[1919 Wimbledon Championships]]. Thomas also finished as runner-up to [[Pat O'Hara Wood]] in men's singles at  the [[1920 Australasian Championships]].<ref name=AOHistory>{{cite web|work=australianopen.com|title=History of the Australian Open – the Grand Slam of Asia/Pacific|url=http://www.australianopen.com/en_AU/event_guide/history/history.html|accessdate=2009-06-28|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090525071428/http://www.australianopen.com/en_AU/event_guide/history/history.html|archivedate=25 May 2009|df=dmy-all}}</ref>
