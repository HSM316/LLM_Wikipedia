'''''Indina Ramayana''''' ({{translation|Today's Ramayana}}) is a 1984 Indian [[Kannada]]-language film, directed by Rajachandra and produced by [[Dwarakish]]. The film stars [[Vishnuvardhan (actor)|Vishnuvardhan]], Gayathri, [[Sridhar (Kannada actor)|Sridhar]] and [[Tulasi (actress)|Tulasi]]. The film has musical score by Vijayanand.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.filmibeat.com/kannada/movies/indina-ramayana.html|title=Indina Ramayana|accessdate=2015-01-23|publisher=filmibeat.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.apunkachoice.com/titles/ind/indina-ramayana/mid_35680/|title=Indina Ramayana|accessdate=2015-01-23|publisher=apunkachoice.com}}</ref> The movie is a remake of the Tamil movie ''[[Oorukku upadesam|Oorukku Upadesam]]''. The movie had a successful run of 15 weeks in Bangalore and Mysore.
