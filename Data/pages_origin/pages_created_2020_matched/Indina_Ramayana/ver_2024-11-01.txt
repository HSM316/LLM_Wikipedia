'''''Indina Ramayana''''' ({{translation|Today's Ramayana}}) is a 1984 Indian [[Kannada]]-language film, directed by Rajachandra and produced by [[Dwarakish]]. The film stars [[Vishnuvardhan (actor)|Vishnuvardhan]], [[Gayatri (actress)|Gayatri]], [[Sridhar (actor)|Sridhar]] and [[Tulasi (actress)|Tulasi]]. The film has music by [[Vijay Anand (composer)|Vijay Anand]]. It is a remake of the Tamil film ''[[Oorukku Upadesam]]''.<ref>{{Cite news |date=16 March 1986 |title=இசை வானில் ஒரு புதிய நிலா |url=https://eap.bl.uk/archive-file/EAP372-6-23-10-137 |access-date=4 June 2024 |work=Anna |pages=3 |language=Ta |via=[[Endangered Archives Programme]] |archive-date=4 June 2024 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240604063759/https://eap.bl.uk/archive-file/EAP372-6-23-10-137 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Indina Ramayana (ಇಂದಿನ ರಾಮಾಯಣ) |url=https://chiloka.com/movie/indina-ramayana-1984 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230805150031/https://chiloka.com/movie/indina-ramayana-1984 |archive-date=5 August 2023 |access-date=4 June 2024 |website=Chiloka}}</ref> The movie had a successful run of 25 weeks in Bangalore and Mysore.
