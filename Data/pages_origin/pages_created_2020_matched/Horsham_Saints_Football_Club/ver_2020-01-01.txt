The '''Horsham Saints Football Netball Club''' is an [[Australian rules football]] and [[netball]] club based in the city of [[Horsham, Victoria]]. The football team competes in the [[Wimmera Football League]] (WFL).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportingpulse.com/club_info.cgi?client=1-6210-81049-0-0| title=Official Club Website| publisher=Sportingpulse}}</ref> 

==History==
The Horsham Saints, formed in 1945, was known as YCW and joined the [[Horsham & District Football League]] (HDFL). In 1947 the club changed its name to St. Michael's. The club won five premierships in the HDFL.

In 1993, the club moved to the [[Wimmera Football League]]. In 2000, the club adopted its current name.
