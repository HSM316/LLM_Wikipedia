'''Rekha''', popularly known as '''Sparsha Rekha''', is an Indian film and television actress known for her work in [[Kannada cinema]].

==Career==
Rekha pursued modeling and subsquently appeared in the films ''[[Sparsha (film)|Sparsha]]'' (2000) and ''[[Majestic (film)|Majestic]]'' (2002), both of which were successful.
She decided to take a hiatus in her career to complete her Bachelor's of Business Management degree at Maharani College, Bangalore.
