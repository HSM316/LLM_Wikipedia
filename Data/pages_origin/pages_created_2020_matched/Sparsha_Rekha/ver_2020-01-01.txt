Rekha, popularly known as "Sparsha Rekha" is a [[Kannada]] actress born to Shankar Prasad and Bhagya Lakshmi in Bangalore, she was brought up in [[Bangalore]] as well.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://chiloka.com/celebrity/sparsha-rekha|title=Sparsha Rekha|publisher=Chiloka}}</ref>

==Early life==
''Rekha'' was born in Bangalore and was brought up in Bangalore as well.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://chiloka.com/celebrity/sparsha-rekha|title=Sparsha Rekha|publisher=Chiloka}}</ref> She completed her Bachelors in Business Management from Maharani's College, Bangalore was a National Player in Rowing, Kayaking,Fencing.Also a certified Mountaineer, Rock climber.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://chiloka.com/celebrity/sparsha-rekha|title=Sparsha Rekha|publisher=Chiloka}}</ref>

==Personal life==
''Rekha'' married Sandesh an Engineer and has two children, son named Sohan and daughter Nidhi.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://chiloka.com/celebrity/sparsha-rekha|title=Sparsha Rekha|publisher=Chiloka}}</ref>
