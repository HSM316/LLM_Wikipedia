'''Aleš Mandous''' (born 21 April 1992) is a [[Czech Republic|Czech]] professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[SK Slavia Prague|Slavia Prague]] and the [[Czech Republic national football team|Czech Republic national team]].

==Club career==
===Viktoria Plzeň===
On 23 March 2013, Mandous made his competitive debut in the second leg of the fourth round of the [[2012–13 Czech Cup|Czech Cup]] against [[FC Hradec Králové|Hradec Králové]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.fcviktoria.cz/eng/clanek.asp?id=7428 |title=Viktorka beat Hradec Králové and celebrates quarterfinals |publisher=FC Viktoria Plzeň |date=23 March 2013 |accessdate=21 August 2015 |language=English}}</ref> Plzeň won the game 1-0 and advanced to the next round.

===FK Bohemians Prague (Střížkov)===
He spent the following season on loan at [[FK Bohemians Prague (Střížkov)|Bohemians Prague (Střížkov)]]. Mandous played 18 games in this campaign, which ended with the club being relegated to the [[Bohemian Football League|third flight of Czech football]].

===Baník Most===
In the summer of 2014, he was sent on loan. This time he joined [[FK Baník Most 1909|Baník Most]], who were at that time in the [[2014–15 Czech National Football League|second tier of Czech football]]. Mandous played in 18 matches and was relegated with the club in 2015.

===MŠK Žilina===
Mandous joined [[MŠK Žilina|Žilina]] as a free agent in June 2015, when he signed a two-year contract with the club.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.zilinskyvecernik.sk/articles/2015/06/22/letna-posila-ales-mandous-zavazili-dobre-vztahy-ziliny-s-plznou |title=Letná posila Aleš Mandous: Zavážili dobré vzťahy Žiliny s Plzňou |publisher=Žilinský Večerník |date=22 June 2015 |accessdate=15 August 2015 |language=Slovak}}</ref> On 12 August 2015, he made his competitive debut in the [[2015–16 Slovak Cup|Slovak Cup]] match against ŠK Strážske, which Žilina won 9-0.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mskzilina.sk/articles/detail/11617 |title=Slovnaft Cup: ŠK Strážske - MŠK Žilina 0:9 (0:4) |publisher=MŠK Žilina |date=12 August 2015 |accessdate=15 August 2015 |language=Slovak |archive-date=5 March 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305074035/http://www.mskzilina.sk/articles/detail/11617 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==International career==
On 4 June 2013, Mandous played his sole game for the [[Czech Republic national under-21 football team|Czech Under-21 team.]] In this match, the Czech Republic lost 3-0 to [[Austria national under-21 football team|Austria.]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://nv.fotbal.cz/scripts/detail.php?id=132772&tmplid=3080 |title=Do turnaje na Ukrajině vstoupila reprezentace do 21 let prohrou 0:3 s Rakouskem a bude hrát o 3. místo s poraženým z duelu domácího celku se Slovinskem. |publisher=The Football Association of the Czech Republic |accessdate=21 August 2015 |language=Czech}}</ref>

In September 2020, Mandous was selected as one of 23 players called up to the national team due to the original squad having to quarantine due to exposure to [[COVID-19|Coronavirus]]. Mandous played the full 90 minutes for [[Czech Republic national football team|Czech Republic]] against [[Scotland national football team|Scotland]] which ended in a 1–2 defeat for the home side.<ref>[https://www.glasgowtimes.co.uk/sport/18703903.amp/ Czech Republic 1 Scotland 2: How the makeshift Czechs rated as they gave Scots a scare], Glasgow Times, 7 September 2020</ref>
