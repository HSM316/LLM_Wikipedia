'''Mario Acevedo''' (born July 6, 1955) is an American [[novelist]] and artist, known for his series of [[urban fantasy]] novels featuring the [[vampire]] [[private investigator]] Felix Gomez.  He lives and works in [[Denver, Colorado]].<ref>https://www.harpercollins.com/author/cr-102087/mario-acevedo/ Profile page at HarperCollins Publishing.</ref> Acevedo was born in El Paso, Texas.<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/729565366|title=An elevated view : Colorado writers on writing|date=2011|publisher=Seven Oaks Pub|others=Jameson, W. C., 1942-|isbn=9780963082961|location=Llano, Tex.|oclc=729565366}}</ref> Before becoming a published writer, Acevedo held jobs as a military helicopter pilot, [[paratrooper]], infantry officer, engineer, art teacher, software programmer, and assorted others.<ref name=":0" /> He was also deployed as a soldier and artist for the [[U.S. Army]] during [[Operation Desert Storm]].<ref name=":0" />
