'''Harmodio Arias Cerjack''' (c. 1956 – February 6, 2014) was a [[Panama]]nian politician. He was the [[Foreign Minister (Panama)|Foreign Minister]] from 2003 to 2004.<ref>[http://www.telemetro.com/nacionales/Fallece-ex-canciller-Harmodio-Arias-sepelio_0_668333467.html Fallece ex-canciller Harmodio Arias, este viernes será el sepelio] {{in lang|es}}</ref>

Cerjack died at the age of 57.
