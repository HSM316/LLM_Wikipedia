{{nihongo|'''Mariko Okamoto'''|岡本 眞理子|Okamoto Mariko}} (born 28 December 1951) is a Japanese [[volleyball|volleyball player]] and Olympic champion.

Okamoto was a member of the Japanese winning team at the 1976 Olympic games.<ref name=db-ol-VOL1976>[http://www.databaseolympics.com/games/gamessport.htm?g=19&sp=VOL "1976 Summer Olympics &ndash; Montreal, Canada &ndash; Volleyball"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923212948/http://www.databaseolympics.com/games/gamessport.htm?g=19&sp=VOL |date=2015-09-23 }} ''databaseOlympics.com'' (Retrieved on September 6, 2008)</ref>
