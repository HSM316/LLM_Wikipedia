'''Liam David Grimshaw''' (born 2 February 1995) is an English professional [[association football|footballer]], who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|right back]] for [[Scottish Premiership]] club [[Motherwell F.C.|Motherwell]]. He can also play as a [[midfielder]].

==Club career==

===Manchester United===
Grimshaw joined United at schoolboy level,<ref name = "Profile">{{cite web|url=http://www.manutd.com/en/Players-And-Staff/Reserves/Liam-Grimshaw.aspx|title=Liam Grimshaw|accessdate=24 September 2015|publisher=[[Manchester United F.C.]] Official Website}}</ref> and later in his career went on to make his debut for the [[Manchester United F.C. Reserves and Academy|Manchester United Academy]] against city rivals [[Manchester City F.C.|Manchester City]].<ref name = "Profile"/> During the 2012–13 season Grimshaw was a regular in the side, notching up a total of 28 appearances and eventually forcing his way into the Manchester United reserve side during the latter stages of that campaign.<ref name = "Profile"/> Also, Grimshaw was appointed as Academy team captain, which he were to take on regularly. His eye-catching form led to [[Paul McGuinness (footballer)|Paul McGuinness]], manager of the under-18's team, comparing Grimshaw with the likes of [[John O'Shea]] and [[Phil Neville]], both of whom progressed through the youth ranks at [[Old Trafford]].<ref name = "Profile"/>

==== Motherwell (loan) ====
Grimshaw's first taste of first-team action as a professional was with Scottish club [[Motherwell F.C.|Motherwell]], whom he joined for the 2015–16 season.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/34124389|title=Manchester United's Liam Grimshaw joins Motherwell on loan|accessdate=24 September 2015|date=2 September 2015|publisher=[[BBC Sport]]}}</ref> He made his debut for the Steelmen from the bench in a 1–1 draw at home to [[Ross County F.C.|Ross County]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/34160630|title=Motherwell 1–1 Ross County|accessdate=24 September 2015|date=12 September 2015|publisher=[[BBC Sport]]}}</ref> In January 2016, Grimshaw was recalled from his loan at Motherwell shortly before it was due to expire.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.motherwellfc.co.uk/2016/01/12/grimshaw-returns-south/ |title=Grimshaw returns south |accessdate=12 January 2016 |date=12 January 2016 |publisher=motherwellfc.co.uk}}</ref>

=== Preston North End ===
On 18 January 2016, Grimshaw joined [[Preston North End F.C.|Preston North End]] for an undisclosed fee, signing a two-and-a-half-year contract.<ref>{{cite web |title=Liam Grimshaw Signs Permanent Deal From Manchester United |url=http://www.pnefc.net/news/article/liam-grimshaw-signs-permanent-deal-from-manchester-united-2908961.aspx |website=pnefc.net |accessdate=18 January 2016 }}</ref> He was released from his contract in August 2017.<ref name = "preston release">{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/41117187 |title=Defender Liam Grimshaw returns to Motherwell |work=BBC Sport |publisher=BBC |date=31 August 2017 |accessdate=31 August 2017}}</ref>

====Chesterfield (loan)====
On 31 January 2017, Grimshaw joined [[Chesterfield F.C.|Chesterfield]] on loan until the end of the season. He made his debut for the club on 4 February against [[Oldham Athletic A.F.C.|Oldham Athletic]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chesterfield-fc.co.uk/news/article/2016-17/report-chesterfield-vs-oldham-athletic-3555640.aspx|title=REPORT: Spireites Frustrated by Oldham Athletic|date=4 February 2017|publisher=chesterfield-fc.co.uk|accessdate=6 February 2017}}</ref>

===Motherwell===
Grimshaw returned to [[Motherwell F.C.|Motherwell]] on a permanent basis in August 2017.<ref name = "preston release"/> On 16 May 2018, Grimshaw signed a new one-year contract with Motherwell.<ref name="Grimshaw and Rose sign new deals">{{cite web|title=Grimshaw and Rose sign new deals|url=http://www.motherwellfc.co.uk/2018/05/16/grimshaw-and-rose-sign-new-deals/|website=motherwellfc.co.uk|publisher=Motherwell F.C.|accessdate=17 May 2018|date=16 May 2018}}</ref> On 12 March 2019, he signed a further one-year contract keeping him at the club until 2020.<ref>{{cite web|title=Liam Grimshaw signs new Motherwell deal until 2020|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/47547622|publisher=BBC Sport|date=12 March 2019|accessdate=18 March 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
Grimshaw's form during the 2012–13 season also saw him make his first appearance for the England Under-18s against Italy.<ref name="Profile" />
