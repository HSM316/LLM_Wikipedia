'''Sixto Vera Espínola''' (born 27 April 1965) is a [[Paraguay]]an former professional [[Boxer (boxing)|boxer]] who competed from 1990 to 2007. As an amateur, he represented his country in the [[Boxing at the 1988 Summer Olympics|flyweight]] division at the [[1988 Summer Olympics]], where he lost his first bout against [[Bishnu Bahadur Singh]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ve/sixto-vera-1.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200418003955/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ve/sixto-vera-1.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=2020-04-18|title=Sixto Vera Bio, Stats, and Results|website=Olympics at Sports-Reference.com}}</ref>

==1988 Olympic results==
Below is the record of Sixto Vera, a flyweight boxer from Paraguay who competed at the 1988 Seoul Olympics:

* Round of 64: lost to Bishnu Bahadur Singh (Nepal) by decision, 0-5
