'''Sixto Vera''' is a [[Paraguayan]] [[Olympic Games|Olympic]] [[boxer]]. He represented his country in the [[Boxing at the 1988 Summer Olympics|flyweight]] division at the [[1988 Summer Olympics]]. He lost his first bout against [[Bishnu Bahadur Singh]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ve/sixto-vera-1.html|title=Sixto Vera Bio, Stats, and Results|website=Olympics at Sports-Reference.com}}</ref>

==1988 Olympic results==
Below is the record of Sixto Vera, a flyweight boxer from Paraguay who competed at the 1988 Seoul Olympics:

* Round of 64: lost to Bishnu Bahadur Singh (Nepal) by decision, 0-5
