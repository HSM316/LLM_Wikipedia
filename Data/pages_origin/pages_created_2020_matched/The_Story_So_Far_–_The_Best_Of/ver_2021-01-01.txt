'''''The Story So Far – The Best Of''''' is a [[compilation album]] by the English rock band [[Def Leppard]]. The album has similar track listing to previous compilations, however it is the first to include tracks from the band's recent studio efforts ''[[Songs from the Sparkle Lounge]]'' and ''[[Def Leppard (album)|Def Leppard]]'' as well as all three studio tracks from ''[[Mirror Ball – Live & More]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.defleppard.com/the-story-so-far/|title=Announcing The Story So Far - Available November 30|date=12 October 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.udiscovermusic.com/news/def-leppard-hits-story-so-far/|title=Def Leppard To Release New Greatest Hits ‘The Story So Far’ And 10-Disc Vinyl Box ‘Hysteria: The Singles’|first=Tim|last=Peacock|date=12 October 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://sleazeroxx.com/def-leppard-release-compilation-album-the-story-so-far-the-best-of-def-leppard/|title=Def Leppard release compilation album ‘The Story So Far: The Best Of Def Leppard’ – Sleaze Roxx|last=Olivier}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/def-leppard-to-release-the-story-so-far---the-best-of-on-november-30-300730159.html|title=DEF LEPPARD To Release 'The Story So Far - The Best Of' on November 30|first=Universal Music|last=Enterprises|website=www.prnewswire.com}}</ref>

==Record Store Day==
On [[Record Store Day]] April 13, 2019, the band released volume two (disc two of the CD version) of ''The Story So Far''.<ref>{{cite web|title=RSD '19 Special Release: Def Leppard - The Story So Far Vol 2/B-Sides|url=http://recordstoreday.com/SpecialRelease/10868|access-date=2019-04-10}}</ref>
