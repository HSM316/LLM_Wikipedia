'''MCI Group''', based in Geneva, Switzerland, is the world's largest [[professional conference organiser]] employing 1,800 people in 31 countries as of April 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://meetingsnet.com/southwest/houston-and-mci-partner-attract-global-medical-meetings|title=Houston and MCI Partner to Attract Global Medical Meetings|website=meetingsnet.com|access-date=2016-05-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://associationsnow.com/2015/03/mci-group-coulter-companies-announce-merger/|title=MCI Group, Coulter Companies Announce Merger|date=2015-03-12|website=Associations Now|language=en-US|access-date=2016-05-30}}</ref> The company also operates as an [[association management company]], or AMC.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mci-group.com/en/what_we_do/our_services/association_management|title=Association Management {{!}} Our Services {{!}} MCI|website=www.mci-group.com|access-date=2016-05-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.meetings-conventions.com/News/Third-Party/MCI-Coulter-association-management-strategic-event-management/|title=MCI Group, Coulter Cos. Merge to Form MCI USA: Meetings & Conventions|website=www.meetings-conventions.com|access-date=2016-05-30}}</ref>

MCI operates marketing-events firm Black Flower Agency; Don't Believe in Style, a cultural creative agency in southeast Asia; technical production firm Dorier; performance measurement services firm FairControl; public-affairs company Logos Public Affairs; destination management company Ovation DMC; and incentive-travel operator Hagen Invent.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mci-group.com/en/about_us/group_brands|title=Group Brands {{!}} Company {{!}} MCI|website=www.mci-group.com|access-date=2016-05-31}}</ref>

In 2015, the company acquired McLean, Va.-based Coulter Cos., an AMC, which has since been rebranded MCI USA.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.meetings-conventions.com/News/Third-Party/Switzerland-based-MCI-Group-and-Virgina-based-Coulter-Join-Forces/|title=Switzerland-based MCI Group and Virginia-based Coulter Join Forces: Meetings & Conventions|website=www.meetings-conventions.com|access-date=2016-05-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mci-group.com/en-US|title=MCI USA|website=www.mci-group.com|access-date=2016-05-31}}</ref>
