'''Mario Sossi''' (6 February 1932 – 6 December 2019) was an [[Italian people|Italian]] magistrate and politician.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ilsecoloxix.it/genova/2019/12/06/news/e-morto-mario-sossi-ex-magistrato-vittima-di-un-sequestro-delle-brigate-rosse-nel-1974-1.38101541|title=Morto Mario Sossi, ex magistrato vittima di un sequestro delle Br nel 1974|date=6 December 2019|work=Il Secolo XIX|language=Italian}}</ref> He was a prosecutor against members of the [[October 22 Group]], and was captured by members of the [[Red Brigades]] on 18 April 1974 in [[Genoa]]. He was released on 22 May of the same year following a prisoner swap with the Italian government.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ina.fr/video/CAF92020824|title=JT 20H : émission du 20 mai 1974|date=20 May 1974|work=ina.fr|language=French}}</ref>
