{{Diving at the 2006 Commonwealth Games}}
The '''women's 3 metre springboard''' was part of the [[Diving at the 2006 Commonwealth Games]] program. The competition was held on 25 March 2006 at [[Melbourne Sports and Aquatic Centre]] in [[Melbourne]], [[Australia]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://m2006.thecgf.com/Sports+and+Venues/Venue+Locations/By+Venue/Melbourne+Sports+and+Aquatic+Centre+%28MSAC%29/|title=Melbourne Sports and Aquatic Centre|website=m2006.thecgf.com/|publisher=Melbourne 2006 Commonwealth Games|accessdate=22 November 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://m2006.thecgf.com/Schedule+and+Results/|title=Schedule|website=m2006.thecgf.com/|publisher=Melbourne 2006 Commonwealth Games|accessdate=22 November 2019}}</ref>

==Format==
The competition was held in two rounds:
* Preliminary round: All 15 divers perform six dives, and the top 12 proceed to the final.
* Final: The 12 divers perform six dives; these are added onto the preliminary round scores and the top three divers win the gold, silver and bronze medals accordingly.
