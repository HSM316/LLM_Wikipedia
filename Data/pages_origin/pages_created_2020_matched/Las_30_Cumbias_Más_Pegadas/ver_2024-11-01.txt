'''''Las 30 Cumbias Más Pegadas''''' ({{langx|en|The 30 Best Cumbias}}) is the title of a [[compilation album]], which features music from [[Los Angeles Azules]], Los Askis, Rayito Colombiano, Grupo Latino, Grupo Maracuya, Los Llayras, Mr. Chivo, Aniceto Molina, Super Grupo G, La Tropa Vallenata, Los Vallenatos, Yahari, among others. This album peaked at number-one in the [[Billboard Top Latin Albums|''Billboard'' Top Latin Albums]] chart for one week.
