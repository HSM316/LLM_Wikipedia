'''Maria Fátima Barone Mora''' (born 17 September 1999), known as '''Fátima Barone''', is a Uruguayan [[women's association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Centre-back|centre back]] for [[Montevideo Wanderers Futbol Club]] and the [[Uruguay women's national football team|Uruguay women's national team]]. She has previously played [[field hockey]] in Argentina.<ref name=CNF/>

==International career==
Barone made her senior debut for Uruguay on 6 October 2019.

==Personal life==
Barone's father, [[Deivis Barone]], is a former footballer.<ref name=CNF/> Her brother, [[Faustino Barone]], is also a footballer, and currently plays for [[Club Atlético River Plate (Montevideo)|River Plate Montevideo]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elobservador.com.uy/nota/el-premonitorio-chat-de-fatima-barone-con-su-hermano-faustino-antes-de-sus-goles-a-penarol-20236198165|title="Hacele un gol a Peñarol y...": El premonitorio chat de Fátima Barone con su hermano Faustino antes de sus goles a Peñarol|trans-title="Score a goal against Peñarol and...": Fátima Barone's premonitory chat with her brother Faustino before their goals against Peñarol|date=19 June 2023|access-date=14 July 2023|website=elobservador.com.uy|language=es}}</ref>
