'''Emile Paul Tendeng''' (born 9 March 1992 in [[Senegal]]) is a [[Senegal]]ese professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as an attacking midfielder for [[IFK Mariehamn]].<ref>http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.paul-emile.tendeng.105720.en.html</ref><ref>http://www.ifkfotboll.ax/nyheter/tendeng-forstarker-ifk-mariehamn</ref>

==Career==
The assistant coach of [[Casa Sport]] describes him as “football car crash, [[Child prodigy|prodigy]] and player with extraordinary potential”, and compares him with [[Iain Dowie]] and a young [[Jonny Hansen (footballer)]]<ref>http://fr.allafrica.com/stories/201003240870.html</ref>

In 2013, Tendeng moved to [[FC Vaslui]] on loan from [[Casa Sport]] for six-months.<ref>{{cite web|title=Paul Emile Tendeng prêté au club roumain FC Vaslui|url=http://www.rts.sn/articles-de-presse/sports/football/paul-emile-tendeng-prete-au-club-roumain-fc-vaslui.html|website=rts.sn|publisher=Radiodiffusion Télévision Sénégalaise|accessdate=19 April 2016|language=French}}{{Dead link|date=December 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

In February 2016, Tendeng signed a two-year contract with Finnish club [[FC Ilves]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Emile Paul Tendeng ja Ilves kaksivuotiseen sopimukseen|url=http://ilvesedustus.fi/emile-paul-tendeng-ja-ilves-kaksivuotiseen-sopimukseen/|website=ilvesedustus.fi|publisher=FC Ilves|accessdate=19 April 2016|language=Finnish|date=7 February 2016}}</ref>

On 30 October 2017, Tendeng signed a one-year contract with [[Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho]], with the option of an additional year.<ref name="Ayarna & Tendeng Sign">{{cite web|title=Reuben Ayarna ja Emile Paul Tendeng SJK-paitaan|url=http://www.sjk2007.fi/w/reuben-ayarna-ja-emile-paul-tendeng-sjk-paitaan|website=sjk2007.fi|publisher=Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho|accessdate=30 October 2017|language=Finnish|date=30 October 2017}}</ref>
