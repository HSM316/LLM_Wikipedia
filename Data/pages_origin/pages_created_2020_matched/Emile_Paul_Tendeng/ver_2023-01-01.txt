'''Emile Paul Tendeng''' (born 31 December  1992) is a [[Senegal]]ese professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as an attacking midfielder for Finnish club [[FF Jaro|Jaro]].<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.paul-emile.tendeng.105720.en.html| title = - Stats and titles won - 2021}} </ref>

==Career==
The assistant coach of [[Casa Sport]] described him as "football car crash, [[Child prodigy|prodigy]] and player with extraordinary potential", and compared him with [[Iain Dowie]].<ref>{{cite web| url = http://fr.allafrica.com/stories/201003240870.html| title = Sénégal: Emile Paul Tendeng (Nouveau métronome du Casa Sports) : Le jeune prodige qui fait oublier Alpha Oumar Sow - allAfrica.com}}</ref>

In 2013, Tendeng moved to [[FC Vaslui]] on loan from [[Casa Sport]] for six-months.<ref>{{cite web|title=Paul Emile Tendeng prêté au club roumain FC Vaslui|url=http://www.rts.sn/articles-de-presse/sports/football/paul-emile-tendeng-prete-au-club-roumain-fc-vaslui.html|website=rts.sn|publisher=Radiodiffusion Télévision Sénégalaise|accessdate=19 April 2016|language=French}}{{Dead link|date=December 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

In February 2016, Tendeng signed a two-year contract with Finnish club [[Ilves (football)|Ilves]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Emile Paul Tendeng ja Ilves kaksivuotiseen sopimukseen|url=http://ilvesedustus.fi/emile-paul-tendeng-ja-ilves-kaksivuotiseen-sopimukseen/|website=ilvesedustus.fi|publisher=FC Ilves|accessdate=19 April 2016|language=Finnish|date=7 February 2016}}</ref>

On 30 October 2017, Tendeng signed a one-year contract with [[Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho|SJK]], with the option of an additional year.<ref name="Ayarna & Tendeng Sign">{{cite web|title=Reuben Ayarna ja Emile Paul Tendeng SJK-paitaan|url=http://www.sjk2007.fi/w/reuben-ayarna-ja-emile-paul-tendeng-sjk-paitaan|website=sjk2007.fi|publisher=Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho|accessdate=30 October 2017|language=Finnish|date=30 October 2017}}</ref>

For the 2021 season, he joined [[IFK Mariehamn|Mariehamn]].<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.ifkfotboll.ax/nyheter/tendeng-forstarker-ifk-mariehamn| title = Tendeng förstärker IFK Mariehamn {{!}} IFK Mariehamn Fotboll}}</ref>

On 29 January 2022, he signed with [[FF Jaro|Jaro]] for the 2022 season.<ref>{{cite press release|publisher=[[FF Jaro|Jaro]]|url=http://www.ffjaro.fi/fi/uutiset/emile-paul-tendeng-jaroon|title=EMILE PAUL TENDENG JAROON|date=29 January 2022|access-date=29 January 2022|language=fi}}</ref>
