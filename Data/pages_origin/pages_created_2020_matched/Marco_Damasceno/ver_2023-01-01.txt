'''Marco Gabriel Damasceno Alves''' (born 11 April 1996), known as '''Marco Damasceno''', is a Brazilian [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as an [[Midfielder#Attacking midfielder|attacking midfielder]] for [[Associação Atlética Caldense|Caldense]].

==Club career==
Born in [[Brasília]], [[Federal District (Brazil)|Distrito Federal]], Damasceno joined [[Clube Atlético Paranaense|Atlético Paranaense]]'s youth setup in 2011, after representing a [[Santos FC|Santos]] youth school in his hometown. He also had trials at [[S.C. Braga|Braga]] and [[Real Madrid C.F.|Real Madrid]] in 2010, but nothing came of it.<ref>[http://globoesporte.globo.com/futebol/times/atletico-pr/noticia/2014/01/joia-2014-marco-damasceno-supera-drama-e-brilha-na-base-do-atletico-pr.html Joia 2014: Marco Damasceno supera drama e brilha na base do Atlético-PR (Pearls 2014: Marco Damasceno overcomes drama and shines in Atlético-PR's youth setup)]; Globo Esporte, 3 January 2014 {{in lang|pt}}</ref>

Damasceno made his first team – and [[Campeonato Brasileiro Série A|Série A]] – debut on 20 September 2014, coming on as a second-half [[Substitute (association football)|substitute]] for fellow youth graduate [[Marcos Guilherme]] in a 0–1 home loss against [[Sport Club Internacional|Internacional]].<ref>[http://globoesporte.globo.com/jogo/brasileirao2014/20-09-2014/atletico-pr-internacional.html Rafael Moura quebra jejum, e Inter vence o Atlético-PR na Baixada (Rafael Moura ends goal drought, and Inter defeats Atlético-PR at ''Baixada'')]; Globo Esporte, 20 September 2014 {{in lang|pt}}</ref>
