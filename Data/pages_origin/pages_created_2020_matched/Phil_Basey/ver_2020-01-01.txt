'''Philip John Basey''' (born 27 August 1948) is a retired Welsh professional [[association football|football]] [[left winger]] who played in the [[the Football League|Football League]] for [[Brentford F.C.|Brentford]].

== Playing career ==
Basey began his career in the [[Brentford F.C. Reserves and Academy#Youth Team|youth team]] at [[Football League Fourth Division|Fourth Division]] club Brentford,<ref name="Timeless">{{Cite book|title=Timeless Bees: Brentford F.C. Who's Who 1920–2006|last=Haynes|first=Graham|last2=Coumbe|first2=Frank|publisher=Yore Publications|year=2006|isbn=978-0955294914|location=Harefield|page=16}}</ref> after writing to the club requesting a trial.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Basey To Stand By |date=5 August 1966 |work=The Brentford & Chiswick Times}}</ref> He progressed to sign a professional contract in July 1966.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Their Big Chance |date=8 July 1966 |work=The Brentford & Chiswick Times}}</ref> At age 18, he made his professional debut in a league match versus [[Tranmere Rovers F.C.|Tranmere Rovers]] on 24 September 1966 and played the full 90 minutes of the 1–1 draw.<ref name="White">{{Cite book |title=100 Years Of Brentford |publisher=Brentford FC |year=1989 |isbn=0951526200 |editor-last=White |editor-first=Eric |page=389}}</ref> His second and final appearance for the club came three days later, in a 2–2 draw with [[Lincoln City F.C.|Lincoln City]].<ref name="White" /> After his release at the end of the [[1966–67 Brentford F.C. season|1966–67]] season,<ref name="Timeless" /> Basey dropped into [[Non-League football|non-league football]] and played for [[Gloucester City A.F.C.|Gloucester City]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tigerroar.co.uk/philbasey.php|title=Tiger Roar – Phil Basey|publisher=|accessdate=18 March 2016}}</ref> [[Crawley Town F.C.|Crawley Town]], [[Maidstone United F.C. (1897)|Maidstone United]], [[Hillingdon Borough F.C.|Hillingdon Borough]], [[Tooting & Mitcham United F.C.|Tooting & Mitcham United]] and [[Bromley F.C.|Bromley]].<ref name="tmufc">{{Cite web |url=http://www.tmu-fc.co.uk/history/season/1970/78-79.htm |title=Tooting & Mitcham United FC Archive – Season 1978–79 |publisher=Tmufc.co.uk |access-date=25 July 2014}}</ref>
