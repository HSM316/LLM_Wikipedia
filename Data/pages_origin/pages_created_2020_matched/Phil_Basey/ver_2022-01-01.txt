'''Philip John Basey''' (born 27 August 1948) is a Welsh retired professional [[association football|footballer]] who played as a [[Midfielder#Winger|left winger]] in the [[the Football League|Football League]] for [[Brentford F.C.|Brentford]]. After his release in 1967, he played for over a decade in [[non-League football]] and notably scored over 100 career goals for [[Southern Football League|Southern League]] club [[Crawley Town F.C.|Crawley Town]].

== Playing career ==
Basey began his career in the [[Brentford F.C. Reserves and Academy#Youth Team|youth team]] at [[Football League Fourth Division|Fourth Division]] club Brentford,<ref name="Timeless">{{Cite book |last=Haynes |first=Graham |title=Timeless Bees: Brentford F.C.
