'''Heather Oesterle''' is the current head coach of [[Central Michigan Chippewas women's basketball|Central Michigan]] women's basketball team.<ref>{{cite web |title=HEATHER OESTERLE |url=https://cmuchippewas.com/coaches.aspx?rc=43 |website=CMUChippewas.com |publisher=Central Michigan University |accessdate=31 July 2019}}</ref> Oesterle was named CMU's head women's basketball coach in July, 2019, succeeding her long-time mentor [[Sue Guevara]]. Oesterle served for nine seasons on Guevara's staff at CMU, helping lead the program to unprecedented heights including three Mid-American Conference championships, two MAC Tournament titles, and five MAC West Division crowns. She earned her bachelor's degree in kinesiology from Michigan in 2002 and her master's degree in sports studies from Miami in 2008.

==Playing career==
She played four years under head coach Sue Guevara at [[Michigan Wolverines women's basketball|Michigan]] from 1998 to 2002.<ref name = "CMU">{{cite web |title=Heather Oesterle Named Women's Basketball Head Coach |url=https://cmuchippewas.com/news/2019/7/12/heather-oesterle-named-womens-basketball-head-coach.aspx?path=wbball |website=CMUChippewas.com |publisher=Central Michigan University |accessdate=31 July 2019}}</ref>

==Coaching career==
Early in her coaching career she was an assistant at [[Stanford Cardinal women's basketball|Stanford]] before coming to the [[Mid-American Conference|MAC]] as assistant at [[Miami RedHawks women's basketball|Miami (OH)]] and [[Northern Illinois Huskies women's basketball|Northern Illinois]]. She joined Guevara's staff at [[Central Michigan Chippewas women's basketball|Central Michigan]] in 2012 where she remained until Guevara's retirement.<ref name = "CMU" />
