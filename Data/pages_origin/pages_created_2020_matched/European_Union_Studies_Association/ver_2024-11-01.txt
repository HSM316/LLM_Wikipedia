The '''European Union Studies Association''' (EUSA), founded 1988, is a scholarly and professional association with its focus on the European Union, the processes of its integration, and its transatlantic relations.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.eustudies.org/ |title=Home |website=eustudies.org}}</ref>

It notes valuable contributions to European Studies via various prizes including the EUSA Award for Lifetime Achievement in European Studies.
