'''Héctor Hugo Roblero Gordillo''' (born 28 April 1948) is a [[Mexican people|Mexican]] politician affiliated with the [[Labor Party (Mexico)|Labor Party]]. As of 2013 he served as Deputy of the [[LXII Legislature of the Mexican Congress]] representing [[Chiapas]], and previously served in the LX and LXII Legislatures of the [[Congress of Chiapas]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Perfil del legislador|url=http://sil.gobernacion.gob.mx/Librerias/pp_PerfilLegislador.php?SID=&Referencia=9217094|publisher=Legislative Information System|accessdate=4 December 2013|language=es}}</ref>
