The '''Tallahassee Marathon''' is a marathon located in [[Tallahassee]], the state capital of [[Florida]]. The 2016 edition of the race was held on February 7, 2016.

Established in 1975, the race has featured 43 editions.

This race is a [[USATF]] certified course and an official qualifier for the [[Boston Marathon]].

== Course ==

The race starts in Downtown, moves through the Midtown district and through [[Myers Park (Tallahassee)|Myers Park]]. Later, the course passes [[Cascade Park (Tallahassee)|Cascade Park]] and the [[Florida State University]] campus.
