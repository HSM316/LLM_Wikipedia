'''Judith C.M. Swinkels''' (born 4 June 1961) is a Dutch politician, she was a member of the [[House of Representatives (Netherlands)|House of Representatives of the Netherlands]] for the [[Democrats 66]] from 3 November 2015 to 23 March 2017. Previously she was a lawyer and judge.

==Career==
Swinkels was born on 4 June 1961 in [[Tilburg]]. She went to the [[Gymnasium (school)|gymnasium]] in the same city between 1973 and 1979. She subsequently studied civil and European law at [[Leiden University]] and graduated in 1984.<ref name=parlement>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.parlement.com/id/viehieo4razn/j_c_m_judith_swinkels |title=Mr. J.C.M. (Judith) Swinkels |language=Dutch |publisher=Parlement.com |date= |accessdate=7 April 2017}}</ref> Swinkels was occupied as a lawyer between 1985 and 1999. Between 1999 and 2002 she worked for the [[Netherlands Competition Authority]]. In 2002 Swinkels was appointed as judge in [[Haarlem]]. The first five years she was judge for civil and trade law. In 2007 this became civil and family and youth law. In 2011 she switched to civil and criminal law.<ref name=parlement/>

In the [[2012 Dutch general election|Parliamentary elections of 2012]] Swinkels occupied number 14 on the Democrats 66 party list and was not elected as the party gained 12 seats.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.tweedekamer.nl/nieuws/kamernieuws/judith-swinkels-nieuw-d66-kamerlid |title=Judith Swinkels nieuw D66-Kamerlid |language=Dutch |publisher=Tweede Kamer |date=3 November 2015 |accessdate=10 October 2015}}</ref>

On 3 November 2015 Swinkels joined the [[House of Representatives (Netherlands)|House of Representatives of the Netherlands]] as replacement of [[Magda Berndsen]]. She took up the security and justice portfolio which Berndsen previously held.<ref>{{cite web|author=Tom Reijner |url=http://www.elsevier.nl/Nederland/achtergrond/2015/11/Rechter-Judith-Swinkels-D66-neemt-plaats-in-Tweede-Kamer-2712291W/ |title=Rechter Judith Swinkels (D66) neemt plaats in Tweede Kamer |language=Dutch |publisher=Elsevier.nl |date=3 November 2015 |accessdate=4 December 2015}}</ref> Swinkels had resigned as a judge on 1 November.<ref name=parlement/> Her term in the House ended on 23 March 2017.<ref name=parlement/>
