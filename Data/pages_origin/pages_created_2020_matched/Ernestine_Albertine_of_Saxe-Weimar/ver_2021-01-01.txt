'''Ernestine Albertine, Countess of Lippe-Alverdissen''' (née '''Princess Ernestine Albertine of Saxe-Weimar'''; 28 December 1722 – 25 November 1769) was the first wife and consort of [[Philip II, Count of Schaumburg-Lippe|Philip II, Count of Lippe-Alverdissen]].

== Biography ==
Princess Ernestine Albertine of Saxe-Weimar was born in [[Weimar]] on 28 December 1722 to [[Ernest Augustus I, Duke of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach]] and [[Eleonore Wilhelmine of Anhalt-Köthen|Princess Eleonore Wilhelmine of Anhalt-Köthen]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.genealogy.euweb.cz/wettin/wettin4.html#EA1|title=Wettin 4|website=www.genealogy.euweb.cz}}</ref>

On 6 May 1756 she married [[Philip II, Count of Schaumburg-Lippe|Philip II, Count of Lippe-Alverdissen]], becoming the Countess consort of [[Lippe-Alverdissen]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.genealogy.euweb.cz/lippe/lippe8.html|title=Lippe 8|website=www.genealogy.euweb.cz}}</ref> They had four children:

*Count Clemens August (1757-1757)
*Count Karl Wilhelm (1759-1780)
*Count Georg Karl (1760-1776)
*Countess Friederike Antoinette (1762-1777)

Ernestine Albertine died on 25 November 1769 in Alverdissen, seven years before her husband succeeded his cousin, [[William, Count of Schaumburg-Lippe|Wilhelm]], as the Count of [[Principality of Schaumburg-Lippe|Schaumburg-Lippe]].
