'''Klebber Queiroz Toledo'''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://f5.folha.uol.com.br/celebridades/2018/06/agora-casados-klebber-toledo-e-camila-queiroz-decidem-adotar-nomes-um-do-outro.shtml |title=Agora casados, Klebber Toledo e Camila Queiroz decidem adotar nomes um do outro |date=17 June 2018 |access-date=1 September 2018 |publisher=[[Folha de São Paulo]]|language= pt }}</ref> (born June 14, 1986) is a Brazilian actor.<ref name="Ref1">{{cite web|url=http://caras.uol.com.br/perfil/klebber-toledo |title=Klebber Toledo Profile on Caras |language=Portuguese |accessdate=2013-05-20 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130603193403/http://caras.uol.com.br/perfil/klebber-toledo |archivedate=2013-06-03 }}</ref>

==Career==
Toledo left home at the age of 15, playing volleyball at teams in [[São Paulo]] for two years,<ref name="extra - Gay em ‘Império">{{cite web |url=http://extra.globo.com/tv-e-lazer/gay-em-imperio-klebber-toledo-afirma-nao-tenho-curiosidade-por-homens-13993018.html |title='Gay em 'Império', Klebber Toledo afirma: 'Não tenho curiosidade por homens' |date=21 September 2014 |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=globo.com }}</ref> as well as working as a waiter, party monitor and modeling. At the age of 20, he went to [[Rio de Janeiro]] to participate in the [[Rede Globo]] Actors Workshop.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.saradosdobrasil.com/2011/05/nao-faco-dieta-genetica-e-boa-diz.html |title="Não faço dieta. A genética é boa" diz Klebber Toledo sobre a boa forma |date=23 May 2011 |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=Sarados do Brasil }}</ref>

In November 2006, with the end of the Rede Globo workshop, he made tests for the cast of ''[[Malhação]]'', which would debut a new phase in January 2007. He passed the tests, winning the role of Mateus Molina in the plot.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://gshow.globo.com/programas/video-show/v2011/VideoShow/Noticias/0,,MUL1645204-16952,00-KLEBBER+TOLEDO+GRAVA+A+PROXIMA+NOVELA+DAS+SETE+NA+PRAIA+DE+IPANEMA+NO+RIO.html |title=Klebber Toledo grava a próxima novela das sete na praia de Ipanema, no Rio |date=1 February 2011 |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=[[globo.com]] }}</ref> His character was part of a troubled love pentagon. In 2009 he was invited to be assistant director and producer of the show that tells the life of singer Isaurinha Garcia, the musical ''Isaurinha - Samba, Jazz & Bossa Nova'' of Júlio Fischer.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://extra.globo.com/tv-e-lazer/telinha/klebber-toledo-sera-assistente-de-direcao-em-espetaculo-sobre-vida-da-cantora-isaurinha-garcia-18444716.html |title=Klebber Toledo será assistente de direção em espetáculo sobre a vida da cantora Isaurinha Garcia |date=11 January 2016 |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=Extra }}</ref> In the same year, he dubbed the character Noah Curtis in the movie ''[[2012 (film)|2012]]''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.purepeople.com.br/famosos/klebber-toledo_p2832 |title=Klebber Toledo no Purepeople - Biografia, notícias e todas as fotos! |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=Pire People }}</ref>

He returns to [[Rede Globo]] in November 2009, where he participated in the final chapters of the soap opera ''[[Caras & Bocas]]'' as Sid, a homosexual.<ref name="globo - gay em Caras e Bocas">{{cite web |url=http://gshow.globo.com/programas/video-show/Bau-TV/noticia/2014/08/antes-e-depois-relembre-klebber-toledo-como-estilista-gay-em-caras-bocas.html |title=Antes e depois: relembre Klebber Toledo como estilista gay em Caras & Bocas |date=13 August 2014 |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=[[globo.com]] }}</ref> Due to his performance in the previous novel, in 2011 the author [[Walcyr Carrasco]] scale him to a character fixed in ''[[Morde & Assopra]]'', playing the villain Guilherme.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://televisao.uol.com.br/novelas/morde-e-assopra/2011/09/11/levo-licao-de-moral-ate-hoje-conta-klebber-sobre-seu-personagem-guilherme-de-morde-e-assopra.jhtm |title="Levo lição de moral até hoje", conta Klebber Toledo sobre seu personagem Guilherme, de "Morde e Assopra"  |date=11 September 2011 |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=UOL }}</ref>

In 2012, he joined the cast of ''[[A Vida da Gente]]'', playing João.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gshow.globo.com/novelas/a-vida-da-gente/Bastidores/noticia/2012/02/klebber-toledo-entra-em-vida-da-gente-para-viver-namorado-de-alice.html|title=Klebber Toledo entra em A Vida da Gente para viver namorado de Alice|author=Gshow|date=2 February 2012|publisher=Bastidores - A Vida da Gente|access-date=30 April 2014}}</ref> In the same year, the actor enters the soap opera ''[[Lado a Lado]]'', playing Umberto, a playboy who likes older women.<ref name="klebber,rafael,daniel e caio">{{cite web|url=http://gshow.globo.com/novelas/lado-a-lado/Bastidores/noticia/2012/08/klebber-toledo-rafael-cardoso-daniel-dalcin-e-caio-blat-serao-amigos-de-farra.html|title=Klebber Toledo, Rafael Cardoso, Daniel Dancin e Caio Blat serão amigos de farra|author=Gshow|date=25 August 2012|publisher=Bastidores - Lado a Lado|access-date=25 December 2012}}</ref> In 2013, he participated in the 10th edition of the ''[[Dança dos Famosos]]'', being in 4th place.

In 2014, he is cast for ''Falso Brilhante'', being initially quoted to play Roberto (but [[Rômulo Neto]] was the one who got the character), brother of Maria Ísis ([[Marina Ruy Barbosa]]). However, due to the changes the novel happened to be called ''[[Império]]'' and Klebber made a character that maintains a case with the personage of Jose Mayer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://televisao.uol.com.br/colunas/flavio-ricco/2014/04/12/alvo-do-pegador-jose-mayer-em-nova-novela-sera-klebber-toledo.htm|title=Alvo do "pegador" José Mayer em nova novela será Klebber Toledo|last=Ricco|first=Flávio|date=12 April 2014|publisher=[[Universo Online|UOL Televisão]]|access-date=12 April 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://extra.globo.com/tv-e-lazer/telinha/falso-brilhante-klebber-toledo-sera-irmao-malandro-de-marina-ruy-barbosa-12069023.html|title='Falso brilhante': Klebber Toledo será o irmão malandro de Marina Ruy Barbosa|last=Bittencourt|first=Carla|date=3 April 2014|publisher=[[Extra (jornal do Rio de Janeiro)|Extra]]|access-date=3 April 2014}}</ref>

In August 2014, Toledo began to record ''Ato Falho'', a Gshow web series, [[Rede Globo]]'s website, which was launched on September 10, 2014.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://gshow.globo.com/programas/webseries/O-Programa/noticia/2014/09/confira-tudo-o-que-rolou-no-chat-com-klebber-toledo-em-ato-falho-no-ar.html |title=Confira tudo o que rolou no chat com Klebber Toledo em Ato Falho No Ar |date=24 September 2014 |access-date=26 August 2016 |publisher=globo.com }}</ref>

In 2015 he participated in the Brazilian comedy film ''[[Vai Que Cola - O Filme|Vai Que Cola]]'', which premiered on October 1, playing himself. Also in 2015 he appeared with the play "Where Are You Now" playing Gabriel, Pedro's best friend.

The following year, he was cast in the soap opera ''[[Êta Mundo Bom!]]'' playing Romeu.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ego.globo.com/televisao/noticia/2015/10/camila-queiroz-ja-se-prepara-para-sua-proxima-novela-eta-mundo-bom.html|title=Camila Queiroz já se prepara para sua próxima novela, 'Êta Mundo Bom'|first=Anderson|last=Dezan|publisher=[[Ego (site)|Ego]]|access-date=20 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.purepeople.com.br/noticia/camila-queiroz-e-klebber-toledo-gravam-juntos-primeiras-cenas-de-eta-mundo-bom_a82423/1|title=Novela das 18h de Walcyr Carrasco inicia gravações |author=Mariana Mastrangelo|date=26 October 2015|publisher=PurePeople|access-date=26 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://kogut.oglobo.globo.com/noticias-da-tv/coluna/noticia/2015/09/novela-de-walcyr-carrasco-se-chamara-eta-mundo-bom.html|title=Após o sucesso de 'Verdades Secretas', Walcyr Carrasco revela título de seu próximo folhetim, "Êta Mundo Bom", a nova novela das 6h|author=Patrícia Kogut O Globo|date=26 October 2015|publisher=Patrícia Kogut|access-date=26 October 2015}}</ref> In 2017, he participated in the first phase of the series ''A Fórmula'', interpreting the protagonist Ricardo Montenegro.

== Personal life ==
Klebber is the son of Maria Aparecida da Silva and José Roberto Toledo and has an older brother named Kristhiano.

During the recordings of the novel [[Morde & Assopra]], in 2011, he began to date the actress [[Marina Ruy Barbosa]], that was his romantic pair in the novel. In August 2014, two weeks after the debut of the novel [[Império]], in which both acted, was announced the end of the dating.

In August of 2016 he took over the relationship with the actress [[Camila Queiroz]], with whom he starred in the telenovela ''[[Êta Mundo Bom!]]''. They became engaged in June 2017. On June 16, 2018, they celebrated the [[civil wedding]] in [[Ribeirão Preto]]. His wife went on to sign his surname, Toledo, and Klebber went on to sign the surname of his wife, Queiroz.<ref>https://f5.folha.uol.com.br/celebridades/2018/06/agora-casados-klebber-toledo-e-camila-queiroz-decidem-adotar-nomes-um-do-outro.shtml</ref>
