'''Amine Khammas''' (born 6 April 1999) is a professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Full back (association football)|left back]] for Belgian side [[Waasland-Beveren]].<ref name="soccerway">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/amine-khammas/489028/|title=Belgium - A. Khammas - Profile with news, career statistics and history - Soccerway|publisher=soccerway.com|accessdate=2017-07-31}}</ref><ref name = "WF">{{Worldfootball.net|amine-khammas}}</ref>

== Career ==
Khammas played with [[K.R.C. Genk|Genk]] as a junior. He made his first team debut with [[K.R.C. Genk]] in the [[Belgian First Division A|Belgian Pro League]] on 29 July 2017 against [[Waasland-Beveren]].<ref name="soccerway2">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2017/07/29/belgium/pro-league/krc-genk/kv-red-star-waasland/2461869/|title=Genk vs. Waasland-Beveren - 29 July 2017 - Soccerway|publisher=soccerway.com|accessdate=2017-07-31}}</ref>

==International career==
Khammas was a youth international for Belgium at the U17 level.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://static.belgianfootball.be/project/publiek/jrinteren/speler_PH_6674.htm#itop|title=Amine Khammas|first=KBVB -|last=URBSFA|website=static.belgianfootball.be}}</ref> He later represented the [[Morocco national under-20 football team|Morocco U18]]s in a pair of friendlies against [[Portugal national under-18 football team]] in January 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.frmf.ma/fr/article/equipe-nationale-u18-%E2%80%93-stage-de-pr%C3%A9paration-et-match-amicaux-face-au-portugal|title=Equipe Nationale U18 – Stage de préparation et match amicaux face au Portugal - الموقع الرسمي للجامعة الملكية المغربية لكرة القدم|website=www.frmf.ma}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://mountakhab.net/www/modules/article_une/index.php?storyid=471679|title=A. Khammas : " Être présent en équipe nationale du Maroc, c'est quelque chose d'indescriptible "|first=Achraf|last=Bakkali|website=Mountakhab.net|access-date=2017-08-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170827051020/http://mountakhab.net/www/modules/article_une/index.php?storyid=471679|archive-date=2017-08-27|url-status=dead}}</ref> He received a call-up to the senior [[Morocco national football team]] in August 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Maroc-herve-renard-convoque-benatia-ziyech-et-alakouch/827651|title=Maroc : Hervé Renard convoque Benatia, Ziyech et Alakouch|first=P.|last=Pr.|publisher=}}</ref>
