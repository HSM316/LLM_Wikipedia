'''Diogo António Cupido Gonçalves''' (born 6 February 1997) is a Portuguese professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as either a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] or a [[Forward (association football)#Winger|winger]] for [[F.C. Famalicão|Famalicão]], on loan from [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]],<ref>{{cite news|date=26 June 2019|title=Três processos resolvidos|trans-title=Three dossiers closed|url=https://www.slbenfica.pt/pt-pt/agora/noticias/2018-2019/06/26/futebol-benfica-diogo-goncalves-guga-e-igor-rodrigues-colocados|language=pt|work=S.L. Benfica|access-date=27 June 2019}}</ref> and the [[Portugal national under-21 football team|Portugal under-21 team]].

==Club career==
Born in [[Almodôvar]], Gonçalves started his career at local club CD Almodôvar in 2005. Two years later, he joined FC Ferreiras, before moving to [[S.L. Benfica]]'s [[youth system]] in 2008.

After being promoted from the [[S.L. Benfica Juniors|youth team]], Gonçalves debuted for the reserve team, [[S.L. Benfica B|Benfica B]], as a substitute, playing 20 minutes in a [[Segunda Liga]] 4–1 home win over [[U.D. Oliveirense|Oliveirense]] on 14 February 2015.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ligaportugal.pt/oou/jogo/20142015/segundaliga/27/7 |title=Benfica B - Oliveirense (Jornada 27 Segunda Liga 2014-2015) - Liga Portugal |language=Portuguese |date=14 February 2015 |publisher=[[LPFP]] |access-date=14 February 2015}}</ref> On 24 May, he scored his first goal for Benfica B in a 2–1 home win over [[Vitória de Guimarães B]]. Five days later, he scored the only goal of a friendly held for the opening of [[F.C. United of Manchester]]'s [[Broadhurst Park]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.mirror.co.uk/sport/football/news/fc-united-manchester-open-new-5788488 |title=FC United of Manchester open new stadium against Benfica... and show what football fans can achieve |last1=Flanagan |first1=Aaron |website=Mirror Online |accessdate=31 May 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/match-reports/fc-united-0-benfica-1-9358282 |title=FC United 0 Benfica 1: Match report of official opening of new Broadhurst Park stadium |author=Ben Collins |date=29 May 2015 |publisher=Manchester Evening News |accessdate=31 May 2015}}</ref>

On 9 August 2017, Gonçalves debuted for Benfica's senior team in a 3–1 victory over [[S.C. Braga|Braga]] in the [[Primeira Liga]].<ref name=sb>{{Soccerbase|106603}}</ref> Later on, he made his [[UEFA Champions League]] debut in a 1–0 home loss to [[Manchester United]] on 18 October.<ref name=sb/>

On 14 June 2018, Gonçalves joined [[EFL Championship]] club [[Nottingham Forest F.C.|Nottingham Forest]] on loan for the [[2018–19 EFL Championship|2018–19 season]] with the option of making the transfer permanent at the end of the season.<ref>{{cite news|date=14 June 2018|title=Forest agree double signing|url=https://www.nottinghamforest.co.uk/news/2018/june/forest-agree-double-signing/|work=Nottingham Forest|access-date=14 June 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|date=14 June 2018|title=Diogo Gonçalves on loan to Nottingham Forest|url=https://www.slbenfica.pt/en-us/agora/noticias/2017_2018/06/14/futebol-benfica-diogo-goncalves-emprestado-ao-nottingham-forest|work=S.L. Benfica|access-date=25 July 2018}}</ref>

==International career==
Gonçalves has represented Portugal at various levels up to [[Portugal national under-21 football team|Portugal U21]]s level.
