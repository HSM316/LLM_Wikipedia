'''Diogo António Cupido Gonçalves''' ({{IPA|pt|diˈoɣu ɣõˈsalvɨʃ}}; born 6 February 1997) is a Portuguese professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[winger (association football)|winger]] for [[Major League Soccer]] club [[Real Salt Lake]].

==Club career==
===Benfica===
Born in [[Almodôvar]], Gonçalves started his career at local club CD Almodôvar in 2005. Two years later, he joined [[F.C. Ferreiras|Ferreiras]], before moving to [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]]'s [[S.L. Benfica (youth)|youth system]] in 2008.{{Citation needed|date=September 2022}}

After being promoted from the [[S.L. Benfica Juniors|youth team]], Gonçalves debuted for the reserve team, [[S.L. Benfica B|Benfica B]], as a substitute, playing 20 minutes in a [[Segunda Liga]] 4–1 home win over [[U.D. Oliveirense|Oliveirense]] on 14 February 2015.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ligaportugal.pt/oou/jogo/20142015/segundaliga/27/7 |title=Benfica B - Oliveirense (Jornada 27 Segunda Liga 2014-2015) - Liga Portugal |language=Portuguese |date=14 February 2015 |publisher=[[LPFP]] |access-date=14 February 2015}}</ref> On 24 May, he scored his first goal for Benfica B in a 2–1 home win over [[Vitória S.C. B|Vitória de Guimarães B]]. Five days later, he scored the only goal of a friendly held for the opening of [[F.C. United of Manchester|FC United of Manchester]]'s [[Broadhurst Park]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.mirror.co.uk/sport/football/news/fc-united-manchester-open-new-5788488 |title=FC United of Manchester open new stadium against Benfica... and show what football fans can achieve |last1=Flanagan |first1=Aaron |website=Mirror Online |accessdate=31 May 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/match-reports/fc-united-0-benfica-1-9358282 |title=FC United 0 Benfica 1: Match report of official opening of new Broadhurst Park stadium |author=Ben Collins |date=29 May 2015 |publisher=Manchester Evening News |accessdate=31 May 2015}}</ref>

On 9 August 2017, Gonçalves debuted for Benfica's senior team in a 3–1 victory over [[S.C. Braga|Braga]] in the [[Primeira Liga]].<ref name=sb>{{Soccerbase|106603}}</ref> Later on, he made his [[UEFA Champions League]] debut in a 1–0 home loss to [[Manchester United]] on 18 October.<ref name=sb/>

====Nottingham Forest (loan)====
On 14 June 2018, Gonçalves joined [[EFL Championship]] club [[Nottingham Forest F.C.|Nottingham Forest]] on loan for the [[2018–19 EFL Championship|2018–19 season]] with the option of making the transfer permanent at the end of the season.<ref>{{cite news|date=14 June 2018|title=Forest agree double signing|url=https://www.nottinghamforest.co.uk/news/2018/june/forest-agree-double-signing/|work=Nottingham Forest|access-date=14 June 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|date=14 June 2018|title=Diogo Gonçalves on loan to Nottingham Forest|url=https://www.slbenfica.pt/en-us/agora/noticias/2017_2018/06/14/futebol-benfica-diogo-goncalves-emprestado-ao-nottingham-forest|work=S.L. Benfica|access-date=25 July 2018}}</ref> He made his only Championship start in the opening day 1–1 draw at [[Bristol City]] and was subsequently used sparingly by managers [[Aitor Karanka]] and [[Martin O'Neill]], totalling ten games in all.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Clapson |first1=Sarah |title=Diogo Goncalves wants to bring 'joy' to new set of fans after Nottingham Forest snub |url=https://www.nottinghampost.com/sport/football/transfer-news/diogo-goncalves-wants-bring-joy-3024842 |accessdate=16 January 2020 |work=Nottingham Post |date=27 June 2019}}</ref>

====Famalicão (loan)====
Gonçalves and Benfica teammate [[Guga (footballer, born 1997)|Guga]] both moved on 26 June 2019 to [[F.C. Famalicão|Famalicão]], who were returning to the Primeira Liga after a quarter-century away; his deal was again a season-long loan.<ref>{{cite news |title=Diogo Gonçalves e Guga Rodrigues reforçam Famalicão|trans-title=Diogo Gonçalves and Guga Rodrigues bolster Famalicão |url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-nos/famalicao/detalhe/diogo-goncalves-e-guga-rodrigues-reforcam-famalicao |accessdate=16 January 2020 |work=Record |date=26 June 2019 |language=Portuguese}}</ref> He scored his first goal on 19 December in a 3–0 home win over [[C.D. Mafra|Mafra]] to reach the quarter-finals of the [[Taça de Portugal]];<ref>{{cite news |title=Famalicão atinge 'quartos' após jogo onde tudo aconteceu |trans-title=Famalicão reach 'quarters' after game where everything happened|url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/taca-de-portugal/detalhe/jogo-entre-famalicao-e-mafra-em-risco |accessdate=16 January 2020 |work=Record |date=19 December 2019 |language=Portuguese}}</ref> there on 15 January 2020 he netted the only goal at [[F.C. Paços de Ferreira|Paços de Ferreira]] to put the team from [[Vila Nova de Famalicão]] into the last four for the first time since 1946.<ref>{{cite news |title=Diogo Gonçalves marcou o golo que colocou o Famalicão nas 'meias' da Taça|trans-title=Diogo Gonçalves scored the goal that put Famalicão in the 'semis' of the Taça  |url=https://www.noticiasaominuto.com/desporto/1394717/diogo-goncalves-marcou-o-golo-que-colocou-o-famalicao-nas-meias-da-taca |accessdate=16 January 2020 |publisher=Notícias ao Minuto |date=15 January 2020 |language=Portuguese}}</ref>

====Return to Benfica====
In August 2020, Gonçalves renewed his contract with Benfica until 2025.<ref>{{Cite web|date=9 August 2020|title=Diogo Gonçalves renewed his contract|url=https://www.slbenfica.pt/en-us/agora/noticias/2020/08/09/futebol-benfica-equipa-principal-diogo-goncalves-renovacao-contrato|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200829163505/https://www.slbenfica.pt//en-us/agora/noticias/2020/08/09/futebol-benfica-equipa-principal-diogo-goncalves-renovacao-contrato |archive-date=29 August 2020 |access-date=11 January 2021|website=Sport Lisboa e Benfica Official Website}}</ref>

===Copenhagen===
On 5 January 2023, Gonçalves signed a four-year contract with [[Danish Superliga]] side [[F.C. Copenhagen]].<ref>{{cite web |title=FCK køber Diogo Gonçalves |url=https://www.fck.dk/nyhed/fck-koeber-diogo-goncalves |website=F.C. København |access-date=2 April 2023 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230105210234/https://www.fck.dk/nyhed/fck-koeber-diogo-goncalves |archive-date=5 January 2023 |language=da |date=5 January 2023}}</ref> He made his debut for the club on 19 February 2023, starting in a 3–0 away win over [[Silkeborg IF|Silkeborg]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Start-11 mod Silkeborg IF |url=https://www.fck.dk/nyhed/start-11-mod-silkeborg-if-8 |website=F.C. København |access-date=2 April 2023 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230315113632/https://www.fck.dk/nyhed/start-11-mod-silkeborg-if-8 |archive-date=15 March 2023 |language=da |date=19 February 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Silkeborg IF – FC København 0:3 (Superligaen 2022/2023, 18. Round) |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/superligaen-2022-2023-silkeborg-if-fc-koebenhavn/ |website=WorldFootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |access-date=2 April 2023}}</ref> On 5 March 2023, Gonçalves scored his first goal for Copenhagen in the 7–0 thrashing of [[Odense Boldklub|OB]], opening the score from the [[Penalty kick (association football)|penalty spot]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Leth |first1=Rasmus Lomholt |title=FCK vinder 7-0 over OB |url=https://sport.tv2.dk/fodbold/2023-03-05-fck-vinder-7-0-over-ob |access-date=2 April 2023 |work=TV 2 Sport |date=5 March 2023 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230402212512/https://sport.tv2.dk/fodbold/2023-03-05-fck-vinder-7-0-over-ob |archive-date=2 April 2023 |language=da-DK}}</ref>

===Real Salt Lake===
On 9 August 2024, Gonçalves signed a two-year contract with [[Major League Soccer]] Side [[Real Salt Lake]], making a move to the United States after his tenure in Europe. This marked his first experience in a non-European league.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rsl.com/news/real-salt-lake-adds-portuguese-no-10-diogo-goncalves-from-fc-copenhagen|title=Real Salt Lake Adds Portuguese No. 10 Diogo Gonçalves from FC Copenhagen &#124; Real Salt Lake|website=rsl}}</ref>

==International career==
Gonçalves has represented Portugal at various levels up to [[Portugal national under-21 football team|under-21]] level.
