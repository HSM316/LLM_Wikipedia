The '''[[1974 in baseball|1974]] [[Senior League World Series]]''' took place from August 12–17 in [[Gary, Indiana|Gary]], [[Indiana]], United States. [[Pingtung City|Pingtung]], [[Taiwan]] defeated [[Charlotte, North Carolina|Charlotte]], [[North Carolina]] in the championship game. It was Taiwan's third straight championship.

This year saw the tournament field reduced, from ten, to eight teams.
