{{Nihongo|'''Atsushi Shirota'''|代田敦資|Shirota Atsushi|extra=born November 6, 1991 in [[Nagano Prefecture|Nagano]], [[Japan]]}} is a retired Japanese footballer. He is a [[Defender (association football)|defender]].

He was educated at and played for the [[National Institute of Fitness and Sports in Kanoya]] before signing for J2 club, V-Varen Nagasaki.  In 2016, he signed for [[Albirex Niigata Singapore FC|Albirex Niigata FC (Singapore)]]<ref>http://www.albirex.com.sg/en/archives/17152/</ref> from the [[S.League]] before moving to Sleauge club, [[Hougang United]].<ref>http://www.albirex.com.sg/en/archives/21521/</ref> In 2018, he signed for Cambodian club [[Phnom Penh Crown FC]]. On 7 March 2019, he announced his retirement from football at the age of 27.<ref>https://www.facebook.com/390730061284456/posts/817560141934777/</ref>
