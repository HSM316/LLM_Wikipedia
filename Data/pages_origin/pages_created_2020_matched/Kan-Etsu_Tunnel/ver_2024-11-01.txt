The {{Nihongo|'''Kan-Etsu Tunnel'''|関越トンネル|Kan'etsu Tonneru}} is a tunnel on the [[Kan-Etsu Expressway]] in [[Japan]] that runs through [[Mt. Tanigawa]] from [[Minakami, Gunma|Minakami]], [[Gunma]] to [[Yuzawa, Niigata|Yuzawa]], [[Niigata Prefecture|Niigata]]. The northern tube was opened in 1985 and the southern tube was opened in 1991.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://structurae.net/en/structures/kan-etsu-tunnel|accessdate=10 November 2019|title=Kan-etsu Tunnel|work=International Database and Gallery of Structures|publisher=Structurae}}</ref>
