The '''Roman Catholic Diocese of Sincelejo''' ({{lang-la|Sinceleiensis}}) is a [[diocese]] located in the city of [[Sincelejo]] in the [[Ecclesiastical province]] of [[Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Cartagena|Cartagena]] in Colombia.

==History==
* 25 April 1969: Established as Diocese of Sincelejo from the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Cartagena

==Special churches==
*Minor Basilicas:
**Señor de los Milagros in Sincelejo (Lord of Miracles)

==Ordinaries==
*Félix María Torres Parra (1969.04.25 – 1980.12.11) Appointed, Bishop of [[Roman Catholic Diocese of Santa Marta|Santa Marta]]
*Héctor Jaramillo Duque, [[S.D.B.]] (1981.08.03 – 1990.09.16)
*Nel Hedye Beltrán Santamaria (1992.04.29 – 2014.03.15)
*[[José Crispiano Clavijo Méndez]] (2015.02.19 - present)
