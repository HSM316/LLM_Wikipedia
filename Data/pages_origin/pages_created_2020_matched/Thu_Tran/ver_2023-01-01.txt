'''Thu Tran''' is a writer and producer, known for Food Party (2009).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm3494426/bio|title=Thu Tran|website=[[IMDb]]}}</ref> She is the star of the [[Independent Film Channel]] television show [http://www.ifc.com/food-party Food Party] and [https://web.archive.org/web/20130910021645/http://mtvother.com/ MTVother] web series [https://archive.today/20130909053450/http://mtvother.com/mtvothershows/late-night-munchies/ Late Night Munchies].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2009/06/06/arts/television/06food.html|title = A Foodie with a Taste for the Surreal|newspaper = The New York Times|date = 6 June 2009|last1 = Itzkoff|first1 = Dave}}</ref>
She is of [[Vietnam]]ese descent and was raised in Cleveland, Ohio.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://splashlife.com/article/30-under-30-futurist-foodies|title = Welcome to SplashLife}}</ref> She now lives in Brooklyn.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://editorial.rottentomatoes.com/article/five-favorite-films-with-food-partys-thu-tran/|title = Five Favorite Films with Food Party's Thu Tran}}</ref> She crafts many of the puppets herself.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://gothamist.com/2008/12/09/thu_tran_food_party.php |title=Thu Tran, Food Party - Gothamist: New York City News, Food, Arts & Events |access-date=2009-06-03 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090719231014/http://gothamist.com/2008/12/09/thu_tran_food_party.php |archive-date=2009-07-19 }}</ref> She is also a cook.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/showtracker/2010/04/food-party-returns-a-qa-with-thu-tran.html|title = 'Food Party' returns: A Q&A with creator Thu Tran|date = 27 April 2010}}</ref> Thu first learned to cook from her parents and then attended the Cleveland Institute for Art. She then moved to New York and created food-themed art installations.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.poortastemag.com/headline/food-party-at-the-farmers-market-with-thu-tran |title=Archived copy |access-date=2010-10-10 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100731182927/http://www.poortastemag.com/headline/food-party-at-the-farmers-market-with-thu-tran |archive-date=2010-07-31 }}</ref>

Thu was named on Splashlife's list of 30 Under 30: Futuristic Foodies.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://splashlife.com/article/30-under-30-futurist-foodies|title = Welcome to SplashLife}}</ref>
