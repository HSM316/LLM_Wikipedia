'''Megan Manthey''' (born July 22, 1988) is a professional American [[association football|soccer]] [[midfielder]] who has played for the [[Seattle Sounders Women]] of the [[United Soccer Leagues]] [[USL W-League|W-League]]<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.sounderswomen.com/home/608418.html |title = Megan Manthey - Danish Champion Returns Home | accessdate = June 2, 2012 | date = June 2, 2012 |publisher = Seattle Sounders Women}}</ref> among other teams in Europe, most recently Stjarnan, Iceland.

==Personal life==
Megan's hometown is [[Ferndale, Washington]]. She played high school soccer at [[IMG Academy]]<ref>{{cite web|title=IMG Soccer Academy alum signs with Seattle Sounders Women|url=http://blog.imgacademies.com/2012/04/16/img-soccer-academy-alum-signs-with-seattle-sounders-women/|accessdate=June 2, 2012}}</ref> while attending [[The Pendleton School]] located in Bradenton, Florida.<ref>{{cite web|title=IMG Academy soccer program alum Megan Manthey dominates in Denmark |url=http://www.imgacademy.com/latest-headlines/2011-06-30/img-academy-soccer-program-alum-megan-manthey-dominates-denmark#sthash.vA1ydzRu.dpuf/|accessdate=March 8, 2014}}</ref>

==Soccer career==
Manthey played with teams internationally in France and most recently in Iceland. She played with the Seattle Sounders Women for the 2012 season. Previously, Manthey played in 61 matches for Fortuna Hjørring in Denmark.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.fortunahjorring.dk/page.aspx?id=6083 | title = Målscorere sæsonen 2011/12 | accessdate = June 2, 2012 | date = June 2, 2012 | publisher = Fortuna Hjørring}}</ref> Manthey helped Fortuna Hjørring to the Danish Championship in 2009, 2010, and 2011. She played Division I soccer for University of Colorado<ref>{{cite web|title=Megan Manthey - CUBuffs.com - Official Athletics Web Site of the University of Colorado|url=http://www.cubuffs.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=600&ATCLID=189529|publisher=University of Colorado|accessdate=June 2, 2012}}</ref>  and College of Charleston.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.cofcsports.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=14800&ATCLID=1139633 | title = Megan Manthey Biography CofCSports.com - Official web site of College of Charleston Athletics | accessdate = June 2, 2012 | publisher = College of Charleston}}</ref> While with College of Charleston she was an All-Southern Conference selection. She scored 14 goals and 6 assists in 21 games for the 2008 season with the Cougars.
