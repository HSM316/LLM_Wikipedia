'''Villa de Mayo''' is a town in the [[Malvinas Argentinas Partido]] of [[Buenos Aires Province]], Argentina. It is located in the north west of [[Greater Buenos Aires]] urban conurbation around 39&nbsp;km from [[Buenos Aires]].
