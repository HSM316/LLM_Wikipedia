'''''Devra Bhail Deewana''''' ({{Lang-bho|देवरा भईल दिवाना}}) is a 2014 Indian, Bhojpuri language film produced and directed by [[Rajkumar R. Pandey]]. The film stars [[Pradeep Pandey]] ("Chintu"), [[Manoj Tiwari (Delhi politician)|Manoj Tiwari]], [[Pakhi Hegde]], [[Antara Biswas|Monalisa]] and  [[Sanjay Pandey]].<ref name="The Times of India">{{cite news|agency=Times News Network |date=18 March 2014 |url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/bhojpuri/movies/news/Devra-Bhail-Deewana-released/articleshow/32240218.cms|title=Devra Bhail Deewana released |work=[[The Times of India]]|accessdate=25 July 2015}}</ref>

The story of the film deals with the bond of two brothers and a big misunderstanding after the elder brother's marriage. They overcome this and become a happy family.
