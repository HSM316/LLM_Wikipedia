'''Georg Fayer''' (1892 – 5 November 1950) was an Austrian photographer of Hungarian origin. His daughter is artist and photographer {{Ill|Lillian Barylli-Fayer|de}}.

Fayer died in [[Cannes]] when he was about 58 years old.
